{{Center|A BILL}}

To exclude the Internal Revenue Service from the provisions of title 5, United States Code, relating to labor-management relations.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Preventing Unionization of Revenue Service Employees Act” or the “PURSE Act” .

=Sec. 2. Exclusion of Internal Revenue Service=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7103 Section 7103(a)(3) of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7103 subparagraph (G)] , by striking “or” ;
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7103 subparagraph (H)] , by striking the period and inserting “; or” ; and
:(3) by adding after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7103 subparagraph (H)] the following:

<blockquote>
::(I) the [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]] ;.
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act, notwithstanding any provision of law, rule, or regulation, or of any preexisting collective bargaining agreement.
