{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 28, United States Code, to prohibit the exclusion of individuals from service on a Federal jury on account of sexual orientation or gender identity.

={{anchor|HAF42211DE63B47B4B401874032114214}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Jury Access for Capable Citizens and Equality in Service Selection Act of 2013”or the“Jury ACCESS Act”.

={{anchor|H99E0284729C04863966E76E3C495845A}}Sec. 2. Exclusion from Federal juries on account of sexual orientation or gender identity prohibited=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/1862 Section 1862 of title 28, United States Code] , is amended by inserting“sexual orientation, gender identity,”after“sex,”.
