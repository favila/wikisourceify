{{Center|A BILL}}

 To provide borrowers of Federal Family Education Loans with the repayment terms available to borrowers of Federal Direct Loans, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Fairness in Student Loan Repayment Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repayment terms for Federal Family Education Loans=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 455 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e] )is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote> 

===(r) Repayment terms for Federal Family Education Loans– ===



====(1) Applicability of programs available under this section– ====

The [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]]shall apply subsections (d), (e), (f), (l), (m), and (o) to all loans made under part B as if such loans were made under this part.

====(2) Payment of balance and interest– ====

In carrying out paragraph (1), the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]]shall pay to the holder of a loan made under part B the amount of principal, interest, or special allowance payments necessary to apply subsections (d), (e), (f), (l), (m), and (o) to such loan, as appropriate. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Consolidation loans– ==

   Section 428C(a)(3)(B)(i) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087–3 20 U.S.C. 1087–3(a)(3)(B)(i)] )is amended—
:(1) in subclause (III), by striking the semicolon and inserting“; and”;
:(2) in subclause (IV), by striking“; and”and inserting a period; and
:(3) by striking subclause (V).
