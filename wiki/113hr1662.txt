{{Center|A BILL}}

To amendchapter 171of title 28, United States Code, and the Federal Flood Control Act of 1928 to provide for liability for the United States Army Corps of Engineers in cases of damage caused by the gross negligence of an officer or employee of the Corps.

={{anchor|HEC4FDDF69BAE483DA1EC63F9C4D1480B}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Army Corps Accountability Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H7CC036226568479ABBAA0875F5D14504}}Sec. 2. United States Army Corps of Engineers not exempt from liability=



=={{anchor|H98528D606F61421992A4E9B6D2720D20}}(a) Liability for gross negligence generally– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/2680 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 2680(a)] of title 28, United States Code] , is amended by inserting“, except in the case of the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|United States Army Corps of Engineers]] , which shall be liable in the case of a claim arising out of damage caused by the gross negligence of an officer or employee of the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps]] ”before the period.

=={{anchor|HDA7DCC189480405E96CDA09DB1D1660C}}(b) Liability for damage from flood waters– ==

 Section 3 of the Federal Flood Control Act of 1928([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 33 U.S.C. 702c] )is amended in the second paragraph, by striking“: Provided, however, That if”and inserting“, except in the case of the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|United States Army Corps of Engineers]] , which shall be liable underchapter 171of title 28, United States Code, for any such damage caused by the gross negligence of an officer or employee of the Corps. If”.

={{anchor|HB2ABEA2786194427AC78338A7122DA25}}Sec. 3. Effective date=

The amendments made by this Act shall apply in the case of damage that occurred on or after August 29, 2005.
