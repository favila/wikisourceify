{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 46, United States Code, to reauthorize and amend the Fishing Safety Training Grant Program and the Fishing Safety Research Grant Program, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “ Fishing Safety Training and Research Act” .

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization and amendment of Fishing Safety Training Grant Program and Fishing Safety Research Grant Program=



==(a) Fishing Safety Training Grant Program– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/4502 Section 4502(i)of title 46, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/4502 paragraph (4)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/4502 paragraph (6)] ;
:(2) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/4502 paragraph (3)] the following:

<blockquote>
::(4) 
:::(A) The [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary]] shall establish a publicly accessible electronic database that lists the names of individuals who have participated in training sessions carried out with assistance under this subsection.
:::(B) The [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary]] shall require, as a condition of including the name of an individual in such database for any period longer than 18 months, that the individual complete retraining.
::(5) The [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary]] shall require that training carried out with a grant under this section shall be made available in languages other than English, as appropriate for the fishing port in which the training is offered.; and
</blockquote>

:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/4502 paragraph (6)] , as so redesignated, by striking “2010 through 2014” and inserting “2015 through 2025” .

==(b) Fishing Safety Research Grant Program– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/4502 Section 4502(j)(4)of title 46, United States Code,] is amended by striking “2010 through 2014” and inserting “2015 through 2025” .
