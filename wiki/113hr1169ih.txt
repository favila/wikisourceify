{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Secretary of the Interior to transfer to the Secretary of the Navy certain Federal land in Churchill County, Nevada.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Naval Air Station Fallon Housing and Safety Development Act”.

=Sec. 2. Transfer of Department of the Interior Land=



==(a) In general– ==

Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] shall transfer to the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] , without consideration, the Federal land described in subsection (b).

==(b) Description of Federal land– ==

The Federal land referred to in subsection (a)is the parcel of approximately 400 acres of land under the jurisdiction of the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] that—
:(1) is adjacent to Naval Air Station Fallon in Churchill County, Nevada; and
:(2) was withdrawn under Public Land Order 6834 (NV–943–4214–10; N–37875).

==(c) Management– ==

On transfer of the Federal land described under subsection (b)to the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] , the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] shall have full jurisdiction, custody, and control of the Federal land.
