{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to extend for 5 years payment parity with Medicare for primary care services furnished under the Medicaid program, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Medicaid Parity Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of Medicaid payment parity with Medicare for primary care services=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 1902(a)(13)(C) of the Social Security Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1396a 42 U.S.C. 1396a(a)(13)(C)] )is amended by striking“2013 and 2014”and inserting“2013 through 2019”.

==(b) Extending increased FMAP for additional payments for primary care services– ==

   Section 1905(dd) of the Social Security Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1396d 42 U.S.C. 1396d(dd)] )is amended by striking“and before January 1, 2015”and inserting“and before January 1, 2020”.
