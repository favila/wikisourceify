{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amendchapter 44of title 18, United States Code, to clarify the circumstances under which the enhanced penalty provisions for subsequent convictions apply.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Firearm Recidivist Sentencing Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Amendments to enhanced penalties provisions=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924  Section 924(c)of title 18, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 paragraph (1)]—
::(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 subparagraph (A)], by striking“Except to the extent that a greater minimum sentence is otherwise provided by this subsection or by any other provision of law, any”and inserting“Any”;
::(B) in each of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 subparagraphs (A) through (C)], by striking“less”each place it appears and inserting“more”; and
::(C) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 subparagraph (C)]—
:::(i) by striking“In the case of a second or subsequent conviction under this subsection”and inserting“If a person is convicted under this subsection after a prior conviction under this subsection has become final”; and
:::(ii) by striking“imprisonment”and inserting“a term of imprisonment for any term of years or”;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 paragraph (5)]—
::(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 subparagraph (A)], by striking“less”and inserting“more”; and
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 subparagraph (B)(i)], by striking“punished by death or”; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
::(6) The provisions of  section 411 of the Controlled Substances Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/21/851 21 U.S.C. 851] ), other than subsections (a)(2) and (e) of such section , as in effect on the date of the enactment of this paragraph, shall apply to sentencing for convictions under this subsection in the same manner in which such provisions apply to sentencing for offenses under part D of title II of such Act . . 
</blockquote>
