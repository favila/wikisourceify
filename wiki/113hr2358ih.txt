{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to direct the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to give preference to local contractors, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Preference for Local Veteran Contractors Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Preference for local contractors=



==(a) Local contractors– ==



===(1) In general– ===

  Chapter 5of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/513 section 513]the following new section:

<blockquote> 

===Sec. 513A. Preference for local contractors===



====(a) In general– ====


:(1) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]shall give preference for awarding a covered contract to a local contractor.
:(2) In giving preference for awarding a covered contract to a local contractor under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/513A paragraph (1)]—
::(A) if such local contractor is a small business concern, such preference shall be given in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 section 8127(i) of this title]; and
::(B) if such local contractor is not a small business concern, the local contractor shall be treated as a contractor covered under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 section 8127(i)(7)].

====(b) Notice required– ====

If the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]awards a covered contract to a contractor that is not a local contractor, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]shall publish a public notice explaining why a local contractor was not awarded the contract.

====(c) Definitions– ====

In this section:
:(1) The term covered contract means a contract for—
::(A) the construction or maintenance of a facility of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]]; or
::(B) services provided to a particular facility or campus of the Department.
:(2) The term local contractor means a prime contractor that—
::(A) submits a bid or proposal for a covered contract; and
::(B) has its principal offices or location within a 60-mile radius of the facility of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]]covered by such contract. . 
</blockquote>


===(2) Clerical amendment– ===

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by inserting after the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/513 section 513]the following new item:

<blockquote>  513A. Preference for local contractors.  . 
</blockquote>


===(3) Conforming amendment– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8106  Section 8106(a)of title 38, United States Code], is amended by striking“The Secretary”and inserting“In accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/513A section 513A]of this title, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] ”.

==(b) Small business concerns– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127  Section 8127(i)of title 38, United States Code], is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 

===(i) Priority for Contracting Preferences– ===

Preferences for awarding contracts to small business concerns shall be applied in the following order of priority:
:(1) Contracts awarded pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 subsection (b)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (c)], or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (d)]to small business concerns owned and controlled by veterans with service-connected disabilities that, for purposes of such a contract, are local contractors under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/513A section 513A of this title].
:(2) Contracts awarded pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 subsection (b)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (c)], or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (d)]to small business concerns owned and controlled by veterans with service-connected disabilities that are not covered by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 paragraph (1)].
:(3) Contracts awarded pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 subsection (b)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (c)], or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (d)]to small business concerns owned and controlled by veterans that—
::(A) are not covered by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 paragraphs (1)]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (2)]; and
::(B) for purposes of such a contract, are local contractors under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/513A section 513A of this title].
:(4) Contracts awarded pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 subsection (b)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (c)], or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (d)]to small business concerns owned and controlled by veterans that are not covered by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 paragraphs (1)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (2)], or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 (3)].
:(5) Contracts awarded pursuant to  section 8(a) of the Small Business Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/637 15 U.S.C. 637(a)] )or  section 31 of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/657a 15 U.S.C. 657a])to a contractor that, for purposes of such a contract, are local contractors under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/513A section 513A of this title].
:(6) Contracts awarded pursuant to any other small business contracting preference to a contractor that, for purposes of such a contract, are local contractors under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/513A section 513A of this title].
:(7) Contracts awarded pursuant to  section 8(a) of the Small Business Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/637 15 U.S.C. 637(a)] )or  section 31 of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/657a 15 U.S.C. 657a])to a contractor that is not covered by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 paragraph (5)].
:(8) Contracts awarded pursuant to any other small business contracting preference to a contractor that is not covered by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127 paragraph (6)]. . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
