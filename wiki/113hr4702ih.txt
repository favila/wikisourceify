{{Center|A BILL}}

 To extend until 2030 the time period required to be covered by the United States Space Protection Strategy.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Space Protection Strategy Extension Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of time period to be covered by the space protection strategy=

   Section 911(d) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2008 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2271  10 U.S.C. 2271note])is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
:(4) Fiscal years 2026 through 2030. . 
</blockquote>
