{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to amend the definition of the term “spouse” to recognize new State definitions of such term for the purpose of the laws administered by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Veteran Spouses Equal Treatment Act” .

=Sec. 2. Definition of spouse for purposes of veteran benefits to reflect new state definitions of spouse=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 Section 101 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 paragraph (3)] , by striking “of the opposite sex” ; and
:(2) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 paragraph (31)] and inserting the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(31) Notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/1/7 section 7 of title 1] , an individual shall be considered a “spouse” if the marriage of the individual is valid in the State in which the marriage was entered into or, in the case of a marriage entered into outside any State, if the marriage is valid in the place in which the marriage was entered into and the marriage could have been entered into in a State. In this paragraph, the term “State” has the meaning given that term in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 paragraph (20)] , except that the term also includes the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands..
</blockquote>
