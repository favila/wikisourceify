{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit a reduction in funding for the defense commissary system in fiscal year 2015 pending the report of the Military Compensation and Retirement Modernization Commission.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Military Commissary Sustainment Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition on reduction in funding for the defense commissary system in fiscal year 2015 pending the report of the Military Compensation and Retirement Modernization Commission=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the monthly amount of funds made available by the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]]for the defense commissary system during fiscal year 2015 may not be reduced below the average monthly amount of funds made available for that system during fiscal year 2014 until the date of the report of the [[w:|Military Compensation and Retirement Modernization Commission]]under  section 674(f) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2013 ( [[Public Law 112-239|Public Law 112–239]]  ;126 Stat. 1792).
