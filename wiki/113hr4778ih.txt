{{Center|A BILL}}

 To authorize the award of a military service medal to members of the Armed Forces who were exposed to ionizing radiation as a result of participation in the testing of nuclear weapons or under other circumstances.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Atomic Veterans Service Medal Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Atomic Veterans Service Medal=



==(a) Service medal required– ==

The [[w:|Secretary of Defense]]shall design and produce a military service medal, to be known as the“Atomic Veterans Service Medal”, to honor retired and former members of the Armed Forces who are radiation-exposed veterans (as such term is defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1112 section 1112(c)(3) of title 38, United States Code]).

==(b) Distribution of medal– ==



===(1) Issuance to retired and former members– ===

At the request of a radiation-exposed veteran, the [[w:|Secretary of Defense]]shall issue the Atomic Veterans Service Medal to the veteran.

===(2) Issuance to next-of-kin– ===

In the case of a radiation-exposed veteran who is deceased, the [[w:|Secretary]]may provide for issuance of the Atomic Veterans Service Medal to the next-of-kin of the person.

===(3) Application– ===

The [[w:|Secretary]]shall prepare and disseminate as appropriate an application by which radiation-exposed veterans and their next-of-kin may apply to receive the Atomic Veterans Service Medal.
