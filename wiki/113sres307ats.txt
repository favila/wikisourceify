 Permitting the collection of clothing, toys, food, and housewares during the holiday season for charitable purposes in Senate buildings.   

=Section 1. Collection of clothing, toys, food, and housewares during the holiday season for charitable purposes in Senate buildings=



==(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of the rules or regulations of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] —
:(1) a Senator, officer of the Senate, or employee of the Senate may collect from another Senator, officer of the Senate, or employee of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] within Senate buildings nonmonetary donations of clothing, toys, food, and housewares for charitable purposes related to serving persons in need or members of the Armed Forces and the families of those members during the holiday season, if the charitable purposes do not otherwise violate any rule or regulation of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] or of Federal law; and
:(2) a Senator, officer of the Senate, or employee of the Senate may work with a nonprofit organization with respect to the delivery of donations described under paragraph (1) .

==(b) Expiration– ==

The authority provided by this resolution shall expire at the end of the first session of the 113th Congress.
