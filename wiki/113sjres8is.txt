Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Internal Revenue Service of the Department of the Treasury relating to taxable medical devices.

= =

Reg. 72924 (December 7, 2012)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
