{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to review the list of veterans designated as former prisoners of war, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   POW Accountability Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Review of lists of former prisoners of war=



==(a) Review of lists of prisoners of war– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]]shall review the VA POW list and the DOD POW list to identify any discrepancies in such lists.

==(b) Inspector General review of process– ==

The [[w:Inspector General|Inspector General of the Department of Veterans Affairs]]shall review the process by which the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]determines that a veteran is a former prisoner of war, including whether the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]is following guidelines established by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]to determine that a veteran is a former prisoner of war.

==(c) Report– ==

Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]]shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]a report on the VA POW list, including the following:
:(1) Any discrepancies, by period of conflict, in the number of prisoners of war included on the VA POW list and the DOD POW list.
:(2) With respect to veterans included on the VA POW list who are not included on the DOD POW list, information regarding how such determinations were made, including what types of evidence were used, in a manner that does not personally identify such veterans.
:(3) The results of the review of the [[w:Inspector General|Inspector General]]under subsection (b) , without change.

==(d) Definitions– ==

In this section:
:(1) The term DOD POW list means the list maintained by the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]], acting through the Defense Prisoner of War/Missing Personnel Office, of members of the Armed Forces who were prisoners of war.
:(2) The term VA POW list means the list maintained by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]]of veterans whom the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]determines are former prisoners of war.
