{{Center|A BILL}}

To exempt National Forest System land in the State of Alaska from the Roadless Area Conservation Rule.

=Section 1. Roadless area conservation rule exemption=

The Roadless Area Conservation Rule established under part 294 of title 36, Code of Federal Regulations (and successor regulations), does not apply with respect to any National Forest System land in the State of Alaska.
