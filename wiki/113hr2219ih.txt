To reauthorize the Integrated Coastal and Ocean Observation System Act of 2009.

=Section 1. Reauthorization of Integrated Coastal and Ocean Observation System Act of 2009=

The Integrated Coastal and Ocean Observation System Act of 2009is amended by striking “are authorized to be appropriated to the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary of Commerce]] for fiscal years 2009 through 2013 such sums as are necessary” and inserting “is authorized to be appropriated to the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary of Commerce]] for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018 $29,600,000” .
