={{anchor|idC42156D138044CA081D2E4AF601F0CA8}}Section 1. Carbon dioxide reductions in China, India, and Russia=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency]] or the head of any other Federal agency shall not regulate carbon dioxide emissions until the date on which the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary of Commerce]] certifies in writing that each of the Peopleâ€™s Republic of China, the Republic of India, and Russia have initiated measures that require carbon dioxide emission reductions that are substantially similar to the carbon dioxide emission reductions proposed for the United States.
