{{Center|A BILL}}

 To permit the televising of Supreme Court proceedings.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Cameras in the Courtroom Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Amendment to title28 =


:(a) In general–   Chapter 45of title 28, United States Code, is amended by inserting at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 678. Televising Supreme Court proceedings==

The [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Supreme Court]]shall permit television coverage of all open sessions of the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]]unless the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]]decides, by a vote of the majority of justices, that allowing such coverage in a particular case would constitute a violation of the due process rights of 1 or more of the parties before the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]]. . 
</blockquote>

:(b) Clerical amendment– The chapter analysis forchapter 45 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by inserting at the end the following:

<blockquote>  678. Televising Supreme Court proceedings.  . 
</blockquote>
