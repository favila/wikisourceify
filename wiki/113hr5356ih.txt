{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3720D section 3720D]of title 31, United States Code, to prohibit wage garnishment by the Environmental Protection Agency.   

=Section 1. Short Title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Preventing the EPA from Garnishing Wages Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition on Wage Garnishment by the Environmental Protection Agency=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3720D  Section 3720Dof title 31, United States Code], is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:(i) This section shall not apply to a debt owed pursuant to a program administered by the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Environmental Protection Agency]]. . 
</blockquote>
