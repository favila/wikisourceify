{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the Department of Veterans Affairs Health Professionals Educational Assistance Program, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Improvements to Department of Veterans Affairs Health Professionals Educational Assistance Program=



==(a) Extension of Department of Veterans Affairs Health Professional Scholarship Program– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7619 Section 7619 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “December 31, 2014” and inserting “December 31, 2019” .

==(b) Repeal of cap on amount of education debt reduction payments under Department of Veterans Affairs Education Debt Reduction Program– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7683 Subsection (d) of section 7683 of such title] is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

===(d) Maximum amount– ===

The total amount payable to a participant in the Education Debt Reduction Program for any year may not exceed the amount of the principal and interest on loans referred to in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7683 subsection (a)] that is paid by the individual during such year..
</blockquote>
