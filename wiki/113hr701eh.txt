 Section 3(b)(2) of the Securities Act of 1933( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/77c 15 U.S.C. 77c(b)(2)] ] )is amended in the matter preceding subparagraph (A)by striking “The Commission” and inserting “Not later than October 31, 2013, the [[w:U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission|Commission]] ” .Passed the House of Representatives May 15, 2013.Karen L.
