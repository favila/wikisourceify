{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Payments in Lieu of Taxes Program to include all lands owned by the United States Government that are under the jurisdiction of the Department of Defense within the definition of entitlement lands for which payments are made, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Inclusion of all Federal lands under the jurisdiction of the Department of Defense within the Payments in Lieu of Taxes Program=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/6901  Section 6901(1)of title 31, United States Code], is amended by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/6901 subparagraph (D)]and inserting the following new[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/6901 subparagraph (D)]:

<blockquote> 
:(D) that is under the jurisdiction of the [[w:|Secretary of Defense]]or the Secretary of a military department; . 
</blockquote>
