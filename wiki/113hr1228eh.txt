{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 123 South 9thStreet in De Pere, Wisconsin, as the“Corporal Justin D. Ross Post Office Building”.   

=Section 1. Corporal Justin D. Ross Post Office Building=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]located at 123 South 9thStreet in De Pere, Wisconsin, shall be known and designated as the“Corporal Justin D. Ross Post Office Building”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Corporal Justin D. Ross Post Office Building”.    Passed the House of Representatives March 26, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
