{{Center|A BILL}}

To save at least $10,000,000,000 by consolidating some duplicative and overlapping Government programs.

=Section 1. Consolidating unnecessary duplicative and overlapping Government programs=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, not later than 150 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget]] shall coordinate with the heads of the relevant department and agencies to—
:(1) use available administrative authority to eliminate, consolidate, or streamline Government programs and agencies with duplicative and overlapping missions identified in—
::(A) the February 2012 [[w:Government Accountability Office|Government Accountability Office]] report to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] , entitled “Opportunities to Reduce Duplication, Overlap and Fragmentation, Achieve Savings, and Enhance Revenue” (GAO–12–342SP); and
::(B) the March 2011 [[w:Government Accountability Office|Government Accountability Office]] report to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] , entitled “Opportunities to Reduce Potential Duplication in Government Programs, Save Tax Dollars, and Enhance Revenue” (GAO–11–318SP) and apply the savings towards deficit reduction;
:(2) identify and report to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] any legislative changes required to further eliminate, consolidate, or streamline Government programs and agencies with duplicative and overlapping missions identified in—
::(A) the February 2012 [[w:Government Accountability Office|Government Accountability Office]] report to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] , entitled “Opportunities to Reduce Duplication, Overlap and Fragmentation, Achieve Savings, and Enhance Revenue” (GAO–12–342SP); and
::(B) the March 2011 [[w:Government Accountability Office|Government Accountability Office]] report to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] , entitled “Opportunities to Reduce Potential Duplication in Government Programs, Save Tax Dollars, and Enhance Revenue” (GAO–11–318SP);
:(3) determine the total cost savings that shall result to each agency, office, and department from the actions described in paragraph (1); and
:(4) rescind from the appropriate accounts the amount greater of—
::(A) $10,000,000,000; or
::(B) the total amount of cost savings estimated by paragraph (3).
