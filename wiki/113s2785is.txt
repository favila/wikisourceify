{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency to publish a health advisory and submit reports with respect to microcystins in drinking water.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Safe and Secure Drinking Water Protection Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Microcystins in drinking water=



==(a) Health advisory– ==

Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency]](referred to in this Act as the“Administrator”) shall develop and publish a health advisory including recommendations on—
:(1) 
::(A) the level of microcystins in drinking water below which the water is expected to be safe for human consumption; and
::(B) feasible treatment techniques and other means for achieving that level; and
:(2) standardized procedures for testing for microcystins in drinking water.

==(b) Reports– ==

Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, and each year thereafter, the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]a report that includes—
:(1) a description of the status of the efforts of the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]to determine whether to regulate drinking water with respect to the level of microcystins;
:(2) a description of the steps taken by the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]to promote testing of drinking water for microcystins in areas that have been affected by harmful algal blooms; and
:(3) an analysis of available treatment techniques and other means for addressing microcystins in drinking water.
