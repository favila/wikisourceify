{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Patient Choice Restoration Act” .

=Sec. 2. Repeal=



==(a) In general– ==

The following Acts, and the amendments made by such Acts, are repealed:
:(1) The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act([[Public Law -|Public Law 111–148]] ).
:(2) The Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010([[Public Law -|Public Law 111–152]] ).

==(b) Effect of law– ==

The provisions of law amended by the Acts referred to in subsection (a)are restored as if such Acts had never been enacted.
