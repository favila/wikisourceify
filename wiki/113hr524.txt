{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to
		  clarify that the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency does not
		  have the authority to disapprove a permit after it has been issued by the
		  Secretary of the Army under section 404 of such Act.


{{section|H8E67E9A7E86D46DFB2EF02AAA48039DF|1. Permits for dredged or fill material}}

        Section 404(c) of the
			 Federal Water Pollution Control Act
 (33 U.S.C. 1344(c)
)
 is amended by striking
			 The [[United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]] is authorized
 and inserting Until such
			 time as the [[United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] has issued a permit under this section, the [[United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]
			 is authorized
.
