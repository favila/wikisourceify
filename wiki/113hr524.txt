{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to
		  clarify that the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency does not
		  have the authority to disapprove a permit after it has been issued by the
		  Secretary of the Army under section 404 of such Act.

={{anchor|H8E67E9A7E86D46DFB2EF02AAA48039DF}}Section 1. Permits for dredged or fill
			 material=

Section 404(c) of the
			 Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 U.S.C. 1344(c)) is amended by striking
			 “The [[Wikipedia:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]] is authorized” and inserting “Until such
			 time as the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] has issued a permit under this section, the [[Wikipedia:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]
			 is authorized”.
