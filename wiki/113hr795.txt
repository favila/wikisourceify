{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the National Labor Relations Act to modify the authority of the National Labor Relations Board with respect to rulemaking, issuance of complaints, and authority over unfair labor practices.

{{section|H57A1CA7F54DD432094DE29AF929FACD9|3. Regulations}}
Not later than 6 months after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[National Labor Relations Board|National Labor Relations Board]] shall review and revise all regulations promulgated before such date to implement the amendments made by this Act.
