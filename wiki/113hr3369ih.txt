{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to inter in national cemeteries individuals who supported the United States in Laos during the Vietnam War era.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Hmong Veterans’ Service Recognition Act” .

=Sec. 2. Eligibility for interment in national cemeteries=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2402 Section 2402(a)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
:(10) Any individual—
::(A) who—
:::(i) was naturalized pursuant to section 2(1) of the Hmong Veterans’ Naturalization Act of 2000([[Public Law -|[[Public Law 106-207|Public Law 106–207]] ]] ; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1423 8 U.S.C. 1423note] ); and
:::(ii) at the time of the individual’s death resided in the United States; or
::(B) who—
:::(i) the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] determines served with a special guerrilla unit or irregular forces operating from a base in Laos in support of the Armed Forces of the United States at any time during the period beginning February 28, 1961, and ending May 7, 1975; and
:::(ii) at the time of the individual’s death—
::::(I) was a citizen of the United States or an alien lawfully admitted for permanent residence in the United States; and
::::(II) resided in the United States..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this Act shall apply with respect to an individual dying on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
