{{Center|A BILL}}

To enhance criminal penalties for straw purchasers of firearms.

={{anchor|H3CB3720364124955901F728467ABA171}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Straw Purchaser Penalty Enhancement Act”.

={{anchor|H5E47DC2F37CE4D4AA4AF8672C435DD1F}}Sec. 2. Sentencing enhancement for certain straw purchasers=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 Section 924 of title 18, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(q) 
::(1) Whoever violates[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 section 922(a)(6)] or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 subsection (a)(1)(A) of this section] —
:::(A) knowing or having reason to believe that the violation will further the transfer of two or more firearms to a person prohibited by law from shipping, transporting, possessing or receiving a firearm; and
:::(B) with the intent to conceal from the transferor of the firearm the identity of the person referred to in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 subparagraph (A)] ,shall, in addition to the punishment otherwise provided for the violation, be sentenced to a term of imprisonment of 2 years.
::(2) Notwithstanding any other provision of law:
:::(A) The court shall not place on probation any person convicted of a violation described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 paragraph (1)] .
:::(B) The court shall not reduce a term of imprisonment to be imposed for such a violation so as to compensate for, or otherwise take into account, a term of imprisonment imposed or to be imposed for a separate such violation.
:::(C) 
::::(i) Except as provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/ii/ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 clause (ii)] ] , a term of imprisonment imposed on a person under this subsection shall not run concurrently with a term of imprisonment imposed on the person under any other provision of law.
::::(ii) A term of imprisonment imposed on a person for such a violation may, in the discretion of the court, run concurrently, in whole or in part, with another term of imprisonment imposed on the person by the court at the same time for a separate such violation, subject to applicable guidelines and policy statements issued by the [[w:United States Sentencing Commission|Sentencing Commission]] pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/994 section 994 of title 28] .
:::(D) 
::::(i) [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/3553 Section 3553(a)] shall apply in sentencing under this subsection.
::::(ii) Before imposing a sentence in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/3553/a section 3553(a)] , the court shall give the parties reasonable notice of the intent of the court to do so and an opportunity to respond.
::::(iii) If the court imposes a sentence in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/3553 section 3553(a)] , the court shall state, in the written statement of reasons, the factors under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/3553 section 3553(a)] that require imposition of a sentence below the statutory minimum.
::(3) The [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] shall issue regulations requiring any form required to be completed by the purchaser of a firearm from a person licensed under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 section 923] to include a notice of the provisions of this subsection..
</blockquote>
