To make improvements to the Children’s Gasoline Burn Prevention Act.

=Section 1. Amendment to the Children’s Gasoline Burn Prevention Act=

 Section 2 of the Children’s Gasoline Burn Prevention Act([[Public Law -|Public Law 110–278]] ;122 Stat. 2602)is amended—
:(1) in the heading, by striking “Child-Resistant Portable Gasoline Containers” and inserting “Portable Fuel Container Standards” ;
:(2) by striking subsection (b)and inserting the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

===(b) Requirements– ===

Effective 6 months after the date of enactment of this Act, each portable fuel container manufactured on or after that date for sale in the United States shall conform to the provisions of ASTM standards F852–08, F2517–09, F839–83 (2006), F976–08, F926–02 (2007), as they are by their terms applicable to gasoline, kerosene, or diesel portable fuel containers, as issued by ASTM International.;
</blockquote>

:(3) in subsection (c), by striking “gasoline” each place that it appears; and
:(4) in subsection (d), by striking “child resistance requirements of ASTM F2517–05” and inserting “provisions of any standard described in subsection (b),” .
