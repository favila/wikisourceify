{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Wagner-Peyser Act to include American Samoa in the employment services provided under that Act, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Inclusion of American Samoa in the Wagner-Peyser Act=

The Wagner-Peyser Act is amended—
:(1) in  section 2(5) ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/49a 29 U.S.C. 49a(5)] ), by inserting“American Samoa,”after“Guam,”;
:(2) in section 5(b)(1) ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/49d 29 U.S.C. 49d(b)(1)] ), by inserting“and American Samoa”after“Guam”; and
:(3) in  section 6(a) ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/49e  29 U.S.C. 49e(a) ])—
::(A) by striking“allot to Guam”and inserting “allot to—

<blockquote> 
:::(1) Guam ; 
</blockquote>

::(B) by striking the period at the end and inserting a semicolon; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:::(2) American Samoa an amount which, in relation to the total amount available for the fiscal year, is equal to the allotment percentage that Guam received of amounts available under this Act in fiscal year 1983 . 
</blockquote>
