{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to provide for the termination of further retirement benefits for Members of Congress, except the right to continue participating in the Thrift Savings Plan, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Amendments relating to the Civil Service Retirement System=



==(a) In general– ==

Subchapter III of chapter 83 of title 5, United States Code, is amended by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335 section 8335] the following:

<blockquote>

==Sec. 8335a. Termination of further retirement coverage of Members of Congress==



===(a) In general– ===

Notwithstanding any other provision of this subchapter, effective as of the date of enactment of this section—
:(1) a Member shall not be subject to this subchapter for any further period of time; and
:(2) no further Government contributions or deductions from basic pay may be made with respect to such Member for deposit in the Treasury of the United States to the credit of the Fund.

===(b) Prior rights not affected– ===

Nothing in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (a)] shall be considered to nullify, modify, or otherwise affect any right, entitlement, or benefit under this subchapter with respect to any Member covering any period prior to the date of enactment of this section.

===(c) Right To participate in Thrift Savings Plan not affected– ===

Nothing in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (a)] shall affect the eligibility of a Member to participate in the Thrift Savings Plan in accordance with otherwise applicable provisions of law.

===(d) Regulations– ===



====(1) In general– ====

Any regulations necessary to carry out this section may—
:(A) except with respect to matters under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subparagraph (B)] , be prescribed by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the Office of Personnel Management]] ; and
:(B) with respect to matters relating to the Thrift Savings Plan, be prescribed by the Executive Director (as defined by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 section 8401(13)] ).

====(2) Refunds– ====

The regulations under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a paragraph (1)(A)] shall, in the case of any Member described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (a)] , provide that the lump-sum credit shall be payable to such Member to the same extent and in the same manner as if such Member satisfied [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8342 paragraphs (1)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8342 (3)] , and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8342 (4) of section 8342(a)] as of the date of enactment of this section.

===(e) Exclusion– ===

For purposes of this section, the term Member does not include the [[w:Office of the Vice President of the United States|Vice President]] ..
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning ofchapter 83 of title 5, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item relating to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335 section 8335] the following:

<blockquote>8335a. Termination of further retirement coverage of Members of Congress..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 2. Amendments relating to the Federal Employees’ Retirement System=



==(a) In general– ==

Subchapter II of chapter 84 of title 5, United States Code, is amended by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425 section 8425] the following:

<blockquote>

==Sec. 8425a. Termination of further retirement coverage of Members of Congress==



===(a) In general– ===

Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, effective as of the date of enactment of this section—
:(1) in the case of an individual who first becomes a Member before such date of enactment—
::(A) such Member shall not be subject to this chapter for any further period of time after such date of enactment; and
::(B) no further Government contributions or deductions from basic pay may be made with respect to such Member for deposit in the Treasury of the United States to the credit of the Fund; and
:(2) in the case of an individual who first becomes a Member on or after such date of enactment—
::(A) such Member shall not be subject to this chapter; and
::(B) no Government contributions or deductions from basic pay may be made with respect to such Member for deposit in the Treasury of the United States to the credit of the Civil Service Retirement and Disability Fund.

===(b) Prior rights not affected– ===

Nothing in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (a)] shall be considered to nullify, modify, or otherwise affect any right, entitlement, or benefit under this chapter with respect to any Member covering any period prior to the date of enactment of this section.

===(c) Right To participate in Thrift Savings Plan not affected– ===

Nothing in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (a)] shall affect the eligibility of a Member to participate in the Thrift Savings Plan in accordance with otherwise applicable provisions of law.

===(d) Regulations– ===



====(1) In general– ====

Any regulations necessary to carry out this section may—
:(A) except with respect to matters under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subparagraph (B)] , be prescribed by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the Office of Personnel Management]] ; and
:(B) with respect to matters relating to the Thrift Savings Plan, be prescribed by the Executive Director (as defined by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 section 8401(13)] ).

====(2) Refunds– ====

The regulations under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a paragraph (1)(A)] shall, in the case of a Member described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (a)(1)] , provide that the lump-sum credit shall be payable to such Member to the same extent and in the same manner as if such Member satisfied [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8424 paragraphs (1)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8424 (3)] , and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8424 (4) of section 8424(a)] as of the date of enactment of this section.

===(e) Exclusion– ===

For purposes of this section, the term Member does not include the [[w:Office of the Vice President of the United States|Vice President]] ..
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning ofchapter 84 of title 5, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item relating to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425 section 8425] the following:

</blockquote>
