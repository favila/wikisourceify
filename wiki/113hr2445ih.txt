{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the corporate average fuel economy standards.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “CAFE Standards Repeal Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Repealing the corporate average fuel economy standards=

Chapter 329of title 49, United States Code, and the item relating to such chapter in the analysis of chapters for [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/ subtitle VI of such title] , are repealed.
