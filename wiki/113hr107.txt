{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to limit the circumstances in which official time may be used by a Federal employee.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Federal Employee Accountability Act of 2013”.

2. Repeal of certain provisions relating to official time


Section 7131 of title 5, United States Code , is amended—
: by striking subsections (a) and (c) ;: by redesignating subsections (b) and (d) as subsections (a) and (b) , respectively; and: in subsection (b) (as so redesignated by paragraph (2) ), by striking “Except as provided in the preceding subsections of this section— ”and inserting “Except as provided in subsection (a) — ”.
