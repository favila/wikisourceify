{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Wagner-Peyser Act to include the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands in the employment services provided under that Act.

=Section 1. Inclusion of the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands in the Wagner-Peyser Act=

The Wagner-Peyser Actis amended—
:(1) in section 2(5)( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/49a 29 U.S.C. 49a(5)] ] ), by inserting “the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands,” after “Guam,” ;
:(2) in section 5(b)(1)( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/49d 29 U.S.C. 49d(b)(1)] ] ), by inserting “and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands” after “Guam” ; and
:(3) in section 6(a)( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/49e 29 U.S.C. 49e(a)] ] )—
::(A) by striking “allot to Guam” and inserting

<blockquote>allot to—
:::(1) Guam;
</blockquote>

::(B) by striking the period at the end and inserting “; and” ; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:::(2) the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands an amount which, in relation to the total amount available for the fiscal year, is equal to the allotment percentage that Guam received of amounts available under this Act in fiscal year 1983..
</blockquote>
