{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to conduct a study on matters relating to the burial of unclaimed remains of veterans in national cemeteries, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Dignified Interment of Our Veterans Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Department of Veterans Affairs study on matters relating to burial of unclaimed remains of veterans in national cemeteries=



==(a) Study and report required– ==

Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]]shall—
:(1) complete a study on matters relating to the interring of unclaimed remains of veterans in national cemeteries under the control of the [[w:National Cemetery Administration|National Cemetery Administration]]; and
:(2) submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]a report on the findings of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]with respect to the study required under paragraph (1).

==(b) Matters studied– ==

The matters studied under subsection (a)(1) shall include the following:
:(1) Determining the scope of issues relating to unclaimed remains of veterans, including an estimate of the number of unclaimed remains of veterans.
:(2) Assessing the effectiveness of the procedures of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]for working with persons or entities having custody of unclaimed remains to facilitate interment of unclaimed remains of veterans in national cemeteries under the control of the [[w:National Cemetery Administration|National Cemetery Administration]].
:(3) Assessing State and local laws that affect the ability of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]to inter unclaimed remains of veterans in national cemeteries under the control of the [[w:National Cemetery Administration|National Cemetery Administration]].
:(4) Developing recommendations for such legislative or administrative action as the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]considers appropriate.

==(c) Methodology– ==



===(1) Number of unclaimed remains– ===

In estimating the number of unclaimed remains of veterans under subsection (b)(1), the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]may review such subset of applicable entities as the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]considers appropriate, including a subset of funeral homes and coroner offices that possess unclaimed veterans remains.

===(2) Assessment of State and local laws– ===

In assessing State and local laws under subsection (b)(3), the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]may assess such sample of applicable State and local laws as the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]considers appropriate in lieu of reviewing all applicable State and local laws.
