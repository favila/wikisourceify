{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the energy credit for certain property under construction.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Renewable Energy Parity Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of energy credit for certain property under construction=



==(a) Solar energy property– ==

Paragraphs[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/48 (2)(A)(i)(II)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/48 (3)(A)(ii) of section 48(a) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986]are each amended by striking“periods ending”and inserting“property the construction of which begins”.

==(b) Qualified fuel cell property– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/48 Section 48(c)(1)(D) of such Code]is amended by striking“for any period after December 31, 2016”and inserting“the construction of which does not begin before January 1, 2017”.

==(c) Qualified microturbine property– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/48 Section 48(c)(2)(D) of such Code]is amended by striking“for any period after December 31, 2016”and inserting“the construction of which does not begin before January 1, 2017”.

==(d) Combined heat and power system property– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/48 Section 48(c)(3)(A)(iv) of such Code]is amended by striking“which is placed in service”and inserting“construction of which begins”.

==(e) Qualified small wind energy property– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/48 Section 48(c)(4)(C) of such Code]is amended by striking“for any period after December 31, 2016”and inserting“the construction of which does not begin before January 1, 2017”.

==(f) Thermal energy property– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/48 Section 48(a)(3)(A)(vii) of such Code]is amended by striking“periods ending”and inserting“property the construction of which begins”.

==(g) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
