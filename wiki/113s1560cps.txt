{{Center|A BILL}}

 To allow certain emergency relief amounts to be made available to the Federal Highway Administration to use for disasters occurring in calendar year 2013.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Deficit Neutral Disaster Relief Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Emergency relief projects=



==(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]]may obligate not more than $450,000,000 of the amounts made available to carry out[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/125  section 125of title 23, United States Code], under  chapter 9 of title X of division A of the Disaster Relief Appropriations Act, 2013 ( [[Public Law 113-2|Public Law 113–2]]  ;127 Stat. 34)under the heading“ Emergency Relief Program ”under the heading“ Federal-aid Highways ”under the heading“   [[w:Federal Highway Administration|Federal Highway Administration]]  ”for emergency relief projects in the State of Colorado arising from damage caused by flooding events in that State in calendar year 2013.

==(b) Emergency Designation for Congressional Enforcement– ==

In the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]], this Act is designated as an emergency requirement pursuant tosection 403(a) of S. Con. Res. 13 (111th Congress), the concurrent resolution on the budget for fiscal year 2010.

==(c) Emergency Designation for Statutory PAYGO– ==

This Act is designated as an emergency requirement pursuant to  section 4(g) of the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010 ( [[Public Law 111-139|Public Law 111–139]]  ; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/933 2 U.S.C. 933(g)] ).
