{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

That at 10:30 a.m. on Monday, January 21, 2013, the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] shall proceed to the West Front of the Capitol for the purpose of attending the inaugural ceremonies of the [[w:President of the United States|President]] and [[w:Office of the Vice President of the United States|Vice President of the United States]] ; and that upon the conclusion of the ceremonies the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] stands adjourned until 10 a.m. on Tuesday, January 22, 2013 for morning-hour debate and noon for legislative business.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
