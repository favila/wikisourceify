{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to provide that Washington’s Birthday be observed on February 22, rather than the third Monday in February, of each year.


{{section|H6B30FDC8B375493AA7A1CD6B59C68A0A|1. Change in date for observance of Washington’s birthday}}


          Section 6103(a) of title 5, United States Code
, is amended by striking the item relating to Washington’s Birthday and inserting the following:


            Washington’s Birthday, February 22.
.
