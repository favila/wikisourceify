{{Center|A BILL}}

 To provide for cost-of-living increases for certain Federal benefits programs based on increases in the Consumer Price Index for the elderly.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   CPI–E Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Cost-of-living increases for certain Federal benefits programs based on increases in the Consumer Price Index for the elderly=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, whenever there is an increase in the Consumer Price Index for the elderly, published by the [[w:Bureau of Labor Statistics|Bureau of Labor Statistics]], the dollar amounts in effect for the payment of the following benefits, as such amounts were in effect immediately before the increase in the Consumer Price Index for the elderly, shall be increased by the same percentage as such price index was increased:
:(1) An annuity under the Civil Service Retirement System or the Federal Employees Retirement System.
:(2)   [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]wartime disability compensation under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1114  section 1114of title 38, United States Code].
:(3)   [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]additional compensation for dependents under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1115 section 1115(1) of such title].
:(4) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]clothing allowance under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1162 section 1162 of such title].
:(5)   [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]dependency and indemnity compensation to surviving spouse under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1311 subsections (a) through (d) of section 1311 of such title].
:(6)   [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]dependency and indemnity compensation to children under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1313 sections 1313(a)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1314 1314 of such title].
:(7) Military retirement and survivor benefit programs, as defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1461  section 1461(b)of title 10, United States Code].
