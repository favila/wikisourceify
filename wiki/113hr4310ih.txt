{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Secretary of Labor to issue implementing regulations for drug testing under State unemployment compensation programs, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Ready to Work Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Deadline to issue regulations=



==(a) In general– ==

Not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]]shall issue a final rule to carry out section 303(l)(1)(A)(ii) of the Social Security Act .

==(b) Special rule if interim rule not issued after 180 days– ==

If the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]]has not issued an interim final rule to carry out such section as of the date that is 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the final rule required to be issued under subsection (a) shall provide that, for purposes of such section, the State agency charged with the administration of the State unemployment compensation law shall determine whether an occupation regularly conducts drug testing.
