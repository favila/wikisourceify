{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to clarify that tar sands are crude oil for purposes of the Federal excise tax on petroleum.

={{anchor|H81EA7E3C51504C2F9D30A2FF170CEE9E}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Tar Sands Tax Loophole Elimination Act”.

={{anchor|H6CD9C91934954228BE78656AD6595D28}}Sec. 2. Clarification of tar sands as crude oil for excise tax purposes=



=={{anchor|H24089440696D45A383A13B3721601F51}}(a) In general– ==

 Paragraph (1) of section 4612(a) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking“and natural gasoline”and inserting“, natural gasoline, and tar sands”.

=={{anchor|HD65DC30CF0084918B0BEA547EDC50E67}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
