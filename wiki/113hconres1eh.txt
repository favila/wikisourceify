Regarding consent to assemble outside the seat of government.That pursuant to clause 4, section 5, article I of the Constitution, during the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Speaker of the House]] and the [[w:United States Senate|Majority Leader of the Senate]] or their respective designees, acting jointly after consultation with the [[w:United States Senate|Minority Leader of the House]] and the [[w:United States Senate|Minority Leader of the Senate]] , may notify the Members of the House and the Senate, respectively, to assemble at a place outside the District of Columbia if, in their opinion, the public interest shall warrant it.Passed the House of Representatives January 3, 2013.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
