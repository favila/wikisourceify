{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the Federal building located at 118 South Mill Street, Fergus Falls, Minnesota, as the “Edward J. Devitt United States Courthouse” .

=Section 1. Edward J. Devitt United States Courthouse=



==(a) Designation– ==

The Federal building located at 118 South Mill Street, Fergus Falls, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the “Edward J. Devitt United States Courthouse” .

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in subsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the “Edward J. Devitt United States Courthouse” .
