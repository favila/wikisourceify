{{Center|A BILL}}

 To authorize certain civil works projects, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Flood Protection Public Safety Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Authorization of certain flood risk management projects=

The [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]], acting through the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers]], is authorized to implement any flood risk management project for which, before the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]]has transmitted to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]a letter that includes—
:(1) a description of the project as recommended in a report by the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers]]that concludes that the project is technically sound, environmentally acceptable, and economically justified; and
:(2) notification that the [[w:Office of Management and Budget|Office of Management and Budget]]has concluded that the recommendations in the report are consistent with the policy and programs of the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]].
