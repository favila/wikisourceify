{{Center|AN ACT}}

To reauthorize the Congressional Award Act.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Congressional Award Program Reauthorization Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Termination=

 Section 108 of the Congressional Award Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/808 2 U.S.C. 808] ] )is amended by striking “October 1, 2013” and inserting “October 1, 2018” .

=Sec. 3. Effective date=

This Act shall take effect as of October 1, 2013.Passed the Senate September 26, 2013.Secretary
