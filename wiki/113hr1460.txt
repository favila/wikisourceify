{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Chief of the Army Corps of Engineers to revise certain authorized purposes described in the Missouri River Mainstem Reservoir System Master Water Control Manual.

={{anchor|H7701485088464FCE800004642210D93B}}Section 1. Master water control manual revision=

Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] , acting through the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Chief of the Army Corps of Engineers]] , shall revise the Missouri River Mainstem Reservoir System Master Water Control Manual and any related regulations to delete fish and wildlife as an authorized purpose of the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps]] .
