{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection, when issuing a research paper, to include all studies, data, and other analyses on which the paper was based.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Bureau Research Transparency Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Research paper transparency=

   Section 1013 of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5493 12 U.S.C. 5493] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(h) Research paper transparency– ==

Any time the [[w:|Bureau]], either through the research unit established by the [[w:|Director]]under subsection (b)(1) or otherwise, issues a research paper that is available to the public, the [[w:|Bureau]]shall accompany such paper with all studies, data, and other analyses on which the paper was based. . 
</blockquote>
