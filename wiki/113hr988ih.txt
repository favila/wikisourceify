{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 to extend the authorization of the Bulletproof Vest Partnership Grant Program through fiscal year 2017.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Bulletproof Vest Partnership Grant Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Extension of authorization of appropriations for bulletproof vest partnership grant program=

 Section 1001(a)(23) of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3793 42 U.S.C. 3793(a)(23)] )is amended by striking “2012” and inserting “2017” .
