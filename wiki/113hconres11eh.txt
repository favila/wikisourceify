Providing for a joint session of Congress to receive a message from the President.That the two Houses of Congress assemble in the Hall of the House of Representatives on Tuesday, February 12, 2013, at 9 p.m., for the purpose of receiving such communication as the [[w:President of the United States|President of the United States]] shall be pleased to make to them.Passed the House of Representatives February 4, 2013.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
