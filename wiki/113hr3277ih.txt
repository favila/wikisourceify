{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit United States voluntary contributions to the regular budget of the United Nations or any United Nations agency.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on United States voluntary contributions to the United Nations and United Nations agencies=



==(a) In general– ==

No funds made available to any Federal department or agency may be used to make United States voluntary contributions to the regular budget of the [[w:United Nations|United Nations]] or any United Nations agency.

==(b) Effective date– ==

  Subsection (a) applies with respect to funds made available for fiscal year 2014 and subsequent fiscal years.
