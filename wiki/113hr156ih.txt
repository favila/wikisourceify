{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the withdrawal of the United States from the North American Free Trade Agreement.

=Section 1. Withdrawal of the United States from the NAFTA=



==(a) Withdrawal of approval– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the approval of the NAFTA by the [[w:|Congress]] provided for in section 101(a) of the North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Actshall cease to be effective beginning on the date that is six months after the date of the enactment of this Act.

==(b) Notification of withdrawal– ==

On the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall provide to the Governments of Canada and Mexico written notice of withdrawal of the United States from the NAFTA in accordance with Article 2205 of the NAFTA.

==(c) NAFTA defined– ==

In this section, the term NAFTA means the North American Free Trade Agreement entered into between the United States, Canada, and Mexico on December 17, 1992.
