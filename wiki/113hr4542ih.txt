{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend expensing of environmental remediation costs.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Brownfields Redevelopment Tax Incentive Reauthorization Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of expensing of environmental remediation costs=



==(a) In general– ==

   [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/198 Section 198(h)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“December 31, 2011”and inserting“December 31, 2018”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to expenditures paid or incurred after December 31, 2011.
