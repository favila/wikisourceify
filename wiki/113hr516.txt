{{Center|A BILL}}

To extend the payroll tax reduction.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Middle Class Payroll Tax Relief Act”.

2. Extension of payroll tax reduction


=(a) In general=

Subsection (c) of section 601 of the Tax Relief, Unemployment Insurance Reauthorization, and Job Creation Act of 2010 ( 26 U.S.C. 1401 note ) is amended by striking “and 2012 ”and inserting “, 2012, and 2013 ”.
=(b) Effective date=

The amendments made by this section shall apply to remuneration received, and taxable years beginning, after December 31, 2012.
