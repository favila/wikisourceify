To amend the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act to require labeling of genetically engineered fish.

=Section 1. Amendment to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act regarding genetically engineered salmon=

 Section 403 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/21/343 21 U.S.C. 343] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(z) If it contains genetically engineered fish unless the food bears a label stating that fact..
</blockquote>
