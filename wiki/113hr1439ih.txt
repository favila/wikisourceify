{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the further extension or establishment of national monuments in Idaho, except by express authorization of Congress, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HA2CE96B818434B7381AAD8A0A7D940C4}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Idaho Land Sovereignty Act”.

={{anchor|H4E8F3D3F4992487E916E4461328AF857}}Sec. 2. Prohibition on new national monuments in idaho without congressional approval=

This proviso of the last sentence of the first section of the Act of September 14, 1950(64 Stat. 849, chapter 950;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/431a 16 U.S.C. 431a] ), is amended by inserting“or Idaho”after“Wyoming”.

={{anchor|HAACB5A691FF24255A2734AF7239B6DC4}}Sec. 3. Prohibition on new wild lands in idaho without congressional approval=

The [[w:Bureau of Land Management|Bureau of Land Management]] shall not implement, administer, or enforce Secretarial Order No. 3310, issued by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] on December 22, 2010, on any lands in Idaho, except by Congressional approval.
