{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 500 North Brevard Avenue in Cocoa Beach, Florida, as the Richard K. Salick Post Office .

={{anchor|H491185D507F44E75ACA9FC3FDC1CE368}}Section 1. Richard K. Salick Post Office=



=={{anchor|H319C63F2F4304B3B823234C64A12ACD2}}(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] located at 500 North Brevard Avenue in Cocoa Beach, Florida, shall be known and designated as the Richard K. Salick Post Office .

=={{anchor|H4D934E1C153F4302B3658BB3FD62CB4E}}(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the Richard K. Salick Post Office .
