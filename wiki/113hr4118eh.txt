{{Center|2d Session}}

 
{{Center|H. R. 4118}}

 
{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}

 
{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to delay the implementation of the penalty for failure to comply with the individual health insurance mandate.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Suspending the Individual Mandate Penalty Law Equals Fairness Act   ”or as the“ SIMPLE Fairness Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Delay in implementation of penalty for failure to comply with individual health insurance mandate=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A  Section 5000A(c)of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986]is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 

===(5) Delay in implementation of penalty– ===

Notwithstanding any other provision of this subsection, the monthly penalty amount with respect to any taxpayer for any month beginning before January 1, 2015, shall be zero. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Delay of certain phase ins and indexing– ==



===(1) Phase in of percentage of income limitation– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A Section 5000A(c)(2)(B) of such Code]is amended—
:(A) by striking“2014”in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A clause (i)]and inserting“2015”, and
:(B) by striking“2015”in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A clauses (ii)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A (iii)]and inserting“2016”.

===(2) Phase in of applicable dollar amount– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A Section 5000A(c)(3)(B) of such Code]is amended—
:(A) by striking“2014”and inserting“2015”, and
:(B) by striking“2015”(before amendment by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A subparagraph (A)]) and inserting“2016”.

===(3) Indexing of applicable dollar amount– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A Section 5000A(c)(3)(D) of such Code]is amended—
:(A) by striking“2016”in the matter preceding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A clause (i)]and inserting“2017”, and
:(B) by striking“2015”in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A clause (ii)]and inserting“2016”.

===(4) Indexing of exemption based on household income– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A Section 5000A(e)(1)(D) of such Code]is amended—
:(A) by striking“2014”(before amendment by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A subparagraph (B)]) and inserting“2015”, and
:(B) by striking“2013”and inserting“2014”.

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to months beginning after December 31, 2013.    Passed the House of Representatives March 5, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
