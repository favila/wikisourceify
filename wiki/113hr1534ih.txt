{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/70107 section 70107] of title 46, United States Code, to authorize appropriations for the port security grant program through 2017.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Port Security Grant Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Port security grant program=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/70107 Section 70107(l) of title 46, United States Code] , is amended by striking “2013” and inserting “2017” .
