{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Public Health Service Act to include occupational therapists as behavioral and mental health professionals for purposes of the National Health Service Corps.

={{anchor|H5D196E27DD9749B58428FB30C301882A}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Occupational Therapy in Mental Health Act”.

={{anchor|H2D6EA16BADDD42CAAB499ED794175C8C}}Sec. 2. Including occupational therapists as behavioral and mental health professionals for purposes of the National Health Service Corps=

 Section 331(a)(1)(E)(i) of the Public Health Service Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254d 42 U.S.C. 254d(a)(1)(E)(i)] )is amended by inserting“occupational therapists,”after“psychiatric nurse specialists,”.
