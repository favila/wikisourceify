{{Center|JOINT RESOLUTION}}

Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of the United States to repeal the sixteenth article of amendment.

= =



=1. =

The following article is proposed as an amendment to the Constitution of the United States, which shall be valid to all intents and purposes as part of the Constitution when ratified by the legislatures of three-fourths of the several States within seven years after the date of its submission for ratification:

<blockquote>
 — Effective 2 years after the ratification of this article of amendment, the sixteenth article of amendment to the Constitution of the United States is repealed, and the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] shall have no power to lay and collect taxes on incomes, from whatever source derived, except in time of war declared by the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] ..
</blockquote>


=2. =

Not later than 180 days after the ratification of the article of amendment in section 1, the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]] shall submit a report to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] containing recommendations for any legislation that may be necessary to implement such article.
