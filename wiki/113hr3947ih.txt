{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend for one year the work opportunity tax credit.

=Section 1. Extension of work opportunity tax credit=



==(a) In general– ==

 Subparagraph (B) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/51 section 51(c)(4)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking “December 31, 2013” and inserting “December 31, 2014” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to individuals who begin work for the employer after December 31, 2013.
