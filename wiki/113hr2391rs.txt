{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 5323 Highway N in Cottleville, Missouri as the Lance Corporal Phillip Vinnedge Post Office .   

=Section 1. Lance Corporal Phillip D. Vinnedge Post Office=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]located at 5323 Highway N in Cottleville, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the Lance Corporal Phillip Vinnedge Post Office .

==(b) References– ==

Any references in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to insubsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the Lance Corporal Phillip Vinnedge Post Office .
