{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit appropriated funds from being used in contravention of section 642(a) of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act of 1996.

=Section 1. Restriction of COPS funding for sanctuary cities=

None of the amounts appropriated in any Act for the Community Oriented Policing Services Program may be used in contravention of section 642(a) of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act of 1996([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1373 8 U.S.C. 1373(a)] ).
