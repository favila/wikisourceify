{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 with respect to high priority corridors on the National Highway System, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “   Route to Opportunity And Development Act of 2014   ”or the“   ROAD Act of 2014   ”.  

=Sec. 2. High priority corridors on National Highway System=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 1105(c) of the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 (105 Stat. 2032)is amended by striking paragraph (13) and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 
:(13) Raleigh-Norfolk Corridor from Raleigh, North Carolina, through Rocky Mount, Williamston, and Elizabeth City, North Carolina, to Norfolk, Virginia. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Inclusion of certain route segments on interstate system– ==

   Section 1105(e)(5)(A) of the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 (109 Stat. 597;115 Stat. 872;118 Stat. 293)is amended in the first sentence by inserting“subsection (c)(13),”after“subsection (c)(9),”.
