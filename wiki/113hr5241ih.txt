{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit United States Government recognition of Russia’s annexation of Crimea.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Crimea Annexation Non-recognition Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition against United States recognition of Russia’s annexation of Crimea=



==(a) Statement of policy– ==

The United States Government may not recognize the de jure or de facto sovereignty of the Russian Federation over Crimea, its airspace, or its territorial waters.

==(b) Prohibition– ==

In accordance with subsection (a) , no Federal department or agency may take any action or extend any assistance that recognizes or implies recognition of the de jure or de facto sovereignty of the Russian Federation over Crimea, its airspace, or its territorial waters.

==(c) Waiver– ==

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]]may waive subsection (a) or (b) if the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]]determines that it is vital to the national security interests of the United States to do so.
