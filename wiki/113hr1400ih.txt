{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to clarify that children of certain veterans are eligible for the Marine Gunnery Sergeant John David Fry scholarship.

=Section 1. Clarification of Marine Gunnery Sergeant John David Fry Scholarship=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 Section 3311(b)(9) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “in line of duty” and all that follows through the period and inserting “as a result of a service-connected disability.” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall take effect as if included in the enactment of section 1002 of the Supplemental Appropriations Act, 2009([[Public Law -|Public Law 111–32]] ;123 Stat. 1889).
