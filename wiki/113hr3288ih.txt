{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 31, United States Code, to exempt expenditures or obligations of funds derived from user fees from certain limitations under the Antideficiency Act, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Fiduciary Responsibility and Fairness Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Exemption for expenditures or obligations of funds derived from user fees=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1341 Section 1341(a)of title 31, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(3) This subsection does not apply in the case of any expenditure or obligation of funds derived from user fees and authorized to be appropriated for such purpose..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply with respect to expenditures and obligations made or authorized after the date of the enactment of this Act.
