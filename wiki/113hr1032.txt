{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to expand access to Coverdell education savings accounts.

={{anchor|HB2FEB2F5325440428E76E732588EA7D2}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Making College Affordable Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H70A4A18D2F7E4509AF75E41E8F79513A}}Sec. 2. Expansion of Coverdell education savings accounts=



=={{anchor|H9850D844ED174AFDA4A418EB23A1E047}}(a) Increase in age limitation of beneficiary after which contributions to account cannot be made– ==

 Clause (ii) of section 530(b)(1)(A) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking“age 18”and inserting“age 26”.

=={{anchor|H8FB22578ABC04030887E8DC63961A1F7}}(b) Increase in annual dollar limitation on contributions to account– ==



==={{anchor|H48CB32968B03457E96EB20702BF75F21}}(1) In general– ===

 Clause (iii) of section 530(b)(1)(A) of such Codeis amended by striking“$2,000”and inserting“$10,000”.

==={{anchor|HE1E4B30D08F94C4F8F724035E53AA213}}(2) Inflation adjustment– ===

 Subsection (b) of section 530 of such Codeis amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H725AFE8B66D6478D9410CFD1257F3063}}(5) Inflation adjustment– ====



====={{anchor|H88B8926827784E3DABC1480D5874BFC8}}(A) In general– =====

In the case of any taxable year beginning in a calendar year after 2013, the dollar amount in paragraph (1)(A)(iii)shall be increased by an amount equal to—
:(i) such dollar amount, multiplied by
:(ii) the cost-of-living adjustment determined under section 1(f)(3)for the calendar year, determined by substituting“calendar year 2012”for“calendar year 1992”in subparagraph (B)thereof.

====={{anchor|H8737459AAC27497DA77D17E38E434A7D}}(B) Rounding rule– =====

If any increase determined under subparagraph (A)is not a multiple of $100, such amount shall be rounded to the nearest multiple of $100..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HB6E7C680C1484614AA2117C00A8B9AD6}}(c) Effective dates– ==



==={{anchor|H9EE35BD2CF1D4B69B06A58B292DB171A}}(1) Increase in age limitation– ===

The amendments made by subsection (a)shall apply to contributions made after the date of the enactment of this Act.

==={{anchor|H21F6C798515342D8BBFCC707377CF9AA}}(2) Increase in annual dollar limitation on contributions– ===

The amendments made by subsection (b)shall apply to contributions made in taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act.
