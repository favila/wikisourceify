{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 40, United States Code, to require that the Administrator of General Services verify that a building to be leased to accommodate a Federal agency is located a certain distance from public transportation before entering into the lease agreement.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Government Services Accessibility Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Lease agreements=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/585  Section 585(a)of title 40, United States Code], is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(3) Distance to public transportation– ==



===(A) Definition of public transportation– ===

In this paragraph, the term“public transportation”has the meaning given the term in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5302  section 5302of title 49].

===(B) Determination– ===

The [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator of General Services]]shall not enter into a lease agreement under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/585 paragraph (1)]unless the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]]determines that—
:(i) the building (or improvement) to be leased would satisfy any requirement concerning access to public transportation listed in a proposed short form lease or solicitation for offers provided to each prospective offeror in accordance with section 570.203–3 of the Federal Acquisition Regulation (or successor regulations); and
:(ii) if the short form lease or solicitation for offers contains a requirement concerning access to public transportation, the building (or improvement) is located not greater than a certain distance from public transportation that operates regularly throughout the normal business hours of the building (or improvement). . 
</blockquote>
