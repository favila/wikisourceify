 To extend the supplemental security income benefits program to Guam, the United States Virgin Islands, and American Samoa.   

=Section 1. Extension of the Supplemental Security Income benefits program to Guam, the United States Virgin Islands, and American Samoa=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 1101(a)(1) of the Social Security Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1301 42 U.S.C. 1301(a)(1)])is amended—
:(1) by striking“, the Virgin Islands, and Guam”each place such term appears; and
:(2) by striking“title IV”and inserting“titles IV and XVI (as in effect pursuant to such amendment after December 31, 1973)”.

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==


:(1)    Section 303(b) of the Social Security Amendments of 1972 (86 Stat. 1484)is amended by striking“, Guam, and the Virgin Islands”.
:(2)   Sections 3(a)(2) , 1003(a)(2) , 1403(a)(2) , and 1603(a)(2) of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/303 42 U.S.C. 303(a)(2)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1203 1203(a)(2)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1353 1353(a)(2)], and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1383 1383 note]) are each amended by striking“, the Virgin Islands, and Guam”.
:(3)    Section 1614(e) of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1382c 42 U.S.C. 1382c(e)])is amended by striking“and the District of Columbia”and inserting“, the District of Columbia, the Virgin Islands, Guam, and American Samoa”.

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the 1st day of the 3rd calendar month that begins after the date of the enactment of this Act.
