{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to provide for more effective online education verification metrics.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Online Education Verification Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Requirement for secure login and passcode=

   Section 496(a)(4)(B)(ii) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1099b 20 U.S.C. 1099b(a)(4)(B)(ii)] )is amended by inserting after“processes”the following:“, in addition to requiring a secure login and passcode for coursework on the Internet,”.
