{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to ensure that members of the Armed Forces serving on active duty who are diagnosed with post-traumatic stress disorder or traumatic brain injury have access to hyperbaric oxygen therapy, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H945F09E9AD20435A8021E84806A78F7E}}Section 1. Provision of hyperbaric oxygen therapy for certain members of the uniformed services=



=={{anchor|HD79A1B148EB74F5DA2F5A7F9263131AE}}(a) HBOT Treatment– ==



==={{anchor|H122AB42C86954C8098A3A9F7AFA6BECD}}(1) In general– ===

Chapter 55 of title 10, United States Code, is amended by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1074m section 1074m] the following new section:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H3D2552D314E649F885C2AD29E0D15B8A}}Sec. 1074n. Provision of hyperbaric oxygen therapy for certain members===



===={{anchor|HBDBBCA6125ED41C9808932CA98FFACDF}}(a) In general– ====

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] shall furnish hyperbaric oxygen therapy to a covered member if such therapy is prescribed by a physician to treat post-traumatic stress disorder or traumatic brain injury.

===={{anchor|H0E428776390C4E1397D57F7D7CA7852B}}(b) Use of private facilities– ====

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] may carry out[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1074n subsection (a)] by furnishing hyperbaric oxygen therapy at private facilities in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1074 section 1074(c) of this title] .

===={{anchor|H2CA83838C019484ABE75B8063A1F23E7}}(c) Covered member defined– ====

In this section, the term covered member means a member of the armed forces who is—
:(1) entitled to medical care under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1074 section 1074 of this title] ; and
:(2) diagnosed with post-traumatic stress disorder or traumatic brain injury..
</blockquote>


==={{anchor|HF39B691854C2450A84B13DD9501116AE}}(2) Clerical amendment– ===

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by inserting after the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1074m section 1074m] the following new item:

<blockquote>1074n. Provision of hyperbaric oxygen therapy for certain members..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H7768B939EC9D4AC9BAC36DA19884B264}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a)shall take effect 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
