 To repeal certain requirements regarding newspaper advertising of Senate stationery contracts.   

=Section 1. Senate stationery procedures=



==(a) In general– ==

  Sections 65 , 66 , 67 , and 68 of the Revised Statutes ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/6569 2 U.S.C. 6569] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/6570 6570],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/6571 6571]) are repealed.

==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

The fifth paragraph after the paragraph under the side heading“ For contingent expenses, namely: ”under the subheading“ Senate. ”under the heading“ Legislative. ”of the  Act of March 3, 1887 (24 Stat. 596, chapter 392; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/6572 2 U.S.C. 6572] ), is amended by striking“sections, sixty-five, sixty six, sixty-seven, sixty-eight, and sixty-nine,”and inserting“  section 69  ”.
