{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the conveyance of certain parcels of National Forest System land to the city of Fruit Heights, Utah.

={{anchor|H10C60796CD824472A7A438094DDB6220}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Fruit Heights Land Conveyance Act”.

={{anchor|idBF41E58DDE7E4324A36DCA52CDB8F25B}}Sec. 2. Definitions=

In this Act:

=={{anchor|idE800969F16204FC69DFD62926BC22BD3}}(1) City– ==

The term“City”means the city of Fruit Heights, Utah.

=={{anchor|id69096B7CE08C40AEB37A066BBDD1CBD2}}(2) Map– ==

The term“map”means the map entitled“Proposed Fruit Heights City Conveyance”and dated September 13, 2012.

=={{anchor|id3F589831C29C4FC988D722A99774A9AC}}(3) National Forest System land– ==

The term“National Forest System land”means the approximately 100 acres of National Forest System land, as depicted on the map.

=={{anchor|idB0543575F5744A0DA0A0BF03BAEBA714}}(4) Secretary– ==

The term“Secretary”means the Secretary of Agriculture.

={{anchor|H0523DF644D9348F3A717496C188FF5C4}}Sec. 3. Conveyance of certain land to the city of Fruit Heights, Utah=



=={{anchor|HC9598D4E53874E4C8CD5D953BD93B9E2}}(a) In general– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall convey to the City, without consideration, all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land.

=={{anchor|H6ACC1E0BE4964B45B1A604052EBA987A}}(b) Survey– ==



==={{anchor|id7989da0c4bea4bbba22058fbd41524be}}(1) In general– ===

If determined by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] to be necessary, the exact acreage and legal description of the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land shall be determined by a survey approved by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] .

==={{anchor|idaabc0eca7c9044699626efaddad102c0}}(2) Costs– ===

The City shall pay the reasonable survey and other administrative costs associated with a survey conducted under paragraph (1).

=={{anchor|idF8C6DD4B3420436CB392BA437C51129E}}(c) Easement– ==

As a condition of the conveyance under subsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall reserve an easement to the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land for the Bonneville Shoreline Trail.

=={{anchor|H7C2C164A71164702BEE2E803A1E1F84A}}(d) Use of national forest system land– ==

As a condition of the conveyance under subsection (a), the City shall use the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land only for public purposes.

=={{anchor|HB490AB32077F47B2B7F9A8845C3EA024}}(e) Reversionary Interest– ==

In the quitclaim deed to the City for the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land, the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall provide that the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land shall revert to the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] , at the election of the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] , if the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land is used for other than a public purpose.
