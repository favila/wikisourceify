{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Providing for the concurrence by the House in the Senate amendment to H.R. 588, with an amendment.That upon the adoption of this resolution the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] shall be considered to have taken from the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Speaker’s]] table the bill, H.R. 588, with the Senate amendment thereto, and to have concurred in the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] amendment with the following amendment:In lieu of the matter proposed to be inserted by the amendment of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] to the text of the bill, insert the following:

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Vietnam Veterans Donor Acknowledgment Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Donor contribution acknowledgments at the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Visitor Center=

[[Public Law 96-297|Section 6(b) of[[Public Law -|Public Law 96–297]] ]] ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/431 16 U.S.C. 431note] )is amended—
:(1) in[[Public Law 96-297|paragraph (4)]] by striking the “and” after the semicolon;
:(2) in[[Public Law 96-297|paragraph (5)]] —
::(A) by striking “2014” and inserting “2018” ; and
::(B) by striking the period and inserting “; and” ; and
:(3) by inserting at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(6) notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8905 section 8905(b)(7)of title 40, United States Code] —
:::(A) the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] shall allow the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Fund, Inc. to acknowledge donor contributions to the visitor center by displaying, inside the visitor center, an appropriate statement or credit acknowledging the contribution;
:::(B) donor contribution acknowledgments shall be displayed in a form approved by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] and for a period of time commensurate with the level of the contribution and the life of the facility;
:::(C) the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Fund shall bear all expenses related to the display of donor acknowledgments;
:::(D) prior to the display of donor acknowledgments, the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Fund, Inc. shall submit to the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] for approval, its plan for displaying donor acknowledgments;
:::(E) such plan shall include the sample text and types of the acknowledgments or credits to be displayed and the form and location of all displays;
:::(F) the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall approve the plan, if the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] determines that the plan—
::::(i) allows only short, discrete, and unobtrusive acknowledgments or credits;
::::(ii) does not permit any advertising slogans or company logos; and
::::(iii) conforms to applicable [[w:National Park Service|National Park Service]] guidelines for indoor donor recognition; and
:::(G) if the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] determines that the proposed plan submitted under this paragraph, does not meet the requirements of this paragraph, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall—
::::(i) advise the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Fund, Inc. not later than 30 days after receipt of the proposed plan of the reasons that such plan does not meet the requirements; and
::::(ii) allow the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Fund, Inc. to submit a revised donor recognition plan..
</blockquote>
Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
