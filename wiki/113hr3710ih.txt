{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to provide for coverage of continuous glucose monitoring systems (CGMS) as durable medical equipment under Medicare, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Medicare CGM Coverage Act” .

=Sec. 2. Medicare coverage of continuous glucose monitoring systems (CGMS) as durable medical equipment (DME)=



==(a) Effective date– ==

 Section 1861(n) of the Social Security Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395x 42 U.S.C. 1395x(n)] )is amended by adding at the end the following: “Such term also includes continuous glucose monitoring systems (including a transmitter, receiver, sensors, and test strips required for use).” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to the implementation of monitoring systems occurring on or after January 1, 2015.
