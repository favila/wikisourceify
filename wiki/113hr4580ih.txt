{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to modify authorities relating to the collective bargaining of employees in the Veterans Health Administration.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Improving Clinical Care Workforce for Our Vets Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Modification of authorities on collective bargaining of employees of the Veterans Health Administration=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7422  Section 7422of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7422 subsections (b)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7422 (c)], and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7422 (d)]; and
:(2) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7422 subsection (e)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7422 subsection (b)].
