{{Center|A BILL}}

To prevent an increase in flight delays and cancellations, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Dependable Air Service Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Prevention of increases in flight delays and cancellations=



==(a) Prevention of increases required– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] shall ensure that flight delays and cancellations do not result from furloughs of employees of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] implemented as a result of any rescission or reduction in funding for fiscal year 2013 provided for under—
:(1) a sequestration order issued by the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] pursuant to section 251A(7)(A) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/901a 2 U.S.C. 901a(7)(A)] );
:(2)  section 3002 or 3004 of the Consolidated and Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2013([[Public Law -|Public Law 113–6]] ); or
:(3)  section 251or 251A of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/901 2 U.S.C. 901] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/901a 901a] ).

==(b) Funding– ==

In carrying out subsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] may—
:(1) use amounts available for the operations of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] for fiscal year 2013 as of the day before the date of the enactment of this Act; or
:(2) notwithstanding division G of the Consolidated and Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2013([[Public Law 113-6|Public Law 113–6]] ), or a sequestration order issued by the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] pursuant to section 251A(7)(A) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/901a 2 U.S.C. 901a(7)(A)] )—
::(A) increase the amount available for the operations of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] for fiscal year 2013 by an amount the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]] determines to be necessary to ensure that flight delays and cancellations do not result from the furloughs described in subsection (a); and
::(B) reduce amounts made available for other programs of the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Department of Transportation]] for fiscal year 2013 by an amount equal to the amount by which funding for the operations of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] is increased under subparagraph (A).
