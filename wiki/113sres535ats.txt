{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 To authorize the printing of a revised edition of the Senate Rules and Manual.   

= =

That—
:(1) the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Rules and Administration|Committee on Rules and Administration]]shall prepare a revised edition of the Senate Rules and Manual for the use of the 113th Congress;
:(2) the manual shall be printed as a Senate document; and
:(3) in addition to the usual number of copies, 1,500 copies of the manual shall be bound, of which—
::(A) 500 paperbound copies shall be for the use of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]]; and
::(B) 1,000 copies shall be bound (550 paperbound; 250 nontabbed black skiver; 200 tabbed black skiver) and delivered as may be directed by the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Rules and Administration|Committee on Rules and Administration]].
