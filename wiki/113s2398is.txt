{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend a provision of title 49, United States Code, relating to motor vehicle safety civil penalties.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Automaker Accountability Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Motor vehicle civil penalties=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/30165  Section 30165(a)of title 49, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/30165 paragraph (1)]—
::(A) in the first sentence—
:::(i) by inserting“or causes the violation of”after“violates”; and
:::(ii) by striking“$5,000”and inserting“$25,000”; and
::(B) by striking the third sentence;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/30165 paragraph (2)]—
::(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/30165 subparagraph (A)], by striking“$10,000”and inserting“$100,000”; and
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/30165 subparagraph (B)], by striking the second sentence; and
:(3) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/30165 paragraph (3)]—
::(A) in the first sentence, by inserting“or causes the violation of”after“violates”;
::(B) in the second sentence, by striking“$5,000”and inserting“$25,000”; and
::(C) by striking the third sentence.
