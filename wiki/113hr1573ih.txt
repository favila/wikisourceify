{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Peace Corps Act to allow former volunteers to use the seal, emblem, or name of Peace Corps on death announcements and grave stones.

={{anchor|H2BEE213B323245D2B0ED8ADE57CA3D7A}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Respect for Peace Corps Volunteers Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H443571166BBB4FFCA6B7CF46A6A42718}}Sec. 2. Use of official seal, emblem, and name of the Peace Corps=

 Subsection (b) of section 19 of the Peace Corps Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 22 U.S.C. 2518] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1), by inserting before the period at the end the following:“, except that the official seal or emblem and the name“ [[w:Peace Corps|Peace Corps]] ”may be used on any death announcement, gravestone, plaque, or other grave marker of any person who served as a volunteer or applicant for enrollment as a volunteer under such rules as may be prescribed by the Director”; and
:(2) in paragraph (2), in the first sentence, by inserting“or in accordance with the exception specified in paragraph (1),”before“shall be fined”.
