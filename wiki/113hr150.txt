{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Legislative Reorganization Act of 1946 to
		  reduce the rates of pay of Members of Congress by 5 percent and eliminate
		  future cost-of-living adjustments in such rates of pay.


{{section|H823E664010E442BA996D899D6888DBE1|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Congressional Pay Reduction
			 Act

        
.

{{section|H3F98728F49024204BF90317D6710EB54|2. Reduction in rates of pay and elimination of future cost-of-living adjustments for members of congress}}

        Section 601(a) of
			 the Legislative Reorganization Act of 1946
 (2 U.S.C. 31
)
 is amended by amending
			 paragraph (2)
 to read as follows:
(2)
Effective with respect to pay periods
				beginning after the date of the regularly scheduled general election for
				Federal office held in November 2014, the annual rate of pay for each of the
				positions described in paragraph (1)
 shall be equal to 95 percent of the annual
				rate of pay for such position for the most recent pay period preceding such
				election.

.
