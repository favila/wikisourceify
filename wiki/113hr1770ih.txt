{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to eliminate the different treatment under the Survivor Benefit Plan accorded members of the reserve components who die from an injury or illness incurred or aggravated in the line of duty during inactive-duty training compared to members of the Armed Forces who die in the line of duty while on active duty.

={{anchor|HE9FDB87F7A054DDB82A6CCB5244867F2}}Section 1. Equal benefits under Survivor Benefit Plan for reserve component members who die in the line of duty during inactive-duty training=



=={{anchor|H869C69734A5D4037B95076D6B3C8F284}}(a) Treatment of inactive-Duty training in same manner as active duty– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1451 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 1451(c)(1)(A)] of title 10, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1451 clause (i)] —
::(A) by inserting“or 1448(f)”after“[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 section 1448(d)] ”; and
::(B) by inserting“or (iii)”after“clause (ii)”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1451 clause (iii)] , by striking“section 1448(f) of this title”and inserting“[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 section 1448(f)(1)(A) of this title] by reason of the death of a member or former member not in line of duty”.

=={{anchor|HAB2D2F5CEA084317B9F71FD142FA00E2}}(b) Consistent treatment of dependent children and deemed elections– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 Section 1448(f) of such title] is amended—
:(1) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 paragraph (2)] and inserting the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H78AD1DF421294B58B74199A86E2AA363}}(2) Dependent children– ====



====={{anchor|HB5E467A110554BE1A743A37068F92F1E}}(A) Annuity when no eligible surviving spouse– =====

In the case of a member described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 paragraph (1)] , the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] concerned shall pay an annuity under this subchapter to the member’s dependent children under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 section 1450(a)(2) of this title] as applicable.

====={{anchor|HF38A0D63A487410ABE1CCA5034C0D589}}(B) Optional annuity when there is an eligible surviving spouse– =====

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] may pay an annuity under this subchapter to the member’s dependent children under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 section 1450(a)(3) of this title] , if applicable, instead of paying an annuity to the surviving spouse under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 paragraph (1)] , if the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] concerned, in consultation with the surviving spouse, determines it appropriate to provide an annuity for the dependent children under this paragraph instead of an annuity for the surviving spouse under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 paragraph (1)] .; and
</blockquote>

:(2) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H5CE22D33EBE2429CB8D77B0C82818DA3}}(5) Deemed election to provide an annuity for dependent– ====

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 Paragraph (6) of subsection (d)] shall apply in the case of a member described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 paragraph (1)] who dies after November 23, 2003, when no other annuity is payable on behalf of the member under this subchapter..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H95A45052B71F4E7390E115A4A5C713D3}}(c) Availability of special survivor indemnity allowance– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 Section 1450(m)(1)(B) of such title] is amended by inserting“or (f)”after“subsection (d)”.

=={{anchor|H571CD69F86CB41D896CD9F233FE4772C}}(d) Effective date; application of amendments– ==



==={{anchor|H8D93ACFB48E34114BFA7BAA989394A7A}}(1) Retroactive application– ===

Except as provided in the amendment made by subsection (b)(2), the amendments made by this section shall take effect as of September 10, 2001, and shall apply with respect to deaths of members of the Armed Forces occurring after that date.

==={{anchor|HD2A1BB1D86794FC4A31902B4BFAC4BD4}}(2) Payment adjustment and lump-sum payments– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall—
:(A) require payments under the Survivor Benefit Plan for months beginning after the date of the enactment of this section to be adjusted to reflect the amendments made by this section; and
:(B) provide a lump-sum payment to cover the period between the death of the member concerned (or October 1, 2008, if later than that date in the case of the special survivor indemnity allowance) and the date the adjustment takes effect under subparagraph (A).
