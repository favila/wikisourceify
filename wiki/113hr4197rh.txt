{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 5, United States Code, to extend the period of certain authority with respect to judicial review of Merit Systems Protection Board decisions relating to whistleblowers, and for other purposes.    

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   All Circuit Review Extension Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Judicial review of Merit Systems Protection Board decisions relating to whistleblowers=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7703  Section 7703(b)(1)(B)of title 5, United States Code], is amended by striking“2-year”and inserting“5-year”.

==(b) Director review– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7703 Section 7703(d)(2) of such title]is amended by striking“2-year”and inserting“5-year”.
