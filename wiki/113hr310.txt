{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide that Members of Congress may not receive pay after October 1 of any fiscal year in which Congress has not approved a concurrent resolution on the budget and passed the regular appropriations bills.

={{anchor|HA5FD2E5FF3494EA2BBA55DB20B6F62C4}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“No Budget, No Pay Act”.

={{anchor|H979EDF0EEFF64889A017A3DDF77E2AC9}}Sec. 2. Definition=

In this section, the term Member of Congress —
:(1) has the meaning given under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2106 section 2106 of title 5, United States Code] ; and
:(2) does not include the Vice President.

={{anchor|H55A173B7355A4EAE85FE892165CB3876}}Sec. 3. Timely approval of concurrent resolution on the budget and the appropriations bills=

If both Houses of Congress have not approved a concurrent resolution on the budget as described under section 301 of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/632 2 U.S.C. 632] )for a fiscal year before October 1 of that fiscal year and have not passed all the regular appropriations bills for the next fiscal year before October 1 of that fiscal year, the pay of each Member of Congress may not be paid for each day following that October 1 until the date on which both Houses of Congress approve a concurrent resolution on the budget for that fiscal year and all the regular appropriations bills.

={{anchor|HD844DACE412E445C8F92CDA6010F37C3}}Sec. 4. No pay without concurrent resolution on the budget and the appropriations bills=



=={{anchor|HEB9ED4C5A3B84D16AA1FF6423758A3DB}}(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no funds may be appropriated or otherwise be made available from the United States Treasury for the pay of any Member of Congress during any period determined by theChairpersons of the Committee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the Senateor theChairpersons of the Committee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the House of Representativesunder section 5.

=={{anchor|HF78FACA7DEF444C6B72E2C962CE482A0}}(b) No retroactive pay– ==

A Member of Congress may not receive pay for any period determined by theChairpersons of the Committee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the Senateor theChairpersons of the Committee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the House of Representativesunder section 5, at any time after the end of that period.

={{anchor|H6BDFDBA8586D41748C3464916E765A6D}}Sec. 5. Determinations=



=={{anchor|H5A1FC1057140407EA9365D7F9147D34C}}(a) Senate– ==



==={{anchor|H9D4DB977D9914576BC6F88CBA8C978E2}}(1) Request for certifications– ===

On October 1 of each year, the [[w:United States Senate|Secretary of the Senate]] shall submit a request to theChairpersons of the Committee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the Senatefor certification of determinations made under paragraphs (2) (A)and (B).

==={{anchor|H0069299E85964F47B11A96EB6C5AFE89}}(2) Determinations– ===

TheChairpersons of the Committee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the Senateshall—
:(A) on October 1 of each year, make a determination of whether [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] is in compliance with section 3and whether Senators may not be paid under that section;
:(B) determine the period of days following each October 1 that Senators may not be paid under section 3; and
:(C) provide timely certification of the determinations under subparagraphs (A)and (B)upon the request of the [[w:United States Senate|Secretary of the Senate]] .

=={{anchor|HA242653657CF448582233DA26E90048E}}(b) House of Representatives– ==



==={{anchor|H533C49CECD054EC6927F4FB2FBE9F0E3}}(1) Request for certifications– ===

On October 1 of each year, the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Chief Administrative Officer of the House of Representatives]] shall submit a request to theChairpersons of the Committee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the House of Representativesfor certification of determinations made under paragraphs (2) (A)and (B).

==={{anchor|H656F775879964608A2ED1AB6AA630D9C}}(2) Determinations– ===

TheChairpersons of the Committee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the House of Representativesshall—
:(A) on October 1 of each year, make a determination of whether [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] is in compliance with section 3and whether Members of the House of Representatives may not be paid under that section;
:(B) determine the period of days following each October 1 that Members of the House of Representatives may not be paid under section 3; and
:(C) provide timely certification of the determinations under subparagraphs (A)and (B)upon the request of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Chief Administrative Officer of the House of Representatives]] .

={{anchor|H0DA8E743EB9746CAAFE1963187B65617}}Sec. 6. Effective date=

This section shall take effect on February 1, 2015.
