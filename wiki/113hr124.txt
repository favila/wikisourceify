{{Center|A BILL}}

To redesignate the Department of the Navy as the
		  Department of the Navy and Marine Corps.


{{section|H1249E69AC81242BEBBB26EDCA6A3687F|1. Redesignation of the Department of the Navy as the Department of the Navy and Marine Corps}}
(a)
Redesignation of
			 Military Department
The military department designated as the
			 [[United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy]] is redesignated as the [[|Department of the Navy and Marine
			 Corps]].

(b)
Redesignation of
			 Secretary and Other Statutory Offices
(1)
Secretary
The
			 position of the [[United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] is redesignated as the [[|Secretary of the
			 Navy and Marine Corps]].

(2)
Other statutory
			 offices
The positions of the [[United States Department of the Navy|Under Secretary of the Navy]], the
			 four [[United States Department of the Navy|Assistant Secretaries of the Navy]], and the [[United States Department of the Navy|General Counsel of the
			 Department of the Navy]] are redesignated as the [[|Under Secretary of the Navy and
			 Marine Corps]], the [[|Assistant Secretaries of the Navy and Marine Corps]], and the
			 [[|General Counsel of the Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]],
			 respectively.

{{section|HD6C4AD75022942CFB226A01B17B5D7C4|3. Other provisions of law and other references}}
(a)
Title 37, United
			 States Code

          Title 37, United States Code
, is amended by striking
			 
            [[United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy]]
          
 and 
            [[United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]]
          

			 each place they appear and inserting [[|Department of the Navy and Marine
			 Corps]]
 and [[|Secretary of the Navy and Marine Corps]]
,
			 respectively.

(b)
Other
			 References
Any reference in any law other than in title 10
 or
			 title 37, United States Code
, or in any regulation, document, record, or other
			 paper of the United States, to the [[United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy]] shall be considered
			 to be a reference to the [[|Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]]. Any such
			 reference to an office specified in section 2(b)
 shall be considered to be a
			 reference to that officer as redesignated by that section.


{{section|H6EB4F31C53F44D4FAB3B1C2A0E529292|4. Effective date}}
This Act and the
			 amendments made by this Act shall take effect on the first day of the first
			 month beginning more than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this
			 Act.
