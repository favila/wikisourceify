{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to allow Federal law enforcement officers and firefighters to make penalty-free withdrawals from governmental plans after age 50.   

=Section 1. Early retirement distributions to Federal law enforcement officers and firefighters in governmental plans=



==(a) In general– ==

  Subparagraph (B) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/72 section 72(t)(10)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended—
:(1) by striking the period at the end and inserting“, or”,
:(2) by striking“means any employee”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> means— 
::(i) any employee , and 
</blockquote>

:(3) by adding at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote> 
::(ii) any Federal law enforcement officer described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8331 section8331(20) ]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401  8401(17)of title 5, United States Code], any Federal customs and border protection officer described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8331 section 8331(31)]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 8401(36)]of such title, or any Federal firefighter described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8331 section 8331(21)]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 8401(14) of such title]. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Application to defined contribution plans– ==

  Subparagraph (A) of section 72(t)(10) of such Code is amended by striking“which is a defined benefit plan”.

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to distributions after the date of the enactment of this Act.
