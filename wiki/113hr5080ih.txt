{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to extend the period of time for which a conditional permit to land temporarily may be granted to an alien crewman.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Landing Pass Extension Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Period of time for a conditional permit to land temporarily extended=

   Section 252(a)(1) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1282 8 U.S.C. 1282(a)(1)] )is amended by striking“(not exceeding twenty-nine days)”and inserting“(not exceeding 90 days)”.
