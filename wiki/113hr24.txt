{{Center|A BILL}}

To require a full audit of the Board of Governors of the
		  Federal Reserve System and the Federal reserve banks by the Comptroller General
		  of the United States, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HBE5FBD5FE8F74D5F814E366A4E99C3CB}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Federal Reserve Transparency Act of
			 2013”.

={{anchor|HE281EE543C484CD4993B95865DC6242D}}Sec. 2. Audit reform and
			 transparency for the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System=



=={{anchor|HDFF7C6F760E845589A6363953A54D25A}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Notwithstanding
			 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 section 714 of title 31, United States Code], or any other provision of law, an
			 audit of the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System]] and the Federal
			 reserve banks under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 subsection (b) of such section 714] shall be completed
			 within 12 months after the date of the enactment of this Act.

=={{anchor|HD18D631D436F49A88948CA7DB9C1BD13}}(b) Report– ==



==={{anchor|HDDA09B51A4E5461AAFD778345A0518E7}}(1) In
			 general– ===

A report on the audit
			 required under subsection (a) shall be submitted by the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] to
			 the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] before the end of the 90-day period beginning on the date on which
			 such audit is completed and made available to the Speaker of the House, the
			 majority and minority leaders of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]], the majority and
			 minority leaders of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]], the Chairman and Ranking Member of the
			 committee and each subcommittee of jurisdiction in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]]
			 and the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]], and any other Member of Congress who requests the
			 report.

==={{anchor|HAF84CC50E88940EE99368B8F27840ECC}}(2) Contents– ===

The
			 report under paragraph (1) shall include a detailed description of the findings
			 and conclusion of the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] with respect to the audit that is the
			 subject of the report, together with such recommendations for legislative or
			 administrative action as the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] may determine to be
			 appropriate.

={{anchor|H6BC2EB118FBB46DFB16F45D81D0C968F}}Sec. 3. Audit of loan
			 file reviews required by enforcement actions=



=={{anchor|H6BFA435050DB450B94CF9896B513BB65}}(a) In
			 general– ==

The [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller
			 General of the United States]] shall conduct an audit of the review of loan files
			 of homeowners in foreclosure in 2009 or 2010, required as part of the
			 enforcement actions taken by the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve
			 System]] against supervised financial institutions.

=={{anchor|HCE8D0873542E44878AD05F663A7B9C90}}(b) Content of
			 audit– ==

The audit carried out pursuant to subsection (a) shall
			 consider, at a minimum—
:(1) the guidance given
			 by the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System]] to independent
			 consultants retained by the supervised financial institutions regarding the
			 procedures to be followed in conducting the file reviews;
:(2) the factors
			 considered by independent consultants when evaluating loan files;
:(3) the results
			 obtained by the independent consultants pursuant to those reviews;
:(4) the determinations
			 made by the independent consultants regarding the nature and extent of
			 financial injury sustained by each homeowner as well as the level and type of
			 remediation offered to each homeowner; and
:(5) the specific
			 measures taken by the independent consultants to verify, confirm, or rebut the
			 assertions and representations made by supervised financial institutions
			 regarding the contents of loan files and the extent of financial injury to
			 homeowners.

=={{anchor|H6ED875DF4B4C461E94DACD86EC5F2A7B}}(c) Report– ==

Not
			 later than the end of the 6-month period beginning on the date of the enactment
			 of this Act, the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] shall issue a report to the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]
			 containing all findings and determinations made in carrying out the audit
			 required under subsection (a).
