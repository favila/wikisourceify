{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 Providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 7) to prohibit taxpayer funded abortions, and providing for consideration of the conference report to accompany the bill (H.R. 2642) to provide for the reform and continuation of agricultural and other programs of the Department of Agriculture through fiscal year 2018, and for other purposes.    That upon adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to consider in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]]the bill ( H.R. 7 ) to prohibit taxpayer funded abortions. All points of order against consideration of the bill are waived. An amendment in the nature of a substitute consisting of the text of Rules Committee Print 113â€“33 shall be considered as adopted. The bill, as amended, shall be considered as read. All points of order against provisions in the bill, as amended, are waived. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the bill, as amended, and on any further amendment thereto, to final passage without intervening motion except: (1) one hour of debate equally divided among and controlled by the chair and ranking minority member of the [[w:United States House Committee on the Judiciary|Committee on the Judiciary]], the chair and ranking minority member of the [[w:United States House Committee on Ways and Means|Committee on Ways and Means]], and the chair and ranking minority member of the [[w:United States House Committee on Energy and Commerce|Committee on Energy and Commerce]]; and (2) one motion to recommit with or without instructions.  

=2. =

Upon adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to consider the conference report to accompany the bill ( H.R. 2642 ) to provide for the reform and continuation of agricultural and other programs of the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Department of Agriculture]]through fiscal year 2018, and for other purposes. All points of order against the conference report and against its consideration are waived. The conference report shall be considered as read. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the conference report to its adoption without intervening motion except: (1) one hour of debate; and (2) one motion to recommit if applicable.    Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
