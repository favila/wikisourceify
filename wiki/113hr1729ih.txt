{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Secretary of Defense to provide the service records of veterans to the Secretary of Veterans Affairs in an efficient, electronic format.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ VA Claims, Operations, and Records Efficiency Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Provision of service records=



==(a) In general– ==

In accordance with subsection (b) , the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] , in consultation with the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] , shall make the covered records of each member of the Armed Forces available to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] in an electronic format.

==(b) Timeline– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall ensure that the covered records of members are made available to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] as follows:
:(1) With respect to a member of the Armed Forces who was discharged or released from the Armed Forces before the date of the enactment of this Act, not later than 45 days after the date of such discharge or release.
:(2) With respect to a member of the Armed Forces who is discharged or released from the Armed Forces on or after the date of the enactment of this Act, not later than 21 days after the date of such discharge or release.

==(c) Certification– ==

For each member of the Armed Forces whose covered records are made available under subsection (a) , the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall transmit to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] a letter certifying that—
:(1) the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] thoroughly reviewed the records of the member;
:(2) the information provided in the covered records of such member is complete as of the date of the letter;
:(3) no other information that should be included in such covered records exist as of such date; and
:(4) if other information is later discovered—
::(A) such other information will be added to such covered records; and
::(B) the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] will notify the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] of such addition.

==(d) Sharing of protected health information– ==

For purposes of the regulations promulgated under  section 264(c) of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1320d–2  42 U.S.C. 1320d–2note]), making medical records available to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] under subsection (a) shall be treated as a permitted disclosure.

==(e) Covered records defined– ==

In this section, the term covered records means, with respect to a member of the Armed Forces—
:(1) service treatment records;
:(2) accompanying personal records;
:(3) relevant unit records; and
:(4) medical records created by reason of treatment or services received pursuant tochapter 55 of title 10, United States Code.
