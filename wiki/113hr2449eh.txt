{{Center|AN ACT}}

To authorize the President to extend the term of the Agreement for Cooperation between the Government of the United States of America and the Government of the Republic of Korea Concerning Civil Uses of Nuclear Energy for a period not to exceed March 19, 2016.

=Section 1. Authority to extend the United States-Republic of Korea Nuclear Cooperation Agreement=

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] is authorized to extend the term of the Agreement for Cooperation between the Government of the United States of America and the Government of the Republic of Korea Concerning Civil Uses of Nuclear Energy for a period not to exceed March 19, 2016, notwithstanding any other provision of law.Passed the House of Representatives September 17, 2013.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
