{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 14, United States Code, to authorize the Commandant of the United States Coast Guard to lease tidelands and submerged lands under control of the Coast Guard for periods longer than five years.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Federal Submerged Lands Transportation Development Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Lease of tidelands and submerged lands under control of the Coast Guard for periods longer than five years=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/14/93  Section 93of title 14, United States Code], is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote> 

==(f) Lease of tidelands and submerged lands– ==


:(1) Notwithstanding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/14/93 subsection (a)(13)], a lease described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/14/93 paragraph (2)]may be for such term in excess of five years as the [[w:United States Coast Guard|Commandant]]considers appropriate.
:(2) A lease referred to in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/14/93 paragraph (1)]is a lease of the following:
::(A) Tidelands under the control of the [[w:United States Coast Guard|Coast Guard]].
::(B) Submerged lands under the control of the [[w:United States Coast Guard|Coast Guard]]. . 
</blockquote>
