{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Secretary of Defense to review the operation of certain ships during the Vietnam Era, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Blue Water Navy Ship Accountability Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Review of operation of certain ships during the Vietnam Era=



==(a) Review required– ==

By not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]]shall review the logs of each ship under the authority of the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]]that is known to have operated in the waters near Vietnam during the Vietnam Era (as that term is defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101  section 101(29)of title 38, United States Code]) to determine—
:(1) whether each such ship operated in the territorial waters of the Republic of Vietnam during the period beginning on January 9, 1962, and ending on May 7, 1975; and
:(2) for each such ship that so operated—
::(A) the date or dates when the ship so operated; and
::(B) the distance from the shore of the location where the ship operated that was the closest proximity to shore.

==(b) Provision of information to the Secretary of Veterans Affairs– ==

Upon a determination that any such ship so operated, the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]]shall provide such determination, together with the information described in subsection (a)(2) about the ship, to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]].

==(c) Public availability of information– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]]shall make publicly available all unclassified information provided to the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]]under subsection (b) .
