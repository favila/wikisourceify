{{Center|A BILL}}

To save at least $10,000,000,000 by consolidating some duplicative and overlapping Government programs.
1. Consolidating unnecessary duplicative and overlapping Government programs


Notwithstanding any other provision of law, not later than 150 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget ]]shall coordinate with the heads of the relevant department and agencies to—
: use available administrative authority to eliminate, consolidate, or streamline Government programs and agencies with duplicative and overlapping missions identified in—:: the February 2012 [[Wikipedia:Government Accountability Office|Government Accountability Office ]]report to [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress ]], entitled “Opportunities to Reduce Duplication, Overlap and Fragmentation, Achieve Savings, and Enhance Revenue ”(GAO–12–342SP); and:: the March 2011 [[Wikipedia:Government Accountability Office|Government Accountability Office ]]report to [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress ]], entitled “Opportunities to Reduce Potential Duplication in Government Programs, Save Tax Dollars, and Enhance Revenue ”(GAO–11–318SP) and apply the savings towards deficit reduction;: identify and report to [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress ]]any legislative changes required to further eliminate, consolidate, or streamline Government programs and agencies with duplicative and overlapping missions identified in—:: the February 2012 [[Wikipedia:Government Accountability Office|Government Accountability Office ]]report to [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress ]], entitled “Opportunities to Reduce Duplication, Overlap and Fragmentation, Achieve Savings, and Enhance Revenue ”(GAO–12–342SP); and:: the March 2011 [[Wikipedia:Government Accountability Office|Government Accountability Office ]]report to [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress ]], entitled “Opportunities to Reduce Potential Duplication in Government Programs, Save Tax Dollars, and Enhance Revenue ”(GAO–11–318SP);: determine the total cost savings that shall result to each agency, office, and department from the actions described in paragraph (1) ; and: rescind from the appropriate accounts the amount greater of—:: $10,000,000,000; or:: the total amount of cost savings estimated by paragraph (3) .
