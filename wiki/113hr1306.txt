{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the partial settlement of certain claims under the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act.

={{anchor|H76CA2F90A9DA4A8BAB7929F638D37C10}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Southeast Alaska Native Land Conveyance Act”.

={{anchor|H2CDD3DEEB2A94DEA966B97A4A297D803}}Sec. 2. Definitions=

In this Act:

=={{anchor|H49A3520742E0449E9777976732E793B9}}(1) Maps– ==

The term maps means the maps entitled“Sealaska Land Entitlement Finalization”, numbered 5 and 9 of 17, and dated October 17, 2012.

=={{anchor|H2F10F6EDFE7044D3BED3E45495F4984F}}(2) Sealaska– ==

The term Sealaska means the Sealaska Corporation, a Regional Native Corporation established under the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/1601 43 U.S.C. 1601 et seq.] ).

=={{anchor|H7D8EAC775B5A4CD5AAFE56B804EFBE93}}(3) Secretary– ==

The term Secretary means the Secretary of the Interior.

=={{anchor|HB4756206AEEF4E44BE20956117012146}}(4) State– ==

The term“State”means the State of Alaska.

={{anchor|H73E87155BCFA4003A979AA442A159CA2}}Sec. 3. Southeast Alaska partial native land conveyance=



=={{anchor|HADBAA4432AC74F29A7ACA14256F64B42}}(a) In general– ==

Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act and subject to valid existing rights and subsection (b), the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall convey to Sealaska the 2 parcels of Federal land comprising approximately 3,380 acres, as generally depicted on the maps, in partial fulfillment of the remaining land entitlement of Sealaska under section 14(h)(8) of the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/1613 43 U.S.C. 1613(h)(8)] ] ).

=={{anchor|H1C440A04060941B2A2A2C797E4685A1B}}(b) Public easements– ==

The conveyance under subsection (a)shall be subject to the condition that the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] identify and reserve, by the date that is 2 years after the date of enactment of this Act, public easements under section 17(b) of the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/1616 43 U.S.C. 1616(b)] ] ;85 Stat. 708)with respect to the Federal land conveyed under subsection (a).

=={{anchor|H33B70E01ACCA45CC9743CED36DA350D5}}(c) Treatment of land conveyed– ==

The Federal land conveyed to Sealaska under subsection (a)shall be considered to be land conveyed by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] under section 14(h)(8) of the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/1613 43 U.S.C. 1613(h)(8)] ).
