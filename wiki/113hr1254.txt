{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal a requirement that new employees of certain employers be automatically enrolled in the employer’s health benefits plan.

={{anchor|H74A681F7FBB840B6A6C9658D1A12F5A0}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Auto Enroll Repeal Act”.

={{anchor|H77B6236E08E84205B07BFF5913316072}}Sec. 2. Repeal=

 Section 18A of the Fair Labor Standards Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 29 U.S.C. 218a] ), as added by section 1511 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, is repealed.
