{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal a requirement that new employees of certain employers be automatically enrolled in the employer’s health benefits plan.


{{section|H74A681F7FBB840B6A6C9658D1A12F5A0|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the 
          Auto Enroll Repeal Act

        
.

{{section|H77B6236E08E84205B07BFF5913316072|2. Repeal}}

        Section 18A of the Fair Labor Standards Act
 (29 U.S.C. 218a
)
, as added by section 1511 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act
, is repealed.
