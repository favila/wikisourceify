{{Center|A BILL}}

 To ensure equal access for HUBZone designations to all tax-paying small business owners.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   HUBZone Equity Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Amendment to definition of HUBZone small business concern=

   Subparagraph (A) of section 3(p)(3) of the Small Business Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/632 15 U.S.C. 632 (p)(3)])is amended by inserting“or lawful permanent residents of the United States”after“United States citizens”.
