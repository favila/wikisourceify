{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to clarify and confirm the authority of the Environmental Protection Agency to deny or restrict the use of defined areas as disposal sites for the discharge of dredged or fill material.

={{anchor|id0A47B6D601B847028F53F9EF4EF00A5F}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“EPA Fair Play Act”.

={{anchor|id675D1E0C70414189B595965E3A95255B}}Sec. 2. Permits for dredged or fill material=



=={{anchor|id6F66041482814C32B8B1616AABCFCB26}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 404(c) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/33/1344 33 U.S.C. 1344(c)] ] )is amended in the first sentence by striking“The Administrator”and inserting“Until such time as a permit under this section has been issued by the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] , the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]] ”.

=={{anchor|idA3D3F05911054CF38D4087F43524F2A4}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)takes effect on October 18, 1972.
