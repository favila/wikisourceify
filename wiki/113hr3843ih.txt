{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to exclude from gross income discharges of Federal student loans as a result of veterans’ service-connected total disability that is permanent in nature.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Veteran Tax Parity Act” .

=Sec. 2. Exclusion from gross income for discharge of Federal student loans of veterans as a result of permanent service-connected total disability=



==(a) In general– ==

 Paragraph (1) of section 108(f) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by inserting “or if such discharge resulted from an individual having a service-connected total disability that is permanent in nature as determined by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] ” before the period at the end.
