{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 to repeal a provision prohibiting Federal agencies from procuring alternative fuels.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “North American Alternative Fuels Act” .

=Sec. 2. Repeal of prohibition of the procurement and acquisition of alternative fuels=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 526 of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007([[Public Law 110-140|Public Law 110–140]] ; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/17142 42 U.S.C. 17142] )is repealed.

==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/51/30310 Section 30310of title 51, United States Code] , is repealed.
