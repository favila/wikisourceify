{{Center|A BILL}}

To remove from the John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System the areas comprising Bay County Unit P–31P in Florida.

=Section 1. Removal from John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System of areas comprising Bay County Unit P-31P, Florida=

The areas comprising John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System Bay County Unit P–31P in Florida immediately before the enactment of this Act are not part of such system, and maps relating to such unit are hereby removed from the maps referred to in section 4(a) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/3503 16 U.S.C. 3503(a)] ).
