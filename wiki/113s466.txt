{{Center|A BILL}}

To assist low-income individuals in obtaining recommended dental care.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Coordination of Pro Bono Medically Recommended Dental Care Act”.

={{anchor|idE7813D3F3619410BA8823CC5D6149D02}}Sec. 2. Grants or contracts to facilitate low-income access to dental care=

 Part B of title III of the Public Health Service Actis amended by inserting after section 317M([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/247b–14a 42 U.S.C. 247b–14a] )the following new section:

<blockquote>

={{anchor|id0539E61ECFD84FE98100FE7A94366589}}Sec. 317M–1. Grants or contracts to facilitate low-income access to dental care=



=={{anchor|idE8142EEC62DD40A4BC1FE85560670F5A}}(a) Grants– ==

The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall award competitive grants to, or enter into contracts with, eligible entities to fund the employment costs of professionals who will use grant or contract funds to—
:(1) coordinate the provision of medically recommended dental care to eligible low-income individuals by volunteer dentists in a manner consistent with State licensing laws; and
:(2) verify the medical, dental, and financial needs of eligible low-income individuals who may be eligible for free dental services.

=={{anchor|id139EB52C62314C67877BB74328023A75}}(b) Eligibility– ==



==={{anchor|id3A5C650769964A5CA6754A2CED7129DD}}(1) Eligible entity– ===

To be eligible to receive a grant or contract under subsection (a), an entity shall—
:(A) be an entity that is exempt from tax under section 501(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986;
:(B) provide for the participation of eligible individuals in a free dental services program on a national basis; and
:(C) submit to the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] an application at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] may require.

==={{anchor|id5EF99AC075BF4356A5F0C99999C8BF8C}}(2) Eligible individual– ===

To be eligible to participate in a program described in paragraph (1)(B), an individual shall be—
:(A) an adult individual entitled to benefits under part A of title XVIII of the Social Security Act;
:(B) an individual enrolled in part B of title XVIII of such Act;
:(C) an individual enrolled in a State plan or waiver under title XIX of such Act; or
:(D) an individual enrolled in a State child health plan under title XXI of such Actor under an approved waiver of such plan.

=={{anchor|id5B92966535D54473B78E5A674E3688CA}}(c) Use of funds– ==

An entity shall use amounts received under a grant or contract under this section to establish, expand, or operate a program to coordinate the provision of free dental care through volunteer dentists to eligible individuals.

=={{anchor|id4B405C214D214286AD39A3C5EB00D48E}}(d) Authorization of appropriations– ==

There are authorized to be appropriatedto carry out this section,$2,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018..
