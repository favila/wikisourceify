{{Center|A BILL}}

To rename the Government Printing Office the Government Publishing Office, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “ Government Publishing Office Act of 2014” .

=Sec. 2. Redesignation of Government Printing Office to Government Publishing Office=



==(a) In general– ==

The [[w:Government Printing Office|Government Printing Office]] is hereby redesignated the [[w:|Government Publishing Office]] .

==(b) References– ==

Any reference to the [[w:Government Printing Office|Government Printing Office]] in any law, rule, regulation, certificate, directive, instruction, or other official paper in force on the date of enactment of this Act shall be considered to refer and apply to the [[w:|Government Publishing Office]] .

=Sec. 3. Redesignation of Public Printer to Director of the Government Publishing Office=



==(a) Title 44, United States Code– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/ Title 44, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “Public Printer” each place that term appears and inserting “Director of the Government Publishing Office” ; and
:(2) in the heading for each of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/301 sections 301] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/302 302] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/303 303] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/304 304] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/305 305] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/306 306] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/307 307] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/502 502] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/710 710] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/1102 1102] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/1111 1111] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/1115 1115] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/1340 1340] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/1701 1701] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/1712 1712] , and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/1914 1914] , by striking “Public Printer” and inserting “ [[w:|Director of the Government Publishing Office]] ” .

==(b) Other references– ==

Any reference in any law other than in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/ title 44, United States Code] , or in any rule, regulation, certificate, directive, instruction, or other official paper in force on the date of enactment of this Act to the [[w:Government Printing Office|Public Printer]] shall be considered to refer and apply to the [[w:|Director of the Government Publishing Office]] .

=Sec. 4. Redesignation of Deputy Public Printer to Deputy Director of the Government Publishing Office=



==(a) Title 44, United States Code– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/ Title 44, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “Deputy Public Printer” each place that term appears and inserting “ [[w:|Deputy Director of the Government Publishing Office]] ” ; and
:(2) in the heading for each of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/302 sections 302] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/303 303] , by striking “Deputy Public Printer” and inserting “ [[w:|Deputy Director of the Government Publishing Office]] ” .

==(b) Other references– ==

Any reference in any law other than in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/ title 44, United States Code] , or in any rule, regulation, certificate, directive, instruction, or other official paper in force on the date of enactment of this Act to the [[w:Government Printing Office|Deputy Public Printer]] shall be considered to refer and apply to the [[w:|Deputy Director of the Government Publishing Office]] .

=Sec. 5. Director requirements=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/301 Section 301of title 44, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in the first sentence, by striking “, who must be a practical printer and versed in the art of bookbinding,” ; and
:(2) in the second sentence, by striking “His” and inserting “The” .

=Sec. 6. Deputy Director requirements=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/44/302 Section 302of title 44, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in the first sentence, by striking “, who must be a practical printer and versed in the art of bookbinding,” ; and
:(2) in the second sentence—
::(A) by striking “He” and inserting “The [[w:|Deputy Director of the Government Publishing Office]] ” ;
::(B) by striking “perform the duties formerly required of the chief clerk,” ;
::(C) by striking “, and perform” and inserting “and perform” ; and
::(D) by striking “of him” .
