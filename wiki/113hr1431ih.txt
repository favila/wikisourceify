{{Center|A BILL}}

 To extend the right of appeal to the Merit Systems Protection Board to certain employees of the United States Postal Service.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Postal Employee Appeal Rights Amendments Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Right of appeal to Merit Systems Protection Board=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/1005  Section 1005(a)(4)(A)(ii)(I)of title 39, United States Code], is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 
:(I) is a career or noncareer employee of the [[w:United States Postal Service|Postal Service]] who—
::(aa) is not represented by a bargaining representative recognized under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/1203 section 1203]; and
::(bb) is in a supervisory, professional, technical, clerical, administrative, or managerial position covered by the Executive and Administrative Schedule; and . 
</blockquote>
