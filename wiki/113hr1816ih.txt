{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide additional educational assistance under the Post-9/11 GI Bill for veterans pursuing a degree in science, technology, engineering, or math.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be referred to as the “Veterans’ STEM Education Program” .

=Sec. 2. Additional educational assistance under Post-9/11 GI Bill for veterans pursuing degree in science, technology, engineering, or math=



==(a) Additional assistance– ==



===(1) In general– ===

Chapter 33of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3316 section 3316] the following new section:

<blockquote>

===Sec. 3316A. Veterans’ STEM Education Program===



====(a) In General– ====


:(1) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may pay to an individual entitled to educational assistance under this chapter such additional amount as the Secretary considers appropriate if—
::(A) the individual is using such assistance to pursue a program of education described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3316A subsection (b)] ; and
::(B) the amount of such assistance otherwise provided under this chapter does not cover the full cost of the established charges for such program of education, as determined after the application of any waiver of, or reduction in, tuition and fees and any scholarship, or other Federal, State, institutional, or employer-based aid or assistance (other than loans and any funds provided under section 401(b) of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1070a 20 U.S.C. 1070a] )) that is provided directly to the institution offering such program of education and specifically designated for the sole purpose of defraying tuition and fees.
:(2) Amounts payable to an individual under this section are in addition to any other amounts payable to such individual under this chapter.

====(b) Covered Programs of Education– ====

A program of education described in this subsection is a program of education with a focus (as determined in accordance with regulations prescribed by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] for purposes of this section) on science, technology, engineering, or math..
</blockquote>


===(2) Clerical Amendment– ===

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by inserting after the item relating to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3316 section 3316] the following new item:

<blockquote>**[[#H9540AE6B5B0D4D57B981A67581F80BEA|3316A. Veterans’ STEM Education Program.]]
.
</blockquote>

:(2) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3325 paragraph (3)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3325 paragraph (4)] ; and
:(3) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3325 paragraph (2)] the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(3) a detailed description of the activities carried out under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3316A section 3316A of this title] during the academic year covered by the report and an evaluation of such activities; and.
</blockquote>
