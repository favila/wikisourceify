{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to direct the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to provide for the repayment of higher education loans for certain employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Doctors’ Education Loans Impact VA Employee Retention Act   ”or the“  DELIVER Act  ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repayment of higher education loans for employees participating in an income-based repayment plan=



==(a) Program– ==

  Chapter 7of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 713. Program for repayment of higher education loans for employees participating in an income-based repayment plan==



===(a) Establishment– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]shall establish and carry out a program to make each monthly payment owed by an employee of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]]on a covered loan during the period of service of such employee to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]].

===(b) Covered loan defined– ===

In this section, the term“covered loan”means a loan—
:(1) made, insured, or guaranteed under part B or part D of  title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1070 20 U.S.C. 1070 et seq.] ); and
:(2) in repayment under an income-based repayment plan under  section 493C of such Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1098e 20 U.S.C. 1098e] ). . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Table of sections amendment– ==

The table of sections for chapter 7of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new item:

<blockquote>  713. Program for repayment of higher education loans for employees participating in an income-based repayment plan.  . 
</blockquote>
