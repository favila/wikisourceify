{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Agricultural Trade Act of 1978 to repeal the market access program.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Eliminate the Misuse and Abuse of Public Funds Act of 2013” or the “Eliminate the MAP Act” .

=Sec. 2. Repeal of market access program=



==(a) Repeal of program– ==

 Section 203 of the Agricultural Trade Act of 1978( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/5623 7 U.S.C. 5623] )is repealed.

==(b) Repeal of funding– ==

 Section 211 of the Agricultural Trade Act of 1978( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/5641 7 U.S.C. 5641] )is amended by striking subsection (c).
