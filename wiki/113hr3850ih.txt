{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Older Americans Act of 1965 to authorize appropriations for fiscal years 2014 through 2018.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Older Americans Act Reauthorization of 2014 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Amendments=



==(a) Authorization of appropriations– ==

   Section 216 of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3020f 42 U.S.C. 3020f] )is amended by striking“2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, and 2011”each place it appears and inserting“   2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018  ”.

==(b) Authorization of appropriations for Grants for state and community programs on aging– ==

The  Older Americans Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3023 42 U.S.C. 3023] )—
:(1) in section 303 —
::(A) in subsections (a) , (b) , and (d) by striking“2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, and 2011”each place it appears and inserting“   2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018  ”, and
::(B) in subsection (e) —
:::(i) by striking paragraph (1) ,
:::(ii) in paragraph (2) by striking“$166,500,000”and all that follows through“2011”, and inserting“   $187,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018  ”, and
:::(iii) by redesignating paragraphs (2) and (3) as paragraphs (1) and (2) , respectively, and
:(2) in section 373(f)(1)(A) is amended by striking“2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, and 2011”and inserting“2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018”.

==(c) Authorization of appropriations for educational activities– ==

   Section 411(b) of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3032 42 U.S.C. 3032(b)] )is amended by striking“2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, and 2011”and inserting“   2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018  ”.

==(d) Authorization of appropriations for community service employment– ==

   Section 517(a) of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3056o 42 U.S.C. 3056o] )is amended by striking“2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, and 2011”and inserting“   2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018  ”.

==(e) Authorization of appropriations for grants for Native Americans– ==

   Section 643 of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3057n 42 U.S.C. 3057n] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1) by striking“2007”and inserting“   2014  ”, and
:(2) by amending paragraph (2 ) to read as follows:

<blockquote>  
::(2)  for part C  , $7,900,000 for each of the fiscal years 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018.  . 
</blockquote>
