{{Center|A BILL}}

 To provide high-skilled visas for nationals of the Republic of Korea, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Partner with Korea Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Reciprocal visas for nationals of South Korea=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 101(a)(15)(E) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1101 8 U.S.C. 1101(a)(15)(E)] )is amended—
:(1) in clause (ii) , by striking“or”after“capital;”; and
:(2) by adding at the end“or (iv) solely to perform services in a specialty occupation in the United States if the alien is a national of the Republic of Korea and with respect to whom the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]]determines and certifies to the [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary of Homeland Security]]and the [[w:United States Department of State|Secretary of State]]that the intending employer has filed with the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]]an attestation under section 212(t)(1) ;”.

==(b) Numerical limitation– ==

   Section 214(g) of such Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1184 8 U.S.C. 1184(g)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:(12) 
::(A) The [[w:United States Department of State|Secretary of State]]may not approve a number of initial applications submitted for aliens described in section 101(a)(15)(E)(iv) that is more than the applicable numerical limitations set out in this paragraph.
::(B) The applicable numerical limitation referred to in subparagraph (A) is 15,000 for each fiscal year.
::(C) The applicable numerical limitation referred to in subparagraph (A) shall only apply to principal aliens and not the spouses or children of such aliens. . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Specialty occupation defined– ==

   Section 214(i)(1) of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1184 8 U.S.C. 1184(i)(1)])is amended by striking“section 101(a)(15)(E)(iii),”and inserting“  clauses (iii) and (iv) of section 101(a)(15)(E) ,”.

==(d) Attestation– ==

   Section 212(t) of such Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1182 8 U.S.C. 1182(t)] ), as added by  section 402(b)(2) of the United States-Chile Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act ([[Public Law -|Public Law 108–77]];117 Stat. 941), is amended—
:(1) by striking“or section 101(a)(15)(E)(iii)”each place it appears and inserting“or clause (iii) or (iv) of section 101(a)(15)(E)  ”; and
:(2) in paragraphs (3)(C)(i)(II) , (3)(C)(ii)(II) , and (3)(C)(iii)(II) , by striking“or 101(a)(15)(E)(iii)”each place it appears.
