{{Center|A BILL}}

To facilitate the completion of an appropriate national memorial to Dwight D. Eisenhower.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Completion Act” .

=Sec. 2. Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial=



==(a) Amendments– ==

[[Public Law 106-79|Section 8162 of[[Public Law -|Public Law 106–79]] ]] ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/431 16 U.S.C. 431note] )is amended as follows:
:(1) By redesignating subsections[[Public Law 106-79|(f), (g), (h), (i), (j), (k), (l), and (m)]] as subsections (g), (h), (i), (j), (k), (l), (m), and (o), respectively.
:(2) By inserting after subsection (e) the following:

<blockquote>

====(f) Terms of members– ====

The appointment of a member of the [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Commission]] shall be for a term of 4 years..
</blockquote>

:(3) In[[Public Law 106-79|subsection (k)]] , (as so redesignated by paragraph (2))—
::(A) in[[Public Law 106-79|paragraph (1)]] , by striking “appropriated or” ;
::(B) in[[Public Law 106-79|paragraph (2)]] , by striking “; and” and inserting a semicolon;
::(C) in[[Public Law 106-79|paragraph (3)]] , by striking the period and inserting “; and” ; and
::(D) by adding after[[Public Law 106-79|paragraph (3)]] the following:

<blockquote>
:::(4) ensure that a design is selected for the memorial as an alternative to any designs submitted or selected before the date of the enactment of this paragraph..
</blockquote>

:(4) By inserting after[[Public Law 106-79|subsection (m)]] , (as so redesignated by paragraph (2)), the following:

<blockquote>

====(n) Expiration of legislative authority– ====

Notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8903 section 8903(e)of title 40, United States Code] , the legislative authority for the memorial shall expire 3 years after the date of the enactment of this subsection..
</blockquote>

:(5) By amending[[Public Law 106-79|subsection (o)]] , (as so redesignated by paragraph (2)), to read as follows:

<blockquote>

====(o) Funding– ====

No Federal funds may be used to carry out this section..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

[[Public Law 106-79|Subsection (f) of section 8162 of[[Public Law -|Public Law 106–79]] ]] ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/431 16 U.S.C. 431note] ), as added by subsection (a) of this Actshall take effect as if enacted on the date of the enactment of[[Public Law -|Public Law 106–79]] .

==(c) Commemorative Works Act Requirements– ==

The text under the headingCapital Constructionin title III of division E of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2012( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8903 40 U.S.C. 8903note] )is amended by striking “expended:Provided,” and all that follows through the final period and inserting “expended.” .
