{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Informing the President of the United States that a quorum of each House is assembled.

= =

That a committee consisting of two Senators be appointed to join such committee as may be appointed by the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] to wait upon the [[w:President of the United States|President of the United States]] and inform him that a quorum of each House is assembled and that the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] is ready to receive any communication he may be pleased to make.
