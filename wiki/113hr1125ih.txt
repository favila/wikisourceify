{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to provide for an exclusion for assistance provided to participants in certain veterinary student loan repayment or forgiveness programs.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Veterinary Medicine Loan Repayment Program Enhancement Act” .

=Sec. 2. Exclusion for assistance provided to participants in certain veterinary student loan repayment or forgiveness programs=



==(a) In general– ==

 Paragraph (4) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/108 section 108(f)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended—
:(1) by striking “or” after “such Act,” ,
:(2) by striking the period at the end and inserting “, under section 1415A of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/3151a 7 U.S.C. 3151a] ), or under any other State loan repayment or loan forgiveness program that is intended to provide for increased access to veterinary services in such State.” , and
:(3) by striking “state” in the heading and inserting “other” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to amounts received by an individual in taxable years beginning after December 31, 2012.
