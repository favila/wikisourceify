{{Center|A BILL}}

 To apply the requirements of the Federal Advisory Committee Act to the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Bureau Advisory Commission Transparency Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Application of FACA=

   Section 1013 of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5493 12 U.S.C. 5493] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(h) Application of FACA– ==

Notwithstanding any provision of the  Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.), such Act shall apply to each advisory committee of the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Bureau]]. . 
</blockquote>
