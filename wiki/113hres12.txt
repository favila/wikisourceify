        {{Center|RESOLUTION}}
     
Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to require that any extension of the public debt limit only be considered in a standalone bill. 
 
 

        =={{section|H532F96560FDC45E5AF12545CFCD03855|
      That rule XXI of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:  
 
 
Consideration of public debt limit extension
          12.
          It shall not be in order to consider any bill or joint resolution extending the limit on the public debt if such measure contains any other matter.
        
        .
