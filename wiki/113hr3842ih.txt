{{Center|A BILL}}

To require the Secretary of Education to conduct a feasibility study for using income tax returns as the primary Federal student aid application.

=Section 1. Study on use of individual income tax returns as primary application for Federal student aid=



==(a) Study– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary of Education]] , in consultation with the [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Commissioner of Internal Revenue]] , shall conduct a study on the feasibility and advantages and disadvantages of using individual income tax returns as the primary form of application for student aid under the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1001 20 U.S.C. 1001 et seq] .).

==(b) Report– ==

Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] , in consultation with the [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Commissioner]] , shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a report containing the results of the study conducted under subsection (a).
