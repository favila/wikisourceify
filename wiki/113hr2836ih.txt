This Act may be cited as the“ Explosive Materials Background Check Act ”.

==(b) Guidelines– ==



===(1) In general– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] shall issue guidelines describing the circumstances under which the [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] will exercise the authority and make determinations under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/843 subsections (d)(1)(B)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/843 (j) of section 843 of title 18, United States Code], as amended by this Act.

===(2) Contents– ===

The guidelines issued under paragraph (1) shall—
:(A) provide accountability and a basis for monitoring to ensure that the intended goals for, and expected results of, the grant of authority under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/843 subsections (d)(1)(B)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/843 (j) ofsection 843of title 18, United States Code], as amended by this Act, are being achieved; and
:(B) ensure that terrorist watch list records are used in a manner that safeguards privacy and civil liberties protections, in accordance with requirements outlines in Homeland Security Presidential Directive 11 (dated August 27, 2004).
