{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize per diem payments under comprehensive service programs for homeless veterans to furnish care to dependents of homeless veterans, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Creating a Reliable Environment for Veterans' Dependents Act” .

=Sec. 2. Authorization of per diem payments for furnishing care to dependents of certain homeless veterans=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2012 Section 2012(a) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
:(4) Services for which a recipient of a grant under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2011 section 2011 of this title] (or an entity described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2012 paragraph (1)] ) may receive per diem payments under this subsection may include furnishing care for a dependent of a homeless veteran who is under the care of such homeless veteran while such homeless veteran receives services from the grant recipient (or entity)..
</blockquote>
