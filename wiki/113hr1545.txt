{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the definition of State in certain Federal agricultural laws to include the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.

={{anchor|H8A7B086CB9E24FD3809B31C2EFB35CCA}}Section 1. Inclusion of the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands as a state under certain federal agricultural laws=



=={{anchor|HCACAC1C5D2954472BC1FC6F59027D10E}}(a) Amendment to the McIntire-Stennis Cooperative Forestry Act– ==

[[Public Law 87-788|Section 8 of[[Public Law -|Public Law 87–788]] ]] ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/582a–7 16 U.S.C. 582a–7] ; commonly known as the McIntire-Stennis Cooperative Forestry Act)is amended by striking“and Guam.”and inserting“Guam, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.”.

=={{anchor|HF2C4B073CDA64587A49C78E112F2E966}}(b) Amendment to the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946– ==

 Section 207 of the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 7 U.S.C. 1626] )is amended by striking“and Guam.”and inserting“, Guam, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.”.
