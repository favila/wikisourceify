{{Center|A BILL}}

 To enhance the operation of the Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission Reform Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial=

==(b) Commission termination; staff; records– ==



===(1) Membership– ===

The membership of the [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission]]in existence on the date of the enactment of this Act shall terminate on the date of enactment of this Act. New members of the [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Commission]]shall be appointed in accordance with[[Public Law 106-79|section 8162]] of[[Public Law -|Public Law 106–79]], this Act, and the amendments made by this Act.

===(2) Staff– ===

The [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Executive Director]]and the staff of the [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission]]in existence on the date of enactment of this Act shall cease to be employed by the [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Commission]]on the date that is 30 days after the date of enactment of this Act.

===(3) Records– ===

Any records of the [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Commission]]in existence on the date that the staff is no longer employed by the [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Commission]]under paragraph (2) shall be retained by the [[w:General Services Administration|General Services Administration]]or the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]]until such time as a new [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission]]is appointed. The records of the former [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Commission]]shall be transferred to the newly appointed [[w:Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission|Commission]]as soon as possible.
