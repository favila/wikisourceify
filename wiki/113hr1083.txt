{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the FAA Modernization and Reform Act of 2012 to establish prohibitions to prevent the use of an unmanned aircraft system as a weapon while operating in the national airspace system, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HB7E55A76418842C3AD0F14A5F228A71F}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“No Armed Drones Act of 2013”or the“NADA Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H7B7B6460844F43658BCDF84EF1A458F6}}Sec. 2. Unmanned aircraft systems=



=={{anchor|HE5730A9587124E4EA7F4EF5960B1F175}}(a) In general– ==

 Subtitle B of title III of the FAA Modernization and Reform Act of 2012(126 Stat. 72;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/40101 49 U.S.C. 40101 note] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H9BEF4B100A2D48769FE1D2C3A58751BD}}Sec. 337. Prohibitions to prevent use of unmanned aircraft system as a weapon while operating in national airspace system==



==={{anchor|HD1273858FCD5447BA14742EC614EFB47}}(a) Prohibitions– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] may not authorize a person to operate an unmanned aircraft system in the national airspace system for the purpose, in whole or in part, of using the unmanned aircraft system as a weapon or to deliver a weapon against a person or property.

==={{anchor|H387FD3CF491F4E3FAA53448C1B06B1BB}}(b) Definitions– ===

In this section, the following definitions apply:

===={{anchor|H891BA9D33DE149FD9020C672C6F96351}}(1) Person– ====

The term person has the meaning given that term in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/40102 section 40102(a) of title 49, United States Code] .

===={{anchor|H073AB445E14448B48A8DA0DAEB7333C2}}(2) Weapon– ====

The term weapon includes lethal and nonlethal weapons..
</blockquote>
