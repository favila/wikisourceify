{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1900 West Gray Street in Houston, Texas, as the“Hazel Hainsworth Young Post Office Building”.

={{anchor|HD25C37ED174D4C8B9AD687820C207138}}Section 1. Hazel Hainsworth Young Post Office Building=



=={{anchor|H46B77F27CBAF41C49E40E54632686151}}(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] located at 1900 West Gray Street in Houston, Texas, shall be known and designated as the“Hazel Hainsworth Young Post Office Building”.

=={{anchor|H510E68978BD1477390A2C5DE959FA12E}}(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Hazel Hainsworth Young Post Office Building”.
