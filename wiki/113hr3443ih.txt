{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend titles 38 and 37, United States Code, to expand eligibility for certain caregiver services provided by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs, and to modify the Department of Defense special compensation program to make eligible members of the uniformed services with serious injuries or illnesses and to exempt payments of such compensation from taxation, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Streamlining Support for Veterans and Military Caregivers Act” .

=Sec. 2. Expansion of eligibility for certain caregiver services provided by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G Section 1720Gof title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “family caregiver” each place it appears and inserting “caregiver” ;
:(2) by striking “family caregivers” each place it appears and inserting “caregivers” ;
:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G subsection (a)] —
::(A) in the subsection heading, by striking “family” ; and
::(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G paragraph (2)(B)] , by inserting “or illness” after “injury” ; and
:(4) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G subsection (d)] —
::(A) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G paragraphs (2) and (3)] ; and
::(B) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G paragraph (4)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G paragraph (2)] .

==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1782 Section 1782(c)(2) of such title] is amended by striking “family caregiver” and inserting “caregiver” .

=Sec. 3. Modification of Department of Defense special compensation to make eligible members of the uniformed services with serious injuries and illness and provide for tax exemption of payments=



==(a) Eligibility– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/439 Section 439of title 37, United States Code] , is amended—
:(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/439 subsection (b)(1)] , by striking “catastrophic” and inserting “serious” ; and
:(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/439 subsection (g)] —
::(i) by striking “catastrophic” and inserting “serious” ; and
::(ii) in the subsection heading, by striking “catastrophic” and inserting “serious” .

===(2) Clerical amendments– ===



====(A) Section heading– ====

The section heading of such section is amended by striking “catastrophic” and inserting “serious” .

====(B) Table of sections– ====

The table of sections at the beginning ofchapter 7 of such titleis amended by striking the item relating to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/439 section 439] and inserting the following:

<blockquote>439. Special compensation: members of the uniformed services with serious injuries or illnesses requiring assistance in everyday living..
</blockquote>


==(b) Tax exempt status of payments– ==

 Subsection (a) of such sectionis amended by adding at the end the following new sentence: “Payments of compensation under this section shall be exempt from taxation.” .

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to payments of special compensation made after the date of the enactment of this Act.
