{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the authority of the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to hire psychiatrists.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Expedited Hiring for VA Trained Psychiatrists Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Appointment of psychiatrists by Veterans Health Administration=



==(a) In general– ==

Chapter 74 of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7406 section 7406] the following new section:

<blockquote>

==Sec. 7406A. Appointment of certain psychiatrists who complete residencies==



===(a) In general– ===


:(1) Subject to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7406A subsection (b)] , the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may appoint, without regard to civil service or classification laws a psychiatrist who completes a residency under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7406 section 7406] of this title to a position under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7401 section 7401] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7405 7405] immediately after such residency, if the psychiatrist meets the qualifications established in regulations prescribed by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] for such position.
:(2) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may begin the process of appointing a psychiatrist under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7406A paragraph (1)] before the psychiatrist completes a residency.

===(b) Positions permitted– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may appoint a psychiatrist under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7406A subsection (a)] if the position to which the psychiatrist is appointed has been unfilled for not less than 35 days as of the date of the appointment..
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by inserting after the item relating to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7406 section 7406] the following new item:

<blockquote>7406A. Appointment of certain psychiatrists who complete residencies..
</blockquote>
