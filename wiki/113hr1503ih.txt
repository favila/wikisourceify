{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal a certain rule relating to nutrition standards in the national school lunch and school breakfast programs, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “No Hungry Kids Act” .

=Sec. 2. Repeal of rule=

The rule prescribed by the [[w:Food and Nutrition Service|Food and Nutrition Service of the Department of Agriculture]] relating to nutrition standards in the national school lunch and school breakfast programs published on January 26, 2012 (77 Fed. Reg. 4088 et seq.) and revising parts 210 and 220 of title 7, Code of Federal Regulations, shall have no force or effect.

=Sec. 3. Limits on certain nutritional requirements=

 Section 9(a)(1)(A)(i) of the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1758 42 U.S.C. 1758(a)(1)(A)(i)] )is amended by inserting before the semicolon the following: “, to establish a calorie maximum for individual school lunches, or to prohibit a child from eating a lunch provided by the child’s parent or legal guardian” .
