{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}

 
{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To provide the Secretary of Transportation with the flexibility to transfer certain funds to prevent reduced operations and staffing of the Federal Aviation Administration, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Reducing Flight Delays Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Authorization to transfer certain funds to prevent reduced operations and staffing of the Federal Aviation Administration=



==(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding  division G of the Consolidated and Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2013 ([[Public Law -|Public Law 113–6]]), any other provision of law, or a sequestration order issued or to be issued by the President pursuant to  section 251A(7)(A) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/901a 2 U.S.C. 901a(7)(A)] ), the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] may transfer during fiscal year 2013 an amount equal to the amount specified in subsection (c) to the appropriations accounts providing for the operations of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] , for any activity or activities funded by that account, from—
:(1) the amount made available for obligation in that fiscal year as discretionary grants-in-aid for airports pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/47117  section 47117(f)of title 49, United States Code]; or
:(2) any other program or account of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] .

==(b) Availability and obligation of transferred amounts– ==

An amount transferred under subsection (a)(1) shall—
:(1) be available immediately for obligation and expenditure as directly appropriated budget authority; and
:(2) be deemed as obligated for grants-in-aid for airports under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/ part B of subtitle VII of title 49, United States Code], for purposes of complying with the limitation on incurring obligations during that fiscal year under the heading“ Grants-in-Aid for Airports ”under  title I of the Transportation, Housing and Urban Development, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2012 ([[Public Law 112-55|division C ofPublic Law 112–55 ]] ;125 Stat. 647), and made applicable to fiscal year 2013 by  division F of the Consolidated and Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2013 ([[Public Law -|Public Law 113–6]]).

==(c) Amount specified– ==

The amount specified in this subsection is the amount, not to exceed $253,000,000, that the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] determines to be necessary to prevent reduced operations and staffing of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] during fiscal year 2013 to ensure a safe and efficient air transportation system; and provided that none of the funds transferred under this subsection may be obligated unless the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]] notifies the [[w:United States House Committee on Appropriations|Committees on Appropriations of the House of Representatives]] and the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Appropriations|Senate]] at least 5 days in advance of such transfer.    Passed the House of Representatives April 26, 2013. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
