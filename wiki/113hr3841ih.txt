{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to provide that foreign income be considered in the determination of eligibility for grants and loans under that Act.

=Section 1. Inclusion of foreign income for purposes of determining total income for higher education grant and loan eligibility=

 Section 480(b) of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087vv 20 U.S.C. 1087vv(b)] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1)—
::(A) by redesignating subparagraphs (H) and (I)as subparagraphs (I) and (J), respectively; and
::(B) by inserting after subparagraph (G)the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote>
:::(H) foreign income excluded for purposes of Federal income taxes;; and
</blockquote>

:(2) in paragraph (2)—
::(A) in subparagraph (D), by striking the semicolon and inserting “; or” ;
::(B) by striking subparagraph (E); and
::(C) by redesignating subparagraph (F)as subparagraph (E).
