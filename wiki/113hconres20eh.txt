 Permitting the use of the rotunda of the Capitol for a ceremony to award the Congressional Gold Medal to Professor Muhammad Yunus.   

=Section 1. Use of rotunda for ceremony to award congressional gold medal to professor muhammad yunus=

The rotunda of the Capitol is authorized to be used on April 17, 2013, for a ceremony to award theCongressional Gold Medal to Professor Muhammad Yunus in recognition of his contributions to the fight against global poverty. Physical preparations for the ceremony shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as the [[w:Architect of the Capitol|Architect of the Capitol]]may prescribe.    Passed the House of Representatives March 6, 2013. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
