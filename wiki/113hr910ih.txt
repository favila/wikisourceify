{{Center|A BILL}}

To reauthorize the Sikes Act.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Sikes Act Reauthorization Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization of Sikes Act=

 Section 108 of the Sikes Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/670f 16 U.S.C. 670f] )is amended by striking “fiscal years 2009 through 2014” each place it appears and inserting “fiscal years 2015 through 2019” .
