{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide limitations on maritime liens on fishing permits, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HB210ED5D58AE4DBBA740A44A7099E6A4}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Maritime Lien Reform Act”.

={{anchor|HF17BDA66A2974049851A3BC4700DFF80}}Sec. 2. Limitation on maritime liens on fishing permit and permit description=



=={{anchor|H0751FF88467E41478A1161C48E66ED65}}(a) In general– ==

Subchapter I ofchapter 313of title 46, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H3F57AD508569404696A9AD1F21E34ED1}}Sec. 31310. Limitation on maritime liens on fishing permit and permit description==



==={{anchor|HC11B036E2C114D838AE9C80089D988EE}}(a) In general– ===

This chapter—
:(1) does not establish a maritime lien on a permit that—
::(A) authorizes a person or use of a vessel to engage in fishing; and
::(B) is issued under State or Federal law; and
:(2) does not authorize any civil action to enforce a maritime lien on such a permit.

==={{anchor|HD7AA143524054235BDC046C1D2C0ECC3}}(b) Fishing permit described– ===

A fishing permit—
:(1) is governed solely by the State or Federal law under which it was issued; and
:(2) is not included in the whole of a vessel or as an appurtenance or intangible of a vessel for any purpose.

==={{anchor|H1C79420244D64F2CA9CD8FF9BD45E0A4}}(c) Limitation on statutory construction– ===

Nothing in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/31310 subsections (a)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/31310 (b)] shall be construed as imposing any limitation upon the authority of the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary of Commerce]] to modify, suspend, revoke, or sanction any Federal fishery permit issued by the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary of Commerce]] or to bring a civil action to enforce such modification, suspension, revocation, or sanction..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H0977FF6542C14A3B871BB9657CCE07E6}}(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by inserting after the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/31309 section 31309] the following:

<blockquote>31310. Limitation on maritime liens on fishing permit and permit description..
</blockquote>
