{{Center|A BILL}}

To redesignate the Department of the Navy as the Department of the Navy and Marine Corps.

={{anchor|H1249E69AC81242BEBBB26EDCA6A3687F}}Section 1. Redesignation of the Department of the Navy as the Department of the Navy and Marine Corps=



=={{anchor|H2E0F22ACF060447FA645EF560E580DA1}}(a) Redesignation of Military Department– ==

The military department designated as the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy]] is redesignated as the [[w:|Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]] .

=={{anchor|H88DA50F2638E48F894E791097BCCCE48}}(b) Redesignation of Secretary and Other Statutory Offices– ==



==={{anchor|HDABA4411A0284FF28DFFE5591DB9A06C}}(1) Secretary– ===

The position of the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] is redesignated as the [[w:|Secretary of the Navy and Marine Corps]] .

==={{anchor|H15A83B9BDD6A412AB3F15FAC4D0ED51B}}(2) Other statutory offices– ===

The positions of the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Under Secretary of the Navy]] , the four [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Assistant Secretaries of the Navy]] , and the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|General Counsel of the Department of the Navy]] are redesignated as the [[w:|Under Secretary of the Navy and Marine Corps]] , the [[w:|Assistant Secretaries of the Navy and Marine Corps]] , and the [[w:|General Counsel of the Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]] , respectively.

={{anchor|H2E23C3E16CF945D294D36D119A9AEB53}}Sec. 2. Conforming amendments to title 10, United States Code=



=={{anchor|HCD4EEE20DF584AD79809679EC6B347B0}}(a) Definition of– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/101 Paragraph (8) of section 101(a) of title 10, United States Code] , is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>
:(8) The term military department means the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Department of the Army]] , the [[w:|Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]] , and the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Department of the Air Force]] ..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H2370B451E3D3485A8F700041C4171EEB}}(b) Organization of Department– ==

The text of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/5011 section 5011 of such title] is amended to read as follows:“The [[w:|Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]] is separately organized under the [[w:|Secretary of the Navy and Marine Corps]] .”.

=={{anchor|HE1BB7B71D3274724B6F051AD602AEC4E}}(c) Position of Secretary– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/5013 Section 5013(a)(1) of such title] is amended by striking“There is a [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] ”and inserting“There is a [[w:|Secretary of the Navy and Marine Corps]] ”.

=={{anchor|HC299A7854F6341BB800BD12CD38658EC}}(d) Chapter Headings– ==

.

:(2) The heading ofchapter 507 of such titleis amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|HFC3F3C4A3F61411C94016B4711EB42B3}}507 Composition of the Department of the Navy and Marine Corps– ====

=={{anchor|H290081EDAEE1411B9EEB216EFBC29938}}(e) Other Amendments– ==


:(1) [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/ Title 10, United States Code] , is amended by striking“ [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy]] ”and“ [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] ”each place they appear other than as specified in subsections (a), (b), (c), and (d)(including in section headings, subsection captions, tables of chapters, and tables of sections) and inserting“ [[w:|Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]] ”and“ [[w:|Secretary of the Navy and Marine Corps]] ”, respectively, in each case with the matter inserted to be in the same typeface and typestyle as the matter stricken.
:(2) 
::(A) [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/5013 Sections 5013(f)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/5014 5014(b)(2)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/5016 5016(a)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/5017 5017(2)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/5032 5032(a)] , and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/5042 5042(a) of such title] are amended by striking“Assistant Secretaries of the Navy”and inserting“ [[w:|Assistant Secretaries of the Navy and Marine Corps]] ”.

={{anchor|HD6C4AD75022942CFB226A01B17B5D7C4}}Sec. 3. Other provisions of law and other references=



=={{anchor|H03391F75BE7E49CDB36CCEE40F289C41}}(a) Title 37, United States Code– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/ Title 37, United States Code] , is amended by striking“ [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy]] ”and“ [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] ”each place they appear and inserting“ [[w:|Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]] ”and“ [[w:|Secretary of the Navy and Marine Corps]] ”, respectively.

=={{anchor|HA83BE2367CCC4FA69EF5EA87C44B738D}}(b) Other References– ==

Any reference in any law other than in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/ title 10] or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/ title 37, United States Code] , or in any regulation, document, record, or other paper of the United States, to the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy]] shall be considered to be a reference to the [[w:|Department of the Navy and Marine Corps]] . Any such reference to an office specified in section 2(b)shall be considered to be a reference to that officer as redesignated by that section.

={{anchor|H6EB4F31C53F44D4FAB3B1C2A0E529292}}Sec. 4. Effective date=

This Act and the amendments made by this Act shall take effect on the first day of the first month beginning more than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
