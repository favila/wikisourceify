{{Center|A BILL}}

 To extend the temporary suspension of duty on Disperse Red 167:1.   

=Section 1. Disperse Red 167:1=



==(a) In general– ==

Heading 9902.03.47 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (relating to Disperse Red 167:1) is amended by striking the date in the effective period column and inserting“12/31/2016”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) applies to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
