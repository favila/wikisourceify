{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit Department of State approval for the Russian space agency to build or operate a ground monitoring station in the United States unless such station does not raise counterintelligence or other national security concerns.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Prevent Russian Infiltration Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Conditional prohibition on building or operating a Russian space agency ground monitoring station in the United States=

The [[w:United States Department of State|Secretary of State]]may not approve a request by the Russian space agency, Roscosmos, to build or operate a ground monitoring station in the United States, including in any territory or possession of the United States, until the [[w:United States Department of State|Secretary of State]], the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]], and the [[w:|Director of National Intelligence]]jointly certify to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]that any such proposed station does not raise counterintelligence or other national security concerns.
