{{Center|A BILL}}

 To modify the small business intermediary lending program.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   SBA Intermediary Lending Program Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Small business intermediary lending program=

   Section 7(l) of the Small Business Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/636 15 U.S.C. 636(l)] )is amended—
:(1) in the subsection heading, by striking“ Pilot ”;
:(2) in paragraph (1)(B) , by striking“pilot”;
:(3) in paragraph (2) —
::(A) by striking“3-year”; and
::(B) by striking“pilot”;
:(4) in paragraph (4) —
::(A) by striking subparagraph (B) and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

====(B) Loan limits– ====



=====(i) In general– =====

No single loan to an eligible intermediary under this subsection may exceed $1,000,000.

=====(ii) Total amount– =====

The total amount outstanding and committed to an eligible intermediary by the [[w:Office of Small Business Programs|Administrator]]under the Program may not exceed $5,000,000. ; and 
</blockquote>

::(B) by striking subparagraph (G) and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 
:::(G) Maximum amounts– The [[w:Small Business Administration|Administrator]]may make loans under the Program—
::::(i) during each of fiscal years 2015, 2016, and 2017, in a total amount of not more than $20,000,000; and
::::(ii) during fiscal year 2018 and each fiscal year thereafter, using such amounts as are made available for the Program. ; and 
</blockquote>

:(5) by striking paragraph (6) .
