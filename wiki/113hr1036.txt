{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the facility of the United States Postal
		  Service located at 103 Center Street West in Eatonville, Washington, as the
		  National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post
		  Office
.


{{section|HF878365FD6F3435190723DD53387F294|1. National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post Office}}
(a)
Designation
The facility of the [[United States Postal Service|United States Postal
			 Service]] located at 103 Center Street West in Eatonville, Washington, shall be
			 known and designated as the National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post
			 Office
.

(b)
References
Any
			 reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the
			 United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)
 shall be deemed to
			 be a reference to the National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post
			 Office
.
