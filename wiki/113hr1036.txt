{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 103 Center Street West in Eatonville, Washington, as the National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post Office .

=Section 1. National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post Office=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] located at 103 Center Street West in Eatonville, Washington, shall be known and designated as the National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post Office .

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post Office .
