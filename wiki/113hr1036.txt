		  National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post

={{anchor|HF878365FD6F3435190723DD53387F294}}Section 1. National Park Ranger Margaret
			 Anderson Post Office=



=={{anchor|H04241F117B7B46819644E8D841F591D5}}(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[Wikipedia:United States Postal Service|United States Postal
			 Service]] located at 103 Center Street West in Eatonville, Washington, shall be
			 known and designated as the National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post
			 Office.

=={{anchor|H7E5F1DF6CAA54688B585416E100B2D4E}}(b) References– ==

Any
			 reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the
			 United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to
			 be a reference to the National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post
			 Office.
