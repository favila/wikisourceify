 Providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 4719) to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permanently extend and expand the charitable deduction for contributions of food inventory.   

= =

That upon adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to consider in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]]the bill (H.R. 4719) to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permanently extend and expand the charitable deduction for contributions of food inventory. All points of order against consideration of the bill are waived. In lieu of the amendment in the nature of a substitute recommended by the [[w:United States House Committee on Ways and Means|Committee on Ways and Means]]now printed in the bill, an amendment in the nature of a substitute consisting of the text of [[w:United States House Committee on Rules|Rules Committee]]Print 113â€“51 shall be considered as adopted. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the bill, as amended, and on any further amendment thereto, to final passage without intervening motion except: (1) one hour of debate equally divided and controlled by the chair and ranking minority member of the [[w:United States House Committee on Ways and Means|Committee on Ways and Means]]; and (2) one motion to recommit with or without instructions.
