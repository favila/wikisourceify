To reauthorize the Reclamation States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 1991, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Extension of Reclamation States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 1991=



==(a) Termination of authority– ==

 Section 104(c) of the Reclamation States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 1991([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/2214 43 U.S.C. 2214(c)] )is amended by striking“2012”and inserting“2018”.

==(b) Authorization of appropriations– ==

 Section 301 of the Reclamation States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 1991([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/2241 43 U.S.C. 2241] )is amended—
:(1) by striking“$90,000,000”and inserting“$110,000,000”; and
:(2) by striking“2012”and inserting“2018”.
