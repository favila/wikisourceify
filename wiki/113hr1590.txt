{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Specialty Crops Competitiveness Act of 2004 to include farmed shellfish as specialty crops.

={{anchor|H8FC0CEB5CB7C4659B34588A4982B497B}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Shellfish Marketing Assistance Fairness Act”.

={{anchor|H33D8A0F1084F4C989EEC97263C32CED4}}Sec. 2. Farmed shellfish as specialty crops=

 Section 3(1) of the Specialty Crops Competitiveness Act of 2004([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/1621 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 7 U.S.C. 1621] note] ;[[Public Law -|Public Law 108–465]] )is amended by inserting“farmed shellfish,”after“fruits,”.
