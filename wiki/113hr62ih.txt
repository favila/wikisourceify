{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 18, United States Code, to provide an alternate release date for certain nonviolent offenders, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Federal Prison Bureau Nonviolent Offender Relief Act of 2013”.

=Sec. 2. Early release for certain nonviolent offenders=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/3624 Section 3624 of title 18, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/3624 subsection (a)] , by inserting“at the early release date provided in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/3624 subsection (g)] , if applicable, or otherwise”after“A prisoner shall be released by the [[w:Federal Bureau of Prisons|Bureau of Prisons]] ”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

====(g) Early release for certain nonviolent offenders– ====

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:Federal Bureau of Prisons|Bureau of Prisons]] , pursuant to a good time policy, shall release from confinement a prisoner who has served one half or more of his term of imprisonment (including any consecutive term or terms of imprisonment) if that prisoner—
:(1) has attained the age of 45 years;
:(2) has never been convicted of a crime of violence; and
:(3) has not engaged in any violation, involving violent conduct, of institutional disciplinary regulations..
</blockquote>
