{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act to establish a weekend and holiday feeding program to provide nutritious food to at-risk school children on weekends and during extended school holidays during the school year.

={{anchor|H5C07794214C44CB4B3115E385D55FE60}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Weekends Without Hunger Act”.

={{anchor|H6C576D87786248DABA09C6FE9915354C}}Sec. 2. Weekends and holidays without hunger=

 Section 18 of the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 42 U.S.C. 1769] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H96C183D7AD92423480CE308E14A6EBA4}}(l) Weekends and holidays without hunger– ==



==={{anchor|HD65DD12801234C4FA319E949205C7150}}(1) Definitions– ===

In this subsection:

===={{anchor|H0F0AF463E50D4A71A5C5D6A2F7D32E47}}(A) At-risk school child– ====

The term at-risk school child has the meaning given the term in section 17(r)(1).

===={{anchor|H207FD01B5DFD44248D81258C63FEE102}}(B) Eligible institution– ====



====={{anchor|HBD00349C7F1B4CCE9894551AA7AAB499}}(i) In general– =====

The term eligible institution means a public or private nonprofit institution that is determined by the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] to be able to meet safe food storage, handling, and delivery standards established by the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] .

====={{anchor|H3FB97565D98E4F3FB76E0473132BAB9D}}(ii) Inclusions– =====

The term eligible institution includes—
:(I) an elementary or secondary school or school food service authority;
:(II) a food bank or food pantry;
:(III) a homeless shelter; and
:(IV) such other type of emergency feeding agency as is approved by the Secretary.

==={{anchor|H2D43F491EC814D89B8539F02AF700BC3}}(2) Establishment– ===

Subject to the availability of appropriations provided in advance in an appropriations Act specifically for the purpose of carrying out this subsection, the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] shall establish a program under which the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] shall provide commodities, on a competitive basis, to eligible institutions to provide nutritious food to at-risk children on weekends and during extended school holidays during the school year.

==={{anchor|H403625E59AE84447A16242FA70336093}}(3) Eligibility– ===



===={{anchor|HBC84344C840A47C9B8980AE8FA5556BF}}(A) In general– ====

To be eligible to receive commodities under this subsection, an eligible institution shall submit an application to the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] may determine.

===={{anchor|H239744421FFA4303BA994DBE7CFECE19}}(B) Plan– ====

An application under subparagraph (A)shall include the plan of the eligible institution for the distribution of nutritious foods to at-risk school children, including—
:(i) methods of food service delivery to at-risk school children;
:(ii) assurances that children receiving foods under the project will not be publicly separated or overtly identified;
:(iii) lists of the types of food to be provided under the project and provisions to ensure food quality and safety;
:(iv) information on the number of at-risk school children to be served and the per-child cost of providing the children with food; and
:(v) such other information as the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] determines to be necessary to assist the Secretary in evaluating projects that receive commodities under this subsection.

==={{anchor|HF199037153384740B32755B212E97973}}(4) Priority– ===

In selecting applications under this subsection, the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] shall give priority to eligible institutions that—
:(A) have on-going programs and experience serving populations with significant proportions of at-risk school children;
:(B) have a good record of experience in food delivery and food safety systems;
:(C) maintain high-quality control, accountability, and recordkeeping standards;
:(D) provide children with readily consumable food of high nutrient content and quality;
:(E) demonstrate cost efficiencies and the potential for obtaining supplemental funding from non-Federal sources to carry out projects; and
:(F) demonstrate the ability to continue projects for the full approved term of the pilot project period.

==={{anchor|H4C35CB78DF8F40CDBDBEDF3D05FEC825}}(5) Guidelines– ===



===={{anchor|H997A2AE08DFF4553BCE1DC63F66F00DC}}(A) In general– ====

The [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] shall issue guidelines containing the criteria for projects to receive commodities under this section.

===={{anchor|HCDEEDC8A33FA4B9796AF130F26A5E78F}}(B) Inclusions– ====

The guidelines shall, to the maximum extent practicable within the funds available and applications submitted, take into account—
:(i) geographical variations in project locations to include qualifying projects in rural, urban, and suburban areas with high proportions of families with at-risk school children;
:(ii) different types of projects that offer nutritious foods on weekends and during school holidays to at-risk school children; and
:(iii) institutional capacity to collect, maintain, and provide statistically valid information necessary for the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] —
::(I) to analyze and evaluate the results of the pilot project; and
::(II) to make recommendations to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] .

==={{anchor|H3108C1FB71054F5D856AA0340BC18189}}(6) Evaluation– ===



===={{anchor|H89D49115C8824254B43CBBD5601FBD03}}(A) Interim evaluation– ====

Not later than November 30, 2016, the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] shall complete an interim evaluation of the pilot program carried out under this subsection.

===={{anchor|HFCF2E73E2B4947DA81ACF056A615798A}}(B) Final report– ====

Not later than December 31, 2018, the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a final report that contains—
:(i) an evaluation of the pilot program carried out under this subsection; and
:(ii) any recommendations of the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] for legislative action.

==={{anchor|HA9FA95BE81854729817B0747ADF015C4}}(7) Funding– ===



===={{anchor|HE76315F239BF4D4083BCEDA6056A99DF}}(A) Authorization of appropriations– ====

There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this sectionsuch sums as are necessary, to remain available until expended.

===={{anchor|HEAF5FA4144A94663814FEC8F12731F15}}(B) Availability of funds– ====

Not more than 3 percent of the funds made available under subparagraph (A)may be used by the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] for expenses associated with review of the operations and evaluation of the projects carried out under this subsection..
</blockquote>
