{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to improve the Transition Assistance Program of the Department of Defense, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Servicemembers' Choice in Transition Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Contents of Transition Assistance Program=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 Section 1144 of title 10, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 subsection (b)] , by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(9) Provide information about disability-related employment and education protections.;
</blockquote>

:(2) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 subsections (c), (d), and (e)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 subsections (d), (e), and (f)] , respectively; and
:(3) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 subsection (b)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 subsection (c)] :

<blockquote>

====(c) Additional elements of program– ====

The mandatory program carried out under this section shall include—
:(1) for any member who plans to use the member’s entitlement to educational assistance under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/ title 38] —
::(A) instruction providing an overview of the use of such entitlement; and
::(B) testing to determine academic readiness for post-secondary education, courses of post-secondary education appropriate for the member, courses of post-secondary education compatible with the member’s education goals, and instruction on how to finance the member’s post-secondary education; and
:(2) instruction in the benefits under laws administered by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] and in other subjects determined by the Secretary concerned..
</blockquote>


==(b) Deadline for implementation– ==

The program carried out under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 section 1144 of title 10, United States Code] , shall comply with the requirements of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 subsections (b)(9)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1144 (c)] of such section, as added by subsection (a), by not later than April 1, 2015.

==(c) Feasibility study– ==

Not later than 270 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall submit to the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committees on Veterans’ Affairs of the Senate]] and the [[w:United States House Committee on Veterans' Affairs|House of Representatives]] the results of a study carried out by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] to determine the feasibility of providing the instruction described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1142 subsection (b) ofsection 1142of title 10, United States Code] , at all overseas locations where such instruction is provided by entering into a contract jointly with the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]] for the provision of such instruction.
