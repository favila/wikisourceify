{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to repeal the excise tax on medical devices.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Medical Device Access and Innovation Protection Act”.

={{anchor|id396D60EE013842699EF546A24FDF2F8B}}Sec. 2. Repeal of medical device excise tax=



=={{anchor|id2666535C86F74FE3AA092628D6966057}}(a) In general– ==

 Chapter 32 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking subchapter E.

=={{anchor|id4F2ECFB2D0B146BBB7706DDB6F1712D8}}(b) Conforming amendments– ==


:(1)  Subsection (a) of section 4221 of such Codeis amended by striking the last sentence.
:(2)  Paragraph (2) of section 6416(b) of such Codeis amended by striking the last sentence.

=={{anchor|id570BCA131998448DBCF18DEE4B0B4C3D}}(c) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of subchapter for chapter 32 of such Codeis amended by striking the item related to subchapter E.

=={{anchor|id4AB447FC3B924A619C5DE0AE8CD4D5CE}}(d) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to sales after December 31, 2012.
