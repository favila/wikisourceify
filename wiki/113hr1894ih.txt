{{Center|A BILL}}

To establish an Office of Tribal Relations in the Department of Agriculture.

=Section 1. Office of tribal relations=



==(a) In general– ==

 Title III of the Department of Agriculture Reorganization Act of 1994is amended by adding after section 308( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/3125a 7 U.S.C. 3125anote] ;[[Public Law 103-354|Public Law 103–354]] )the following new section:

<blockquote>

==Sec. 309. Office of tribal relations==

The [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall establish in the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Office of the Secretary an Office of Tribal Relations]] to advise the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] on policies related to Indian tribes..
</blockquote>


==(b) Conforming amendments– ==

 Section 296(b) of the Department of Agriculture Reorganization Act of 1994( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/7014 7 U.S.C. 7014(b)] ] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (6)(C), by striking “or” at the end;
:(2) in paragraph (7), by striking the period at the end and inserting “; or” ; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(8) the authority of the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] to establish in the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Office of the Secretary the Office of Tribal Relations]] in accordance with section 309..
</blockquote>
