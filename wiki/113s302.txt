{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to make voting in a Federal election by an unlawfully present alien an aggravated felony and for other purposes.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Voter Integrity Protection Act”.

={{anchor|id0F0852768B974D9E9B3E5888F0AC7CD1}}Sec. 2. Unlawful voting=



=={{anchor|idA052C4F4DD8A407CB40BE7B0A7068A8D}}(a) Aggravated felony– ==

 Paragraph (43) of section 101(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1101 8 U.S.C. 1101(a)] )is amended—
:(1) in subparagraph (T), by striking“and”at the end;
:(2) in subparagraph (U), by striking the period at the end and inserting a semicolon and“and”; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(V) an offense described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/611 section 611 of title 18, United States Code] , committed by an alien who is unlawfully present in the United States..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|id62434CAAF8194730B12B75E7DBB8B7DB}}(b) Deportable offense– ==

 Paragraph (2) of section 237(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1227 8 U.S.C. 1227(a)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|id175006BA2AA040E69CC492743989A0EB}}(G) Voting offenses– ===

Any alien who is unlawfully present in the United States and who knowingly commits a violation of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/611 section 611 of title 18, United States Code] ..
</blockquote>
