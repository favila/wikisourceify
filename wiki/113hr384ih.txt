{{Center|A BILL}}

To establish the position of Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs in the Department of Housing and Urban Development, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Homes for Heroes Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Special assistant for Veterans Affairs in the Department of Housing and Urban Development=

 Section 4 of the Department of Housing and Urban Development Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3533 42 U.S.C. 3533] )is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

==(g) Special assistant for veterans affairs– ==



===(1) Establishment– ===

There shall be in the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department]] a [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs]] , who shall be a special assistant to the Secretary and shall report directly to the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary]] .

===(2) Appointment– ===

The [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs]] shall be appointed based solely on merit and shall be covered under the provisions of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/ title 5, United States Code] , governing appointments in the competitive service.

===(3) Responsibilities– ===

The [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs]] shall be responsible for—
:(A) ensuring veterans have fair access to housing and homeless assistance under each program of the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department]] providing either such assistance;
:(B) coordinating all programs and activities of the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department]] relating to veterans;
:(C) serving as a liaison for the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department]] with the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] , including establishing and maintaining relationships with the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] ;
:(D) serving as a liaison for the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department]] , and establishing and maintaining relationships with the [[w:United States Interagency Council on Homelessness|United States Interagency Council on Homelessness]] and officials of State, local, regional, and nongovernmental organizations concerned with veterans;
:(E) providing information and advice regarding—
::(i) sponsoring housing projects for veterans assisted under programs administered by the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department]] ; or
::(ii) assisting veterans in obtaining housing or homeless assistance under programs administered by the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department]] ;
:(F) coordinating with the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary of Housing and Urban Development]] and the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] in carrying out section 3 of the Homes for Heroes Act of 2013; and
:(G) carrying out such other duties as may be assigned to the Special Assistant by the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary]] or by law..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. Annual supplemental report on veterans homelessness=



==(a) In general– ==

The [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary of Housing and Urban Development]] and the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] , in coordination with the [[w:United States Interagency Council on Homelessness|United States Interagency Council on Homelessness]] , shall submit annually to the Committees of the Congress specified in subsection (b), together with the annual reports required by such Secretaries under section 203(c)(1) of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/11313 42 U.S.C. 11313(c)(1)] ), a supplemental report that includes the following information with respect to the preceding year:
:(1) The same information, for such preceding year, that was included with respect to 2010 in the report by the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary of Housing and Urban Development]] and the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] entitled “Veterans Homelessness: A Supplemental Report to the 2010 Annual Homeless Assessment Report to Congress” .
:(2) Information regarding the activities of the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department of Housing and Urban Development]] relating to veterans during such preceding year, as follows:
::(A) The number of veterans provided assistance under the housing choice voucher program for Veterans Affairs supported housing (VASH) under section 8(o)(19) of the United States Housing Act of 1937( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1437f 42 U.S.C. 1437f(o)(19)] ), the socioeconomic characteristics of such homeless veterans, and the number, types, and locations of entities contracted under such section to administer the vouchers.
::(B) A summary description of the special considerations made for veterans under public housing agency plans submitted pursuant to section 5A of the United States Housing Act of 1937( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1437c–1 42 U.S.C. 1437c–1] )and under comprehensive housing affordability strategies submitted pursuant to section 105 of the Cranston-Gonzalez National Affordable Housing Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/12705 42 U.S.C. 12705] ).
::(C) A description of the activities of the Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs of the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department of Housing and Urban Development]] .
::(D) A description of the efforts of the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department of Housing and Urban Development]] and the other members of the [[w:United States Interagency Council on Homelessness|United States Interagency Council on Homelessness]] to coordinate the delivery of housing and services to veterans.
::(E) The cost to the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department of Housing and Urban Development]] of administering the programs and activities relating to veterans.
::(F) Any other information that the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary of Housing and Urban Development]] and the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] consider relevant in assessing the programs and activities of the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department of Housing and Urban Development]] relating to veterans.

==(b) Committees– ==

The Committees of the Congress specified in this subsection are as follows:
:(1) The [[w:United States Senate Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs|Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs of the Senate]] .
:(2) The [[w:United States Senate Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans' Affairs of the Senate]] .
:(3) The [[w:United States Senate Committee on Appropriations|Committee on Appropriations of the Senate]] .
:(4) The [[w:United States House Committee on Financial Services|Committee on Financial Services of the House of Representatives]] .
:(5) The [[w:United States House Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans' Affairs of the House of Representatives]] .
:(6) The [[w:United States House Committee on Appropriations|Committee on Appropriations of the House of Representatives]] .
