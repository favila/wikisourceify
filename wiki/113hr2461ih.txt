{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Small Business Act to make permanent the Small Loan Advantage program, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “SBA Loan Paperwork Reduction Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Small Loan Advantage program=

 Section 7(a) of the Small Business Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/636 15 U.S.C. 636(a)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==(36) Small loan advantage– ==



===(A) Permanent establishment– ===

The [[w:Small Business Administration|Administrator]] shall carry out a [[w:Small Business Administration|Small Loan Advantage program]] in accordance with this paragraph.

===(B) Administration of program– ===

Except as otherwise provided under this paragraph, the [[w:Small Business Administration|Administrator]] shall carry out the [[w:Small Business Administration|Small Loan Advantage program]] in the same manner as the Small Loan Advantage loan initiative of the [[w:Small Business Administration|Administration]] —
:(i) under which lenders were eligible to begin submitting loans on February 15, 2011; and
:(ii) as in effect on June 1, 2013.

===(C) Nonapplicability of pilot program limitations– ===

No limitation under paragraph (25)(A)applies to the [[w:Small Business Administration|Small Loan Advantage program]] under this paragraph.

===(D) Increased flexibility– ===

The [[w:Small Business Administration|Administrator]] is authorized to increase the program’s maximum loan amount, to reduce the program’s fees, and to further streamline the program’s loan approval process if the [[w:Small Business Administration|Administrator]] determines that such adjustments would be fair and appropriate..
</blockquote>
