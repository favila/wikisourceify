{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend chapter 2 of title I of the United States Code
		  to establish the style for amending laws.


{{section|H60FFD7AE92374AC69F98606C4E4F73BA|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Readable Legislation Act of
			 2013

        
.

{{section|HE0765F5715434FB3B01950E240B05B1B|2. Amending laws}}
Chapter 2 of title 1 of
			 the United States Code
 is amended by adding at the end the following new
			 section:

{{section|HF299EFC5E2874DDE8DB206E97D04E060|115. Amending laws}}
No Act shall be revised
				or amended by mere reference to it. Every bill or joint resolution which amends
				an existing section, subsection, or other subdivision of any Act shall set
				forth the section, subsection, or other subdivision sufficiently to enable the
				intent and effect of the bill or joint resolution to be clearly understood.
				Omissions and insertions proposed shall be shown by appropriate typographical
				devices.
.


{{section|HC7E031FA9113463DADB5ECCC1F036339|3. Conforming amendment}}
The table of
			 sections at the beginning of chapter 2 of title 1 of the United States Code
 is
			 amended by adding after the item relating to section 114
 the following new
			 item:
__NOTOC__
115. Amending
				laws.
.


{{section|HD9044012184444D283AB5D695D9F50DC|4. Effective date}}
This Act and the
			 amendments made by it shall take effect on January 3, 2015.
