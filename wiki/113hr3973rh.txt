{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/530D section 530D]of title 28, United States Code.    

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Faithful Execution of the Law Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Amendment to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/530D section 530D]of title 28, United States Code=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/530D  Section 530D(a)(1)(A)of title 28, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by inserting“or any other Federal officer”before“establishes or implements a formal or informal policy”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/530D clause (i)], by striking“on the grounds that such provision is unconstitutional”and inserting“and state the grounds for such policy”.
