{{Center|A BILL}}

 To designate the building occupied by the Federal Bureau of Investigation located at 801 Follin Lane, Vienna, Virginia, as the Michael D. Resnick Terrorist Screening Center .    

=Section 1. Designation=

The building occupied by the [[w:Federal Bureau of Investigation|Federal Bureau of Investigation]]located at 801 Follin Lane, Vienna, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Michael D. Resnick Terrorist Screening Center during the period in which the building is occupied by the Federal Bureau of Investigation. 

=Sec. 2. References=

During the period in which the building referred to in section 1 is occupied by the [[w:Federal Bureau of Investigation|Federal Bureau of Investigation]], any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to that building shall be deemed to be a reference to the Michael D. Resnick Terrorist Screening Center .
