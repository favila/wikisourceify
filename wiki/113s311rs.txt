{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Secretary of the Interior to study the suitability and feasibility of designating sites in the Lower Mississippi River Area in the State of Louisiana as a unit of the National Park System, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Lower Mississippi River Area Study Act” .

=Sec. 2. Definitions=

In this Act:

==(1) Secretary– ==

The term Secretary means the Secretary of the Interior.

==(2) Study area– ==

The term study area includes Fort St. Philip, Fort Jackson, the Head of Passes, and any related and supporting historical, cultural, and recreational resources located in Plaquemines Parish, Louisiana.

=Sec. 3. Study=



==(a) In general– ==

Not later than 3 years after the date on which funds are made available to carry out this Act, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] , in consultation with the State of Louisiana and other interested organizations, shall complete a special resource study that evaluates—
:(1) the national significance of the study area; and
:(2) the suitability and feasibility of designating the study area as a unit of the [[w:National Park Service|National Park System]] .

==(b) Criteria– ==

In conducting the study under subsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall use the criteria for the study of areas for potential inclusion in the [[w:National Park Service|National Park System]] in section 8(c) of National Park System General Authorities Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1a–5 16 U.S.C. 1a–5(c)] ).

==(c) Content– ==

The study described in subsection (a)shall—
:(1) include cost estimates for the potential acquisition, development, operation, and maintenance of the study area; and
:(2) identify alternatives for the management, administration, and protection of the study area.

=Sec. 4. Donations=

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] may accept the donation of funds to carry out this Act.

=Sec. 5. Authorization of appropriation=

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this Act.
