To extend the temporary suspension of duty on Direct Black 22.

={{anchor|H815F2E42E620422EAB102BD05C8ED4E0}}Section 1. Direct Black 22=



=={{anchor|H334A6DF856A14072BD0872564FD78B0D}}(a) In general– ==

Heading 9902.25.25 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (relating to Direct Black 22) is amended by striking the date in the effective period column and inserting“12/31/2016”.

=={{anchor|HEC68AEEFDB20440C8B1C503A615857E1}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)applies to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
