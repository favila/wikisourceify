{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Secretary of Defense to establish an electronic means by which members of the Ready Reserves of the Armed Forces may track their active-duty service.   

=Section 1. Electronic tracking of certain reserve duty=

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]]shall establish an electronic means by which members of the Ready Reserve of the Armed Forces can track their operational active-duty service performed after January 28, 2008, under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12301 section 12301(a)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12301 12301(d)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12301 12301(g)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12302 12302], or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12304 12304 of title 10, United States Code]. The tour calculator shall specify early retirement credit authorized for each qualifying tour of active duty, as well as cumulative early reserve retirement credit authorized to date under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12731 section 12731(f) of such title].
