{{Center|A BILL}}

To make the moratorium on Internet access taxes and multiple and discriminatory taxes on electronic commerce permanent.

={{anchor|H314983B236334E3A83CEE86349565D47}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Permanent Internet Tax Freedom Act”.

={{anchor|H8B87B7FF39F2428198E387F5B0ECE2C2}}Sec. 2. Permanent moratorium on Internet access taxes and multiple and discriminatory taxes on electronic commerce=



=={{anchor|H8B3B235BC36348AA97D5126A94B683C5}}(a) In General– ==

 Section 1101(a) of the Internet Tax Freedom Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/47/151 47 U.S.C. 151 note] )is amended by striking“taxes during the period beginning November 1, 2003, and ending November 1, 2014:”and inserting“taxes:”.

=={{anchor|H0C73E98EFF6C4346A60EA60CF1A1AEC5}}(b) Effective Date– ==

The amendment made by this sectionshall apply to taxes imposed after the date of the enactment of this Act.
