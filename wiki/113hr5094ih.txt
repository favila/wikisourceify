{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to recoup certain bonuses or awards paid to employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs.   

=Section 1. Authority to recoup bonuses or awards paid to employees of Department of Veterans Affairs=



==(a) In general– ==

  Chapter 7of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 712. Recoupment of bonuses or awards paid to employees of Department.==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]may issue an order directing an employee of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]]to repay the amount of any award or bonus paid to the employee under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/ title 5], including under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/45 chapters 45]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/53 53 of such title], or this title if—
:(1) the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]determines such repayment appropriate pursuant to regulations prescribed by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]to carry out this section; and
:(2) the employee is afforded notice and an opportunity for a hearing. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by adding at the end the following new item:

<blockquote>  712. Recoupment of bonuses or awards paid to employees of Department.  . 
</blockquote>
