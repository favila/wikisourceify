{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to repeal the
		  excise tax on medical devices.

={{anchor|HBB93A7A5956E4F779D93AB0946E52BB5}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Protect Medical Innovation Act of
			 2013”.

={{anchor|H70BEDBC2FAC64384BE0AEFC3E7E5A7D6}}Sec. 2. Repeal of medical
			 device excise tax=



=={{anchor|HFD39F2FBB8554F68B21595651CA01BFA}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Chapter 32 of the
			 Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking subchapter E.

=={{anchor|HDEC1EC3D4AF64229967128C826B9E5E7}}(b) Conforming
			 amendments– ==


:(1) Subsection (a) of
			 section 4221 of such Code is amended by striking the last sentence.
:(2) Paragraph (2) of
			 section 6416(b) of such Code is amended by striking the last sentence.

=={{anchor|HFD12C8CD460841E485FEFAC46375D055}}(c) Clerical
			 amendment– ==

The table of subchapters for chapter 32 of such Code is
			 amended by striking the item relating to subchapter E.

=={{anchor|HF7AFF48F4F064F56A6FE55033A63E996}}(d) Effective
			 date– ==

The amendments made by
			 this section shall apply to sales after December 31, 2012.
