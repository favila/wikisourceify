{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to ensure the issuance of regulations applicable to the Coast Guard regarding consideration of a request for a permanent change of station or unit transfer submitted by a member of the Coast Guard who is the victim of a sexual assault.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Coast Guard STRONG Act” .

=Sec. 2. Issuance of regulations applicable to the Coast Guard regarding consideration of request for permanent change of station or unit transfer by victim of sexual assault=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/673 Section 673(b) of title 10, United States Code] , is amended by striking “The Secretaries of the military departments” and inserting “The Secretary concerned” .
