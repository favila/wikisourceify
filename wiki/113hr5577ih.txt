{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to eliminate origination fees for Federal Direct Loans.   

=Section 1. Elimination of origination fees for Federal Direct Loans=



==(a) Sense of Congress– ==

It is the sense of [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]that no origination fees should be charged on any future Federal Direct Loans.

==(b) Repeal of origination fees– ==

   Subsection (c) of section 455 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e(c)] )is repealed.

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (b) shall apply with respect to loans made under  part D of title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087a 20 U.S.C. 1087a et seq.] )for which the first disbursement of principal is made, or, in the case of a Federal Direct Consolidation Loan, the application is received, on or after July 1, 2015.
