{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to permit certain veterans who were discharged or released from the Armed Forces by reason of service-connected disability to transfer benefits under the Post-9/11 Educational Assistance Program, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Service-connected death, disability, or injury exception for transferability of entitlement to educational assistance under the Post-9/11 Educational Assistance program=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 Section 3319 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subsection (b)] —
::(A) by striking“(b)Eligible individuals.—An individual,”and inserting the following:

<blockquote>

====(b) Eligible Individuals– ====



=====(1) In general– =====

An individual;
</blockquote>

::(B) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 paragraphs (1)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 (2)] as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subparagraphs (A)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 (B)] , respectively, and moving them two ems to the right; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

====(2) Approval after death in line of duty– ====

Notwithstanding the service requirements under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 paragraph (1)] , an individual referred to in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subsection (a)] shall also include an individual who elected to transfer educational assistance as described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subsection (a)] but whose application for such transfer was not approved before such individual died in line of duty.; and
</blockquote>

:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subsection (f)] —
::(A) by striking“(1)Time for transfer.—Subject to”and inserting the following:

<blockquote>

====(1) Time for transfer– ====



=====(A) In general– =====

Except as provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subparagraph (B)] and subject to; and
</blockquote>

::(B) by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote>

====(B) Exception– ====



=====(i) Time for transfer for service-connected disability or injury in line of duty– =====

The limitation under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subparagraph (A)] with respect to the time to transfer entitlement benefits under this section shall not apply if an individual is discharged or released from active duty in the Armed Forces in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 section 3311(c) of this title] —
:(I) due to a service-connected disability; or
:(II) after sustaining an injury in line of duty that requires hospital, nursing home, or domiciliary care or treatment.

=====(ii) Extended period of eligibility– =====

An individual referred to in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 clause (i)] may transfer entitlement benefits under this section—
:(I) before the end of the 36-month period beginning on the date of the individual’s discharge or release from active duty in the Armed Forces; or
:(II) before the end of the 48-month period beginning on the date of such discharge or release if the Secretary concerned determines special circumstances exist.

=====(iii) Alternative period of eligibility– =====

In the case of an individual described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 clause (i)] who is discharged or released from active duty in the Armed Forces on or after August 1, 2009, but before the date of the enactment of this subparagraph, the extended period of eligibility described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 clause (ii)] shall begin on the date of the enactment of this subparagraph.

====(C) Additional opportunity to make election– ====

An individual described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subparagraph (B)(i)] may elect to transfer the individual’s entitlement under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3319 subsection (a)] , or to make changes to the dependent or dependents designated as the transferee or transferees of the individual’s entitlement under this section, one or more times during the time period referred to in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 subparagraph (B)(ii)] ..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 2. Effective Date=

The amendments made by this Act to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319 section 3319 of title 38, United States Code] , shall apply with respect to individuals who became eligible to transfer benefits under such section on or after August 1, 2009.
