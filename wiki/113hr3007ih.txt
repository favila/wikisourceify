{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to require States to recognize the military experience of veterans when issuing licenses and credentials to veterans, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Requirement that States recognize military experience of veterans when issuing licenses and credentials to veterans=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A Section 4102A(c) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A paragraph (9)] and inserting the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A paragraph (9)] :

<blockquote>
:(9) 
::(A) As a condition of a grant or contract under which funds are made available to a State under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A subsection (b)(5)] in order to carry out [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4103A section 4103A] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4104 4104 of this title] , the State shall—
:::(i) establish a program under which the State administers an examination to each veteran seeking a license or credential issued by the State and issues such license or credential to such veteran without requiring such veteran to undergo any training or apprenticeship if the veteran—
::::(I) receives a satisfactory score on completion of such examination, as determined by the State; and
::::(II) has not less than 10 years of experience in a military occupational specialty that, as determined by the State, is similar to a civilian occupation for which such license or credential is required by the State; and
:::(ii) submits each year to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] a report on the exams administered under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A clause (i)] during the most recently completed 12-month period that includes, for the period covered by the report, the number of veterans who completed an exam administered by the State under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A clause (i)] and a description of the results of such exams, disaggregated by occupational field.
::(B) Not less frequently than once each year, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] and the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] a report summarizing the information received by the Secretary under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A subparagraph (A)(ii)] ..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==



===(1) Exams– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A Subparagraph (A) of section 4102A(c)(9) of such title] , as added by subsection (a), shall take effect on the date that is one year after the date of the enactment of this Act and shall apply with respect to grants and contracts described in such subparagraph awarded after such date.

===(2) Reports– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4102A Subparagraph (B) of section 4102A(c)(9)] , as added by subsection (a), shall take effect on the date that is one year after the date of the enactment of this Act and the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]] shall submit the first report under such subparagraph not later than two years after the date of the enactment of this Act.
