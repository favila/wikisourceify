{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 13, United States Code, to provide that individuals in prison shall, for the purposes of a decennial census, be attributed to the last place of residence before incarceration.

=Section 1. Residence of incarcerated individuals=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/13/141 Section 141 of title 13, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by resdesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/13/141 subsection (g)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/13/141 subsection (h)] ; and
:(2) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/13/141 subsection (f)] the following:

<blockquote>
::(g) 
:::(1) Effective beginning with the 2020 decennial census of population, in taking any tabulation of total population by States under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/13/141 subjection (a)] for purposes of the apportionment of Representatives in [[w:|Congress]] among the several States, the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary]] shall, with respect to an individual incarcerated in a State, Federal, county, or municipal correctional center as of the date on which such census is taken, attribute such individual to such individual’s last place of residence before incarceration.
:::(2) In carrying out this subsection, the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary]] shall consult with each State department of corrections to collect the information necessary to make the determination required under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/13/141 paragraph (1)] ..
</blockquote>
