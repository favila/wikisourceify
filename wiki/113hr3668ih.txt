To amend the Medicare, Medicaid, and SCHIP Extension Act of 2007 to make permanent the exemption of grandfathered long-term care hospitals from the Medicare 25 percent threshold payment adjustment.

=Section 1. Making permanent the exemption of grandfathered long-term care hospitals from the Medicare 25 percent patient threshold payment adjustment=

 Section 114(c)(1) of the Medicare, Medicaid, and SCHIP Extension Act of 2007( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395ww 42 U.S.C. 1395wwnote] ), as amended by[[Public Law 111-148|section 3106 ofPublic Law 111–148]] , is further amended—
:(1) in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking “for a 5-year period” ; and
:(2) in subparagraph (A), by inserting “for a 5-year period,” before “section 412.536” .
