{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the Corps of Engineers from taking any action to establish a restricted area prohibiting public access to waters downstream of a dam, and for other purposes.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Freedom to Fish Act of 2013 ”.

2. Restricted areas at Corps of Engineers dams


=(a) In general=

The [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army ]], acting through the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers ]], shall not take any action to establish a restricted area prohibiting public access to waters downstream of a dam owned by the [[Wikipedia:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps of Engineers ]].
=(b) Effective date=


==(1) In general==

Subject to paragraph (2) , this section shall apply to an action described in subsection (a) on or after August 1, 2012.
==(2) Existing restrictions==

If the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army ]], acting through the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers ]], has taken an action described in subsection (a) during the period beginning on August 1, 2012, and ending on the date of enactment of this Act, the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Secretary ]]shall—: cease implementing the restricted area resulting from the action; and: remove any barriers constructed in connection with the restricted area.
