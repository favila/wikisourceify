{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Small Business Investment Act of 1958 to
		  provide for increased limitations on leverage for multiple licenses under
		  common control, and for other purposes.


{{section|HEBC65E9FF77B4EC0B9E8FD05B3D3EC01|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Small Business Investment Company
			 Modernization Act of 2013

        
.

{{section|H8F19101145744EFE9971BA9006CD1838|2. In general}}

        Section 303(b)(2)(B)
			 of the Small Business Investment Act of 1958
 (15 U.S.C. 683(b)(2)(B)

)
 is
			 amended by striking $225,000,000
 and inserting
			 $350,000,000
.
