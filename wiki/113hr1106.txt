{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Small Business Investment Act of 1958 to provide for increased limitations on leverage for multiple licenses under common control, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HEBC65E9FF77B4EC0B9E8FD05B3D3EC01}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Small Business Investment Company Modernization Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H8F19101145744EFE9971BA9006CD1838}}Sec. 2. In general=

 Section 303(b)(2)(B) of the Small Business Investment Act of 1958([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/683 15 U.S.C. 683(b)(2)(B)] ] )is amended by striking“$225,000,000”and inserting“$350,000,000”.
