{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require a full audit of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System and the Federal reserve banks by the Comptroller General of the United States, and for other purposes.    

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Federal Reserve Transparency Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Audit reform and transparency for the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System=



==(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714  section 714of title 31, United States Code], or any other provision of law, the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]]shall complete an audit of the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System]]and the Federal reserve banks under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 subsection (b) of such section 714]within 12 months after the date of the enactment of this Act.

==(b) Report– ==



===(1) In general– ===

Not later than 90 days after the audit required pursuant to subsection (a) is completed, the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]]—
:(A) shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]a report on such audit; and
:(B) shall make such report available to the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Speaker of the House]], the majority and minority leaders of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]], the majority and minority leaders of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]], the Chairman and Ranking Member of the committee and each subcommittee of jurisdiction in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]]and the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]], and any other Member of Congress who requests the report.

===(2) Contents– ===

The report under paragraph (1) shall include a detailed description of the findings and conclusion of the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]]with respect to the audit that is the subject of the report, together with such recommendations for legislative or administrative action as the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]]may determine to be appropriate.

==(c) Repeal of certain limitations– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 Subsection (b) ofsection 714of title 31, United States Code], is amended by striking the second sentence.

==(d) Technical and conforming amendments– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714  Section 714of title 31, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in subsection (d)(3)—
::(A) in subparagraph (A)—
:::(i) by striking“or (f)”;
:::(ii) in clause (i), by striking“or (f)”; and
:::(iii) in clause (ii), by striking“or (f)”; and
::(B) in subparagraph (C), by striking“or (f)”; and
:(2) by striking subsection (f).
