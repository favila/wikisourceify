{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To extend the application of certain space launch liability provisions through 2014.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Space Launch Liability Indemnification Extension Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Indemnification extension=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/51/50915  Section 50915(f)of title 51, United States Code], is amended by striking“December 31, 2013”and inserting“December 31, 2014”.    Passed the House of Representatives December 2, 2013. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
