{{Center|JOINT RESOLUTION}}

Providing for the appointment of John Fahey as a citizen regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution.

= =

That, in accordance with section 5581 of the Revised Statutes of the United States( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/43 20 U.S.C. 43] ), the vacancy on the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution]] , in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term of Roger W. Sant of the District of Columbia, on October 24, 2013, is filled by the appointment of John Fahey of the District of Columbia. The appointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on the date of enactment of this joint resolution.Passed the Senate January 29, 2014.Secretary
