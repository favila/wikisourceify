{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 23, United States Code, with respect to the operation of longer combination vehicles on the Interstate System in Idaho, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Idaho Safe and Efficient Vehicle Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Longer combination vehicles in Idaho=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/127 Section 127of title 23, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==(j) Longer combination vehicles in Idaho– ==

No limit or other prohibition under this section, except as provided in this subsection, applies to a longer combination vehicle operating on a segment of the Interstate System in Idaho if such vehicle—
:(1) has a gross vehicle weight of 129,000 pounds or less;
:(2) complies with the single axle, tandem axle, and bridge formula limits set forth in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/127 subsection (a)] ; and
:(3) is authorized to operate on such segment under Idaho State law..
</blockquote>
