={{anchor|H7C0DE01FDD8647B68D1AC06EF00674BA}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Flood Protection Public Safety Act of
			 2013”.

={{anchor|H944584A1DBD94A98A7AF066977B8E858}}Sec. 2. Authorization of
			 certain flood risk management projects=

The [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]], acting through
			 the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers]], is authorized to implement any flood risk management
			 project for which, before the date of enactment of this Act, the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of
			 the Army]] has transmitted to [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress]] a letter that includes—
:(1) a
			 description of the project as recommended in a report by the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers]]
			 that concludes that the project is technically sound, environmentally
			 acceptable, and economically justified; and
:(2) notification that
			 the [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Office of Management and Budget]] has concluded that the recommendations in
			 the report are consistent with the policy and programs of the [[Wikipedia:Executive Office of the President|President]].
