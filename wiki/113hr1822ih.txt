{{Center|A BILL}}

To extend the authorization period for certain uses of funds from the San Gabriel Basin Restoration Fund.

=Section 1. San Gabriel Basin restoration=



==(a) Extension– ==

 Section 110(a)(3)(A)(ii) of division B of the Miscellaneous Appropriations Act, 2001(as enacted into law by[[Public Law 106-554|section 1(a)(4) ofPublic Law 106–554]] ) is amended by striking “10” and inserting “15” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to any project constructed under Section 110 of such Actbefore, on, or after the date of enactment of this Act.
