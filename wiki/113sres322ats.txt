{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 To authorize the printing of a collection of the rules of the committees of the Senate.   

= =

That a collection of the rules of the committees of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] , together with related materials, be printed as a Senate document, and that there be printed 200 additional copies of such document for the use of the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Rules and Administration|Committee on Rules and Administration]] .
