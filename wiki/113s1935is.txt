{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to restore access to diabetic testing supplies for Medicare beneficiaries.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Diabetic Testing Supply Access Act of 2014” .

=Sec. 2. Authority for retail community pharmacies to deliver diabetic testing supplies to Medicare beneficiaries=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 1847(a) of the Social Security Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395w–3 42 U.S.C. 1395w–3(a)] )is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
:(8) Nothing in this section shall be construed as authorizing the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] (through regulation, guidance, instruction, or otherwise) to restrict or eliminate an individual’s option of electing, regardless of delivery method (except by mail and private parcel service), to have diabetic testing supplies delivered to the individual by a retail community pharmacy (as defined in section 1927(k)(10)), including a retail community pharmacy that contracts with a long-term care facility, assisted living facility, group home, or other type of residential setting recognized by the State. This paragraph shall not be construed as changing the amount of payment made under this part for diabetic testing supplies, but only as authorizing delivery of such supplies to individuals through retail community pharmacies..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to diabetic testing supplies delivered on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
