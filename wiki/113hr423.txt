{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to provide for the
		  termination of further retirement benefits for Members of Congress, except the
		  right to continue participating in the Thrift Savings Plan, and for other
		  purposes.

={{anchor|H0B6CE453E1B54892B1D5B3885ED2EE4A}}Section 1. Amendments relating to the
			 Civil Service Retirement System=



=={{anchor|H8FE343202740434A865ABD5488B161D3}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Subchapter III of
			 chapter 83 of title 5, United States Code, is amended by inserting after
			 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335 section 8335] the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|HADA9E7A138714F00B42CC249BACC6FEE}}Sec. 8335a. Termination
				of further retirement coverage of Members of Congress ==



==={{anchor|HF548E9E4FF6C46338B621636FB96354C}}(a) In
				general– ===

Notwithstanding any
				other provision of this subchapter, effective as of the date of enactment of
				this section—
:(1) a Member shall not be subject to this
				subchapter for any further period of time; and
:(2) no further
				Government contributions or deductions from basic pay may be made with respect
				to such Member for deposit in the Treasury of the United States to the credit
				of the Fund.

==={{anchor|H17A01970A1A84713893D6C5F78E2D2A6}}(b) Prior rights not
				affected– ===

Nothing in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (a)] shall be considered to
				nullify, modify, or otherwise affect any right, entitlement, or benefit under
				this subchapter with respect to any Member covering any period prior to the
				date of enactment of this section.

==={{anchor|H8FC05A1FC74D4576A89437C3C41C3743}}(c) Right To
				participate in Thrift Savings Plan not affected– ===

Nothing in
				[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (a)] shall affect the eligibility of a Member to participate in the
				Thrift Savings Plan in accordance with otherwise applicable provisions of
				law.

==={{anchor|HAA6944E4D81B43FF97AE6244857B8094}}(d) Regulations– ===



===={{anchor|H4A0B82B325E34080BBE0737DD76B1C61}}(1) In
				general– ====

Any regulations necessary to carry out this section
				may—
:(A) except with
				respect to matters under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subparagraph (B)], be prescribed by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the
				Office of Personnel Management]]; and
:(B) with respect to
				matters relating to the Thrift Savings Plan, be prescribed by the Executive
				Director (as defined by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 section 8401(13)]).

===={{anchor|HB236AA0A92A8459E9C9CDD24C07BA8F3}}(2) Refunds– ====

The
				regulations under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a paragraph (1)(A)] shall, in the case of any Member described
				in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (a)], provide that the lump-sum credit shall be payable to such
				Member to the same extent and in the same manner as if such Member satisfied
				[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8342 paragraphs (1)], [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8342 (3)], and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8342 (4) of section 8342(a)] as of the date of enactment of
				this section.

==={{anchor|H35E4D545928A4895942F8FC4E83DDD9C}}(e) Exclusion– ===

For
				purposes of this section, the term Member does not include the
				[[w:Office of the Vice President of the United States|Vice President]]..
				
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H37936890E70C44ECB56D65365A633F25}}(b) Clerical

={{anchor|H09894BDC38614A3BB4FE6F9711AE477A}}Sec. 2. Amendments relating to
			 the Federal Employees’ Retirement System=



=={{anchor|H8675D17989EF476B8AB289C5E1BB1C59}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Subchapter II of
			 chapter 84 of title 5, United States Code, is amended by inserting after
			 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425 section 8425] the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H2EC860534ACD4D48919F093B9F636FA3}}Sec. 8425a. Termination
				of further retirement coverage of Members of Congress==



==={{anchor|HD435DA9E887F49E0B1500D321A02ED10}}(a) In
				general– ===

Notwithstanding any
				other provision of this chapter, effective as of the date of enactment of this
				section—
:(1) in the case of an
				individual who first becomes a Member before such date of enactment—
::(A) such Member shall
				not be subject to this chapter for any further period of time after such date
				of enactment; and
::(B) no further Government contributions or
				deductions from basic pay may be made with respect to such Member for deposit
				in the Treasury of the United States to the credit of the Fund; and
:(2) in the case of an
				individual who first becomes a Member on or after such date of
				enactment—
::(A) such Member shall not be subject to this
				chapter; and
::(B) no Government contributions or deductions
				from basic pay may be made with respect to such Member for deposit in the
				Treasury of the United States to the credit of the Civil Service Retirement and
				Disability Fund.

==={{anchor|H98AA74ED8C704BDAB5E4DDB2A9A90000}}(b) Prior rights not
				affected– ===

Nothing in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (a)] shall be considered to
				nullify, modify, or otherwise affect any right, entitlement, or benefit under
				this chapter with respect to any Member covering any period prior to the date
				of enactment of this section.

==={{anchor|HAE47BFDA86B443249E78F993A6A6240E}}(c) Right To
				participate in Thrift Savings Plan not affected– ===

Nothing in
				[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (a)] shall affect the eligibility of a Member to participate in the
				Thrift Savings Plan in accordance with otherwise applicable provisions of
				law.

==={{anchor|H715D83891B4349BCB846E59396472970}}(d) Regulations– ===



===={{anchor|H5067C96A2AF849D982857096DD1B77A6}}(1) In
				general– ====

				may—
:(A) except with respect to matters under
				[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subparagraph (B)], be prescribed by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the Office of Personnel
				Management]]; and
:(B) with respect to
				matters relating to the Thrift Savings Plan, be prescribed by the Executive
				Director (as defined by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 section 8401(13)]).

===={{anchor|H9F06CDBEF3504C68975718CAB261C226}}(2) Refunds– ====

				regulations under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a paragraph (1)(A)] shall, in the case of a Member described in

</blockquote>
