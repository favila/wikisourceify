{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to temporarily exclude capital gain from gross income.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Growing Jobs Through Capital Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Two-year capital gains tax relief=



==(a) In general– ==

  Part I of subchapter P ofchapter 1of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (relating to treatment of capital gains) is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 1203. Two-year exclusion of gain from sale of capital assets==



===(a) In general– ===

Gross income shall not include gain from the sale or exchange of an asset which is a capital asset in the hands of the taxpayer.

===(b) Termination– ===

  Subsection (a) shall not apply to any sale or exchange later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this section. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

  Part I of subchapter P ofchapter 1of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new item:

<blockquote>  Sec. 1203. Two-year exclusion of gain from sale of capital assets.  . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to sales or exchanges after the date of the enactment of this Act, in taxable years ending after such date.
