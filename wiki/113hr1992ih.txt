{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the requirements relating to assessment of Israel’s qualitative military edge over military threats, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H43A6C02AB5614F6DAEFF750F365F7730}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Israel QME Enhancement Act”.

={{anchor|HBDD1A64DF7654CA78762710C0B2E7FA2}}Sec. 2. Amendments to requirements relating to assessment of Israel’s qualitative military edge over military threats=



=={{anchor|HEE82B22C3BE04AFC9B6DAEA99EDE77E8}}(a) Assessment required; reports– ==

[[Public Law 110-429|Section 201 of[[Public Law -|Public Law 110–429]] ]] (122 Stat. 4843;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/2776 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 22 U.S.C. 2776] note] )is amended—
:(1) in[[Public Law 110-429|subsection (a)]] , by striking“an ongoing basis”and inserting“a biennial basis”; and
:(2) in[[Public Law 110-429|subsection (c)(2)]] —
::(A) in the heading, by striking“Quadrennial”and inserting“Biennial”; and
::(B) in the text, by striking“Not later than four years after the date on which the President transmits the initial report under paragraph (1), and every four years thereafter,”and inserting“Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of the Israel QME Enhancement Act, and biennially thereafter,”.

=={{anchor|HC08F147F9ECA4A7E91FD066CE0250636}}(b) Report– ==



==={{anchor|H03CA4A41852848669F909641B805687E}}(1) In general– ===

Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a report on criteria used to include cyber and asymmetric threats for purposes of the assessment required under[[Public Law 110-429|section 201(a) of[[Public Law -|Public Law 110–429]] ]] (122 Stat. 4843;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/2776 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 22 U.S.C. 2776] note] ; relating to Israel’s qualitative military edge over military threats to Israel) (as amended by subsection (a)(1)).

==={{anchor|H64A16A94813A4A659B93D3B4EBA3721C}}(2) Definition– ===

In this subsection, the term qualitative military edge has the meaning given the term in section 36(h) of the Arms Export Control Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/2776 22 U.S.C. 2776(h)] ] ).
