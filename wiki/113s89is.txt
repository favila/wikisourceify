{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Migratory Bird Treaty Act to authorize hunting under certain circumstances.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Migratory Bird Treaty Amendment Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Migratory birds=

 Section 3 of the Migratory Bird Treaty Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/704 16 U.S.C. 704] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==(c) Exception– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of this Act, subsection (b)shall not apply to an agricultural producer, or a hunter with the permission of the agricultural producer, if—
:(1) the manipulation occurs on land of the agricultural producer on which a crop during the current or immediately preceding crop year was not harvestable due to a natural disaster, as determined by the [[w:Rural Utilities Service|Secretary of Agriculture]] ; and
:(2) a local representative of the [[w:Rural Utilities Service|Secretary of Agriculture]] confirms that—
::(A) the crop has been destroyed; and
::(B) it would not be economically practicable to harvest the crop..
</blockquote>
