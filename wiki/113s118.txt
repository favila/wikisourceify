{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to prohibit the use of public funds for political party conventions.

={{anchor|HA299C58B91E7464E85F28C862EDE2924}}Section 1. Prohibiting use of presidential election campaign funds for party conventions=



=={{anchor|H2AB29C6B516C4B1C921BD3A2E19A68D4}}(a) In general– ==

 Chapter 95 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking section 9008.

=={{anchor|HB7BF6FE023A14347833511995B0DF9EE}}(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections of chapter 95 of such Codeis amended by striking the item relating to section 9008.

={{anchor|HE3D3FD03FA384FAE83923DC1B7408FE7}}Sec. 2. Conforming amendments=



=={{anchor|H91D0E5B6F6EA4100AD3D96EF5C9AAC4C}}(a) Availability of payments to candidates– ==

The third sentence of section 9006(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking“, section 9008(b)(3),”.

=={{anchor|HF729B962FCCB485EAB3BB946D55F0F5B}}(b) Reports by Federal election commission– ==

 Section 9009(a) of such Codeis amended—
:(1) by adding“and”at the end of paragraph (2);
:(2) by striking the semicolon at the end of paragraph (3)and inserting a period; and
:(3) by striking paragraphs (4), (5), and (6).

=={{anchor|H5918E2A1EBAD44FA86336B98DDF6820E}}(c) Penalties– ==

 Section 9012 of such Codeis amended—
:(1) in subsection (a)(1), by striking the second sentence; and
:(2) in subsection (c), by striking paragraph (2)and redesignating paragraph (3)as paragraph (2).

=={{anchor|H6A03736E1B674783AE9F778CC92D3D01}}(d) Availability of payments from presidential primary matching payment account– ==

The second sentence of section 9037(a) of such Codeis amended by striking“and for payments under section 9008(b)(3)”.

={{anchor|H51A3594336524B9F93454D06F75FC4C4}}Sec. 3. Effective date=

The amendments made by this Act shall apply with respect to elections occurring after December 31, 2012.
