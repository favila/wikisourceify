{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Workforce Investment Act of 1998 to
		  integrate public libraries into State and local workforce investment boards,
		  and for other purposes.


{{section|HB73BF382629F489E88B888D58EB493FD|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Workforce Investments through Local
			 Libraries Act

        
 or the 
          WILL Act

        
.
