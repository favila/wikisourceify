={{anchor|HF2211BCC01F043CB98390680E7B88FAB}}Section 1. Kay Bailey Hutchison Spousal IRA=

The heading of subsection (c) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 219] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking“Special rules for certain married individuals”and inserting“Kay Bailey Hutchison Spousal IRA”.
