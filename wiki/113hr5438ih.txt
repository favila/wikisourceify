{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to repeal the excise tax on indoor tanning services.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Tanning Tax Repeal Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal of excise tax on indoor tanning services=



==(a) In general– ==

  Subtitle D of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking chapter 49 and by striking the item relating to such chapter in the table of chapters of such subtitle.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to services performed after the date of the enactment of this Act.
