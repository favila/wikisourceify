{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/117 section 117]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to exclude Federal student aid from taxable gross income.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Make Student Grants Truly Tax-Free Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Expansion of qualified scholarship exclusion from gross income=



==(a) In general– ==

  Paragraph (1) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/117 section 117(b)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended—
:(1) by striking“received by an individual as a scholarship”and inserting

<blockquote> received by an individual— 
::(A) as a scholarship , 
</blockquote>

:(2) by striking the period at the end and inserting a comma, and
:(3) by adding at the end the following new subparagraphs:

<blockquote> 
::(B) through a scholarship or fellowship grant program administered by the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary of Education]]that is based on financial need, including a  Federal Pell Grant under section 401 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1070a 20 U.S.C. 1070a] ), or 
::(C) as an Iraq and Afghanistan Service Grant under  section 420R of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1070h 20 U.S.C. 1070h]). . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this Act shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2014.
