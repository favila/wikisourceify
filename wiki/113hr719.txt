by

(D)
with respect to any insured credit union
				other than a low-income credit union, includes uninsured non-share accounts as
				authorized by the [[National Credit Union Administration|Board]], that—
(i)
do
				not alter the cooperative nature of the credit union;

(ii)
are subordinate
				to all other claims against the credit union, including the claims of
				creditors, shareholders, and the Fund;

(iii)
are available to
				be applied to cover operating losses of the credit union in excess of its
				retained earnings and, to the extent so applied, will not be
				replenished;

(iv)
if they have a
				stated maturity, have an initial maturity of at least 5 years;

(v)
if they have a stated maturity, the net
				worth value of such accounts may be discounted at the discretion of the [[National Credit Union Administration|Board]]
				when the remaining maturity is less than 5 years;

(vi)
are subject to disclosure and consumer
				protection requirements as determined by the [[National Credit Union Administration|Board]];

(vii)
are offered by a
				credit union that is determined by the [[National Credit Union Administration|Board]] to be sufficiently capitalized and
				well-managed; and

(viii)
are subject to
				such rules and regulations as the [[National Credit Union Administration|Board]] may
				establish.
