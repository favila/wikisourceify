{{Center|A BILL}}

To clarify the National Credit Union Administration authority to improve credit union safety and soundness.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Capital Access for Small Businesses and Jobs Act”.

2. Improving credit union safety and soundness


The Federal Credit Union Act ( 12 U.S.C. 1751 et seq. ) is amended—
: in section 107 —:: in paragraph (16) , by striking “and ”at the end;:: in paragraph (17) , by striking the period and inserting “; and ”; and:: by adding at the end the following:
::: to receive payments on uninsured non-share accounts described under section 216(o)(2)(D) , subject to such terms, rates, and conditions as may be established by the board of directors, within limitations prescribed by the Board.; and
: in section 216 —:: in subsection (b)(1)(B)(ii) , by striking “must rely ”and inserting “rely predominantly ”; and:: in subsection (o)(2) —::: in subparagraph (B) , by striking “and ”at the end;::: in subparagraph (C)(ii) , by striking the period and inserting “; and ”; and::: by adding at the end the following:
:::: with respect to any insured credit union other than a low-income credit union, includes uninsured non-share accounts as authorized by the [[Wikipedia:National Credit Union Administration|Board ]], that—::::: do not alter the cooperative nature of the credit union;::::: are subordinate to all other claims against the credit union, including the claims of creditors, shareholders, and the Fund;::::: are available to be applied to cover operating losses of the credit union in excess of its retained earnings and, to the extent so applied, will not be replenished;::::: if they have a stated maturity, have an initial maturity of at least 5 years;::::: if they have a stated maturity, the net worth value of such accounts may be discounted at the discretion of the [[Wikipedia:National Credit Union Administration|Board ]]when the remaining maturity is less than 5 years;::::: are subject to disclosure and consumer protection requirements as determined by the [[Wikipedia:National Credit Union Administration|Board ]];::::: are offered by a credit union that is determined by the [[Wikipedia:National Credit Union Administration|Board ]]to be sufficiently capitalized and well-managed; and::::: are subject to such rules and regulations as the [[Wikipedia:National Credit Union Administration|Board ]]may establish..
