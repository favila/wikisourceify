{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Coast Guard Authorization Act of 1989 to expand the Coast Guard Junior Reserve Officers Training Program Pilot Program to include a Coast Guard unit at Pinellas Park High School in Pinellas Park, Florida, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Expansion of Coast Guard Junior Reserve Officers Training Program Pilot Program=



==(a) Inclusion of additional school– ==

   Subsection (a) of section 204 of the Coast Guard Authorization Act of 1989 ( [[Public Law 101-225|Public Law 101–225]]  ;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/14/92 14 U.S.C. 92 note])is amended—
:(1) by striking“in cooperation”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 
::(1) in cooperation ; 
</blockquote>

:(2) by striking the period at the end and inserting“; and”; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
::(2) in cooperation with the Pinellas County School District of Pinellas County, Florida, as part of Pinellas Park High School (hereinafter in this section referred to as the“High School”). . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Conforming amendments– ==

Such section is further amended—
:(1) by inserting“and the High School”after“Academy”in subsections (b)(2), (c), and (d) (the first and third places“Academy”appears); and
:(2) by inserting“or the High School, as the case may be”after“Academy”in subsection (d) (the second place“Academy”appears).
