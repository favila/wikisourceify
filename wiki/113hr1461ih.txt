{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the renewable fuel program of the Environmental Protection Agency.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Renewable Fuel Standard Elimination Act” .

=Sec. 2. Repeal of renewable fuel program=

 Section 211(o) of the Clean Air Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/7545 42 U.S.C. 7545(o)] )is repealed.
