To amend title 38, United States Code, to expand the definition of homeless veteran for purposes of benefits under the laws administered by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Expansion of definition of homeless veteran for purposes of benefits under the laws administered by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2002 Section 2002(1) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “in section 103(a) of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/11302 42 U.S.C. 11302(a)] )” and inserting “in subsection (a)or (b) of section 103 of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/11302 42 U.S.C. 11302] )” .
