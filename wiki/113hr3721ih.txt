{{Center|A BILL}}

 To reauthorize the America’s Agricultural Heritage Partnership.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ America’s Agricultural Heritage Partnership Reauthorization Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization of the America’s agricultural heritage partnership=

   Section 707 of division II of the Omnibus Parks and Public Lands Management Act of 1996  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/461 (16 U.S.C. 461note]; [[Public Law 104-333|Public Law 104–333]]  )is amended by striking“September 30, 2013”and inserting“September 30, 2023”.
