{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the continued lease or eventual conveyance of certain Federal land within the boundaries of Fort Wainwright Military Reservation in Fairbanks, Alaska.

={{anchor|HD1E7C9B20CF74CE996149956BA2AB72D}}Section 1. Land lease and conveyance, Fort Wainwright Military Reservation, Fairbanks, Alaska=



=={{anchor|H2F5A9A681CE14E49A3F44F60A374A970}}(a) Covered land defined– ==

In this section, the term covered land means a parcel of Federal land consisting of approximately 76 acres in Fairbanks, Alaska, that—
:(1) is under the jurisdiction of the [[w:Bureau of Land Management|Bureau of Land Management]] ;
:(2) was withdrawn for military use pursuant to Executive Order 7596 of 1937 and is administered by the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] as part of Fort Wainwright Military Reservation; and
:(3) contains a 400-home rental housing community pursuant to a Department of the Army lease (Lease No. DACA 85–1–86–71) dated June 26, 1986.

=={{anchor|HF56BB1C9D42C4E2BAD8EF8BF633C9CCB}}(b) Authority To extend current lease– ==



==={{anchor|H96B803EEF29F4B6E8FC345C0FA33717C}}(1) Authority to extend; term– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] may offer to extend the lease described in subsection (a)(3)for one or more periods of 15 years.

==={{anchor|H1F59183A32C84CD484F2DAAF9C6CD226}}(2) Notice of exercise of discretion to extend– ===

If the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] intends to offer any extension of the lease, as authorized by paragraph (1), the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] shall provide written notice of the offer to the current lessee and the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] not later than 36 months before the date of the expiration of the lease.

=={{anchor|H12FB49FD04D5458292AB2A4F98DA1702}}(c) Conveyance option– ==

If the lease described in subsection (a)(3)is not extended under subsection (b), then upon the expiration of the lease, the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] , with the concurrence of the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] , shall use the authority provided by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2878 section 2878 of title 10, United States Code] , to convey all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to the covered land.

=={{anchor|H6CFD50F237014B78819C38F1AD6B6C1C}}(d) National security waiver– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] may waive the conveyance requirement under subsection (c)if the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] determines that retention and military use of the covered land are in the national security interests of the United States. The waiver shall not take effect until the end of the 90-day period beginning on the date on which the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] notifies the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] and [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] of the need for the waiver.

=={{anchor|HC2C0C014B6E045C9BC4F5BEB93AFD9A2}}(e) Appraisal required– ==

In the event of the conveyance of the covered land under subsection (c), the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] shall require an appraisal of the land, which shall be performed by an independent appraiser in accordance with the Uniform Appraisal Standards for Federal Land Acquisitions and the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice .

=={{anchor|H112BE9DCE072408E90151B33A333AFB1}}(f) Consideration– ==

As consideration for the conveyance of the covered land under subsection (c), the purchaser shall pay to the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] an amount equal to the appraised value of the covered land, as determined under subsection (e).

=={{anchor|H1E0376BEBFBD4478A2758CCF54B8DC1E}}(g) Costs of Conveyance– ==

All costs of the conveyance under subsection (c), including appraisal costs, shall be paid by the purchaser.

=={{anchor|H6E73C3A4486B46EC9C4DA50FF53F234F}}(h) Revocation of withdrawal order– ==

In the event the covered land is conveyed under subsection (c), Executive Order 7596 of 1937 is revoked to the extent necessary to permit conveyance of the land.
