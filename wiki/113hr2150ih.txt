To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide for a five-year extension to the homeless veterans reintegration programs.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Homeless Veterans’ Reintegration Programs Reauthorization Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Five-year extension of homeless veterans reintegration programs=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2021 Section 2021(e)(F)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “2013” and inserting “2018” .
