{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act to repeal certain limitations on health care benefits.

={{anchor|HF6385D64E72E48F390269B339D7752AC}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Family Health Care Flexibility Act”.

={{anchor|H9E468DB5A6E641B19185B722BA40986D}}Sec. 2. Repeal of distributions for medicine qualified only if for prescribed drug or insulin=

 Section 9003 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act([[Public Law -|Public Law 111–148]] )and the amendments made by such section are repealed; and the Internal Revenue Code of 1986shall be applied as if such section, and amendments, had never been enacted.

={{anchor|H15E20777C9C24B7D976E9E0E99516BF6}}Sec. 3. Repeal of limitation on health flexible spending arrangements under cafeteria plans=

 Sections 9005and 10902 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act([[Public Law -|Public Law 111–148]] )and section 1403 of the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010([[Public Law -|Public Law 111–152]] )and the amendments made by such sections are repealed.
