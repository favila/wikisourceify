{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit funding to the Institute of Peace.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on funding the Institute of Peace=

No funds available to the [[w:United States Department of State|Department of State]]or any other department or agency may be used to provide contributions directly or indirectly to the [[w:|Institute of Peace]].
