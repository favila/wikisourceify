{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 to require responsible parties to pay the full cost of offshore oil spills, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Big Oil Bailout Prevention Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Removal of limits on liability for offshore facilities=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 1004(a) of the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/33/2704 33 U.S.C. 2704(a)] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (2) , by inserting“and”after the semicolon;
:(2) by striking paragraph (3) ; and
:(3) by redesignating paragraph (4) as paragraph (3) .

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==

   Section 1004(b)(2) of the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/33/2704 33 U.S.C. 2704(b)(2)] )is amended by striking the second sentence.
