={{anchor|H579E1F2958414679B14E6EE11C8D5DF0}}Section 1. Disperse Orange 30=



=={{anchor|H3BCCA4A541994FE0B3198E525B6D587B}}(a) In general– ==

Heading 9902.03.45 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (relating to Disperse Orange 30) is amended by striking the date in the effective period column and inserting“12/31/2016”.

=={{anchor|H44606046C8F049EFA04B3CA58C8A188C}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)applies to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
