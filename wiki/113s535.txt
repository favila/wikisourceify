{{Center|A BILL}}

To require a study and report by the Small Business Administration regarding the costs to small business concerns of Federal regulations.

={{anchor|idD3CD74BA45FD46978BC3D0185F531B8C}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Regulation Costs to Small Business Act”.

={{anchor|idF87564FD912441E7B943E91691514048}}Sec. 2. Small Business Administration study on the cost of Federal regulations=



=={{anchor|idDBA34F296F804D489382FAFA1DBC1261}}(a) In general– ==

The [[w:Small Business Administration|Administrator]] shall conduct an annual study of the total costs to small business concerns of Federal regulations.

=={{anchor|id01EB2EB237F04308A5238F7C305F625B}}(b) Requirement– ==

In conducting the study required under subsection (a), the [[w:Small Business Administration|Administrator]] shall use a methodology that is substantially similar to the methodology used in conducting the study described in the report entitled“The Impact of Regulatory Costs on Small Firms”(September 2010), prepared on behalf of the Office of Advocacy of the Administration.

=={{anchor|id95ED26659AD74860BB3449227FC15E5A}}(c) Report– ==

Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, and annually thereafter, the [[w:Small Business Administration|Administrator]] shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a report on the findings of the study conducted under subsection (a), which shall include an estimate of the total annual costs of Federal regulations to small business concerns, by agency, as such term is defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/551 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 551] of title 5, United States Code] .

=={{anchor|idF69AF357164345C5A836C56B69B5D2AA}}(d) Funding– ==



==={{anchor|id3FB986AE18A44077BA9148C219D56567}}(1) In general– ===

The [[w:Small Business Administration|Administration]] shall carry out this section using unobligated funds otherwise made available to the [[w:Small Business Administration|Administration]] .

==={{anchor|id1949251138A04F56B411E177D5C75B36}}(2) Sense of Congress regarding funding– ===

It is the Sense of [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] that no additional funds should be made available to the [[w:Small Business Administration|Administration]] to carry out this Act.

={{anchor|id22B104C9B6A841C88E32D4C4A5D52AA1}}Sec. 3. Definitions=

In this Act—
:(1) the terms“Administration”and“Administrator”mean the [[w:Small Business Administration|Small Business Administration]] and the Administrator thereof, respectively; and
:(2) the term“small business concern”has the same meaning as in section 3 of the Small Business Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/632 15 U.S.C. 632] ] ).
