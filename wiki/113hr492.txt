{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Reserve Act to remove the mandate on the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System and the Federal Open Market Committee to focus on maximum employment.


{{section|HE3FDA8B0F0A74C24B753C0E35C6C67DF|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the Focusing the Fed on the Currency of the United States Act of 2013
 or the FFOCUS Act of 2013
.

{{section|H2F29E189126242D09F83169FD9EB5EDB|2. Removal of dual mandate}}

        Section 2A of the Federal Reserve Act
 (12 U.S.C. 225a
)
 is amended by striking maximum employment, stable prices,
 and inserting stable prices
.
