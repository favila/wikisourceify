{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Reserve Act to remove the mandate on the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System and the Federal Open Market Committee to focus on maximum employment.

={{anchor|HE3FDA8B0F0A74C24B753C0E35C6C67DF}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Focusing the Fed on the Currency of the United States Act of 2013”or the“FFOCUS Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H2F29E189126242D09F83169FD9EB5EDB}}Sec. 2. Removal of dual mandate=

 Section 2A of the Federal Reserve Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/225a 12 U.S.C. 225a] )is amended by striking“maximum employment, stable prices,”and inserting“stable prices”.
