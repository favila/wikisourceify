{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize certain programs relating to traumatic brain injury and to trauma research.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Traumatic Brain Injury Reauthorization Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. CDC programs for prevention and surveillance of traumatic brain injury=

   Section 394A of the Public Health Service Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/280b-3 42 U.S.C. 280b–3])is amended—
:(1) by striking the section heading and all that follows through“For the purpose”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 394. Authorization of appropriations==



===(a) In general– ===

For the purpose ; and 
</blockquote>

:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

===(b) Traumatic brain injury– ===

To carry out sections 393B and 393C , there are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018. . 
</blockquote>
 

=Sec. 3. State grants for projects regarding traumatic brain injury=

   Section 1252 of the Public Health Service Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300d-52 42 U.S.C. 300d–52])is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a) , by striking“, acting through the Administrator of the Health Resources and Services Administration,”; and
:(2) in subsection (j) , by striking“2012”and inserting“2018”. 

=Sec. 4. State grants for protection and advocacy services=

   Section 1253 of the Public Health Service Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300d-53 42 U.S.C. 300d–53])is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a) , by striking“, acting through the [[w:Health Resources and Services Administration|Administrator of the Health Resources and Services Administration]] (referred to in this section as the“Administrator”),”;
:(2) in subsections (c) , (d)(1) , (e)(1) , (e)(4) , (g) , (h) , and (j)(1) , by striking“Administrator”each place it appears and inserting“Secretary”;
:(3) in subsection (i) —
::(A) by striking“  [[w:Health Resources and Services Administration|Administrator of the Health Resources and Services Administration]]  ”and inserting“Secretary”; and
::(B) by striking“by the Administrator”and inserting“by the Secretary”; and
:(4) in subsection (l) , by striking“2012”and inserting“2018”. 

=Sec. 5. Interagency program for trauma research=

   Section 1261(i) of the Public Health Service Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300d-61 42 U.S.C. 300d–61(i)])is amended by striking“2012”and inserting“2018”.
