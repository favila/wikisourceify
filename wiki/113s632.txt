{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 to repeal a duplicative program relating to inspection and grading of catfish.

={{anchor|id7A27E15FE6384AF5B9F80435C06234E6}}Section 1. Repeal of duplicative program=



=={{anchor|idC973DEBE379F428DBE1EE39EAD183F52}}(a) In general– ==

Effective on the date of enactment of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/8701 7 U.S.C. 8701 et seq.] ), section 11016 of that Act([[Public Law -|Public Law 110–246]] ;122 Stat. 2130)and the amendments made by that section are repealed.

=={{anchor|id97F63F280C5542D2A92C9DE3703EDB77}}(b) Application– ==

The Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/1621 7 U.S.C. 1621 et seq.] ] )and the Federal Meat Inspection Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/21/601 21 U.S.C. 601 et seq.] ] )shall be applied and administered as if section 11016 of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008([[Public Law 110-246|Public Law 110–246]] ;122 Stat. 2130)and the amendments made by that section had not been enacted.
