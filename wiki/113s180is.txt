{{Center|A BILL}}

To delay the enforcement of any rulings of the National Labor Relations Board until there is a final resolution in pending lawsuits.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “NLRB Freeze Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Delay in enforcement of certain rules=



==(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any determinations made by the [[w:National Labor Relations Board|National Labor Relations Board]] with respect to which unconstitutional members were part of the [[w:National Labor Relations Board|Board's]] quorum, shall not be given any force or effect until the date on which final judgment is entered in all cases challenging the constitutionality of the appointment of members to such [[w:National Labor Relations Board|Board]] during a recess of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] that are pending before a Federal court on the date of enactment of this Act.

==(b) Definition– ==

The term unconstitutional member means a member of the [[w:National Labor Relations Board|National Labor Relations Board]] who was appointed during a recess of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] and who is the subject of a cause of action relating to such appointment that is pending before a Federal court on the date of enactment of this Act.
