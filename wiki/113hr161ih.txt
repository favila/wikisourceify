{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Water Resources Development Act of 1976 to direct the Secretary of the Army to evaluate the feasibility of continuing Federal participation in a beach nourishment project, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Bringing Economic Advancement and Coastal Health Act” .

=Sec. 2. Beach nourishment=

 Section 156 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1976( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1962d–5f 42 U.S.C. 1962d–5f] )is amended—
:(1) by striking “The” and inserting “(a)Continuing Federal participation in a project.—The” ;
:(2) by inserting before the period at the end the following: “or after the date of the last estimated periodic nourishment as contemplated in the Chief’s Report, whichever is later” ; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

===(b) Feasibility review– ===

Before the end of the fifty-year period referred to in subsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] , acting through the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers]] , shall undertake, subject to the availability of appropriations, a review of a project to which subsection (a)applies to evaluate the feasibility of continuing Federal participation in the project and shall make a recommendation concerning such participation to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] ..
</blockquote>
