{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make members of health care sharing ministries eligible to establish health savings accounts.

={{anchor|H3E9F47AEDBF742DDAD1F72C1D75EBD01}}Section 1. Members of health care sharing ministries eligible to establish health savings accounts=



=={{anchor|H49E0D48597814FCB85D91289A24E59C9}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 223 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|HB8B7F5F5F7884C34A2E4C4EDF7EFF7E9}}(i) Application to health care sharing ministries– ===

For purposes of this section, membership in a health care sharing ministry (as defined in section 5000A(d)(2)(B)(ii)) shall be treated as coverage under a high deductible health plan..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H59001941EA7A4A5DA298E7085FD3AE6F}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
