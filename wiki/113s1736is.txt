{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend titles 5 and 38, United States Code, to clarify the veteran status of an individual based on the attendance of the individual at a preparatory school of a service academy, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Support Earned Recognition for Veterans Act   ”or the“ SERVe Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Clarification of veteran status=



==(a) Clarification of definition of military service– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101  Section 101of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 paragraph (21)(D)], by inserting after“Naval Academy”the following:“(but, except for purposes ofchapter 17of this title in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 section 107(e)(2)], does not include any service performed by a student at a preparatory school of a service academy who is not otherwise a member of the Armed Forces)”;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 paragraph (22)], by inserting before the period at the end the following:“or, except for purposes ofchapter 17of this title in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 section 107(e)(2)], duty performed by a student at a preparatory school of a service academy who is not otherwise a member of the Armed Forces”; and
:(3) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 paragraph (23)], by adding after the period at the end the following:“Except for purposes ofchapter 17of this title in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 section 107(e)(2)], such term does not include duty performed by a student at a preparatory school of a service academy who is not otherwise a member of the Armed Forces.”.

==(b) Service deemed not To be active service– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107  Section 107of title 38, United States Code], is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote> 
:(e) 
::(1) Except as provided by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 paragraph (2)], duty performed by a student at a preparatory school of a service academy who is not otherwise a member of the Armed Forces shall not be deemed to have been active military, naval, or air service for the purposes of any of the laws administered by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] , regardless of whether the student was injured or disabled as a result of such duty.
::(2)  Chapter 17of this title shall apply to an individual described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 paragraph (1)]with respect to furnishing hospital care and medical services solely for an injury or disability incurred by the individual as a result of military training related to future active duty service performed as a student during the course of required training at a preparatory school of a service academy. An individual who receives such care and services under this paragraph may not be treated as a veteran for the purposes of any other provision of law solely by reason of receiving such care and services under this paragraph. . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Small business concerns– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8127  Section 8127(l)of title 38, United States Code], is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
:(3) The term veteran , in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 sections 101]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 107]of this title, does not include an individual whose veteran status is based solely on the attendance of the individual as a student at a preparatory school of a service academy, regardless of whether the individual was injured or disabled as a result of duty performed as such a student. . 
</blockquote>


==(d) Preference eligible– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108  Section 2108of title 5, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 paragraph (4)(B)], by striking“; and”and inserting a semicolon;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 paragraph (5)], by striking the period at the end and inserting“; and”; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
::(6) an individual whose veteran status is based solely on the attendance of the individual as a student at a preparatory school of a service academy, regardless of whether the individual was injured or disabled as a result of duty performed as such a student, may not be treated as a“veteran”,“disabled veteran”, or“preference eligible”. . 
</blockquote>
