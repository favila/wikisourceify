{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to prohibit the conversion of leadership PAC funds to personal use.

={{anchor|HC2737C54469F49548721EDE2D27C5DD8}}Section 1. Short Title=

This Act may be cited as the“Leadership PAC Limitation Act”.

={{anchor|H64ACF9F13ADB40F1801270B83F6CFE6E}}Sec. 2. Prohibiting Conversion of Leadership PAC Funds to Personal Use=



=={{anchor|H32F31801B36F4F2DA0A323D9A3438640}}(a) Prohibition– ==

 Section 313(b)(1) of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/439a 2 U.S.C. 439a(b)(1)] )is amended by inserting after“subsection (a)”the following:“or funds of a leadership PAC (as defined in section 304(i)(8)(B))”.

=={{anchor|HF7F0D20D4BD5492BA795D865B021FA87}}(b) Effective Date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply with respect to elections occurring on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
