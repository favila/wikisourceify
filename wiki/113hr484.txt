{{Center|A BILL}}

To recognize a primary measure of national unemployment for purposes of the Federal Government.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Real Unemployment Calculation Act”.

2. Primary measure of unemployment


The measure of unemployment issued by the [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Labor Statistics|Bureau of Labor Statistics ]]known as U5 shall be treated by the [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Labor Statistics|Bureau ]]as the official unemployment rate and shall be considered by the Federal Government as the primary measure of unemployment in the United States.
