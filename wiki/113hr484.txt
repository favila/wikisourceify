{{Center|A BILL}}

To recognize a primary measure of national unemployment
		  for purposes of the Federal Government.


{{section|H7CCFAD93F78743CA8939B6FCDCA59F33|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Real Unemployment Calculation
			 Act

        
.

{{section|HBED65F94CB6D46DE9872C911DBABEB17|2. Primary measure of unemployment}}
The measure of
			 unemployment issued by the [[Bureau of Labor Statistics|Bureau of Labor Statistics]] known as U5 shall be
			 treated by the [[Bureau of Labor Statistics|Bureau]] as the official unemployment rate and shall be considered
			 by the Federal Government as the primary measure of unemployment in the United
			 States.
