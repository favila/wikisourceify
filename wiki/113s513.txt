{{Center|A BILL}}

To clarify the role of the Cherokee Nation of Oklahoma regarding the maintenance of the W.D. Mayo Lock and Dam in the State of Oklahoma.

={{anchor|HC6C417F5BFDB49AA90F02619AD1D5C13}}Section 1. Rights and responsibilities of Cherokee Nation of Oklahoma regarding W.D. Mayo Lock and Dam, Oklahoma=

 Section 1117 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1986([[Public Law -|Public Law 99–662]] ;100 Stat. 4236)is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

={{anchor|H21D6B7796E0B4D00AD7CFC18C75BA3B4}}Sec. 1117. W.D. Mayo Lock and Dam, Oklahoma=



=={{anchor|HF5DFE975F8964FA0A5CED317529811E9}}(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Cherokee Nation of Oklahoma may—
:(1) design and construct 1 or more hydroelectric generating facilities at the W.D. Mayo Lock and Dam on the Arkansas River in the State of Oklahoma, subject to the requirements of subsection (b)and in accordance with the conditions specified in this section; and
:(2) market the electricity generated from any such hydroelectric generating facility.

=={{anchor|HBD21DD4BB453416BB74AD5BB52F8FE75}}(b) Preconstruction requirements– ==



==={{anchor|id58CA3A06F55044F3BC886EC8C4ED733D}}(1) In general– ===

The Cherokee Nation shall obtain any permit required by Federal or State law before the date on which construction begins on any hydroelectric generating facility under subsection (a), except that the Cherokee Nation shall be exempt from any licensing requirements under the Federal Power Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/791a 16 U.S.C. 791a et seq.] ] )that may otherwise be required for the construction, operation, or maintenance of a hydroelectric generating facility.

==={{anchor|H30FDD9EE51B448389E55866F67CE5B28}}(2) Review by Secretary– ===

The Cherokee Nation may initiate the design or construction of a hydroelectric generating facility under subsection (a)only after the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] reviews and approves the plans and specifications for the design and construction.

=={{anchor|H3B0E0A13D6CB49A39BBAC339A7B32952}}(c) Payment of design and construction costs– ==



==={{anchor|H952F6D7ACA454BCE9BD1A9CA1E408CAB}}(1) In general– ===

The Cherokee Nation shall—
:(A) bear all costs associated with the design and construction of any hydroelectric generating facility under subsection (a); and
:(B) provide any funds necessary for the design and construction to the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] prior to the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] initiating any activities relating to the design and construction of the hydroelectric generating facility.

==={{anchor|id970D29D709F44683ACAD67C1287A62B3}}(2) Use by Secretary– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] may—
:(A) accept funds offered by the Cherokee Nation under paragraph (1); and
:(B) use the funds to carry out the design and construction of any hydroelectric generating facility under subsection (a).

=={{anchor|HC0CA53069B764653906B863CF910580D}}(d) Assumption of liability– ==

The Cherokee Nation—
:(1) shall hold all title to any hydroelectric generating facility constructed under this section;
:(2) may, subject to the approval of the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] ,assign that title to a third party;
:(3) shall be solely responsible for—
::(A) the operation, maintenance, repair, replacement, and rehabilitation of any such facility; and
::(B) the marketing of the electricity generated by any such facility; and
:(4) shall release and indemnify the United States from any claims, causes of action, or liabilities that may arise out of any activity undertaken to carry out this section.

=={{anchor|HE03DE7A3D919487BAD93EFFE1D71FDEF}}(e) Assistance available– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] may provide any technical and construction management assistance requested by the Cherokee Nation relating to the design and construction of any hydroelectric generating facility under subsection (a).

=={{anchor|H6FFD60D249A34D7F9BF9A38972D8964D}}(f) Third-Party agreements– ==

The Cherokee Nation may enter into agreements with the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] or a third party that the Cherokee Nation or the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] determines to be necessary to carry out this section..
</blockquote>
