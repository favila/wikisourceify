{{Center|A BILL}}

To replace the Director of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection with a 5-person Commission, to bring the Bureau into the regular appropriations process, to provide for a safety and soundness check, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H39CF9F6FB9914FD79F62A87AEEF32074}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Responsible Consumer Financial Protection Regulations Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HD401FBC13737417CABB9605FACB8AF4F}}Sec. 2. Establishment of the Commission=

 Section 1011 of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5491 12 U.S.C. 5491] )is amended—
:(1) by striking subsections (b), (c), and (d);
:(2) by redesignating subsection (e)as subsection (j); and
:(3) by inserting after subsection (a)the following new subsections:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|HCF4E6AED150848BB8CE1A2603824DDF5}}(b) Establishment of the Commission– ===



===={{anchor|H3A1F8E4F42914DFE9009D71F3249400D}}(1) In general– ====

There is hereby established a commission (hereafter in this title referred to as the“Commission”) that shall serve as the head of the Bureau.

===={{anchor|HBD22E2DA1DD14FD58D79E3E9C9132C85}}(2) Authority to prescribe regulations– ====

The [[w:|Commission]] may prescribe such regulations and issue such orders in accordance with this title as the [[w:|Commission]] may determine to be necessary for carrying out this title and all other laws within the jurisdiction of the [[w:|Commission]] , and shall exercise any authorities granted under this title and all other laws within the jurisdiction of the [[w:|Commission]] .

==={{anchor|H5D6AD538B00244889E039A2BC4A15053}}(c) Composition of the Commission– ===



===={{anchor|H6AB9FEC1C63C499F81AABF63954E0B83}}(1) In general– ====

The [[w:|Commission]] shall be composed of 5 members, who shall be appointed by the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] , by and with the advice and consent of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] , from among individuals who—
:(A) are citizens of the United States; and
:(B) have strong competencies and experiences related to consumer financial protection.

===={{anchor|HC3DAAE326CE34B73BEA5F22D6FBCD38D}}(2) Staggering– ====

The members of the [[w:|Commission]] shall serve staggered terms, which initially shall be established by the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] for terms of 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 years, respectively.

===={{anchor|HEBD45BD2526C422EA889F8850A0122C0}}(3) Terms– ====



====={{anchor|H26AF37E6EA8045FBAA207F98DD8B207D}}(A) In general– =====

Each member of the [[w:|Commission]] , including the Chair, shall serve for a term of 5 years.

====={{anchor|HEF5BFB7212D647A1898B65817CF6C7F6}}(B) Removal for cause– =====

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] may remove any member of the [[w:|Commission]] only for inefficiency, neglect of duty, or malfeasance in office.

====={{anchor|H212C4920E7834A04BF76DD40A2FDB5A7}}(C) Vacancies– =====

Any member of the [[w:|Commission]] appointed to fill a vacancy occurring before the expiration of the term to which the predecessor of that member was appointed (including the Chair) shall be appointed only for the remainder of the term.

====={{anchor|H2975AC92D8D04698AE424791FD3FEC0F}}(D) Continuation of service– =====

Each member of the [[w:|Commission]] may continue to serve after the expiration of the term of office to which that member was appointed until a successor has been appointed by the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] and confirmed by the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] , except that a member may not continue to serve more than 1 year after the date on which the term of that member would otherwise expire.

====={{anchor|H892102673AE94CC6BC29BA4C63DDD077}}(E) Other employment prohibited– =====

No member of the [[w:|Commission]] shall engage in any other business, vocation, or employment during the term of service of that member on the [[w:|Commission]] .

==={{anchor|H93559F50434B4938830EE4654F8CBE57}}(d) Affiliation– ===

With respect to members appointed pursuant to subsection (c), not more than 3 shall be members of any one political party.

==={{anchor|HCA810D6773244E47AF49A04A5C8E8E91}}(e) Chair of the Commission– ===



===={{anchor|HFD6824EA3A424761ACBE42B32CCB37D4}}(1) Appointment– ====

The [[w:|Chair of the Commission]] shall be appointed by the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] from among the members of the [[w:|Commission]] .

===={{anchor|H5416D3FE98C243159C8407ADCCEF285B}}(2) Authority– ====

The [[w:|Chair]] shall be the principal executive officer of the Bureau, and shall exercise all of the executive and administrative functions of the Bureau, including with respect to—
:(A) the appointment and supervision of personnel employed under the Bureau (other than personnel employed regularly and full time in the immediate offices of members of the [[w:|Commission]] other than the Chair);
:(B) the distribution of business among personnel appointed and supervised by the [[w:|Chair]] and among administrative units of the Bureau; and
:(C) the use and expenditure of funds.

===={{anchor|HF0AA0FBEF9074B029F0CB5892F668EE6}}(3) Limitation– ====

In carrying out any of the functions of the [[w:|Chair]] under this subsection, the [[w:|Chair]] shall be governed by general policies of the [[w:|Commission]] and by such regulatory decisions, findings, and determinations as the [[w:|Commission]] may by law be authorized to make.

===={{anchor|HA53147EE11FC4FFCBB04F6F4BF5A8A94}}(4) Requests or estimates related to appropriations– ====

Requests or estimates for regular, supplemental, or deficiency appropriations on behalf of the [[w:|Commission]] may not be submitted by the [[w:|Chair]] without the prior approval of the [[w:|Commission]] .

==={{anchor|H89D6E51DCC7848709C0058F7296059A1}}(f) No impairment by reason of vacancies– ===

No vacancy in the membership of the [[w:|Commission]] shall impair the right of the remaining members of the [[w:|Commission]] to exercise all the powers of the [[w:|Commission]] . Three members of the [[w:|Commission]] shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business, except that if there are only 3 members serving on the [[w:|Commission]] because of vacancies in the membership of the [[w:|Commission]] , 2 members of the [[w:|Commission]] shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business. If there are only 2 members serving on the [[w:|Commission]] because of vacancies in the membership of the [[w:|Commission]] , 2 members shall constitute a quorum for the 6-month period beginning on the date of the vacancy which caused the number of [[w:|Commission]] members to decline to 2.

==={{anchor|H3D9A92D8BB50414590492E116ACE65A9}}(g) Seal– ===

The [[w:|Commission]] shall have an official seal.

==={{anchor|H8E4D47786D004695BCF6590DD1ACB81D}}(h) Compensation– ===



===={{anchor|H81A06ECDBD404DD1812E7ACD4B969EFD}}(1) Chair– ====

The [[w:|Chair]] shall receive compensation at the rate prescribed for level I of the Executive Schedule under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5313 section 5313 of title 5, United States Code] .

===={{anchor|H5ED891489D394E32868B301816CD6CD6}}(2) Other members of the Commission– ====

The 4 members of the [[w:|Commission]] other than the [[w:|Chair]] shall each receive compensation at the rate prescribed for level II of the Executive Schedule under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5314 section 5314 of title 5, United States Code] .

==={{anchor|HD403A24830E44E9FA32A198EC6860815}}(i) Initial quorum established– ===

During any time period prior to the date of confirmation of at least 2 members of the [[w:|Commission]] , one member of the [[w:|Commission]] shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business. Following the confirmation of at least 2 additional commissioners, the quorum requirements of subsection (f)shall apply..
</blockquote>
