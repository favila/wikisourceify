{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to increase the Federal medical assistance percentage for the District of Columbia under the Medicaid Program to 75 percent.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “District of Columbia Medicaid Reimbursement Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Increasing the Medicaid Federal medical assistance percentage for the District of Columbia to 75 percent=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 1905(b)(3) of the Social Security Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1396d 42 U.S.C. 1396d(b)(3)] ] )is amended by striking “70 percent” and inserting “75 percent” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to calendar quarters beginning on or after October 1, 2013.
