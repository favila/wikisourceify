{{Center|A BILL}}

To require the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives to make video recordings of the examination and testing of firearms and ammunition, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Fairness in Firearm Testing Act”.

=Sec. 2. Video recording of examination and testing of firearms and ammunition by the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives=

 Section 1111 of the Homeland Security Act of 2002([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/6/531 6 U.S.C. 531] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==(e) Additional responsibilities regarding examination and testing of firearms and ammunition– ==



===(1) Video recording required– ===

The [[w:Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives|Bureau]] shall make a video recording of the entire process of the examination and testing by the [[w:Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives|Bureau]] of an item for the purpose of determining, pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921 section 921(a)(3)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 922(o)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 922(p)] , or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/925 subsection (a)(3)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/925 (a)(4)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/925 (d)] , or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/925 (e) of section 925 of title 18, United States Code] , or section 5845(a) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, whether the item is a firearm and if so, the type of firearm, or of determining pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921 section 921(a)(17) of title 18, United States Code] , whether the item is ammunition, and shall not edit or erase any recording made pursuant to this paragraph.

===(2) Availability– ===



====(A) To owners– ====

On request of a person who claims an ownership interest in an item with respect to which a recording is made under paragraph (1), the [[w:Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives|Bureau]] shall make available to the person, without charge, a digital video disc that contains a copy of the recording.

====(B) To defendants– ====

The [[w:Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives|Bureau]] shall make available to a defendant in a criminal proceeding involving an item with respect to which a recording is made under paragraph (1)a digital video disc that contains a copy of the recording.

===(3) Admissibility as evidence– ===

An item which the [[w:Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives|Bureau]] has determined is a firearm or a type of firearm or ammunition pursuant to any provision of law specified in paragraph (1)shall not be admissible as evidence in any judicial or administrative proceeding unless—
:(A) the [[w:Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives|Bureau]] has complied with paragraph (2)with respect to the item; or
:(B) such compliance has been waived, in writing, by the person against whom the item is offered as evidence..
</blockquote>
