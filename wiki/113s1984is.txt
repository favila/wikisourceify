{{Center|A BILL}}

 To enhance penalties for computer crimes, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Credit Card Theft Sentencing Act of 2014 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Enhanced penalty=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1030  Section 1030of title 18, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1030 subsection (a)(2)(A)], by striking“in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/1602 section 1602(n)]of title 15”and inserting“in  section 103(o) of the Consumer Credit Protection Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/1602 15 U.S.C. 1602(o)] ) ”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1030 subsection (c)(2)]—
::(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1030 subparagraph (B)(iii)], by striking“and”at the end;
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1030 subparagraph (C)], by inserting“and”after the semicolon; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:::(D) a fine under this title, imprisonment for any term of years not less than 25 or for life, or both, in the case of an offense under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1030 subsection (a)(2)]in which information of 1,000,000 or more cardholders (as defined in  section 103(n) of the Consumer Credit Protection Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/1602 15 U.S.C. 1602(n)] )) is obtained; . 
</blockquote>
