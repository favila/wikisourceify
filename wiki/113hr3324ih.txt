{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Lacey Act Amendments of 1981 to reduce burdensome paperwork, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as “The Lacey Act Paperwork Reduction Act” .

=Sec. 2. Amendments to Lacey Act Amendments of 1981=

 Section 3(f) of the Lacey Act Amendments of 1981( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/3372 16 U.S.C. 3372(f)] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1), by—
::(A) striking “files” and inserting “possesses and has available for inspection by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] , acting through the [[w:U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service|United States Fish and Wildlife Service]] , if so requested,” ; and
::(B) in subparagraph (B), striking clause (i)and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
:::(i) the declared value of the plant or the merchandise containing the plant;; and
</blockquote>

:(2) by striking paragraph (6)and inserting the following:

<blockquote>

===(6) Regulations– ===



====(A) In general– ====

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] , acting through the [[w:U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service|United States Fish and Wildlife Service]] and in consultation with the Animal and Health Inspection Service, may issue, in accordance with [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/553 section 553of title 5, United States Code] , such regulations as may be necessary to carry out this subsection.

====(B) Distinction; limitation on applicability– ====

In promulgating such regulations, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] may—
:(i) distinguish among different species; and
:(ii) limit the applicability of this subsection to a particular class or type of species if the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] determines that application of this subsection would not be feasible, practicable, or effective..
</blockquote>
