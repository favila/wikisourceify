{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit the use of Federal funds and the provision of technical assistance for the Heritage Partnership Program and National Heritage Areas.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on Federal assistance for National Heritage Areas=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be used and no Federal technical assistance may be provided for the following:
:(1) The Heritage Partnership Program.
:(2) Any National Heritage Area.
