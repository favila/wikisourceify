{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 10, United States Code, to require cost or price to the Federal Government be given at least equal importance as technical or other criteria in evaluating competitive proposals for defense contracts.   

=Section 1. Requirement that cost or price to the Federal Government be given at least equal importance as technical or other criteria in evaluating competitive proposals for defense contracts=



==(a) Requirement– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2305 Subparagraph (A) ofsection 2305(a)(3)of title 10, United States Code], is amended by striking“proposals; and”at the end of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2305 clause (ii)]and all that follows through the end of the subparagraph and inserting the following:“proposals and that must be assigned importance at least equal to all evaluation factors other than cost or price when combined.”.

==(b) Waiver– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2305 Section 2305(a)(3) of such title]is further amended by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2305 subparagraph (B)]and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 
:(B) The requirement of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2305 subparagraph (A)(ii)]relating to assigning at least equal importance to evaluation factors of cost or price may be waived by the head of the agency. . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Report– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2305 Section 2305(a)(3) of such title]is further amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote> 
:(C) Not later than 180 days after the end of each fiscal year, the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] , and post on a publicly available website of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] , a report containing a list of each waiver issued by the head of an agency under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2305 subparagraph (B)]during the preceding fiscal year. . 
</blockquote>
