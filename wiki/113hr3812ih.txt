{{Center|A BILL}}

 To repeal sections 1341 and 1342 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ No Bailouts for Insurance Industry Act of 2014 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal of sections 1341 and 1342 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act=



==(a) In general– ==

  Sections 1341 and 1342 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18061 42 U.S.C. 18061] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18062 18062]) are each repealed.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The repeals made by subsection (a) shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act, but the repeal of sections 1341 and 1342 shall not apply to plan years and calendar years, respectively, beginning before such date of enactment.
