{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to provide an extension of the work opportunity tax credit for veterans.   

=Section 1. Extension of work opportunity tax credit for veterans=



==(a) In general– ==

  Subparagraph (B) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/51 section 51(c)(4)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by inserting“(December 31, 2018, in the case of any qualified veteran)”before the period at the end.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to individuals who begin work for the employer after December 31, 2013.
