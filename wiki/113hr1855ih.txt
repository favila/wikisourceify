{{Center|A BILL}}

To require the Secretary of Defense to establish a process to determine whether individuals claiming certain service in the Philippines during World War II are eligible for certain benefits despite not being on the Missouri List, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Determination of certain service in Philippines during World War II=



==(a) In general– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] , in consultation with the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] and such military historians as the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] considers appropriate, shall establish a process to determine whether a covered individual served as described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 subsection (a)] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 (b) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 107] of title 38, United States Code] , for purposes of determining whether such covered individual is eligible for benefits described in such subsections.

==(b) Covered individuals– ==

For purposes of this section, a covered individual is any individual who—
:(1) claims service described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 subsection (a)] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/107 (b) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 107] of title 38, United States Code] ; and
:(2) is not included in the Approved Revised Reconstructed Guerilla Roster of 1948, known as the Missouri List .
