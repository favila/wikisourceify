{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide that the only health plans that the Federal Government may make available to the President, Vice President, Members of Congress, and Federal employees are those created under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act or offered through a health insurance exchange.

=Section 1. Health plans created under PPACA or offered through Exchanges to be only health plans Federal Government may make available to President, Vice President, Members of Congress, and Federal employees=

 Section 1312(d)(3)(D) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18032 42 U.S.C. 18032(d)(3)(D)] )is amended—
:(1) in the subparagraph heading, by striking “Members of Congress” and inserting “President, Vice President, Members of Congress, and Federal employees” ;
:(2) in clause (i), in the matter preceding subclause (I)—
::(A) by striking “Members of Congress and congressional staff” and inserting “the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] , [[w:Office of the Vice President of the United States|Vice President]] , Members of Congress, and Federal employees” ; and
::(B) by striking “a Member of Congress or congressional staff” and inserting “the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] , the [[w:Office of the Vice President of the United States|Vice President]] , a Member of Congress, or a Federal employee” ; and
:(3) in clause (ii), by amending subclause (II)to read as follows:

<blockquote>

===(II) Federal employee– ===

The term Federal employee means—
:(aa) an “employee” , as such term is defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2105 section 2105of title 5, United States Code] ; and
:(bb) includes an individual to whom [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2105 subsection (c)] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2105 (f) of such section 2105] pertains (whether or not such individual satisfies item (aa))..
</blockquote>
