{{Center|A BILL}}

To assist low-income individuals in obtaining medically recommended dental care.

={{anchor|H6FCC35E0543B45ADA6D4B08C8FE67AF1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Coordination of Pro Bono Medically Recommended Dental Care Act”.

={{anchor|HB80DCFC346414E908BC97EA7B0FAFA20}}Sec. 2. Grants or contracts to facilitate low-income access to dental care=

 Part B of title III of the Public Health Service Actis amended by inserting after section 317M([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/247b–14a 42 U.S.C. 247b–14a] )the following new section:

<blockquote>

={{anchor|H7D331C1BC70349FDAFE539BEA5E50320}}Sec. 317M–1. Grants or contracts to facilitate low-income access to dental care=



=={{anchor|H79C363EACA8340768303B469B31E0BEC}}(a) Grants– ==

The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall award competitive grants to, or enter into contracts with, eligible entities to maximize the number of eligible low-income individuals receiving dental care.

=={{anchor|H2A034C5AC0BB476683CB324FA61930A0}}(b) Use of funds– ==

An entity shall use amounts received under a grant or contract under this section to fund the employment costs of a program to—
:(1) coordinate the provision of free, medically recommended dental care to eligible low-income individuals by volunteer dentists in a manner consistent with State licensing laws; and
:(2) verify the medical, dental, and financial needs of eligible low-income individuals who may be eligible for such dental services.

=={{anchor|H9B9EB6096C4C4B379B6AECF6C8924B7F}}(c) Eligibility– ==



==={{anchor|H0B05A7D9584F4C1494D198A4C6034120}}(1) Eligible entity– ===

To be eligible to receive a grant or contract under subsection (a), an entity shall—
:(A) be an entity that is exempt from tax under section 501(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986;
:(B) provide for the participation of eligible individuals in a free dental services program; and
:(C) submit to the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] an application at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] may require.

==={{anchor|HFCE2D5D6520B4AC7A00BD3FFC95641D3}}(2) Eligible individual– ===

To be eligible to participate in a program described in paragraph (1)(B), an individual shall be—
:(A) an adult individual entitled to benefits under part A of title XVIII of the Social Security Act;
:(B) an individual enrolled in part B of title XVIII of such Act;
:(C) an individual enrolled in a State plan or waiver under title XIX of such Act; or
:(D) an individual enrolled in a State child health plan under title XXI of such Actor under an approved waiver of such plan.

=={{anchor|H5D147E1AB3AE4D15AB093DB97D58FB95}}(d) Authorization of appropriations– ==

There is authorized to be appropriatedto carry out this section,$2,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018..
