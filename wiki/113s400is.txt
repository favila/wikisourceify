{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act to include the Corps of Engineers as a Federal land management agency, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Corps of Engineers Recreation Improvement Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Amendments=



==(a) Definitions– ==

   Section 802 of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/6801 16 U.S.C. 6801])is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (4) , by inserting“the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps of Engineers]],”after“  [[w:United States Bureau of Reclamation|Bureau of Reclamation]],”;
:(2) in paragraph (10) —
::(A) in subparagraph (A) , by striking“and”after the semicolon;
::(B) in subparagraph (B) , by striking the period at the end and inserting“; and”; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:::(C) the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]], with respect to the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps of Engineers]]. ; and 
</blockquote>

:(3) in paragraph (11) , by inserting“, the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]],”after“  [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Interior]] ”.

==(b) Recreation fee authority– ==

   Section 803 of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/6802 16 U.S.C. 6802])is amended—
:(1) in subsection (d)(1) , in the matter preceding subparagraph (A) , by inserting“the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps of Engineers]],”after“  [[w:United States Forest Service|Forest Service]],”;
:(2) in subsection (f) , in the matter preceding paragraph (1) , by inserting“the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps of Engineers]],”after“  [[w:United States Bureau of Reclamation|Reclamation]],”; and
:(3) in subsection (g)(2) , in the matter preceding subparagraph (A) , by inserting“the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps of Engineers]],”after“  [[w:Bureau of Land Management|Land Management]],”.
