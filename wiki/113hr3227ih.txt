 To remove from the John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System certain properties in South Carolina.   

=Section 1. Removal of properties in South Carolina from John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System=



==(a) In general– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]]shall revise one or more maps included in the set of maps entitled Coastal Barrier Resources System referred to in  section 4(a) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/3503 16 U.S.C. 3503(a)] ), and relating to John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System Unit SC–03 in South Carolina, as necessary to remove from such System the parcels of land at the following addresses in South Carolina:
