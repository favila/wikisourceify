{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit royalty incentives for deepwater drilling, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Deepwater Drilling Royalty Relief Prohibition Act”.

={{anchor|ID545a564ef9b5481097705898263aba5c}}Sec. 2. Prohibition on royalty incentives for deepwater drilling=



=={{anchor|idCBD9AE6F2FDE4B57A98C52B7FFC5CDF6}}(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] shall not issue any oil or gas lease sale under the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/1331 43 U.S.C. 1331 et seq.] ] )with royalty-based incentives in any tract located in water depths of 400 meters or more on the outer Continental Shelf.

=={{anchor|ID4f8dd0bede36477bb0cba564a01f0c25}}(b) Royalty relief for deepwater production– ==

 Section 345 of the Energy Policy Act of 2005([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/15905 42 U.S.C. 15905] ] )is repealed.

=={{anchor|id4C5405FFC7D54AD98BF7BF3F8034C2A8}}(c) Royalty relief– ==

 Section 8(a)(3) of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/1337 43 U.S.C. 1337(a)(3)] ] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|id303AAAD93CDA4774A5FC7474440FF705}}(D) Prohibition– ===

Notwithstanding subparagraphs (A) through (C)or any other provision of law, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall not reduce or eliminate any royalty or net profit share for any lease or unit located in water depths of 400 meters or more on the outer Continental Shelf..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|ID3b27b18e2c9f47f4a029a9144c628659}}(d) Application– ==

This section and the amendments made by this section—
:(1) apply beginning with the first lease sale held on or after the date of enactment of this Act for which a final notice of sale has not been published as of that date; and
:(2) do not apply to a lease in effect on the date of enactment of this Act.
