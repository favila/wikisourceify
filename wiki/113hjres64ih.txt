{{Center|JOINT RESOLUTION}}

Disapproving a rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to “Standards of Performance for Greenhouse Gas Emissions from New Stationary Sources: Electric Utility Generating Units” .

= =

That [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] disapproves the rule submitted by the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Environmental Protection Agency]] relating to “Standards of Performance for Greenhouse Gas Emissions from New Stationary Sources: Electric Utility Generating Units” , signed by the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency]] on September 20, 2013, and identified by docket ID number EPA–HQ–OAR–2013–0495, and such rule shall have no force or effect.
