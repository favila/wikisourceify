This Act may be cited as the “Section 526 Repeal Act” .

=Sec. 2. Repeal=

 Section 526 of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/17142 42 U.S.C. 17142] )is repealed.
