{{Center|A BILL}}

 To reauthorize the National Geological and Geophysical Data Preservation Program Act of 2005 through 2019.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Data Preservation Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization of National Geological and Geophysical Data Preservation Program Act of 2005=

The  National Geological and Geophysical Data Preservation Program Act of 2005 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/15908 42 U.S.C. 15908] )is amended in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/15908 subsection (k)]by striking“2006 through 2010”and inserting“   2015 through 2019  ”.
