{{Center|A BILL}}

To exempt certain payments to States from sequestration.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Return Our State Shares Act” .

=Sec. 2. Exemption of certain payments from sequestration=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 255(g)(1)(A) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/905 2 U.S.C. 905(g)(1)(A)] )is amended—
:(1) by inserting before “Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, Deposit Insurance Fund (51–4596–0–4–373).” the following:

<blockquote>
:: Federal Aid in Wildlife Restoration (14–5029–0–2–303).;
</blockquote>

:(2) by inserting after “Financial Agent Services (20–1802–0–1–803).” the following:

<blockquote>
:: Financial assistance to States pursuant to section 105(a)(2)(B) of the Gulf of Mexico Energy Security Act of 2006( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/1331 43 U.S.C. 1331note] )from the Land Acquisition and State Assistance account (14–5035–0–2–303).;
</blockquote>

:(3) by inserting before “Hazardous Waste Management, Conservation Reserve Program (12–4336–0–3–999).” the following:

<blockquote>
:: Gulf Coast Restoration Trust Fund (20–8625–0–7–452).;
</blockquote>

:(4) by inserting before “Payments to Copyright Owners (03–5175–0–2–376).” the following:

<blockquote>
:: Payments of States' shares from certain Gulf of Mexico leases (14–5535–0–2–302).;
</blockquote>

:(5) by inserting before “Payments to the United States Territories, Fiscal Assistance (14–0418–0–1–806).” the following:

<blockquote>
:: Payments to States under the Mineral Leasing Act and Associated Payments (14–5003–0–2–999).; and
</blockquote>

:(6) by inserting before “Tennessee Valley Authority Fund, except nonpower programs and activities (64–4110–0–3–999).” the following:

<blockquote>
:: Sport Fish Restoration (14–8151–0–7–303)..
</blockquote>


==(b) Applicability– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to any sequestration order issued under the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/900 2 U.S.C. 900 et seq.] )on or after the date of enactment of this Act.
