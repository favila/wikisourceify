{{Center|A BILL}}

 To reinstate and extend the deadline for commencement of construction of a hydroelectric project involving the Little Wood River Ranch.   

=Section 1. Extension of time for Federal Energy Regulatory Commission project involving Little Wood River Ranch=

Notwithstanding the time period specified in  section 13 of the Federal Power Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/806 16 U.S.C. 806] )that would otherwise apply to the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Federal Energy Regulatory Commission]] project numbered 12063, the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Federal Energy Regulatory Commission]] shall, at the request of the licensee for the project, and after reasonable notice and in accordance with the procedures of the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Commission]] under that sectionâ€”
:(1) extend the time period during which the licensee is required to commence the construction of project works to the end of the 3-year period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act; or
:(2) if the license for Project No. 12063 has been terminated, reinstate the license and extend the time period during which the licensee is required to commence the construction of project works to the end of the 3-year period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act.
