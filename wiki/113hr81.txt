{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend subtitle B of title I of the Patient Protection
		  and Affordable Care Act to extend the temporary high-risk insurance pool
		  program to the territories.

={{anchor|HA1FF794E68FF4B6BA8DB662120F8C94F}}Section 1. Inclusion of territories in
			 temporary high-risk insurance pool program=



=={{anchor|H452D7E8EF9114E37A082F21634928400}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Section 1101 of the
			 Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18001 42 U.S.C. 18001]) is amended by
			 adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|HE863A65E5A8947D1A48E6B23CF5BB717}}(h) State
				defined– ===

Notwithstanding
				section 1304(d), for purposes of this section the term State means
				each of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, American Samoa, Guam, the
				Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico,
				the Virgin Islands of the United States, and any other territory or possession
				of the United
				States..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HD8DD4C1E7B544105BFAEC400484807F8}}(b) Conforming
			 amendment– ==

Section 1304(d) of
			 the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18024 42 U.S.C. 18024(d)]) is amended
			 by inserting “(except for purposes of section 1101)” after
			 “In this title”.
