{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Public Health Services Act and the Social Security Act to extend health information technology assistance eligibility to behavioral health, mental health, and substance abuse professionals and facilities, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Behavioral Health Information Technology Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Extension of health information technology assistance for behavioral health and mental health and substance abuse=

 Section 3000(3) of the Public Health Services Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300jj 42 U.S.C. 300jj(3)] )is amended by inserting “a behavioral or mental health professional (as defined in section 331(a)(3)(E)(i)), a substance abuse professional, a psychiatric hospital (as defined in section 1861(f) of the Social Security Act), a community mental health center that meets the criteria specified in section 1913(c)(including community mental health centers that are operated by county behavioral health agencies), a residential or outpatient mental health or substance abuse treatment facility,” before “and any other category” .
