{{Center|A BILL}}

 To appropriately limit the authority to award bonuses to employees.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “   Stop Wasteful Bonuses in Department of Veterans Affairs Act of 2014   ”or the“   VA Bonus Accountability Act   ”.  

=Sec. 2. Bonuses=



==(a) Adverse findings and employees under investigation– ==

  Chapter 45of title 5, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==IV Limitations on Bonus Authority– ==



==Sec. 4531. Certain forms of misconduct==



===(a) Definitions– ===

In this section—
:(1) the term“adverse finding”relating to an employee means a determination that the conduct of the employee—
::(A) violated a policy of the agency for which the employee may be removed or suspended; or
::(B) violated a law for which the employee may be imprisoned of more than 1 year;
:(2) the term“agency”has the meaning given that term under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/551 section 551]; and
:(3) the term“bonus”means any bonus or cash award, including—
::(A) an award under this chapter;
::(B) an award under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5384 section 5384]; and
::(C) a retention bonus under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5754 section 5754].

===(b) Adverse findings– ===



====(1) In general– ====

The head of an agency shall not award a bonus to an employee of the agency until 5 years after the end of the fiscal year in which the [[w:Inspector General|Inspector General]]or another senior ethics official of the agency or the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General of the United States]]makes an adverse finding relating to the employee.

====(2) Previously awarded bonuses– ====

If the [[w:Inspector General|Inspector General]]or another senior ethics official of the agency or the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General of the United States]]makes an adverse finding relating to an employee, the head of the agency employing the employee, after notice and an opportunity for a hearing, shall issue an order directing the employee to repay the amount of any bonus awarded to the employee during the year during which the adverse finding is made. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Technical and conforming amendment– ==

The table of sections for chapter 45of title 5, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>  Subchapter IV—Limitations on Bonus Authority 4531. Certain forms of misconduct.  . 
</blockquote>
