{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to ensure that veterans in each of the 48 contiguous States are able to receive services in at least one full-service hospital of the Veterans Health Administration in the State or receive comparable services provided by contract in the State.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Veterans Health Equity Act of 2013”.

=Sec. 2. Availability of full-service hospital of the Veterans Health Administration in certain States or provision of comparable services through contract with other health care providers in the State=



==(a) In general– ==

Chapter 17 of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1716 section 1716] the following new section:

<blockquote>

==Sec. 1716A. Access to full-service hospitals in certain States or comparable services through contract==



===(a) Requirement– ===

With respect to each of the 48 contiguous States, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall ensure that veterans in the State eligible for hospital care and medical services under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1710 section 1710] of this title have access—
:(1) to at least one full-service hospital of the [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration]] in the State; or
:(2) to hospital care and medical services comparable to the services provided in full-service hospitals through contract with other health care providers in the State.

===(b) Rule of construction– ===

Nothing in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1716A subsection (a)] shall be construed to restrict the ability of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] to provide enhanced care to an eligible veteran who resides in one State in a hospital of the [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration]] in another State..
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by inserting after the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1716 section 1716] the following new item:

<blockquote>__NOTOC__
1716A. Access to full-service hospitals in certain States or comparable services through contract..
</blockquote>


==(c) Report on implementation– ==

Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a report describing the extent to which the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] has complied with the requirement imposed by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1716A section 1716A of title 38, United States Code] , as added by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1716A subsection (a)] , including the effect of such requirement on improving the quality and standards of care provided to veterans.
