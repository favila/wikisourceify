{{Center|A BILL}}

To reduce Federal spending and the deficit by terminating
		  taxpayer financing of presidential election campaigns and party
		  conventions.

={{anchor|H528AB7F13A314E21B0C00EA2E1C86A91}}Section 1. Termination of taxpayer
			 financing of presidential election campaigns=



=={{anchor|H36076F62195D48548D66BAA2B54132B1}}(a) Termination of
			 designation of income tax payments– ==

Section 6096 of the Internal
			 Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new
			 subsection:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H014E668B1229409BB70C7402D050E367}}(d) Termination– ===

This
				section shall not apply to taxable years beginning after December 31,
				2011..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H20B76483AB874D248BC8C7B205A0E8BF}}(b) Termination of
			 fund and account– ==



==={{anchor|H75133DC39EDF49E8A24FFEA2F5BA0D86}}(1) Termination of
			 presidential election campaign fund– ===



===={{anchor|H9BFE26C22BC340968C7CD6CEA8F8D143}}(A) In
			 general– ====

Chapter 95 of subtitle H of such Code is amended by
			 adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H574A1DB260F447D5A01F89E26247C563}}Sec. 9014. Termination====

The provisions of this chapter shall not
				apply with respect to any presidential election (or any presidential nominating
				convention) after the date of the enactment of this section, or to any
				candidate in such an
				election..
</blockquote>


===={{anchor|H01EE5968ED8D4AF5A2A461AD59B6FBFC}}(B) Transfer of
			 excess funds to general fund– ====

Section 9006 of such Code is amended
			 by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

====={{anchor|H30E6D2E653C44A758CA3AFAB7AFF2727}}(d) Transfer of
				funds remaining after termination– =====

The [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] shall transfer
				all amounts in the fund after the date of the enactment of this section to the
				general fund of the Treasury, to be used only for reducing the
				deficit..
</blockquote>


==={{anchor|HE07D020771504307AD182824129D71FC}}(2) Termination of
			 account– ===

Chapter 96 of subtitle H of such Code is amended by
			 adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H441D697A14E44A4AA19C81E0CE2AEBF3}}Sec. 9043. Termination===

The provisions of this chapter shall not
				apply to any candidate with respect to any presidential election after the date
				of the enactment of this
				section..
</blockquote>
