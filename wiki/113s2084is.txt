{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Endangered Species Act of 1973 to require the Secretary of the Interior to publish and make available for public comment a draft economic analysis at the time a proposed rule to designate critical habitat is published.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Community Protection Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Draft economic analysis for critical habitat designation=

   Section 4(b)(2) of theEndangered Species Act of 1973  ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1533 16 U.S.C. 1533(b)(2)] )is amended—
:(1) in the first sentence, by striking“(2) The Secretary shall”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

===(2) Critical habitat designation– ===



====(A) In general– ====

The Secretary shall ; 
</blockquote>

:(2) in the second sentence, by striking“The Secretary may”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

===(B) Exclusions– ===

The Secretary shall ; and 
</blockquote>

:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

===(C) Draft economic analysis– ===

At the time a proposed rule to designate critical habitat is published, the Secretary shall publish and make available for public comment a draft analysis that—
:(i) examines the public and private economic effects of the proposed designation, including any effects on—
::(I) possible uses of land and property values;
::(II) employment; and
::(III) revenues available for State and local governments; and
:(ii) is quantitative. . 
</blockquote>
