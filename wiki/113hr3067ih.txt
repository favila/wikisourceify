{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act to prohibit a government subsidy for the purchase of a health plan by a Member of Congress.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “No Obamacare Subsidies for Members of Congress Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. No health care subsidies for Members of Congress=

 Section 1312(d)(3)(D) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18032 42 U.S.C. 18032(d)(3)(D)] )is amended—
:(1) by redesignating clause (ii)as clause (iii); and
:(2) by inserting after clause (i)the following new clause:

<blockquote>

===(ii) No premium assistance for Members of Congress– ===

Notwithstanding any other provision of law or any regulation or guidance promulgated by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the Office of Personnel Management]] , no Federal funds shall be expended to pay any portion of the premium for a health plan purchased by a Member of Congress pursuant to clause (i)..
</blockquote>
