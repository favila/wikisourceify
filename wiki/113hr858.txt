{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend section 520 of the Housing Act of 1949 to revise the requirements for areas to be considered as rural areas for purposes of such Act.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Rural Housing Preservation Act of 2013”.

2. Definition of Rural Area


The second sentence of section 520 of the Housing Act of 1949 ( 42 U.S.C. 1490 ) is amended by striking: “1990 or 2000 decennial census shall continue to be so classified until the receipt of data from the decennial census in the year 2010 ”and inserting “1990, 2000, or 2010 decennial census shall continue to be so classified until the receipt of data from the decennial census in the year 2020 ”.
