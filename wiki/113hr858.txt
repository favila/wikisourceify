{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend section 520 of the Housing Act of 1949 to revise
		  the requirements for areas to be considered as rural areas for purposes of such
		  Act.


{{section|HE0FD1EF8D7E746F4AB655534CFECDA21|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Rural Housing Preservation Act of
			 2013

        
.

{{section|HDF6720D27A1D4281B0E2BAD91D8E1859|2. Definition of Rural Area}}
The second sentence
			 of section 520 of the Housing Act of 1949
 (42 U.S.C. 1490
)
 is amended by
			 striking: 1990 or 2000 decennial census shall continue to be so
			 classified until the receipt of data from the decennial census in the year
			 2010
 and inserting 1990, 2000, or 2010 decennial census shall
			 continue to be so classified until the receipt of data from the decennial
			 census in the year 2020
.
