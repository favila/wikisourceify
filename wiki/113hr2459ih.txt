{{Center|A BILL}}

To reinstate overnight delivery standards for market-dominant products, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Protect Overnight Delivery Act” .

=Sec. 2. Market-dominant product service standards=

With respect to market-dominant products (as that term is described insubchapter I ofchapter 36of title 39, United States Code)—
:(1) the service standards implemented by the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] on July 1, 2012, are repealed and shall have no force or effect; and
:(2) the [[w:United States Postal Service|Postal Service]] shall reinstate the service standards that were in effect on December 31, 2011.
