{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 5, United States Code, to provide additional points to competitive service entrance exam of preference eligibles applying for positions at the Department of Veterans Affairs, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Veterans’ Assistance to Lower Unemployment and Enhance VA Services Act   ”or the“  VALUES Act  ”. 

=Sec. 2. Additional competitive service entrance exam points for preference eligibles applying for positions at the Department of Veterans Affairs=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3309  Section 3309of title 5, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by striking“A preference eligible”and inserting“(a) A preference eligible”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
::(b) Notwithstanding the requirements of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3309 subsection (a)], any preference eligible who receives a passing grade in an examination for entrance into the competitive service at a position within the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]is entitled to additional points above his earned rating, as follows:
:::(1) A preference eligible under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 section 2108(3)(C)–(G) of this title]—15 points.
:::(2) A preference eligible under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 section 2108(3)(A)]–[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 (B) of this title]—10 points. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Application– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to any examination for entrance into the competitive service at the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]taken on or after the date of enactment of this section.
