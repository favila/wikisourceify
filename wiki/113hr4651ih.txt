{{Center|A BILL}}

 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 601 West Baker Road in Baytown, Texas, as the“Specialist Keith Erin Grace, Jr. Memorial Post Office”.   

=Section 1. Specialist Keith Erin Grace, Jr. Memorial Post Office=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]located at 601 West Baker Road in Baytown, Texas, shall be known as the“Specialist Keith Erin Grace, Jr. Memorial Post Office”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referenced tosubsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Specialist Keith Erin Grace, Jr. Memorial Post Office”.
