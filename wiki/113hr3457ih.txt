{{Center|A BILL}}

To authorize an additional district judgeship for the district of Idaho.

=Section 1. District judgeship for the district of Idaho=



==(a) In general– ==

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall appoint, by and with the advice and consent of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] , 1 additional district judge for the district of Idaho.

==(b) Technical and conforming amendment– ==

In order that the table contained in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/133 section 133(a)of title 28, United States Code] , will reflect the change in the number of judgeships authorized by subsection (a), such table is amended by striking the item relating to Idaho and inserting the following:

<blockquote>Idaho3.
</blockquote>
