{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act to repeal the requirement to establish catch limits for the Gulf of Mexico red snapper fishery.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Supporting New Avenues to Protect Our Fishermen’s Resources Act   ”or the“ SNAPR Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal of catch limits requirement for Gulf of Mexico red snapper fishery=

   Subsection (d) of section 407 of the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1884 16 U.S.C. 1884])is repealed.
