={{anchor|H320D524CF5B24901BC6CEB6110C0D69B}}Section 1. Disperse Red 177=



=={{anchor|HF0C6DFA42D3F400487649FB4D7278D6A}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized Tariff
			 Schedule of the United States is amended by inserting in numerical sequence the
			 following new heading:

<blockquote>9902.01.00Disperse Red 177 (CAS No. 68133–69–7) (provided for in
						subheading 3204.11.50)FreeNo changeNo changeOn or before 12/31/2016.
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HAB3AA1E363414314AA1A32A57003F7C6}}(b) Effective
			 date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) applies to articles
			 entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day
			 after the date of the enactment of this Act.
