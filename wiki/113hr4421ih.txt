 To extend the authorization for the Automobile National Heritage Area in Michigan.   

=Section 1. Automobile National Heritage Area Authorization extended=



==(a) Short title– ==

This Act may be cited as the“   MotorCities National Heritage Area Extension Act   ”.

==(b) Authorization– ==

   Section 109 of the Automobile National Heritage Area Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/461  16 U.S.C. 461note]; [[Public Law 105-355|Public Law 105–355]]  )is amended by striking“September 30, 2014”and inserting“September 30, 2030”.
