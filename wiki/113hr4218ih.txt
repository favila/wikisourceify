=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Yuma Crossing National Heritage Area Reauthorization Act   ”.

   Section 7 of the Yuma Crossing National Heritage Area Act of 2000 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/461  16 U.S.C. 461note];[[Public Law 106-319|Public Law 106–319]] ;114 Stat. 1284)is amended by striking“2015”and inserting“2030”.
