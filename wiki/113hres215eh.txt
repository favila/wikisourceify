{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 Providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 45) to repeal the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act and health care-related provisions in the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010.   

= =

That upon the adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to consider in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] the bill ( H.R. 45 ) to repeal the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act and health care-related provisions in the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010 . All points of order against consideration of the bill are waived. The amendment printed in the report of the [[w:United States House Committee on Rules|Committee on Rules]] accompanying this resolution shall be considered as adopted. The bill, as amended, shall be considered as read. All points of order against provisions in the bill, as amended, are waived. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the bill, as amended, and on any amendment thereto to final passage without intervening motion except: (1) two hours of debate equally divided among and controlled by the respective chairs and ranking minority members of the [[w:United States House Committee on Education and the Workforce|Committees on Education and the Workforce]] , [[w:United States House Committee on Energy and Commerce|Energy and Commerce]] , and [[w:United States House Committee on Ways and Means|Ways and Means]] ; and (2) one motion to recommit with or without instructions.    Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
