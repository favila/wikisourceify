{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the expiration of certain provisions of the USA PATRIOT Act and the FISA Amendments Act of 2008 on December 31, 2014.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “National Security Agency Data Collection Review Act of 2014” .

=Sec. 2. Sunset of certain provisions of the USA PATRIOT Act and the FISA Amendments Act of 2008=



==(a) USA PATRIOT Act– ==

 Section 102(b)(1) of the USA PATRIOT Improvement and Reauthorization Act of 2005([[Public Law 109-177|Public Law 109–177]] ; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/50/1805 50 U.S.C. 1805note] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/50/1861 50 U.S.C. 1861note] , and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/50/1862 50 U.S.C. 1862note] )is amended by striking “June 1, 2015” and inserting “December 31, 2014” .

==(b) FISA Amendments Act of 2008– ==



===(1) Sunset– ===

 Section 403(b) of the FISA Amendments Act of 2008([[Public Law 110-261|Public Law 110–261]]  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/50/1801 ;50 U.S.C. 1801note] )is amended—
:(A) in paragraph (1), by striking “December 31, 2017” and inserting “December 31, 2014” ; and
:(B) in paragraph (2)in the material preceding subparagraph (A), by striking “December 31, 2017” and inserting “December 31, 2014” .

===(2) Conforming Amendment– ===

The heading of section 404(b)(1) of the FISA Amendments Act of 2008([[Public Law 110-261|Public Law 110–261]] ; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/50/1801 50 U.S.C. 1801note] )is amended by striking “December 31, 2017” and inserting “December 31, 2014” .
