{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permanently extend the 15-year recovery period for qualified leasehold improvement property, qualified restaurant property, and qualified retail improvement property.

={{anchor|H7B32D81E76BF4624B87114B57D8704A4}}Section 1. Permanent extension of treatment of qualified leasehold improvement property as 15-year property for purposes of depreciation deduction=



=={{anchor|HFC6A26B838964F7A99F9A2801EFCEC71}}(a) In general– ==

 Clause (iv) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 168(e)(3)(E)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended by section 311(a) of the American Taxpayer Relief Act of 2012([[Public Law 112-240|Public Law 112–240]] ), is amended by striking“placed in service before January 1, 2014”.

=={{anchor|H0817314E959748798DE023E7B52B4D26}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2012.

={{anchor|H4533BC8393C4469FAEA79C67053B5D73}}Sec. 2. Permanent extension of treatment of qualified restaurant property as 15-year property for purposes of depreciation deduction=



=={{anchor|H375D249A7D334285AE7FB0A85BC52CE5}}(a) In general– ==

 Clause (v) of section 168(e)(3)(E) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended by section 311(a) of the American Taxpayer Relief Act of 2012([[Public Law -|[[Public Law 112-240|Public Law 112–240]] ]] ), is amended by striking“placed in service before January 1, 2014”.

=={{anchor|H9703C16F83524F4EA5CD6C00FB8AF1A6}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2012.

={{anchor|HBE7AC7246FDA409EBAB5CB5956734C36}}Sec. 3. Permanent extension of treatment of qualified retail improvement property as 15-year property for purposes of depreciation deduction=



=={{anchor|H3FDED6B2574E41CC9160E642DAC3FBE3}}(a) In general– ==

 Clause (ix) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 168(e)(3)(E)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended by section 311(a) of the American Taxpayer Relief Act of 2012([[Public Law 112-240|Public Law 112–240]] ), is amended by striking“, and before January 1, 2014”.

=={{anchor|HE45B016C51C3449EAD0A11674EFA8843}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2012.
