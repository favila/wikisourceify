{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Adam Walsh Child Protection and Safety Act of 2006 to expand the authority of governmental social service agencies with child protection responsibilities to access the national crime information databases.   

=Section 1. Expansion of authority of governmental social service agencies with child protection responsibilities to access the national crime information databases=

   Section 151(a)(2) of the Adam Walsh Child Protection and Safety Act of 2006 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/16961 42 U.S.C. 16961(a)(2)] )is amended by striking“, to be used by such agencies only in investigating or responding to reports of child abuse, neglect, or exploitation”.
