{{Center|A BILL}}

To remove the authority of the Agricultural Marketing Service to inspect apples.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Apple Red Tape Elimination Act of 2013”.

=Sec. 2. Removal of AMS inspection authority over apples=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary of Agriculture]] , acting through the [[w:|Agricultural Marketing Service]] , shall have no authority to inspect apples prior to export.
