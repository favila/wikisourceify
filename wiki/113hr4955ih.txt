{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States to extend the tariff preference level on imports of certain cotton and man-made fiber, fabric, apparel, and made-up goods from Bahrain under the United States-Bahrain Free Trade Agreement.   

=Section 1. Extension of tariff preference level on imports of certain cotton and man-made fiber, fabric, apparel, and made-up goods from Bahrain under the United States-Bahrain Free Trade Agreement=



==(a) In general– ==

U.S. Note 13 to subchapter XIV of chapter 99 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States is amended—
:(1) in the matter preceding paragraph (a), by striking“2015”and inserting“2025”; and
:(2) in the matter following paragraph (d), by striking“December 31, 2015”and inserting“December 31, 2025”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to articles entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after January 1, 2016.
