=Section 1. Interest rate extension=

 Subparagraph (D) of section 455(b)(7) of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e(b)(7)] ] )is amended—
:(1) in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking “2013” and inserting “2015” ; and
:(2) in clause (v), by striking “2013” and inserting “2015” .
