{{Center|113th CONGRESS}}

 
{{Center|2d Session}}

 
{{Center|H. R. 4193}}

 
{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}

 
{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To amend title 5, United States Code, to change the default investment fund under the Thrift Savings Plan, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Smart Savings Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Thrift Savings Plan default investment fund=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8438  Section 8438(c)(2)of title 5, United States Code], is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 
:(2) 
::(A) Consistent with the requirements of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8438 subparagraph (B)], if an election has not been made with respect to any sums available for investment in the Thrift Savings Fund, the [[w:Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board|Executive Director]]shall invest such sums in an age-appropriate target date asset allocation investment fund, as determined by the [[w:Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board|Executive Director]]. Such investment fund shall consist of any of the funds described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8438 subsection (b)].
::(B) If an election has not been made by an eligible member under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8440e section 8440e]with respect to any sums available for investment in such member’s Thrift Savings Fund account, the [[w:Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board|Executive Director]]shall invest such sums in the Government Securities Investment Fund. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Acknowledgment of risk– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8439  Section 8439(d)of title 5, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8439 (1)] ”before“Each employee”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
::(2) Prior to enrollment in the Thrift Savings Fund, or as soon as practicable thereafter, an individual who is automatically enrolled pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8432 section 8432(b)(2)]shall receive the risk acknowledgment information described under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8439 paragraph (1)]. . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Technical and conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8472  Section 8472(g)(2)of title 5, United States Code], is amended by striking“required by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8438 section 8438 of this title]to be invested in securities of the Government”and inserting“under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8438 section 8438(c)(2)(B)] ”.

==(d) Guidance– ==

Not later than 9 months after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board|Executive Director]](as that term is defined under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401  section 8401(13)of title 5, United States Code]) shall develop and issue guidance implementing the requirements of this Act.

==(e) Effective date and application– ==

The amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall—
:(1) take effect on the date that the [[w:Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board|Executive Director]]issues guidance under subsection (d) ; and
:(2) apply to individuals enrolled in the Thrift Savings Plan on or after such date. 

=Sec. 3. Clarification of fiduciary protections=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8477  Section 8477(e)(1)(C)(ii)of title 5, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8477 subclause (II)]—
::(A) by inserting“or beneficiary”after“participant”; and
::(B) by inserting“or option”after“fund”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8477 subclause (III)]—
::(A) by inserting“or beneficiary”after“participant”; and
::(B) by inserting“or beneficiaries’”after“participants’”.    Passed the House of Representatives July 14, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
