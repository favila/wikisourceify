{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Help America Vote Act of 2002 to eliminate
		  straight-party voting from any voting system used for Federal
		  elections.

={{anchor|H0C0C94A9741C4BED898E4E60A244FA20}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “People Before Party Act of
			 2013”.

={{anchor|H9C173737C4ED471D8175928B01941180}}Sec. 2. Elimination of
			 straight-party voting in Federal elections=

Section 301(a)(6) of the Help America Vote
			 Act of 2002 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/15481 42 U.S.C. 15481(a)(6)]) is amended by striking the period at the
			 end of the sentence and inserting “, except that no State may provide a
			 voter with the opportunity to indicate the selection of a political party as a
			 representation of the selection of an individual candidate.”.
