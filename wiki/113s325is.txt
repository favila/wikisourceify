{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to increase the maximum age for children eligible for medical care under the CHAMPVA program, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Increase of maximum age for children eligible for medical care under CHAMPVA program=



==(a) Increase– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1781 Subsection (c) of section 1781 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>
:(c) 
::(1) Notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 clauses (i)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 (iii) of section 101(4)(A) of this title] and except as provided in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1781 paragraph (2)] , for purposes of this section, a child who is eligible for benefits under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1781 subsection (a)] shall remain eligible for benefits under this section until the child's 26th birthday, regardless of the child's marital status.
::(2) Before January 1, 2014, [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1781 paragraph (1)] shall not apply to a child who is eligible to enroll in an eligible employer-sponsored plan (as defined in section 5000A(f)(2) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986).
::(3) This subsection shall not be construed to limit eligibility for coverage of a child described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 section 101(4)(A)(ii) of this title] ..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

Such subsection, as so amended, shall apply with respect to medical care provided on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
