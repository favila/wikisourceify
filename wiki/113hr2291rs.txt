{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 450 Lexington Avenue in New York, New York, as the“Vincent R. Sombrotto Post Office”.   

=Section 1. Vincent R. Sombrotto Post Office=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]located at 450 Lexington Avenue in New York, New York, shall be known and designated as the“Vincent R. Sombrotto Post Office”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to insubsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Vincent R. Sombrotto Post Office”.
