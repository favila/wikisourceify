{{Center|113th CONGRESS}}

 
{{Center|2d Session}}

 
{{Center|H. R. 801}}

 
{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}

 
{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To amend the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 to make the shareholder threshold for registration of savings and loan holding companies the same as for bank holding companies.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Holding Company Registration Threshold Equalization Act of 2014 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Registration threshold for savings and loan holding companies=

The  Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/78a 15 U.S.C. 78a et seq].)is amended—
:(1) in section 12(g) —
::(A) in paragraph (1)(B) , by inserting after“is a bank”the following:“, a savings and loan holding company (as defined in section 10 of the Home Owners' Loan Act ),”; and
::(B) in paragraph (4) , by inserting after“case of a bank”the following:“, a savings and loan holding company (as defined in section 10 of the Home Owners' Loan Act ),”; and
:(2) in section 15(d) , by striking“case of bank”and inserting the following:“case of a bank, a savings and loan holding company (as defined in section 10 of the Home Owners' Loan Act ),”.    Passed the House of Representatives January 14, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
