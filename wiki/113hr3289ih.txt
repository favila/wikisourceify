{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide funds during the lapse of appropriations for the payment of military death gratuities and funeral and related transportation and housing expenses through the transfer of unobligated amounts in the Health Insurance Reform Implementation Fund.

=Section 1. Source of funds for payment of military death gratuities and funeral and related transportation and housing expenses=

During any period for which interim or full-year appropriations for fiscal year 2014 are not in effect for a purpose specified in this section, the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary of Health and Human Services]] shall transfer, from unobligated amounts in the Health Insurance Reform Implementation Fund established by section 1005 of the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010([[Public Law 111-152|Public Law 111–152]] ), to the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] sufficient funds to enable the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] to provide the following benefits on behalf of members of the Armed Forces who die during such period:
:(1) The payment of a death gratuity under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1475..1477 sections1475–1477] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1489 1489of title 10, United States Code] .
:(2) The payment or reimbursement for funeral and burial expenses authorized under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1481 sections1481] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1482 1482of title 10, United States Code] .
:(3) The payment or reimbursement of authorized funeral travel and travel related to the dignified transfer of remains and unit memorial services under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/481f section 481fof title 37, United States Code] .
:(4) The temporary continuation of a basic allowance of housing for dependents of members dying on active duty, as authorized by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/403 section 403(l)of title 37, United States Code] .
