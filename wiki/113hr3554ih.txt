{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Public Health Service Act to designate certain medical facilities of the Department of Veterans Affairs as health professional shortage areas, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Veterans Access to Care Act” .

=Sec. 2. Designation of medical facilities of the Department of Veterans Affairs as health professional shortage areas=



==(a) HPSA– ==

 Section 332(a)(1) of the Public Health Service Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254e 42 U.S.C. 254e(a)(1)] )is amended in the second sentence by inserting “and medical facilities of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] (including State homes, as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 section 101(19)of title 38, United States Code] )” after “( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395x 42 U.S.C. 1395x(aa)] ),” .

==(b) Concurrent benefits– ==



===(1) Scholarship program– ===

 Section 338A(b) of the Public Health Service Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254l 42 U.S.C. 254l(b)] )is amended—
:(A) in paragraph (3), by striking “and” ;
:(B) in paragraph (4), by striking the period and inserting “; and” ; and
:(C) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(5) not be participating in the Department of Veterans Affairs Health Professionals Educational Assistance Program underchapter 76of title 38, United States Code..
</blockquote>


===(2) Debt reduction program– ===

 Section 338B(b) of the Public Health Service Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254l–1 42 U.S.C. 254l–1(b)] )is amended—
:(A) in paragraph (2), by striking “and” ;
:(B) in paragraph (3), by striking the period and inserting “; and” ; and
:(C) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(4) not be participating in the Department of Veterans Affairs Health Professionals Educational Assistance Program underchapter 76of title 38, United States Code..
</blockquote>


==(c) Consultation– ==

In carrying out the National Health Service Corps Program under subpart II of part D of title III of the Public Health Service Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254d 42 U.S.C. 254d et seq.] ), the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary of Health and Human Services]] shall consult with the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] with respect to health professional shortage areas that are medical facilities of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] (including State homes, as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 section 101(19)of title 38, United States Code] ).

==(d) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date that is 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
