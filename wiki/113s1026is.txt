{{Center|A BILL}}

To assist survivors of stroke in returning to work.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Return to Work Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Assisting stroke survivors in returning to work=

The [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]] , acting through the [[w:Office of Disability Employment Policy|Job Accommodation Network]] , may promote awareness and assistance among employers to enable survivors of stroke to return to work.
