{{Center|A BILL}}

To condition the provision of premium and cost-sharing subsidies under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act upon a certification that a program to verify household income and other qualifications for such subsidies is operational, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Conditioning provision of ACA premium and cost-sharing subsidies upon certification that a program to verify household income and other qualifications for those subsidies is operational=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no premium tax credits shall be allowed under  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/36B section 36B] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986and no reductions in cost-sharing shall be allowed under section 1402 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18071 42 U.S.C. 18071] )before the date that the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary of Health and Human Services]] certifies to the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] that there is in place a program that verifies, consistent with section 1411 of such Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18081 42 U.S.C. 18081] ), the household income and coverage requirements of individuals applying for such credits and cost-sharing reduction.
