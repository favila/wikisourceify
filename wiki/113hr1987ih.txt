{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to increase the amount of benefits payable for the burial and funeral expenses of certain veterans.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Veterans’ Dignity and Honor Act” .

=Sec. 2. Increase in amount of burial and funeral benefits for veterans=



==(a) Funeral expenses– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2302 Section 2302(a) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “$300” and inserting “$1918” .

==(b) Plot allowance– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2303 Section 2303(b) of such title] is amended by striking “$700” both places it appears and inserting “$1150” .

==(c) Death from service-Connected disability– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2307 Section 2307 of such title] is amended by striking “$2,000” and inserting “$6160” .

==(d) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to deaths occurring after the date of the enactment of this Act.
