{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide that rates of pay for Members of Congress
		  shall not be adjusted under section 601(a)(2) of the Legislative Reorganization
		  Act of 1946 in the year following any fiscal year in which outlays of the
		  United States exceeded receipts of the United States.

={{anchor|HEF6D33C95754403CA86B6892B8E16C43}}Section 1. Amendments to the Legislative
			 Reorganization Act of 1946=



=={{anchor|H7E6E2D92B79547EF95A48DF3B3339B3A}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Section 601(a)(2) of
			 the Legislative Reorganization Act of 1946 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/31 2 U.S.C. 31(2)]) is amended by
			 adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(C) An adjustment in rates of pay may be made
				under this paragraph in a year only if the aggregate outlays of the United
				States during the last completed fiscal year did not exceed the aggregate
				receipts of the United States during such fiscal year, as determined by the
				[[w:Congressional Budget Office|Congressional Budget
				Office]]..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H4D35479EFC044097AED21E7D4B855A71}}(b) Technical
			 amendment– ==

Section 601(a)(2)(A) of such Act is amended by striking
			 “Subject to subparagraph (B),” and inserting “Subject to
			 subparagraphs (B) and (C),”.
