{{Center|A BILL}}

To reauthorize the Multinational Species Conservation Funds Semipostal Stamp, and for other purposes.
1. Short Title


This Act may be cited as the “Multinational Species Conservation Funds Semipostal Stamp Reauthorization Act”.

2. Reauthorization


Section 2(c) of the Multinational Species Conservation Funds Semipostal Stamp Act of 2010 ( Public Law 111–241 ; 39 U.S.C. 416 note ) is amended—
: in paragraph (2) , by striking “2 years ”and inserting “6 years ”; and: by adding at the end the following:

==(5) Stamp depictions==

Members of the public shall be offered a choice of 5 stamps under this Act, depicting an African elephant or an Asian elephant, a rhinoceros, a tiger, a marine turtle, and a great ape, respectively..
