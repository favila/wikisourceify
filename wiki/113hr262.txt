{{Center|A BILL}}

To reauthorize the Multinational Species Conservation
		  Funds Semipostal Stamp, and for other purposes.


{{section|HF63CB42333AF42EFB4EFA7F4022E70E0|1. Short Title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Multinational Species Conservation
			 Funds Semipostal Stamp Reauthorization Act

        
.

{{section|HE37B12001A7547BAA71B0C66DEC4910C|2. Reauthorization}}

        Section 2(c) of the Multinational Species
			 Conservation Funds Semipostal Stamp Act of 2010
 (Public Law 111–241
; 39 U.S.C.
			 416 note
)
 is amended—
(1)
in paragraph (2)
,
			 by striking 2 years
 and inserting 6 years
;
			 and

(2)
by adding at the
			 end the following:
(5)
Stamp
				depictions
Members of the
				public shall be offered a choice of 5 stamps under this Act, depicting an
				African elephant or an Asian elephant, a rhinoceros, a tiger, a marine turtle,
				and a great ape,
				respectively.

.
