{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution from charging a fee for admission to any exhibit which is part of the permanent collection of any museum or facility which is part of any bureau established in or under the Smithsonian Institution, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Prohibiting Charging of Fees for Admission to Permanent Collections of Smithsonian Museums or Facilities=



==(a) Prohibition– ==

Except as provided in subsections (a)and (b), the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution]] may not charge a fee for admission to any exhibit which is part of the permanent collection of any museum or facility which is part of any bureau established in or under the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Smithsonian Institution]] , including the National Gallery of Art.

==(b) Exception for necessary and unavoidable fees– ==

The [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution]] may charge a fee for admission to an exhibit described in subsection (a)if—
:(1) the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Secretary]] determines that charging a fee for admission to the exhibit is necessary and unavoidable;
:(2) the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Secretary]] submits a statement explaining the determination to the [[w:United States House Committee on House Administration|Committee on House Administration of the House of Representatives]] , the [[w:United States House Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure|Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives]] , the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Environment and Public Works|Committee on Environment and Public Works of the Senate]] , and the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Rules and Administration|Committee on Rules and Administration of the Senate]] ; and
:(3) the charging of the fee does not take effect until after the expiration of the 45-day period which begins on the date the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Secretary]] submits the statement under paragraph (1).

==(c) Exception for Certain Current Exhibit– ==

This section does not apply to the exhibit entitled “Butterfly Pavilion” at the National Museum of Natural History.

=Sec. 2. Plan for Elimination of Fee for Current Exhibit=

Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution]] shall submit to the [[w:United States House Committee on House Administration|Committee on House Administration of the House of Representatives]] , the [[w:United States House Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure|Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives]] , the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Environment and Public Works|Committee on Environment and Public Works of the Senate]] , and the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Rules and Administration|Committee on Rules and Administration of the Senate]] a plan for eliminating the admission fee for the exhibit entitled “Butterfly Pavilion” at the National Museum of Natural History, and shall include in the plan such steps as the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Secretary]] considers appropriate to cover the expenses incurred in carrying out the exhibit.
