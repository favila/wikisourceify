{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Communications Act of 1934 to promote the expansion of spectrum-based services to exceptionally hard-to-serve populations in unserved and underserved geographic locations.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Promoting Rural Broadband Act of 2014” .

=Sec. 2. Promotion of spectrum-based services in unserved and underserved locations=

 Section 309(j)(4) of the Communications Act of 1934( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/47/309 47 U.S.C. 309(j)(4)] )is amended—
:(1) in subparagraph (E), by striking “and” at the end;
:(2) in subparagraph (F), by striking the period at the end and inserting “; and” ; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(G) promote the expansion of spectrum-based services to exceptionally hard-to-serve populations in unserved and underserved geographic locations..
</blockquote>
