To amend title 10, United States Code, to direct the Secretary of Defense to provide support for Boy Scout Jamborees.

=Section 1. Department of Defense support for Boy Scout Jamborees=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2554 Section 2554of title 10, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2554 subsection (a)] —
::(A) by striking “is hereby authorized” and inserting “shall” ;
::(B) by striking “to lend to” and inserting “lend to” ; and
::(C) by striking “to the extent that such items are in stock and items or services are available” ;
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2554 subsection (d)] —
::(A) by striking “is hereby authorized” and inserting “shall” ;
::(B) by striking “to provide” and inserting “provide” ; and
::(C) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2554 paragraph (2)] , by striking “the requirements of military operations” and inserting “the national security of the United States” ; and
:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2554 subsection (g)] , by striking “may provide” and inserting “shall provide” .
