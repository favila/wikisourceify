{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require the President to designate a legal public holiday to be known as National First Responders Day.   

=Section 1. Designation of National First Responders Day=

Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall issue a proclamation that designates a date as a legal public holiday to be known as“National First Responders Day”, which shall be treated in the same manner as a legal public holiday described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/6103 section 6103(a) of title 5, United States Code].
