{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 to require oil polluters to pay the full cost of oil spills, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Big Oil Bailout Prevention Unlimited Liability Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|id0081926B52484EFFB28B2F4348FB8FFB}}Sec. 2. Removal of limits on liability for offshore facilities=

 Section 1004(a)(3) of the Oil Pollution Act of 1990([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/33/2704 33 U.S.C. 2704(a)(3)] ] )is amended by striking“plus $75,000,000”and inserting“and the liability of the responsible party under section 1002”.
