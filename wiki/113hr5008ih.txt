{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit United States voluntary contributions to the United Nations Democracy Fund.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on United States voluntary contributions to the United Nations Democracy Fund=



==(a) In general– ==

No funds made available to any Federal department or agency may be used to make United States voluntary contributions to the United Nations Democracy Fund (UNDEF).

==(b) Effective date– ==

 Subsection (a)applies with respect to funds made available for fiscal year 2015 and subsequent fiscal years.
