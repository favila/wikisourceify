{{Center|A BILL}}

To extend the hold harmless provisions of the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program pending reauthorization of the overall program.

=Section 1. Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program technical amendments=

For any fiscal year after fiscal year 2013 for which appropriations are made to carry out title XXVI of the Public Health Service Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300ff–11 42 U.S.C. 300ff–11 et seq.] )but for which the authorizations of appropriations to carry out such title have not been extended—
:(1)  sections 2603(a)(4)(C)and 2618(a)(2)(H)(v) of such Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300ff–13 42 U.S.C. 300ff–13(a)(4)(C)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300ff–28 300ff–28(a)(2)(H)(v)] ) shall not apply; and
:(2)  sections 2603(a)(4)(A), 2610(c)(2)(B)(ii), and 2618(a)(2)(H)(iii) of such Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300ff–13 42 U.S.C. 300ff–13(a)(4)(A)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300ff–20 300ff–20(c)(2)(B)(ii)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300ff–28 300ff–28(a)(2)(H)(iii)] ) shall apply in the same manner as such respective sections apply for fiscal year 2013.
