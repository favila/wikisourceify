{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the authority of the Internal Revenue Service to require truncated social security numbers on Form W–2 wage and tax statements.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Taxpayer Identity Protection Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of IRS authority to require truncated Social Security numbers on Form W–2=



==(a) In general– ==

  Paragraph (2) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/6051 section 6051(a)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“his social security account number”and inserting“an identifying number for the employee”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
