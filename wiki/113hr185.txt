{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the United States courthouse located at 101
		  East Pecan Street in Sherman, Texas, as the Paul Brown United States
		  Courthouse.

={{anchor|H3E76C98BE4F646BA86D1E14567C97CF5}}Section 1. Designation=

The United States courthouse located at 101
			 East Pecan Street in Sherman, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
			 Paul Brown United States Courthouse.

={{anchor|H042A31AFD464413CA9FA508752E1DFF9}}Sec. 2. References=

Any reference in a law, map, regulation,
			 document, paper, or other record of the United States to the United States
			 courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
			 Paul Brown United States Courthouse.
