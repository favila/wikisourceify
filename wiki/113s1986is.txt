{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Older Americans Act of 1965 to provide for outreach, and coordination of services, to veterans.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Streamlining Services for Older Veterans Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Outreach and coordination of services to veterans=



==(a) Definition– ==

   Section 102 of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3002 42 U.S.C. 3002] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (24) —
::(A) in subparagraph (B) , by striking“and”at the end;
::(B) in subparagraph (C) , by striking the period and inserting“; and”; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:::(D) status as a veteran. ; and 
</blockquote>

:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
::(55) The term veteran has the meaning given the term in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101  section 101of title 38, United States Code]. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Area plan– ==

   Section 306(a) of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3026 42 U.S.C. 3026(a)])is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (16) , by striking“and”at the end;
:(2) in paragraph (17) , by striking the period and inserting“; and”; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
::(18) include information describing—
:::(A) how the area agency on aging will engage in outreach to veterans who are eligible for services under this Act; and
:::(B) effective and efficient procedures for the coordination of services provided under this Act with services provided to veterans by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] and other providers. . 
</blockquote>


==(c) State plan– ==

   Section 307(a) of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3027 42 U.S.C. 3027(a)])is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:(31) The plan shall include information describing—
::(A) how the State agency will engage in outreach to veterans who are eligible for services under this Act; and
::(B) effective and efficient procedures for the coordination of services provided under this Act with services provided to veterans by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] and other providers. . 
</blockquote>
