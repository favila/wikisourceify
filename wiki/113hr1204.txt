{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 49, United States Code, to direct the
		  Assistant Secretary of Homeland Security (Transportation Security
		  Administration) to establish an Aviation Security Advisory Committee, and for
		  other purposes.

={{anchor|HAFEDF18408644676B0326705E2A1589A}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Aviation Security Stakeholder
			 Participation Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H0EC3F51906914B279917405CE24C7DAE}}Sec. 2. Aviation Security
			 Advisory Committee=



=={{anchor|H03B6F8AE09A045F7AB18D5A5576B2E14}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Subchapter II of
			 chapter 449 of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end
			 the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H09D22324F8B14B9F854177C253ACC377}}Sec. 44946. Aviation
				Security Advisory Committee==



==={{anchor|H9FF5BCE2A8C34798AD9C0BA9F51EC5D0}}(a) Establishment– ===

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]] shall establish
				within the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Transportation Security Administration]] an advisory committee to be
				known as the “[[w:Transportation Security Administration|Aviation Security Advisory Committee]]”.

==={{anchor|H74F19B8DAA9A462B9E262EDEB834D65B}}(b) Duties– ===



===={{anchor|HB9164A2AE2324DA9A4F4044F5A0EF8D2}}(1) In
				general– ====

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant
				Secretary]] shall consult the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] on aviation security matters,
				including on the development and implementation of policies, programs,
				rulemaking, and security directives pertaining to aviation security, while
				adhering to sensitive security guidelines.

===={{anchor|H717341856C674F2FA2D10AAF77077D0B}}(2) Recommendations– ====



====={{anchor|H220DD9A2D8ED4CE2BEF7A35E5B673643}}(A) In
				general– =====

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] shall develop, at the request of
				the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]], recommendations for improvements to aviation
				security.

====={{anchor|HC0915810046A44E5A71F1FEB38B289DE}}(B) Recommendations
				of subcommittees– =====

Recommendations agreed upon by the subcommittees
				established under this section shall be approved by the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] for
				transmission to the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]].

===={{anchor|H05C261992202413C9D92FAD8B1578BCC}}(3) Periodic
				reports– ====

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] shall periodically submit to the
				[[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]]—
:(A) reports on matters
				identified by the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]]; and
:(B) reports on other
				matters identified by a majority of the members of the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory
				Committee]].

===={{anchor|H3EA7D6FC34114C80B229CE4FED21252A}}(4) Annual
				report– ====

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]]
				shall submit to the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]] an annual report providing information
				on the activities, findings, and recommendations of the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]],
				including its subcommittees, for the preceding year.

==={{anchor|H4E4CCF67CA8947A7B6F612B9995784AC}}(c) Membership– ===



===={{anchor|H98327D05DCB94B49A759136A82B37AD1}}(1) Appointment– ====



====={{anchor|HED474BABA44347C2BF6BB7A061FC255D}}(A) In
				general– =====

Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of
				this section, the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]] shall appoint the members of the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory
				Committee]].

====={{anchor|H92B00BD34DE74891B892B88897BF37A9}}(B) Composition– =====

The
				membership of the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] shall consist of individuals representing
				not more than 30 member organizations. Each organization shall be represented
				by one individual (or the individual’s designee).

====={{anchor|H4D5E570C22A040A18CE3A19CA708D119}}(C) Representation– =====

The
				membership of the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] shall include representatives of air
				carriers, all cargo air transportation, indirect air carriers, labor
				organizations representing air carrier employees, aircraft manufacturers,
				airport operators, general aviation, privacy organizations, the travel
				industry, airport based businesses, including minority owned small businesses,
				passenger advocacy groups, and the aviation technology security industry,
				including biometrics.

===={{anchor|HDDED5834EA0E41D790BD3B5C7CC72D95}}(2) Removal– ====

The
				[[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]] may review the participation of a member of the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory
				Committee]] and remove such member for cause at any time.

===={{anchor|H39C206DF16524F52A8454EC7324E142F}}(3) Prohibition on
				compensation– ====

The members of the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] shall not
				receive pay, allowances, or benefits from the Government by reason of their
				service on the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]].

===={{anchor|H23BC1E7E09DA4E2EA65A46BC88C50D73}}(4) Meetings– ====

The
				[[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]] shall require the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] to meet at least
				semiannually and may convene additional meetings as necessary.

==={{anchor|H8B8A4DBF53A9444793897C3FDC696AE4}}(d) Air cargo
				security subcommittee– ===



===={{anchor|HCDA39F48038F40228B74E7416C1A3E07}}(1) In
				general– ====

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant
				Secretary]] shall establish within the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] an air cargo security
				subcommittee to provide recommendations on air cargo security issues, including
				the implementation of the air cargo security programs established by the
				[[w:Transportation Security Administration|Transportation Security Administration]] to screen air cargo on passenger
				aircraft and all-cargo aircraft in accordance with established cargo screening
				mandates.

===={{anchor|HFD91B6C64AB24844AEFDEA3A82FCCC85}}(2) Meetings and
				reporting– ====

The subcommittee
				shall meet at least quarterly and submit to the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] for
				inclusion in the annual report required under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44946 subsection (b)(4)] information,
				including recommendations, regarding air cargo security. Such submissions shall
				include recommendations to improve the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Transportation Security Administration’s]]
				cargo security initiatives established to meet the requirements of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44901 section
				44901(g)].

===={{anchor|H913C03381C1940F9A4C620EE7C881A33}}(3) Membership– ====

The subcommittee shall—
:(A) include members of
				the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] with expertise in air cargo operations; and
:(B) be cochaired by a
				Government and industry official.

==={{anchor|H5778750BCDB74ECE81B5737F4ECA822A}}(e) General aviation
				security subcommittee– ===



===={{anchor|HA1C75AC8948E478093DD2736FF8E8AF4}}(1) In
				general– ====

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant
				Secretary]] shall establish within the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] a general aviation
				subcommittee to provide recommendations on transportation security issues for
				general aviation facilities, general aviation aircraft, and helicopter
				operations at general aviation and commercial service airports.

===={{anchor|H64CA66BB5EE84FEE8DF201A063066FCB}}(2) Meetings and
				reporting– ====

The subcommittee
				shall meet at least quarterly and submit to the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] for
				inclusion in the annual report required under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44946 subsection (b)(4)] information,
				including recommendations, regarding aviation security at general aviation
				airports.

===={{anchor|H16AD6322E23F4612B6312AACBCED7CA3}}(3) Membership– ====

The subcommittee shall—
:(A) include members of
				the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] with expertise in general aviation; and
:(B) be cochaired by a
				Government and industry official.

==={{anchor|HB010B13D940C45789FF26B5D5019AAA4}}(f) Perimeter
				security subcommittee– ===



===={{anchor|H2A9DF3E99AB243489587997109071FB4}}(1) In
				general– ====

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant
				Secretary]] shall establish within the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] an airport perimeter
				security subcommittee to provide recommendations on airport perimeter security
				and access control issues.

===={{anchor|HA9BF9845D70F480B92171B7890C65652}}(2) Meetings and
				reporting– ====

The subcommittee
				shall meet at least quarterly and submit to the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] for
				inclusion in the annual report required under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44946 subsection (b)(4)] information,
				including recommendations, regarding improving perimeter security and access
				control procedures at commercial service and general aviation airports.

===={{anchor|H6F8CE27142F34BDA8CA3B309550F1C93}}(3) Membership– ====

The subcommittee shall—
:(A) include members of
				the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] with expertise in airport perimeter security and access
				control issues; and
:(B) be cochaired by a
				Government and industry official.

==={{anchor|H84B64233E5BF4218847C4D6CFFF166AE}}(g) Risk-Based
				subcommittee– ===



===={{anchor|H2E15477D072A47AAA8DD3B40F589F33D}}(1) In
				general– ====

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant
				Secretary]] shall establish within the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] a risk-based
				subcommittee to provide recommendations on passenger screening policies and
				cargo.

===={{anchor|H471B29618888401C8F1092B767C8D000}}(2) Meetings and
				reporting– ====

The subcommittee shall meet at least quarterly and
				submit to the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] for inclusion in the annual report required
				under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44946 subsection (b)(4)] information, including recommendations, regarding the
				[[w:Transportation Security Administration|Transportation Security Administration’s]] risk-based security programs.

===={{anchor|H0E400A1E90924AF784822CD1F181465D}}(3) Membership– ====

The
				subcommittee shall—
:(A) include members of
				the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] with expertise in passenger advocacy and airport
				security operations; and
:(B) be cochaired by a
				Government and industry official.

==={{anchor|H09FABEDB25384222AD61CA7C1BEC731E}}(h) Other
				subcommittees– ===

The [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]], in consultation with the
				[[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]], may establish within the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] any other
				subcommittee that the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Assistant Secretary]] and [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Advisory Committee]] determine
				necessary.

==={{anchor|HE75585F702564591A021FA32343CEE0D}}(i) Nonapplicability
				of FACA– ===

The Federal Advisory
				Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.) shall not apply to the Advisory Committee and its
				subcommittees.

==={{anchor|H598A6E0290C54E1DAEE66D3865CB955E}}(j) Definitions– ===

In
				this section, the following definitions apply:

===={{anchor|H84B5CE576B9A4CE28942B307B6E53848}}(1) Advisory
				Committee– ====

The term
				“Advisory Committee” means the Aviation Security Advisory
				Committee established under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44901 subsection (a)].

===={{anchor|H3622ED6750AB4C4E9883B89FDACF8819}}(2) Assistant
				Secretary– ====

The term
				“Assistant Secretary” means the Assistant Secretary of Homeland
				Security (Transportation Security Administration).

===={{anchor|H1FF5A5BEC2004014A0AA8DCA9D082CE1}}(3) Perimeter
				security– ====

The term “perimeter security”—
:(A) means procedures or systems to monitor,
				secure, and prevent unauthorized access to an airport, including its airfield
				and terminal; and
:(B) includes the fence area surrounding an
				airport, access gates, and access
				controls..
</blockquote>
