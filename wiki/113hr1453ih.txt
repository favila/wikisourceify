{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend the authority to provide work-study allowance for certain activities by individuals receiving educational assistance by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Work-Study for Student Veterans Act” .

=Sec. 2. Work-study allowance=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3485 Section 3485(a)(4) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “June 30, 2013” each place it appears and inserting “June 30, 2018” .
