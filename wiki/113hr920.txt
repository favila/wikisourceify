{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Public Health Service Act to provide for the participation of optometrists in the National Health Service Corps scholarship and loan repayment programs, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HE172BCEB13584A10B2728B071CA7AB04}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“National Health Service Corps Improvement Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H11FA1AA8C4B74A4EA628D7347F5D11CD}}Sec. 2. Participation of optometrists in National Health Service Corps=



=={{anchor|HBC2191A772A243FBBF1E103BDBBEF743}}(1) Primary health care services– ==

 Section 331 of the Public Health Service Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254d 42 U.S.C. 254d] )is amended—
:(A) in subsection (a)(3)(D), by striking“or mental health,”and inserting“optometry, or mental health,”; and
:(B) in subsection (b)(2), by striking“and physician assistants”and inserting“optometrists, and physician assistants”.

=={{anchor|HB51D48562D8B41E7811D2674DD74D932}}(2) Scholarship program– ==

 Section 338A of the Public Health Service Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254l 42 U.S.C. 254l] )is amended—
:(A) in subsection (a)(1), by striking“and physician assistants”and inserting“optometrists, and physician assistants”; and
:(B) in subsection (b)(1), by striking“or other health profession”and inserting“optometry, or other health profession”.

=={{anchor|H9285E547D4CD400F89A9137290BDCCB8}}(3) Loan repayment program– ==



==={{anchor|H9005C35A37AA4D4D8FE19F761F5C4B9A}}(A) Participation– ===

 Section 338B of the Public Health Service Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254l–1 42 U.S.C. 254l–1] )is amended—
:(i) in subsection (a)(1), by striking“and physician assistants”and inserting“optometrists, and physician assistants”;
:(ii) in subsection (b)(1)(A), by striking“or another health profession”and inserting“optometry, or another health profession”; and
:(iii) in subsections (b)(1)(B)and (b)(1)(C)(ii), by striking the term or other health profession each place such term appears and inserting“optometry, or other health profession”.

==={{anchor|H365A8EF5DD37435E8B54D3320898A507}}(B) Period of obligated service– ===

 Clauses (ii)and (iii) of section 338C(b)(5)(C) of the Public Health Service Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/254m 42 U.S.C. 254m(b)(5)(C)] )are amended by striking the term or other health profession each place such term appears and inserting“optometry, or other health profession”.
