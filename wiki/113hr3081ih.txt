{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Missing Children’s Assistance Act to better enable law enforcement to identify, locate, and recover child victims of sex trafficking.

=Section 1. Amendment to the Missing Children’s Assistance Act=

 Section 404(b)(1) of the Missing Children’s Assistance Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/5773 42 U.S.C. 5773(b)(1)] )is amended—
:(1) in subparagraph (R)by striking at the end “and” ;
:(2) in subparagraph (S)by striking the period at the end and inserting “; and” ; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(T) provide technical assistance to law enforcement agencies and first responders in identifying, locating, and recovering victims of child sex trafficking..
</blockquote>
