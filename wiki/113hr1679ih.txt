{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Expedited Funds Availability Act to clarify the application of that Act to American Samoa.

={{anchor|H39A34CA539E34482B2EC2670D7822872}}Section 1. Application of the Expedited Funds Availability Act=

The Expedited Funds Availability Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4001 12 U.S.C. 4001 et seq.] ] )is amended—
:(1) in section 602(20)([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4001 12 U.S.C. 4001(20)] ] )by inserting“, located in the United States,”after“ATM”;
:(2) in section 602(21)([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4001 12 U.S.C. 4001(21)] ] )by inserting“American Samoa,”after“Puerto Rico,”;
:(3) in section 602(23)([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4001 12 U.S.C. 4001(23)] ] )by inserting“American Samoa,”after“Puerto Rico,”; and
:(4) by adding at the end of section 603(d)([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4002 12 U.S.C. 4002(d)] ] )the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|HD62A239F4D8F4A099ABD0E91E4915A9B}}(3) Extension for certain deposits in American Samoa– ===

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any time period established under subsection (b), (c), or (e)shall be extended by 2 business days in the case of any deposit which is both—
:(A) deposited in an account at a depository institution which is located in American Samoa; and
:(B) deposited by a check drawn on an originating depository institution which is not located in the same State as the receiving depository institution..
</blockquote>
