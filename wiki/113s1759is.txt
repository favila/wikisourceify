{{Center|A BILL}}

To reauthorize the teaching health center program.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Teaching Health Center Reauthorization Act” .

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization of the teaching health centers program=

 Section 340H of the Public Health Service Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/256h 42 U.S.C. 256h] )is amended—
:(1) in subsection (g)—
::(A) by inserting before the period the following: “, andnot to exceed $800,000,000, for the period of fiscal years 2016 through 2020” ; and
::(B) by adding at the end the following: “Any amounts appropriated under this subsection for any of fiscal years 2011 through 2020 and remaining unexpended at the end of the fiscal year involved may be used in subsequent fiscal years to carry out this section.” ;
:(2) in subsection (h)(2)—
::(A) in the paragraph heading, by adding at the end the following: “; submission to [[w:|Congress]] ” ; and
::(B) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

====(C) Submission to Congress– ====

The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall annually submit to [[w:|Congress]] a report that contains a compilation of the data submitted to the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] under paragraph (1)for the year involved.;
</blockquote>

:(3) by redesignating subsections (h) through (j)as subsections (i) through (k), respectively; and
:(4) by inserting after subsection (g), the following:

<blockquote>

===(h) Limitation– ===

The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall establish a minimum per resident per year payment amount for funding of all approved teaching health center graduate medical education positions under this section that shall be not less than the per resident per year payment amount as of January 1, 2013, and ensure that not less than such amount is provided to all teaching health center graduate medical education programs for all approved positions..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. Teaching health centers development grants=

 Section 749A(g) of the Public Health Service Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/293l–1 42 U.S.C. 293l–1(g)] )is amended by striking “each fiscal year thereafter” and inserting “each of fiscal years 2013 through 2020 and each fiscal year thereafter” .
