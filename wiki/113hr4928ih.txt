{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit certain closures of Senior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps programs of the Army.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Readying Our Troops for Combat Act of 2014   ”or the“  ROTC Act of 2014  ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition of closures of Senior ROTC programs of the Army=

During the period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act and ending on July 1, 2020, the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]]may not, pursuant to [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]]Instruction 1215.08, disestablish, or initiate the disestablishment of, a Senior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps program of the Army established pursuant to chapter 103of title 10, United States Code.
