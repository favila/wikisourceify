{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 37, United States Code, to require the Secretary of Defense to ensure that members of the Armed Forces serving in a combat zone automatically receive the education benefits to which they are entitled.

={{anchor|H40AC817DFE1448D7BF1C9C91AF69C554}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Accessing Military Education Benefits Act”.

={{anchor|HA152B30EE61145469DAC837B295EE081}}Sec. 2. Department of Defense efforts to ensure operation of current prohibition on accrual of interest on direct student loans of members of the Armed Forces receiving imminent danger pay or hazardous duty pay=



=={{anchor|H3D6F3CE29F204274A2B0305B0A94BD6F}}(a) Members receiving imminent danger pay– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/310 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 310] of title 37, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|HD8FFDBA316FE42668F29D3C9EEE76837}}(f) No accrual of student loan interest for members serving in areas of hostilities– ===

When a member is assigned to duty in an area for which special pay is available under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/310 subsection (a)] , the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall ensure that, if the member has any direct student loans, the member automatically obtains the benefits provided under section 455(o) of the Higher Education Act of 1965([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e(o)] ] ). The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] may waive application of this subsection if the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] certifies that such application may adversely impact the national security interests of the United States..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HE4D7CBE7E546480BB0FC4BA32A9EA630}}(b) Members receiving hazardous duty pay– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/351 Section 351 of such title] is amended—
:(1) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/351 subsection (h)] as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/351 subsection (i)] ; and
:(2) by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/351 subsection (g)] the following new[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/351 subsection (h)] :

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H958EC93BD572482FB27D928F74DF8621}}(h) No accrual of student loan interest for members serving in areas of hostilities– ====

When a member is assigned to duty in an area for which special pay is available under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/351 paragraph (1)] or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/351 (3) of subsection (a)] , the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall ensure that, if the member has any direct student loans, the member automatically obtains the benefits provided under section 455(o) of the Higher Education Act of 1965([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e(o)] ). The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] may waive application of this subsection if the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] certifies that such application may adversely impact the national security interests of the United States..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H5605FBA8AFB34B668A83A611F0A82F18}}(c) Implementation– ==

Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall submit to theCommittees on Armed Services of the SenateandHouse of Representativesa plan describing how the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] will implement the amendments made by this section to ensure that members of the Armed Forces automatically obtain the benefits provided under section 455(o) of the Higher Education Act of 1965([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e(o)] ). Implementation of the plan shall commence not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
