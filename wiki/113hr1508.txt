{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the issuance of an Alzheimer’s Disease Research Semipostal Stamp.

={{anchor|H8B51D1DDC895430E92A2E36FEBBBCD08}}Section 1. Alzheimer's Disease Research Semipostal Stamp=



=={{anchor|H4C90181705DA4BC085738A72D8658F10}}(a) In general– ==

In order to afford a convenient way for members of the public to contribute to funding for medical research relating to Alzheimer's disease, the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] shall issue a semipostal stamp (referred to in this Act as the“Alzheimer's Disease Research Semipostal Stamp”) in accordance with the provisions of this section.

=={{anchor|H4C83C2BE5FC444AAB3EA5A4875B35ADD}}(b) Terms and conditions– ==



==={{anchor|H6C65C70B63444C88AEC2C1E3D6B187E0}}(1) In general– ===

The issuance and sale of the Alzheimer's Disease Research Semipostal Stamp shall be governed by the provisions of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/416 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 416] of title 39, United States Code] , and regulations issued under such section, subject to the requirements of this subsection.

==={{anchor|H471D6F9902C34D66BCD5A5E3D80C012A}}(2) Duration– ===

The Alzheimer's Disease Research Semipostal Stamp shall be made available to the public for a period of 6 years, beginning no later than 12 months after the date of the enactment of this Act.

==={{anchor|H6157B90A3CEE4CB1AEF17CC249B8F280}}(3) Disposition of proceeds– ===

All amounts becoming available from the sale of the Alzheimer's Disease Research Semipostal Stamp (as determined under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/416 section 416(d) of such title 39] ) shall be transferred to the [[w:National Institutes of Health|National Institutes of Health]] , for the purpose described in subsection (a), through payments which shall be made at least twice a year.

=={{anchor|H6D1D866A4C3E4EADA440BA50A36DC84F}}(c) Definition– ==

For purposes of this Act, the term semipostal stamp refers to a stamp described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/416 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 416(a)(1)] of title 39, United States Code] .
