{{Center|A BILL}}

To rescind funds made available to the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency if the Administrator fails to meet certain deadlines.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Environmental Protection Agency Accountability Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Definition of Administrator=

In this Act, the term Administrator means the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency]] .

=Sec. 3. Interim assessment of regulatory requirements and applicable penalties=



==(a) In general– ==

Not later than 30 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]] shall ensure that the requirements described in subsection (b)are satisfied.

==(b) Requirements– ==

The [[w:|Administrator]] shall satisfy—
:(1) section 4 of Executive Order 12866 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/601 5 U.S.C. 601 note] ) (relating to regulatory planning and review) and Executive Order 13563 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/601 5 U.S.C. 601 note] ) (relating to improving regulation and regulatory review) (or any successor Executive order establishing requirements applicable to the uniform reporting of regulatory and deregulatory agendas);
:(2)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/602 section 602 of title 5, United States Code] ;
:(3) section 8 of Executive Order 13132 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/601 5 U.S.C. 601 note] ) (relating to federalism); and
:(4)  section 202(a) of the Unfunded Mandates Reform Act of 1995( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/1532 2 U.S.C. 1532(a)] ).

==(c) Financial penalty provisions– ==



===(1) In general– ===

Subject to paragraph (2), if the [[w:|Comptroller General of the United States]] determines that the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]] fails to satisfy any requirement described in subsection (b), an amount of funding equal to $20,000 per week shall be rescinded from the [[w:|Office of the Administrator]] , and once each week thereafter until all the requirements are satisfied.

===(2) Limitations– ===



====(A) 1 day grace period– ====

 Paragraph (1)shall not apply if each requirement listed in subsection (b)is satisfied not later than 1 day after the deadline for the requirement.

====(B) Maximum amount of rescinded funds– ====

No rescission of funds under paragraph (1)shall exceed, in any fiscal year, an amount equal to 7 percent of the funds made available to the [[w:|Office of the Administrator]] .

===(3) Audits– ===

In any fiscal year in which any funds are rescinded from the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]] pursuant to this Act, the [[w:|Inspector General of the Environmental Protection Agency]] shall—
:(A) conduct an audit to assess compliance with the requirements of this paragraph; and
:(B) not later than 120 days after the end of the fiscal year during which any funds are rescinded under paragraph (1), submit to the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Environment and Public Works|Committee on Environment and Public Works of the Senate]] , the [[w:United States House Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure|Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives]] , the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Appropriations|Committee on Appropriations of the Senate]] , and the [[w:United States House Committee on Appropriations|Committee on Appropriations of the House of Representatives]] a report describing the reasons why the funds were rescinded, including allocations of resources.

===(4) Effect of paragraph– ===

Nothing in this paragraph affects or limits the application of, or obligation to comply with, any Federal, State, local, or tribal law.
