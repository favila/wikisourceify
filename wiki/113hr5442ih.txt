{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 to require the disclosure of total corporate tax paid by a corporation in each annual report required to be filed under such Act.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Corporate Transparency and Accountability Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Requirement to disclose total corporate taxes paid=

   Section 13 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/78m 15 U.S.C. 78m] )is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote> 

==(s) Disclosure of total corporate taxes paid– ==



===(1) Disclosure requirement– ===

Each issuer required to file an annual or quarterly report under subsection (a) shall disclose in that report—
:(A) the total pre-tax profit of the issuer during the period covered by the report; and
:(B) the total amount paid by the issuer in State and Federal taxes during the period covered by the report.

===(2) Availability of information– ===

The [[w:U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission|Commission]]shall ensure that, to the greatest extent practicable, the information required by this subsection is publicly available through the [[w:U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission|Commission]]website in a manner that is searchable, sortable, and downloadable. . 
</blockquote>
