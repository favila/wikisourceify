{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the further extension or establishment of national monuments in Utah except by express authorization of Congress.

={{anchor|H6987CC8692114CB39541F0DE4F7CD973}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Utah Land Sovereignty Act”.

={{anchor|HA8F93CD52E92476891B1172A6C16F4DF}}Sec. 2. Limitation on further extension or establishment of national monuments in Utah=

The proviso of the last sentence of the first section of the Act of September 14, 1950(64 Stat. 849, chapter 950;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/431a 16 U.S.C. 431a] ), is amended by inserting“or Utah”after“Wyoming”.
