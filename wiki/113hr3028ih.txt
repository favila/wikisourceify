{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the hold harmless provision for career and technical education assistance grants to States.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Career and Technical Education Equity Act” .

=Sec. 2. Hold harmless=

 Section 111(a)(5)(A) of the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act of 2006( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/2321 20 U.S.C. 2321(a)(5)(A)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

==(A) In general– ==

No State shall receive an allotment under this section for a fiscal year that is less than 90 percent of the allotment the State received under this section for the preceding fiscal year..
</blockquote>
