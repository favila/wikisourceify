{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to eliminate the marriage penalty in the dollar limitation on the student loan interest deduction.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Student Loan Interest Deduction Fairness Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Elimination of marriage penalty in dollar limitation on the student loan interest deduction=



==(a) In general– ==

   [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/221 Section 221(b)(1)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“shall not exceed”and all that follows and inserting“shall not exceed $2,500 (twice such amount in the case of a joint return)”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
