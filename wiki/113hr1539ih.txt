{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for certain tunnel life safety and rehabilitation projects for Amtrak.

={{anchor|H9AEB69C8688E496ABFB97798BDB36226}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Northeast Corridor Rail Tunnel Safety Act”.

={{anchor|HAA366AAB6E04436A9E182C9C4573F5AE}}Sec. 2. Authorization of appropriations=

There are authorized to be appropriated to the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] $898,000,000, to remain available until expended,for the use of Amtrak to complete New York tunnel life safety projects and to rehabilitate tunnels in Washington, DC, and Baltimore, Maryland.

={{anchor|H1ACA0811CC6140CF9B0D8FD896078184}}Sec. 3. Availability of funds=

The [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]] shall not make funds appropriated under section 2available to Amtrak for a project unless the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]] has received from Amtrak and has approved an engineering and financial plan for that project.

={{anchor|HC0A59F14CC1243C2ABCDE399C3C7ADF5}}Sec. 4. Inspector General review=

The [[w:|Inspector General of the Department of Transportation]] shall conduct and transmit to the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] an annual review to determine whether the expenditure and obligation of funds made available under this Act are consistent with the purposes for which those funds are made available.
