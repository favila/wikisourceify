{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to enter into contracts and agreements for the transfer of veterans to non-Department medical foster homes for certain veterans who are unable to live independently.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Long-Term Care Veterans Choice Act” .

=Sec. 2. Secretary of Veterans Affairs contract authority for transfer of veterans non-Department medical foster homes=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720 Section 1720 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>
:(h) 
::(1) At the request of a veteran for whom the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] is required to provide nursing home care under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1710A section 1710A of this title] , the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may transfer the veteran to a medical foster home that meets [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]] standards, at the expense of the United States, pursuant to a contract or agreement entered into between the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] and the medical foster home for such purpose. A veteran who is transferred to a medical foster home under this subsection shall agree, as a condition of such transfer, to accept home health services furnished by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1717 section 1717 of this title] .
::(2) For purposes of this subsection, the term medical foster home means a home designed to provide non-institutional, long-term, supportive care for veterans who are unable to live independently and prefer a family setting..
</blockquote>
