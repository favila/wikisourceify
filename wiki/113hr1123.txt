{{Center|A BILL}}

To promote consumer choice and wireless competition by
		  permitting consumers to unlock mobile wireless devices, and for other
		  purposes.

={{anchor|H90F6E3EDC5064245AF3B194E3063DA44}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Unlocking Consumer Choice and Wireless
			 Competition Act”.

={{anchor|H4C48389D404B455CAA810E572AC872D3}}Sec. 2. Repeal of
			 existing rule and additional rulemaking by Librarian of Congress=



=={{anchor|H3FE2FEDDC9184C40B28CAEDC69A2F991}}(a) Repeal and
			 replace– ==

Paragraph (3) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 201.40(b)] of title 37, Code of
			 Federal Regulations, as amended and revised by the [[w:Library of Congress|Librarian of Congress]] on
			 October 28, 2012, pursuant to his authority under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/1201 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 1201(a)] of title 17,
			 United States Code], shall have no force and effect, and such paragraph shall
			 read, and shall be in effect, as such paragraph was in effect on July 27,
			 2010.

=={{anchor|HD19512D5874542BFA8D3DA05550A1AD7}}(b) Rulemaking– ==

Not
			 later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:Library of Congress|Librarian of
			 Congress]], upon the recommendation of the Register of Copyrights, who shall
			 consult with the [[w:|
            Assistant Secretary for Communications and Information of the
			 Department of Commerce]] and report and comment on his or her views in making
			 such recommendation, shall determine, consistent with the requirements set
			 forth under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/1201 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 1201(a)(1)] of title 17, United States Code], whether to
			 extend the exemption for the class of works described in section 201.40(b)(3)
			 of title 37, Code of Federal Regulations, as amended by subsection (a), to
			 include any other category of wireless devices in addition to wireless
			 telephone handsets.

=={{anchor|HB8A334D45E3F4057AA115151CFC8C67E}}(c) Rule of
			 construction– ==

Nothing in this Act alters, or shall be construed to
			 alter, the authority of the [[w:Library of Congress|Librarian of Congress]] under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/1201 section 1201(a)(1) of
			 title 17, United States Code].
