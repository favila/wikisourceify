{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 232 Southwest Johnson Avenue in Burleson, Texas, as the“Sergeant William Moody Post Office Building”.   

=Section 1. Sergeant William Moody Post Office Building=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]located at 232 Southwest Johnson Avenue in Burleson, Texas, shall be known and designated as the“Sergeant William Moody Post Office Building”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to insubsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Sergeant William Moody Post Office Building”.
