{{Center|A BILL}}

 To authorize the construction of the Upper San Joaquin River Storage in California.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Upper San Joaquin River Storage Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Upper San Joaquin river storage=

 [[Public Law 108-361|Section 103(d)(1)(B) of title I ofPublic Law 108–361(]] the Calfed Bay-Delta Authorization Act ,118 Stat. 1681) is amended by—
:(a) adding after[[Public Law 108-361|clause (iii)]] the following:

<blockquote> 

===(iv) Construction authorization– ===

If the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]determines a project described in[[Public Law 108-361|clause (ii)]] , subclause (II) of subparagraph (A) is feasible, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]], subject to appropriations, is authorized to carry out the project in a manner that is substantially in accordance with the recommended plan, and subject to the conditions described in the feasibility study. ; and 
</blockquote>

:(b) striking in[[Public Law 108-361|clause (ii)]]  “the project”and inserting“a project described in[[Public Law 108-361|clause (ii)]] , subclause (II) of subparagraph (A)”.
