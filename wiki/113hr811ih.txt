{{Center|A BILL}}

To add the 9/11 Health and Compensation Programs to the list of exempt programs under PAYGO.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Never Forget 9/11 Heroes Act” .

=Sec. 2. Amendment to exempt programs=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 255(g)(1)(B) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/905 2 U.S.C. 905(g)(1)(B)] )is amended by—
:(1) inserting after the item relating to Retirement Pay and Medical Benefits for Commissioned Officers, [[w:United States Public Health Service|Public Health Service]] the following:

<blockquote>
:: September 11th Victim Compensation Fund (15–0340–0–1–754).; and
</blockquote>

:(2) inserting after the item relating to the Voluntary Separation Incentive Fund the following:

<blockquote>
:: World Trade Center Health Program Fund (75–0946–0–1–551)..
</blockquote>


==(b) Fiscal year 2014– ==

If the amendment made by subsection (a)takes effect after the date the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] has issued the final sequester order for fiscal year 2014, the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall revise the order to account for the amendment and issue a new order.
