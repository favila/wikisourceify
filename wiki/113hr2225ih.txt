{{Center|A BILL}}

To restore the traditional day of observance of Memorial Day, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Restoration of traditional day of observance of Memorial Day=



==(a) Designation of Legal Public Holiday– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/6103 Section 6103(a)of title 5, United States Code] , is amended by striking “Memorial Day, the last Monday in May.” and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
: Memorial Day, May 30..
</blockquote>


==(b) Observances and Ceremonies– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/116 Section 116of title 36, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/116 subsection (a)] , by striking “The last Monday in May” and inserting “May 30” ; and
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/116 subsection (b)] —
::(A) by striking “and” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/116 paragraph (3)] ;
::(B) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/116 paragraph (4)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/116 paragraph (5)] ; and
::(C) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/116 paragraph (3)] the following:

<blockquote>
:::(4) calling on the people of the United States to observe Memorial Day as a day of ceremonies to show respect for United States veterans of wars and other military conflicts; and.
</blockquote>


==(c) Display of Flag– ==



===(1) Time and occasions for flag display– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/4/6 Section 6(d)of title 4, United States Code] , is amended by striking “the last Monday in May;” and inserting “May 30;” .

===(2) National League of Families POW/MIA flag– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/902 Section 902(c)(1)(B)of title 36, United States Code] , is amended by striking “the last Monday in May” and inserting “May 30” .
