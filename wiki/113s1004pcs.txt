{{Center|A BILL}}

To permit voluntary economic activity.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Anti-Trust Freedom Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Voluntary economic coordination by individuals=

The Sherman Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/1 15 U.S.C. 1 et seq.] ), the Clayton Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/12 15 U.S.C. 12 et seq.] ), and section 5 of the Federal Trade Commission Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/45 15 U.S.C. 45] )shall not be construed to prohibit, ban, or otherwise extend to any voluntary economic coordination, cooperation, agreement, or other association, compact, contract, or covenant entered into by or between any individual or group of individuals.
