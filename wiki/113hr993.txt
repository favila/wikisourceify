{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the conveyance of certain parcels of
		  National Forest System land to the city of Fruit Heights,
		  Utah.

={{anchor|H16ABF0280ADB4641AA69378E449A87A0}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Fruit Heights Land Conveyance
			 Act”.

={{anchor|H3AF0AF8F41AE4C6392ED18A09E5D69BE}}Sec. 2. Definitions=

In this Act:

=={{anchor|HA8CE983D23BD49B6A7B8A04174C3B2B7}}(1) City– ==

The
			 term City means the city of Fruit Heights, Utah.

=={{anchor|H6428A97E786F4110BBF7D52CEE58E01A}}(2) Map– ==

The
			 term map means the map entitled Proposed Fruit Heights City
			 Conveyance and dated September 13, 2012.

=={{anchor|H51B0D57915994EADA92C322446D823CE}}(3) National Forest
			 System land– ==

The term National Forest System land
			 means the approximately 100 acres of National Forest System land, as depicted
			 on the map.

=={{anchor|H6727552AC377406D8D016EF3D9523144}}(4) Secretary– ==

The
			 term Secretary means the Secretary of Agriculture.

={{anchor|HBFCE8316AB5146D98435F1EBA0868EDC}}Sec. 3. Conveyance of
			 certain land to the city of Fruit Heights, Utah=



=={{anchor|H177242BE07294DA794FF59BA63A5B9E5}}(a) In
			 general– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall
			 convey to the City, without consideration, all right, title, and interest of
			 the United States in and to the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land.

=={{anchor|HB83C25706BAA4E2F8697CA22E404DA20}}(b) Survey– ==



==={{anchor|H5A6F98AF8217443D9B985028E881BC84}}(1) In
			 general– ===

If determined by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] to be necessary, the exact
			 acreage and legal description of the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land shall be
			 determined by a survey approved by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]].

==={{anchor|HDBCC755F086D41E8999CA866041472E4}}(2) Costs– ===

The
			 City shall pay the reasonable survey and other administrative costs associated
			 with a survey conducted under paragraph (1).

=={{anchor|HC4A6B55F53664269958EE78769E0D1FB}}(c) Easement– ==

As
			 a condition of the conveyance under subsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall reserve
			 an easement to the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land for the Bonneville Shoreline
			 Trail.

=={{anchor|HAE951C8ADD024559867DE8F3DF1DFB27}}(d) Use of national
			 forest system land– ==

As a
			 condition of the conveyance under subsection (a), the City shall use the
			 [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land only for public purposes.

=={{anchor|HE3441CC011FD45B6AAC84EB0DE93504A}}(e) Reversionary
			 Interest– ==

In the quitclaim
			 deed to the City for the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land, the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall
			 provide that the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land shall revert to the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]], at
			 the election of the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]], if the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] land is used for
			 other than a public purpose.
