{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the Secretary of Homeland Security from implementing proposed policy changes that would permit passengers to carry small, non-locking knives on aircraft.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Keep Knives Out of Our Skies Act”.

={{anchor|idFFDFC0CB6D10439FA8ABB355C6C5906B}}Sec. 2. Prohibition on implementation of policy change to permit small, non-locking knives on aircraft=



=={{anchor|idD3AE45BCC1DE47F394611B77A1855B2F}}(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, on and after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary of Homeland Security]] may not implement any change to the prohibited items list of the [[w:Transportation Security Administration|Transportation Security Administration]] that would permit passengers to carry small, non-locking knives through passenger screening checkpoints at airports, into sterile areas at airports, or on board passenger aircraft.

=={{anchor|id400F2C93BB9447499AECEA6C5BCEDA93}}(b) Prohibited items list defined– ==

In this section, the term prohibited items list means the list of items passengers are prohibited from carrying as accessible property or on their persons through passenger screening checkpoints at airports, into sterile areas at airports, and on board passenger aircraft pursuant tosection 1540.111of title 49, Code of Federal Regulations (as in effect on January 1, 2013).
