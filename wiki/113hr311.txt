{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Administrator of the Environmental
		  Protection Agency to change the Spill Prevention, Control, and Countermeasure
		  rule with respect to certain farms.

={{anchor|HA1C90A43E82E47E18ED8A64A2477DC4D}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Farmers Undertake Environmental Land
			 Stewardship Act” or the “FUELS Act”.

={{anchor|H48EA33220DDF4FEC81B05B9DA8912F86}}Sec. 2. Applicability of Spill
			 Prevention, Control, and Countermeasure rule=



=={{anchor|HE08A3039546D4B979A1128AA5B03ABC4}}(a) In
			 general– ==

The [[Wikipedia:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]], in
			 implementing the Spill Prevention, Control, and Countermeasure rule with
			 respect to any farm, shall—
:(1) require
			 certification of compliance with such rule by—
::(A) a professional
			 engineer for a farm with—
:::(i) an
			 individual tank with an aboveground storage capacity greater than 10,000
			 gallons;
:::(ii) an
			 aggregate aboveground storage capacity greater than or equal to 42,000 gallons;
			 or
:::(iii) a
			 history that includes a spill, as determined by the [[Wikipedia:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]; or
::(B) the owner or
			 operator of the farm (via self-certification) for a farm with—
:::(i) an aggregate aboveground storage capacity
			 greater than 10,000 gallons but less than 42,000 gallons; and
:::(ii) no history of spills, as determined by the
			 [[Wikipedia:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]; and
:(2) exempt from all
			 requirements of such rule any farm—
::(A) with an aggregate aboveground storage
			 capacity of less than or equal to 10,000 gallons; and
::(B) no history of spills, as determined by the
			 [[Wikipedia:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]].

=={{anchor|HED5CC5EE22C0461FA1C6301BB6CDA4C9}}(b) Calculation of
			 aggregate aboveground storage capacity– ==

For the purposes of subsection (a), the
			 aggregate aboveground storage capacity of a farm excludes all containers on
			 separate parcels that have a capacity that is less than 1,320 gallons.

={{anchor|HCCFB65A79B414651A53B07F0596C57D9}}Sec. 3. Definitions=

In this Act, the following terms
			 apply:

=={{anchor|H7A7EB717BB0B4D5C8935337FFED64AB2}}(1) Administrator– ==

The term “Administrator” means
			 the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency.

=={{anchor|H49EEF01EF275401DB2DBF79D42B32E53}}(2) Farm– ==

The
			 term “farm” has the meaning given such term in section 112.2 of
			 title 40, Code of Federal Regulations.

=={{anchor|HDC2439F5BFD44B75BE985B0F1C1B8CD0}}(3) Gallon– ==

The
			 term “gallon” refers to a United States liquid gallon.

=={{anchor|HD7D14C296A16427FBC47A01351BED502}}(4) Spill
			 Prevention, Control, and Countermeasure rule– ==

The term
			 “Spill Prevention, Control, and Countermeasure rule” means the
			 regulation promulgated by the [[Wikipedia:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Environmental Protection Agency]] under part 112 of
			 title 40, Code of Federal Regulations.
