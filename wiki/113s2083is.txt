{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Natural Gas Act to promote economic growth and job creation in the United States, to strengthen strategic partnerships with allies of the United States, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   American Job Creation and Strategic Alliances LNG Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Expedited approval of exportation of natural gas to World Trade Organization member countries=

   Section 3(c) of the Natural Gas Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/717b 15 U.S.C. 717b(c)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking“(c) For purposes”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

===(c) Expedited application and approval process– ===



====(1) Definition of World Trade Organization member country– ====

In this subsection, the term World Trade Organization member country has the meaning given the term WTO member country in  section 2 of the Uruguay Round Agreements Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/19/3501 19 U.S.C. 3501] ).

====(2) Expedited application and approval process– ====

For purposes ; and 
</blockquote>

:(2) in paragraph (2) (as so designated), by inserting ‘‘or to a World Trade Organization member country’’ after“trade in natural gas”. 

=Sec. 3. Effective date=

The amendments made by section 2 shall apply to applications for the authorization to export natural gas under  section 3 of the Natural Gas Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/717b 15 U.S.C. 717b])that are pending on, or filed on or after, the date of enactment of this Act.
