{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to authorize the Internal Revenue Service to permit truncated social security numbers on wage reporting provided to employees.

={{anchor|H289971C268D04BEB821E57978A707AE9}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“SAFE ID Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H88250B20FE574446B5E4814A73F908BA}}Sec. 2. Truncated social security numbers permitted on wage reporting to employees=



=={{anchor|H77B575E41D5845F298AC3C9C467A54E4}}(a) In general– ==

 Paragraph (2) of section 6051(a) of the Internal Revenue Code for 1986is amended by striking“social security number”and inserting“taxpayer identification number”.

=={{anchor|HD09D1ED26EEC4FE2B03B66F41B3BA7F3}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to statements furnished after the date of the enactment of this Act.
