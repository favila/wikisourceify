{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act to repeal a duplicative program relating to inspection and grading of catfish.

={{anchor|H7E5E9A1D7A6646E99B8A6905DDEB0AE3}}Section 1. Repeal of duplicative catfish inspection program=



=={{anchor|H068CBF8A3C264C2BA7E817C438112176}}(a) In general– ==

Effective on the date of the enactment of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/8701 7 U.S.C. 8701 et seq.] ), section 11016 of such Act([[Public Law -|Public Law 110–246]] ;122 Stat. 2130)and the amendments made by such section are repealed.

=={{anchor|H9E078DD1EB1641AEA41DCF31733EB427}}(b) Application– ==

The Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/1621 7 U.S.C. 1621 et seq.] )and the Federal Meat Inspection Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/21/601 21 U.S.C. 601 et seq.] )shall be applied and administered as if section 11016([[Public Law 110-246|Public Law 110–246]] ;122 Stat. 2130) of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/8701 7 U.S.C. 8701 et seq.] )and the amendments made by such section had not been enacted.
