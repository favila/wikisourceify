{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Tsunami Warning and Education Act to direct
		  the Administrator of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration,
		  through the National Weather Service, to establish, maintain, and operate an
		  additional tsunami forecast and warning center for the improvement of the
		  evaluation of tsunami risk to, and the dissemination of forecasts and tsunami
		  warning bulletins for, the public in Puerto Rico and the United States Virgin
		  Islands.
