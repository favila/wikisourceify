{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Department of Veterans Affairs Health Care Programs Enhancement Act of 2001 and title 38, United States Code, to require the provision of chiropractic care and services to veterans at all Department of Veterans Affairs medical centers and to expand access to such care and services.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Chiropractic Care Available to All Veterans Act” .

=Sec. 2. Program for provision of chiropractic care and services to veterans=

 Section 204(c) of the Department of Veterans Affairs Health Care Programs Enhancement Act of 2001( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1710 38 U.S.C. 1710 note] )is amended—
:(1) by inserting “(1)” before “The program” ; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(2) The program shall be carried out at not fewer than 75 medical centers by not later than December 31, 2014, and at all medical centers by not later than December 31, 2016..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. Expanded chiropractor services available to veterans=



==(a) Medical services– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 Paragraph (6) of section 1701 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote>
:(H) Chiropractic services..
</blockquote>


==(b) Rehabilitative services– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 Paragraph (8)] of such section is amended by inserting “chiropractic,” after “counseling,” .

==(c) Preventive health services– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 Paragraph (9)] of such section is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 subparagraphs (F) through (K)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 subparagraphs (G) through (L)] , respectively; and
:(2) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 subparagraph (E)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 subparagraph (F)] :

<blockquote>
::(F) periodic and preventative chiropractic examinations and services;.
</blockquote>
