:(b) Conforming amendment– The fifth paragraph after the paragraph under the side heading“ For contingent expenses, namely: ”under the subheading“ Senate. ”under the heading“ Legislative. ”of the Act of March 3, 1887 (24 Stat. 596, chapter 392; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/6572 2 U.S.C. 6572] ), is amended by striking“sections, sixty-five, sixty six, sixty-seven, sixty-eight, and sixty-nine,”and inserting“section 69”.
