{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 450 Lexington Avenue in New York, New York, as the“Vincent R. Sombrotto Post Office”.

={{anchor|HE4CC4FC3F0EA46F0B4736DFE0E078573}}Section 1. Vincent R. Sombrotto Post Office=



=={{anchor|HA6794FDF8DE94CB5B244964AAC57E8D2}}(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] located at 450 Lexington Avenue in New York, New York, shall be known and designated as the“Vincent R. Sombrotto Post Office”.

=={{anchor|H8489D37E7BDD47679637F2770FE482E0}}(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Vincent R. Sombrotto Post Office”.
