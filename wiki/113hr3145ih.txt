{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permit students who were homeless youths or homeless veterans to occupy low-income housing units.   

=Section 1. Students who were homeless youths or homeless veterans permitted to occupy low-income housing units=



==(a) In general– ==

   [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/42 Section 42(i)(3)(D)(i)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by redesignating subclauses (II) and (III) as subclauses (IV) and (V) and inserting after subclause (I) the following new subclauses:

<blockquote> 
:(II) a student who was (prior to occupying such unit) a homeless child or youth (as defined in section 725 of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act ), 
:(III) a student who was (prior to occupying such unit) a homeless veteran (as defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2002  section 2002of title 38, United States Code]), . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to determinations made on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
