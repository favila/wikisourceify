{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the definition of State in certain Federal agricultural laws to include the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.

=Section 1. Inclusion of the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands as a state under certain federal agricultural laws=



==(a) Amendment to the McIntire-Stennis Cooperative Forestry Act– ==

[[Public Law 87-788|Section 8 of[[Public Law -|Public Law 87–788]] ]] ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/582a–7 16 U.S.C. 582a–7] ; commonly known as the McIntire-Stennis Cooperative Forestry Act)is amended by striking “and Guam.” and inserting “Guam, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.” .

==(b) Amendment to the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946– ==

 Section 207 of the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/1626 7 U.S.C. 1626] )is amended by striking “and Guam.” and inserting “, Guam, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.” .
