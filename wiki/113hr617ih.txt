{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the Federal Building and United States Courthouse located at 1125 Chapline Street in Wheeling, West Virginia, as the Frederick P. Stamp, Jr. Federal Building and United States Courthouse .

=Section 1. Designation=

The Federal building and United States courthouse located at 1125 Chapline Street in Wheeling, West Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Frederick P. Stamp, Jr. Federal Building and United States Courthouse .

=Sec. 2. References=

Any reference in law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal Building and United States Courthouse referred to in section 1shall be deemed to be a reference to the Frederick P. Stamp, Jr. Federal Building and United States Courthouse .
