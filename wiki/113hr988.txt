{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe
		  Streets Act of 1968 to extend the authorization of the Bulletproof Vest
		  Partnership Grant Program through fiscal year 2017.

={{anchor|HFF94ED66EFAE446EAC8C3048FC04119F}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Bulletproof Vest Partnership Grant Act
			 of 2013”.

={{anchor|H8A43766F777F4AE3A7F573FEEB308704}}Sec. 2. Extension of
			 authorization of appropriations for bulletproof vest partnership grant
			 program=

Section 1001(a)(23) of
			 title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C.
			 3793(a)(23)) is amended by striking “2012” and inserting
			 “2017”.
