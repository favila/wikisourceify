{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to authorize use of grants under the Urban Area Security Initiative and the State Homeland Security Grant Program to work in conjunction with a Department of Energy national laboratory.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “National Laboratories Mean National Security Act” .

=Sec. 2. Use of homeland security grant funds in conjunction with Department of Energy national laboratories=

 Section 2008(a) of the Homeland Security Act of 2002( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/6/609 6 U.S.C. 609(a)] )is amended in the matter preceding paragraph (1)by inserting “including by working in conjunction with a National Laboratory (as defined in section 2(3) of the Energy Policy Act of 2005( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/15801 42 U.S.C. 15801(3)] ),” after “plans,” .
