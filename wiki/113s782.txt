{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend[[Public Law -|Public Law 101–377]] to revise the boundaries of the Gettysburg National Military Park to include the Gettysburg Train Station, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Gettysburg National Military Park Expansion Act”.

=Sec. 2. Gettysburg National Military Park expansion=



==(a) Gettysburg National Military Park boundary revision– ==

[[Public Law 101-377|Section 1 of[[Public Law -|Public Law 101–377]] ]] ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/430g–4 16 U.S.C. 430g–4] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

===(d) Additional land– ===

In addition to the land described in[[Public Law 101-377|subsections (a)]] and[[Public Law 101-377|(b)]] , the park shall include—
:(1) the property commonly known as the Gettysburg Train Station (including land located in close proximity to the Gettysburg Train Station) that is—
::(A) located in the Borough of Gettysburg; and
::(B) depicted on the map entitled Gettysburg National Military Park, Proposed Boundary , numbered 305/80,045, and dated September 2008; and
:(2) the property that is—
::(A) located adjacent to Plum Run in Cumberland Township; and
::(B) depicted on the map entitled Gettysburg National Military Park, Proposed Boundary Addition , numbered 305/80,046, and dated February 2009..
</blockquote>


==(b) Acquisition and disposal of land– ==

[[Public Law 101-377|Section 2(a) of[[Public Law -|Public Law 101–377]] ]] ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/430g–5 16 U.S.C. 430g–5(a)] ] )is amended—
:(1) by striking“The Secretary”and inserting the following:

<blockquote>

====(1) In general– ====

The Secretary; and
</blockquote>

:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

====(2) Acquisition of publicly owned land– ====



=====(A) In general– =====

Subject to[[Public Law 101-377|subparagraph (B)]] , the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] may acquire only by purchase from a willing seller publicly owned property that is located within the property described in[[Public Law 101-377|section 1(d)(1)]] if the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] determines that efforts to acquire the property without cost have been exhausted.

=====(B) Eminent domain– =====

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] may not acquire by eminent domain property that is located within the property described in[[Public Law 101-377|section 1(d)(1)]] ..
</blockquote>
