{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to increase the deduction for business start-up expenditures from $5,000 to $10,000.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Reward and Encourage New Business Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Increased deduction for business start-up expenditures=



==(a) In general– ==

 Clause (ii) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/195 section 195(b)(1)(A)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking “$5,000” and inserting “$10,000” .

==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 Section 195(b) of such Codeis amended by striking paragraph (3).

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or incurred in taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
