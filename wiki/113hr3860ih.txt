{{Center|A BILL}}

To revise the formula for allocating funding to States under the Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Act of 1981.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Low Income Heating Improvement Act” .

=Sec. 2. LIHEAP State allocation formula=

 Section 2604(a)(2) of the Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Act of 1981( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/8623 42 U.S.C. 8623(a)(2)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>
:(2) For purposes of paragraph (1), a State's allotment percentage is the percentage which that State would have received under this section as enacted in[[Public Law 97-35|Public Law 97–35]] ..
</blockquote>
