{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Communications Act of 1934 to facilitate paid television service in certain counties, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Colorado News, Emergency, Weather, and Sports Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Facilitating delivery of relevant television programming to unserved consumers=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 341(a) of the Communications Act of 1934 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/47/341 47 U.S.C. 341(a)] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1)(A) , by striking“in the county on January 1, 2004; or”and inserting the following: “in the county—

<blockquote> 
::(i) on January 1, 2004, in the case of a county described in paragraph (3)(A) ; or 
::(ii) on January 1, 2014, in the case of a county described in paragraph (3)(B) ; or ; and 
</blockquote>

:(2) in paragraph (3) —
::(A) by redesignating subparagraphs (A) through (C) as clauses (i) through (iii) , respectively, and adjusting the margins accordingly;
::(B) in the matter preceding clause (i) , as redesignated, by striking“means any 1 of 4 counties that—”and inserting the following: “means—

<blockquote> 
:::(A) any 1 of 4 counties that— ; and 
</blockquote>

::(C) by striking the period at the end and inserting the following: “; or

<blockquote> 
:::(B) any 1 of 2 counties that—
::::(i) are all in a single State;
::::(ii) on January 1, 2014, were each in designated market areas in which the majority of counties were located in another State; and
::::(iii) as a group had a combined total of 27,540 television households according to the U.S. Television Household Estimates by Nielsen Media Research for 2013–2014. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122  Section 122(a)(4)(C)of title 17, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 clauses (i)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 (ii)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 subclauses (I)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 (II)], respectively, and adjusting the margins accordingly;
:(2) in the matter preceding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 clause (I)], as redesignated, by striking“In the case of”and all that follows and inserting the following: “In the case of—

<blockquote> 
::(i) that State in which are located 4 counties that— ; 
</blockquote>

:(3) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 subclause (II)], as redesignated, by adding“or”at the end;
:(4) by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 subclause (II)], as redesignated, the following:

<blockquote> 
::(ii) that State in which are located 2 counties that—
:::(I) on January 1, 2014, were in local markets principally comprised of counties in another State, and
:::(II) had a combined total of 27,540 television households, according to the U.S. Television Household Estimates by Nielsen Media Research for 2013–2014, ; and 
</blockquote>

:(5) by inserting before the period at the end the following:“, in the case of the State described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 clause (i)], or on January 1, 2014, in the case of the State described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/122 clause (ii)] ”.
