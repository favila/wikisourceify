{{Center|A BILL}}

 To repeal the annual adjustment of retired pay and retainer pay amounts for retired members of the Armed Forces under age 62, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Repeal of annual adjustment of retired pay and retainer pay amounts for retired members of the Armed Forces under age 62=



==(a) Repeal– ==

  Section 403 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013 is hereby repealed.

==(b) Offset– ==

To offset the costs to be incurred by the repeal made by subsection (a) , the unobligated balances of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]]shall be reduced as necessary to generate savings in the total amount of $6,200,000,000.
