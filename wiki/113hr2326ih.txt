{{Center|A BILL}}

To authorize the Secretary of the Interior to conduct a special resource study of the Medgar Evers House, located in Jackson, Mississippi, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H30F46D0671B14297892D1629C38580BB}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Medgar Evers House Study Act”.

={{anchor|H71249517C0F842BD84774C14844CD836}}Sec. 2. Special resource study=



=={{anchor|H5705494333D74B47BCCBCE866DB24182}}(a) Study– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] shall conduct a special resource study of the home of the late civil rights activist Medgar Evers, located at 2332 Margaret Walker Alexander Drive in Jackson, Mississippi.

=={{anchor|H071C889C0FBE4EDFAC516965488BBFE9}}(b) Contents– ==

In conducting the study under subsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall—
:(1) evaluate the national significance of the site;
:(2) determine the suitability and feasibility of designating the site as a unit of the [[w:National Park Service|National Park System]] ;
:(3) consider other alternatives for preservation, protection, and interpretation of the site by Federal, State, or local governmental entities, or private and nonprofit organizations;
:(4) consult with interested Federal, State, or local governmental entities, private and nonprofit organizations or any other interested individuals; and
:(5) identify cost estimates for any Federal acquisition, development, interpretation, operation, and maintenance associated with the alternatives.

=={{anchor|HC844906E4E254879A7885D429258E6B8}}(c) Applicable law– ==

The study required under subsection (a)shall be conducted in accordance with section 8 of National Park Service General Authorities Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1a–5 16 U.S.C. 1a–5] ).

=={{anchor|HA0987B2741EB487C9341D54F2A49563D}}(d) Report– ==

Not later than 3 years after the date on which funds are first made available for the study under subsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall submit to the [[w:United States House Committee on Natural Resources|Committee on Natural Resources of the House of Representatives]] and the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources|Committee on Energy and Natural Resources of the Senate]] a report containing the results of the study and any conclusions and recommendations of the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] .
