{{Center|A BILL}}

 To include under Federal laws granting rights and responsibilities to married couples other couples in other legal unions similar to marriage, including domestic partnerships and civil unions.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Federal Benefits Equality Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Definition of marriage=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/1/7  Section 7of title 1, United States Code], is amended to read as follows—

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 7.   Marriage as including civil unions and domestic partnerships; spouse as including members of such unions==

In determining the meaning of any Act of Congress, the term marriage includes a marriage, a domestic partnership, a civil union, or any other similar legal union between 2 individuals that is recognized by a State, the District of Columbia, a territory or possession of the United States, or a federally recognized Indian tribe, and the word spouse refers to either member of such a legal union. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The item for[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/1/7 section 7]in the table of sections at the beginning of chapter 1 is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>  7.“Marriage”as including civil unions and domestic parnerships;“spouse”as including members of such legal unions.  . 
</blockquote>
