{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the National Wildlife Refuge System Administration Act of 1966 to require that any expansion of a national wildlife refuge must be expressly authorized by statute.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ National Wildlife Refuge Expansion Limitation Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition on expansion of national wildlife refuges=

   Section 4(a) of the National Wildlife Refuge System Administration Act of 1966 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/668dd 16 U.S.C. 668dd(a)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:(6) The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]may not expand any national wildlife refuge except as expressly authorized by a law enacted after January 3, 2013. . 
</blockquote>
