{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to treat certain misfiled documents as motions for reconsideration of decisions by the Board of Veterans' Appeals, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Treatment of certain misfiled documents as motions for reconsideration of decisions by Board of Veterans' Appeals=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 Section 7103 of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 subsection (c)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 subsection (d)]; and
:(2) by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 subsection (b)]the following new[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 subsection (c)]:

<blockquote> 
::(c) 
:::(1) Except as provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 paragraph (2)], if a person adversely affected by a final decision of the [[w:Board of Veterans Appeals|Board]], who has not filed a notice of appeal with the [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims|United States Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims]]under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7266 section 7266(a)]of this title within the period set forth in that section, files a document with the [[w:Board of Veterans Appeals|Board]]or the agency of original jurisdiction referred to in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7105 section 7105(b)(1) of this title]that expresses disagreement with such decision not later than 120 days after the date of such decision, such document shall be treated as a motion for reconsideration of such decision under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 subsection (a)].
:::(2) A document described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 paragraph (1)]shall not be treated as a motion for reconsideration of the decision under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 paragraph (1)]if—
::::(A) the [[w:Board of Veterans Appeals|Board]]or the agency of original jurisdiction referred to in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 paragraph (1)]—
:::::(i) receives the document described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7103 paragraph (1)];
:::::(ii) determines that such document expresses an intent to appeal the decision to the [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims|United States Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims]]; and
:::::(iii) forwards such document to the [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims|United States Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims]]; and
::::(B) the [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims|United States Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims]]receives such document within the period set forth by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7266 section 7266(a) of this title]. . 
</blockquote>
