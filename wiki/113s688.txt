={{anchor|idA8272EF0E8A14DEF9FA4B389A45CD7A2}}Section 1. Permanent extension of deduction for mortgage insurance premiums=



=={{anchor|id3BA39DCB4BEE4EFA96604A0EA54C4D35}}(a) In general– ==

 Subparagraph (E) of section 163(h)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking clause (iv).

=={{anchor|id5E574E3ECAF94FF0B4E37ECB8070A440}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or accrued after December 31, 2013, and to amounts properly allocable to periods after such date.
