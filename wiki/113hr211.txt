{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to waive
		  the requirement for proof of citizenship during the first year of life for
		  children born in the United States to a Medicaid-eligible
		  mother.


{{section|H50BB618BF21D4D378DE4D832DA564F42|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Medicaid Newborn Coverage Act of
			 2013

        
.

In

 (42 U.S.C. 1396b(x)(2)
