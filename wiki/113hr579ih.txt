{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the United States courthouse located at 501 East Court Street in Jackson, Mississippi, as the“R. Jess Brown United States Courthouse”.

={{anchor|HEA023A2CD2F443C5B54F7FE7A33784E4}}Section 1. Designation=

The United States courthouse located at 501 East Court Street in Jackson, Mississippi, shall be known and designated as the“R. Jess Brown United States Courthouse”.

={{anchor|HB75045A1949843D4874222EE0485ED67}}Sec. 2. References=

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the United States courthouse referred to in section 1shall be deemed to be a reference to the“R. Jess Brown United States Courthouse”.
