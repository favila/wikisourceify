{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend for one year the business research credit.   

=Section 1. Extension of research credit=



==(a) In general– ==

  Subparagraph (B) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/41 section 41(h)(1)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“December 31, 2013”and inserting“December 31, 2014”.

==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

  Subparagraph (D) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/45C section 45C(b)(1)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“December 31, 2013”and inserting“December 31, 2014”.

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or incurred after December 31, 2013.
