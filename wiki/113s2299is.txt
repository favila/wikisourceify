=Section 1. Reauthorization of Native American languages program=

   Section 816(e) of the Native American Programs Act of 1974 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/2992d 42 U.S.C. 2992d(e)] )is amended by striking“2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, and 2012”and inserting“   2015 through 2019  ”.
