{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require that any new contract to provide project-based rental assistance under section 8 of the United States Housing Act of 1937 have a term of 40 years, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Section 8 Relief Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. 40-year terms for section 8 project-based contracts=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any housing assistance payments contract to provide rental assistance under  section 8 of the United States Housing Act of 1937 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1437f 42 U.S.C. 1437f] )that is attached to a structure and is entered into after the date of the enactment of this Act shall have a term of 40 years.
