{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, to make the shareholder threshold for registration of savings and loan holding companies the same as for bank holding companies.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Holding Company Registration Threshold Equalization Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Registration threshold for savings and loan holding companies=

The Securities Exchange Act of 1934( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/78a 15 U.S.C. 78a et seq.] )is amended—
:(1) in section 12(g)( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/78l 15 U.S.C. 78l(g)] )—
::(A) in paragraph (1)(B), by inserting after “is a bank” the following: “, a savings and loan holding company (as such term is defined in section 10 of the Home Owners' Loan Act),” ; and
::(B) in paragraph (4)—
:::(i) by inserting after “case of a bank” the following: “, a savings and loan holding company (as such term is defined in section 10 of the Home Owners' Loan Act),” ; and
:::(ii) by striking “persons persons” and inserting “persons” ; and
:(2) in section 15(d)( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/78o 15 U.S.C. 78o(d)] ), by striking “case of bank” and inserting the following: “case of a bank, a savings and loan holding company (as such term is defined in section 10 of the Home Owners' Loan Act),” .
