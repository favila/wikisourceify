{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title XI of the Social Security Act to exempt from manufacturer transparency reporting certain transfers used for educational purposes, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Exempting from manufacturer transparency reporting certain transfers used for educational purposes=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 1128G(e)(10)(B) of the Social Security Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1320a–7h 42 U.S.C. 1320a–7h(e)(10)(B)])is amended—
:(1) in clause (iii), by inserting“, including peer-reviewed journals, journal reprints, journal supplements, and medical textbooks”after“patient use”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote> 
::(xiii) A transfer of anything of value to a covered recipient who is a physician if the thing of value is intended solely for purposes of providing continuing medical education to the physician. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to transfers of value made on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
