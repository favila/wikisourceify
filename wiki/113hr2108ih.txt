{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to provide information to foster youth on their potential eligibility for Federal student aid.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Foster Youth Higher Education Opportunities Act” .

=Sec. 2. Informing foster youth of their potential eligibility for Federal student aid=

 Section 483 of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1090 20 U.S.C. 1090] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==(i) Informing foster youth of their potential eligibility for Federal student aid– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] shall inform each student who indicates on a FAFSA form, in either paper or electronic format, that the student is a foster youth or was in the foster care system of the student's potential eligibility for Federal student aid, including the specific Federal programs under which such student may be eligible to receive assistance such as the John H. Chafee Foster Care Independence Program under section 477 of the Social Security Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/677 42 U.S.C. 677] )..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. Information for foster youth on Department Web site=

 Section 485E(b) of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1092f 20 U.S.C. 1092f(b)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==(5) Information for foster youth on department Web site– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]] shall include on the Department's Web site information for foster youth regarding such youth's potential eligibility for Federal student aid, including the specific Federal programs under which such youth may be eligible to receive assistance..
</blockquote>
