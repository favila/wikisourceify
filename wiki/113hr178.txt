{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to provide for the
		  termination of further retirement benefits for Members of Congress, except the
		  right to continue participating in the Thrift Savings Plan, and for other
		  purposes.

={{anchor|H27EA40B787C7449496605F5CE7A75BC2}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “End Pensions in Congress
			 Act” or the “EPIC
			 Act”.

={{anchor|H07B223ACD9884AFEB90E11A3C47D27D8}}Sec. 2. Amendments
			 relating to the Civil Service Retirement System=



=={{anchor|HA892E13C68CE4AF68DF95A7403D02865}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Subchapter III of chapter 83 of title 5, United States
			 Code, is amended by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335 section 8335] the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H336121B880F441DC88278DD514C29B21}}Sec. 8335a. Termination
				of further retirement coverage of Members of Congress==



==={{anchor|H8CE1D12AB61E488ABB8A92CC66BACCAC}}(a) In
				general– ===

Notwithstanding any
				other provision of this subchapter and subject to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (f)], effective as
				of the date of enactment of this section—
:(1) a Member shall not
				be subject to this subchapter for any further period of time; and
:(2) no further
				Government contributions or deductions from basic pay may be made with respect
				to such Member for deposit in the Treasury of the United States to the credit
				of the Fund.

==={{anchor|HC68F935FE6E044FCA3AAF6C79724928E}}(b) Prior rights not
				affected– ===

Nothing in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (a)] shall be considered to
				nullify, modify, or otherwise affect any right, entitlement, or benefit under
				this subchapter with respect to any Member covering any period prior to the
				date of enactment of this section.

==={{anchor|H4A5F1F3D66D84073AD6996C964357DB6}}(c) Right To
				participate in thrift savings plan not affected– ===

Nothing in
				[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335a subsection (a)] shall affect the eligibility of a Member to participate in the
				Thrift Savings Plan in accordance with otherwise applicable provisions of
				law.

==={{anchor|HFCD280BA2C6B4378A45B4677210049A8}}(d) Regulations– ===

Any
				regulations necessary to carry out this section may—
:(1) except with
				respect to matters under subparagraph (B), be prescribed by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the
				Office of Personnel Management]]; and
:(2) with respect to
				matters relating to the Thrift Savings Plan, be prescribed by the Executive
				Director (as defined by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 section 8401(13)]).

==={{anchor|H1A65A9953BA2461F89A0EDED274659CC}}(e) Exclusion– ===

For
				purposes of this section, the term Member does not include the
				Vice President.

==={{anchor|H064347DD9B7E4FF6A6F20FFD7AE6D558}}(f) Opt-In– ===

Not later than 90 days after the date of
				enactment of this section, a Member covered by this subchapter as of such date
				may elect, by giving notice in writing to the official by whom he is paid, to
				remain subject to this
				subchapter..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H6FF683C64E7349C58C630539FF77F012}}(b) Clerical
			 amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of chapter 83 of
			 title 5, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item relating to
			 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8335 section 8335] the following:

<blockquote>__NOTOC__
8335a. Termination of further retirement
				coverage of Members of
				Congress..
</blockquote>


={{anchor|H2BF0691CB2AE45C8A8B3CC3FB38138D9}}Sec. 3. Amendments
			 relating to the Federal Employees’ Retirement System=



=={{anchor|H83337559960345B9834CB1ADF2A5EA1F}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Subchapter II of chapter 84 of title 5, United States
			 Code, is amended by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425 section 8425] the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H74333AB42CA24164B42D4D3AA5F84F92}}Sec. 8425a. Termination
				of further retirement coverage of Members of Congress==



==={{anchor|H11D2892F99444B70A4141712E53A1621}}(a) In
				general– ===

Notwithstanding any
				other provision of this chapter, effective as of the date of enactment of this
				section—
:(1) subject to
				[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (f)], in the case of an individual who first becomes a Member before
				such date of enactment—
::(A) such Member shall
				not be subject to this chapter for any further period of time after such date
				of enactment; and
::(B) no further
				Government contributions or deductions from basic pay may be made with respect
				to such Member for deposit in the Treasury of the United States to the credit
				of the Fund; and
:(2) in the case of an
				individual who first becomes a Member on or after such date of
				enactment—
::(A) such Member shall
				not be subject to this chapter; and
::(B) no Government
				contributions or deductions from basic pay may be made with respect to such
				Member for deposit in the Treasury of the United States to the credit of the
				Civil Service Retirement and Disability Fund.

==={{anchor|HFD8419DA2CF043FA95BCB5644284843C}}(b) Prior rights not
				affected– ===

Nothing in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (a)] shall be considered to
				nullify, modify, or otherwise affect any right, entitlement, or benefit under
				this chapter with respect to any Member covering any period prior to the date
				of enactment of this section.

==={{anchor|HFEF412AE34DB4A07AC9B6537D94A282C}}(c) Right To
				participate in thrift savings plan not affected– ===

Nothing in
				[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (a)] shall affect the eligibility of a Member to participate in the
				Thrift Savings Plan in accordance with otherwise applicable provisions of
				law.

==={{anchor|H367B434C0F1446F89F8CB67B69CBCC47}}(d) Regulations– ===



===={{anchor|H8BE76A0F0B2C4ACEBE31AB48E1A88416}}(1) In
				general– ====

Any regulations necessary to carry out this section
				may—
:(A) except with
				respect to matters under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subparagraph (B)], be prescribed by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the
				Office of Personnel Management]]; and
:(B) with respect to
				matters relating to the Thrift Savings Plan, be prescribed by the Executive
				Director (as defined by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 section 8401(13)]).

===={{anchor|HC4F2CBBB7BF04F3DBFB76492C173CD5A}}(2) Refunds– ====

Notwithstanding
				[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a subsection (b)], the regulations under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8425a paragraph (1)(A)] shall, in the case of a
				Member who has not completed at least 5 years of civilian service as of the
				date of enactment of this section, provide that the lump-sum credit shall be
				payable to such Member to the same extent and in the same manner as if such
				Member satisfied [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8424 paragraphs (1) through (4) of section 8424(a)] as of such date
				of enactment.

==={{anchor|H40B81958D8A046D99DD8DF8D55432CD2}}(e) Exclusion– ===

For
				purposes of this section, the term Member does not include the
				Vice President.

==={{anchor|H44806A13C2A64C00ACCC63F6475AC07E}}(f) Opt-In– ===

Not later than 90 days after the date of
				enactment of this section, a Member covered by this chapter as of such date may
				elect, by giving notice in writing to the official by whom he is paid, to
				remain subject to this
				chapter..
</blockquote>
