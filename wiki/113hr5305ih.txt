{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 11 of the United States Code to treat Puerto Rico as a State for purposes of chapter 9 of such title relating to the adjustment of debts of municipalities.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Puerto Rico Chapter 9 Uniformity Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Amendment=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/101  Section 101(52)of title 11, United States Code], is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 
:(52)  The term“State”includes Puerto Rico and, except for the purpose of defining who may be a debtor underchapter 9 of this title, includes the District of Columbia. . 
</blockquote>
