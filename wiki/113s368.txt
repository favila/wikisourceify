{{Center|A BILL}}

To reauthorize the Federal Land Transaction Facilitation Act, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Federal Land Transaction Facilitation Act Reauthorization of 2013”.

=Sec. 2. Federal Land Transaction Facilitation Act=

The Federal Land Transaction Facilitation Actis amended—
:(1) in section 203(2)([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/2302 43 U.S.C. 2302(2)] ), by striking“on the date of enactment of this Act was”and inserting“is”;
:(2) in section 205([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/2304 43 U.S.C. 2304] )—
::(A) in subsection (a), by striking“this Act”and inserting“the Federal Land Transaction Facilitation Act Reauthorization of 2013”; and
::(B) in subsection (d), by striking“11”and inserting“21”;
:(3) in section 206([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/2305 43 U.S.C. 2305] ), by striking subsection (f); and
:(4) in section 207(b)([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/43/2306 43 U.S.C. 2306(b)] )—
::(A) in paragraph (1)—
:::(i) by striking“96–568”and inserting“96–586”; and
:::(ii) by striking“; or”and inserting a semicolon;
::(B) in paragraph (2)—
:::(i) by inserting“[[Public Law 105-263|Public Law 105–263]] ;”before“112 Stat.”; and
:::(ii) by striking the period at the end and inserting a semicolon; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:::(3) the White Pine County Conservation, Recreation, and Development Act of 2006([[Public Law 109-432|Public Law 109–432]] ;120 Stat. 3028);
:::(4) the Lincoln County Conservation, Recreation, and Development Act of 2004([[Public Law 108-424|Public Law 108–424]] ;118 Stat. 2403);
:::(5)  subtitle F of title I of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1132 16 U.S.C. 1132 note] ;[[Public Law 111-11|Public Law 111–11]] );
:::(6)  subtitle O of title I of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/460www 16 U.S.C. 460www note] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1132 1132 note] ;[[Public Law 111-11|Public Law 111–11]] );
:::(7)  section 2601 of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009([[Public Law 111-11|Public Law 111–11]] ;123 Stat. 1108); or
:::(8)  section 2606 of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009([[Public Law 111-11|Public Law 111–11]] ;123 Stat. 1121)..
