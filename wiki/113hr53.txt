To amend title 18, United States Code, to increase from 1 to 2 years the post employment restrictions on Members of the House of Representatives.
1. Increase in post employment restrictions for Members of the House of Representatives


=(a) In general=

Section 207(e)(1) of title 18, United States Code , is amended—: in subparagraph (A) —:: in the subparagraph heading, by inserting “and members of the house of representatives”after “Senators”;:: by inserting “or a Member of the House of Representatives ”after “who is a Senator ”; and:: by inserting “or Member of the House of Representatives ”after “such former Senator ”;: in subparagraph (B) —:: in the subparagraph heading. by striking “Members and officers”and inserting “Officers”;:: in clause (i) —::: by striking “Any person who is a Member of the House of Representatives or ”and inserting “Any person who is ”;::: by striking “or (iii) ”; and::: by striking “such former Member of Congress or ”and inserting “such former ”;:: by striking clause (ii) ; and:: by redesignating clause (iii) as clause (ii) .
=(b) Effective date=

The amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply to individuals who leave office on or after the date of the enactment of this Act .
