        {{Center|A BILL}}
    
		To temporarily increase the borrowing authority of the
		  Federal Emergency Management Agency for carrying out the National Flood
		  Insurance Program.
	
	
		
        =={{section|H0B4C4C0E5B324BC297F5D4FCE25F1915|
      1.
      Temporary increase in
			 borrowing authority for National Flood Insurance Program
			
        (a)
        
          
            Section 1309(a) of the National Flood
			 Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4016(a)) is amended by striking
			 $20,725,000,000 and inserting
			 $30,425,000,000.
			
      
        (b)
        The amount provided by this section is
			 designated by the 
                [[United States Congress|Congress]]
             as an emergency requirement pursuant to section
			 403(a) of S. Con. Res. 13 (111th Congress), the concurrent resolution on the
			 budget for fiscal year 2010, and as an emergency pursuant to 
            section 4(g) of
			 the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010 (2 U.S.C. 933(g)).
