{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 18, United States Code, to prohibit the importation or exportation of mussels of a certain genus, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Protecting Lakes Against Quaggas Act of 2013 ”or as the“ PLAQ Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. In general=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/42 Section 42(a)(1) of title 18, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by striking“of the zebra mussel of the species Dreissena polymorpha;”; and
:(2) by inserting after“Hypophthalmichthys nobilis;”the following:“of the species of mussels of the genus Dreissena”.
