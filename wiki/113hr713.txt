{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to repeal the Medicare outpatient rehabilitation therapy caps.

={{anchor|H95F64D4C82C54AEC87D2E00A0637CF88}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Medicare Access to Rehabilitation Services Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H590BC00D1CE74348AD1211E4A4A6C6EC}}Sec. 2. Outpatient therapy cap repeal=

Section 1833 of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395l) is amended by striking subsection (g).
