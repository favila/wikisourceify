{{Center|A BILL}}

 To establish a separate Inspector General for the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection.   

=Section 1. Creating an Inspector General for the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 12 of the Inspector General Act of 1978 (5 U.S.C. App.)is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1) , by inserting“the [[w:|Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]],”after“  [[w:Federal Emergency Management Agency|Federal Emergency Management Agency]],”; and
:(2) in paragraph (2) , by inserting“the [[w:|Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]],”after“  [[w:Export-Import Bank of the United States|Export-Import Bank]],”.

==(b) Technical and conforming amendments– ==

  Section 8G of such Act is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a)(2) , by striking“and the [[w:|Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]] ”;
:(2) in subsection (c) , by striking the third and fourth sentences; and
:(3) in subsection (g)(3) , by striking“and the [[w:|Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]] ”. 

=Sec. 2. Requirements for the Inspector General for the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection=



==(a) Establishment– ==

   Section 1011 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5491 12 U.S.C. 5491] )is amended—
:(1) in subsection (b) —
::(A) in the subsection heading, by striking“ and Deputy Director ”and inserting“ , Deputy Director, and Inspector General ”; and
::(B) by inserting after paragraph (5) the following:

<blockquote> 

=====(6) Inspector General– =====

There is established the position of the Inspector General. ; and 
</blockquote>

:(2) in subsection (d) , by striking“or Deputy Director”each place it appears and inserting“, Deputy Director, or Inspector General”.

==(b) Hearings– ==

  Section 1016 of such Act is amended by inserting after subsection (c) the following:

<blockquote> 

===(d) Additional Requirement for Inspector General– ===

On a separate occasion from that described in subsection (a) , the [[w:|Inspector General of the Bureau]]shall appear, upon invitation, before the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs|Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs of the Senate]]and the [[w:United States House Committee on Financial Services|Committee on Financial Services]]and the [[w:United States House Committee on Energy and Commerce|Committee on Energy and Commerce of the House of Representatives]]at semi-annual hearings regarding the reports required under subsection (b) and the reports required under  section 5 of the Inspector General Act of 1978 (5 U.S.C. App.). . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Funding for Office of Inspector General– ==

  Section 1017(a)(2) of such Act is amended—
:(1) by redesignating subparagraph (C) as subparagraph (D) ; and
:(2) by inserting after subparagraph (B) the following:

<blockquote> 

====(C) Funding for Office of Inspector General– ====

Each fiscal year, the [[w:|Bureau]]shall dedicate 0.25 percent of the funds transferred pursuant to paragraph (1) to the [[w:|Office of the Inspector General]]. . 
</blockquote>


==(d) Participation in the Council of Inspectors General on Financial Oversight– ==

  Section 989E(a)(1) of such Act is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:(J) The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection. . 
</blockquote>


==(e) Deadline for appointment– ==

Not later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]]shall appoint an [[w:|Inspector General for the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]]in accordance with   section 3of the Inspector General Act of 1978 (5 U.S.C. App.). 

=Sec. 3. Effective date=

The amendments made by this Act shall take effect 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act. 

=Sec. 4. Transition period=

The [[w:|Inspector General of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System]]and the [[w:|Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]]shall serve in that position until the confirmation of an [[w:|Inspector General for the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]]. At that time, the [[w:|Inspector General of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System]]and the [[w:|Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]]shall become the [[w:|Inspector General of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System.]]
