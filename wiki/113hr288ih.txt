{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to increase the maximum age for children eligible for medical care under the CHAMPVA program.

={{anchor|H954EACC2C0744E8E8A1775372C5F96DC}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“CHAMPVA Children’s Protection Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H9806EB112F024AC18CBE838906EE50BE}}Sec. 2. Increase of maximum age for children eligible for medical care under CHAMPVA program=



=={{anchor|HEEFC02F17BF14F7D871C074A4C879757}}(a) Increase– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1781 Section 1781(c) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking“twenty-three”and inserting“twenty-six”; and
:(2) by striking“twenty-third birthday”and inserting“twenty-sixth birthday”.

=={{anchor|H4B71BAB131AE4ADDA44EA0127E2A03A1}}(b) Effective Date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a)shall apply with respect to medical care provided on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
