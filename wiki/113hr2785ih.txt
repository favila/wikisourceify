{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to improve the hiring of veterans by the Federal Government and State governments, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Military Reserve Jobs Act” .

=Sec. 2. Preference eligibility for members of reserve components of the armed forces appointed to competitive service; clarification of appeal rights=



==(a) Preference eligibility– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 Section 2108of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 paragraph (3)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 subparagraph (G)(iii)] , by striking “and” at the end;
::(B) by inserting the following after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 subparagraph (H)] :

<blockquote>
:::(I) an individual who is a member of a reserve component of the armed forces:
::::(i) who has—
:::::(I) successfully completed officer candidate training or entry level and skill training; and
:::::(II) incurred, or is performing, an initial period of obligated service in a reserve component of the armed forces of not less than 6 consecutive years; or
::::(ii) who has completed at least 10 years of service in a reserve component of the armed forces in each of which the individual was credited with at least 50 points under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12732 section 12732of title 10] toward the computation of years of service under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12732 section 12732of title 10] for purposes of eligibility for retired pay underchapter 1223of title 10; and
:::(J) an individual who is—
::::(i) retired from service in a reserve component of the armed forces; and
::::(ii) eligible for, but has not yet commenced receipt of, retired pay for non-regular service underchapter 1223 of title 10;;
</blockquote>

:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 paragraph (4)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 subparagraph (A)] , by striking “or” at the end;
::(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 subparagraph (B)] , by striking “and” at the end and inserting “or” ; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:::(C) the individual is a retiree described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 paragraph (3)(J)] ;;
</blockquote>

:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 paragraph (5)] by striking “and” at the end; and
:(4) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(6)  entry level and skill training has the meaning given that term in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3301 section 3301(2) of title 38] ; and
::(7)  reserve component of the armed forces means a reserve component specified in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 section 101(27) of title 38] ..
</blockquote>


==(b) Tiered hiring preference for members of reserve components of the armed forces– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3309 Section 3309of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3309 paragraph (1)] , by striking “and” at the end; and
:(2) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3309 paragraph (2)] and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
::(2) a preference eligible under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 subparagraph (A)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 (B)] , or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 (J) of section 2108(3) of this title] —5 points;
::(3) a preference eligible under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 section 2108(3)(I)(ii) of this title] —4 points; and
::(4) a preference eligible under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2108 section 2108(3)(I)(i) of this title] —3 points..
</blockquote>


==(c) Clarification of appeal rights– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3330a Section 3330aof title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3330a subsection (a)(1)(A)] , by inserting “, including a preference eligible appointed pursuant to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7401 section 7401of title 38] or otherwise employed by the [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration of the Department of Veterans Affairs]] ,” after “A preference eligible” ; and
:(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3330a subsection (d)(1)] , by inserting “, including a complaint so filed by a preference eligible appointed pursuant to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7401 section 7401of title 38] or otherwise employed by the [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration]] ,” after “If the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]] is unable to resolve a complaint under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3330a subsection (a)] ” .

===(2) Coordination rule– ===

<blockquote>
:(f) If any part of this section is deemed to be inconsistent with any provision ofchapter 74of title 38, this section shall be deemed to supersede, override or otherwise modify such provision ofchapter 74 of title 38..
</blockquote>
