{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to provide veterans service organizations with the same access to Department of Veterans Affairs facilities during the Government shutdown as such organizations had immediately prior to the shutdown, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Veterans Services Support Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Access of veterans service organizations to allocated space in Department of Veterans Affairs facilities during the Government shutdown=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, during any period after September 30, 2013, for which interim or full-year appropriations for the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]for fiscal year 2014 have not been enacted, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]]shall ensure that employees of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]]who are excepted from furlough during any such period provide—
:(1) veterans service organizations with the same access to [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]facilities as such organizations had on September 30, 2013;
:(2) veterans with the same access to veterans service organizations using such facilities as was provided on September 30, 2013; and
:(3) such services as may be necessary to ensure the safety and security of such veterans and veterans service organizations in the same manner as was provided on September 30, 2013.
