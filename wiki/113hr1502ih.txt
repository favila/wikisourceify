{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title II of the Social Security Act to prevent concurrent receipt of unemployment benefits and Social Security disability insurance, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HE0B9E918B8DC4EA1B86506C41BF0AF9C}}Section 1. Short Title=

This Act may be cited as the“Social Security Disability Insurance and Unemployment Benefits Double Dip Elimination Act”.

={{anchor|HFB26F63FF5F14CEE9654F34C8FE64F77}}Sec. 2. Disqualification on receipt of disability insurance benefits in a month for which unemployment compensation is received=



=={{anchor|H760DD22B8020456AA3A5BA5D29AF81FA}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 223(d)(4) of the Social Security Act(42 U.S.C. 423(d)(4))is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(C) 
::(i) If for any month an individual is entitled to unemployment compensation, such individual shall be deemed to have engaged in substantial gainful activity for such month.
::(ii) For purposes of clause (i), the term“unemployment compensation”means—
:::(I) “regular compensation”,“extended compensation”, and“additional compensation”(as such terms are defined by section 205 of the Federal-State Extended Unemployment Compensation Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/3304 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 26 U.S.C. 3304] note] )); and
:::(II) trade adjustment assistance under title II of the Trade Act of 1974([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/19/2251 19 U.S.C. 2251 et seq.] )..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H65522A325C894ABC85AB50AABBA6BD49}}(b) Trial work period– ==

 Section 222(c) of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/422 42 U.S.C. 422(c)] ] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(6) 
::(A) For purposes of this subsection, an individual shall be deemed to have rendered services in a month if the individual is entitled to unemployment compensation for such month.
::(B) For purposes of subparagraph (A), the term“unemployment compensation”means—
:::(i) “regular compensation”,“extended compensation”, and“additional compensation”(as such terms are defined by section 205 of the Federal-State Extended Unemployment Compensation Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/3304 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 26 U.S.C. 3304] note] )); and
:::(ii) trade adjustment assistance under title II of the Trade Act of 1974([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/19/2251 19 U.S.C. 2251 et seq.] )..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H70CB708C6D764768897EAA4E3CB011BF}}(c) Data matching– ==

The [[w:Social Security Administration|Commissioner of Social Security]] shall implement the amendments made by this section using appropriate electronic data.

=={{anchor|HB8B89F6CCB58407DAD1C1255C3877EA9}}(d) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to months after December 2013.
