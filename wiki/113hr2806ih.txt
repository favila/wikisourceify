{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Tariff Act of 1930 to provide that importation of certain containers containing de minimis residual matter shall be excepted from the Customs laws of the United States.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Residue Entries and Streamlining Trade Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Importation of certain containers containing de minimis residual matter to be excepted from the Customs laws of the United States=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 322(a) of the Tariff Act of 1930 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/19/1322 19 U.S.C. 1322(a)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking“(a)”and inserting“(a)(1)”;
:(2) by inserting after“other instruments of international traffic”the following:“, including instruments of international traffic containing de minimis residual matter”; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
::(2) In this subsection, the term“de minimis residual matter”means material remaining in an instrument of international traffic after its contents have been unloaded to the maximum extent practicable and before the instrument of international traffic is either refilled or cleaned. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a) take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act and apply with respect to the importation of instruments of international traffic containing de minimis residual matter on or after such date of enactment.
