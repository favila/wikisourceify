{{Center|A BILL}}

 To improve the transition between experimental permits and commercial licenses for commercial reusable launch vehicles.   

=Section 1. Commercial space launch licensing=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/51/50906  Section 50906of title 51, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/51/50906 subsection (e)(1)], by striking“suborbital rocket design”and inserting“suborbital rocket or rocket design”; and
:(2) by amending[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/51/50906 subsection (g)]to read as follows:

<blockquote> 
::(g) A permit may be issued, and a permit that has already been issued shall remain valid for the uses described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/51/50906 subsection (d)], for a particular reusable suborbital rocket or rocket design after a license has been issued for the launch or reentry of a rocket of that design. . 
</blockquote>
