{{Center|A BILL}}

To exempt the natural aging process in the determination of the production period for distilled spirits under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/263A section 263A] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Aged Distilled Spirits Competitiveness Act” .

=Sec. 2. Exemption of natural aging process in determination of production period for distilled spirits under section263A=



==(a) In general– ==

  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/263A Section 263A(f)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986(relating to general exceptions) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

===(5) Exemption of natural aging process in determination of production period for distilled spirits– ===

For purposes of this subsection, the production period for distilled spirits shall be determined without regard to any period allocated to the natural aging process..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to production periods beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
