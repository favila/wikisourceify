{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to exclude dividends from controlled foreign corporations from the definition of personal holding company income for purposes of the personal holding company rules.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Personal Holding Company Tax Parity and Reinvestment Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Exclusion of dividends from controlled foreign corporations from the definition of personal holding company income for purposes of the personal holding company rules=



==(a) In general– ==

  Paragraph (1) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/543 section 543(a)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended—
:(1) by redesignating subparagraphs (C) and (D) as subparagraphs (D) and (E) , respectively, and
:(2) by inserting after subparagraph (B) the following:

<blockquote> 
::(C) dividends received by a United States shareholder (as defined in section 951(b) ) from a controlled foreign corporation (as defined in section 957(a) ), . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this Act shall apply to taxable years ending on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
