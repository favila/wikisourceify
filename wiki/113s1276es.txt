{{Center|1st Session}}


{{Center|S. 1276}}


{{Center|IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES}}


{{Center|AN ACT}}

To increase oversight of the Revolving Fund of the Office of Personnel Management.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Security Clearance Oversight and Reform Enhancement Act” .

=Sec. 2. Oversight of the Revolving Fund of the Office of Personnel Management=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/1304 Section 1304(e)of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/1304 paragraph (1)] , in the first sentence, by inserting before the period the following: “, and for the cost of audits, investigations, and oversight activities relating to the fund and the functions financed by the fund, conducted by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Inspector General of the Office]] ” ; and
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/1304 paragraph (5)] , by adding at the end the following: “Each budget submitted under this paragraph shall include an estimate from the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Inspector General of the Office]] of the amount required to pay the reasonable expenses to adequately audit, investigate, and perform other oversight activities relating to the fund and the functions financed by the fund for the applicable fiscal year, which shall not exceed 0.33 percent of the total budgetary authority requested in the budget estimates submitted to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Office]] for that fiscal year.” .Passed the Senate October 10, 2013.Secretary
