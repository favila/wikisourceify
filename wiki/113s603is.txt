{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the annual fee on health insurance providers enacted by the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Jobs and Premium Protection Act” .

=Sec. 2. Protecting patients from higher premiums=

 Section 9010 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act([[Public Law -|Public Law 111–148]] ), as amended by section 10905 of such Actand by section 1406 of the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010([[Public Law -|Public Law 111–152]] ), is repealed.
