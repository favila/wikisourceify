{{Center|JOINT RESOLUTION}}

Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of the United States authorizing Congress to prohibit the physical desecration of the flag of the United States.

= =

That the following article is proposed as an amendment to the Constitution of the United States, which shall be valid to all intents and purposes as part of the Constitution when ratified by the legislatures of three-fourths of the several States within 7 years after the date of its submission for ratification:

<blockquote>
 — The [[w:|Congress]] shall have power to prohibit the physical desecration of the flag of the United States..
</blockquote>
