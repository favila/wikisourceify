{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 to provide that military technicians (dual status) shall be included in military personnel accounts for purposes of any order issued under that Act.

={{anchor|HEFA93F293A2942C18512D21FF45B088B}}Section 1. Treatment of military technicians (dual status)=



=={{anchor|H13E93303BBAF49649846598D95AC55A2}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 251(a)(3) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/901 2 U.S.C. 901(a)(3)] )is amended by adding at the end the following new sentence:“For purposes of this paragraph, military technicians (dual status) shall be included in military personnel accounts.”.

=={{anchor|HC8741C992AE94EE6BBE5F9E0C7D607BA}}(b) Application of amendment– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to any order of the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] to exempt military personnel accounts from sequestration issued under section 255(f)(1) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/905 2 U.S.C. 905(f)(1)] )after January 1, 2013.
