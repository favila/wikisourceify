{{Center|A BILL}}

 To make technical corrections to the Pay Our Military Act to include midshipmen at the United States Merchant Marine Academy who are appointed as midshipmen in the Navy Reserve.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Pay Our Military Technical Corrections Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Continuing appropriations for midshipmen at the United States Merchant Marine Academy=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 2 of the Pay Our Military Act ( [[Public Law 113-39|Public Law 113–39]]  )is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a) —
::(A) in paragraph (1) , by inserting“ (including those specified in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/51311  section 51311(b)of title 46, United States Code]) ”after“ reserve components thereof ”; and
::(B) in paragraph (2) —
:::(i) by striking“ (and the Department ”and inserting“ (the Department ”;
:::(ii) by inserting“ and the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Department of Transportation]]in the case of the [[w:|United States Merchant Marine Academy]]  ”after“   [[w:United States Coast Guard|Coast Guard]]  ”; and
:::(iii) by inserting“ or those in training for such service ”before the semicolon; and
:(2) in subsection (b) —
::(A) in paragraph (1) , by striking“ and ”at the end;
::(B) in paragraph (2) , by striking the period at the end and inserting“ ; and ”; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:::(3) the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]]with respect to matters concerning the [[w:|United States Merchant Marine Academy]]. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a) shall take effect as if enacted as part of the  Pay Our Military Act ( [[Public Law 113-39|Public Law 113–39]]  ).
