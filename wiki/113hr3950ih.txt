{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend for one year the credit for energy-efficient existing homes.   

=Section 1. Extension of credit for energy-efficient existing homes=



==(a) In general– ==

  Paragraph (2) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/25C section 25C(g)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“December 31, 2013”and inserting“December 31, 2014”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2013.
