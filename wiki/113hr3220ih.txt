{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to deny Federal retirement benefits to individuals convicted of certain offenses, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Denial of Federal retirement benefits=



==(a) Conviction of certain offenses– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 Section 8312(a)of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(A) by striking “or” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 paragraph (1)] ;
:(B) by striking the period at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 paragraph (2)] and inserting “; or” ; and
:(C) by adding after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 paragraph (2)] the following:

<blockquote>
::(3) was convicted, after the date of the enactment of this paragraph, of an offense named by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 subsection (e)] , to the extent provided by that subsection..
</blockquote>


===(2) Applicability– ===

The last sentence of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 section 8312(a) of such title 5] is amended—
:(A) by striking “and” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 subparagraph (A)] ;
:(B) by striking the period at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 subparagraph (B)] and inserting “; and” ; and
:(C) by adding after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 subparagraph (B)] the following:

<blockquote>
::(C) with respect to the offenses named by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 subsection (e)] , to the period after the date of conviction..
</blockquote>


===(3) Offenses specified– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 Section 8312 of such title 5] is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(e) The following are offenses to which [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 subsection (a)] applies:
::(1) An offense within the purview of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/641 section 641of title 18] , committed after the date of the enactment of this subsection.
::(2) Perjury committed under the statutes of the United States or the District of Columbia in falsely denying the commission of an act which constitutes an offense within the purview of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 paragraph (1)] .
::(3) Subornation of perjury committed in connection with the false denial of another individual as specified by paragraph (3)..
</blockquote>


==(b) Absence from United States To avoid prosecution– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8313 Section 8313(a)(1)of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “or” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8313 subparagraph (A)] ;
:(2) by striking “; and” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8313 subparagraph (B)] and inserting “; or” ; and
:(3) by adding after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8313 subparagraph (B)] the following:

<blockquote>
::(C) after the date of the enactment of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 subsection (e) of section 8312] , for an offense named by such subsection; and.
</blockquote>


==(c) Refund of contributions and deposits– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8316 Section 8316(b)of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “or” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8316 paragraph (1)] ;
:(2) by striking the period at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8316 paragraph (2)] and inserting “; or” ; and
:(3) by adding after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8316 paragraph (2)] the following:

<blockquote>
::(3) if the individual was convicted of an offense named by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8312 subsection (e) of section 8312] , for the period after the conviction..
</blockquote>
