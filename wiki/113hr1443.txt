{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to recognize tinnitus as a mandatory condition for research and treatment by the Department of Veterans Affairs, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H28DDFAB843004067B01458BA4E043F64}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Tinnitus Research and Treatment Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H6C68078BC8BE450A8EC80D00D64884E5}}Sec. 2. Recognition and treatment of tinnitus at Department of Veterans Affairs Auditory Centers of Excellence=

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall recognize tinnitus as a mandatory condition for research and treatment by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs Auditory Centers of Excellence]] .

={{anchor|H4702287FD394423A840ECBDBEA0C456F}}Sec. 3. Research on prevention and treatment of tinnitus=

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall ensure that research is conducted at [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] facilities on the prevention and treatment of tinnitus. Such research shall include—
:(1) an assessment of the efficacy of multidisciplinary tinnitus treatment modalities on different subsets of patients;
:(2) studies on the underlying etiology of tinnitus in veteran populations that occur as a result of different causal factors, including blast-related tinnitus where there is no measurable hearing loss versus other forms of noise-induced tinnitus where there is hearing loss; and
:(3) a study of the underlying mechanisms between hearing loss and tinnitus, including cases in which one or the other condition is present, but not both.

={{anchor|H048FE70EB89D4208ACFE38EC2DA202FE}}Sec. 4. Interdepartmental cooperation=

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall ensure the cooperation of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] with the Hearing Center of Excellence established by the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] to further research on tinnitus.
