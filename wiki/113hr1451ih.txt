{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 14 Main Street in Brockport, New York, as the“Staff Sergeant Nicholas J. Reid Post Office Building”.

={{anchor|H69621AD282474D8EAD71DAFFDBF8D681}}Section 1. Staff Sergeant Nicholas J. Reid Post Office Building=



=={{anchor|H0766EA2576004E609C5EC968F65E1245}}(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] located at 14 Main Street in Brockport, New York, shall be known and designated as the“Staff Sergeant Nicholas J. Reid Post Office Building”.

=={{anchor|HC8BE9CC2D5184FB69075EA6515712017}}(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Staff Sergeant Nicholas J. Reid Post Office Building”.
