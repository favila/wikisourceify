{{Center|A BILL}}

 To strengthen the disclosure requirements for creditors under the Truth in Lending Act.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Online Credit Card Disclosure Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Online payment timing disclosures=

   Section 127(b)(11)(D) of the Truth in Lending Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/1637 15 U.S.C. 1637(b)(11)(D)] )is amended—
:(1) in clause (i), by striking“; and”and inserting a semicolon;
:(2) in clause (ii), by striking the period and inserting“; and”; and
:(3) by inserting at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote> 
::(iii) if the consumer has an online account with the creditor under the consumer credit plan, be disclosed in a conspicuous and prominent location on a webpage of such creditor. . 
</blockquote>
