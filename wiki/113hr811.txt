{{Center|A BILL}}

To add the 9/11 Health and Compensation Programs to the list of exempt programs under PAYGO.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Never Forget 9/11 Heroes Act”.

2. Amendment to exempt programs


=(a) In general=

Section 255(g)(1)(B) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 ( 2 U.S.C. 905(g)(1)(B) ) is amended by—: inserting after the item relating to Retirement Pay and Medical Benefits for Commissioned Officers, [[Wikipedia:United States Public Health Service|Public Health Service ]]the following:
:: September 11th Victim Compensation Fund (15–0340–0–1–754).; and
: inserting after the item relating to the Voluntary Separation Incentive Fund the following:
:: World Trade Center Health Program Fund (75–0946–0–1–551)..

=(b) Fiscal year 2014=

If the amendment made by subsection (a) takes effect after the date the [[Wikipedia:Executive Office of the President|President ]]has issued the final sequester order for fiscal year 2014, the [[Wikipedia:Executive Office of the President|President ]]shall revise the order to account for the amendment and issue a new order.
