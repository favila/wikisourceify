{{Center|A BILL}}

To add the 9/11 Health and Compensation Programs to the
		  list of exempt programs under PAYGO.


{{section|HE53279A09C2B4E3B96C97E6E5187C0C0|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Never Forget 9/11 Heroes
			 Act

        
.

{{section|H89674912BBCE47958B5D674FB3EADEA7|2. Amendment to exempt programs}}
(a)
In
			 general
Section 255(g)(1)(B)
			 of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985
 (2 U.S.C.
			 905(g)(1)(B)
)
 is amended by—
(1)
inserting after the item relating to
			 Retirement Pay and Medical Benefits for Commissioned Officers, [[United States Public Health Service|Public Health
			 Service]] the following:

September
				11th Victim Compensation Fund
				(15–0340–0–1–754).

;
				and


(2)
inserting after
			 the item relating to the Voluntary Separation Incentive Fund the
			 following:

World Trade
				Center Health Program Fund
				(75–0946–0–1–551).

.



(b)
Fiscal year
			 2014
If the amendment made by subsection (a)
 takes effect after
			 the date the [[Executive Office of the President|President]] has issued the final sequester order for fiscal year
			 2014, the [[Executive Office of the President|President]] shall revise the order to account for the amendment and
			 issue a new order.
