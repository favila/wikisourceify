{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the Secretary of the Treasury and the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency from devising or implementing a carbon tax.

={{anchor|H549E4ED9C5344637BBCE6FE536E538F7}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“No Carbon Tax Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H4C15C4D4C99C471A9A9DEF2CC02BA257}}Sec. 2. Carbon tax prohibition=



=={{anchor|HA41164EB839E45409987C7B92F8925AD}}(a) In general– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]] and the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency]] are prohibited from devising or implementing a carbon tax.

=={{anchor|H0588EDA458674196A32EEE1E4A7E9725}}(b) Definition– ==

For purposes of this section, the term“carbon tax”means—
:(1) a tax, fee, or price on emissions, including carbon dioxide emissions generated by the burning of coal, natural gas, or oil; or
:(2) a tax, fee, or price on coal, natural gas, or oil based on emissions, including carbon dioxide emissions that would be generated through the fuel’s combustion.
