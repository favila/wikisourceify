{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Real ID Act of 2005 to repeal provisions requiring uniform State driver’s licenses and State identification cards, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Repeal ID Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal of requirements for uniform State driver's licenses and State identification cards=



==(a) Repeal– ==

   Title II of the Real ID Act of 2005 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/30301  49 U.S.C. 30301note])is repealed.

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==



===(1) Title 18, U.S.C– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1028  Section 1028(a)(8)of title 18, United States Code], is amended by striking“false or actual authentication features”and inserting“false identification features”.

===(2) Intelligence Reform and Terrorism Prevention Act of 2004– ===

   Section 7212 of the Intelligence Reform and Terrorism Prevention Act of 2004 ( [[Public Law 108-458|Public Law 108–458]]  )is amended to read as if section 206 of the Real ID Act , as in effect on the date of the enactment of that Act, were not enacted.
