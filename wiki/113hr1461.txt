{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the renewable fuel program of the Environmental Protection Agency.

={{anchor|HD3DD68D683E04EE480ABE3182AA268E2}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Renewable Fuel Standard Elimination Act”.

={{anchor|HF9D4CBD7CC7C4E789AAD79BBEEA5FC8D}}Sec. 2. Repeal of renewable fuel program=

 Section 211(o) of the Clean Air Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/7545 42 U.S.C. 7545(o)] ] )is repealed.
