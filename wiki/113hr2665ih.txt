{{Center|A BILL}}

To ensure secure gun storage and gun safety devices.

=Section 1. Gun storage and safety devices for all firearms=



==(a) Secure gun storage or safety devices by federal firearms licensees– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 Section 922(z) of title 18, United States Code] , is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>
:(z) It shall be unlawful for any licensed importer, licensed manufacturer, or licensed dealer to sell, transfer, or deliver any firearm to any person (other than a licensed importer, licensed manufacturer, or licensed dealer) unless the transferee is provided with a secure gun storage or safety device..
</blockquote>


==(b) Penalties– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/924 Section 924(p)] of such title is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>
:(p) The [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] may, after notice and opportunity for hearing, suspend or revoke any license issued under this chapter or may subject the licensee to a civil penalty of not more than $10,000 if the holder of such license has knowingly violated [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 section 922(z)] . The actions of the [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] under this subsection may be reviewed only as provided in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 section 923(f)] ..
</blockquote>


==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall be effective 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
