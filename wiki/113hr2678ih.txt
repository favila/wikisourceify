{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 10360 Southwest 186th Street in Miami, Florida, as the Larcenia J. Bullard Post Office Building .

=Section 1. Larcenia J. Bullard Post Office Building=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] located at 10360 Southwest 186th Street in Miami, Florida, shall be known and designated as the Larcenia J. Bullard Post Office Building .

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the Larcenia J. Bullard Post Office Building .
