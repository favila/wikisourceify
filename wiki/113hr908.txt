          Green Mountain Lookout Heritage Protection
			 Act

{{section|H2F39CCF396AB47FB9C2491BD25020343|3. Preservation of Green Mountain Lookout location}}
The [[United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary of Agriculture]], acting through
			 the Chief of the Forest Service, may not move Green Mountain Lookout from its
			 current location on Green Mountain in the Mount Baker-Snoqualmie National
			 Forest unless the [[United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] determines that moving Green Mountain Lookout is
			 necessary to preserve the Lookout or to ensure the safety of individuals on or
			 around Green Mountain. If the [[United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] makes such a determination, the
			 [[United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall move the Green Mountain Lookout to a location outside of the
			 lands described in section 3(5) of the Washington State Wilderness Act of 1984

			 and designated as a wilderness area in section 4(b) of such Act
.
