{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Secretary of Labor to include programs that teach technology literacy in any job training program for ex-offenders offered under the Workforce Investment Act of 1998.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Re-Integration of Ex-Offenders Through Technology Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Inclusion of technology literacy in ex-offender job training programs=

The [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]]shall include programs that teach technology literacy in any job training program for ex-offenders offered under the  Workforce Investment Act of 1998 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2801 29 U.S.C. 2801 et seq].).
