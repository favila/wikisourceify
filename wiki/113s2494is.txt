{{Center|A BILL}}

 To expedite applications to export natural gas, to require the public disclosure of liquefied natural gas export destinations, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Natural Gas Export Promotion Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Applications to export natural gas=



==(a) In general– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary of Energy]](referred to in this section as the“Secretary”) shall issue a final decision on any application for authorization to export natural gas under  section 3 of the Natural Gas Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/717b 15 U.S.C. 717b] )that is pending on or filed on or after the date of enactment of this Act not later than 45 days after conclusion of the review required by the  National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/4321 42 U.S.C. 4321 et seq.]).

==(b) Judicial review– ==



===(1) In general– ===

Except for review in the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Supreme Court]], the [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit|United States Court of Appeals]]for the circuit in which the export facility will be located pursuant to an application described in subsection (a) shall have original and exclusive jurisdiction over any civil action for the review of—
:(A) an order issued by the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary]]with respect to the application; or
:(B) the failure of the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary]]to issue a decision on the application.

===(2) Order– ===

If the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]]in a civil action described in paragraph (1) finds that the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary]]has failed to issue a decision on the application as required under subsection (a) , the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]]shall order the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary]]to issue the decision not later than 30 days after the order of the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]].

===(3) Expedited consideration– ===

The [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]]shall—
:(A) set any civil action brought under this subsection for expedited consideration; and
:(B) set the matter on the docket as soon as practicable after the filing date of the initial pleading. 

=Sec. 3. Public disclosure of export destinations=

   Section 3 of the Natural Gas Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/717b 15 U.S.C. 717b] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(g) Public disclosure of liquefied natural gas export destinations– ==



===(1) In general– ===

In the case of any authorization to export liquefied natural gas, the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary of Energy]]shall require the applicant to report to the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary of Energy]]the names of the one or more countries of destination to which the exported liquefied natural gas is delivered.

===(2) Timing– ===

The applicant shall file the report required under paragraph (1) not later than—
:(A) in the case of the first export, the last day of the month following the month of the first export; and
:(B) in the case of subsequent exports, the date that is 30 days after the last day of the applicable month concerning the activity of the previous month.

===(3) Disclosure– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary of Energy]]shall publish the information reported under this subsection on the website of the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Department of Energy]]and otherwise make the information available to the public. . 
</blockquote>
