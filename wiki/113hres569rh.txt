{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 Providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 4438) to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to simplify and make permanent the research credit.   

= =

That upon adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to consider in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]]the bill ( H.R. 4438 ) to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to simplify and make permanent the research credit. All points of order against consideration of the bill are waived. The amendment in the nature of a substitute recommended by the [[w:United States House Committee on Ways and Means|Committee on Ways and Means]]now printed in the bill shall be considered as adopted. The bill, as amended, shall be considered as read. All points of order against provisions in the bill, as amended, are waived. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the bill, as amended, and on any amendment thereto to final passage without intervening motion except: (1) 90 minutes of debate equally divided and controlled by the chair and ranking minority member of the [[w:United States House Committee on Ways and Means|Committee on Ways and Means]]; and (2) one motion to recommit with or without instructions.
