{{Center|A BILL}}

 To extend the Public Interest Declassification Act of 2000.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Public Interest Declassification Board Reauthorization Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension=

   Section 710(b) of the Public Interest Declassification Act of 2000 ( [[Public Law 106-567|Public Law 106–567]]   [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/50/3161 ;50 U.S.C. 3161note])is amended by striking“2014.”and inserting“2015.”.
