{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act to grant to States on the Gulf of Mexico jurisdiction over fisheries out to 9 nautical miles from shore, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   State Fisheries Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. State jurisdiction over fisheries in the Gulf of Mexico=

   Section 306(b) ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1856 16 U.S.C. 1856(b)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:(3) Notwithstanding section 3(11) and subsection (a) of this section , for purposes of managing fisheries in the Gulf of Mexico, the seaward boundary of a coastal State in the Gulf of Mexico is a line 9 nautical miles seaward from the baseline from which the territorial sea of the United States is measured. . 
</blockquote>
