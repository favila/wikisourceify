{{Center|A BILL}}

 To permit health insurance issuers to offer additional plan options to individuals.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Removing Limitations on Insurance Effectiveness and Flexibility Act of 2013 ”or the“ReLIEF Act”. 

=Sec. 2. Authority to offer additional plan options=



==(a) Catastrophic plans– ==

Notwithstanding  title I of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ( [[Public Law 111-148|Public Law 111–148]]  ), a catastrophic plan as described in section 1302(e) of such Act shall be deemed to be a qualified health plan (including for purposes of receiving tax credits under  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/36B section 36B]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 and cost-sharing assistance under section 1402 of this Act ), except that for purposes of enrollment in such plans, the provisions of paragraph (2) of such section 1302(e) shall not apply.

==(b) Individual mandate– ==

Coverage under a catastrophic plan under subsection (a) shall be deemed to be minimum essential coverage for purposes of  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5000A section 5000A]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 .
