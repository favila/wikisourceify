{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit funding to the Voice of America.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on funding the Voice of America=

No funds available to the [[w:United States Department of State|Department of State]]or any other department or agency may be used to provide contributions directly or indirectly to the Voice of America.
