{{Center|A BILL}}

To permit the televising of Supreme Court proceedings.

={{anchor|H9942F3CE4EBB41F1A0289E4E2DC56F59}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Cameras in the Courtroom Act”.

={{anchor|HBB11D06DF5FC44FAABAC84296121BF5B}}Sec. 2. Amendment to title 28=



=={{anchor|HA5153D9C138B4A06851CF454FA0DB1BA}}(a) In general– ==

Chapter 45 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H85C166CA21824E1585C20166CE0FA8F8}}Sec. 678. Televising Supreme Court proceedings==

The [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Supreme Court]] shall permit television coverage of all open sessions of the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]] unless the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]] decides, by a vote of the majority of justices, that allowing such coverage in a particular case would constitute a violation of the due process rights of one or more of the parties before the [[w:Supreme Court of the United States|Court]] ..
</blockquote>
