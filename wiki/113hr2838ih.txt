{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 46, United States Code, with respect to coastwise endorsements and Puerto Rico, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Puerto Rico Interstate Commerce Improvement Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Coastwise endorsements and Puerto Rico=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/12120 Section 12120 of title 46, United States Code], is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 12120. Coastwise endorsements and Puerto Rico==



===(a) Exemption from requirements– ===

With respect to engaging in the coastwise trade, no requirement under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/12112 section 12112(a)(2)]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/12132 section 12132]applies to a vessel that—
:(1) is self propelled;
:(2) is transporting bulk cargo, including liquified natural gas or liquified petroleum gas bulk cargo; and
:(3) is transporting such cargo—
::(A) to Puerto Rico from another port in the United States; or
::(B) from Puerto Rico to another port in the United States.

===(b) Bulk cargo defined– ===

In this section, the term bulk cargo has the meaning given that term in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/53101 section 53101]. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The analysis for chapter 121of title 46, United States Code, is amended by striking the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/46/12120 section 12120]and inserting the following:

<blockquote>  12120. Coastwise endorsements and Puerto Rico.  . 
</blockquote>
