{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit land management modifications relating to the Lesser Prairie Chicken.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on land management modifications relating to Lesser Prairie Chicken=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law (including regulations), the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary of Agriculture]]and the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]]shall not implement or limit any modification to a public or private land-related policy or subsurface mineral right-related policy or practice that is in effect on the date of enactment of this Act relating to the listing of the Lesser Prairie Chicken as a threatened species or endangered species under the  Endangered Species Act of 1973 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1531 16 U.S.C. 1531 et seq.] ).
