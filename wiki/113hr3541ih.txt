=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Obamacare Taxpayer Bailout Prevention Act” .

=Sec. 2. Prevention of bailout of health insurance issuers=

The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Actis amended by striking section 1342( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18062 42 U.S.C. 18062] ).
