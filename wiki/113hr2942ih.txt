{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to reestablish the Professional Certification and Licensure Advisory Committee of the Department of Veterans Affairs.

=Section 1. Reestablishment of Professional Certification and Licensure Advisory Committee=



==(a) Reestablishment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3689 Section 3689(e)(5)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “December 31, 2006” and inserting “December 31, 2019” .

==(b) Appointment of new members– ==

In reestablishing the Professional Certification and Licensure Advisory Committee under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3689 subsection (e) ofsection 3689of title 38, United States Code] , pursuant to subsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] may appoint new members of the Committee under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3689 paragraph (3)(A) of such subsection (e)] without regard to the individuals who served as members before the date of the enactment of this Act.
