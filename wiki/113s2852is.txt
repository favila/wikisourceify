{{Center|A BILL}}

 To clarify membership requirements for the Board of Directors of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   State Regulatory Representation Clarification Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Purposes=

The purposes of this Act are—
:(1) to reaffirm the importance of having the perspective of State banking regulators on the [[w:Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation|Board of Directors of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation]]; and
:(2) to ensure that the [[w:Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation|Board of Directors of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation]]includes a member who has served as a State banking supervisor. 

=Sec. 3. State banking supervisor experience on Board of Directors of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation=

   Section 2(a)(1)(C) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/1812 12 U.S.C. 1812(a)(1)(C)] )is amended by striking“State bank supervisory experience”and inserting“served as a State bank supervisor, as defined in section 3(r)(1) of this Act  ”. 

=Sec. 4. Rule of construction=

Nothing in this Act shall be construed as affecting the appointment or reappointment of individuals who were members of the [[w:Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation|Board of Directors of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation]]as of January 1, 2014.
