{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to extend the reduced interest rate for Federal Direct Stafford Loans.

={{anchor|HF1B86B9F24A64C728B043DC4FDB19C6C}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Student Loan Relief Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HF6359BAAEE9047E8AA336025ADEA8F9A}}Sec. 2. Interest rate extension=

 Subparagraph (D) of section 455(b)(7) of the Higher Education Act of 1965([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e(b)(7)] ] )is amended—
:(1) in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking“2013”and inserting“2015”; and
:(2) in clause (v), by striking“2013”and inserting“2015”.
