{{Center|A BILL}}

To extend the authorization of appropriations for allocation to carry out approved wetlands conservation projects under the North American Wetlands Conservation Act through fiscal year 2017.

={{anchor|HD81CB67076DA498EA870243EE19E9302}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“North American Wetlands Conservation Extension Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H430DD526E7FD4AEB8452CBDF5ECC9194}}Sec. 2. Authorization of appropriations=

 Section 7(c) of the North American Wetlands Conservation Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/4406 16 U.S.C. 4406(c)] ] )is amended by striking“2012”and inserting“2017”.
