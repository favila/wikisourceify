{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Personal Responsibility and Work Opportunity
		  Reconciliation Act of 1996 to repeal the denial of food stamp eligibility of
		  ex-offenders.

={{anchor|HDA21ABBBAC0546EEAE8BFCFB78AB22A0}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Food Assistance to Improve
			 Reintegration Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HD51DCFD2D8154F73BFB95AE5B5F636D8}}Sec. 2. Repeal of denial
			 of benefits=

Section 115 of the
			 Personal Responsibility and Work Opportunity Reconciliation Act of 1996 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/21/862a 21
			 U.S.C. 862a]) is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a)
			 by striking paragraph (2);
:(2) in subsection (b)
			 by striking paragraph (2); and
:(3) in subsection (e)
			 by striking paragraph (2).
