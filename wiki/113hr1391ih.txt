{{Center|A BILL}}

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 25 South Oak Street in London, Ohio, as the“Lance Corporal Joshua B. McDaniels and Veterans Memorial Post Office Building”.

={{anchor|H0995290A827D4DEC8CFAEC4C32D2C032}}Section 1. Lance Corporal Joshua B. McDaniels and Veterans Memorial Post Office Building=



=={{anchor|H9A0C3235A3EF4A30B4D0A5426D8AF341}}(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] located at 25 South Oak Street in London, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the“Lance Corporal Joshua B. McDaniels and Veterans Memorial Post Office Building”.

=={{anchor|H35204F70B1C648DEA3E2FEE2D05F3451}}(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Lance Corporal Joshua B. McDaniels and Veterans Memorial Post Office Building”.
