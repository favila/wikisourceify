{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require notification of a Governor of a State if an unaccompanied alien child is placed in a facility or with a sponsor in the State and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   UAC State Notification Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Notification of State Governors=

   Section 235 of the William Wilberforce Trafficking Victims Protection Reauthorization Act of 2008 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1232 8 U.S.C. 1232] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(j) Notification to States– ==

The [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary of Homeland Security]]or the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary of Health and Human Services]]shall notify the Governor of a State not later than 48 hours prior to the placement of an unaccompanied alien child in the custody of such Secretary in the care of a facility or sponsor in such State. . 
</blockquote>
