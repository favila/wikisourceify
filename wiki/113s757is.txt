{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the implementation of the multispecies habitat conservation plan for the Virgin River, Nevada, and Lincoln County, Nevada, to extend the authority to purchase certain parcels of public land, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Multispecies Habitat Conservation Plan Implementation Act” .

=Sec. 2. Implementation of multispecies habitat conservation plans=



==(a) Virgin River, Nevada– ==

[[Public Law 99-548|Section 3(d)(3)(B) of[[Public Law -|Public Law 99–548]] ]] (100 Stat. 3061,116 Stat. 2018)is amended by inserting “and implementation” after “development” .

==(b) Lincoln County, Nevada– ==

 Section 5(b)(1)(B) of the Lincoln County Land Act of 2000([[Public Law -|Public Law 106–298]] ;114 Stat. 1048), is amended by inserting “and implementation” after “development” .

=Sec. 3. Extension of purchase authority and withdrawals=

[[Public Law 99-548|Section 3 of[[Public Law -|Public Law 99–548]] ]] (100 Stat. 3061,113 Stat. 1501A–166)is amended—
:(1) in[[Public Law 99-548|subsection (e)]] —
::(A) in[[Public Law 99-548|paragraph (1)(A)]] , by striking “For a period of 12 years after the date of the enactment of this Act” and inserting “Until November 29, 2021” ;
::(B) in[[Public Law 99-548|paragraph (3)]] , by striking “Not later than 10 years after the date of the enactment of this subsection” and inserting “Not later than November 29, 2021” ; and
::(C) in[[Public Law 99-548|paragraph (5)]] , by striking “the date that is 12 years after the date of the enactment of this subsection” and inserting “November 29, 2021” ; and
:(2) in[[Public Law 99-548|subsection (f)(3)]] , by striking “the date that is 12 years after the date of the enactment of this subsection” and inserting “November 29, 2021” .
