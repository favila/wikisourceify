To amend title 38, United States Code, to require the Secretary of Veterans Affairs, in cases of overpayments of educational assistance under Post-9/11 Educational Assistance, to deduct amounts for repayment from the last months of educational assistance entitlement, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Delayed repayment of overpayments made under Post-9/11 Educational Assistance=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 Section 5314 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 subsections (c) and (d)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 subsections (d) and (e)] , respectively;
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 subsection (a)] , by striking “to subsections (b) and (d)” and inserting “to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 subsections (b)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 (c)] , and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 (e)] ” ; and
:(3) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 subsection (b)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 subsection (c)] :

<blockquote>
::(c) 
:::(1) Subject to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 paragraph (2)] , in the case of a deduction under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 subsection (a)] with respect to an overpayment made by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] to a person underchapter 33 of this title, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall, to the degree practicable, make such deduction from the last payment or payments (as necessary) to which such person is entitled under such chapter.
:::(2)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 Paragraph (1)] shall no longer apply to, and amounts shall be deducted as otherwise provided in this section with respect to, a person described in such paragraph who, after the overpayment described in such paragraph—
::::(A) completes the program of education with respect to which the overpayment was made; or
::::(B) fails, during the next two academic semesters beginning after such overpayment, to attend classes in a manner consistent with normal pursuit of a program of education.
:::(3) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may charge an individual described in paragraph (1) such amount of interest on the amount of indebtedness as is necessary so that the delayed repayment is actuarially equivalent to immediate repayment.
:::(4) This subsection shall expire on the date that is nine years after the date of the enactment of this subsection..
</blockquote>


==(b) Applicability– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5314 Subsection (c) of section 5314 of such title] , as added by subsection (a), shall apply with respect to overpayments made on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
