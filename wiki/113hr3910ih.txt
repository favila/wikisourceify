{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 39, United States Code, to allow the United States Postal Service to provide nonpostal services, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Expand Nonpostal Services to Americans Act” .

=Sec. 2. Authority to offer nonpostal services=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/404 Section 404of title 39, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/404 subsection (a)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/404 paragraph (7)] , by striking “and” at the end;
::(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/404 paragraph (8)] , by striking the period and inserting “; and” ; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:::(9) to provide nonpostal services.; and
</blockquote>

:(2) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/404 subsection (e)] .
