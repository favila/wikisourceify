{{Center|A BILL}}

 To increase the mileage reimbursement rate for members of the armed services during permanent change of station and to authorize the transportation of additional motor vehicles of members on change of permanent station to or from nonforeign areas outside the continental United States.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Service Members Permanent Change of Station Relief Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Mileage reimbursement rate for members of the uniformed services for travel related to change of permanent station=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/474  Section 474(d)(1)(A)of title 37, United States Code], is amended by striking“monetary allowance”and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting the following: “monetary allowance in place of the cost of transportation—

<blockquote> 
:(i) in the case of a member for whom travel has been authorized in connection with a change of permanent station or for travel described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/474 paragraph (2)]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/474 (3) of subsection (a)], at the business standard mileage rate set by the Internal Revenue Service pursuant to section 1.274.5(j)(2) of title 26, Code of Federal Regulations; and 
:(ii) in the case of a member's dependent for whom such travel has been authorized, at the rate provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5704 section 5704 of title 5]. . 
</blockquote>
 

=Sec. 3. Transportation of additional motor vehicle of members on change of permanent station to or from nonforeign areas outside the continental United States=



==(a) Authority To transport additional motor vehicle– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2634 Subsection (a) of section 2634 of title 10, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by striking the sentence following[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2634 paragraph (4)];
:(2) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2634 paragraphs (1), (2), (3), and (4)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2634 subparagraphs (A), (B), (C), and (D)], respectively;
:(3) by inserting“(1)”after“(a)”; and
:(4) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
::(2) One additional motor vehicle of a member (or a dependent of the member) may be transported as provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2634 paragraph (1)]if—
:::(A) the member is ordered to make a change of permanent station to or from a nonforeign area outside the continental United States and the member has at least one dependent of driving age who will use the motor vehicle; or
:::(B) the Secretary concerned determines that a replacement for the motor vehicle transported under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2634 paragraph (1)]is necessary for reasons beyond the control of the member and is in the interest of the United States and the Secretary approves the transportation in advance. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Technical and conforming amendments– ==

Such subsection is further amended—
:(1) by striking“his dependents”and inserting“a dependent of the member”;
:(2) by striking“him”and inserting“the member”;
:(3) by striking“his)”and inserting“the member)”;
:(4) by striking“his new”and inserting“the member’s new”; and
:(5) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2634 paragraph (1)(C)], as redesignated by subsection (a)—
::(A) by striking“clauses (1) and (2)”and inserting“subparagraphs (A) and (B)”; and
::(B) by inserting“or”after the semicolon. 

=Sec. 4. Effective date=

The amendments made by this Act shall take effect on January 1, 2014, and apply with respect to a permanent change of station order issued on or after that date to a member of the uniformed services.
