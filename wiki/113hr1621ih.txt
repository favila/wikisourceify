{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make permanent the 2010 increase in the deduction for start-up expenditures.

={{anchor|HBA87AC87A85A4A61B38CB2A043080322}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Help Entrepreneurs Create American Jobs Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HBFB01657916B44F1924F60B28613472E}}Sec. 2. Increase in deduction for start-up expenses made permanent=



=={{anchor|HED025E1341BA4C2C96DC88552936646C}}(a) In general– ==

 Clause (ii) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 195(b)(1)(A)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended—
:(1) by striking“$5,000”and inserting“$10,000”, and
:(2) by striking“$50,000”and inserting“$60,000”.

=={{anchor|H2A8CD3C71F4F4847BF52E951EA8A216A}}(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 Subsection (b) of section 195 of such Codeis amended by striking paragraph (3).

=={{anchor|H681272F75C834723973E93F19AA21163}}(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
