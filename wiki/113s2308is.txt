{{Center|A BILL}}

 To designate Union Station in Washington, DC, as“Harry S. Truman Union Station”.   

=Section 1. Harry S. Truman Union Station=



==(a) Designation– ==

Union Station, located at 40 Massachusetts Avenue, NE., Washington, DC, shall be known and designated as“Harry S. Truman Union Station”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to insubsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Harry S. Truman Union Station”.
