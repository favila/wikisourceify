{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to transport individuals to and from facilities of the Department of Veterans Affairs in connection with rehabilitation, counseling, examination, treatment, and care, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|id88217F35CC634EF68281C2FCF5398626}}Section 1. Transportation of individuals to and from facilities of Department of Veterans Affairs=



=={{anchor|id320DBA7A041E4BA7B8A8E6D4075BC939}}(a) In general– ==

Chapter 1 of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/111 section 111] the following new section:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|id4F4C03833A7F43C08363B8A6A0EA9074}}Sec. 111A. Transportation of individuals to and from Department facilities==



==={{anchor|idFB16888EC91E4D7698B46AE5F3E46119}}(a) Transportation by Secretary– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may transport any person to or from a [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]] facility or other place in connection with vocational rehabilitation, counseling required by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] pursuant tochapter 34or35 of this title, or for the purpose of examination, treatment, or care..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|id0F84FF5119AC44149656757A1D0F0E4D}}(b) Conforming amendment– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/111 Subsection (h) of section 111 of such title] is—
:(1) transferred to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/111A section 111A of such title] , as added by subsection (a);
:(2) redesignated as subsection (b);
:(3) inserted after subsection (a)of such section; and
:(4) amended by inserting“Transportation by third-Parties.—”before“The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] ”.

=={{anchor|idE294CEDFCCD94777818A5E5097C644AF}}(c) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning ofchapter 1 of such titleis amended by inserting after the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/111 section 111] the following new item:

<blockquote>__NOTOC__
111A. Transportation of individuals to and from Department facilities..
</blockquote>
