{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 49, United States Code, with respect to employee protective arrangements, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Employee protective arrangements=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5333 Section 5333 of title 49, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “(a)Prevailing wages requirement.—” ; and
:(2) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5333 subsection (b)] .

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==



===(1) Formula grants for rural areas– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5311 Section 5311(i)of title 49, United States Code] , is repealed.

===(2) Administrative provisions– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5334 Section 5334(a)(1) of title 49, United States Code] , is amended by striking “(except terms the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]] prescribes under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5333 section 5333(b)] of this title)” .
