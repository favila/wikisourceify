{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, regarding restrictions on abortions at medical facilities of the Department of Defense.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Military Access to Reproductive Care and Health for Military Women Act” or the “MARCH for Military Women Act” .

=Sec. 2. Restoration of previous policy regarding restrictions on use of Department of Defense medical facilities=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1093 Section 1093 of title 10, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1093 subsection (b)] ; and
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1093 subsection (a)] , by striking “(a)Restriction on use of funds.—” .
