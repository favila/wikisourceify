{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve and make permanent the Department of Veterans Affairs loan guarantee for the purchase of residential cooperative housing units, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Fair Access to Co-ops for Veterans Act” .

=Sec. 2. Improvement of Department of Veterans Affairs loan guarantee for purchase of residential cooperative housing units=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 Section 3710 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 subsection (a)(12)] , by striking “and before the date that is five years after that date,” ; and
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 subsection (h)] —
::(A) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 paragraphs (2)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 (3)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 paragraphs (3)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 (4)] , respectively; and
::(B) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 paragraph (1)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 paragraph (2)] :

<blockquote>
:::(2) In prescribing regulations to carry out [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 subsection (a)(12)] the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall ensure that a loan for the purchase of stock or membership in a cooperative housing corporation that has been reviewed and approved by a lender approved by the [[w:Fannie Mae|Federal National Mortgage Association]] is not denied..
</blockquote>


==(b) Authority To advertise– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall use the authority of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/532 section 532 of title 38, United States Code] , to advertise the availability of loan guarantees for housing cooperative share loans under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3710 section 3710(a)(12)] of such title and shall take such other appropriate actions as may be necessary to notify eligible veterans, participating lenders, and interested realtors of the availability of such loan guarantees and the procedures and requirements that apply to the obtaining of such guarantees.
