{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend to all veterans with a serious service-connected injury eligibility to participate in the family caregiver services program.

={{anchor|id2711BCA6F9124A719D80DE876C3DA62A}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Caregivers Expansion and Improvement Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|id8E5F907B509E4B21ACBA53B1A750C351}}Sec. 2. Extension to all veterans with a serious service-connected disability of eligibility for participation in family caregiver program=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 1720G(a)(2)(B)] of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking“on or after September 11, 2001”.
