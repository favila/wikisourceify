To amend the Fish and Wildlife Act of 1956 to reauthorize the volunteer programs and community partnerships for the benefit of national wildlife refuges, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Reauthorization of National Wildlife Refuge System volunteer, community partnership, and education programs=

 Section 7(g) of the Fish and Wildlife Act of 1956( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/742f 16 U.S.C. 742f(g)] )is amended by striking “2011 through 2014” and inserting “2015 through 2017” .
