{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend[[Public Law -|Public Law 94–241]]with respect to the Northern Mariana Islands.   

=Section 1. Amendments=

  [[Public Law 94-241|Public Law 94–241]] (90 Stat. 263;122 Stat. 854)is amended—
:(1) in[[Public Law 94-241|subsection (a)(2)]] , by striking“December 31, 2014, except as provided in subsections (b) and (d)”and inserting“December 31, 2019”; and
:(2) in[[Public Law 94-241|subsection (d)]] —
::(A) in the third sentence of[[Public Law 94-241|paragraph (2)]] , by striking“not to extend beyond December 31, 2014, unless extended pursuant to paragraph 5 of this subsection”and inserting“ending on December 31, 2019”;
::(B) by striking[[Public Law 94-241|paragraph (5)]] ; and
::(C) by redesignating[[Public Law 94-241|paragraph (6)]] as[[Public Law 94-241|paragraph (5)]] .
