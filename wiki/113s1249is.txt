{{Center|A BILL}}

To rename the Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking of the Department of State the Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons and to provide for an Assistant Secretary to head such Bureau, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Redesignation of office=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 105(e) of the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/7103 22 U.S.C. 7103(e)] ) is amended—
:(1) in the subsection heading, by striking “Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] ” ;
:(2) in paragraph (1)—
::(A) in the first sentence, by striking “Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] ” ;
::(B) in the second sentence, by striking “Office” and inserting “Bureau” ; and
::(C) in the sixth sentence, by striking “Office” and inserting “Bureau” ; and
:(3) in paragraph (2)(A), by striking “Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] ” .

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==


:(1)  Section 112A(b)(3) of the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/7109a 22 U.S.C. 7109a(b)(3)] ) is amended by striking “Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] ” .
:(2)  Section 113(a) of the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/7110 22 U.S.C. 7110(a)] ) is amended by striking “Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] ” .
:(3)  Section 105 of the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/7112 22 U.S.C. 7112] ) is amended—
::(A) by striking “Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking” both places it appears and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] ” ; and
::(B) in subsection (a)(2), by striking “focus of the Office” and inserting “focus of the Bureau” .
:(4)  Section 708(a) of the Foreign Service Act of 1980( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/4028 22 U.S.C. 4028(a)] ) is amended by striking “Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] ” .

=Sec. 2. Assistant Secretary of the Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 105(e) of the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/7103 22 U.S.C. 7103(e)] ), as amended by section 1(a), is further amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1)—
::(A) by striking “Director” each place it appears and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Assistant Secretary]] ” ; and
::(B) by striking “, with the rank of Ambassador-at-Large” ; and
:(2) in paragraph (2), by striking “Director” both places it appears and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Assistant Secretary]] ” .

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==


:(1)  Section 112A(b)(3) of the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/7109a 22 U.S.C. 7109a(b)(3] )), as amended by section 1(b)(1), is further amended by striking “Director” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Assistant Secretary]] ” .
:(2)  Section 105(a)(2) of the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000(22 U.S.C. 7112(a)(2)), as amended by section 1(b)(3), is further amended by striking “Director” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Assistant Secretary]] ” .
:(3)  Section 708(a) of the Foreign Service Act of 1980( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/4028 22 U.S.C. 4028(a)] ), as amended by section 1(b)(4), is further amended by striking “Director” and inserting “ [[w:United States Department of State|Assistant Secretary]] ” .

==(c) Increase in authorized Assistant Secretary positions– ==


:(1)  Section 1(c)(1) of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/2651a 22 U.S.C. 2651a(c)(1)] ) is amended by striking “not more than 24 Assistant Secretaries” and inserting “not more than 25 Assistant Secretaries” .
:(2)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5315 Section 5315of title 5, United States Code] , is amended by striking “Assistant Secretaries of State (24)” and inserting “Assistant Secretaries of State (25)” .

=Sec. 3. References=



==(a) Office To Monitor and Combat Trafficking– ==

Any reference to the [[w:United States Department of State|Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking]] in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be deemed to be a reference to the [[w:United States Department of State|Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] .

==(b) Assistant Secretary of the Bureau To Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons– ==

Any reference to the [[w:United States Department of State|Director of the Office to Monitor and Combat Trafficking]] in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be deemed to be a reference to the [[w:United States Department of State|Assistant Secretary of the Bureau to Monitor and Combat Trafficking in Persons]] .
