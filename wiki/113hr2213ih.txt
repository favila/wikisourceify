{{Center|A BILL}}

To incorporate into the design and construction of reconfigured and new ports of entry certain concerns relating to border location-dependent businesses, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Border Communities Economic Security and Sustainability Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Incorporation into the design and construction of reconfigured and new ports of entry of certain concerns relating to border location-dependent businesses=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary of Homeland Security]] and the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator of the General Services Administration]] , as an ongoing part of the efforts of the [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Department of Homeland Security]] and the [[w:General Services Administration|General Services Administration]] to reconfigure and make more secure the country’s ports of entry, shall coordinate to the extent practicable to ensure that appropriate measures are taken to accommodate preexisting and border location-dependent businesses into the design and construction of reconfigured and new ports of entry.
