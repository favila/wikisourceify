To suspend temporarily the duty on Disperse Red 92.

={{anchor|HAA60A0A1DB004D39B393A8B288161A82}}Section 1. Disperse Red 92=



=={{anchor|H76C6717677CB4AB9A6D58520F9B2863D}}(a) In general– ==

Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States is amended by inserting in numerical sequence the following new heading:

<blockquote>9902.01.00Disperse Red 92 (CAS No. 72363–26–9) (provided for in subheading 3204.11.50)FreeNo changeNo changeOn or before 12/31/2016.
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HCE58A770ED884F3CA0919973C20FB7E8}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)applies to articles entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
