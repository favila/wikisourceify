{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Small Business Jobs Act of 2010 to extend and expand the State Trade and Export Promotion (STEP) Grant Program.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Small Business Growth Through Exports Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension and Expansion of STEP grant program=

   Section 1207 of the Small Business Jobs Act of 2010 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/649b  15 U.S.C. 649bnote])is amended—
:(1) in subsection (i) , by striking“ 2013 ”and inserting“    2013, and $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014, 2015, and 2016   ”; and
:(2) in subsection (j) , by striking“ 3 ”and inserting“ 6 ”.
