{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide replacement automobiles for certain disabled veterans and members of the Armed Forces, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Replacement automobiles for certain disabled veterans and members of Armed Forces=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3903 Section 3903of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3903 subsection (a)] and inserting the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3903 subsection (a)] :

<blockquote>
:(a) 
::(1) Subject to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3903 paragraph (2)] and except as provided in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3903 paragraph (3)] , an eligible person entitled to receive an automobile or other conveyance under the provisions of this chapter is entitled—
:::(A) to receive only one automobile or other conveyance under the provisions of this chapter; and
:::(B) to replace such automobile or other conveyance twice under the provisions of this chapter.
::(2) Except as provided in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3903 paragraph (3)] , the aggregate amount that the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may expend under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3902 subsection (a) of section 3902] of this title for an eligible person (including amounts expended for replacement automobiles or other conveyances) shall not exceed the amount set forth in such subsection (as adjusted from time to time under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3902 subsection (e)] of such section).
::(3) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may provide or assist in providing an eligible person with a replacement automobile or other conveyance under this chapter that is not subject to the limitations of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3903 paragraphs (1)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3903 (2)] and does not count against the eligible person's entitlement under this chapter if—
:::(A) the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] receives satisfactory evidence that the automobile or other conveyance of the eligible person previously purchased with assistance under this chapter was destroyed—
::::(i) as a result of a natural or other disaster, as determined by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] ; and
::::(ii) through no fault of the eligible person; and
:::(B) the eligible person does not otherwise receive from a property insurer compensation for the loss.
::(4) No payment shall be made under this chapter for the repair or maintenance of an automobile or other conveyance..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 2. Codification of cost-of-living adjustment in amounts of assistance for providing automobiles for certain disabled veterans and members of the Armed Forces=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3902 Section 3902(a)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “$18,900” and inserting “$30,000” .
