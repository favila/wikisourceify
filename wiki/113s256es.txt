To amend[[Public Law -|Public Law 93–435]] with respect to the Northern Mariana Islands, providing parity with Guam, the Virgin Islands, and American Samoa.

=Section 1. Amendment=


:(a) In general– The[[Public Law 93-435|first section and section 2 of Public Law 93–435]] ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/48/1705 48 U.S.C. 1705] , 1706) are amended by inserting “the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands,” after “Guam,” each place it appears.
:(b) References to date of enactment– For the purposes of the amendment made by subsection (a), each reference in[[Public Law -|[[Public Law 93-435|Public Law 93–435]] ]] to the “date of enactment” shall be considered to be a reference to the date of the enactment of this section.

=Sec. 2. Adjustment of scheduled wage increases in the commonwealth of the northern mariana islands=

 Section 8103(b)(1)(B) of the Fair Minimum Wage Act of 2007( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/206 29 U.S.C. 206] note;[[Public Law -|[[Public Law -|Public Law 110–28]] ]] ) is amended by striking “2011” and inserting “2011, 2013, and 2015” .Passed the Senate August 1, 2013.Secretary
