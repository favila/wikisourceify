{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to raise the limitation on the election to accelerate the AMT credit in lieu of bonus depreciation for 2013.

=Section 1. Temporary increase in limitation on election to accelerate the AMT credit in lieu of bonus depreciation=



==(a) In general– ==

 Subparagraph (J) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 168(k)(4)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote>

===(v) Special maximum increase amount for 2013– ===

In the case of round 3 extension property placed in service by a corporation—
:(I)  subparagraph (C)(iii)shall not apply, and
:(II) the term maximum increase amount means an amount that is 50 percent of the AMT credit increase amount determined with respect to such corporation under subparagraph (E)..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2012, in taxable years ending after such date.
