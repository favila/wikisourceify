{{Center|H. CON. RES. 75}}

 
{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}

 
{{Center|CONCURRENT RESOLUTION}}

 Providing for a joint session of Congress to receive a message from the President.    That the two Houses of Congress assemble in the Hall of the House of Representatives on Tuesday, January 28, 2014, at 9 p.m., for the purpose of receiving such communication as the [[w:President of the United States|President of the United States]]shall be pleased to make to them.     Passed the House of Representatives January 16, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
