{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 18, United States Code, to provide a penalty for knowingly selling advertising that offers certain commercial sex acts.    

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Stop Advertising Victims of Exploitation Act of 2014 ”or the“ SAVE Act of 2014 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Advertising that offers certain commercial sex acts=



<blockquote> 

=Sec. 1591A. Advertising that offers certain commercial sex acts=



==(a) Offense– ==

Whoever, in or affecting interstate or foreign commerce, knowingly benefits financially from, receives anything of value from, or distributes advertising that offers a commercial sex act in a manner prohibited under section 1591, shall be fined under this title, imprisoned not more than 5 years, or both.

==(b) Definitions– ==

In this section, the terms have the meanings given those terms in section 1591. . 
</blockquote>
 

=Sec. 3. Extra-territorial jurisdiction=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1596 Section 1596]of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking“or 1591”and inserting“1591, or 1591A”. 

=Sec. 4. Clerical amendment=

The table of sections at the beginning ofchapter 77of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591 section 1591]the following:

<blockquote>  1591A. Advertising that offers certain commercial sex acts.  . 
</blockquote>
   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Stop Advertising Victims of Exploitation Act of 2014   ”or the“SAVE Act of 2014”. 

=Sec. 2. Advertising that offers certain commercial sex acts=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591  Section 1591of title 18, United States Code], is amended in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591 subsection (a)(1)], by inserting after“obtains,”the following:“advertises,”.

==(b) Mens rea requirement– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591  Section 1591of title 18, United States Code], is amended in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591 subsection (a)], by inserting after“knowing, or”the following:“, except where, in an offense under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591 paragraph (2)], the act constituting the violation of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591 paragraph (1)]is advertising,”.

==(c) Conforming amendments– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591  Section 1591(b)of title 18, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591 paragraph (1)], by striking“or obtained”and inserting“obtained, or advertised”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1591 paragraph (2)], by striking“or obtained”and inserting“obtained, or advertised”.
