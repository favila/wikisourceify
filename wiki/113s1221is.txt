{{Center|A BILL}}

 To rename section 219(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 as the Kay Bailey Hutchison Spousal IRA.   

=Section 1. Kay Bailey Hutchison Spousal IRA=

The heading of subsection (c) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/219 section 219]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“ Special rules for certain married individuals ”and inserting“ Kay Bailey Hutchison Spousal IRA ”.
