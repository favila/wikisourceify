{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title II of the Social Security Act to repeal the Government pension offset and windfall elimination provisions.

={{anchor|H2E5662B3CFFA43109E4F7CD8B4A3188B}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Social Security Fairness Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H87E0C56049664404A5BD63A3C394B1C2}}Sec. 2. Repeal of government pension offset provision=



=={{anchor|H2FAB1B3E541740408D48CBEAEA71D8D3}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 202(k) of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/402 42 U.S.C. 402(k)] ] )is amended by striking paragraph (5).

=={{anchor|HBEB9042E5BFD41BAA98F71F977B0CA1A}}(b) Conforming amendments– ==


:(1)  Section 202(b)(2) of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/402 42 U.S.C. 402(b)(2)] ] )is amended by striking“subsections (k)(5) and (q)”and inserting“ subsection (q)”.
:(2)  Section 202(c)(2) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/402 42 U.S.C. 402(c)(2)] ] )is amended by striking“subsections (k)(5) and (q)”and inserting“ subsection (q)”.
:(3)  Section 202(e)(2)(A) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/402 42 U.S.C. 402(e)(2)(A)] ] )is amended by striking“subsection (k)(5), subsection (q),”and inserting“ subsection (q)”.
:(4)  Section 202(f)(2)(A) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 42 U.S.C. 402(f)(2)(A)] )is amended by striking“subsection (k)(5), subsection (q)”and inserting“ subsection (q)”.

={{anchor|HEFE899D729214BA3AB9D05698E1B288D}}Sec. 3. Repeal of windfall elimination provisions=



=={{anchor|H7893C04CA68C47448285E57CA08A3138}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 215 of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 42 U.S.C. 415] )is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a), by striking paragraph (7);
:(2) in subsection (d), by striking paragraph (3); and
:(3) in subsection (f), by striking paragraph (9).

=={{anchor|H87AC083138BE4DCAAE9E82AFE156C43F}}(b) Conforming amendments– ==

 Subsections (e)(2)and (f)(2) of section 202 of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 42 U.S.C. 402] )are each amended by striking“section 215(f)(5), 215(f)(6), or 215(f)(9)(B)”in subparagraphs (C)and (D)(i)and inserting“ paragraph (5)or (6) of section 215(f)”.

={{anchor|H596189F4625C4AAD9DFDE83B62C072DF}}Sec. 4. Effective date=

The amendments made by this Act shall apply with respect to monthly insurance benefits payable under title II of the Social Security Actfor months after December 2013. Notwithstanding section 215(f) of the Social Security Act, the [[w:Social Security Administration|Commissioner of Social Security]] shall adjust primary insurance amounts to the extent necessary to take into account the amendments made by section 3.
