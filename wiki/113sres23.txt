{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Expressing the sense of the Senate that a postage stamp should be issued to commemorate the 500th anniversary of Juan Ponce de Leon landing on Florida.

={{anchor|S1}} =

That it is the sense of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] that—
:(1) the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] should issue a postage stamp to commemorate the 500th anniversary of Juan Ponce de Leon landing on Florida; and
:(2) the [[w:Citizens' Stamp Advisory Committee|Citizens’ Stamp Advisory Committee]] of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] should recommend to the [[w:United States Postal Service|Postmaster General]] that such a stamp be issued.
