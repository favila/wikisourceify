{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to allow Delegates and the Resident Commissioner to file, sign, and call up discharge petitions.

= =

That clause 2 of rule XV of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended as follows:
:(1) In paragraph (b), after “Member” each place it appears insert “, Delegate, or Resident Commissioner” .
:(2) In paragraph (c), after “Member” insert “, Delegate, or Resident Commissioner” and after “Members” each place it appears insert “, Delegates, or Resident Commissioner” .
:(3) In the fifth sentence of paragraph (c), after “a majority of the total membership of the House” , insert “, plus all Delegates and the Resident Commissioner,” .
:(4) In paragraph (d)(1), after “Member” insert “, Delegate, or Resident Commissioner” .
:(5) In paragraph (e)(2), after “Member” insert “, Delegate, or Resident Commissioner” .
