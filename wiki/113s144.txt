{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act to authorize additional funding for the pregnancy assistance fund.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Pregnancy Assistance Fund Expansion Act”.

=Sec. 2. Additional funding for the Pregnancy Assistance Fund=

 Section 10214 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18204 42 U.S.C. 18204] )is amended by adding at the end the following:“In addition to amounts authorized to be appropriated in the previous sentence,there are authorized to be appropriatedto carry out section 10210, an additional$25,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2019.”.
