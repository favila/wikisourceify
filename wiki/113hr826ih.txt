{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit the Corps of Engineers from taking any action to establish a restricted area prohibiting public access to waters downstream of a dam, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Freedom to Fish Act of 2013”. 

=Sec. 2. Restricted areas at Corps of Engineers dams=



==(a) In general– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] , acting through the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers]] , shall not take any action to establish a restricted area prohibiting public access to waters downstream of a dam owned by the [[w:United States Army Corps of Engineers|Corps of Engineers]] .

==(b) Effective date– ==



===(1) In general– ===

Subject to paragraph (2) , this section shall apply to an action described in subsection (a) on or after August 1, 2012.

===(2) Existing restrictions– ===

If the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] , acting through the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers]] , has taken an action described in subsection (a) during the period beginning on August 1, 2012, and ending on the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]] shall—
:(A) cease implementing the restricted area resulting from the action; and
:(B) remove any barriers constructed in connection with the restricted area.
