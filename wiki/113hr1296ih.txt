{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to clarify a maintenance exemption regarding the removal of sediment, debris, and vegetation from certain structures.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Flood Control Facility Maintenance Clarification Act” .

=Sec. 2. Permits for dredged or fill material=

 Section 404(f) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1344 42 U.S.C. 1344(f)] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1)(B)by inserting “channels, basins,” after “such as” ; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(3) For the purposes of paragraph (1)(B), the term maintenance includes the removal of sediment, debris, and vegetation from structures described in that paragraph..
</blockquote>
