{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Congressional Budget Act of 1974 to provide for an expedited process for increasing the statutory limit on the public debt.

={{anchor|H8F6A062B186C4449BBE9275BE5E8ACD2}}Section 1. Debt increase as measure of deficit=



=={{anchor|H3BF3C9B871DF423B8ECA55BB25194247}}(a) Content for the concurrent resolution on the budget– ==

 Section 301(a) of the Congressional Budget Act of 1974is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (6), by striking“; and”and inserting a semicolon;
:(2) in paragraph (7), by striking the period at the end and inserting“; and”; and
:(3) by inserting after paragraph (7)the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(8) a heading entitled Debt Increase as Measure of Deficit which sets forth the amounts by which the public debt limit (in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 section 3101(b) of title 31 of the United States Code] ) shall increase in each of such years..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H1EDE930C3BEC4369AC1462F1E90F5F01}}(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 Section 301(b) of the Congressional Budget Act of 1974is amended by striking paragraph (5)and redesignating the succeeding paragraphs accordingly.

={{anchor|HD8F66A8D2B4E4664BBF5D79EA527F7BB}}Sec. 2. Consideration of debt subject to limit=



=={{anchor|HF364C36C816E4F338B361178EC1EB657}}(a) Consideration– ==

 Section 305(a) of the Congressional Budget Act of 1974is amended by redesignating paragraph (7)as paragraph (8)and by inserting after paragraph (6)the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
:(7) Upon passage in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representative]] of the conference report on any concurrent resolution on the budget, it shall be immediately in order to consider a privileged bill offered by the chairman of the Committee on the Budget amending the debt subject to limit (in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 section 3101 of title 31 of the United States Code] ) at the level proposed under the heading“Debt Increase as a Measure of Deficit”for the fiscal year of the concurrent resolution on the budget. Debate on the bill shall be limited to not more than 1 hour. The bill shall not be amendable and a motion to commit shall not be in order..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H1C21293FF3FB49CC8DAB0776B5EEA725}}(b) Consideration– ==

 Section 305 of the Congressional Budget Act of 1974is amended by inserting at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H6DF2E29E70974A4BAF94D5B3B9D31822}}(e) Action on debt subject to limit– ===


:(1) Except as provided in paragraph (2), the provisions of this section for the consideration of the conference report on any concurrent resolution shall also apply to the consideration in the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] of a bill passed by the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] pursuant to subsection (a)(7).
:(2) It shall not be in order in the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] to consider a bill pursuant to paragraph (1)before the passage of the conference report on the concurrent resolution on the budget for the fiscal year of the concurrent resolution on the budget..
</blockquote>


={{anchor|HFAAA537ADF0F403DA6ABBEEC1D746460}}Sec. 3. Increase of debt subject to limit=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 Section 3101(b) of title 31, United States Code] , is amended by striking the dollar limitation contained therein and inserting“$17,072,810,000,000”.
