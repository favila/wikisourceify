{{Center|A BILL}}

To abolish the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System and the Federal reserve banks, to repeal the Federal Reserve Act, and for other purposes.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Federal Reserve Board Abolition Act”.

2. Federal Reserve Board abolished


=(a) In General=

Effective at the end of the 1-year period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System ]]and each Federal reserve bank are hereby abolished.
=(b) Repeal of Federal Reserve Act=

Effective at the end of the 1-year period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, the Federal Reserve Act is hereby repealed.
=(c) Disposition of Affairs=


==(1) Management during dissolution period==

During the 1-year period referred to in subsection (a) , the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Chairman of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System ]]—: shall, for the sole purpose of winding up the affairs of the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System ]]and the Federal reserve banks—:: manage the employees of the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board ]]and each such bank and provide for the payment of compensation and benefits of any such employee which accrue before the position of such employee is abolished; and:: manage the assets and liabilities of the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board ]]and each such bank until such assets and liabilities are liquidated or assumed by the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury ]]in accordance with this subsection; and: may take such other action as may be necessary, subject to the approval of the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury ]], to wind up the affairs of the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board ]]and the Federal reserve banks.
==(2) Liquidation of assets==


===(A) In general===

The [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget ]]shall liquidate all assets of the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board ]]and the Federal reserve banks in an orderly manner so as to achieve as expeditious a liquidation as may be practical while maximizing the return to the Treasury.
===(B) Transfer to treasury===

After satisfying all claims against the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board ]]and any Federal reserve bank which are accepted by the [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget ]]and redeeming the stock of such banks, the net proceeds of the liquidation under subparagraph (A) shall be transferred to the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury ]]and deposited in the General Fund of the Treasury.
==(3) Assumption of liabilities==

All outstanding liabilities of the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System ]]and the Federal reserve banks at the time such entities are abolished, including any liability for retirement and other benefits for former officers and employees of the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board ]]or any such bank in accordance with employee retirement and benefit programs of the [[Wikipedia:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board ]]and any such bank, shall become the liability of the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury ]]and shall be paid from amounts deposited in the general fund pursuant to paragraph (2) which are hereby appropriated for such purpose until all such liabilities are satisfied.
=(d) Report=

At the end of the 18-month period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury ]]and the [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget ]]shall submit a joint report to the [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress ]]containing a detailed description of the actions taken to implement this Act and any actions or issues relating to such implementation that remain uncompleted or unresolved as of the date of the report.
