{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to permit direct payment to pharmacies for certain compounded drugs that are prepared by the pharmacies for a specific beneficiary for use through an implanted infusion pump.

={{anchor|H01F73F0FF86C463989F312C812402A88}}Section 1. Medicare direct payment to pharmacies for certain compounded drugs that are prepared by the pharmacies for a specific beneficiary for use through an implanted infusion pump=



=={{anchor|HDCF35278A9814BCBB6377A0B505C4D31}}(a) In general– ==

The first sentence of section 1842(b)(6) of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395u 42 U.S.C. 1395u(b)(6)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking“and”before“(H)”; and
:(2) by inserting before the period at the end the following:“, and (I) in the case of covered compounded drugs that are prepared by a pharmacy for a specific individual, are dispensed, directly or indirectly, to the individual, are necessary for the effective use of, or therapeutic benefit from, an implanted infusion pump (regardless of who refills the pump), and are billed directly by the pharmacy, payment shall be made to the pharmacy”.

=={{anchor|H7284C547F02F49939C25DA63943CB709}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a)shall apply to drugs dispensed on or after January 1, 2013.
