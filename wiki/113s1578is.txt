{{Center|A BILL}}

To authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to cover the costs associated with the care of veterans at medical foster homes.

=Section 1. Coverage of costs of care for veterans at medical foster homes=

In conducting the medical foster home program pursuant tosection 17.73of title 38, Code of Federal Regulations, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] may cover the costs associated with the care of veterans at medical foster homes.
