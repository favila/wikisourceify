{{Center|A BILL}}

To establish requirements relating to the provision of
		  certain products to the Government of Afghanistan, and for other
		  purposes.

={{anchor|H79EF06B3738B4324B339CA5CC6AF48E8}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Accountability of Taxpayer Funding for
			 Afghanistan Fuels Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HBCCF0DD5EE774B63942B5B5B8C57F28B}}Sec. 2. Requirements
			 relating to provision of covered products to the Government of
			 Afghanistan=



=={{anchor|H2C005268CA804984BA667DC53C0BD40B}}(a) In
			 general– ==

No covered product
			 may be provided directly or indirectly to the Government of Afghanistan unless
			 and until the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] submits to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a report that
			 contains—
:(1) a
			 determination of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] that the requirements described in subsection
			 (b) have been met; and
:(2) a
			 detailed justification for such determination.

=={{anchor|H5C52219C175941128915304579B9888A}}(b) Requirements– ==

The
			 requirements described in this subsection are the following:
:(1) The provision of
			 the covered product meets an existing or future need of the Government of
			 Afghanistan.
:(2) The financial
			 documents relating to the provision of the covered product are fully accounted
			 for and will be maintained by the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] for a period of not
			 less than 10 years.
:(3) The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of
			 Defense]] has established controls to monitor the use of the covered
			 product.

=={{anchor|H882F55B2A122441980522BF983E01D28}}(c) Effective
			 date– ==

This section takes
			 effect 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act and applies with
			 respect to the provision of a covered product on or after 60 days after such
			 date of enactment.

={{anchor|HE1563CE8BA3B41149C4B89615E0DE309}}Sec. 3. Prohibition on
			 financing assistance for purposes of procuring any covered product for use by
			 the Government of Afghanistan=



=={{anchor|H9A92AE2918D84FD1BE3834F2E2C1D99B}}(a) In
			 general– ==

No funds available to
			 the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] may be provided directly or indirectly to the
			 Government of Afghanistan for purposes of procuring any covered product unless
			 and until the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] submits to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a report that
			 contains—
:(1) a
			 determination of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] that the requirements described in subsection
			 (b) have been met; and
:(2) a
			 detailed justification for such determination.

=={{anchor|HE5406E66339143819FDD13D93DFE77A9}}(b) Requirements– ==

The
			 requirements described in this subsection are the following:
:(1) The Government of Afghanistan is capable of
			 providing reliable accounting and reporting to the [[w:Counterintelligence Field Activity|Department of Defense]] with
			 respect to the purchase, delivery, and consumption of a covered product.
:(2) The Special Inspector General for
			 Afghanistan Reconstruction or beginning on the date described in subsection (c)
			 the [[w:|Inspector General of the Department of Defense]] has certified to the
			 [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] that the requirements described in paragraph (1) have been
			 met.

=={{anchor|H8AAD628951084B34817E599E31AC2C56}}(c) Date
			 described– ==

The date described in this subsection is the date of
			 termination of the Office of the Special Inspector General for Afghanistan
			 Reconstruction as required under section 1229 of the National Defense
			 Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2008 ([[Public Law 110-181|Public Law 110–181]]; 122 Stat.
			 378).

=={{anchor|H716054A1EA9A4794A29FBBFE823759FF}}(d) Effective
			 date– ==

This section takes effect on the date of the enactment of
			 this Act and applies with respect to funds available to the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of
			 Defense]] that are unobligated or unexpended as of such date of enactment.

={{anchor|H588C8C7F41D945059E6A0076B2A74A21}}Sec. 4. Definitions=

In this Act:

=={{anchor|H391AAD5A1E2C4F718EA135D1BA941BCE}}(1) Covered
			 product– ==

The term
			 covered product means petroleum, oil, or a lubricant (including
			 diesel fuel, gasoline, jet or aviation fuel, kerosene, or other fuels) or
			 firewood that is to be used for purposes of equipping, training, or sustaining
			 the Afghan National Security Forces.

=={{anchor|H41E7D32492894E919DBAAF12834BC7A0}}(2) Government of
			 Afghanistan– ==

The term Government of Afghanistan
			 includes any agency of instrumentality of Afghanistan.
