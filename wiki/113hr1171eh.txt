{{Center|1st Session}}


{{Center|H. R. 1171}}


{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}


{{Center|AN ACT}}

To amend title 40, United States Code, to improve veterans service organizations access to Federal surplus personal property.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Formerly Owned Resources for Veterans to Express Thanks for Service Act of 2013” or the “FOR VETS Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Veterans access to Federal excess and surplus personal property=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/549 Section 549(c)(3)of title 40, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/549 subparagraph (A)] , by striking “or” at the end;
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/549 subparagraph (B)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/549 clause (viii)] , by adding “or” at the end; and
::(B) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/549 clause (x)] ; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(C) for purposes of providing services to veterans (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 section 101of title 38] ), to an organization whose—
:::(i) membership comprises substantially veterans; and
:::(ii) representatives are recognized by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5902 section 5902of title 38] ..
</blockquote>
Passed the House of Representatives July 8, 2013.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
