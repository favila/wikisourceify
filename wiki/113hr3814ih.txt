{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Commodity Exchange Act to require the de minimis quantity of swap dealing needed to qualify for exemption from designation as a swap dealer to be changed by a vote of the Commodity Futures Trading Commission.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Risk Management Certainty Act” .

=Sec. 2. Commission vote required before automatic change of swap dealer de minimis level=

 Section 1a(49)(D) of the Commodity Exchange Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/1a 7 U.S.C. 1a(49)(D)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

==(D) De minimis exception– ==

The [[w:Commodity Futures Trading Commission|Commission]] shall exempt from designation as a swap dealer an entity that engages in a de minimis quantity of swap dealing (which shall not be less than $8,000,000,000) in connection with transactions with or on behalf of its customers. The [[w:Commodity Futures Trading Commission|Commission]] shall promulgate regulations to establish the factors to be used in a determination to so exempt, including any monetary or other levels established by the [[w:Commodity Futures Trading Commission|Commission]] , and such levels shall only be amended or changed through an affirmative action of the [[w:Commodity Futures Trading Commission|Commission]] undertaken by rule or regulation..
</blockquote>
