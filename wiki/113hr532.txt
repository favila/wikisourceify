{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 11 of the United States Code to modify the dischargeability of debts for certain educational payments and loans.

={{anchor|H8693B4903A8C4D04B3FAE03B8BAE22CA}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Private Student Loan Bankruptcy Fairness Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HB81EA9414CF94DAE8601DD9909789235}}Sec. 2. Exceptions to discharge=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/523 Section 523(a)(8) of title 11, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/523 subparagraph (B)] , and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/523 subparagraph (A)] —
::(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/523 clause (i)] —
:::(i) by striking“[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/523 (i)] ”, and
:::(ii) by inserting“any program for which substantially all of the funds are provided by a”after“unit or”, and
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/523 clause (ii)] —
:::(i) by striking“[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/523 (ii)] ”and inserting“[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/523 (B)] ”, and
:::(ii) by striking“or”at the end.

={{anchor|H248FA16F47D2487A9F4A3EE920838D13}}Sec. 3. Effective date; application of amendments=



=={{anchor|HE9DEDB4D78424DBC94EA29F925C41075}}(a) Effective date– ==

Except as provided in subsection (b), this Act and the amendments made by this Act shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.

=={{anchor|HBD952C78305D45D79C5E2C40A203D267}}(b) Application of amendments– ==

The amendments made by this Act shall apply only with respect to cases commenced under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/11/ title 11 of the United States Code] on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
