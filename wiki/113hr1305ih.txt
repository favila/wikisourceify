{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide clarification regarding eligibility for services under the Homeless Veterans Reintegration Program.

=Section 1. Clarification of eligibility for services under the Homeless Veterans Reintegration Program=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2021 Subsection (a) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 2021] of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking“reintegration of homeless veterans into the labor force.”and inserting the following:“reintegration into the labor force of—”

<blockquote>
:(1) homeless veterans;
:(2) veterans participating in the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] supported housing program for which rental assistance provided pursuant to section 8(o)(19) of the United States Housing Act of 1937( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1437f 42 U.S.C. 1437f(o)(19)] ] ); and
:(3) veterans who are transitioning from being incarcerated..
</blockquote>
