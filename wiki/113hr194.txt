{{Center|A BILL}}

To clarify the applicability of the Buy American Act to products purchased for the use of the legislative branch, to prohibit the application of any of the exceptions to the requirements of such Act to products bearing an official Congressional insignia, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H8CF2F22B5D22493AA2BB7D7C812C6173}}Section 1. Short Title=

This Act may be cited as the“Congressional Made in America Promise Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H5064F0B8B50847209538CF5B52C64400}}Sec. 2. Applicability of Buy American Act to Legislative Branch; No Exceptions for Products Bearing Official Congressional Insignia=



=={{anchor|HEAF77E6B1103437AA3621172E8FD5349}}(a) In general– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 Section 8302 of title 41, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 subsection (b)] as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 subsection (c)] ; and
:(2) by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 subsection (a)] the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|HD50A8372A4D24BFB87485E499D61D38D}}(b) Clarification of Applicability to Articles, Materials, and Supplies for Use of Legislative Branch– ====



====={{anchor|H89417677D4C341849D1A26EFCC261F8F}}(1) Applicability to legislative branch– =====

Except as provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 paragraph (2)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 subsection (a)] applies with respect to articles, materials, and supplies acquired for the use of any office in the legislative branch, including the House of Representatives and the Senate, in the same manner as such subsection applies with respect to articles, materials, and supplies acquired for the use of a department or independent establishment.

====={{anchor|H6AE2E7698C5848EA99F4FAEAAABA98B7}}(2) Special rule for products bearing official Congressional insignia– =====

In the case of any product which bears an official insignia (including a mark resembling an official seal) of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|United States House of Representatives]] , the [[w:United States Senate|United States Senate]] , or the [[w:United States Congress|United States Congress]] and which is acquired for the use of an office of the legislative branch, the following shall apply:
:(A) The head of the office may not make a determination under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 subsection (a)(1)] that it is inconsistent with the public interest to enter into a contract in accordance with this Act.
:(B) The exception under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 subparagraph (B) of subsection (a)(2)] shall not apply.
:(C) The exception under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/8302 subparagraph (C) of subsection (a)(2)] shall not apply..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H0E9F8763B7274307979EA8AC79E1D74E}}(b) Conforming Amendment– ==

Section 69 of the Revised Statutes of the United States ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/109 2 U.S.C. 109] ) is repealed.

={{anchor|H3C8F13D241BB4628BC446BF51F4F576E}}Sec. 3. Effective Date=

The amendments made by this Act shall take effect upon the expiration of the 180-day period which begins on the date of the enactment of this Act.
