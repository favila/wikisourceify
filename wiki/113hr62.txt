        {{Center|A BILL}}
    
		To amend title 18, United States Code, to provide an
		  alternate release date for certain nonviolent offenders, and for other
		  purposes.
	
	
		
        =={{section|H92ACBBE14C874C0C98BA620B589783F7|
      1.
      Short title
      This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Federal Prison Bureau Nonviolent
			 Offender Relief Act of 2013
        .
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|H8F617A2A8C7442868BB12EE1A37742A3|
      2.
      Early release for
			 certain nonviolent offenders
			
        (a)
        In
			 general
        
          Section 3624 of title 18, United States Code, is
			 amended—
				
          (1)
          in subsection (a),
			 by inserting at the early release date provided in subsection (g), if
			 applicable, or otherwise after A prisoner shall be released by
			 the 
                [[Federal Bureau of Prisons|Bureau of Prisons]]
            
            ; and
				
        
          (2)
          by adding at the
			 end the following:
					
						
              (g)
              Early release
				for certain nonviolent offenders
              Notwithstanding any other
				provision of law, the 
                [[Federal Bureau of Prisons|Bureau of Prisons]]
            , pursuant to a good time policy, shall
				release from confinement a prisoner who has served one half or more of his term
				of imprisonment (including any consecutive term or terms of imprisonment) if
				that prisoner—
							
                (1)
                has attained the
				age of 45 years;
							
              
                (2)
                has never been
				convicted of a crime of violence; and
							
              
                (3)
                has not engaged in
				any violation, involving violent conduct, of institutional disciplinary
				regulations.
							
            
            .
          
				
      
    }}==
