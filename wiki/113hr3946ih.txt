{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend for one year the employer wage credit for employees who are active duty members of the uniformed services.

=Section 1. Extension of employer wage credit for employees who are active duty members of the uniformed services=



==(a) In general– ==

 Subsection (f) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/45P section 45P] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking “December 31, 2013” and inserting “December 31, 2014” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to payments made after December 31, 2013.
