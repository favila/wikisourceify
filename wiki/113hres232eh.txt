{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 1911) to amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to establish interest rates for new loans made on or after July 1, 2013, and for other purposes.

= =

That upon the adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to consider in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] the bill ( H.R. 1911) to amend the Higher Education Act of 1965to establish interest rates for new loans made on or after July 1, 2013. All points of order against consideration of the bill are waived. In lieu of the amendment in the nature of a substitute recommended by the [[w:United States House Committee on Education and the Workforce|Committee on Education and the Workforce]] now printed in the bill, an amendment in the nature of a substitute consisting of the text of Rules Committee Print 113–12 shall be considered as adopted. The bill, as amended, shall be considered as read. All points of order against provisions in the bill, as amended, are waived. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the bill, as amended, and on any further amendment thereto, to final passage without intervening motion except: (1) one hour of debate equally divided and controlled by the chair and ranking minority member of the [[w:United States House Committee on Education and the Workforce|Committee on Education and the Workforce]] ; and (2) one motion to recommit with or without instructions.

=2. =

In the engrossment of H.R. 1911, the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Clerk]] shall—
:(a) await the disposition of H.R. 1949;
:(b) add the text of H.R. 1949, as passed by the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] , as new matter at the end of H.R. 1911;
:(c) conform the title of H.R. 1911to reflect the addition of the text of H.R. 1949, as passed by the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] , to the engrossment;
:(d) assign appropriate designations to provisions within the engrossment; and (e) conform cross-references and provisions for short titles within the engrossment.

=3. =

On any legislative day during the period from May 24, 2013, through May 31, 2013—
:(a) the Journal of the proceedings of the previous day shall be considered as approved; and
:(b) the Chair may at any time declare the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] adjourned to meet at a date and time, within the limits of clause 4, section 5, article I of the Constitution, to be announced by the Chair in declaring the adjournment.

=4. =

The [[w:United States House of Representatives|Speaker]] may appoint Members to perform the duties of the Chair for the duration of the period addressed by section 3 of this resolutionas though under clause 8(a) of rule I.

=5. =

The [[w:United States House Committee on Appropriations|Committee on Appropriations]] may, at any time before 6 p.m. on Wednesday, May 29, 2013, file privileged reports to accompany measures making appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2014.

=6. =

The [[w:United States House Committee on Agriculture|Committee on Agriculture]] may, at any time before 6 p.m. on Wednesday, May 29, 2013, file a report to accompany H.R. 1947.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
