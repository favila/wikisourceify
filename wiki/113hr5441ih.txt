{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Federal charter of the Veterans of Foreign Wars of the United States to reflect the service of women in the Armed Forces of the United States.   

=Section 1. Reflection of service of women in the Armed Forces in the Federal charter of the Veterans of Foreign Wars of the United States=



==(a) Organization– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/230101  Section 230101(a)of title 36, United States Code], is amended by striking“men”and inserting“veterans”.

==(b) Purposes– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/36/230102 Section 230102(3) of such title]is amended by striking“widows”and inserting“surviving spouses”.
