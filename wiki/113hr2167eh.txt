{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}

 
{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To authorize the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development to establish additional requirements to improve the fiscal safety and soundness of the home equity conversion mortgage insurance program.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Reverse Mortgage Stabilization Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Additional safety and soundness requirements for home equity conversion mortgage insurance program=

   Subsection (h) of section 255 of the National Housing Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/1715z–20 12 U.S.C. 1715z–20(h)] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1) , by striking“and”at the end;
:(2) in paragraph (2) , by striking the period at the end and inserting“; and”; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
::(3) establish, by notice or mortgagee letter, any additional or alternative requirements that the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary]], in the [[w:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary’s]]discretion, determines are necessary to improve the fiscal safety and soundness of the program authorized by this section, which requirements shall take effect upon issuance. . 
</blockquote>
    Passed the House of Representatives June 12, 2013. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
