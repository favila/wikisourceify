{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to expand the Marine Gunnery Sergeant John David Fry scholarship to include spouses of members of the Armed Forces who die in the line of duty, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Spouses of Heroes Education Act” .

=Sec. 2. Expansion of Marine Gunnery Sergeant John David Fry scholarship=



==(a) Expansion of entitlement– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 Subsection (b)(9) ofsection 3311of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by inserting “or spouse” after “child” .

==(b) Limitation and election on certain benefits– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 Subsection (f) of such section] is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 paragraph (2)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 paragraph (4)] ;
:(2) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 paragraph (1)] the following new paragraphs:

<blockquote>

====(2) Limitation– ====

The entitlement of an individual to assistance under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 subsection (a)] pursuant to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 paragraph (9) of subsection (b)] because the individual was a spouse of a person described in such paragraph shall expire on the earlier of—
:(A) the date that is 15 years after the date on which the person died; and
:(B) the date on which the individual remarries.

====(3) Election on receipt of certain benefits– ====

A surviving spouse entitled to assistance under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 subsection (a)] pursuant to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 paragraph (9) of subsection (b)] who is also entitled to educational assistance under chapter 35 of this title may not receive assistance under both this section and such chapter, but shall make an irrevocable election (in such form and manner as the Secretary may prescribe) under which section or chapter to receive educational assistance.; and
</blockquote>

:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 paragraph (4)] , as redesignated by paragraph (1), by striking “that paragraph” and inserting “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 paragraph (9) of subsection (b)] ” .

==(c) Conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3321 Section 3321(b)(4)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “an individual” and inserting “a child” .

==(d) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date that is 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
