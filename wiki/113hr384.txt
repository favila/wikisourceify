{{Center|A BILL}}

To establish the position of Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs in the Department of Housing and Urban Development, and for other purposes.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Homes for Heroes Act of 2013”.

2. Special assistant for Veterans Affairs in the Department of Housing and Urban Development


Section 4 of the Department of Housing and Urban Development Act ( 42 U.S.C. 3533 ) is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:


=(g) Special assistant for veterans affairs=


==(1) Establishment==

There shall be in the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department ]]a Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs, who shall be a [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|special assistant to the Secretary ]]and shall report directly to the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary ]].
==(2) Appointment==

The Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs shall be appointed based solely on merit and shall be covered under the provisions of title 5, United States Code , governing appointments in the competitive service.
==(3) Responsibilities==

The Special Assistant for Veterans Affairs shall be responsible for—: ensuring veterans have fair access to housing and homeless assistance under each program of the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department ]]providing either such assistance;: coordinating all programs and activities of the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department ]]relating to veterans;: serving as a liaison for the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department ]]with the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs ]], including establishing and maintaining relationships with the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs ]];: serving as a liaison for the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department ]], and establishing and maintaining relationships with the [[Wikipedia:United States Interagency Council on Homelessness|United States Interagency Council on Homelessness ]]and officials of State, local, regional, and nongovernmental organizations concerned with veterans;: providing information and advice regarding—:: sponsoring housing projects for veterans assisted under programs administered by the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department ]]; or:: assisting veterans in obtaining housing or homeless assistance under programs administered by the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Department ]];: coordinating with the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary of Housing and Urban Development ]]and the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs ]]in carrying out section 3 of the Homes for Heroes Act of 2013 ; and: carrying out such other duties as may be assigned to the Special Assistant by the [[Wikipedia:Department of Housing and Urban Development|Secretary ]]or by law..
