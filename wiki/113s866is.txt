{{Center|A BILL}}

To make improvements to the transitional program for covered business method patents, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|idEA6781AB595D4675B2DBD6EC32C0EF77}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Patent Quality Improvement Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|S1}}Sec. 2. Improvements to transitional program for covered business method patents=

 Section 18 of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/35/321 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 35 U.S.C. 321] note] )is amended—
:(1) by striking subsection (a)(3); and
:(2) in subsection (d)(1), by striking“a financial product or”and inserting“an enterprise, product, or”.
