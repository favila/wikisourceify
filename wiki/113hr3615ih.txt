{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the hiring of veterans by the Federal Government.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Careers for Heroes Act” .

=Sec. 2. Employment of veterans with the Federal Government=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 Section 4214of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subsection (b)] , by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(4) 
:::(A) The requirement under this paragraph is in addition to the appointment of qualified covered veterans under the authority under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 paragraph (1)] by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] and the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] .
:::(B) The head of each agency (excluding the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] and the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] ), in consultation with the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the Office of Personnel Management]] , shall develop and implement a plan for exercising the authority specified in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subparagraph (C)] during the five-year period beginning on the date of the enactment of this paragraph.
:::(C) The authority specified in this subparagraph is the authority as follows:
::::(i) The authority under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 paragraph (1)] .
::::(ii) The authority available to the agency concerned under the Veterans Employment Opportunities Act of 1998([[Public Law -|[[Public Law 105-339|Public Law 105–339]] ]] )and the amendments made by that Act.
:::(D) The [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the Office of Personnel Management]] shall ensure that under the plans developed under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subparagraph (B)] , agencies shall appoint to existing vacancies not fewer than 15,000 qualified covered veterans during the five-year period beginning on the date of the enactment of this paragraph. For purposes of complying with this subparagraph, an appointment pursuant to the authority referred to in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subparagraph (C)(ii)] shall not count toward the number required by this subparagraph unless the appointment is to a vacancy in a full-time, permanent position.;
</blockquote>

:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subsection (d)] , in the third sentence, by inserting “(including, during the five-year period beginning on the date of the enactment of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 paragraph (4) of subsection (b)] , the development and implementation by each agency of the plan required under such paragraph, which shall include information regarding the grade or pay level of appointments by the agency under the plan and whether the appointments are, or are converted to, career or career-conditional appointments)” after “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subsection (b) of this section] ” ; and
:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subsection (e)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 paragraph (1)] —
:::(i) in the matter before [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subparagraph (A)] , by striking “to the Congress” and inserting “to the appropriate committees of [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] ” ; and
:::(ii) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 subparagraph (A)] , by inserting “(including, during the five-year period beginning on the date of the enactment of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 paragraph (4) of subsection (b)] , the development and implementation by the agency of the plan required under such paragraph, which shall include information regarding the grade or pay level of appointments by the agency under the plan and whether the appointments are, or are converted to, permanent appointments)” before the period; and
::(B) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
:::(3) In this subsection, the term appropriate committees of Congress means—
::::(A) the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans’ Affairs]] and the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs|Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs of the Senate]] ; and
::::(B) the [[w:United States House Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans’ Affairs]] and the [[w:United States House Committee on Oversight and Government Reform|Committee on Oversight and Government Reform of the House of Representatives]] ..
</blockquote>


==(b) Report– ==

Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Director of the Office of Personnel Management]] shall submit to the appropriate committees of [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] (as defined under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/4214 section 4214(e)(3)of title 38, United States Code] , as amended by subsection (a)) a report containing a plan to carry out the amendments made by subsection (a).
