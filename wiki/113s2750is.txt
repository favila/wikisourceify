{{Center|A BILL}}

 To encourage investments in airports through public-private partnerships, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Airport Public-Private Partnership Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Elimination of restrictions on number of airports in the pilot program on airport privatization=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/47134 Section 47134 of title 49, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in subsection (b), in the matter preceding paragraph (1), by striking“, with respect to not more than 10 airports,”; and
:(2) by striking subsection (d).
