:(3) by inserting after paragraph (8)the following:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H9DCFC1B9EA6444D5A1313DC7E85A87EA}}(9) Penalty for negative error rate– ====



====={{anchor|H620C73BED61A4ED8A6E2436E289EFF0D}}(A) Definitions– =====

In this paragraph:

======{{anchor|HD9538F1D7DF9452F84E8E06A68D5A215}}(i) Affected State agency– ======

The term affected State agency means a State agency that maintains, for 2 or more consecutive fiscal years, a negative error rate that is more than 50 percent higher than the national average negative error rate, as determined by the Secretary.

======{{anchor|H20108F0F51D64A9BA4051319D4A01A40}}(ii) Average negative error rate– ======

The term average negative error rate means the product obtained by multiplying—
:(I) the negative error rate of a State agency; and
:(II) the proportion of the total negative caseload of that State agency for the fiscal year, as calculated under the quality control sample at the time of the notifications issued under subparagraph (C), as determined by the Secretary.

======{{anchor|HB6A435633558411B914BF74332406290}}(iii) Negative error rate– ======



======={{anchor|H79C34E027A6E415EA69E0CB66A4350B8}}(I) In general– =======

The term negative error rate means, for a State agency, the proportion that—
:(aa) the total number of actions erroneously taken by the State agency to deny applications or suspend or terminate benefits of a household participating in the supplemental nutrition assistance program established under this Act, as determined by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] , in that fiscal year; bears to
:(bb) the total number of actions taken by the State agency to deny applications or suspend or terminate benefits of households participating in the supplemental nutrition assistance program established under this Act in that fiscal year.

======={{anchor|H8DAFA2E9B0904CC48E2EB9F19D86C92B}}(II) Exclusions– =======

The term negative error rate does not include—
:(aa) an error resulting from the application of regulations promulgated under this Act during the period—
::(AA) beginning on the date of enactment of this clause; and
::(BB) ending on the date that is 121 days after the date on which the regulation is implemented; and
:(bb) an error resulting from—
::(AA) the use by a State agency of correctly processed information concerning households or individuals received under a Federal program; or
::(BB) an action that is based on policy information that is approved or disseminated, in writing, by the Secretary or a designee of the Secretary.

====={{anchor|HA7FC8A8D28EB4B84A10D0045FD559FE0}}(B) Penalty amount– =====

For fiscal year 2012 and each subsequent fiscal year, the amount of the penalty for an affected State agency shall be equal to 5 percent of the amount otherwise payable under subsection (a).

====={{anchor|HFDB1DD18DD294F7F9BD172B9DDB56157}}(C) Information reporting by States– =====



======{{anchor|HDC29551BD9674294A858E886AFF13595}}(i) In general– ======

For each fiscal year, each State agency shall expeditiously submit to the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] data concerning the operations of the State agency sufficient for the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] to establish the negative error rate and penalty amount of the State agency.

======{{anchor|HD3C66A97BC404EC5A90C8520B1CD69E8}}(ii) Relevant information– ======

The [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] may require a State agency to report any factors necessary to determine the negative error rate of the State agency.

======{{anchor|HCCEFAD96CEF94E868CF58869A5A0CDAC}}(iii) Information not reported– ======

If a State agency fails to report information required by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] , the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] may use any information, as the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] considers appropriate, to establish the negative error rate of the State agency for the applicable year.

======{{anchor|HAB08C89FCE3148CD989313D409999109}}(iv) National average error rate– ======

If a State agency fails to report information required by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] , the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] may use the national average negative error rate to establish the negative error rate for the State agency.

====={{anchor|H8DEA3DA88FE8452A8FDFF6FB2A4ABD12}}(D) Announcement of error rates– =====



======{{anchor|H681FB45A09A147789303B81035ED8679}}(i) Case review– ======

Not later than May 31 of each fiscal year, the case review and all arbitration of State-Federal differences on negative error rates for the previous fiscal year shall be completed.

======{{anchor|H141079988482409F8417FADAA9AF562F}}(ii) Determination and announcement– ======

Not later than June 30 of each fiscal year, the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall, for the previous fiscal year—
:(I) determine—
::(aa) final negative error rates;
::(bb) the national average negative error rate; and
::(cc) penalty amounts;
:(II) notify affected State agencies of the penalty amounts;
:(III) provide a copy of the notification under subclause (II)to the chief executive officer and the legislature of the affected State; and
:(IV) establish a claim against the State agency for the monetary penalty amount assessed against the State agency.

====={{anchor|HEFBB57F654B94F888265ADBAC364AFEC}}(E) Review– =====



======{{anchor|HB6DAB636882F46DAB1A90998DF6809CF}}(i) In general– ======

For any fiscal year, if the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] imposes a penalty amount against a State agency under subparagraph (D)(ii), the following determinations of the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] shall be subject to administrative and judicial review:
:(I) The final negative error rate of the State agency.
:(II) A determination of the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] that the negative error rate of the State agency exceeds 50 percent of the national average negative error rate.
:(III) The monetary penalty amount assessed against the State agency.

======{{anchor|H08D1BDD16F8F4E089223A5171EA050C7}}(ii) Determination not reviewable– ======

The national average negative error rate under this paragraph shall not be subject to administrative or judicial review.

====={{anchor|H7FF27B0F2485482EBAB810453C6662E4}}(F) Payment of penalty amount– =====



======{{anchor|H0B061850C06B4AB5AE293A52B5F6AFEE}}(i) In general– ======

On completion of administrative and judicial review under subparagraph (E), an affected State agency shall pay to the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] the penalty amount designated under subparagraph (D)(ii), subject to the findings of the administrative or judicial review, not later than September 30 of the fiscal year for which the claim has been issued to the State agency.

======{{anchor|H5FA4B7DA72EC44D3BFEAB4714A4A78F6}}(ii) Alternative method of collection– ======



======={{anchor|HB876FCD2C5584F4AAFE379E988EDFA0D}}(I) In general– =======

If a State agency fails to make a payment under clause (i)by September 30 of the fiscal year for which the claim has been issued to the State agency, the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] may reduce any amount due to the State agency under any other provision of this Act by the amount of the monetary penalty established under subparagraph (D)(ii).

======={{anchor|HBDE844B2A2E24A41B2A465039749CF99}}(II) Accrual of interest– =======

Interest on the amount owed shall not accrue until after September 30 of the applicable fiscal year..
</blockquote>
