{{Center|A BILL}}

To increase the portion of community development block grants that may be used to provide public services, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HFA3A1999C13645118B51D7A649E367A6}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“CDBG Public Services Flexibility Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H632CE29796E14968B422E011DCADE9BA}}Sec. 2. Increase in public services cap=

 Section 105(a)(8) of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/5305 42 U.S.C. 5305(a)(8)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking“15 per centum”each place it appears and inserting“25 percent”; and
:(2) by striking“amount, except”and all that follows through the semicolon at the end and inserting“amount;”.
