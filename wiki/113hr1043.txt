={{anchor|HD5A6646272964285B606906C2A6CC3DC}}Section 1. Disperse Blue 60=



=={{anchor|H53284135021143A6A15DB8D50EF43B9A}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Heading 9902.03.50 of
			 the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (relating to Disperse Blue
			 60) is amended by striking the date in the effective period column and
			 inserting “12/31/2016”.

=={{anchor|H86D69D5A492A4AE7B9E6649288DB9E6C}}(b) Effective
			 date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) applies to goods
			 entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day
			 after the date of the enactment of this Act.
