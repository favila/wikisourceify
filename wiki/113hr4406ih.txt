{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title I of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act to impose restrictions on the risk corridor program.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Taxpayer Bailout Protection Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Restrictions on PPACA risk corridor program=

   Section 1342(b) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18062 42 U.S.C. 18062(b)] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1) , by striking“The Secretary”and inserting“Subject to paragraph (3), the Secretary”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 

===(3) Safeguard to protect taxpayers– ===



====(A) In general– ====

The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]shall ensure that the amount of payments to plans under paragraph (1) for a plan year beginning during calendar year 2014, 2015, or 2016 does not exceed the amount of payments to the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]under paragraph (2) for such plan year.

====(B) Adjustment to protect taxpayers– ====

The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]shall proportionately decrease the amount of payments to plans under paragraph (1) in order to ensure that the requirement of subparagraph (A) is satisfied each year. . 
</blockquote>
