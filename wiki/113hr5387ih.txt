{{Center|A BILL}}

 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 14373 Manchester Road in St. Louis, Missouri, as the“Sgt. Zachary M. Fisher Post Office”.   

=Section 1. Sgt. Zachary M. Fisher Post Office=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]located at 14373 Manchester Road in St. Louis, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the“Sgt. Zachary M. Fisher Post Office”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Sgt. Zachary M. Fisher Post Office”.
