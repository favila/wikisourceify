{{Center|A BILL}}

To authorize the National Desert Storm Memorial Association to establish the National Desert Storm and Desert Shield Memorial as a commemorative work in the District of Columbia, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HDE3B2AC060C848B9ADE597F3D4AEFA3B}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“National Desert Storm and Desert Shield War Memorial Act”.

={{anchor|H28D950253CEE46CBB934CF8FC2CA1BC3}}Sec. 2. Definitions=

For the purposes of this Act:

=={{anchor|H24EF5A690E0145FDB1AE6A0BF2A1D466}}(1) Association– ==

The term Association means the National Desert Storm Memorial Association, a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Arkansas and described in section 501(c)(3)and exempt from taxation under section 501(a) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.

=={{anchor|H5077D0E4A46648F79F87F2CB6D41E9B8}}(2) Memorial– ==

The term memorial means the National Desert Storm and Desert Shield Memorial authorized to be established under section 3.

={{anchor|H7F2F3CFF96E241EA8FC638D67EB41449}}Sec. 3. Memorial to commemorate=



=={{anchor|H5AA75162CA074F0996000A6F06B9F870}}(a) Authorization To establish commemorative work– ==

The Association may establish the National Desert Storm and Desert Shield Memorial as a commemorative work, on Federal land in the District of Columbia to commemorate and honor those who, as a member of the Armed Forces, served on active duty in support of Operation Desert Storm or Operation Desert Shield.

=={{anchor|HF9F56F911C684F01BA26D32CE2A616BC}}(b) Compliance with standards for commemorative works act– ==

The establishment of the commemorative work shall be in accordance withchapter 89 of title 40, United States Code(commonly known as the“ Commemorative Works Act”).

=={{anchor|HEE629DFAE58A4A508EFC001B8D6366D8}}(c) Use of federal funds prohibited– ==

Federal funds may not be used to pay any expense of the establishment of the memorial. The Association shall be solely responsible for acceptance of contributions for, and payment of the expenses of, the establishment of the memorial.

=={{anchor|H83052EF00FD74282B13F89C3E02B18B7}}(d) Deposit of excess funds– ==

If, upon payment of all expenses for the establishment of the memorial (including the maintenance and preservation amount required by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8906 section 8906(b)(1) of title 40, United States Code] ), or upon expiration of the authority for the commemorative work under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8903 section 8903(e) of title 40, United States Code] , there remains a balance of funds received for the establishment of the commemorative work, the Association shall transmit the amount of the balance to the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] for deposit in the account provided for in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8906 section 8906(b)(3) of title 40, United States Code] .
