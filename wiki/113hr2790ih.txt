{{Center|A BILL}}

 To authorize private nonprofit organizations to administer permanent housing rental assistance provided through the Continuum of Care Program under the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Housing Assistance Efficiency Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Authority to administer rental assistance=

   Subsection (g) of section 423 of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/11383 42 U.S.C. 11383(g)] )is amended by inserting“private nonprofit organization,”after“unit of general local government,”. 

=Sec. 3. Reallocation of funds=

   Paragraph (1) of section 414(d) of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/11373 42 U.S.C. 11373(d)(1)] )is amended by striking“twice”and inserting“once”.
