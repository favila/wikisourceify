{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to exempt certain State-provided loan programs from being subject to preferred lender arrangement requirements.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Student Aid Expansion Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Exemption of certain State-provided loan programs from preferred lender arrangement requirements=

   Section 151(8)(B) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1019 20 U.S.C. 1019(8)(B)] )is amended—
:(1) in clause (i) , by striking“or”at the end;
:(2) in clause (ii) , by striking the period and inserting“; or”; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote> 
::(iii) arrangements or agreements with respect to interest-free private education loans that are provided and administered by a State and that are eligible for complete forgiveness under conditions determined by such State. . 
</blockquote>
