{{Center|A BILL}}

 To designate the United States Customs and Border Protection Port of Entry located at First Street and Pan American Avenue in Douglas, Arizona, as the“Raul Hector Castro Port of Entry”.   

=Section 1. Raul Hector Castro Port of Entry=



==(a) Designation– ==

The [[w:U.S. Customs and Border Protection|United States Customs and Border Protection]]Port of Entry located at First Street and Pan American Avenue in Douglas, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the“Raul Hector Castro Port of Entry”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the port of entry referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Raul Hector Castro Port of Entry”.
