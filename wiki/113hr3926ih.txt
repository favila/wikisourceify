{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to discharge student loans for borrowers who are determined by the Commissioner of the Social Security Administration to be under a disability without expectation of medical or functional improvement.

=Section 1. Discharge of students loans for borrowers who are disabled without expectation of medical improvement=

 Section 437(a) of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087 20 U.S.C. 1087(a)] )is amended—
:(1) in the paragraph heading for paragraph (2), by inserting “by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] ” after “determinations” ; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

===(3) Disability determinations by the Commissioner of the Social Security Administration– ===

A borrower who has been determined by the [[w:Social Security Administration|Commissioner of Social Security]] to be under a disability (within the meaning of section 223(d) of the Social Security Act) without expectation of medical or functional improvement and who provides documentation of such determination to the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary of Education]] , shall be considered permanently and totally disabled for the purpose of discharging such borrower’s loans under this subsection, and such borrower shall not be required to present additional documentation for purposes of this subsection..
</blockquote>
