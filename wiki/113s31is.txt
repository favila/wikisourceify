{{Center|A BILL}}

To make the moratorium on Internet access taxes and multiple and discriminatory taxes on electronic commerce permanent.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Permanent Internet Tax Freedom Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Permanent moratorium on Internet access taxes and multiple and discriminatory taxes on electronic commerce=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 1101(a) of the Internet Tax Freedom Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/47/151 47 U.S.C. 151 note] )is amended by striking “taxes during the period beginning November 1, 2003, and ending November 1, 2014:” and inserting “taxes:” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxes imposed after the date of the enactment of this Act.
