{{Center|A BILL}}

To require that the prevailing wage utilized for purposes of subchapter IV of chapter 31 of title 40, United States Code (commonly referred to as the Davis-Bacon Act), be determined by the Bureau of Labor Statistics.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Responsibility in Federal Contracting Act”.

=Sec. 2. Bureau of Labor Statistics determination of prevailing wage=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/3142 Section 3142(b) of title 40, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by inserting“(acting through the [[w:Bureau of Labor Statistics|Bureau of Labor Statistics of the Department of Labor]] )”after“ [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]] ”; and
:(2) by inserting before the period at the end the following:“, using surveys carried out by the [[w:Bureau of Labor Statistics|Bureau of Labor Statistics]] that use proper random statistical sampling techniques”.
