{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to extend the basis for the denial of retirement credit, for service as a Member of Congress, to include conviction of any felony under Federal or State law, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Trust Returned to the United States Taxpayer Act”or the“TRUST Act”.

=Sec. 2. Amendments=



==(a) Relating to CSRS– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 Section 8332(o)(2) of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subparagraph (A)] —
::(A) by repealing [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 clause (ii)] ; and
::(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 clause (iii)] —
:::(i) by striking“or”at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subclause (I)(bb)] ;
:::(ii) by inserting“or”at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subclause (II)(bb)] ; and
:::(iii) by adding after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subclause (II)] the following:

<blockquote>
::::(III) is committed after the date of enactment of the Trust Returned to the United States Taxpayer Actand is described under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subparagraph (B)(xxxii)] .; and
</blockquote>

:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subparagraph (B)] , by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(xxxii) 
:::(I) An offense, not otherwise described under this subparagraph, which is a felony under the laws of a State or the United States.
:::(II) For purposes of this clause, the term State includes the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, American Samoa, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, and the Virgin Islands..
</blockquote>


==(b) Relating to FERS– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 Section 8411(l)(2) of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by repealing [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 subparagraph (B)] ; and
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 subparagraph (C)] , by striking“subsection.”and inserting“subsection, or, in the case of an offense described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 section 8332(o)(2)(B)(xxxii)] , after the date of enactment of the Trust Returned to the United States Taxpayer Act.”.
