{{Center|A BILL}}

To correct the boundaries of the John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System Unit L06, Topsail, North Carolina.

={{anchor|H27296EC69EAB4EE689405E04FB414DF8}}Section 1. Replacement of John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System map=



=={{anchor|H04F3CE258333441E8B321DD1FDA0A30E}}(a) In general– ==

The map included in the set of maps entitled Coastal Barrier Resources System referred to in section 4(a) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/3503 16 U.S.C. 3503(a)] )and relating to Unit L06 in North Carolina is hereby replaced by another map relating to the same unit entitled John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System Corrected Unit L06 and dated ______.

=={{anchor|H2D76239B33D24E5DACA83D6249CCEEFB}}(b) Availability– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] shall keep the replacement map referred to in subsection (a)on file and available for inspection in accordance with section 4(b) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 16 U.S.C. 3503(b)] ).
