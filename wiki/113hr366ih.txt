{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit attendance of an animal fighting venture, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Animal Fighting Spectator Prohibition Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Prohibition on attending an animal fight or causing a minor to attend an animal fight=

 Section 26 of the Animal Welfare Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2156 7 U.S.C. 2156] )is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a)—
::(A) in the heading, by striking “Sponsoring or Exhibiting an Animal in” and inserting “Sponsoring or Exhibiting an Animal in, Attending, or Causing a Minor To Attend” ;
::(B) in paragraph (1)—
:::(i) in the heading, by striking “In general” and inserting “Sponsoring or Exhibiting” ; and
:::(ii) by striking “ paragraph (2)” and inserting “ paragraph (3)” ;
::(C) by redesignating paragraph (2)as paragraph (3); and
::(D) by inserting after paragraph (1)the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

====(2) Attending or causing a minor to attend– ====

It shall be unlawful for any person to—
:(A) knowingly attend an animal fighting venture; or
:(B) knowingly cause a minor to attend an animal fighting venture.; and
</blockquote>

:(2) in subsection (g), by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(5) the term minor means a person under the age of 18 years old..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. Enforcement of animal fighting prohibitions=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/49 Section 49 of title 18, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “Whoever” and inserting “(a)In general.—Whoever” ;
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/49 subsection (a)] , as designated by paragraph (1)of this section, by striking “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/49 subsection (a)] ,” and inserting “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/49 subsection (a)(1)] ,” ; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following new subsections:

<blockquote>

===(b) Attending an animal fighting venture– ===

Whoever violates subsection (a)(2)(A) of section 26 of the Animal Welfare Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2156 7 U.S.C. 2156] )shall be fined under this title, imprisoned for not more than 1 year, or both, for each violation.

===(c) Causing a minor To attend an animal fighting venture– ===

Whoever violates subsection (a)(2)(B) of section 26( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2156 7 U.S.C. 2156] )of the Animal Welfare Act shall be fined under this title, imprisoned for not more than 3 years, or both, for each violation..
</blockquote>
