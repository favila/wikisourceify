{{Center|S. CON. RES. 30}}

 
{{Center|IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES}}

 
{{Center|CONCURRENT RESOLUTION}}

 Providing for a conditional adjournment or recess of the Senate and an adjournment of the House of Representatives.   

= =

That when the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]]recesses or adjourns on any day from Friday, December 20, 2013, through Tuesday, December 31, 2013, on a motion offered pursuant to this concurrent resolution by its Majority Leader or his designee, it stand adjourned until 11:45 a.m. on Friday, January 3, 2014, or until the time of any reassembly pursuant to section 2 of this concurrent resolution , whichever occurs first; and that when the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]]adjourns on any legislative day from Monday, December 23, 2013, through Tuesday, December 31, 2013, on a motion offered pursuant to this concurrent resolution by its Majority Leader or his designee, it stand adjourned until 11:00 a.m. on Friday, January 3, 2014, or until the time of any reassembly pursuant to section 3 of this concurrent resolution , whichever occurs first. 

=2. =

(a) The [[w:United States Senate|Majority Leader of the Senate]]or his designee, after consultation with the [[w:United States Senate|Minority Leader of the Senate]], shall notify the Members of the Senate to reassemble at such place and time he may designate if, in his opinion, the public interest shall warrant it.
:(b) After reassembling pursuant to subsection (a) , when the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]]adjourns on a motion offered pursuant to this subsection by the Majority Leader or his designee, the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]]shall again stand adjourned pursuant to the first section of this concurrent resolution. 

=3. =

(a) The [[w:United States House of Representatives|Speaker]]or his designee, after consultation with the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Minority Leader of the House]], shall notify the Members of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]]to reassemble at such place and time he may designate if, in his opinion, the public interest shall warrant it.
:(b) After reassembling pursuant to subsection (a) , when the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]]adjourns on a motion offered pursuant to this subsection by the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Speaker]]or his designee, the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]]shall again stand adjourned pursuant to the first section of this concurrent resolution.    Passed the Senate December 20, 2013.  Secretary.
