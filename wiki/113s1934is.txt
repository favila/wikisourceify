{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the Administrator of General Services to convey the Clifford P. Hansen Federal Courthouse back to Teton County, Wyoming.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Clifford P. Hansen Federal Courthouse Conveyance Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Definitions=

In this Act:

==(1) Administrator– ==

The term Administrator means the Administrator of General Services.
:(2) County– The term County means Teton County, Wyoming. 

=Sec. 3. Conveyance of Federal courthouse to teton county, wyoming=


:(a) In general– Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]]shall convey to the County, without consideration, all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to the building and site located at 145 East Simpson Street, Jackson, Wyoming, known as the Clifford P. Hansen Federal Courthouse .
:(b) Administrative costs– The Secretary shall require the County to pay all survey costs and other administrative costs necessary for the conveyance required under subsection (a) .

==(c) Application of other laws– ==

The conveyance under subsection (a) is not subject to the provisions of the  McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/11301 42 U.S.C. 11301 et seq.] ).
