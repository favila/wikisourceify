{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend to all veterans with a serious service-connected injury eligibility to participate in the family caregiver services program.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Caregivers Expansion and Improvement Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Extension to all veterans with a serious service-connected disability of eligibility for participation in family caregiver program=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720G Section 1720G(a)(2)(B)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “on or after September 11, 2001” .
