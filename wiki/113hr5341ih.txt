{{Center|A BILL}}

 To delay for 1 year the application of Revenue Ruling 2012–18 with respect to the characterization of payments as tips or service charges.   

=Section 1. Delay in application of Revenue Ruling 2012–18 with respect to characterization of tips or service charges=

The [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]]shall not apply Revenue Ruling 2012–18 (or any other similar guidance) for purposes of determining whether any payment made before January 1, 2015, is a tip as opposed to a service charge.
