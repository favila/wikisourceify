{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 49, United States Code, to exempt certain flights from increased aviation security service fees.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Passenger Fee Restructuring Exemptions Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Exceptions to restructuring of passenger fee=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44940  Section 44940(c)of title 49, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by striking“Fees imposed”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

====(1) In general– ====

Except as provided by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44940 paragraph (2)], fees imposed ; and 
</blockquote>

:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

====(2) Exceptions– ====

Fees imposed under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44940 subsection (a)(1)]may not exceed $2.50 per enplanement, and the total amount of such fees may not exceed $5.00 per one-way trip, for passengers—
:(A) boarding to an eligible place undersubchapter II of chapter 417for which essential air service compensation is paid under that subchapter; or
:(B) on flights, including flight segments, between 2 or more points in Hawaii or 2 or more points in Alaska. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

  Section 601(d) of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013 is amended in the matter preceding paragraph (1) by inserting“, as modified by the amendments made by section 2 of the Passenger Fee Restructuring Exemptions Act of 2014  ”after“subsection (b)”.
