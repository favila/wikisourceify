{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to require the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to include an appeals form in any notice of decision issued for the denial of a benefit sought.

={{anchor|HD0A62C81664F48998027AB4A705139BB}}Section 1. Inclusion of appeals forms in notices of decisions of benefits denials issued by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs=



=={{anchor|HE133259533FD4281905790B9F712D46E}}(a) In general– ==

Section 5104(b) of title 38, United States Code, is amended—
:(1) by striking“and (2)”and inserting“(2)”; and
:(2) by inserting before the period at the end the following:“, and (3) a form that may be used to file an appeal of the decision”.

=={{anchor|H0C0315D0CA244117B33891729B507C3A}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to decisions made by the Secretary under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 511] of title 38, United States Code, on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
