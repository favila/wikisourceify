{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to provide for forgiveness of certain overpayments of retired pay paid to deceased retired members of the Armed Forces following their death.

={{anchor|HB55D628E42A3418B95E15A0CE62A99FF}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Military Retiree Survivor Comfort Act”.

={{anchor|H161098597001437780E22D1BAA1DBCFB}}Sec. 2. Forgiveness of certain overpayments of retired pay paid for periods after death of retired members=



=={{anchor|HF14B61EF008244D9BA3C5BD7F561F6CC}}(a) Forgiveness When Payments Deposited to Joint Accounts– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 2771] of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>
:(e) The United States shall forgive any overpayment of retired or retainer pay arising from payment of such retired or retainer pay for any period after the date of the death of the recipient through the last day of the month in which such death occurs if such payment is electronically deposited in an accredited financial institution to a joint account bearing the name of the decedent and another individual who is the decedent’s designated beneficiary under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2771 subsection (a)(1)] ..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H7AB190EC41654260981A8880BF198BAC}}(b) Coordination With Survivor Benefit Plan– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 1450] of title 10, United States Code, is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 subsection (a)] , by inserting“or that applies under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 subsection (n)] ”after“under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 subsection (j)] ”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H39FDE7233E114C13B31DA3B5B46A9E69}}(n) Special Effective Date Rule When Final Retired Pay Overpayment Is Forgiven– ====



====={{anchor|HF6F062C5B12E47619DBD4652A919D966}}(1) Annuity effective on first day of month after death– =====

In a case covered by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 paragraph (2)] , an annuity payable to a surviving spouse shall be effective on the first day of the first month beginning after the death of the person to whom[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 section 1448 of this title] applies, rather than on the date otherwise applicable under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 subsection (a)] .

====={{anchor|HFB4BC55CD80842E983CB5E96718D1F1F}}(2) Covered cases– =====

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 Paragraph (1)] applies in a case in which—
:(A) there is an overpayment of retired pay of a person to whom[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1448 section 1448 of this title] applies that is forgiven pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2771 section 2771(e) of this title] ; and
:(B) the person who is the designated beneficiary of such person under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2771 section 2771(a)(1)] of this title is the surviving spouse of such person..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H5C518C5421044E658EF13D35D3A02F4E}}(c) Effective Date– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2771 Subsection (e) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 2771] of title 10, United States Code] , as added by subsection (a), shall apply with respect to payments made to persons dying on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
