{{Center|A BILL}}

To impose a moratorium on conferences held by the Internal Revenue Service.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Stop Playing on Citizen’s Cash Act” .

=Sec. 2. Moratorium on IRS conferences=

The [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]] shall not hold any conference until the [[w:Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration|Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration]] submits a report to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] —
:(1) certifying that the [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]] has implemented all of the recommendations set out in such [[w:Inspector General|Inspector General’s]] report titled Review of the August 2010 Small Business/Self-Employed Division’s Conference in Anaheim, California , and
:(2) describing such implementation.
