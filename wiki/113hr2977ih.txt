{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to provide for coverage, as supplies associated with the injection of insulin, of containment, removal, decontamination and disposal of home-generated needles, syringes, and other sharps through a sharps container, decontamination/destruction device, or sharps-by-mail program or similar program under part D of the Medicare program.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Medicare Safe Needle Disposal Coverage Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Coverage of containment, removal, decontamination and disposal of home-generated needles, syringes, and other sharps through a sharps container, decontamination/destruction device, or sharps-by-mail program or similar program under Medicare part D=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 1860D–2(e)(1)(B) of the Social Security Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395w–102 42 U.S.C. 1395w–102(e)(1)(B)])is amended by inserting after“regulations of the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]  ”the following:“and including devices approved for home use by the [[w:Food and Drug Administration|Food and Drug Administration]] for the safe and effective containment, removal, decontamination and disposal of home-generated needles, syringes, and other sharps through a sharps container, decontamination/destruction device, or sharps-by-mail program or similar program”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on January 1, 2014.
