{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the employer wage credit for activated military reservists.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Military Reserve Small Business Jobs Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Extension of employer wage credit for activated military reservists=



==(a) In general– ==

  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/45P Section 45P(f)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking “December 31, 2013” and inserting “December 31, 2018” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to payments made after December 31, 2013.
