{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 Relating to the application of Article II, section 3, clause 5, of the Constitution of the United States.   

= =

That the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]]has failed to meet the requirement of Article II, section 3, clause 5, of the Constitution of the United States to take care that a law be faithfully executed, with respect to the policy of the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Department of the Treasury]]pertaining to the 1-year delay in the application of the reporting requirements of sections 6055 and 6056 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (and related requirements of section 4980H of such Code ), as provided under Department of the Treasury Notice 2013â€“45, as announced by the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Department of the Treasury]]on July 2, 2013; and that the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]]shall bring a civil action in the [[w:U.S. District and Territorial Courts|United States District Court for the District of Columbia]]for declaratory or injunctive relief to challenge the policy of the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Department of the Treasury]].
