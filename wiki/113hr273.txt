{{Center|A BILL}}

To eliminate the 2013 statutory pay adjustment for Federal employees.

={{anchor|HB820A23339514D449C8ECB06905D19C8}}Section 1. Elimination of 2013 pay adjustment=



=={{anchor|HDBC8754EF0034653BDEC95D0B04FC293}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 147 of the Continuing Appropriations Act, 2011([[Public Law 111-242|Public Law 111–242]] ;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5303 5 U.S.C. 5303 note] ), as amended by section 114(a) of the Continuing Appropriations Resolution, 2013([[Public Law 112-175|Public Law 112–175]] ;126 Stat. 1316), is amended—
:(1) in subsection (b)(1), by striking the matter after“ending on”and before“shall be made”and inserting“December 31, 2013,”; and
:(2) in subsection (c), by striking the matter after“ending on”and before“no senior executive”and inserting“December 31, 2013,”.

=={{anchor|H920624DC3E534914AC48E7CD55D41767}}(b) Elimination of delayed adjustment– ==

 Section 114(b) of the Continuing Appropriations Resolution, 2013is repealed.
