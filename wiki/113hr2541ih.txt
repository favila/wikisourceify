{{Center|A BILL}}

To allow certain off-duty law enforcement officers and retired law enforcement officers to carry a concealed firearm to protect children in a school zone.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Police Officers Protecting Children Act” .

=Sec. 2. Certain off-duty law enforcement officers and retired law enforcement officers allowed to carry a concealed firearm, and discharge a firearm, in a school zone=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 Section 922(q)of title 18, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 paragraph (2)(B)] —
::(A) by striking “or” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 clause (vi)] ; and
::(B) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 clause (vii)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 clause (ix)] and inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 clause (vi)] the following:

<blockquote>
:::(vii) by an off-duty law enforcement officer who is a qualified law enforcement officer (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/926B section 926B] ) and is authorized under such section to carry a concealed firearm, if the firearm is concealed;
:::(viii) by a qualified retired law enforcement officer (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/926C section 926C] ) who is authorized under such section to carry a concealed firearm, if the firearm is concealed; or; and
</blockquote>

:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 paragraph (3)(B)] —
::(A) by striking “or” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 clause (iii)] ;
::(B) by striking the period at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 clause (iv)] and inserting a semicolon; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:::(v) by an off-duty law enforcement officer who is a qualified law enforcement officer (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/926B section 926B] ) and is authorized under such section to carry a concealed firearm; or
:::(vi) by a qualified retired law enforcement officer (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/926C section 926C] ) who is authorized under such section to carry a concealed firearm..
</blockquote>
