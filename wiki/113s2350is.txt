{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 10, United States Code, to expand the role of the Chief of the National Guard Bureau in the assignment of Directors and Deputy Directors of the Army National Guard and Air National Guard.   

=Section 1. Role of the Chief of the National Guard Bureau in assignment of Directors and Deputy Directors of the Army National Guard and Air National Guard=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506  Section 10506(a)of title 10, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 paragraph (1)]—
::(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 subparagraph (A)], by striking“selected by the Secretary of the Army”and inserting“recommended by the [[w:National Guard Bureau|Chief of the National Guard Bureau]], from not less than three candidates identified by the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]],”; and
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 subparagraph (B)], by striking“selected by the Secretary of the Air Force”and inserting“recommended by the [[w:National Guard Bureau|Chief of the National Guard Bureau]], from not less than three candidates identified by the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary of the Air Force]],”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 paragraph (2)], by striking“The officers so selected”and inserting“The Director and Deputy Director, [[w:Army National Guard|Army National Guard]], and the Director and Deputy Director, [[w:Air National Guard|Air National Guard]],”.

==(b) Conforming amendments regarding appointment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 Paragraph (3) of such section]is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 subparagraph (A)], by striking“The President”and inserting“Consistent with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 paragraph (1)], the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] ”;
:(2) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 subparagraphs (B)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 (D)]; and
:(3) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 subparagraphs (C)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 (E)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 subparagraphs (B)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/10506 (C)], respectively.
