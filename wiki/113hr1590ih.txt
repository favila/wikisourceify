{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Specialty Crops Competitiveness Act of 2004 to include farmed shellfish as specialty crops.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Shellfish Marketing Assistance Fairness Act” .

=Sec. 2. Farmed shellfish as specialty crops=

 Section 3(1) of the Specialty Crops Competitiveness Act of 2004( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/1621 7 U.S.C. 1621note] ;[[Public Law -|Public Law 108–465]] )is amended by inserting “farmed shellfish,” after “fruits,” .
