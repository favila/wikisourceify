{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Reserve Act to alter the terms and
		  conditions applicable to members of the Board of Governors of the Federal
		  Reserve System, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H272E9564B4B340CAA2CE442226E0167E}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Democratizing the Federal Reserve
			 System Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H32D51B1756A84665A5A5ED7A692412E3}}Sec. 2. Amendments to
			 Federal Reserve Act=



=={{anchor|H9593A6B8AAFF4B8FAC96F63C8C4B3D61}}(a) Reduction in
			 length of terms– ==


:(1) The first sentence
			 of the first undesignated paragraph of section 10 of the Federal Reserve Act
			 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/241 12 U.S.C. 241]) is amended by striking “fourteen years” and
			 inserting “7 years”.
:(2) The 2nd sentence of the 2nd undesignated
			 paragraph of section 10 of the Federal Reserve Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/242 12 U.S.C. 242]) is amended
			 by striking “fourteen years” the 2nd place such term appears and
			 inserting “7 years”.
:(3) The ultimate
			 sentence of the 2nd undesignated paragraph of section 10 of the Federal Reserve
			 Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/242 12 U.S.C. 242]) is amended by striking “fourteen years” and
			 inserting “7 years”.

=={{anchor|H0A5A988830564853A15B42A3C806036A}}(b) Increase in
			 post-Service restriction– ==

The first sentence of the 2nd
			 undesignated paragraph of section 10 of the Federal Reserve Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/242 12 U.S.C. 242])
			 is amended by striking “two years” and inserting “4
			 years”.

=={{anchor|HA3DBE78207244AB9B2AAEA5D3A082C8A}}(c) Ongoing service
			 on Board requirement for Chairman and Vice Chairman– ==



==={{anchor|HEF9BC004BD194EC4B8AAB6C18AE0FB69}}(1) Chairman– ===

The 3rd sentence of the 2nd undesignated
			 paragraph of section 10 of the Federal Reserve Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/242 12 U.S.C. 242]) is amended
			 by striking “1 shall be designated” and inserting “a member
			 who is currently serving on the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board]] and has already served at least 2
			 consecutive years on the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board]] shall be designated”.

==={{anchor|HA3D7E9B9D799404F83AC62A9B3541987}}(2) Vice
			 Chairman– ===

The 3rd sentence of
			 the 2nd undesignated paragraph of section 10 of the Federal Reserve Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/242 12
			 U.S.C. 242]) is amended by striking “2 shall be designated” and
			 inserting “2 members who are currently serving on the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board]] and have
			 already served at least 1 year on the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board]], shall be
			 designated”.

=={{anchor|H7BBBB2C254B24B09B403141BDAA848CF}}(d) Limitation on
			 continuing vacancies– ==

The 4th undesignated paragraph of section 10
			 of the Federal Reserve Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/244 12 U.S.C. 244]) is amended—
:(1) in the ultimate
			 sentence, by inserting “, before the end of the 90-day period beginning
			 on the date such vacancy occurs, ” after “shall be
			 appointed”; and
:(2) by adding at the
			 end the following new sentences: “If, as of 30 days after the end of such
			 90-day period, no nomination has been made by the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]], the chairperson
			 and ranking Member of the Committees of the House of Representatives and the
			 Senate which have jurisdiction over this Act, under the rules of the respective
			 House, the Speaker and the minority leader of the House of Representatives, and
			 the majority leader and minority leader of the Senate shall jointly create a
			 list of 5 candidates in rank order to present to the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] for
			 consideration for the vacancy. Not later than 30 days after the transmittal of
			 such list to the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]], the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall nominate a person, whether from
			 such list or another person, to fill the vacancy.”.

=={{anchor|HA1F18DB21D2F46049F63D889A3D9A38D}}(e) Amendments
			 relating to federal open market committee– ==



==={{anchor|HA98CA2BCEF59437B969156C39DDF1F2E}}(1) Increase in
			 federal reserve bank representation– ===

The 1st sentence of section 12A(a) of the
			 Federal Reserve Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/263 12 U.S.C. 263]) is amended by striking “five”
			 and inserting “6”.

==={{anchor|HE15178540AB547579D716D0295D402BB}}(2) Distribution of
			 federal reserve bank membership– ===

Section 12A(a) of the Federal Reserve Act
			 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/263 12 U.S.C. 263]) is amended by striking the 2nd sentence and inserting the
			 following: “Such representatives shall be presidents or first vice
			 presidents of Federal reserve banks and shall be elected annually as follows:
			 In even numbered calendar years, from among the presidents and first vice
			 presidents of the Federal Reserve Banks of Boston, Kansas City, Philadelphia,
			 San Francisco, Richmond, and St. Louis and in odd numbered calendar years from
			 among the presidents and first vice presidents of the Federal Reserve Banks of
			 New York, Dallas, Chicago, Cleveland, Atlanta, and Minneapolis.”.
