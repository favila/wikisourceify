{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the conveyance of unused Federal property administered by the Department of the Navy at the site of the former Oxnard Air Force Base, Ventura County, California.

={{anchor|H5E850B1A72A84FE4ABCEC58769A75928}}Section 1. Land conveyance, former Oxnard Air Force Base, Ventura County, California=



=={{anchor|HA353F614F5374265A81759CA76EBB0F0}}(a) Conveyance authorized– ==

At such time as the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Department of the Navy]] vacates the parcel of real property known as DOD Camarillo Airport, which was originally deeded to the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] at no cost by Ventura County, California (in this section referred to as the“County”), is located at the site of former Oxnard Air Force Base and adjacent to County-operated Camarillo Airport, and is administered by Naval Base Ventura County, the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] may convey, without consideration, to the County all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to the real property, including any improvements thereon, for the purpose of permitting the County to use the property for airport-related activities.

=={{anchor|H6FC3B69EFFE943939E481FA2830505E1}}(b) Payment of costs of conveyance– ==



==={{anchor|H7A7AB7D88CFB4A9AB22067C261BFE4E6}}(1) Payment required– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] shall require the County to cover costs (except costs for environmental remediation of the property) to be incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] , or to reimburse the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] for such costs incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] , to carry out the conveyance under subsection (a), including survey costs, costs for environmental documentation, and any other administrative costs related to the conveyance. If amounts are collected from the County in advance of the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] incurring the actual costs, and the amount collected exceeds the costs actually incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] to carry out the conveyance, the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] shall refund the excess amount to the County.

==={{anchor|H54F65E599A9B4E35983620D646DD84B4}}(2) Treatment of amounts received– ===

Amounts received as reimbursement under paragraph (1)shall be credited to the fund or account that was used to cover those costs incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] in carrying out the conveyance. Amounts so credited shall be merged with amounts in such fund or account, and shall be available for the same purposes, and subject to the same conditions and limitations, as amounts in such fund or account.

=={{anchor|H750CED9B83664EA38A465F55D59932A3}}(c) Description of property– ==

The exact acreage and legal description of the property to be conveyed under subsection (a)shall be determined by a survey satisfactory to the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] .

=={{anchor|H91B24DDACB6A4789BA7EF9529061DD65}}(d) Additional terms– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] may require such additional terms and conditions in connection with the conveyance as the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] considers appropriate to protect the interests of the United States.
