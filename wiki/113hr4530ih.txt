{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require the Secretary of State to offer rewards of up to $5,000,000 for information regarding the attacks on the United States diplomatic mission at Benghazi, Libya, that began on September 11, 2012.   

=Section 1. Rewards authorized=

In accordance with the Rewards for Justice program authorized under  section 36 of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/2708 22 U.S.C. 2708] ), the [[w:United States Department of State|Secretary of State]]shall offer a reward of not more than $5,000,000 to individuals who furnish informationâ€”
:(1) regarding the attacks on the United States diplomatic mission at Benghazi, Libya, that began on September 11, 2012; or
:(2) leading to the capture of an individual who committed, conspired to commit, attempted to commit, or aided in the commission of the attacks described inparagraph (1).
