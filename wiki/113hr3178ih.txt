{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the National Historic Preservation Act to direct the Secretary of the Interior to provide technical or financial assistance to Hispanic-serving institutions for the establishment of historic preservation training and degree programs.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Preservation Research at Institutions Serving Minorities Act” or “PRISM Act” .

=Sec. 2. Historic preservation training and degree programs for Hispanic-serving institutions=

 Subparagraph (C) of section 101(j)(2) of the National Historic Preservation Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/470a 16 U.S.C. 470a(j)(2)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking “and” after “tribal colleges,” ; and
:(2) by inserting after “Hawaiians,” the following: “and to Hispanic-serving institutions as defined in section 502(a)(5) of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1101a 20 U.S.C. 1101a(a)(5)] ),” .
