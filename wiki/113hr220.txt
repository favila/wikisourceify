{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend section 5112 of title 31, United States Code, to limit the face value of coins that the Secretary of the Treasury may issue, and for other purposes.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Stop the Coin Act”.

2. Limitation on face value of coins


Section 5112 of title 31, United States Code , is amended—
: in subsection (k) —:: by inserting “having such nominal, or face, values as the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary ]]may determine but not in any case exceeding $200 ”after “platinum coins ”; and:: by striking “denominations, ”; and: by adding at the end the following new subsection:

==(w) Limitation on face value==

The [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary ]]may not mint or issue any coin having a nominal, or face, value exceeding $200..
