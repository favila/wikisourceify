{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend section 5112 of title 31, United States Code, to
		  limit the face value of coins that the Secretary of the Treasury may issue, and
		  for other purposes.


{{section|H1E8CF242F2C84CC7B1BF1DC9FE198FF5|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Stop the Coin
			 Act

        
.

{{section|H634BFCC0E83F474799410DF1E0254688|2. Limitation on face value of coins}}

        Section
			 5112 of title 31, United States Code
, is amended—
(1)
in subsection
			 (k)
—
(A)
by inserting
			 having such nominal, or face, values as the [[United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] may determine but
			 not in any case exceeding $200
 after platinum coins
;
			 and

(B)
by striking
			 denominations,
; and


(2)
by adding at the
			 end the following new subsection:
(w)
Limitation on
				face value
The [[United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] may
				not mint or issue any coin having a nominal, or face, value exceeding
				$200.

.
