{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend section 5112 of title 31, United States Code, to limit the face value of coins that the Secretary of the Treasury may issue, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H1E8CF242F2C84CC7B1BF1DC9FE198FF5}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Stop the Coin Act”.

={{anchor|H634BFCC0E83F474799410DF1E0254688}}Sec. 2. Limitation on face value of coins=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5112 Section 5112 of title 31, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5112 subsection (k)] —
::(A) by inserting“having such nominal, or face, values as the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] may determine but not in any case exceeding $200”after“platinum coins”; and
::(B) by striking“denominations,”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H0ADED053109C41E2BB378C44786FCC54}}(w) Limitation on face value– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] may not mint or issue any coin having a nominal, or face, value exceeding $200..
</blockquote>
