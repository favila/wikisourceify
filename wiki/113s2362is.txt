{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit the payment of performance awards in fiscal year 2015 to employees in the Veterans Health Administration, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on payment of performance awards to employees in Veterans Health Administration=

Notwithstanding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5384  section 5384of title 5, United States Code], in fiscal year 2015, no performance award shall be paid under such section to employees in the [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration]].
