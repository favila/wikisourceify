{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 31, United States Code, to clarify the use of credentials by enrolled agents.   

=Section 1. Clarification of enrolled agent credentials=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330  Section 330of title 31, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330 subsections (b)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330 (c)], and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330 (d)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330 subsections (c)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330 (d)], and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330 (e)], respectively, and
:(2) by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330 subsection (a)]the following new subsection:

<blockquote> 
::(b) Any enrolled agents properly licensed to practice as required under rules promulgated under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/330 subsection (a)]shall be allowed to use the credentials or designation of“enrolled agent”,“EA”, or“E.A.”. . 
</blockquote>
