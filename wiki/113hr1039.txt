{{Center|A BILL}}

To rescind unobligated amounts for foreign assistance to Egypt, and to appropriate funds for the Department of Defense tuition assistance program.
1. Rescission; appropriation for tuition assistance


=(a) Rescission of foreign assistance to Egypt=


==(1) In general==

Of the aggregate unobligated amount available for foreign assistance to Egypt for fiscal year 2013, there is hereby rescinded $500,000,000.
==(2) Report==

Not later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget ]]shall submit to the committees on appropriations of the [[Wikipedia:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives ]]and the [[Wikipedia:United States Senate|Senate ]]a report describing the amount and account of each rescission under paragraph (1) .
=(b) Appropriation for tuition assistance=

There is appropriated $500,000,000 for an additional amount to the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense ]]for fiscal year 2013 for tuition assistance under sections 1784a and 2007 of title 10, United States Code .
