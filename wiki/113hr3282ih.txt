To amend the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act to reauthorize the predisaster hazard mitigation program.

=Section 1. Predisaster hazard mitigation program=

 Section 203(m) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/5133 42 U.S.C. 5133(m)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

==(m) Authorization of appropriations– ==

There is authorized to be appropriatedto carry out this section$200,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018..
</blockquote>
