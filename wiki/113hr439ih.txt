{{Center|A BILL}}

To ensure that the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority complies with certain Federal regulations and statutes.

=Section 1. Application of Federal regulations and statutes=



==(a) Federal acquisition regulation– ==

The Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority shall comply with the Federal Acquisition Regulation.

==(b) Employment of relatives; restrictions– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3110 Section 3110(a)(1) of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3110 subparagraph (C)] by striking “and” ;
:(2) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3110 subparagraph (D)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3110 subparagraph (E)] ; and
:(3) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3110 subparagraph (C)] the following:

<blockquote>
::(D) the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority; and.
</blockquote>
