{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the International Religious Freedom Act of 1998 to include the desecration of cemeteries among the many forms of violations of the right to religious freedom.   

=Section 1. Amendment to International Religious Freedom Act of 1998=

   Section 2(a)(4) of theInternational Religious Freedom Act of 1998  ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/6401 22 U.S.C. 6401(a)(4)] )is amended in the fourth sentence by inserting“desecration of cemeteries,”after“confiscations of property,”.
