{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To amend the Expedited Funds Availability Act to clarify the application of that Act to American Samoa and the Northern Mariana Islands.   

=Section 1. Application of the Expedited Funds Availability Act=



==(a) In general– ==

The  Expedited Funds Availability Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4001 12 U.S.C. 4001 et seq.] )is amended—
:(1) in  section 602(20) ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4001 12 U.S.C. 4001(20)] )by inserting“, located in the United States,”after“ATM”;
:(2) in  section 602(21) ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4001 12 U.S.C. 4001(21)] )by inserting“American Samoa, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands,”after“Puerto Rico,”;
:(3) in  section 602(23) ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4001 12 U.S.C. 4001(23)] )by inserting“American Samoa, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands,”after“Puerto Rico,”; and
:(4) in  section 603(d)(2)(A) ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4002 12 U.S.C. 4002(d)(2)(A)] ), by inserting“American Samoa, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands,”after“Puerto Rico,”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

This Act shall take effect on January 1, 2016.    Passed the House of Representatives June 9, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
