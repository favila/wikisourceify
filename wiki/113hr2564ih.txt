To extend the additional duty on ethanol.

=Section 1. Extension of additional duty on ethanol=



==(a) In general– ==

Heading 9901.00.50 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States is amended by striking “1/1/2012” in the effective period column and inserting “1/1/2016” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)applies to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
