{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the health care provided to veterans of World War II at facilities of the Department of Veterans Affairs.   

=Section 1. Provision of health care to veterans of World War II=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1705  Section 1705(a)(6)of title 38, United States Code], is amended by striking the period at the end and inserting the following:“and veterans not covered by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1705 paragraphs (1) through (5)]who served during World War II.”.
