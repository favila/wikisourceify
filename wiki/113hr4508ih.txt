{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the East Bench Irrigation District Water Contract Extension Act to permit the Secretary of the Interior to extend the contract for certain water services.   

=Section 1. East Bench Irrigation District contract extension=

   Section 2(1) of the East Bench Irrigation District Water Contract Extension Act ([[Public Law 112-139|Public Law 112–139]] ;126 Stat. 390)is amended by striking“4 years”and inserting“10 years”.
