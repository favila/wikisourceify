{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to provide for certain forms of physical therapy under the TRICARE program.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Rehabilitative Therapy Parity for Military Beneficiaries Act”.

=Sec. 2. Inclusion of certain forms of physical therapy under TRICARE=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1077 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 1077] of title 10, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1077 subsection (a)(17)] , by inserting“In accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1077 subsection (g)] ,”before“Any”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>
::(g) Rehabilitative therapy provided pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1077 subsection (a)(17)] may include additional therapeutic exercises or therapeutic activities if such exercises or activities are included in the authorized individual plan of care of the individual receiving such therapy. Such exercises or activities may include, in addition to other therapeutic exercises or therapeutic activities, therapies provided on a horse, balance board, ball, bolster, and bench..
</blockquote>
