{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to provide
		  that reimbursements for costs of using passenger automobiles for charitable and
		  other organizations are excluded from gross income.


{{section|H648C0304505F442F9AA0A08BDB3CDE53|1. Short title}}
