{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal an annual adjustment of retired pay and retainer pay amounts for retired members of the Armed Forces under age 62, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Repeal of annual adjustment of retired pay and retainer pay amounts for retired members of the Armed Forces under age 62=



==(a) Repeal– ==

 Section 403 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013is hereby repealed, and the provisions of law amended by such section are restored as if such section had never been enacted.

==(b) Offset– ==

 Section 4002(b) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/300u–11 42 U.S.C. 300u–11(b)] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (2), by striking “2017” and inserting “2014” ;
:(2) by redesignating paragraph (5)as paragraph (7);
:(3) by striking paragraphs (3)and (4)and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
::(3) for each of fiscal years 2015 through 2017, $0;
::(4) for each of fiscal years 2018 and 2019, $750,000,000;
::(5) for each of fiscal years 2020 and 2021, $1,000,000,000;
::(6) for fiscal year 2022, $1,300,000,000; and; and
</blockquote>

:(4) in paragraph (7), as so redesignated, by striking “2022” and inserting “2023” .
