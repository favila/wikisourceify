To amend title 38, United States Code, to update the Service-Disabled Veterans Insurance program to base premium rates on the Commissioners 2001 Standard Ordinary Mortality Table instead of the Commissioners 1941 Standard Ordinary Table of Mortality.

=Section 1. Revision of basis for premium rates under Service-Disabled Veterans Insurance program=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1922 Section 1922(a)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “Commissioners 1941 Standard Ordinary Table of Mortality” both places it appears and inserting “Commissioners 2001 Standard Ordinary Mortality Table” .
