{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the use of funds for the Lifeline program, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H43479CD91FC74030BC0D019E69381762}}Section 1. Rescission; prohibition on use of funds for the Lifeline program=



=={{anchor|HBD310CE0BAC74D42B1D92B9BC17BA149}}(a) Rescission– ==

Of the unobligated balances of funds available for —Salaries and Expenses for fiscal year 2013, $2,200,000,000 are hereby rescinded.

=={{anchor|H26C29F46525A408386544FAE6802FDD1}}(b) Limitation– ==

None of the funds made available for —Salaries and Expenses for fiscal year 2013 may be used for the Lifeline program under sections 54.400 through 54.417 of title 47, code of Federal Regulations (as in effect on the date of the enactment of this Act).

=={{anchor|H67F0852A1FCA44E8B5F27B69F9B48345}}(c) Use of rescinded amounts– ==


:(1) The amount otherwise made available for Department of Transportation——Operations for fiscal year 2013 is hereby increased by $500,000,000.
:(2) The amount otherwise made available for —Operation and Maintenance, Navy for fiscal year 2013 is hereby increased by $1,600,000,000.
