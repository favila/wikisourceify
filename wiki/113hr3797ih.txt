 To repeal an annual adjustment of retired pay and retainer pay amounts for retired members of the Armed Forces under age 62, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Repeal of annual adjustment of retired pay and retainer pay amounts for retired members of the Armed Forces under age 62=

  Section 403 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013 is hereby repealed, and the provisions of law amended by such section are restored as if such section had never been enacted.
