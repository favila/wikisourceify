{{Center|A BILL}}

 To reverse the Department of the Interior’s listing of the lesser prairie chicken as a threatened species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, to prevent further consideration of listing of such species as a threatened species or endangered species under that Act pending implementation of the Western Association of Fish and Wildlife Agencies’ Lesser Prairie-Chicken Range-Wide Conservation Plan and other conservation measures, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Lesser Prairie Chicken Voluntary Recovery Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Implementation of Lesser Prairie-Chicken Range-Wide Conservation Plan and other conservation measures=



==(a) Prohibition on treatment as threatened or endangered species– ==



===(1) In general– ===

Notwithstanding any prior action by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]], the lesser prairie chicken shall not be treated as a threatened species or endangered species under the  Endangered Species Act of 1973 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1531 16 U.S.C. 1531 et seq.] )before January 31, 2020.

===(2) Prohibition on proposal– ===

On and after January 31, 2020, the lesser prairie chicken may not be treated as a threatened species or endangered species under the  Endangered Species Act of 1973 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1531 16 U.S.C. 1531 et seq.])unless the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]publishes a determination, based on the totality of the scientific evidence, that conservation (as that term is used in that Act) under the Range-Wide Plan and the agreements, programs, and efforts referred to in subsection (b) has not achieved the conservation goals established by the Range-Wide Plan.

==(b) Monitoring of progress of conservation programs– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]shall monitor and annually report to the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]]on progress in conservation of the Lesser Prairie Chicken under the Range-Wide Plan and all related Candidate Conservation Agreements and Candidate and Conservation Agreements With Assurances, other Federal conservation programs administered by the [[w:U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service|United States Fish and Wildlife Service]], the [[w:Bureau of Land Management|Bureau of Land Management]], and the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Department of Agriculture]], State conservation programs, and private conservation efforts.

==(c) Definitions– ==

In this section:

===(1) Candidate Conservation Agreements– ===

Each of the terms“Candidate Conservation Agreement”and“Candidate and Conservation Agreement With Assurances”means, respectively, such an agreement referred to in—
:(A) the Announcement of Final Policy for Candidate Conservation Agreements with Assurances published by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Department of the Interior]]and the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Department of Commerce]]on June 17, 1999 (64 Fed. Reg. 32726); and
:(B) sections 17.22(d) and 17.32(d) of title 50, Code of Federal Regulations.

===(2) Range-Wide Plan– ===

The term“Range-Wide Plan”means the Lesser Prairie-Chicken Range-Wide Conservation Plan of the Western Association of Fish and Wildlife Agencies, as endorsed by the [[w:U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service|United States Fish and Wildlife Service]]on October 23, 2013, and published for comment on January 29, 2014 (79 Fed. Reg. 4652).
