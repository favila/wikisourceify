{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to exempt sports betting from the tax on authorized wagers.   

=Section 1. Sports betting exempt from the excise tax on authorized wagers=



==(a) In general– ==

   [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/4402 Section 4402]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 

===(4) Sports betting authorized under State law– ===

On any wager which is authorized under the law of the State in which accepted and which is placed with respect to any sporting event. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to wagers placed after the date of the enactment of this Act.
