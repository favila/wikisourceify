{{Center|A BILL}}

 To rescind $45 billion of unobligated discretionary appropriations, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Decrease Spending Now Act”. 

=Sec. 2. Rescission of unobligated discretionary appropriations=



==(a) In general– ==

Of the unobligated balances of discretionary appropriations on the date of enactment of this Act, $45,000,000,000 is rescinded.

==(b) Implementation– ==



===(1) In general– ===

The [[w:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget]] shall determine which appropriation accounts the rescission under subsection (a) shall apply to and the amount that each such account shall be reduced by pursuant to such rescission.

===(2) Report– ===

Not later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget]] shall submit a report to the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]] and [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] listing the accounts reduced by the rescission in subsection (a) and the amounts rescinded from each such account.

==(c) Exceptions– ==

The rescission under subsection (a) shall not apply to the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] , the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] , or the [[w:Social Security Administration|Social Security Administration]] .
