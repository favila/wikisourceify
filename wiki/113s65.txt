{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the provision of law that provides automatic pay adjustments for Members of Congress.

={{anchor|ID9F52400697CF4204AC54A9128D46BEA7}}Section 1. Elimination of automatic pay adjustments for Members of Congress=



=={{anchor|IDAF6979AA86384907ADF608455869FD00}}(a) In general– ==

 Paragraph (2) of section 601(a) of the Legislative Reorganization Act of 1946([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/31 2 U.S.C. 31] )is repealed.

=={{anchor|IDE78F57C41823426CB9F8CB983407B084}}(b) Technical and conforming amendments– ==

 Section 601(a)(1) of such Actis amended—
:(1) by striking“(a)(1)”and inserting“(a)”;
:(2) by redesignating subparagraphs (A), (B), and (C)as paragraphs (1), (2), and (3), respectively; and
:(3) by striking“as adjusted by paragraph (2)of this subsection”and inserting“adjusted as provided by law”.

=={{anchor|IDBDE592ECB5564E5D8BB3D45728282EFF}}(c) Effective date– ==

This section and the amendments made by this section shall take effect on February 1, 2015.
