 To amend title 38, United States Code, to include contracts and grants for residential care for veterans in the exception to the requirement that the Federal Government recover a portion of the value of certain projects.   

=Section 1. Inclusion of contracts and grants for residential care for veterans in exception to certain recapture requirements=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8136 Section 8136(b) of title 38, United States Code], is amended by inserting“, or the provision by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] of a contract or grant for residential care for veterans,”after“outpatient clinic”.
