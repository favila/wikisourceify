{{Center|A BILL}}

To require the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to allow public access to research of the Department, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Veterans Affairs Research Transparency Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|idD2BC75D30D6149B5B28CC3145582783D}}Sec. 2. Public access to Department of Veterans Affairs research and data sharing between Departments=



=={{anchor|id9E66AAEC59B44E7D8BBCBBA2A39C0E43}}(a) Establishment of Internet website– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall make available on an Internet website of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] available to the public the following:
:(1) Data files that contain information on research of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]] .
:(2) A data dictionary on each data file.
:(3) Instructions for how to obtain access to each data file for use in research.

=={{anchor|idba56453bc2794ccd951ded1c323f704d}}(b) Public access to manuscripts on Department funded research– ==



==={{anchor|id54BAF845DA004FEBBCF20E91A9DBC099}}(1) In general– ===

Beginning not later than 540 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall require, as a condition on the use of any data gathered or formulated from research funded by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]] , that any final, peer-reviewed manuscript prepared for publication that uses such data be submitted to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] for deposit in the digital archive established under paragraph (2)and publication under paragraph (3).

==={{anchor|id8EC5DF12132E448E957FC7DF5F154172}}(2) Establishment of digital archive– ===

Not later than 540 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall establish a digital archive consisting of manuscripts described in paragraph (1).

==={{anchor|id791BCAD9F13F42F993C2B9BED218A0AC}}(3) Public availability– ===



===={{anchor|id319566F0E36841AB81ABAF0162873D9F}}(A) Availability of archive– ====

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall make the digital archive established under paragraph (2)and the contents of such archive available to the public via a publicly accessible Internet website at not cost to the public.

===={{anchor|id6B808CD4E0614285B4A2208ADC438286}}(B) Availability of manuscripts– ====

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall ensure that each manuscript submitted to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] under paragraph (1)is available to the public under subparagraph (A)not later than one year after the official date on which the manuscript is otherwise published.

==={{anchor|id46ED62A8B3BD418CB56B3D85030DB812}}(4) Consistent with copyright law– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall carry out this subsection in a manner consistent with applicable copyright law.

==={{anchor|id40806f779eae470d9654109ea94df93a}}(5) Annual report– ===



===={{anchor|idF54719260B324E719FFC3DD63DDE10F2}}(A) In general– ====

Not later than one year after the date the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] begins making manuscripts available to the public under this subsection and not less frequently than once each year thereafter, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall submit to the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans’ Affairs of the Senate]] and the [[w:United States House Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans’ Affairs of the House of Representatives]] a report on the implementation of this subsection during the most recent one-year period.

===={{anchor|id2B41B9C9D84E4906B83A2A9AFC7E0FBB}}(B) Contents– ====

Each report submitted under subparagraph (A)shall include for the period of the report:
:(i) The number of manuscripts submitted under paragraph (1).
:(ii) The titles of such manuscripts.
:(iii) The authors of such manuscripts.
:(iv) For each such manuscript, the name and issue number or volume number, as the case may be, of the journal or other publication in which such manuscript was published.

=={{anchor|idE2B32082EA6445C8A71CB3488F7989BF}}(c) Recommendations for data sharing between Department of Veterans Affairs and Department of Defense– ==

Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Department of Veterans Affairs-Department of Defense Joint Executive Committee established by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/320 section 320(a) of title 38, United States Code] , shall submit to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] and the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] options and recommendations for establishment of a program for long-term cooperation and data-sharing between and within the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] and the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] to facilitate research on outcomes of military service, readjustment after combat deployment, and other topics of importance to the following:
:(1) Veterans.
:(2) Members of the Armed Forces.
:(3) Family members of veterans.
:(4) Family members of members of the Armed Forces.
:(5) Members of communities that have a significant population of veterans or members of the Armed Forces.
