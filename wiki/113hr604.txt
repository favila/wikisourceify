{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide for the
		  eligibility under the Post-9/11 Educational Assistance Program of certain
		  individuals with service-connected disabilities who transfer to reserve
		  components before discharge from the Armed Forces.

={{anchor|H7A644C68F6D34B4C9D39B443789FF097}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Chris Kotch VA Education Access Act”.

={{anchor|H6CE6F8863AAE47D898D67A81A03B747B}}Sec. 2. Eligibility for
			 Post-9/11 Educational Assistance Program of certain individuals with
			 service-connected disabilities who transfer to reserve components before
			 discharge from the Armed Forces=



=={{anchor|H42DAFC201C2D426FBED841E7D57C6FE5}}(a) Eligibility– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 Section 3311(b)(2)(B) of title 38, United
			 States Code], is amended by striking “is discharged or released from
			 active duty in the Armed Forces” and inserting “is discharged or
			 released from the Armed Forces or transferred to the temporary disability
			 retirement list”.

=={{anchor|H25002C40A04E4F70938C417D58145666}}(b) Effective
			 date– ==

The amendment made by
			 subsection (a) shall take effect as if included in the Post-9/11 Veterans
			 Educational Assistance Act of 2008 ([[Public Law 110-252|Public Law 110–252]]).
