{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to confirm the scope of the authority of the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency to deny or restrict the use of defined areas as disposal sites.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Regulatory Fairness Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Permits for dredged or fill material=

   Section 404 of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/33/1344 33 U.S.C. 1344] )is amended by striking subsection (c) and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

==(c) Denial or restriction of use of specified disposal sites– ==



===(1) In general– ===

During and only during the period beginning on the date on which the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]]publishes the notice required under subsection (a) of this section and ending once the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]]issues a permit under such subsection, the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]may deny or restrict the use of any defined area identified in such notice as a specified disposal site if the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]determines, after notice and opportunity for public comment, that the discharge of dredged or fill material into such defined area will have an unacceptable adverse effect on municipal water supplies, shellfish beds and fishery areas (including spawning and breeding areas), wildlife, or recreational areas.

===(2) Consultation– ===

Before making a determination under paragraph (1) , the Administrator shall consult with the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]].

===(3) Explanation of determination– ===

The [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]shall set forth in writing and make publicly available—
:(A) any findings of the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]];
:(B) the basis of and reasons for making a determination under paragraph (1) ; and
:(C) all information and data reviewed in making any determination under paragraph (1) .

===(4) Previous action in absence of notice invalid– ===

No previous action by the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]to deny or restrict the use or prohibit the specification of any defined area as a disposal site, and which occurred in the absence of the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary’s]]publishing of a notice under subsection (a) of this section , is valid or otherwise enforceable.

===(5) Previous action after permit issued invalid– ===

No previous action by the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]to deny or restrict the use or prohibit the specification of any defined area as a disposal site and for which the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]]had issued a permit under subsection (a) of this section , and which occurred after the [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary]]issued the permit, is valid or otherwise enforceable. . 
</blockquote>
