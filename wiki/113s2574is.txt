{{Center|A BILL}}

 To make the United States Preventive Services Task Force subject to the Federal Advisory Committee Act.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Healthy Families Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. United States Preventive Services Task Force=

   Section 915(a) of the Public Health Service Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/299b–4 42 U.S.C. 299b–4(a)] )is amended by striking paragraph (5) and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

==(5) Applicability of FACA– ==

The  Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.)shall apply to the Task Force. . 
</blockquote>
