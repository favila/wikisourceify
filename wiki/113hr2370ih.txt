{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 to reauthorize the juvenile accountability block grants program through fiscal year 2016.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Juvenile Accountability Block Grants Program Reauthorization Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization of Juvenile Accountability Block Grants program through fiscal year 2016=

 Part R of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3796ee 42 U.S.C. 3796ee et seq.] )is amended—
:(1) in section 1801A(a), by striking “section 1810(b)” and inserting “ section 1810(c)” ;
:(2) in section 1810(a), by inserting “and each of the fiscal years 2014 through 2016” after “2009” ; and
:(3) in section 1810(b), by inserting “and each of the fiscal years 2014 through 2016” after “2004” .
