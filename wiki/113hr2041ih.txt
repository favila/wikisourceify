{{Center|A BILL}}

To modify the definition of fiduciary under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 to exclude appraisers of employee stock ownership plans.

=Section 1. Fiduciary exclusion=

 Section 3(21)(A) of the Employee Retirement Income and Security Act of 1974( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/1002 29 U.S.C. 1002(21)(A)] )is amended by inserting“and except to the extent a person is providing an appraisal or fairness opinion with respect to qualifying employer securities (as defined in section 407(d)(5)) included in an employee stock ownership plan (as defined in section 407(d)(6)),”after“subparagraph (B),”.
