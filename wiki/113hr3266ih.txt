{{Center|A BILL}}

Eliminating the debt ceiling for a period defined, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Fiscal Sanity Act for the National Debt” .

=Sec. 2. Nonapplicability of debt ceiling=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 Section 3101(b)of title 31, United States Code] , shall not apply for the period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act and ending 240 days thereafter.
