{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to allow certain veterans to use educational assistance provided by the Department of Veterans Affairs for franchise training.

={{anchor|H4DB8037E393A4194B2123ECDE64516F9}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Franchise Education for Veterans Act”.

={{anchor|HC5C018E15105409EBC60C8122004FC80}}Sec. 2. Use of educational assistance for franchise training=



=={{anchor|H093A45A366B24ACFACF3D561C7B826B1}}(a) GI Bill– ==



==={{anchor|H34900951B3194AE9A3B9CBCF181C6FE2}}(1) Program of education– ===

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3002 Subparagraph (C) of section 3002(3) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>
:(C) in the case of an individual who is not serving on active duty, includes—
::(i) a full-time program of apprenticeship or of other on-job training approved as provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3687 paragraph (1)] or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3687 (2), as appropriate, of section 3687(a) of this title] ;
::(ii) a cooperative program (as defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3482 section 3482(a)(2) of this title] ); and
::(iii) a training program at a training establishment described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3452 section 3452(e)(2) of this title] that—
:::(I) is approved for purposes ofchapter 32or34 of this title; and
:::(II) is for a franchise (as defined in section 436.1 of title 16, Code of Federal Regulations)..
</blockquote>


==={{anchor|H711D66E9CD954FA0990B53B98387611A}}(2) Limitations– ===

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3032 Section 3032 of such title] is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(h) 
::(1) Subject to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3032 paragraphs (3)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3032 (4)] , the amount of educational assistance payable under this chapter for a program of education consisting of a training program at a training establishment described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3452 section 3452(e)(2) of this title] shall be, with respect to any 12-month period in which such training is pursued, the lesser of the following:
:::(A) The sum of—
::::(i) the fees assessed by the training establishment concerned for the training;
::::(ii) a monthly housing stipend for each month (or pro rata amount for each partial month) of training pursued equal to the monthly amount of the basic allowance for housing payable under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/403 section 403 of title 37] for a member with dependents in pay grade E–5 residing in the military housing area that encompasses all or the majority portion of the ZIP code area in which is located the training establishment; and
::::(iii) a monthly stipend in an amount equal to $83 for each month (or pro rata amount for each partial month) of training pursued for books supplies, equipment, and other educational costs.
:::(B) $15,000.
::(2) The number of months of entitlement charged in the case of any individual for a training program described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3032 paragraph (1)] is equal to the number (including any fraction) determined by dividing the total amount of educational assistance paid such individual for such program by the full-time monthly institutional rate of educational assistance such individual would otherwise be paid under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3015 subsection (a)(1)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3015 (b)(1)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3015 (d)] , or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3015 (e)(1) of section 3015 of this title] , as the case may be.
::(3) 
:::(A) In no event shall payment of educational assistance under this subsection for training described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3032 paragraph (1)] exceed the amount of the individual's available entitlement under this chapter.
:::(B) With respect to an individual entitled to educational assistance under this subsection who is not described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3015 subsection (a)(1)] or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3015 (c)(1) of section 3015 of this title] , the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall adjust the amount payable under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3032 paragraph (1)] by an amount determined by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] to reflect the difference in the rate of educational assistance such individual would be paid under such section as compared to the rate an individual described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3015 subsection (a)(1)] or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3015 (c)(1) of such section] would be paid under such section.
::(4) A veteran may not receive—
:::(A) more than 12 months of educational assistance under this subsection; and
:::(B) a total amount of educational assistance under this subsection that is more than $15,000..
</blockquote>

==={{anchor|HF1E068EE87C3414393DEBAA86FC0C310}}(2) Franchise training– ===

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3313 Section 3313(g)(3) of such title] is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote>
:(E) Subject to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3313 clauses (ii)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3313 (iii)] , in the case of an individual pursuing a program of education consisting of a training program at a training establishment described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3452 section 3452(e)(2) of this title] , amounts, with respect to any 12-month period in which such training is pursued, as follows:
::(i) An amount equal to the lesser of—
:::(I) the sum of—
::::(aa) the fees assessed by the training establishment concerned for the training;
::::(bb) a monthly housing stipend for each month (or pro rata amount for each partial month) of training pursued equal to the monthly amount of the basic allowance for housing payable under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/37/403 section 403 of title 37] for a member with dependents in pay grade E–5 residing in the military housing area that encompasses all or the majority portion of the ZIP code area in which is located the training establishment; and
::::(cc) a monthly stipend in an amount equal to $83 for each month (or pro rata amount for each partial month) of training pursued for books supplies, equipment, and other educational costs; or
:::(II) $15,000.
::(ii) In the case of an individual entitled to educational assistance by reason of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3311 paragraphs (3) through (8) of sections 3311(b)] , the amounts payable pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3313 clause (i)] shall be the amounts otherwise determined pursuant to such clause multiplied by the same percentage applicable to the monthly amounts payable to the individual under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3313 paragraphs (2) through (7) of subsection (c)] .
::(iii) A veteran may not receive—
:::(I) more than 12 months of educational assistance under this subsection; and
:::(II) a total amount of educational assistance under this subsection that is more than $15,000..
</blockquote>


==={{anchor|H156FE6363DEE4619BC827449FA76C516}}(3) Payments– ===

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3313 Section 3313(g)(4)(C) of such title] is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(iii) Payment for the amount payable under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3313 paragraph (3)(E)] for pursuit of a program of education consisting of a training program at a training establishment described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3452 section 3452(e)(2) of this title] shall be paid to the individual upon the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] receiving a certification of enrollment with respect to the individual made by such establishment..
</blockquote>
