{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the National Historic Preservation Act to make Hispanic-serving institutions eligible for technical and financial assistance for the establishment of preservation training and degree programs.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “National Historic Preservation Act Amendment Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Eligibility of Hispanic-serving institutions for assistance for preservation education and training programs=

 Section 101(j)(2)(C) of the National Historic Preservation Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/470a 16 U.S.C. 470a(j)(2)(C)] )is amended by inserting “to Hispanic-serving institutions (as defined in section 502(a) of theHigher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1101a 20 U.S.C. 1101a(a)] ] )),” after “universities,” .
