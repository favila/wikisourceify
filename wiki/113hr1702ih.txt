{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to make permanent the authority of the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to transport individuals to and from facilities of the Department of Veterans Affairs in connection with rehabilitation, counseling, examination, treatment, and care.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Veterans Transportation Service Act” .

=Sec. 2. Transportation of individuals to and from facilities of Department of Veterans Affairs=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/111A Section 111A(a)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “(1) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] ” and inserting “The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] ” ; and
:(2) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/ paragraph (2)] .
