{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the further extension or establishment of national monuments in Arizona except by express authorization of Congress.

={{anchor|H089F9333D4254409BDE932CC7B392217}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Arizona Land Sovereignty Act”.

={{anchor|H618BC2EB45BD4E9EB8E93848FAAC3AEC}}Sec. 2. Limitation on further extension or establishment of national monuments in Arizona=

The proviso of the last sentence of the first section of the Act of September 14, 1950(64 Stat. 849, chapter 950;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 16 U.S.C. 431a] ), is amended by inserting“or Arizona”after“Wyoming”.
