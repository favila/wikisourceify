To modify the project for the improvement of the Shallotte River, North Carolina, to change the authorized depth to 8 feet.
1. Shallotte River, North Carolina


The project for the improvement of the Shallotte River, North Carolina, authorized by the Act of March 4, 1913 ( 37 Stat. 807 ) , is modified to change the authorized maintenance depth to 8 feet.
