{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}

 
{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To authorize the Administrator of General Services to convey a parcel of real property in Albuquerque, New Mexico, to the Amy Biehl High School Foundation.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Albuquerque, New Mexico, Federal Land Conveyance Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Definitions=

In this Act:

==(1) Administrator– ==

The term“Administrator”means the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator of General Services]].

==(2) Federal land– ==

The term“Federal land”means the real property, including any improvements thereon, located in Albuquerque, New Mexico, that, as determined by the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]], subject to survey, generally consists of lots 12 through 19, and for the westerly boundary, the portion of either lot 19 or 20 which is the outside west wall of the basement level of the Old Post Office building, and which has a municipal address of 123 Fourth Street, SW., in Block 18, New Mexico Town Company’s Original Townsite, Albuquerque, New Mexico.

==(3) Foundation– ==

The term“Foundation”means the Amy Biehl High School Foundation. 

=Sec. 3. Conveyance of real property in Albuquerque, New Mexico, to the Amy Biehl High School Foundation=



==(a) Conveyance– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]]shall offer to convey to the Foundation, by quitclaim deed, all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to the Federal land.

==(b) Consideration– ==

As consideration for conveyance of the Federal land under subsection (a) , the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]]shall require the Foundation to pay to the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]]consideration in an amount equal to the fair market value of the Federal land, as determined based on an appraisal that is acceptable to the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]].

==(c) Costs of conveyance– ==

The Foundation shall be responsible for paying—
:(1) the costs of an appraisal conducted under subsection (b) ; and
:(2) any other costs relating to the conveyance of the Federal land under this Act.

==(d) Proceeds– ==



===(1) Deposit– ===

Net proceeds received under subsection (b) shall be paid into the Federal Buildings Fund established under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/592  section 592of title 40, United States Code].

===(2) Expenditure– ===

Amounts paid into the Federal Buildings Fund under paragraph (1) shall be available to the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]], in amounts specified in appropriations Acts, for expenditure for any lawful purpose consistent with existing authorities granted to the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]].

==(e) Additional terms and conditions– ==

The [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]]may require that any conveyance under subsection (a) be subject to such additional terms and conditions as the [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]]considers appropriate to protect the interests of the United States.

==(f) Deadline– ==

The conveyance of the Federal land under this Act shall occur not later than 3 years after the date of enactment of this Act.    Passed the House of Representatives June 17, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
