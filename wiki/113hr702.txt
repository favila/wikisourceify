{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Public Health Service Act to direct the
		  Secretary of Health and Human Services to establish a Frontline Providers Loan
		  Repayment Program.

={{anchor|HA93998D4C00A484ABBF54714E3747DD2}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Access to Frontline Health Care Act of
			 2013”.

={{anchor|H9CF5A4DE5C674638A7A49D3194FB3EBD}}Sec. 2. Frontline
			 providers loan repayment program=

Part D of title III of the Public Health
			 Service Act (42 U.S.C. 254b et seq.) is amended—
:(1) by redesignating
			 the second subpart XI (as added by section 10333 of Public Law 111–148) as
			 subpart XII;
:(2) by redesignating
			 the second section 340H (as added by such section 10333) as section 340I;
			 and
:(3) by adding at the
			 end the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|HC6A48BAACC524A2B8DF4DF158E80D328}}XIII Frontline
				Health Care Services– ==



=={{anchor|H8895D5D8700D4D0FA1B0A6AF1DC2BFAA}}Sec. 340J. Frontline
				Providers Loan Repayment Program==



==={{anchor|HBAA9D65D07814ED2B72C7412806DB3F9}}(a) In
				General– ===

The [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall establish and carry out a Frontline
				Providers Loan Repayment Program (in this section referred to as the Loan
				Repayment Program) under which, pursuant to contracts in accordance with
				this section—
:(1) the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] agrees to make student loan
				repayments; and
:(2) the individual agrees to serve as a health
				professional for a period of full-time service of not less than 2 years at a
				health care facility serving a frontline care scarcity area.

==={{anchor|HC4D6083979B74D77ACC09075AAAD53FB}}(b) Eligibility– ===

To
				be eligible to participate in the Loan Repayment Program, an individual
				must—
:(1) submit an
				application to participate in the Loan Repayment Program in such form and
				manner and at such time as specified by the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]; and
:(2) sign and submit to
				the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]], at the time of submittal of such application, a written contract
				(described in subsection (d)).

==={{anchor|HEC152984AFBA4DF7AF31E8324264DF8E}}(c) Participation in
				Program– ===



===={{anchor|H92DDEB9F080E4733874E9D3F5998C122}}(1) In
				general– ====

An individual becomes a participant in the Loan Repayment
				Program only upon the approval of the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] of the individual’s application
				submitted under subsection (b)(1) and the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary’s]] acceptance of the
				contract submitted by the individual under subsection (b)(2).

===={{anchor|HF1D57DD6289F4944818C9775E314F934}}(2) Preference– ====

In
				awarding contracts under this section, the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall give preference to
				applicants who have undertaken training or coursework in interdisciplinary
				studies.

===={{anchor|H6E0CF22F6DFB497A9D1BD679CBA283C0}}(3) Recruitment for
				Interdisciplinary Programs– ====

The [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall—
:(A) determine the
				frontline care scarcity areas in which to place contract recipients under this
				section; and
:(B) in making such
				determination, give preference to areas with a demonstrated program of
				interdisciplinary health care, or with demonstrated plans to initiate
				interdisciplinary approaches to community health care.

===={{anchor|HD11E7CC8D026433A8250B501C857E019}}(4) Notice– ====

The
				[[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall provide written notice to an individual promptly upon the
				[[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary’s]] approving, under paragraph (1), of the individual’s participation
				in the Loan Repayment Program.

==={{anchor|H20503D9C8202404DB05D83354AEE7AA2}}(d) Contract– ===

The
				contract described in this subsection is a written contract between the
				[[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] and an individual that contains—
:(1) an agreement
				that—
::(A) the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]
				agrees to provide the individual with student loan repayment (described in
				subsection (e)) for a period of time as determined by the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]], to pay off
				debts incurred during the course of the study or program described in
				subsection (g)(2)(B); and
::(B) the individual
				agrees—
:::(i) to
				accept provision of such a student loan repayment to the individual; and
:::(ii) to provide frontline care services for a
				period of full-time service of not less than 2 years at a health care facility
				serving a frontline care scarcity area;
:(2) a provision that
				any financial obligation of the United States arising out of a contract entered
				into under this section and any obligation of the individual which is
				conditioned thereon, is contingent upon funds being appropriated for student
				loan repayment under this section;
:(3) a statement of the
				damages to which the United States is entitled, under subsection (f), for the
				individual’s breach of the contract; and
:(4) such other
				statements as the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] deems appropriate of the rights and liabilities of
				the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] and of the individual, not inconsistent with the provisions of
				this section.

==={{anchor|HB29DF9558ECD428D8C47FB21EC40171E}}(e) Student Loan
				Repayment– ===



===={{anchor|HB29D4E0C204546619BF39D00AB502BBF}}(1) Amount– ====

The
				amount of an annual student loan repayment under this section on behalf of an
				individual shall be determined by the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]], and shall take into
				consideration the need to pay a sufficient amount to enable recruiting of
				health care providers into the loan repayment program under this
				section.

===={{anchor|H0D42D8FDEA874629A7D1DF28EF286596}}(2) Payments
				Directly to Loan Provider– ====

The [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] may contract with an
				individual’s loan provider, for the payment to the loan provider, on behalf of
				the individual, of the amounts of a student loan repayment described in
				paragraph (1).

==={{anchor|H78B7DA562C0C4CB480042BB0B6304D57}}(f) Breach of
				contract– ===

If an individual
				breaches a written contract under this section by failing to begin such
				individual’s service obligation, or to complete such service obligation, the
				United States shall be entitled to recover from the individual an amount that
				is equal to the sum of—
:(1) the total amount
				which has been paid to the individual, or on behalf of the individual, under
				the contract; and
:(2) any amount of
				interest, as determined by the [[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]].

==={{anchor|H6AD6FD5BDD6641D2A6E7677A54D65E18}}(g) Definitions– ===

In
				this section:
:(1) The term
				frontline care scarcity area means an area, population group, or
				facility that—
::(A) is designated as a
				health professional shortage area under section 332; or
::(B) is designated by
				the State in which the area is located as having a shortage of frontline care
				services.
:(2) The term
				frontline care services means health care services—
::(A) in the field of general surgery, optometry,
				ophthalmology, chiropractic, physical therapy, audiology, speech language
				pathology, pharmacies, public health, podiatric medicine, dietetics,
				occupational therapy, general pediatrics, respiratory therapy, medical
				technology, otolaryngology, or radiologic technology; and
::(B) provided by a general surgeon, optometrist,
				ophthalmologist, chiropractor, physical therapist, audiologist, speech language
				pathologist, pharmacist, public health professional, podiatric physician,
				registered dietician, occupational therapist, pediatrician, respiratory
				therapist, medical technologist, otolaryngologist, or radiologic technologist
				who has completed an appropriate course of study or program, offered by an
				accredited institution of higher education in the United States.

==={{anchor|HDA8931E7AA04481B9BCF5966CA0B9F6A}}(h) Implementation– ===

The
				[[Wikipedia:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall begin implementation of the loan repayment program under this
				section within 180 days of the date of the enactment of this
				section..
</blockquote>
