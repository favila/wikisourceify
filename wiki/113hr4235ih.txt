{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to remove the maximum payment amount for certain qualified losses under the Traumatic Injury Protection under the Ser­vice­mem­bers’ Group Life Insurance program.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Taylor Morris Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Removal of maximum payment amounts for certain qualified losses under Traumatic Injury Protection under Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance program=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1980A  Section 1980A(d)of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1980A paragraph (1)], by striking“and the maximum payment”and inserting“and, except as provided by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1980A paragraph (3)(A)], the maximum”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
::(3) 
:::(A) The maximum payment amount described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1980A paragraph (1)]shall not apply to a qualifying loss directly caused by a traumatic event described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1980A subparagraph (B)]. The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]shall determine the payment schedule for such qualifying losses as the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]determines appropriate without regard to such maximum payment amount.
:::(B) A traumatic event described in this subparagraph is one of the following:
::::(i) Armed conflict.
::::(ii) Training under conditions simulating armed conflict.
::::(iii) Hazardous service.
::::(iv) A traumatic event caused through an instrumentality of war. . 
</blockquote>
