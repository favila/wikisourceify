{{Center|A BILL}}

 To limit the level of premium subsidy provided by the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation to agricultural producers.   

=Section 1. Limitation on payment of portion of premium by Corporation=

   Section 508(e) of the Federal Crop Insurance Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/1508 7 U.S.C. 1508(e)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(9) Limitation– ==



===(A) In general– ===

Notwithstanding any other provision of this title, the total amount of premium paid by the [[w:Federal Crop Insurance Corporation|Corporation]]on behalf of a person or legal entity, directly or indirectly, with respect to all policies issued to the person or legal entity under this title for a crop year shall be limited to a maximum of $70,000.

===(B) Relationship to other law– ===

To the maximum extent practicable, the [[w:Federal Crop Insurance Corporation|Corporation]]shall carry out this paragraph in accordance with  section 1001 of the Food Security Act of 1985 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/1308 7 U.S.C. 1308]). . 
</blockquote>
