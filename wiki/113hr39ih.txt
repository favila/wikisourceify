{{Center|A BILL}}

To reauthorize the African Elephant Conservation Act, the Rhinoceros and Tiger Conservation Act of 1994, and the Asian Elephant Conservation Act of 1997.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Multinational Species Conservation Funds Reauthorization Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization of African Elephant Conservation Act=

 Section 2306(a) of the African Elephant Conservation Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/4245 16 U.S.C. 4245(a)] )is amended by striking “2007 through 2012” and inserting “2014 through 2018” .

=Sec. 3. Reauthorization of Rhinoceros and Tiger Conservation Act of 1994=

 Section 10(a) of the Rhinoceros and Tiger Conservation Act of 1994( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/5306 16 U.S.C. 5306(a)] )is amended by striking “2007 through 2012” and inserting “2014 through 2018” .

=Sec. 4. Reauthorization of Asian Elephant Conservation Act of 1997=

 Section 8(a) of the Asian Elephant Conservation Act of 1997( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/4266 16 U.S.C. 4266(a)] )is amended by striking “2007 through 2012” and inserting “2014 through 2018” .
