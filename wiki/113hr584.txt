={{anchor|H796A9D577A0147A0A14518BACBC854B4}}Section 1. Amendment to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act regarding genetically engineered salmon=

Section 403 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (21 U.S.C. 343) is further amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(z) If it contains genetically engineered fish unless the food bears a label stating that the food contains genetically engineered fish..
</blockquote>
