To amend the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act to require labeling of genetically engineered fish.
1. Amendment to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act regarding genetically engineered salmon


Section 403 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ( 21 U.S.C. 343 ) is further amended by adding at the end the following:

: If it contains genetically engineered fish unless the food bears a label stating that the food contains genetically engineered fish..
