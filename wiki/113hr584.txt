{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act to require labeling of genetically engineered fish.


{{section|H796A9D577A0147A0A14518BACBC854B4|1. Amendment to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act regarding genetically engineered salmon}}

        Section 403 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act
 (21 U.S.C. 343
)
 is further amended by adding at the end the following:
(z)
If it contains genetically engineered fish unless the food bears a label stating that the food contains genetically engineered fish.
