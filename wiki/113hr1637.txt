{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the provisions of title 40, United States Code, commonly known as the Davis-Bacon Act, to raise the threshold dollar amount of contracts subject to the prevailing wage requirements of such provisions.

={{anchor|H8313A993B7924BF593390A1460BB2B81}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Adjusting Davis-Bacon for Inflation Act”.

={{anchor|H60B152707EC64E968707CD5351CF66F8}}Sec. 2. Threshold of contracts subject to Davis-Bacon=

Section 3142(a) ofchapter 31of title 40, United States Codeis amended by striking“$2,000”and inserting“$50,000”.
