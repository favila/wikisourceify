Electing Members to the Joint Committee of Congress on the Library and the Joint Committee on Printing.

=Section 1. Election of Members to Joint Committee of Congress on the Library and Joint Committee on Printing=



==(a) Joint Committee of Congress on the Library– ==

The following Members are hereby elected to the [[w:Joint Committee on the Library|Joint Committee of Congress on the Library]] , to serve with the chair of the [[w:United States House Committee on House Administration|Committee on House Administration]] and the chair of the [[w:United States House Appropriations Subcommittee on the Legislative Branch|Subcommittee on the Legislative Branch of the Committee on Appropriations]] :
:(1) 
[[w:Gregg Harper|Mr. Harper]] .
:(2) 
[[w:Bob Brady|Mr. Brady of Pennsylvania]] .
:(3) 
[[w:Zoe Lofgren|Ms. Zoe Lofgren of California]] .

==(b) Joint Committee on Printing– ==

The following Members are hereby elected to the [[w:Joint Committee on Printing|Joint Committee on Printing]] , to serve with the chair of the [[w:United States House Committee on House Administration|Committee on House Administration]] :
:(1) 
[[w:Gregg Harper|Mr. Harper]] .
:(2) 
[[w:Rich Nugent|Mr. Nugent]] .
:(3) 
[[w:Bob Brady|Mr. Brady of Pennsylvania]] .
:(4) 
[[w:Juan Vargas|Mr. Vargas]] .
