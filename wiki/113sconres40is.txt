{{Center|CONCURRENT RESOLUTION}}

 Authorizing the use of Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center for a ceremony to award Congressional Gold Medals in honor of the men and women who perished as a result of the terrorist attacks on the United States on September 11, 2001.   

=Section 1. Use of Emancipation Hall for gold medal ceremony in honor of fallen heroes of 9/11=

Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center is authorized to be used on September 10, 2014, for a ceremony to award Congressional Gold Medals in honor of the men and women who perished as a result of the terrorist attacks on the United States on September 11, 2001. Physical preparations for the conduct of the ceremony shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as may be prescribed by the [[w:Architect of the Capitol|Architect of the Capitol]].
