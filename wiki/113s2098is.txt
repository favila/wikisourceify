{{Center|A BILL}}

 To ratify and approve certain payments to school districts serving Yellowstone National Park.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Yellowstone Community Education Fairness Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Ratification of payments to school districts serving Yellowstone National Park=

All payments made to school districts under thefirst section of the Act of June 4, 1948 (62 Stat. 338, chapter 417;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/40a 16 U.S.C. 40a]), during the period beginning in fiscal year 1976 and ending on the date of enactment of this Act are ratified and approved, notwithstanding the payments made underchapter 69 of title 31, United States Code, to the units of general local government.
