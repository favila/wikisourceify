{{Center|A BILL}}

 To provide for a lifetime National Recreational Pass for any veteran with a service-connected disability, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Wounded Veterans Recreation Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. National recreational passes for disabled veterans=

   Section 805(b)(2) of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/6804 16 U.S.C. 6804(b)(2)] )is amended as follows:
:(1) By inserting“and for the lifetime of the passholder”after“without charge”.
:(2) By striking“charge, to”and inserting“charge, to the following:”.
:(3) By striking“any United States”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 
::(A) Any United States . 
</blockquote>

:(4) By inserting after“residency.”the following:

<blockquote> 
::(B) Any veteran with a service-connected disability, as defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101  section 101of title 38, United States Code]. . 
</blockquote>

:(5) By striking the last sentence.
