{{Center|A BILL}}

 To allow States to waive regulations promulgated under the Clean Air Act relating to electric generating units under certain circumstances.   

=Section 1. Waiver of certain regulations promulgated under the Clean Air Act=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a State may permanently waive any regulation relating to electric generating units promulgated by the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency]]under the  Clean Air Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/7401 42 U.S.C. 7401 et seq].)if the Governor of the State certifies that the electric generating units are necessary for reasons of power reliability and affordability.
