{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide for annual cost-of-living adjustments to be made automatically by law each year in the rates of disability compensation for veterans with service-connected disabilities and the rates of dependency and indemnity compensation for survivors of certain service-connected disabled veterans.

={{anchor|H77079901764D4B469851D1629D10AFDC}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“American Heroes COLA Act”.

={{anchor|HE70964128E894F44851D9FE67D2A8E69}}Sec. 2. Automatic annual increase in rates of disability compensation and dependency and indemnity compensation=



=={{anchor|H3ED26F1EA71D4EF08755B42C71CBED0D}}(a) Indexing to social security increases– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5312 Section 5312 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>
:(d) 
::(1) Whenever there is an increase in benefit amounts payable under title II of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/401 42 U.S.C. 401 et seq.] )as a result of a determination made under section 215(i) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/415 42 U.S.C. 415(i)] ), the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall, effective on the date of such increase in benefit amounts, increase the dollar amounts in effect for the payment of disability compensation and dependency and indemnity compensation by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] , as specified in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5312 paragraph (2)] , as such amounts were in effect immediately before the date of such increase in benefit amounts payable under title II of the Social Security Act, by the same percentage as the percentage by which such benefit amounts are increased.
::(2) The dollar amounts to be increased pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5312 paragraph (1)] are the following:

====={{anchor|H32DEDC172FC84E26B7F2418584233063}}(A) Wartime disability compensation– =====

Each of the dollar amounts in effect under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1114 section 1114 of this title] .

====={{anchor|H512CA5CCEE3040258BA3B606E1E937D8}}(B) Additional compensation for dependents– =====

Each of the dollar amounts in effect under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1115 section 1115(1) of this title] .

====={{anchor|H185965F16C9142B4832AAB60B922C1B3}}(C) Clothing allowance– =====

The dollar amount in effect under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1162 section 1162 of this title] .

====={{anchor|H69D667E9F82044658BA90DEC2948F34E}}(D) Dependency and indemnity compensation to surviving spouse– =====

Each of the dollar amounts in effect under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1311 subsections (a) through (d) of section 1311 of such title] .

====={{anchor|HF23A265E7AC54B7FB76A7D984E24A3BC}}(E) Dependency and indemnity compensation to children– =====

Each of the dollar amounts in effect under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1313 sections 1313(a)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1314 1314 of such title] .
::(3) Whenever there is an increase under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5312 paragraph (1)] in amounts in effect for the payment of disability compensation and dependency and indemnity compensation, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall publish such amounts, as increased pursuant to such paragraph, in the Federal Register at the same time as the material required by section 215(i)(2)(D) of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/415 42 U.S.C. 415(i)(2)(D)] )is published by reason of a determination under section 215(i) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/415 42 U.S.C. 415(i)] ).
::(4) Each dollar amount increased under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5312 paragraph (1)] , if not a whole dollar amount, shall be rounded to the next lower whole dollar amount.
::(5) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] may adjust administratively, consistent with the increases made under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5312 subsection (a)] , the rates of disability compensation payable to persons under[[Public Law 85-857|section 10 of Public Law 85–857]] (72 Stat. 1263)who have not received compensation underchapter 11 of this title..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HCEEC3EB3B49D41729B1A8F81C1B2153C}}(b) Effective date– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5312 Subsection (d) of section 5312 of title 38, United States Code] , as added by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5312 subsection (a)] of this section, shall take effect on December 1, 2014.
