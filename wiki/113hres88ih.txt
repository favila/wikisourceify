{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to prohibit the consideration of any bill or joint resolution that appropriates foreign assistance for more than one country.That rule XXI of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote>
12. Limitation on foreign assistance for more than one countryIt shall not be in order to consider any bill or joint resolution that appropriates foreign assistance for more than one country..
</blockquote>
