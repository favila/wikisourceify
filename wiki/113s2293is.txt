{{Center|A BILL}}

 To clarify the status of the North Country, Ice Age, and New England National Scenic Trails as units of the National Park System, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   National Scenic Trails Parity Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. North Country National Scenic Trail=

   Section 5(a)(8) of the National Trails System Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1244 16 U.S.C. 1244(a)(8)] )is amended in the third sentence by inserting“as a unit of the [[w:National Park Service|National Park System]] ”before the period at the end. 

=Sec. 3. Ice Age National Scenic Trail=

   Section 5(a)(10) of the National Trails System Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1244 16 U.S.C. 1244(a)(10)] )is amended by striking the third and fourth sentences and inserting“The trail shall be administered by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]]as a unit of the [[w:National Park Service|National Park System]].”. 

=Sec. 4. New England National Scenic Trail=

   Section 5(a)(28) of the National Trails System Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1244 16 U.S.C. 1244(a)(28)] )is amended in the third sentence by inserting“as a unit of the [[w:National Park Service|National Park System]],”after“administer the trail”.
