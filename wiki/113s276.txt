{{Center|A BILL}}

To reinstate and extend the deadline for commencement of construction of a hydroelectric project involving the American Falls Reservoir.

={{anchor|ID92DC392DF5EE4D9496F40000BAEF0943}}Section 1. Extension of time for Federal Energy Regulatory Commission project involving American Falls Reservoir=

Notwithstanding the time period specified in section 13 of the Federal Power Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/806 16 U.S.C. 806] ] )that would otherwise apply to the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Federal Energy Regulatory Commission]] project numbered 12423, the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Federal Energy Regulatory Commission]] shall, at the request of the licensee for the project, and after reasonable notice and in accordance with the procedures of the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Commission]] under that section, reinstate the license and extend the time period during which the licensee is required to commence the construction of project works to the end of the 3-year period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act.
