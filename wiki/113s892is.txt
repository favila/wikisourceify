{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Iran Threat Reduction and Syria Human Rights Act of 2012 to impose sanctions with respect to certain transactions in foreign currencies, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Iran Sanctions Loophole Elimination Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|idE54F656CE4234DDAB45DEE28745C655D}}Sec. 2. Imposition of sanctions with respect to certain transactions in foreign currencies=



=={{anchor|id854E34A33AB940E0AEEBBB9CA0D107F5}}(a) Imposition of sanctions– ==

 Subtitle B of title II of the Iran Threat Reduction and Syria Human Rights Act of 2012([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/8721 22 U.S.C. 8721 et seq.] ] )is amended by inserting after section 220the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|id793BC7815013492BB9D9DC2CFA4894DC}}Sec. 220A. Imposition of sanctions with respect to certain transactions in foreign currencies==



==={{anchor|id9E608329FC3D4E60874439C7887955EC}}(a) In general– ===

the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] —
:(1) shall prohibit the opening, and prohibit or impose strict conditions on the maintaining, in the United States of a correspondent account or a payable-through account by a foreign financial institution that is a person described in subsection (b); and
:(2) may impose sanctions pursuant to the International Emergency Economic Powers Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/50/1701 50 U.S.C. 1701 et seq.] )with respect to any other person described in subsection (b).

==={{anchor|id4C0E385560EB4D50B02645F318A15E5B}}(b) Person described– ===

A person described in this subsection is a person the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] determines has, on or after May 9, 2013—
:(1) knowingly conducted or facilitated a significant transaction involving the currency of a country other than the country in which the person is operating at the time of the transaction with, for, or on behalf of—
::(A) the Central Bank of Iran or another Iranian financial institution designated by the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]] for the imposition of sanctions pursuant to the International Emergency Economic Powers Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/50/1701 50 U.S.C. 1701 et seq.] ] ); or
::(B) a person described in section 1244(c)(2) of the Iran Freedom and Counter-Proliferation Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/8803 22 U.S.C. 8803(c)(2)] ] )(other than a person described in subparagraph (C)(iii) of that section); or
:(2) knowingly transferred funds for, or held funds on behalf of, a person described in paragraph (1)in relation to a transaction described in that paragraph, without regard to the currency of those funds.

==={{anchor|id4C6E09E491DF44B8ABB42348A47B8350}}(c) Waiver– ===



===={{anchor|id896713CD406A44E9B19D2E8A153F199F}}(1) In general– ====

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] may waive the application of subsection (a)with respect to a person for a period of not more than 180 days, and may renew that waiver for additional periods of not more than 180 days, if the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] —
:(A) determines that the waiver is vital to the national security of the United States; and
:(B) not less than 7 days before the waiver or the renewal of the waiver, as the case may be, takes effect, submits a report to the appropriate congressional committees on the waiver and the reason for the waiver.

===={{anchor|idB1CB873A7D3B47D69071A455EC8D819B}}(2) Form of report– ====

Each report submitted under paragraph (1)(B)shall be submitted in unclassified form but may include a classified annex.

==={{anchor|idA4351503EE5D428198608A2E431A2B44}}(d) Rule of construction– ===

Nothing in this section shall be construed to prohibit any person from, or authorize or require the imposition of sanctions with respect to any person for, conducting or facilitating any transaction in the currency of the country in which the person is operating at the time of the transaction for the sale of agricultural commodities, food, medicine, or medical devices.

==={{anchor|id5cfadd8501314db7a864bc6c24005b6c}}(e) Definitions– ===

In this section:

===={{anchor|id653a9d2efb9e4df3addf8dd478fce367}}(1) Account; correspondent account; payable-through account– ====

The terms account , correspondent account , and payable-through account have the meanings given those terms in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5318A [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 5318A] of title 31, United States Code] .

===={{anchor|P5270DE25400732B9B68361B365BAC23}}(2) Agricultural commodity– ====

The term agricultural commodity has the meaning given that term in section 102 of the Agricultural Trade Act of 1978([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/5602 7 U.S.C. 5602] ).

===={{anchor|id4c67c936b2ae4f83bdca35c31dbcf2d6}}(3) Foreign financial institution– ====

The term foreign financial institution has the meaning given that term in section 561.308 of title 31, Code of Federal Regulations (or any corresponding similar regulation or ruling).

===={{anchor|id658F3CC4308048118A9F646634FCE767}}(4) Iranian financial institution– ====

The term Iranian financial institution has the meaning given that term in section 104A(d) of the Comprehensive Iran Sanctions, Accountability, and Divestment Act of 2010([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/22/8513b 22 U.S.C. 8513b(d)] ).

===={{anchor|P02B5627242033D5536E0A384C8C4558}}(5) Medical device– ====

The term medical device has the meaning given the term device in section 201 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/21/321 21 U.S.C. 321] ] ).

===={{anchor|P7393AAFB4668B4F66C68BFB6AE4C78B}}(6) Medicine– ====

The term medicine has the meaning given the term drug in section 201 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/21/321 21 U.S.C. 321] ] ).

===={{anchor|id3C19ED5487B04AEABBE6D184ADB11456}}(7) Transaction– ====

The term transaction includes a foreign exchange swap, a foreign exchange forward, and any other type of currency exchange or conversion or derivative instrument..
</blockquote>
