{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for additional Federal district judgeships for the eastern district of California, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Central Valley Judicial Relief Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Federal district judgeships in California=



==(a) Additional permanent district judgeships– ==

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall appoint, by and with the advice and consent of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] , 6 additional district judges for the eastern district of California.

==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

The table contained in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/133 section 133(a)of title 28, United States Code] , is amended by striking the item relating to California and inserting the following:

<blockquote>California:Northern14Eastern12Central27Southern13.
</blockquote>


==(c) Temporary judgeship– ==



===(1) In general– ===

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall appoint, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate, one additional district judge for the eastern district of California.

===(2) Vacancy not filled– ===

The first vacancy in the office of district judge in the eastern district of California, occurring 10 years or more after the confirmation date of the judge named to fill the temporary district judgeship created by this subsection, shall not be filled.

=Sec. 3. Additional place of holding court=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/84 Section 84(b)of title 28, United States Code] , is amended in the second sentence by inserting “Bakersfield,” after “shall be held at” .
