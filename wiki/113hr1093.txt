{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Assistant Secretary of Homeland Security (Transportation Security Administration) to prohibit airplane passengers from bringing aboard a passenger aircraft any item that was prohibited as of March 1, 2013.


{{section|H4746D4862A83423E83FA93195AE05A7E|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the 
          No Knives Act of 2013

        
.

{{section|H70D73EBE612240E897354A9FBE4983B4|2. Requirement to prohibit items on passenger airplanes}}
The Assistant Secretary of Homeland Security ([[Transportation Security Administration|Transportation Security Administration]]) shall prohibit carriage aboard a passenger aircraft operated by an air carrier or foreign air carrier in air transportation or intrastate air transportation of any item that was prohibited from being carried aboard such an aircraft on March 1, 2013.
