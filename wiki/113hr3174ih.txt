{{Center|A BILL}}

 To authorize the Secretary of Transportation to obligate funds for emergency relief projects arising from damage caused by severe weather events in 2013, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Emergency relief projects=

The [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]]may obligate any funds made available to implement[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/125  section 125of title 23, United States Code], for emergency relief projects arising from damage caused by severe weather events in 2013, without any limitation on obligations for such projects resulting from a single natural disaster or a single catastrophic failure in a State.
