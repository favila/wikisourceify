{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit funding of the Rural Utilities Service High Energy Cost Grant Program.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on funding of the Rural Utilities Service High Energy Cost Grant Program=

No funds available to the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Department of Agriculture]]or any other department or agency may be used to provide funding directly or indirectly for grants under section 19(a)(1) of the Rural Electrification Act of 1936 .
