{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the National Marine Sanctuaries Act to prescribe an additional requirement for the designation of marine sanctuaries off the coast of Alaska.   

=Section 1. Limitation on authority to designate marine sanctuaries=

   Section 303 of the National Marine Sanctuaries Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1433 16 U.S.C. 1433] )is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a), by striking“The Secretary may”and inserting“Subject to subsection (c), the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary]]may”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

===(c) Limitation– ===

The [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary]]may not designate as a national marine sanctuary any area of the marine environment off the coast of Alaska seaward of the high tide line unless an Act of Congress—
:(1) requires the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary]]to make such a designation; and
:(2) prescribes the boundaries of the area to be given such designation by the [[w:United States Department of Commerce|Secretary]]. . 
</blockquote>
