 Providing for a correction in the enrollment of H.R. 3547.    That in the enrollment of the billH.R. 3547, the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Clerk of the House of Representatives]]shall amend the long title so as to read:“Making consolidated appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2014, and for other purposes.”.     Passed the House of Representatives January 15, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
