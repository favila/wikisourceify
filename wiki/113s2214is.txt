{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prevent a taxpayer bailout of health insurance issuers.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Obamacare Taxpayer Bailout Protection Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prevention of bailout of health insurance issuers=

   Section 1342 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18062 42 U.S.C. 18062])is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(d) Ensuring budget neutrality– ==

In implementing this section, the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]shall ensure that payments out and payments in under paragraphs (1) and (2) of subsection (b) are provided for in amounts that the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]determines are necessary to reduce to zero the cost (as defined in  section 502 of the Federal Credit Reform Act of 1990 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/661a 2 U.S.C. 661a])) to the Federal Government of carrying out the program under this section. . 
</blockquote>
