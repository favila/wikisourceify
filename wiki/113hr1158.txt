{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Secretary of the Interior to continue stocking fish in certain lakes in the North Cascades National Park, Ross Lake National Recreation Area, and Lake Chelan National Recreation Area.

={{anchor|H615FFDDEACD64C26902ADB796FCF783B}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“North Cascades National Park Service Complex Fish Stocking Act”.

={{anchor|H19A842C29CDD4F579DA5BF886446EEB4}}Sec. 2. Definitions=

In this Act:

=={{anchor|H19802344CA194957979E3586099C20E7}}(1) North cascades national park service complex– ==

The term North Cascades National Park Service Complex means collectively the North Cascades National Park, Ross Lake National Recreation Area, and Lake Chelan National Recreation Area.

=={{anchor|H1151EE6D8DF443F8833B68274ABE98F8}}(2) Plan– ==

The term plan means the document entitled“North Cascades National Park Service Complex Mountain Lakes Fishery Management Plan and Environmental Impact Statement”and dated June 2008.

=={{anchor|H265788963D104EB8BC9DDFE1DE216C5B}}(3) Secretary– ==

The term Secretary means the Secretary of the Interior.

={{anchor|H6EBA38D6DC10451195362781AF6BFA4F}}Sec. 3. Stocking of certain lakes in the north cascades national park service complex=



=={{anchor|HEF343D0EC39E42F49168C931A0E2EE73}}(a) In general– ==

Subject to subsection (b), the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall authorize the stocking of fish in lakes in the [[w:National Park Service|North Cascades National Park Service Complex]] .

=={{anchor|HF079BA11584D47EEB595F1FE3312F502}}(b) Conditions– ==



==={{anchor|H25CBA0B478DD4A05A9191A6A5AB3356F}}(1) In general– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] is authorized to allow stocking of fish in not more than 42 of the 91 lakes in the [[w:National Park Service|North Cascades National Park Service Complex]] that have historically been stocked with fish.

==={{anchor|HFBD56DFC75014B20AABE7D7A0266DB4F}}(2) Native nonreproducing fish– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall only stock fish that are—
:(A) native to the slope of the Cascade Range on which the lake to be stocked is located; and
:(B) nonreproducing, as identified in management alternative B of the plan.

==={{anchor|HA5CB67344E27456DA5CE9BD0CACCFDA8}}(3) Considerations– ===

In making fish stocking decisions under this Act, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall consider relevant scientific information, including the plan and information gathered under subsection (c).

==={{anchor|HCA4E043EACBB47BFB8B734A45288391D}}(4) Required coordination– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall coordinate the stocking of fish under this Act with the State of Washington.

=={{anchor|H2FF8022421C14AD283A71242DB218BFB}}(c) Research and monitoring– ==

The Secretary shall—
:(1) continue a program of research and monitoring of the impacts of fish stocking on the resources of the applicable unit of the [[w:National Park Service|North Cascades National Park Service Complex]] ; and
:(2) beginning on the date that is 5 years after the date of enactment of this Act and every 5 years thereafter, submit to theCommittee on Energy and Natural Resources of the Senateand theCommittee on Natural Resources of the House of Representativesa report that describes the results of the research and monitoring under paragraph (1).
