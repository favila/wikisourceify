{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Expressing the condolences of the House on the death of the Honorable C.W. Bill Young, a Representative from the State of Florida.That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of the
[[w:Bill Young|Honorable C.W. Bill Young]] , a Representative from the State of Florida.That a committee of such Members of the House as the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Speaker]] may designate, together with such Members of the Senate as may be joined, be appointed to attend the funeral.That the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Sergeant-at-Arms of the House]] be authorized and directed to take such steps as may be necessary for carrying out the provisions of these resolutions and that the necessary expenses in connection therewith be paid out of applicable accounts of the House.That the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Clerk]] communicate these resolutions to the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] and transmit a copy thereof to the family of the deceased.That when the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] adjourns today, it adjourn as a further mark of respect to the memory of the deceased.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
