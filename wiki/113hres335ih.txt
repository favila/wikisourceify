{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

To refer H.R. 3068, a bill making congressional reference to the United States Court of Federal Claims pursuant to sections [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/1492 1492] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/2509 2509] of title 28, United States Code, the Indian trust-related claims of the Grand Traverse Band of Ottawa and Chippewa Indians of Michigan as well as its individual members.

=Section 1. Referral=

Pursuant to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/1492 section 1492of title 28, United States Code] , the bill (H.R. 3068), entitled “ A Bill relating to members of the Grand Traverse Band of Ottawa and Chippewa Indians of Michigan,” now pending in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] , is referred to the chief judge of the [[w:U.S. Court of Federal Claims|United States Court of Federal Claims]] for a determination as to whether the tribe and its members have Indian trust-related legal or equitable claims against the United States other than the legal claims that are pending in the [[w:U.S. Court of Federal Claims|Court of Federal Claims]] on the date of enactment of this resolution.

=Sec. 2. Proceeding and report=

Upon receipt of the bill, the chief judge shall—
:(1) proceed according to the provisions of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/1492 sections1492] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/2509 2509of title 28, United States Code] , notwithstanding the bar of any statute of limitations; and
:(2) report back to the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] , at the earliest practicable date, providing—
::(A) findings of fact and conclusions of law that are sufficient to inform the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] of the nature, extent, and character of the Indian-trust related claims of the Grand Traverse Band of Ottawa and Chippewa Indians of Michigan and its tribal members for compensation as legal or equitable claims against the United States other than the legal claims that are pending in the [[w:U.S. Court of Federal Claims|Court of Federal Claims]] on the date of enactment of this resolution; and
::(B) the amount, if any, legally or equitably due from the United States to the claimants.
