{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend Public Law 93–435 with respect to the Northern Mariana Islands, providing parity with Guam, the Virgin Islands, and American Samoa.

=Section 1. Amendment=



==(a) In general– ==

The first section and[[Public Law 93-435|section 2 of Public Law 93–435]] ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/48/1705 48 U.S.C. 1705] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/48/1706 1706] )are amended by inserting “the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands,” after “Guam,” each place it appears.

==(b) References to date of enactment– ==

For the purposes of the amendment made by subsection (a), each reference in[[Public Law 93-435|Public Law 93–435]] to the “date of enactment” shall be considered to be a reference to the date of the enactment of this section.
