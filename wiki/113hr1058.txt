{{Center|A BILL}}

To suspend temporarily the duty on Acid Black 194.

={{anchor|H1F5598515FC4475B8577FA6B059CB389}}Section 1. Acid Black 194=



=={{anchor|HCF2466D5770C4BBCBA58F1AE30202BB5}}(a) In general– ==

Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States is amended by inserting in numerical sequence the following new heading:

<blockquote>9902.01.00Acid Black 194 (CAS No. 57693–14–8) (provided for in subheading 3204.12.20)FreeNo changeNo changeOn or before 12/31/2016.
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HD30290D11371420CB8FEBFD86E76CD6D}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)applies to articles entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
