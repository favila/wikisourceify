To reauthorize the Undetectable Firearms Act of 1988 for 1 year.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Undetectable Firearms Reauthorization Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Extension of sunset provision=

 Section 2(f)(2) of the Undetectable Firearms Act of 1988([[Public Law -|[[Public Law 100-649|Public Law 100–649]] ]] ; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 18 U.S.C. 922note] )is amended in the matter preceding subparagraph (A)by striking “25” and inserting “26” .
