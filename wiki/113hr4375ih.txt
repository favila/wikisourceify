{{Center|A BILL}}

 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 100 North Taylor Lane in Patagonia, Arizona, as the“Jim Kolbe Post Office”.   

=Section 1. Jim Kolbe Post Office=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]located at 100 North Taylor Lane in Patagonia, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the“Jim Kolbe Post Office.”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the“Jim Kolbe Post Office.”.
