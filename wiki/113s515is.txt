{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend the Yellow Ribbon G.I. Education Enhancement Program to cover recipients of Marine Gunnery Sergeant John David Fry scholarship, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Expansion of Yellow Ribbon G.I. Education Enhancement Program=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3317 Section 3317(a) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “in paragraphs (1) and (2)” and inserting “in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3317 paragraphs (1)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3317 (2)] , and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3317 (9)] ” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply with respect to academic years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
