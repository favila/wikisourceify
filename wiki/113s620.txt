{{Center|A BILL}}

To withhold the salary of the Director of OMB upon failure to submit the President’s budget to Congress as required by section 1105 of title 31, United States Code.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“No Budget, No OMB Pay Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|id42ff6f3d08584827bf0c4faffd63bce2}}Sec. 2. Determination of compliance with statutory requirement to submit the President's budget=

Not later than 3 days after the President's budget is due, the Inspector General of the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Office of Personnel Management]] shall—
:(1) make an annual determination of whether the Director of the [[w:Office of Management and Budget|Office of Management and Budget]] (OMB) and the President are in compliance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1105 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 1105] of title 31, United States Code] ; and
:(2) provide a written notification of such determination to the Chairpersons of theCommittee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the Senateand the Chairpersons of theCommittee on the Budgetand theCommittee on Appropriations of the House of Representatives.

={{anchor|id1da28771d4164effb6654b05a0265bed}}Sec. 3. No pay upon failure to timely submit the President’s budget to congress=



=={{anchor|id39f0d9888f554a079eedfe022e10a25f}}(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no funds may be appropriated or otherwise be made available from the United States Treasury for the pay of the Director of [[w:Office of Management and Budget|OMB]] , [[w:|Deputy Director of OMB]] , and the [[w:|Deputy Director for Management of OMB]] during any period of noncompliance determined by the Inspector General of the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Office of Personnel Management]] under section 2.

=={{anchor|id5f7cb45511b34e0dbbc39894b01bbdf8}}(b) No retroactive pay– ==

The Director of [[w:Office of Management and Budget|OMB]] , [[w:|Deputy Director of OMB]] , and the [[w:|Deputy Director for Management of OMB]] may not receive pay for any period of noncompliance determined by the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Inspector General of the Office of Personnel Management]] under section 2at any time after the end of that period.

={{anchor|id01ddf041db0b414fb16ba3a5acd30c14}}Sec. 4. Effective date=

This Act shall take effect upon the date of enactment of this Act.
