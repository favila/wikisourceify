{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to exempt certain small businesses from the employer health insurance mandate and to modify the definition of full-time employee for purposes of such mandate.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Small Business Fairness in Obamacare Act” .

=Sec. 2. Restrictions on application of employer health insurance mandate=



==(a) Exception for small business concerns– ==

  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/4980H Section 4980H(c)(2)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote>

===(F) Exception for small business concerns– ===

The term “applicable large employer” shall not include any employer which is a small business concern (within the meaning of section 3 of the Small Business Act)..
</blockquote>


==(b) Definition of full-Time employee– ==

 Section 4980H(c) of such Codeis amended—
:(1) in paragraph (2)(E), by striking “by 120” and inserting “by 174” , and
:(2) in paragraph (4)(A), by striking “30 hours” and inserting “40 hours” .

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to months beginning after December 31, 2013.
