{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 Directing the Secretary of Defense to transmit to the House of Representatives copies of any emails in the possession of the Department of Defense or the National Security Agency that were transmitted to or from the email account(s) of former Internal Revenue Service Exempt Organizations Division Director Lois Lerner between January 2009 and April 2011.   

= =

That the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]]direct the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]]to transmit to the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]], no later than 14 days after the date of the adoption of this resolution, copies of any electronic communication in the possession of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]], the [[w:National Security Agency/Central Security Service|Director of the National Security Agency]], or any office that reports to the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]]or the [[w:National Security Agency/Central Security Service|Director]]that was transmitted to or from any electronic mail account(s) used by former [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]]Exempt Organizations Division Director Lois Lerner at any time between January 1, 2009, and April 30, 2011.
