{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To prohibit officers and employees of the Internal Revenue Service from using personal email accounts to conduct official business.   

=Section 1. IRS employees prohibited from using personal email accounts for official business=

No officer or employee of the [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]]may use a personal email account to conduct any official business of the Government.    Passed the House of Representatives September 16, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
