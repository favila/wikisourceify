{{Center|A BILL}}

To require that all foreign terrorists with links to
		  terrorist networks who attack the United States or its Government be considered
		  enemy combatants to be tried by military tribunals instead of civilian
		  courts.


{{section|H112018310C0242E490769D83A5E4B39B|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Military Tribunals for Terrorists Act
			 of 2013

        
.
