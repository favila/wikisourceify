{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to repeal the suspension of eligibility for grants, loans, and work assistance for drug-related offenses.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Stopping Unfair Collateral Consequences from Ending Student Success Act” or “SUCCESS Act” .

=Sec. 2. Repeal of suspension of eligibility under the Higher Education Act of 1965 for grants, loans, and work assistance for drug-related offenses=



==(a) Repeal– ==

 Subsection (r) of section 484 of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1091 20 U.S.C. 1091(r)] ] )is repealed.

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==

The Higher Education Act of 1965is amended—
:(1) in section 428(b)(3)( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1078 20 U.S.C. 1078(b)(3)] ] )—
::(A) in subparagraph (C), by striking “485(l)” and inserting “ 485(k)” ; and
::(B) in subparagraph (D), by striking “485(l)” and inserting “ 485(k)” ;
:(2) in section 435(d)(5)( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1085 20 U.S.C. 1085(d)(5)] ] )—
::(A) in subparagraph (E), by striking “485(l)” and inserting “ 485(k)” ; and
::(B) in subparagraph (F), by striking “485(l)” and inserting “ 485(k)” ;
:(3) in section 484( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1091 20 U.S.C. 1091] ] ), by redesignating subsections (s) and (t)as subsections (r) and (s), respectively; and
:(4) in section 485( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1092 20 U.S.C. 1092] ] )—
::(A) by striking subsection (k); and
::(B) by redesignating subsections (l) and (m)as subsections (k) and (l), respectively.
