{{Center|A BILL}}

 To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Glendale, New York.   

=Section 1. Single, unique ZIP Code for Glendale, New York=

Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this section, the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing only Glendale, New York.
