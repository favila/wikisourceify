{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to reinstate the requirement for an annual report on the capacity of the Department of Veterans Affairs to provide for specialized treatment and rehabilitative needs of disabled veterans.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Appropriate Care for Disabled Veterans Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of requirement for report on the capacity of the Department of Veterans Affairs to provide for specialized treatment and rehabilitative needs of disabled veterans=

The first sentence of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1706  section 1706(b)(5)(A)of title 38, United States Code,]is amended by striking“through 2008”.
