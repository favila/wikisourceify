{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit the Secretary of Homeland Security from granting a work authorization to an alien found to have been unlawfully present in the United States.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Equal Protection for American Workers Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition on certain work authorizations=

Effective beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary of Homeland Security]]is prohibited from granting a work authorization to an alien who has been finally determined, in a valid immigration proceeding during which due process was afforded, to have been at any time unlawfully present in the United States.
