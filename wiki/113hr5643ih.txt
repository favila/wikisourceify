{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 to reauthorize the public safety and community policing grant program, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Reauthorization of public safety and community policing grants=

   Section 1101(a)(11) of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/3793 42 U.S.C. 3793(a)(11)])is amended—
:(1) in subparagraph (A), by striking“2006 through 2009”and inserting“   2015 through 2019  ”; and
:(2) by amending subparagraph (B) to read as follows:

<blockquote> 
::(B) Of funds available under part Q in any fiscal year, not less than $30,000,000 shall be allocated for grants pursuant to applications submitted by units of local government or law enforcement agencies in order to carry out the purpose described in section 1701(b)(12) . . 
</blockquote>
