{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the Federal estate and gift
		  taxes.


{{section|HA6790D8FF3A8497CB5484CEA1AEEBB00|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Death Tax Repeal
			 Act

        
.

{{section|H050919B665B640E4BCBA87BF5CC33F1E|2. Repeal of estate and gift taxes}}
(a)
In
			 general

          Subtitle B of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986
 (relating
			 to estate, gift, and generation-skipping taxes) is hereby repealed.

(b)
Effective
			 date
The repeal made by subsection (a)
 shall apply to estates of
			 decedents dying, gifts made, and generation-skipping transfers made after the
			 date of the enactment of this Act.
