{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act to require the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection to notify and obtain permission from consumers before collecting nonpublic personal information about such consumers, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Consumer Right to Financial Privacy Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Requirement of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection to obtain permission before collecting nonpublic personal information=



==(a) Required notification and permission– ==

 Section 1022(c)(9)(A) of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5512 12 U.S.C. 5512(c)(9)(A)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking “may not obtain from a covered person or service provider” and inserting “may not request, obtain, access, collect, use, retain, or disclose” ;
:(2) by striking “personally identifiable financial” and inserting “nonpublic personal” ; and
:(3) by striking “from the financial records” and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting

<blockquote>unless—
::(i) the [[w:|Bureau]] clearly and conspicuously discloses to the consumer, in writing or in an electronic form, what information will be requested, obtained, accessed, collected, used, retained, or disclosed; and
::(ii) before such information is requested, obtained, accessed, collected, used, retained, or disclosed, the consumer informs the [[w:|Bureau]] that such information may be requested, obtained, accessed, collected, used, retained, or disclosed..
</blockquote>


==(b) Application of requirement to contractors of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection– ==

 Section 1022(c)(9)(B) of such Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5512 12 U.S.C. 5512(c)(9)(B)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

===(B) Application of requirement to contractors of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection– ===

 Subparagraph (A)shall apply to any person directed or engaged by the [[w:|Bureau]] to collect information to the extent such information is being collected on behalf of the [[w:|Bureau]] ..
</blockquote>


==(c) Definition of nonpublic personal information– ==

 Section 1022(c)(9) of such Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5512 12 U.S.C. 5512(c)(9)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

===(C) Definition of nonpublic personal information– ===

In this paragraph, the term nonpublic personal information has the meaning given the term in section 509 of the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/6809 15 U.S.C. 6809] )..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. Removal of exemption for the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection from the Right to Financial Privacy Act=

 Section 1113 of the Right to Financial Privacy Act of 1978( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/3413 12 U.S.C. 3413] )is amended by striking subsection (r).
