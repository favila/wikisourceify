{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the National Wildlife Refuge System Administration Act of 1966 to require that any new national wildlife refuge may not be established except as expressly authorized by statute.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ National Wildlife Refuge Review Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition on establishment of new national wildlife refuges=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 4(a) of the National Wildlife Refuge System Administration Act of 1966 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/668dd 16 U.S.C. 668dd(a)] ) is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
:(6) The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]may not establish any national wildlife refuge except as expressly authorized by a law enacted after January 3, 2013. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Existing refuges not affected– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) shall not apply with respect to any national wildlife refuge established on or before January 3, 2013.
