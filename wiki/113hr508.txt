{{Center|A BILL}}

To extend the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program for five years.

={{anchor|H4C8A9EAE57164825B4F0E6828FDD6854}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 Reauthorization Act of 2013”or the“TRIA Reauthorization Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H70BA26023C444C459C5631E8E92DAF7C}}Sec. 2. Five-year extension of Terrorism Risk Insurance Program=



=={{anchor|H1C39884D0C8E457EB72785009B39E7A5}}(a) Program years– ==

 Section 102(11)(G) of the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/6701 15 U.S.C. 6701 note] )is amended by striking“2014”and inserting“2019”.

=={{anchor|HD867B5BD192348CDB323B20567185A24}}(b) Timing of mandatory recoupment– ==

 Section 103(e)(7)(E)(i)(III) of such Actis amended by striking“2017”and inserting“2024”.

=={{anchor|H1B014F6621B94B4BBBC72591B698D56A}}(c) Termination date– ==

 Section 108(a) of such Actis amended by striking“2014”and inserting“2019”.
