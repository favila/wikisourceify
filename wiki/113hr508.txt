This Act may be cited as the
			 “Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 Reauthorization Act of 2013”
			 or the “TRIA Reauthorization Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H70BA26023C444C459C5631E8E92DAF7C}}Sec. 2. Five-year
			 extension of Terrorism Risk Insurance Program=



=={{anchor|H1C39884D0C8E457EB72785009B39E7A5}}(a) Program

			 the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 (15 U.S.C. 6701 note) is amended by
			 striking “2014” and inserting “2019”.

			 mandatory recoupment– ==

Section 103(e)(7)(E)(i)(III) of such Act is
			 amended by striking “2017” and inserting “2024”.

			 date– ==

Section 108(a) of such
			 Act is amended by striking “2014” and inserting
			 “2019”.
