{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 39, United States Code, to improve the efficiency and competitiveness of mail service within the State of Alaska.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Alaska Bypass Fair Competition Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Mail service within the State of Alaska=



==(a) Definition of existing mainline carrier– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/5402  Section 5402(a)(12)of title 39, United States Code], is amended by striking“on January 1, 2001,”and inserting“for each of the preceding 36 months”.

==(b) Definition of new carrier– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/5402  Section 5402(a)(15)(B)of title 39, United States Code], is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 
:(B) has been providing nonpriority bypass mail service on a city pair route in the State of Alaska for fewer than 36 consecutive months in the immediately preceding period; and . 
</blockquote>
