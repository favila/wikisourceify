{{Center|AN ACT}}

To temporarily increase the borrowing authority of the Federal Emergency Management Agency for carrying out the National Flood Insurance Program.

=Section 1. TEMPORARY INCREASE IN BORROWING AUTHORITY FOR NATIONAL FLOOD INSURANCE PROGRAM=


:(a)  Section 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/4016 42 U.S.C. 4016(a)] )is amended by striking “$20,725,000,000” and inserting “$30,425,000,000” .
:(b) The amount provided by this section is designated by the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] as an emergency requirement pursuant to section 403(a) of S. Con. Res. 13 (111th Congress), the concurrent resolution on the budget for fiscal year 2010, and as an emergency pursuant to section 4(g) of the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/933 2 U.S.C. 933(g)] ).Passed the House of Representatives January 4, 2013.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
