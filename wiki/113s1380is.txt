{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Secretary of Transportation to ensure that on-duty time does not include waiting time at a natural gas or oil well site for certain commercial motor vehicle operators, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Preserving Jobs in the Oilfield Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Waiting time exception=


:(a) Definition of covered operator– In this section, the term covered operator means the operator of a commercial motor vehicle transporting supplies, equipment, or materials (including produced fluids, drilling and completion fluids, and any other fluids or materials used in the drilling, completion, and production of an oil or gas well) to or from a natural gas or oil well site.
:(b) Exception– The [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] shall ensure that the exception described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/395 section 395.1(d)(2)] of title 49, Code of Federal Regulations (relating to on-duty time not including waiting time at a natural gas or oil well site) applies to covered operators regardless of whether the operators have received special training or operate vehicles specially constructed to service wells.
