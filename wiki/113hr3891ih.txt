{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to authorize the Secretary of Homeland Security to award Urban Area Security Initiative grants to nonprofit organizations not located in high-risk urban areas.

=Section 1. Department of Homeland Security authority to award Urban Area Security Initiative grants to nonprofit organizations not located in high-risk urban areas=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 2003 of the Homeland Security Act of 2002( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/6/604 6 U.S.C. 604] )is amended—
:(1) by redesignating subsection (e)as subsection (f); and
:(2) by inserting after subsection (d)the following new subsection (e):

<blockquote>

====(e) Special rule for grants to nonprofit organizations– ====

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary]] may award a grant under this section to an organization (as described under  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/501 section 501(c)(3)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986and exempt from tax section 501(a) of such code) located in an area that is not designated as a high-risk urban area..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a)apply with respect to a grant made after the date of the enactment of this Act.
