{{Center|A BILL}}

 To repeal the authority of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection to restrict mandatory pre-dispute arbitration.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Bureau Arbitration Fairness Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal of authority to restrict mandatory pre-dispute arbitration=

   Section 1028 of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5518 12 U.S.C. 5518] )is amended—
:(1) by striking“  (a)Study and report.— ”; and
:(2) by striking subsections (b) , (c) , and (d) .
