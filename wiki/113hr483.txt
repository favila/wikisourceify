{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the Federal estate and gift
		  taxes.


{{section|H5195DD3BC0104930A23F77249EF1B17C|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Farmers Against Crippling Taxes
			 Act

        
.

{{section|HBEB82D99E4FB413A96D6463F1F0F6954|2. Repeal of estate and gift taxes}}
(a)
In
			 general

          Subtitle B of the
			 Internal Revenue Code of 1986
 (relating to estate, gift, and
			 generation-skipping taxes) is hereby repealed.

(b)
Effective
			 date
The repeal made by subsection (a)
 shall apply to estates of
			 decedents dying, gifts made, and generation-skipping transfers made after the
			 date of the enactment of this Act.
