{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the Federal estate and gift
		  taxes.

={{anchor|H5195DD3BC0104930A23F77249EF1B17C}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Farmers Against Crippling Taxes
			 Act”.

={{anchor|HBEB82D99E4FB413A96D6463F1F0F6954}}Sec. 2. Repeal of estate
			 and gift taxes=



=={{anchor|H5F93F0F6A1AD4D30A98D2AA07053F395}}(a) In
			 general– ==

Subtitle B of the
			 Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (relating to estate, gift, and
			 generation-skipping taxes) is hereby repealed.

=={{anchor|HF310727CBE40423CA1B169932008DAB1}}(b) Effective
			 date– ==

The repeal made by subsection (a) shall apply to estates of
			 decedents dying, gifts made, and generation-skipping transfers made after the
			 date of the enactment of this Act.
