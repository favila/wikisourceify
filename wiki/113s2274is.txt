{{Center|A BILL}}

 To expedite decisions on applications for authorization to export natural gas, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Authorization to export natural gas=



==(a) Decision deadline– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary of Energy]]shall issue a decision on any application for authorization to export natural gas under  section 3 of the Natural Gas Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/717b 15 U.S.C. 717b] )not later than 90 days after the later of—
:(1) the end of the comment period for the decision as set forth in the applicable notice published in the Federal Register; or
:(2) the date of enactment of this Act.

==(b) Judicial action– ==



===(1) In general– ===

The [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit|United States Court of Appeals]]for the circuit in which the export facility will be located pursuant to an application described insubsection (a)shall have original and exclusive jurisdiction over any civil action for the review of —
:(A) an order issued by the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary of Energy]]with respect to the application; or
:(B) the failure of the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary]]to issue a decision on the application.

===(2) Order– ===

If the [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit|Court]]in a civil action described inparagraph (1)finds that the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary]]has failed to issue a decision on the application as required undersubsection (a), the [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit|Court]]shall order the [[w:United States Department of Energy|Secretary]]to issue the decision not later than 30 days after the order of the [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit|Court]].

===(3) Expedited consideration– ===

The [[w:U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit|Court]]shall—
:(A) set any civil action brought under this subsection for expedited consideration; and
:(B) set the matter on the docket as soon as practicable after the filing date of the initial pleading.
