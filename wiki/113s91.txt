{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to clarify eligibility for the child tax credit.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Child Tax Credit Integrity Preservation Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|id4B1F63DA2C8B42299151DF55C3C66F2A}}Sec. 2. Eligibility for child tax credit=



=={{anchor|id426DA270E1124F2BB5454780A1E4FE3F}}(a) In general– ==

 Subsection (e) of section 24 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking“under this section to a taxpayer”and all that follows and inserting “under this section to any taxpayer unless—

<blockquote>
:(1) such taxpayer includes the taxpayer's valid identification number (as defined in section 6428(h)(2)) on the return of tax for the taxable year, and
:(2) with respect to any qualifying child, the taxpayer includes the name and taxpayer identification number of such qualifying child on such return of tax..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|idB010F967F5FF4AC3A57E087EE0A8666A}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
