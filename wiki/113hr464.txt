{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to reduce the limit on the amount of certain contributions which may be made to a candidate with respect to an election for Federal office.

={{anchor|H584E55E54FCC484082FDDA5BADDAB86E}}Section 1. Reduction in Contribution Limits for Contributions to Individual Candidates=



=={{anchor|HB28137A0955C4D74A51882B74470B003}}(a) Reduction in Limit– ==

 Section 315(a)(1)(A) of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/441a 2 U.S.C. 441a(a)(1)(A)] )is amended by striking“$2,000”and inserting“$1,000”.

=={{anchor|H53EF23756D8746A99F34B46D81C28CCE}}(b) Application of Indexing– ==



==={{anchor|HEE766531FB6F4651942A79623CD5BA50}}(1) In general– ===

 Section 315(c)(1)(B) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/441a 2 U.S.C. 441a(c)(1)(B)] )is amended by striking“after 2002”and inserting“after 2002 (or, in the case of the limitation established under subsection (a)(1)(A), after 2016)”.

==={{anchor|H2BAFD2A8D76042FF90088017A16F1CD0}}(2) Determination of base period– ===

 Section 315(c)(2)(B) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/441a 2 U.S.C. 441a(c)(2)(B)] )is amended—
:(A) in clause (i), by striking“and”at the end;
:(B) in clause (ii)—
::(i) by striking“ (a)(1)(A),”, and
::(ii) by striking the period at the end and inserting“; and”; and
:(C) by adding at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote>
::(iii) for purposes of subsection (a)(1)(A), calendar year 2015..
</blockquote>


={{anchor|HCD4C2D1506854CC2BABDECBC3E999D19}}Sec. 2. Effective Date=

The amendments made by this Act shall apply with respect to elections occurring after December 2014.
