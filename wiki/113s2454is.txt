{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 17, United States Code, to extend expiring provisions of the Satellite Television Extension and Localism Act of 2010.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Satellite Television Access Reauthorization Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization=

  Chapter 1of title 17, United States Code, is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/111 section 111(d)(3)]—
::(A) in the matter preceding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/111 subparagraph (A)], by striking“clause”and inserting“paragraph”; and
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/111 subparagraph (B)], by striking“clause”and inserting“paragraph”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119 section 119]—
::(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119 subsection (a)(6)(E)], in the undesignated matter following[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119 clause (iii)], by striking“clause (i)”and inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119 subparagraph (B)(i)] ”;
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119 subsection (c)(1)(E)], by striking“2014”and inserting“2019”;
::(C) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119 subsection (e)], by striking“2014”and inserting“2019”; and
::(D) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119 subsection (g)(7)(C)], by inserting“the”before“Communications”. 

=Sec. 3. Termination of license=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119  Section 119of title 17, United States Code], as amended in section 2 , is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

===(h) Termination of license– ===

This section shall cease to be effective on December 31, 2019. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

   Section 107(a) of the Satellite Television Extension and Localism Act of 2010 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/119  17 U.S.C. 119note])is repealed.
