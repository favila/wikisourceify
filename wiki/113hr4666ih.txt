{{Center|A BILL}}

 To provide for the conveyance of a portion of the former Air Force Norwalk Defense Fuel Supply Point in Norwalk, California.   

=Section 1. Land conveyance, former Air Force Norwalk Defense Fuel Supply Point, Norwalk, California=



==(a) Land conveyance authorized– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary of the Air Force]]may convey, without consideration, to the City of Norwalk, California (in this section referred to as the“City”), all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to a parcel of real property, including any improvements thereon, consisting of up to 15 acres at the former Air Force Norwalk Defense Fuel Supply Point for the purpose of permitting the City to use the property for public purposes.

==(b) Condition on conveyance– ==

The conveyance undersubsection (a)is contingent upon, or may be executed concurrent with, the City actively assisting the United States in disposing of the residual portion of the former Norwalk Defense Fuel Supply Point (approximately 36 acres) using [[w:General Services Administration|General Services Administration]]disposal authority.

==(c) Application of environmental laws– ==

Nothing in this section shall affect the applicability of Federal, State, or local environmental laws and regulations, including the  Comprehensive Environmental Response, Compensation, and Liability Act of 1980 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/9601 42 U.S.C. 9601 et seq.] ), to the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Department of the Air Force]].

==(d) Payment of costs of conveyances– ==



===(1) Payment required– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary of the Air Force]]shall require the City to cover costs to be incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]], or to reimburse the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]]for administrative costs incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]]to carry out the conveyance undersubsection (a), including survey costs, costs for environmental documentation related to the conveyance, and any other administrative costs related to the conveyance. If amounts are collected from the City in advance of the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]]incurring the actual costs, and the amount collected exceeds the costs actually incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]]to carry out the conveyance, the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]]shall refund the excess amount to the City.

===(2) Treatment of amounts received– ===

Amounts received underparagraph (1)as reimbursement for costs incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]]to carry out the conveyance undersubsection (a)shall be credited to the fund or account that was used to cover the costs incurred by the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]]in carrying out the conveyance. Amounts so credited shall be merged with amounts in such fund or account and shall be available for the same purposes, and subject to the same conditions and limitations, as amounts in such fund or account.

==(e) Description of property– ==

The exact acreage and legal description of the real property to be conveyed undersubsection (a)shall be determined by a survey satisfactory to the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary of the Air Force]].

==(f) Additional terms and conditions– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary of the Air Force]]may require such additional terms and conditions in connection with the conveyance undersubsection (a)as the [[w:United States Department of the Air Force|Secretary]]considers appropriate to protect the interests of the United States.
