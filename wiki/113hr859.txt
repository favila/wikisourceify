{{Center|A BILL}}

To rescind certain excess conference costs from any
		  agency that overspends on conferences, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H68828E7F83D14E4BB45E2D0458157C90}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Taxpayers’ Conference Overspending
			 Prevention Act” or the “Taxpayers’ COP Act”.

={{anchor|H033C3FD247FE4F7993481ABCFB23FDDB}}Sec. 2. Report and
			 rescission of excess conference costs=



=={{anchor|H17178235A49644E8BE3201BB8457B38F}}(a) Report– ==

Not later than October 31 of each fiscal
			 year, each executive agency shall submit to the [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress]] a report listing the
			 amount of funds that such agency obligated for conferences in the previous
			 fiscal year. The funds described in the report shall include pre-planning,
			 travel, and subsistence costs.

=={{anchor|H0C4F6110F69342559880BDA983DCEA8E}}(b) Rescission– ==

On the date that is 30 days after the date
			 a report is submitted by an executive agency under subsection (a), from the
			 unobligated funds made available to such agency in the current fiscal year for
			 conferences (including pre-planning, travel, and subsistence costs), there is
			 rescinded the total amount by which the total costs for any conference
			 described in such report exceeds $3,000 per agency employee attending such
			 conference, or the total amount by which the costs for any conference described
			 in such report exceeds $600 per day per agency employee attending such
			 conference, whichever is higher.

=={{anchor|HFD27B4527F8043F39294F58D6D7C1079}}(c) Exception for
			 critical foreign travel– ==



==={{anchor|H580E59B80E4B450CB4D3E80B24B6EFE3}}(1) Certification– ===

The
			 rescission required under subsection (b) shall not apply to amounts described
			 in a report that are greater than the limits provided in such subsection
			 if—
:(A) the head of the applicable executive agency
			 certifies in writing to the [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget]]
			 that the amounts were for foreign travel and that such travel was critical to
			 the agency’s mission; and
:(B) the [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Director]]
			 determines that such certification is valid.

==={{anchor|H61FAD8A61B2547DB99695B8E5AFC1FD9}}(2) Report– ===

Any
			 determination by the [[Wikipedia:Office of Management and Budget|Director]] under paragraph (1) shall be included in the
			 report submitted under subsection (a).

=={{anchor|HD329F1ABD1B6443FB9F41FA6709E4BF5}}(d) Definitions– ==

In this section—
:(1) the term
			 “Director” means the Director of the Office of Management and
			 Budget;
:(2) the term
			 “executive agency” has the meaning given such term in section 105
			 of title 5, United States Code; and
:(3) the term
			 “conference” means a meeting, retreat, seminar, symposium, or
			 event that—
::(A) is held for
			 consultation, education, discussion, or training; and
::(B) is not held
			 entirely at a Government facility.
