{{Center|A BILL}}

To require Senate candidates to file designations, statements, and reports in electronic form.

={{anchor|idEB7BDA670B3A47A9919DEDC150D5389E}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Senate Campaign Disclosure Parity Act”.

={{anchor|H3AEAA4DFC631415CB4D9D095ED868676}}Sec. 2. Filing by Senate candidates with Commission=

 Section 302(g) of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/432 2 U.S.C. 432(g)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|id6C0C8C84500A4CBE95523E09540D23EB}}(g) Filing with the commission– ==

All designations, statements, and reports required to be filed under this Act shall be filed with the [[w:Federal Election Commission|Commission]] ..
</blockquote>
