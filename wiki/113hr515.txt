{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Family and Medical Leave Act of 1993 to
		  provide leave because of the death of a son or daughter.

={{anchor|H045ED24FDCC44C33856FEA223F06F778}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Parental Bereavement Act of
			 2013” or the “Sarah Grace-Farley-Kluger
			 Act”.

={{anchor|H55A3BCAE6690453AA178A73F656AAB4B}}Sec. 2. Family leave
			 because of the death of a son or daughter=



=={{anchor|H84B634C6DC6D49BBA104E0C0669E45F8}}(a) Family
			 leave– ==



==={{anchor|H54208F02EDF24281BD5728B28F40F66F}}(1) Entitlement to
			 leave– ===

Section 102(a)(1) of the Family and Medical Leave Act of
			 1993 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2612 29 U.S.C. 2612(a)(1)]) is amended by adding at the end the following new
			 subparagraph:

<blockquote>
:(F) Because of the
				death of a son or
				daughter..
</blockquote>


==={{anchor|HF5A2E753229D4835A73A06DA4F507D2B}}(2) Requirements
			 relating to leave– ===



===={{anchor|H785FC7FC2FC144A88B6DE725AAA337A9}}(A) Schedule– ====

Section
			 102(b)(1) of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2612 29 U.S.C. 2612(b)(1)]) is amended by inserting after the
			 third sentence the following new sentence: “Leave under subsection
			 (a)(1)(F) shall not be taken by an employee intermittently or on a reduced
			 leave schedule unless the employee and the employer of the employee agree
			 otherwise.”.

===={{anchor|H81F93FBB9B954143A4C297D7CAC40F4C}}(B) Substitution of
			 paid leave– ====

Section 102(d)(2)(B) of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2612 29 U.S.C.
			 2612(d)(2)(B)]) is amended, in the first sentence, by striking “(C) or
			 (D)” and inserting “(C), (D), or (F)”.

===={{anchor|HC734547281674C58B30F06123982BADC}}(C) Notice– ====

Section
			 102(e) of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2612 29 U.S.C. 2612(e)]) is amended by adding at the end the
			 following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

====={{anchor|HD7E262C4FD9B448A858B62EBE356303A}}(4) Notice for leave
				due to death of a son or daughter– =====

In any case in which the
				necessity for leave under subsection (a)(1)(F) is foreseeable, the employee
				shall provide such notice to the employer as is reasonable and
				practicable..
</blockquote>


===={{anchor|H715623A181E149B0B7E99CFC11374108}}(D) Spouses employed
			 by same employer– ====

Section 102(f)(1)(A) of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2612 29 U.S.C.
			 2612(f)(1)(A)]) is amended by striking “subparagraph (A) or (B)”
			 and inserting “subparagraph (A), (B), or (F)”.

===={{anchor|HC5AD1EEFDA6A42C99BA5AD93A90746A8}}(E) Certification
			 requirements– ====

Section 103 of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2613 29 U.S.C. 2613]) is amended
			 by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

====={{anchor|HB9963F6E089D4F21BDE9E9EF16910FA9}}(g) Certification
				related to the death of a son or daughter– =====

An employer may require
				that a request for leave under section 102(a)(1)(F) be supported by a
				certification issued at such time and in such manner as the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary]] may by
				regulation prescribe. If the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary]] issues a regulation requiring such
				certification, the employee shall provide, in a timely manner, a copy of such
				certification to the employer..
				
</blockquote>


===={{anchor|HD1D20E879D7D4BC3A6359CD545AEE029}}(F) Failure to
			 return from leave– ====

Section 104(c) of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2614 29 U.S.C. 2614(c)])
			 is amended—
:(i) in
			 paragraph (2)(B)(i), by inserting before the semicolon the following: “,
			 or a death that entitles the employee to leave under section
			 102(a)(1)(F)”; and
:(ii) in
			 paragraph (3)(A)—
::(I) in the matter
			 preceding clause (i), by inserting “, or the death,” before
			 “described”;
::(II) in clause (ii),
			 by striking “or” at the end;
::(III) by redesignating
			 clause (iii) as clause (iv); and
::(IV) by inserting
			 after clause (ii) the following:

<blockquote>
:::(iii) a certification
				that meets such requirements as the [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary]] may by regulation prescribe, in
				the case of an employee unable to return to work because of a death specified
				in section 102(a)(1)(F);
				or.
</blockquote>


===={{anchor|H38E45387A9494806802979D167159C3C}}(G) Employees of
			 local educational agencies– ====

Section 108 of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/2618 29 U.S.C.
			 2618]) is amended—
:(i) in
			 subsection (c)—
::(I) in paragraph
			 (1)—
:::(aa) in
			 the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by inserting after “medical
			 treatment” the following: “, or under section 102(a)(1)(F) that is
			 foreseeable,”; and
:::(bb) in
			 subparagraph (A), by inserting after “to exceed” the following:
			 “(except in the case of leave under section 102(a)(1)(F))”;
			 and
::(II) in paragraph (2),
			 by striking “section 102(e)(2)” and inserting “paragraphs
			 (2) and (4) of section 102(e), as applicable”; and
:(ii) in
			 subsection (d), in paragraph (2) and (3), by striking “or (C)
                ”
			 each place it appears and inserting “
                  (C), or (F)
                ”.

			 taken by an employee intermittently or on a reduced leave schedule unless the
			 employee and the employing agency of the employee agree
			 otherwise.”.

===={{anchor|H351498D740224DD5AFA5619A5FDF907D}}(B) Substitution of

			 sentence, by striking “
                or (E)
              ” and inserting “
                (E), or
			 (F)
              ”.

===={{anchor|H4BB57C83413F42A0B8FAD6EE56FC93AE}}(C) Notice– ====

Section
			 6382(e) of such title is amended by adding at the end the following new
			 paragraph:

<blockquote>
:(4) In any case in which the necessity
				for leave under subsection (a)(1)(F) is foreseeable, the employee shall provide
				such notice to the employing agency as is reasonable and
				practicable..
</blockquote>


===={{anchor|HEC6BA4057AEC4C79B5FD4829B1ED8954}}(D) Certification

			 the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(g) An employing
				agency may require that a request for leave under section 6382(a)(1)(F) be
				supported by a certification issued at such time and in such manner as the
				[[w:Office of Personnel Management|Office of Personnel Management]] may by regulation prescribe. If the [[w:Office of Personnel Management|Office]]
				issues a regulation requiring such certification, the employee shall provide,
				in a timely manner, a copy of such certification to the
				employer..
</blockquote>
