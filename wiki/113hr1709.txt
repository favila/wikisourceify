{{Center|A BILL}}

To authorize the Attorney General to award grants to eligible entities to prevent or alleviate community violence by providing education, mentoring, and counseling services to children, adolescents, teachers, families, and community leaders on the principles and practice of nonviolence.

={{anchor|HD69E1D8D52DB4EA9B067CFDA01F59BF8}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Securing American Families by Educating and Training Youth (SAFETY) Through Nonviolence Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HECD3E22B1C144143B96FE9ED48266710}}Sec. 2. Grants to educate Americans about the principles of nonviolence=



=={{anchor|H043F0034B35E4973996B41E7FFA3D8C1}}(a) Grants– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] may make grants to eligible entities to prevent or alleviate the effects of community violence by providing education, mentoring, and counseling to youth regarding the principles and application of nonviolence in conflict resolution.

=={{anchor|HBC0E51ECD96240A4AFF356AEB3637135}}(b) Priority– ==

In awarding grants under this section, the [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] shall give priority to applicants that agree to use the grant in 1 or more eligible urban, rural, Tribal, and suburban communities that can certify—
:(1) an increase in community, especially youth violence; and
:(2) lack the monetary or other resources to address violence prevention.

=={{anchor|HA879FCE199BE4EE19FF64895324FE231}}(c) Limitation– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] may not make a grant to an eligible entity under this section unless the entity agrees to use not more than 40 percent of such grant for nonviolence-prevention education and program development.

=={{anchor|H8CB1441CF97149BEABD6B06537D49107}}(d) Definitions– ==

In this section, the term“eligible entity”means a State or local government entity, educational institution, nonprofit, or faith-based organization.

=={{anchor|H0847D29C8AE4412C8F9EAA83696D1234}}(e) Authorization of Appropriations– ==

To carry out this section, there is authorized to be appropriated$5,000,000 for each of the fiscal years from 2014 through 2019.
