{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 31, United States Code, to provide that the President’s annual budget submission to Congress list the current fiscal year spending level for each proposed program and a separate amount for any proposed spending increases, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Current service level budgeting amendments=



==(a) President’s budget submission– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1105 Section 1105 of title 31, United States Code] , is amended as follows:
:(1) In [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1105 subsection (a)] —
::(A) by striking “(37) the list” and inserting “(39) the list” ; and
::(B) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:::(40) a list of each program, and the proposed funding level for such program, that will be a one-time expense for the fiscal year for which the budget is submitted.
:::(41) in addition to the performance standards requirements of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1105 paragraph (28)] , a proposal, by budget function and agency, for spending reductions in spending for each of the 4 fiscal years after the fiscal year for which the budget is submitted..
</blockquote>

:(2) By adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(i) 
:::(1) Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, with respect to each item in the budget submitted under this section that is being conducted in the current fiscal year, the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall provide the following:
::::(A) The amount appropriated or otherwise made available for such item in such year.
::::(B) The amount (if any) of a proposed adjustment in the funding level for such item and the justification for such change.
:::(2) If the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] proposes an adjustment under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1105 paragraph (1)(B)] that will result in an increase in funding with respect to an item in the budget, the President shall include with such proposal the following:
::::(A) The amount of such adjustment which is a result of inflation.
::::(B) The amount of such adjustment that is a result of an increase in salaries or benefits for employees..
</blockquote>


==(b) Current programs and activities estimates– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1109 Section 1109of title 31, United States Code] , is amended as follows:
:(1) In [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1109 subsection (a)] —
::(A) in the first sentence, by striking “were carried” and all that follows through “change in policy” and inserting “received the same amount of budget authority as provided in the current fiscal year” ; and
::(B) in the third sentence, by striking “The President” and inserting “Consistent with the requirements of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1109 subsection (c)] , the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] ” .
:(2) By adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(c) With respect to each program listed under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1109 subsection (a)] , the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall provide—
:::(1) the amount appropriated or otherwise made available for such program in the current fiscal year; and
:::(2) a separate amount (if any) of the expected increase in the following fiscal year for such program due to inflation, pay increases, or benefit increases..
</blockquote>
