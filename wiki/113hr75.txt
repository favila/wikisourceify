        {{Center|A BILL}}
    
		To end membership of the United States in the United
		  Nations.
	
	
		
        =={{section|H7285A83DB30F45E88B4EF85D24F3B118|
      1.
      Short title
      This Act may be cited as the
			 
          American Sovereignty Restoration Act
			 of 2013
        .
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|H6FFEC676667C41438F273C666AAAF1FF|
      2.
      Repeal of United
			 Nations Participation Act of 1945
			
        (a)
        Repeal
        The
			 
            United Nations Participation Act of 1945 (Public Law 79–264; 22 U.S.C. 287 et
			 seq.) is repealed.
			
      
        (b)
        Termination of
			 membership in United Nations
        The 
                [[Executive Office of the President|President]]
             shall terminate all
			 membership by the United States in the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
            , and in any organ,
			 specialized agency, commission, or 
                [[United Nations|other formally affiliated body of the United
			 Nations]]
            .
			
      
        (c)
        Closure of
			 United States Mission to United Nations
        The United States Mission
			 to the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             is closed. Any remaining functions of such office shall
			 not be carried out.
			
    }}==
    
    
        =={{section|H50D363634C6B425181A101727F0303B7|
      3.
      Repeal of United
			 Nations Headquarters Agreement Act
			
        (a)
        Repeal
        The
			 
            United Nations Headquarters Agreement Act (Public Law 80–357) is
			 repealed.
			
      
        (b)
        Withdrawal
        The
			 United States withdraws from the agreement between the United States of America
			 and the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             regarding the headquarters of the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             (signed
			 at Lake Success, New York, on June 26, 1947, which was brought into effect by
			 the United Nations Headquarters Agreement Act).
			
    }}==
    
    
        =={{section|HB94620D4E5F449D38B0CF83F415D98FE|
      4.
      United States
			 assessed and voluntary contributions to the United Nations
      No funds are authorized to be appropriated
			 or otherwise made available for assessed or voluntary contributions of the
			 United States to the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             or to any organ, specialized agency,
			 commission or 
                [[United Nations|other formally affiliated body of the United Nations]]
            , except that
			 funds may be appropriated to facilitate termination of United States membership
			 and withdrawal of United States personnel and equipment, in accordance with
			 sections 2 and 3, respectively. Upon termination of United States membership,
			 no payments shall be made to the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             or to any organ, specialized
			 agency, commission or other formally affiliated body of the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
            , out
			 of any funds appropriated prior to such termination or out of any other funds
			 available for such purposes.
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|HFA47DEB701BD4598A6A39BD8C761A073|
      5.
      United Nations
			 peacekeeping operations
			
        (a)
        Termination
        No
			 funds are authorized to be appropriated or otherwise made available for any
			 United States contribution to any 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             military or peacekeeping
			 operation or force.
			
      
        (b)
        Terminations of
			 United States participation in United Nations peacekeeping
			 operations
        No funds may be obligated or expended to support the
			 participation of any member of the Armed Forces of the United States as part of
			 any 
                [[|United Nations]]
             military or peacekeeping operation or force. No member of
			 the Armed Forces of the United States may serve under the command of the 
                [[United Nations|United
			 Nations]]
            .
			
    }}==
    
    
        =={{section|H4A7AD37E3DEA4C46815E9771BEBCB223|
      6.
      Withdrawal of
			 United Nations presence in facilities of the government of the United States
			 and repeal of diplomatic immunity
			
        (a)
        Withdrawal from
			 United States Government property
        The 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             (including any organ,
			 specialized agency, commission or 
                [[United Nations|other formally affiliated body of the United
			 Nations]]
            ) may not occupy or use any property or facility of the United States
			 Government.
			
      
        (b)
        Diplomatic
			 immunity
        No officer or
			 employee of the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             (including any organ, specialized agency,
			 commission or 
                [[United Nations|other formally affiliated body of the United Nations]]
            ) or any
			 representative, officer, or employee of any mission to the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             of
			 any foreign government shall be entitled to enjoy the privileges and immunities
			 of the Vienna Convention on Diplomatic Relations of April 18, 1961, nor may any
			 such privileges and immunities be extended to any such individual. The
			 privileges, exemptions, and immunities provided for in the 
            International
			 Organizations Immunities Act of December 29, 1945 (59 Stat. 669; 22 U.S.C. 288
			 et seq.), or in any agreement or treaty to which the United States is a party,
			 including the agreement entitled 
            Agreement Between the United Nations
			 and the United States of America Regarding the Headquarters of the United
			 Nations, signed June 26, 1947 (22 U.S.C. 287 note), and the Convention
			 on Privileges and Immunities of the United Nations, entered into force with
			 respect to the United States on April 29, 1970 (21 UST 1418; TIAS 6900; UNTS
			 16), shall not apply to the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             or to any organ, specialized agency,
			 commission or other formally affiliated body of the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
            , to the
			 officers and employees of the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
            , or of any organ, specialized
			 agency, commission or other formally affiliated body of the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
            , or
			 to the families, suites, or servants of such officers or employees.
			
    }}==
    
    
        =={{section|HA7D85DF5AC38489F81BFCE4EEC8E3AE8|
      7.
      Repeal of United
			 States membership and participation in the United Nations Educational,
			 Scientific, and Cultural Organization
      
        The joint resolution entitled A joint
			 resolution providing for membership and participation by the United States in
			 the United Nations Educational, Scientific, and Cultural Organization, and
			 authorizing an appropriation therefor, approved July 30, 1946 (Public
			 Law 79–565, 22 U.S.C. 287m et seq.), is repealed.
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|HCB668B12D45148BEA655F20D713C0EBB|
      8.
      Repeal of United
			 Nations Environment Program Participation Act of 1973
      The 
          United Nations Environment Program
			 Participation Act of 1973 (22 U.S.C. 287 note) is repealed.
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|H2A6C3321FBE34F0B9154285756D12971|
      9.
      Repeal of United
			 States participation in the World Health Organization
      The joint resolution entitled 
          Joint
			 Resolution providing for membership and participation by the United States in
			 the World Health Organization and authorizing an appropriation
			 therefor, approved June 14, 1948 (22 U.S.C. 290
        , is repealed.
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|H4CAD8615B42F46F8B5BB615046C968E0|
      10.
      Repeal of
			 involvement in United Nations conventions and agreements
      Effective on the date of the enactment of
			 this Act, the United States will end any participation in any conventions and
			 agreements with the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             and any organ, specialized agency,
			 commission, or 
                [[United Nations|other formally affiliated body of the United Nations]]
            . Any
			 remaining functions of such conventions and agreements shall not be carried
			 out.
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|HAED75766774F4E8394546EBA11F85EA5|
      11.
      Reemployment
			 with United States Government after service with an international
			 organization
      Nothing in this
			 Act shall be construed to affect the rights of employees under subchapter IV of
			 chapter 35 of title 5, United States Code, relating to reemployment after
			 service with an international organization.
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|H05025F4EFFB84840A061986590ABA1F7|
      12.
      Notification
      Effective on the date of the enactment of
			 this Act, the 
                [[|Secretary of State]]
             shall notify the 
                [[United Nations|United Nations]]
             and any organ,
			 specialized agency, commission, or other formally affiliated body of the 
                [[United Nations|United
			 Nations]]
             of the provisions of this Act.
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|HE711AF7E70A8485E9392879D9227A11B|
      13.
      Effective
			 date
      Except as otherwise
			 provided, this Act and the amendments made by this Act shall take effect on the
			 date that is two years after the date of the enactment of this Act.
		}}==
