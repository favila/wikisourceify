{{Center|A BILL}}

 To ensure funding for certain payments to Indian tribes and tribal organizations, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Self Determination Commitment to Tribes Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Indian self-determination contract support costs=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/1305  Section 1305of title 31, United States Code], is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(11) Indian self-determination contract support costs– ==

To make payments required under  paragraphs (2) , (3) , and (5) of section 106(a) of theIndian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/25/450j–1 25 U.S.C. 450j–1] )to Indian tribes and tribal organizations for contract support costs arising out of self-determination or self-governance contracts, grants, compacts, or annual funding agreements entered into pursuant to that Act. . 
</blockquote>
