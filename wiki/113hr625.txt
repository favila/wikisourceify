{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend chapter 178 of title 28 of the United States
		  Code to permit during a 4-year period States to enact statutes that exempt from
		  the operation of such chapter, lotteries, sweepstakes, and other betting,
		  gambling, or wagering schemes involving professional and amateur
		  sports.

={{anchor|H2B8FC2F56A9647F9A5A0C516409E038E}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Sports Gaming Opportunity Act of
			 2013”.

={{anchor|HE295277608674B71B4ACBFC50F7523D5}}Sec. 2. Amendment=

Section 3704(a) of title 28, United States
			 Code, is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (3)
			 by striking “or” at the end,
:(2) in paragraph (4)
			 by striking the period at the end and inserting “; or”, and
:(3) by adding at the
			 end the following:

<blockquote>
::(5) a lottery, sweepstakes, or other betting,
				gambling, or wagering scheme in a State authorized by such State by an
				applicable statute—
:::(A) enacted on or
				after January 1, 2013, and
:::(B) as in effect not
				later than January 1,
				2017..
</blockquote>
