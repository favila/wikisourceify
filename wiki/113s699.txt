{{Center|A BILL}}

To reallocate Federal judgeships for the courts of appeals, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|idAEE8B37E94F648E0A1CF3AC65DCC5C69}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Court Efficiency Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|id09449580D3F044F0B1F935FE16E137AF}}Sec. 2. Reallocation of Federal judgeships=



=={{anchor|id022F3AC472D74677985FC417EF029CF0}}(a) In general– ==

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] shall appoint, by and with the advice and consent of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] —
:(1) 1 additional circuit judge for the second circuit court of appeals; and
:(2) 1 additional circuit judge for the eleventh circuit court of appeals.

=={{anchor|id504E6B5F15C04ECB8631C83660FBCB66}}(b) Conforming amendment– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/44 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 44(a)] of title 28, United States Code] , is amended in the table—
:(1) in the item relating to the District of Columbia circuit court of appeals, by striking“11”and inserting“8”;
:(2) in the item relating to the second circuit court of appeals, by striking“13”and inserting“14”; and
:(3) in the item relating to the eleventh circuit court of appeals, by striking“12”and inserting“13”.
