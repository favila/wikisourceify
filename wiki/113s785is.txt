{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to eliminate the use of official time by Federal employees.

={{anchor|H5889DDE906804FB598C34FCBD339373B}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Federal Employee Accountability Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H7C4DAC5235E24C9493196DC245190068}}Sec. 2. Elimination of official time=



=={{anchor|H67540E8C048F4A818E9C9A19A6042410}}(a) In general– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 Section 7131 of title 5, United States Code] , is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H89E712B86C61475A882F40C16BEE2016}}Sec. 7131. Elimination of official time==

Any activities performed by an employee relating to the internal business of a labor organization (including the solicitation of membership, elections of labor organization officials, and collection of dues) shall be performed during the time the employee is in a non-duty status..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HDD402A3153F343EF8547C4D8550EEEB9}}(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections forchapter 71of title 5, United States Code, is amended by striking the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 section 7131] and inserting the following:

<blockquote>7131. Elimination of official time..
</blockquote>
