{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act to repeal the risk corridor program, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Protecting Americans from Illegal Bailouts Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal of the risk corridor program established in PPACA=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 1342 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18062 42 U.S.C. 18062] )is repealed.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as of the date of the enactment of this Act.
