{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for exemptions from municipal advisor registration requirements.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Municipal Advisors Relief Act of 2013”.

=Sec. 2. Bank exemption from municipal advisor registration=

 Section 15B(e)(4) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/78o–4 15 U.S.C. 78o–4(e)(4)] ] )is amended—
:(1) in subparagraph (A), by striking“entity)”and inserting“entity, or a person appointed to or volunteering on a board, commission, committee, or similar function of a municipal entity)”; and
:(2) in subparagraph (C), by inserting after“include”the following:“a bank (as defined in section (3)(a)(6)) or an agency or branch of a foreign bank (as defined in paragraphs (1)and (3), respectively, of section 1(b) of the International Banking Act of 1978([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 12 U.S.C. 3101] )),”.
