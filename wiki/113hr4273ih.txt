{{Center|A BILL}}

 To name the Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic in The Dalles, Oregon, as the“Loren R. Kaufman Memorial Veterans’ Clinic”.   

=Section 1. Name of Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic, The Dalles, Oregon=

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]community-based outpatient clinic located at 704 Veterans Drive, The Dalles, Oregon, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the“Loren R. Kaufman Memorial Veterans’ Clinic”. Any reference to such community-based outpatient clinic in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be considered to be a reference to the Loren R. Kaufman Memorial Veterans’ Clinic.
