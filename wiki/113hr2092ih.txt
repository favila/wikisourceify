{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to provide that retirement credit for service as a Member of Congress be denied in the case of a former Member convicted of a felony, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Respecting the Institution of Congress Act” .

=Sec. 2. Amendments relating to the Civil Service Retirement System=



==(a) Extension to former Members, etc– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 Paragraph (2)(A) ofsection 8332(o)of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 clause (i)] , by striking the period at the end the inserting the following: “, or a former Member, former President, or former Vice President.” ;
:(2) by repealing [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 clause (ii)] ; and
:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 clause (iii)] —
::(A) by striking “or” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subclause (I)(bb)] ;
::(B) by inserting “or” at the end of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subclause (II)(bb)] ; and
::(C) by adding after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subclause (II)] the following:

<blockquote>
:::(III) is committed after the date of enactment of theRespecting the Institution of Congress Actand is described under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 subparagraph (B)(xxxii)] ..
</blockquote>


==(b) Extension to all felonies– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 Section 8332(o)(2)(B) of such title] is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(xxxii) 
::(I) An offense, not otherwise described under this subparagraph, which is a felony under the laws of a State or the United States.
::(II) For purposes of this clause, the term State includes the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, American Samoa, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, and the Virgin Islands..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. Amendments relating to the Federal Employees’ Retirement System=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 Section 8411(l)(2) of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 subparagraph (A)] , by striking the period at the end the inserting the following: “, or a former Member, former President, or former Vice President.” ;
:(2) by repealing [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 subparagraph (B)] ; and
:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 subparagraph (C)] , by striking all that follows “is committed” and inserting “after the date determined in accordance with [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8332 section 8332(o)(2)(A)(iii)] .” .

=Sec. 4. Applicability=

None of the amendments made by this Act shall apply in the case of any individual whose last day of service as a Member (within the meaning of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8331 section8331(2)] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8401 8401(20)of title 5, United States Code] ) occurs on or before the date of the enactment of this Act.
