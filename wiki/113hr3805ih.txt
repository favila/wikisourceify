{{Center|A BILL}}

 To provide for the redesignation of the Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies as the Daniel K. Inouye Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies.   

=Section 1. Redesignation of the Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies as the Daniel K. Inouye Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies=



==(a) Redesignation– ==

The [[w:|Department of Defense]]regional center for security studies known as the [[w:|Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies]]is hereby renamed the“  [[w:|Daniel K. Inouye Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies]] ”.

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==



===(1) Reference to regional centers for strategic studies– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/184  Section 184(b)(2)(B)of title 10, United States Code], is amended by striking“Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies”and inserting“  [[w:|Daniel K. Inouye Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies]] ”.

===(2) Acceptance of gifts and donations– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2611 Section 2611(a)(2)(B) of such title]is amended by striking“Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies”and inserting“  [[w:|Daniel K. Inouye Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies]] ”.

==(c) References– ==

Any reference to the [[w:|Department of Defense Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies]]in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be deemed to be a reference to the [[w:|Daniel K. Inouye Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies]].
