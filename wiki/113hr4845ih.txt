{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make permanent the deduction for mortgage insurance premiums.   

=Section 1. Permanent deduction for mortgage insurance premiums=



==(a) In general– ==

  Subparagraph (E) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/163 section 163(h)(3)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking clause (iv) .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or accrued after December 31, 2013, and to amounts properly allocable to periods after such date.
