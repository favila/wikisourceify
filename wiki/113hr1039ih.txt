{{Center|A BILL}}

To rescind unobligated amounts for foreign assistance to Egypt, and to appropriate funds for the Department of Defense tuition assistance program.

=Section 1. Rescission; appropriation for tuition assistance=



==(a) Rescission of foreign assistance to Egypt– ==



===(1) In general– ===

Of the aggregate unobligated amount available for foreign assistance to Egypt for fiscal year 2013, there is hereby rescinded $500,000,000.

===(2) Report– ===

Not later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Office of Management and Budget|Director of the Office of Management and Budget]] shall submit to the committees on appropriations of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] and the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] a report describing the amount and account of each rescission under paragraph (1).

==(b) Appropriation for tuition assistance– ==

There is appropriated$500,000,000for an additional amount to the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] for fiscal year 2013 for tuition assistance under sections [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1784a 1784a] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2007 2007] of title 10, United States Code.
