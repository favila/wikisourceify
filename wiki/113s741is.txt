{{Center|A BILL}}

To extend the authorization of appropriations to carry out approved wetlands conservation projects under the North American Wetlands Conservation Act through fiscal year 2017.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “North American Wetlands Conservation Extension Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Authorization of appropriations=

 Section 7(c)(5) of the North American Wetlands Conservation Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/4406 16 U.S.C. 4406(c)(5)] )is amended by striking “2012” and inserting “2017” .
