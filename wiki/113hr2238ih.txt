{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 to exempt the Crime Victims Fund from sequestration.   

=Section 1. Exemption from sequestration=

  Subsection (g)(1)(A) of section 255 of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 is amended by inserting after the item relating to Continuing Fund, [[w:United States Department of Energy|Southwestern Power Administration]](the second place it appears) the following new item:

<blockquote>  Crime Victims Fund (15–5041–0–2–754).  . 
</blockquote>
