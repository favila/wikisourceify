{{Center|A BILL}}

To improve the H–2A agricultural worker program for use by dairy workers, sheepherders, and goat herders, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HACE656C19B8D420CBED2E0C080FABD01}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“H–2A Improvement Act”.

={{anchor|HFB4F1129B79E44AEB47379D2E5BCC53A}}Sec. 2. Nonimmigrant status for dairy workers, sheepherders, and goat herders=

 Section 101(a)(15)(H)(ii)(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1101 8 U.S.C. 1101(a)(15)(H)(ii)(a)] ] )is amended by inserting“who is coming temporarily to the United States to perform agricultural labor or services as a dairy worker, sheepherder, or goat herder, or”after“abandoning”.

={{anchor|HD80DA2F409F74C12A7867E0DBB39F0D6}}Sec. 3. Special rules for aliens employed as dairy workers, sheepherders, or goat herders=

 Section 218 of the Immigration and Nationality Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 8 U.S.C. 1188] )is amended—
:(1) by redesignating subsections (h) and (i)as subsections (i) and (j), respectively; and
:(2) by inserting after subsection (g)the following:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H96CF821EA8BD45EDBE98D88DC5D9D8AD}}(h) Special rules for aliens employed as dairy workers, sheepherders, or goat herders– ===



===={{anchor|H6C2615E2F9974109822E459D5672AB50}}(1) In general– ====

Notwithstanding any other provision of this Act, an alien admitted as a nonimmigrant under section 101(a)(15)(H)(ii)(a)for employment as a dairy worker, sheepherder, or goat herder—
:(A) may be admitted for an initial period of 3 years; and
:(B) subject to paragraph (3)(E), may have such initial period of admission extended for an additional period of up to 3 years.

===={{anchor|HE971E6A9D65D4CF19DBFB51AC2463330}}(2) Exemption from temporary or seasonal requirement– ====

Notwithstanding section 101(a)(15)(H)(ii)(a), an employer filing a petition to employ H–2A workers in positions as dairy workers, sheepherders, or goat herders shall not be required to show that such positions are of a seasonal or temporary nature..
</blockquote>
