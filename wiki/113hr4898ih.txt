{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to direct the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to provide hospital care and medical services in non-Department facilities for veterans waiting longer than 14 days for an appointment in a Department facility, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Access to Veteran-Centered Community Care Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Department of Veterans Affairs provision of hospital care and medical services in non-Department facilities for veterans waiting 14 days or longer for an appointment=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1703  Section 1703of title 38, United States Code], is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote> 
:(e) 
::(1) In carrying out this section, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]]shall determine that if any veteran has been waiting for 14 days or longer for an appointment to receive hospital care or medical services, including primary care, in a [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]]facility that the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]]facility is not capable of furnishing the care or services required. The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]shall ensure that such care or services are provided to such a veteran at a non-Department facility under a contract under this section.
::(2) If, in the course of receiving hospital care or medical services in a non-Department facility, a veteran is referred to another non-Department facility for specialty care, such referral shall be submitted to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]who may reject such referral during the 72-hour period following such submittal. If the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]does not reject the referral during such period, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]shall ensure that such specialty care is provided to the veteran at a non-Department facility under a contract under this section. . 
</blockquote>
