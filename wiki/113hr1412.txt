{{Center|A BILL}}

To improve and increase the availability of on-job training and apprenticeship programs carried out by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HAD8A403661054B618139652C29A8526B}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Improving Job Opportunities for Veterans Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H6ED08091C16F4F718678DFE54C385788}}Sec. 2. Public relations campaign=

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall carry out a public relations campaign, including exercising the authority under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 532] of title 38, United States Code, to advertise in national media outlets, to promote [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] on-job training and apprenticeship programs available to veterans as highly efficient and cost-effective ways of obtaining jobs.

={{anchor|H173D38D5624A4DDCAB64EB866D569120}}Sec. 3. Authority to increase availability of private sector on-job training programs=

During the three-year period beginning on the date that is one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall carry out[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3677 section 3677(b)(1)(A) of title 38, United States Code] , by substituting“75 per centum”for“85 per centum”.

={{anchor|H344FA37DA16847FFBDA44D4520921F08}}Sec. 4. On-job training at Federal departments and agencies=

Beginning on the date that is one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall enter into agreements with the heads of other Federal departments and agencies to operate programs of training on the job under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 3677] of title 38, United States Code, to train eligible veterans or persons to perform skills necessary for employment by the department or agency operating the program.

={{anchor|HB1324F1D90524E0AA860FB8196E091E8}}Sec. 5. Extension of reduced pension for certain veterans covered by medicaid plans for services furnished by nursing facilities=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5503 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 5503(d)(7)] of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking“November 30, 2016”and inserting“January 31, 2017”.
