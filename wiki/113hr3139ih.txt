{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Chesapeake Bay Initiative Act of 1998 to provide for the reauthorization of the Chesapeake Bay Gateways and Watertrails Network.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Chesapeake Bay Gateways and Watertrails Network Reauthorization Act” .

=Sec. 2. Authorization of appropriations=

 Section 502(c) of the Chesapeake Bay Initiative Act of 1998( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/461 16 U.S.C. 461note] ;[[Public Law -|[[Public Law 105-312|Public Law 105–312]] ]] )is amended by striking “fiscal years” and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting “fiscal years 2014 through 2018.” .
