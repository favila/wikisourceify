{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to qualify homeless youth and veterans who are full-time students for purposes of the low income housing tax credit.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Housing for Homeless Students Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Homeless youth and veterans who are full-time students qualified for purposes of the low income housing tax credit=



==(a) In General– ==

  Clause (i) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/42 section 42(i)(3)(D)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by redesignating subclauses (II) and (III) as subclauses (IV) and (V), respectively, and by inserting after subclause (I) the following new subclauses:

<blockquote> 
:(II) a full-time student who, during any portion of the 5-year period ending with the commencement of such individual's continuous occupation of any low-income unit or units, was an individual described in  section 725(2) of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/11434a 42 U.S.C. 11434a] ), 
:(III) a full-time student who, during any portion of the 5-year period ending with the commencement of such individual's continuous occupation of any low-income unit or units, was an individual described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2002  section 2002(1)of title 38, United States Code], . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply to determinations made before, on, or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
