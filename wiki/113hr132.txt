{{Center|A BILL}}

To repeal the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act
		  and the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010.

={{anchor|HB827B12CD86F43C1BC594A20A8A5185A}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “ObamaCare Repeal
			 Act”.

={{anchor|H0063E18976FE4F96B877F480DC638018}}Sec. 2. Repeal of the
			 Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act and the Health Care and Education
			 Reconciliation Act of 2010=



=={{anchor|H1441EAA90F5F4B80AC49272B29F757E9}}(a) Patient
			 Protection and Affordable Care Act– ==

Effective as of the enactment
			 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ([[Public Law 111-148|Public Law 111–148]]), such
			 Act is repealed, and the provisions of law amended or repealed by such Act are
			 restored or revived as if such Act had not been enacted.

=={{anchor|H5D44F076EA254E4793B202723E183B7D}}(b) Health Care and
			 Education Reconciliation Act of 2010– ==

Effective as of the
			 enactment of the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010 ([[Public Law 111-152|Public
			 Law 111–152]]), such Act is repealed, and the provisions of law amended or
			 repealed by such Act are restored or revived as if such Act had not been
			 enacted.
