{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require the termination of any employee of the Department of Veterans Affairs who is found to have retaliated against a whistleblower.   

=Section 1. Termination of employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs who retaliate against whistleblowers=



==(a) In general– ==

Subject to chapter 12of title 5, United States Code, including the right of an employee to obtain judicial review under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/1215 section 1215(a)(4) of that title], and notwithstanding any other provision of law, any employee of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]]who is determined under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/1215 section 1215 of that title]to have violated[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2302 paragraph (8)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2302 (9)], or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2302 (13) of section 2302(b) of that title]shall be terminated.

==(b) Applicability– ==

 Subsection (a)shall apply to an act committed before, on, or after the date of enactment of this Act.
