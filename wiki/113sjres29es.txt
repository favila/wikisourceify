{{Center|JOINT RESOLUTION}}

 Providing for the appointment of Risa Lavizzo-Mourey as a citizen regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution.   

= =

That, in accordance with  section 5581 of the Revised Statutes of the United States ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/43 20 U.S.C. 43] ), the vacancy on the [[w:Smithsonian Institution|Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution]], in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term of Patricia Q. Stonesifer of Washington, DC, on December 21, 2013, is filled by the appointment of Risa Lavizzo-Mourey of Pennsylvania. The appointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on the later of December 22, 2013, or the date of enactment of this joint resolution.    Passed the Senate January 29, 2014.  Secretary
