{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 28, United States Code, to prohibit the
		  exclusion of individuals from service on a Federal jury on account of sexual
		  orientation or gender identity.

={{anchor|HC4AB77F614474CA5938DB612D85BA3C6}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Juror Non-Discrimination Act of
			 2013”.

={{anchor|HA8FAF6050EE0474F88879A771ACF8A1A}}Sec. 2. Exclusion from
			 Federal juries on account of sexual orientation or gender identity
			 prohibited=

Section 1862 of
			 title 28, United States Code, is amended by inserting “sexual
			 orientation, gender identity,” after “sex”.
