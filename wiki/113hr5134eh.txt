{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To extend the National Advisory Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity and the Advisory Committee on Student Financial Assistance for one year.   

=Section 1. Extension of National Advisory Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity=

   Section 114(f) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1011c 20 U.S.C. 1011c(f)] )is amended by striking“2014”and inserting“2015”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of Advisory Committee on Student Financial Assistance=

   Section 491(k) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1098 20 U.S.C. 1098(k)] )is amended by striking“2014”and inserting“2015”.    Passed the House of Representatives July 23, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
