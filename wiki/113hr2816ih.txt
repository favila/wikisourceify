{{Center|A BILL}}

 To extend the pilot program under section 258 of the National Housing Act that establishes an automated process for providing alternative credit rating information for mortgagors and prospective mortgagors under certain mortgages.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ FHA Alternative Credit Pilot Program Reauthorization Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of pilot program=

   Section 258(d) of the National Housing Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/1715z–24 12 U.S.C. 1715z–24(d)] )is amended by striking“5-year”and inserting“10-year”.
