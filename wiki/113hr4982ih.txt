{{Center|A BILL}}

 To simplify the application used for the estimation and determination of financial aid eligibility for postsecondary education.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Simplifying the Application for Student Aid Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Using data from second preceding year=

   Section 480(a)(1)(B) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087vv 20 U.S.C. 1087vv(a)(1)(B)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 
:(B) Notwithstanding section 478(a) and beginning not later than the second January 1 after the date of enactment of the  Simplifying the Application for Student Aid Act  , the [[w:United States Department of Education|Secretary]]shall provide for the use of data from the second preceding tax year to carry out the simplification of applications (including simplification for a subset of applications) used for the estimation and determination of financial aid eligibility. Such simplification shall include the sharing of data between the [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]]and the [[w:United States Department of Education|Department]], pursuant to the consent of the taxpayer. . 
</blockquote>
