{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to allow Medicare beneficiaries participating in a Medicare Advantage MSA to contribute their own money to their MSA.   

=Section 1. Medicare beneficiaries participating in Medicare Advantage MSA may contribute their own money to their MSA=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/138 Subsection (b) ofsection 138of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986]is amended by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/138 paragraph (2)]and by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/138 paragraphs (3)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/138 (4)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/138 paragraphs (2)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/138 (3)], respectively.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
