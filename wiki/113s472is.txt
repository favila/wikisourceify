{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the further extension or establishment of national monuments in the State of Nevada except by express authorization of Congress, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Limitation on further extension or establishment of national monuments in the State of Nevada=

The proviso of the last sentence of the first section of the Act of September 14, 1950(64 Stat. 849, chapter 950; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/431a 16 U.S.C. 431a] ), is amended by inserting “or Nevada” after “Wyoming” .

=Sec. 2. Prohibition on enforcement of Secretarial Order No. 3310 in Nevada without congressional approval=

Except by express authorization of [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] , the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] shall not implement, administer, or enforce Secretarial Order No. 3310, issued by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] on December 22, 2010.
