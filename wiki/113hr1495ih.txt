{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the further extension or establishment of national monuments in Arizona except by express authorization of Congress.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Arizona Land Sovereignty Act” .

=Sec. 2. Limitation on further extension or establishment of national monuments in Arizona=

The proviso of the last sentence of the first section of the Act of September 14, 1950(64 Stat. 849, chapter 950; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/431a 16 U.S.C. 431a] ), is amended by inserting “or Arizona” after “Wyoming” .
