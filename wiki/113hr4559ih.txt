{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the time period for contributing military death gratuities to Roth IRAs and Coverdell education savings accounts.   

=Section 1. Extension of time period for contributing military death gratuities to Roth IRAs and Coverdell education savings accounts=



==(a) In general– ==

  Sections[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/408A 408A(e)(2)(A)]  and  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/530 530(d)(9)(A)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 are each amended by striking“1-year period”and inserting“3-year period”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to amounts received under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1477  section 1477of title 10, United States Code], or under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1967  section 1967of title 38 of such Code], after the date of the enactment of this Act.
