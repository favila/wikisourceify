{{Center|AN ACT}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to reestablish the Professional Certification and Licensure Advisory Committee of the Department of Veterans Affairs.   

=Section 1. Reestablishment of Professional Certification and Licensure Advisory Committee=



==(a) Reestablishment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3689  Section 3689(e)(5)of title 38, United States Code], is amended by striking“December 31, 2006”and inserting“December 31, 2019”.

==(b) Appointment of new members– ==

In reestablishing the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Professional Certification and Licensure Advisory Committee]]under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3689 subsection (e) ofsection 3689of title 38, United States Code], pursuant tosubsection (a), the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]]may appoint new members of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Committee]]under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3689 paragraph (3)(A) of such subsection (e)]without regard to the individuals who served as members before the date of the enactment of this Act.    Passed the House of Representatives May 28, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
