{{Center|A BILL}}

To make a technical amendment to the T’uf Shur Bien Preservation Trust Area Act, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Sandia Pueblo Settlement Technical Amendment Act”.

={{anchor|id33380F3286444D9FAA55124AC839479D}}Sec. 2. Sandia Pueblo Settlement technical amendment=

 Section 413(b) of the T’uf Shur Bien Preservation Trust Area Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/539m–11 16 U.S.C. 539m–11(b)] ] )is amended—
:(1) in the first sentence of paragraph (4), by striking“conveyance”and inserting“the title to be conveyed”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|IDa0a9763e596041fe96bd4ab9575c345d}}(6) Failure to exchange– ===



===={{anchor|ID6e61c45a11ba4f0db37f61edb773aff6}}(A) In general– ====

If the land exchange authorized under paragraph (1)is not completed by the date that is 90 days after the date of enactment of this paragraph, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] , on receipt of consideration under subparagraph (B)and at the request of the Pueblo and the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] , shall transfer the National Forest land generally depicted as Land to be Held in Trust on the map entitled Sandia Pueblo Settlement Technical Amendment and dated December 7, 2011, to the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]] to be held in trust by the United States for the Pueblo, subject to the condition that the land remain as open space, with the natural characteristics of the land to be preserved in perpetuity.

===={{anchor|ID235c3703dba9445cb1373686e346b4a4}}(B) Consideration– ====

In consideration for the National Forest land to be held in trust under subparagraph (A), the Pueblo shall—
:(i) convey to the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] the La Luz tract; and
:(ii) pay to the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] the amount that is equal to the difference between—
::(I) the amount that is equal to the fair market value of the National Forest land, as subject to the condition that the National Forest land remain as open space, with the natural characteristics of the land to be preserved in perpetuity; and
::(II) the amount that is equal to the sum of—
:::(aa) the amount of compensation owed to the Pueblo by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] for the right-of-way and conservation easement on the Piedra Lisa tract under subsection (c)(2); and
:::(bb) the amount that is equal to the fair market value of the La Luz tract.

===={{anchor|id7583F674C9434E52B9165CCE75DD66D6}}(C) Use of funds– ====

Any amounts received by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] under this paragraph shall be—
:(i) deposited in the fund described in paragraph (3); and
:(ii) available to the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] , without further appropriation and until expended, for the acquisition of land or interests in land in the [[w:United States Forest Service|National Forest System]] ..
</blockquote>
