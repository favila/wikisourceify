{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to provide an extension of the work opportunity tax credit for certain targeted groups.

=Section 1. Extension of work opportunity tax credit for certain targeted groups=



==(a) In general– ==

 Subparagraph (B) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/51 section 51(c)(4)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by inserting “(December 31, 2018, in the case of any member of a targeted group described in subparagraph (C), (E), (F), or (G) of subsection (d)(1))” before the period at the end.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to individuals who begin work for the employer after December 31, 2013.
