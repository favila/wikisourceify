{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 28, United States Code, to add a Federal defender representative as a nonvoting member of the United States Sentencing Commission, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Federal defender representative as a nonvoting member of U.S. Sentencing Commission=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/991 Subsection (a) ofsection 991of title 28, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “one nonvoting member.” at the end of the first sentence and inserting “two nonvoting members.” ; and
:(2) by inserting before the last sentence the following: “A Federal defender representative designated by the Judicial Conference of the United States shall be a nonvoting member of the [[w:United States Sentencing Commission|Commission]] .” .

=Sec. 2. Conforming amendment=

The final sentence of section 235(b)(5) of the Comprehensive Crime Control Act of 1984( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/3551 18 U.S.C. 3551 note] )is amended by striking “nine members, including two ex officio, nonvoting members” and inserting “ten members, including three nonvoting members” .
