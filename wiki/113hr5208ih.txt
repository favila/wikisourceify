{{Center|A BILL}}

 To make technical corrections to the National Parks and Recreation Act of 1978, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Petersburg National Battlefield land acquisition=

   Section 313(a) of the National Parks and Recreation Act of 1978 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/461  16 U.S.C. 461note])is amended by striking“May 1978.”and inserting“May 1978, and in addition such lands located as the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]deems appropriate, not to exceed four acres.”.
