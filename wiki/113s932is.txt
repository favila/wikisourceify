{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide for advance appropriations for certain discretionary accounts of the Department of Veterans Affairs.

={{anchor|id3A84E2B268094AA7A3EBC816CDFE9E9F}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Putting Veterans Funding First Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HA212B0AFF4C743D79CA611CDF176092A}}Sec. 2. Advance appropriations for certain discretionary accounts of the Department of Veterans Affairs=



=={{anchor|H0EDF39580E7941D683B266FB2707DABB}}(a) In general– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/117 Section 117 of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking“medical care accounts of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]] ”each place it appears and inserting“discretionary accounts of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]] ”;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/117 subsection (c)] —
::(A) by striking“medical care accounts of the [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration]] , [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] account”and inserting“accounts of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] account”;
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/117 paragraph (1)] , by inserting“ [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration]] ,”after“[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/117 (1)] ”;
::(C) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/117 paragraph (2)] , by inserting“ [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration]] ,”after“[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/117 (2)] ”;
::(D) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/117 paragraph (3)] , by inserting“ [[w:Veterans Health Administration|Veterans Health Administration]] ,”after“[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/117 (3)] ”;
::(E) by adding at the end the following new paragraphs:

<blockquote>
:::(4) Veterans Health Administration, Medical Research and Support.
:::(5)  [[w:National Cemetery Administration|National Cemetery Administration]] .
:::(6) Veterans Benefits Administration, Native American Veteran Housing Loan Program Account.
:::(7) Departmental Administration, General Administration, General Operating Expenses.
:::(8) Departmental Administration, General Operating Expenses, Veterans Benefits Administration.
:::(9) Departmental Administration, Information Technology Systems.
:::(10) Departmental Administration, Office of the Inspector General.
:::(11) Departmental Administration, Construction, Major Projects.
:::(12) Departmental Administration, Construction, Minor Projects.
:::(13) Departmental Administration, Grants for Construction of State Extended Care Facilities.
:::(14) Departmental Administration, Grants for Construction of Veterans Cemeteries.; and
</blockquote>

::(F) in the subsection heading, by striking“Medical care accounts”and inserting“Discretionary accounts”; and
:(3) in the section heading, by striking“certain medical care accounts”and inserting“certain discretionary accounts”.

=={{anchor|HA85CD8B8D60B4AB388B5E8199AE671C8}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a)shall apply with respect to fiscal year 2016 and each subsequent fiscal year.
