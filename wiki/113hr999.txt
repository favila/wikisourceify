{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 49, United States Code, to require that
		  individuals seeking training in the operation of certain aircraft be checked
		  against immigration information in the possession of the Secretary of Homeland
		  Security to ensure that such individuals are citizens or nationals of the
		  United States, lawful permanent resident aliens, or nonimmigrants admitted for
		  a limited period to obtain such training, and for other
		  purposes.
