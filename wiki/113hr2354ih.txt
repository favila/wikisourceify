{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to require the prompt replacement of military decorations upon the request of the recipients of the decorations or their immediate next of kin.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Proper Replacement Of Medals and Performance Tracking Act” or “PROMPT Act” .

=Sec. 2. Replacement of military decorations=



==(a) Prompt replacement required; annual report– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1135 Section 1135of title 10, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1135 subsection (b)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1135 subsection (d)] ; and
:(2) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1135 subsection (a)] the following new subsections:

<blockquote>

====(b) Prompt replacement required– ====

When a request for the replacement of a military decoration is received under this section or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/3747 section 3747] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/3751 3751] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/6253 6253] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/8747 8747] , or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/8751 8751 of this title] , the Secretary concerned shall ensure that—
:(1) all actions to be taken with respect to the request, including verification of the service record of the recipient of the military decoration, are completed within one year; and
:(2) the replacement military decoration is mailed to the person requesting the replacement military decoration within 60 days after verification of the service record.

====(c) Annual report– ====

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall submit to the congressional defense committees an annual report regarding compliance by the military departments with the performance standards imposed by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1135 subsection (b)] . Each report shall include—
:(1) for the one-year period covered by the report—
::(A) the average number of days it took to verify the service record and entitlement of members and former members of the armed forces for replacement military decorations;
::(B) the average number of days between receipt of a request and the date on which the replacement military decoration was mailed; and
::(C) the average number of days between verification of a service record and the date on which the replacement military decoration was mailed; and
:(2) an estimate of the funds necessary for the next fiscal year to meet or exceed such performance standards..
</blockquote>


==(b) Plan required– ==

Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall submit to the congressional defense committees (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/101 section 101(a)(16) of title 10, United States Code] ) a plan to implement the amendments made by subsection (a), including an estimate of the funds necessary for fiscal year 2015 to meet or exceed the performance standards imposed by such amendments.
