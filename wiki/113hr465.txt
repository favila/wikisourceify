        {{Center|A BILL}}
    
		To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to
		  prohibit the conversion of leadership PAC funds to personal
		  use.
	
	
		
        =={{section|HC2737C54469F49548721EDE2D27C5DD8|
      1.
      Short Title
      This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Leadership PAC Limitation
			 Act
        .
		}}==
    
    
        =={{section|H64ACF9F13ADB40F1801270B83F6CFE6E|
      2.
      Prohibiting
			 Conversion of Leadership PAC Funds to Personal Use
			
        (a)
        Prohibition
        
          
            Section 313(b)(1) of the Federal Election
			 Campaign Act of 1971 (2 U.S.C. 439a(b)(1)) is amended by inserting after
			 subsection (a) the following: or funds of a leadership
			 PAC (as defined in section 304(i)(8)(B)).
			
      
        (b)
        Effective
			 Date
        The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with
			 respect to elections occurring on or after the date of the enactment of this
			 Act.
			
    }}==
