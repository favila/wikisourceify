 Providing for a correction in the enrollment of H.R. 3547.   

= =

That in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 3547 , the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Clerk of the House of Representatives]]shall amend the long title so as to read:“Making consolidated appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2014, and for other purposes.”.
