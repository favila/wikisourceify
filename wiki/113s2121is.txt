 To repeal title II of the REAL ID Act of 2005.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Repeal REAL ID Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal of title II of the REAL ID Act of 2005=

   Title II of the REAL ID Act of 2005 (division B of [[Public Law 109-13|Public Law 109–13]]  ;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/30301  49 U.S.C. 30301note])is repealed.
