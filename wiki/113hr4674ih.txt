{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the specially adapted housing assistance program for individuals with terminal illnesses, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Guarantee Housing for Terminally Ill Veterans Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Specially adapted housing assistance for individuals with terminal illnesses=



==(a) Individuals with terminal illnesses– ==

  Chapter 21of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 2110. Specially adapted housing assistance for individuals with terminal illnesses==



===(a) Priority– ===

In providing assistance under this chapter (not including[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2108 section 2108]), the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]shall give priority to an individual who is—
:(1) eligible for such assistance; and
:(2) diagnosed with a terminal illness.

===(b) Reimbursement– ===


:(1) If a veteran diagnosed with a terminal illness is eligible for assistance under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2101 subsection (a)]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2101 (b) of section 2101 of this title]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2102A section 2102A]and applies for, and has not yet received, such assistance, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]shall reimburse the veteran for costs relating to specially adapted housing if—
::(A) such costs are for acquisitions or adaptions that the veteran is eligible for under such assistance;
::(B) the veteran paid such costs after applying for such assistance; and
::(C) such reimbursement does not exceed the amount of such assistance for which the veteran is eligible, in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2102 section 2102].
:(2) A reimbursement by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]]under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2110 paragraph (1)]shall be treated as a grant for assistance under this chapter. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by adding after the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2109 section 2109]the following:

<blockquote>  2110. Specially adapted housing assistance for individuals with terminal illnesses.  . 
</blockquote>
