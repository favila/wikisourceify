To authorize the appropriation of $500,000,000 for fiscal year 2014 to provide grants to States for surface transportation projects of national and regional significance.

=Section 1. Projects of national and regional significance=

 Section 1301(m) of SAFETEA–LU([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/101 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 23 U.S.C. 101] note] )is amended by striking“fiscal year 2013”and inserting“each of the fiscal years 2013 and 2014”.
