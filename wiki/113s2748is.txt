{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Endangered Species Act of 1973 to conform citizen suits under that Act with other existing law, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Endangered Species Litigation Reasonableness Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Award of litigation costs to prevailing parties in accordance with existing law=

   Section 11(g)(4) of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/1540 16 U.S.C. 1540(g)(4)] )is amended by striking“to any”and all that follows through the end of the sentence and inserting“to any prevailing party in accordance with[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/2412  section 2412of title 28, United States Code].”.
