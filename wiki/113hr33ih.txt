{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 31, United States Code, to reform the manner in which the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System is audited by the Comptroller General of the United States and the manner in which such audits are reported, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Audit The Fed Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Audit reform and transparency for the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 Subsection (b) of section 714 of title 31, United States Code] , is amended by striking all after “shall audit an agency” and inserting a period.

==(b) Audit– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 Section 714 of title 31, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

===(e) Audit and report of the Federal Reserve System– ===



====(1) In general– ====

The audit of the [[w:Federal Reserve Board of Governors|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System]] and the Federal reserve banks under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 subsection (b)] shall be completed before the end of 2014.

====(2) Report– ====



=====(A) Required– =====

A report on the audit referred to in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 paragraph (1)] shall be submitted by the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] to the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] before the end of the 90-day period beginning on the date on which such audit is completed and made available to the Speaker of the House, the majority and minority leaders of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] , the majority and minority leaders of the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] , the Chairman and Ranking Member of the committee and each subcommittee of jurisdiction in the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] and the [[w:United States Senate|Senate]] , and any other Member of Congress who requests it.

=====(B) Contents– =====

The report under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/714 subparagraph (A)] shall include a detailed description of the findings and conclusion of the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] with respect to the audit that is the subject of the report, together with such recommendations for legislative or administrative action as the [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] may determine to be appropriate..
</blockquote>
