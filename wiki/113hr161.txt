{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Water Resources Development Act of 1976 to direct the Secretary of the Army to evaluate the feasibility of continuing Federal participation in a beach nourishment project, and for other purposes.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Bringing Economic Advancement and Coastal Health Act”.

2. Beach nourishment


Section 156 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1976 ( 42 U.S.C. 1962d–5f ) is amended—
: by striking “The ”and inserting “(a) Continuing Federal participation in a project .—The ”;: by inserting before the period at the end the following: “or after the date of the last estimated periodic nourishment as contemplated in the Chief’s Report, whichever is later ”; and: by adding at the end the following:

==(b) Feasibility review==

Before the end of the fifty-year period referred to in subsection (a) , the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army ]], acting through the [[Wikipedia:United States Department of the Army|Chief of Engineers ]], shall undertake, subject to the availability of appropriations, a review of a project to which subsection (a) applies to evaluate the feasibility of continuing Federal participation in the project and shall make a recommendation concerning such participation to [[Wikipedia:United States Congress|Congress ]]..
