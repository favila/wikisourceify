{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide for certain requirements relating to the immunization of veterans, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Access to Appropriate Immunizations for Veterans Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Ensuring veteran access to appropriate immunizations=



==(a) Inclusion of recommended adult vaccinations as medical services– ==



===(1) Covered benefit– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 Subparagraph (F) ofsection 1701(9)of title 38, United States Code], is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 
:(F) immunizations against infectious diseases, including each immunization on the recommended adult immunization schedule at the time such immunization is indicated on that schedule; . 
</blockquote>


===(2) Definition– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1701 Section 1701 of such title]is amended by adding after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1709 paragraph (9)]the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
:(10) The term recommended adult immunization schedule means the schedule established (and periodically reviewed and, as appropriate, revised) by the [[w:Centers for Disease Control and Prevention|Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices]]established by the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary of Health and Human Services]]and delegated to the [[w:Centers for Disease Control and Prevention|Centers for Disease Control and Prevention]]. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Report to Congress– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1704 Section 1704(1)(A) of such title]is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1704 clause (i)], by striking“and”at the end;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1704 clause (ii)], by striking the period at the end and inserting“; and”; and
:(3) by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1704 clause (ii)]the following new clause:

<blockquote> 
::(iii) to ensure that veterans have received each immunization on the recommended adult immunization schedule at the time such immunization is indicated on that schedule. . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Assurance that administration of vaccinations constitutes appropriate management and cost-Effective delivery of health care services– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1706 Section 1706(a) of such title]is amended—
:(A) by striking“In managing the provision”and inserting“(1) In managing the provision”; and
:(B) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote> 
::(2) 
:::(A) In managing the provision of medical services under such section, the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]shall develop and implement quality measures and metrics to ensure that veterans receiving medical services under this title receive each immunization on the recommended adult immunization schedule at the time such immunization is indicated on that schedule.
:::(B) Quality measures and metrics developed and implemented under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1706 subparagraph (A)]shall include targets for compliance.
:::(C) The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]shall, to the maximum extent feasible, develop and implement measures and metrics required by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1706 subparagraph (A)]concurrently and consistently with the metrics in place, as of the date of the enactment of this paragraph, with respect to influenza and pneumococcal vaccinations. . 
</blockquote>


===(2) Report to Congress– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1704 Section 1704(1) of such title]is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote> 
:(H) Statistics related to the quality measures and metrics developed and implemented under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1706 section 1706(a)(2) of this title]. . 
</blockquote>


===(3) Effective dates– ===



====(A) Development of initial schedule– ====

The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]shall establish the quality measures and metrics described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1706 paragraph (2) of section 1706(a) of such title], as added by paragraph (1) , through notice and comment rulemaking. The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]shall publish in the Federal Register proposed measures and metrics by no later than July 1, 2013, and shall finalize such measures and metrics by no later than December 31, 2013.

====(B) Implementation of measures and metrics– ====

The [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]]shall implement[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1706 paragraph (2) of such section]on January 1, 2014.

====(C) Report to Congress– ====

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1704 Subparagraph (H) of paragraph (1) of section 1704 of such title], as added by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1706 paragraph (2)], shall apply with respect to the first report submitted under such section after January 1, 2014.
