{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permanently extend the depreciation rules for property used predominantly within an Indian reservation.   

=Section 1. Permanent extension of depreciation rules for property on Indian reservations=



==(a) In general– ==

  Subsection (j) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/168 section 168]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking paragraph (8) .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2013.
