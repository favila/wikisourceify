{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 40, United States Code, to extend the
		  authorization of the Northern Border Regional Commission, and for other
		  purposes.

={{anchor|H58F246AD1ED346E1A1595F6A62A34983}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Northern Border Regional Commission Reauthorization Act of
			 2013”.

={{anchor|H2A0FD6F3A1F9482993101CB6277EA5E7}}Sec. 2. Northern Border
			 Regional Commission=



=={{anchor|H92BBFEEB7ED04ECB95C3F4E750D5F438}}(a) Authorization of
			 appropriations– ==

Section 15751
			 of title 40, United States Code, is amended—
:(1) by redesignating
			 subsection (b) as subsection (c);
:(2) by inserting after
			 subsection (a) the following:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H714E9537A0D94201AAE8D245C0C943E2}}(b) Northern Border
				Regional Commission– ====

There is
				authorized to be appropriated to the [[Wikipedia:|Northern Border Regional Commission]]to
				carry out this subtitle$30,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through
				2018.;
				and
</blockquote>

:(3) in subsection (c),
			 as so redesignated in paragraph (1) of this subsection, by inserting “by
			 the [[Wikipedia:|Commission]], if in that fiscal year at least $10,000,000 is made available
			 to the [[Wikipedia:|Commission]] under this section” before the period.

=={{anchor|H582760EFA5DA48778FCB34F810ABA09A}}(b) Technical
			 amendment– ==

Subtitle V of title
			 40, United States Code, is amended by redesignating chapters 1, 2, 3, and 4 as
			 chapters 151, 153, 155, and 157, respectively.
