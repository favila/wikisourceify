{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 40, United States Code, to extend the authorization of the Northern Border Regional Commission, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H58F246AD1ED346E1A1595F6A62A34983}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Northern Border Regional Commission Reauthorization Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H2A0FD6F3A1F9482993101CB6277EA5E7}}Sec. 2. Northern Border Regional Commission=



=={{anchor|H92BBFEEB7ED04ECB95C3F4E750D5F438}}(a) Authorization of appropriations– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/15751 Section 15751 of title 40, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/15751 subsection (b)] as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/15751 subsection (c)] ;
:(2) by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/15751 subsection (a)] the following:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H714E9537A0D94201AAE8D245C0C943E2}}(b) Northern Border Regional Commission– ====

There is authorized to be appropriated to the [[w:|Northern Border Regional Commission]] to carry out this subtitle$30,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018.; and
</blockquote>

:(3) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/15751 subsection (c)] , as so redesignated in paragraph (1)of this subsection, by inserting“by the [[w:|Commission]] , if in that fiscal year at least $10,000,000 is made available to the [[w:|Commission]] under this section”before the period.

=={{anchor|H582760EFA5DA48778FCB34F810ABA09A}}(b) Technical amendment– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/ Subtitle V of title 40, United States Code] , is amended by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/1,2,3,4 chapters 1, 2, 3, and 4] as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/151,153,155,157 chapters 151, 153, 155, and 157] , respectively.
