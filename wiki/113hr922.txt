{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 40, United States Code, to extend the authorization of the Northern Border Regional Commission, and for other purposes.
1. Short title


This Act may be cited as the “Northern Border Regional Commission Reauthorization Act of 2013”.

2. Northern Border Regional Commission


=(a) Authorization of appropriations=

Section 15751 of title 40, United States Code , is amended—: by redesignating subsection (b) as subsection (c) ;: by inserting after subsection (a) the following:

===(b) Northern Border Regional Commission===

There is authorized to be appropriated to the [[Wikipedia:|Northern Border Regional Commission ]]to carry out this subtitle $30,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018 .; and
: in subsection (c) , as so redesignated in paragraph (1) of this subsection, by inserting “by the [[Wikipedia:|Commission ]], if in that fiscal year at least $10,000,000 is made available to the [[Wikipedia:|Commission ]]under this section ”before the period.
=(b) Technical amendment=

Subtitle V of title 40, United States Code , is amended by redesignating chapters 1, 2, 3, and 4 as chapters 151, 153, 155, and 157 , respectively.
