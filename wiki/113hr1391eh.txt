{{Center|AN ACT}}

 Designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 25 South Oak Street in London, Ohio, as the“London Fallen Veterans Memorial Post Office”.   

=Section 1. London Fallen Veterans Memorial Post Office=



==(a) Designation– ==

The facility of the [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]]located at 25 South Oak Street in London, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the“London Fallen Veterans Memorial Post Office”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to insubsection (a)shall be deemed to be a reference to the“London Fallen Veterans Memorial Post Office”.    Passed the House of Representatives June 17, 2014. Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
