{{Center|A BILL}}

 To place a 6-month moratorium on the authority of the Financial Stability Oversight Council to make financial stability determinations.   

=Section 1. Moratorium=

Before the end of the 6-month period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Financial Stability Oversight Council]]may not make a determination under  section 113 of the Financial Stability Act of 2010 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5323 12 U.S.C. 5323] )that a nonbank financial company (as defined under section 102 of such Act ) shall be supervised by the [[w:|Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System]]and subject to prudential standards.
