{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Authorizing expenditures by the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources.

=Section 1. General Authority=

In carrying out its powers, duties, and functions under the Standing Rules of the Senate, in accordance with its jurisdiction under Rule XXV of the Rules, including holding hearings, reporting the hearings, and making investigations as authorized by paragraphs 1 and 8 of Rule XXVI of the Standing Rules of the Senate, the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources|Committee on Energy and Natural Resources]] (referred to in this resolution as the Committee ) is authorized for the period beginning October 1, 2013, and ending September 30, 2014, and for the period beginning October 1, 2014, and ending February 28, 2015, in its discretion—
:(1) to make expenditures from the contingent fund of the [[w:|Senate]] ;
:(2) to employ personnel; and
:(3) with the prior consent of the Government department or agency concerned and the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Rules and Administration|Committee on Rules and Administration]] , to use on a reimbursable or nonreimbursable basis the services of personnel of any such department or agency.

=Sec. 2. Expenses=



==(a) Initial period– ==

The expenses of the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources|Committee]] for the period beginning October 1, 2013, and ending September 30, 2014, under this resolution shall not exceed $5,463,481.

==(b) Subsequent period– ==

The expenses of the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources|Committee]] for the period beginning October 1, 2014, and ending February 28, 2015, under this resolution shall not exceed $2,276,450.

=Sec. 3. Reporting of findings and recommendations=

The [[w:United States Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources|Committee]] shall report its findings, together with such recommendations for legislation as it considers advisable, to the [[w:|Senate]] at the earliest practicable date, but not later than February 28, 2015.

=Sec. 4. Payment from contingent fund=



==(a) In general– ==

Expenses of the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources|Committee]] under this resolution shall be paid from the contingent fund of the Senate on vouchers approved by the chairman of the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources|Committee]] .

==(b) Exceptions– ==

Vouchers shall not be required for—
:(1) the disbursement of salaries of employees paid at an annual rate;
:(2) the payment of telecommunications provided by the [[w:|Office of the Sergeant at Arms and Doorkeeper of the Senate]] ;
:(3) the payment of stationery supplies purchased through the [[w:|Keeper of the Stationery of the Senate]] ;
:(4) payments to the [[w:|Postmaster of the Senate]] ;
:(5) the payment of metered charges on copying equipment provided by the [[w:|Office of the Sergeant at Arms and Doorkeeper of the Senate]] ;
:(6) the payment of [[w:|Senate Recording and Photographic Services]] ; or
:(7) the payment of franked and mass mail costs by the [[w:|Sergeant at Arms and Doorkeeper of the Senate]] .

=Sec. 5. Agency contributions=

There are authorizedsuch sums as are necessaryfor agency contributions related to the compensation of employees of the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Energy and Natural Resources|Committee]] for the period beginning October 1, 2013, and ending September 30, 2014, and for the period beginning October 1, 2014, and ending February 28, 2015, to be paid from theAppropriations account for Expenses of Inquiries and Investigations .
