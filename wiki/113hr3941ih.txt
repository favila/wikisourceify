{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend for one year the deduction for mortgage insurance premiums.   

=Section 1. Extension of mortgage insurance premiums treated as qualified residence interest=



==(a) In general– ==

  Subclause (I) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/163 section 163(h)(3)(E)(iv)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“December 31, 2013”and inserting“December 31, 2014”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or accrued after December 31, 2013.
