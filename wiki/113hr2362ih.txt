{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 49, United States Code, with respect to urbanized area formula grants, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Transportation for Heroes Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Urbanized area formula grants=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5307 Section 5307(c)(1)(D) of title 49, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5307 clause (ii)]by striking“and”at the end;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/5307 clause (iii)]by inserting“and”after the semicolon at the end; and
:(3) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 
::(iv) veteran (as such term is defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 section 101 of title 38]); . 
</blockquote>
