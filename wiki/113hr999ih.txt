{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 49, United States Code, to require that individuals seeking training in the operation of certain aircraft be checked against immigration information in the possession of the Secretary of Homeland Security to ensure that such individuals are citizens or nationals of the United States, lawful permanent resident aliens, or nonimmigrants admitted for a limited period to obtain such training, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Requirement that individuals seeking flight training are checked against immigration records=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 Subsection (a) of section 44939 of title 49, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 subparagraphs (A) through (F) of paragraph (1)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 clauses (i) through (vi)] , respectively;
:(2) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 paragraphs (1)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 (2)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 subparagraphs (A)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 (B)] , respectively;
:(3) by striking “12,500 pounds” and inserting “12,500 pounds—” ;
:(4) by inserting before [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 subparagraph (A)] , as redesignated by paragraph (2) of this subsection, a new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 paragraph (2)] ;
:(5) by moving the text beginning with “to an” and all that follows through “only if—” to such [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 paragraph (2)] ;
:(6) by inserting before such [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 paragraph (2)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 paragraph (1)] :

<blockquote>
::(1) an individual seeking such training only upon receipt of information from the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Assistant Secretary]] that the individual has been checked against databases available to the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Assistant Secretary]] and the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Assistant Secretary]] has determined that the individual is—
:::(A) a citizen of the United States;
:::(B) a national of the United States;
:::(C) an alien lawfully admitted to the United States for permanent residence; or
:::(D) an alien temporarily admitted to the United States as a nonimmigrant for the purpose of obtaining such training; and; and
</blockquote>

:(7) in the subsection heading by striking “Waiting period.—” and inserting “Requirements; waiting period.—” .

==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44949 Subparagraph (B) of paragraph (2) of subsection (a) of section 44949] , as redesignated by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 subsection (a)] , is amended by striking “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 paragraph (1)] ” and inserting “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44949 subparagraph (A)] ” .

==(c) Clerical amendment– ==

The amendments made by subsection (a)shall apply with respect to an individual seeking flight training on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.

=Sec. 2. Requiring flight schools to be certified by FAA=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/44939 Section 44939 of title 49, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

==(j) Certification by FAA– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]] shall require flight schools to be certified by the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration]] pursuant to part 141 or part 142 of title 14, Code of Federal Regulations (or similar successor regulations)..
</blockquote>
