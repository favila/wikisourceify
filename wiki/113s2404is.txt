{{Center|A BILL}}

 To make permanent the extended period of protections for members of uniformed services relating to mortgages, mortgage foreclosure, and eviction, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Foreclosure Relief and Extension for Servicemembers Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Making permanent extended period of protections for members of uniformed services relating to mortgages, mortgage foreclosure, and eviction=

   Section 710(d) of the Honoring America's Veterans and Caring for Camp Lejeune Families Act of 2012 ( [[Public Law 112-154|Public Law 112–154]]  ;126 Stat. 1208)is amended by striking paragraphs (1) and (3) .
