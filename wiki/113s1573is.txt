{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide for the payment of temporary compensation to a surviving spouse of a veteran upon the death of the veteran, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Military Family Relief Act” .

=Sec. 2. Authorization for temporary payment of dependency and indemnity compensation or death pension upon the death of a veteran=



==(a) Temporary dependency and indemnity compensation– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 Section 1318of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 subsections (d)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 (e)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 subsections (e)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 (f)] , respectively; and
:(2) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 subsection (c)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 subsection (d)] :

<blockquote>
::(d) 
:::(1) Notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 subsection (b)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5101 section 5101(a)(1)] of this title and subject to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 paragraphs (2)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 (3)] , the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may pay temporary dependency and indemnity compensation under this chapter to an individual described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1318 paragraph (2)] without regard to whether that individual has submitted a claim for such compensation if, at the time of the death of the veteran, the veteran was in receipt of or entitled to receive (or but for the receipt of retired or retirement pay was entitled to receive) compensation for a service-connected disability continuously rated as total for not less than one year immediately preceding the death of the veteran.
:::(2) An individual described in this paragraph is an individual determined by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] , based on evidence in the file of the deceased veteran on the date of the death of the veteran, to be the surviving spouse of the deceased veteran.
:::(3) Any compensation paid under this subsection shall be made for a period not to exceed six months beginning on the first day of the month in which the veteran died.
:::(4) No individual may receive compensation under this subsection and receive during the same period—
::::(A) dependency and indemnity compensation under any other provision of this title; or
::::(B) pension under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 section 1541] of this title, except as provided in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 subsection (h)(5)] of that section..
</blockquote>


==(b) Temporary pension– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 Section 1541] of such title is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 subsection (h)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 subsection (i)] ; and
:(2) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 subsection (g)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 subsection (h)] :

<blockquote>
::(h) 
:::(1) Notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5101 section 5101(a)(1)] of this title, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may pay pension at the monthly rate of1/12of the annual rate specified in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 subsection (b)] to an individual described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1541 paragraph (2)] without regard to whether the individual has submitted a claim for such pension if, at the time of the death of the veteran, the veteran was in receipt of pension under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1513 section 1513] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1521 1521] of this title as a married veteran based on the marriage of the veteran to the individual.
:::(2) An individual described in this paragraph is an individual determined by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] , based on evidence in the file of the deceased veteran on the date of the death of the veteran, to be the surviving spouse of the deceased veteran.
:::(3) Any pension paid under this subsection shall be made for a period not to exceed six months beginning on the first day of the month in which the veteran died.
:::(4) Pension authorized by this subsection shall be paid without regard to the annual income and net worth of the individual.
:::(5) If an individual who is paid pension under this subsection is entitled to pension under another provision of this chapter or to dependency and indemnity compensation underchapter 13of this title for the period for which the individual was paid pension under this subsection or any part of that period, the individual shall be paid pension under such other provision of this chapter or dependency and indemnity compensation underchapter 13of this title for that period in the amount by which the pension or compensation to which the individual is entitled exceeds the amount of pension paid under this section for that period..
</blockquote>


==(c) Conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5101 Section 5101(a)(1)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by striking “A specific claim” and inserting “Except as otherwise provided in this title, a specific claim” .
