To rename [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 219(c)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 as the Kay Bailey Hutchison Spousal IRA.

=Section 1. Kay Bailey Hutchison Spousal IRA=

The heading of subsection (c) of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 219] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking “Special rules for certain married individuals” and inserting “Kay Bailey Hutchison Spousal IRA” .Passed the House of Representatives June 25, 2013.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
