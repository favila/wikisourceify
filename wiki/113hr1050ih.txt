={{anchor|H45BFE17442164CF8886034A5C35F2B9A}}Section 1. Disperse Red 73=



=={{anchor|HBACAD0EB6EDD458A894F29CF5EB98534}}(a) In general– ==

Heading 9902.03.57 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (relating to Disperse Red 73) is amended by striking the date in the effective period column and inserting“12/31/2016”.

=={{anchor|HC7AFD9202A164E1C8D705DEEB18794FE}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)applies to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
