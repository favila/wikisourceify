{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to furnish headstones and markers for certain deceased veterans buried in veterans’ cemeteries of Indian tribes.

=Section 1. Headstones and markers for placement in tribal veterans’ cemeteries=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2306  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 2306] of title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2306 subsection (a)(4)] , by inserting“or in a veterans’ cemetery on land owned by or held in trust for a tribal organization (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3765 section 3765(4) of this title] )”after“State”; and
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/2306 subsection (b)(1)] , by inserting“a veterans’ cemetery on land owned by or held in trust for a tribal organization,”after“a veterans’ cemetery owned by a State,”.
