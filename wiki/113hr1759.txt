{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to direct the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to submit to Congress quarterly reports on the timeliness in which the Department of Veterans Affairs receives certain information from other departments or agencies of the United States.

={{anchor|HBE826D48F7EC4FEF9B6F6FFEB2D07E29}}Section 1. Quarterly reports to Congress=



=={{anchor|HD1A2D874815D4441A203993E674F9B39}}(a) In general– ==

Chapter 77 of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H649ED66E38E94A3CBB007C33C6B41CCE}}Sec. 7735. Quarterly reports to Congress==



==={{anchor|H209AB990BB7B47E48A3C360EA7D62B6C}}(a) Reports– ===

Not later than 30 days after the end of each fiscal quarter, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a report on covered requests.

==={{anchor|HBBC9805EC7974AC0957A71EC55320CE6}}(b) Matters included– ===

Each report under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7735 subsection (a)] shall include—
:(1) the number of covered requests made during the fiscal quarter covered by the report; and
:(2) the average response time for such covered requests made during each month of such fiscal quarter, as determined based on the period beginning on the date on which the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] made the request and ending on the date on which the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] determines that the request is completed.

==={{anchor|H99EC6817131545539F7F74A0DF84B08B}}(c) Covered request defined– ===

In this section, the term covered request means a request by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] to the head of another department or agency of the United States for information required by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] in adjudicating a claim for disability compensation underchapter 11 of this title..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H5B9FE240046A495B81F336B1F52199FA}}(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by adding after the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7734 section 7734] the following new item:

<blockquote>__NOTOC__
7735. Quarterly reports to Congress..
</blockquote>
