{{Center|A BILL}}

To reauthorize the African Elephant Conservation Act, the
		  Rhinoceros and Tiger Conservation Act of 1994, and the Asian Elephant
		  Conservation Act of 1997.

={{anchor|H4E723D00F0CD47EB8FBA9CC052FD26DF}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Multinational Species Conservation
			 Funds Reauthorization Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H9693F87147264373A4B3BE6D7360FD8D}}Sec. 2.  Reauthorization of
			 African Elephant Conservation Act=

Section 2306(a) of the African Elephant
			 Conservation Act (16 U.S.C. 4245(a)) is amended by striking “2007 through
			 2012” and inserting “2014 through 2018”.

={{anchor|HE7FDB8FBA9D5477DBD580A08585FAA31}}Sec. 3. 
			 Reauthorization of Rhinoceros and Tiger Conservation Act of 1994=

Section 10(a) of the Rhinoceros and Tiger
			 Conservation Act of 1994 (16 U.S.C. 5306(a)) is amended by striking “2007
			 through 2012” and inserting “2014 through 2018”.

={{anchor|H233FC7BEE26B40B38F9B4E2CC642963F}}Sec. 4.  Reauthorization of
			 Asian Elephant Conservation Act of 1997=

Section 8(a) of the Asian Elephant
			 Conservation Act of 1997 (16 U.S.C. 4266(a)) is amended by striking “2007
			 through 2012” and inserting “2014 through 2018”.
