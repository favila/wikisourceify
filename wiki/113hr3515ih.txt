{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to increase the age limit for children using transferred Post-9/11 Educational Assistance.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Increased Age Limit for Post-9/11 Education Assistance Dependents Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Increase of age limit to use transferred Post-9/11 Educational Assistance=



==(a) Increase in age limit– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3319  Section 3319(h)(5)of title 38, United States Code], is amended by striking“26 years”each place it appears and inserting“29 years”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this Act shall take effect as if included in the enactment of the  Post-9/11 Veterans Educational Assistance Act of 2008 ([[Public Law 110-252|title V ofPublic Law 110–252 ]] ).
