{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives that the President should refrain from any further taxpayer-funded vacations until the White House can be re-opened for public tours.Whereas the Obama Administration announced on Tuesday March 5, 2013, that it would cancel all White House tours due to sequestration;Whereas the annual cost to the [[w:United States Secret Service|Secret Service]] of maintaining the White House open for public tours is approximately $3.7 million;Whereas the [[w:President of the United States|President]] has taken four trips to Hawaii in the past four years, at a cost to the taxpayers of at least $4 million each, as well as numerous other vacations at enormous taxpayer expense;Whereas the [[w:President of the United States|President]] has plans for additional vacations in the coming months;Whereas the [[w:President of the United States|President]] has the complete discretion to transfer funds in such a way as to keep the [[w:|White House]] open for tours; andWhereas [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] , under similar budgetary pressures, has maintained the Capitol open for public tours: Now, therefore, be it

= =

That it is the sense of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] that the [[w:President of the United States|President]] should refrain from any further vacations at the taxpayers’ expense until such sums have been saved that the people’s [[w:|White House]] can be re-opened for public tours.
