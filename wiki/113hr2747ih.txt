{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 40, United States Code, to transfer certain functions from the Government Accountability Office to the Department of Labor relating to the processing of claims for the payment of workers who were not paid appropriate wages under certain provisions of such title.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Streamlining Claims Processing for Federal Contractor Employees Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Transfer of administrative authority to the Department of Labor=



==(a) Authority of Comptroller General To pay wages and list contractors violating contracts– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/3144 Section 3144 of title 40, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in the section heading, by striking“ of [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]]  ”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/3144 subsection (a)(1)], by striking“  [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] ”and inserting“  [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]] ”.

==(b) Report of violations and withholding of amounts for unpaid contracts and liquidated damages– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/3703  Section 3703(b)(3)of title 40, United States Code], is amended by striking“  [[w:Government Accountability Office|Comptroller General]] ”both places it appears and inserting“  [[w:United States Department of Labor|Secretary of Labor]] ”.
