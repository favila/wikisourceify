={{anchor|H979376602D234BE4BB5F4C032C8A5697}}Section 1. Disperse Blue 79:1=



=={{anchor|HF77455CEBF63454BA27F2A022E53E1AC}}(a) In general– ==

Heading 9902.03.46 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (relating to Disperse Blue 79:1) is amended by striking the date in the effective period column and inserting“12/31/2016”.

=={{anchor|HF6C225BBEF6B4FF4A1D159303B1F8CD6}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)applies to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
