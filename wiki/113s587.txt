For the relief of Ruben Mkoian, Asmik Karapetian, and Arthur Mkoyan.

={{anchor|ID0A51D7415BF344E38A0EA43A530FDC0A}}Section 1. Permanent resident status for Ruben Mkoian, Asmik Karapetian, and Arthur Mkoyan=



=={{anchor|ID4F8E778C5D7E46EE8D0BE859E7B8371E}}(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding subsections (a)and (b) of section 201of theImmigration and Nationality Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1151 8 U.S.C. 1151] ), Ruben Mkoian, Asmik Karapetian, and Arthur Mkoyan shall each be eligible for the issuance of an immigrant visa or for adjustment of status to that of an alien lawfully admitted for permanent residence upon filing an application for issuance of an immigrant visa under section 204 of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1154 8 U.S.C. 1154] )or for adjustment of status to lawful permanent resident.

=={{anchor|ID484036A1856B4B79B08AE0AB662B151D}}(b) Adjustment of status– ==

If Ruben Mkoian, Asmik Karapetian, or Arthur Mkoyan enters the United States before the filing deadline specified in subsection (c), Ruben Mkoian, Asmik Karapetian, or Arthur Mkoyan, as appropriate, shall be considered to have entered and remained lawfully in the United States and shall be eligible for adjustment of status under section 245 of theImmigration and Nationality Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 8 U.S.C. 1255] )as of the date of the enactment of this Act.

=={{anchor|ID600E241F44CB4B12B2C43AF3C7F5E4DE}}(c) Application and payment of fees– ==

 Subsections (a)and (b)shall apply only if the application for the issuance of an immigrant visa or the application for adjustment of status is filed with appropriate fees not later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this Act.

=={{anchor|ID3AE204551DB2419FBB2F8E316AF5A6F4}}(d) Reduction of immigrant visa numbers– ==

Upon granting an immigrant visa or permanent resident status to Ruben Mkoian, Asmik Karapetian, and Arthur Mkoyan, the [[w:United States Department of State|Secretary of State]] shall instruct the proper officer to reduce by 3, during the current or subsequent fiscal year, the total number of immigrant visas that are made available to natives of the country of birth of Ruben Mkoian, Asmik Karapetian, and Arthur Mkoyan under section 203(a) of theImmigration and Nationality Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1153 8 U.S.C. 1153(a)] ] )or, if applicable, the total number of immigrant visas that are made available to natives of the country of birth of Ruben Mkoian, Asmik Karapetian, and Arthur Mkoyan under section 202(e) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1152 8 U.S.C. 1152(e)] ] ).

=={{anchor|ID9601a209219144eaaad6fea8e72b6f82}}(e) PAYGO– ==

The budgetary effects of this Act, for the purpose of complying with the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010, shall be determined by reference to the latest statement titled“Budgetary Effects of PAYGO Legislation”for this Act, submitted for printing in the Congressional Record by theChairman of the Senate Budget Committee, provided that such statement has been submitted prior to the vote on passage.
