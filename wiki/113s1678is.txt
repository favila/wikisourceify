{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend subchapter II ofchapter 84of title 5, United States Code, to prohibit coverage for annuity purposes for new Federal employees, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Public-Private Employee Retirement Parity Act” .

=Sec. 2. Termination of annuities under the Federal Employees Retirement System=



==(a) Creditable service– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 Section 8411 of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 subsection (b)(1)] , by inserting “except as provided under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 subsection (m)] ,” after “(1)” ; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(m) Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, any period of service performed by an employee or Member on or after the date that is 6 months after the date of enactment of the Public-Private Employee Retirement Parity Actshall not be creditable service for purposes of this subchapter, if that employee or Member did not perform any period of creditable service under this section before the date that is 6 months after the date of enactment of the Public-Private Employee Retirement Parity Act(including any military service creditable under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 subsection (c)] )..
</blockquote>


==(b) Employee contributions– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8422 Section 8422 of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8422 subsection (a)(1)] , by striking “The” and inserting “Except as provided under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8422 subsection (j)] , the” ; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(j) Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, an employing agency shall not make any deduction or withholding under this section from the basic pay of any employee or Member for any period of service performed on or after the date that is 6 months after the date of enactment of the Public-Private Employee Retirement Parity Act, if that period of service is not creditable service under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 section 8411(m)] ..
</blockquote>


==(c) Agency contributions– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8423 Section 8423 of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8423 subsection (a)(1)] , by striking “Each” and inserting “Except as provided under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8423 subsection (d)] , each” ; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(d) Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, an employing agency shall not make any contribution under this section for any employee or Member for any period of service performed on or after the date that is 6 months after the date of enactment of the Public-Private Employee Retirement Parity Act, if that period of service is not creditable service under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 section 8411(m)] ..
</blockquote>


==(d) Limitation on credit for military service– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 Section 8411(c)of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 paragraph (1)] , by striking “paragraphs (2), (3), and (5)” and inserting “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 paragraphs (2)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 (3)] , [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 (5)] , and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 (6)] ” ; and
:(B) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(6) Notwithstanding any other provision of this subsection, any period of military service performed on or after the date that is 6 months after the date of enactment of the Public-Private Employee Retirement Parity Actshall not be allowed credit under this subchapter, if the employee or Member did not perform any period of creditable service under this section before the date that is 6 months after the date of enactment of the Public-Private Employee Retirement Parity Act..
</blockquote>


===(2) Contributions– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8422 Section 8422(e)(1)(A) of title 5, United States Code] , is amended by striking “Except as provided in subparagraph (B), and subject to paragraph (6),” and inserting “Except as provided under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8411 section 8411(c)(6)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8422 subparagraph (B)] of this paragraph, and subject to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8422 paragraph (6)] of this subsection,” .

=Sec. 3. Reporting regarding CSRDF=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8347 Section 8347(f) of title 5, United States Code] , is amended by striking “The [[w:|Board]] shall report annually on the actuarial status of the System” and inserting “Not later than 6 months after the date of enactment of the Public-Private Employee Retirement Parity Act, and every year thereafter, the [[w:|Board]] shall issue an annual report, including making the report available online, on the actuarial status of the System and the Federal Employees Retirement System underchapter 84” .

==(b) Availability of previous reports– ==

The [[w:|Board of Actuaries of the Civil Service Retirement System]] shall make available online all reports under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/8347 section 8347(f)] prepared on or after June 6, 1986.
