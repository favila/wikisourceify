{{Center|A BILL}}

 To improve services for victims of sexual assault and domestic violence.   

=Section 1. Sexual Assault Services grants=



==(a) Amendment– ==

   Paragraph (4) of section 14601(b) of the Violence Against Women Act of 1994 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/14043g 42 U.S.C. 14043g(b)])is amended—
:(1) in the first sentence, by striking“, except that the United States Virgin Islands, American Samoa, Guam, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands shall each be allocated 0.125 percent of the total appropriations”; and
:(2) by striking the third sentence.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by  subsection (a)  shall apply with respect to grants under  section 14601 of the Violence Against Women Act of 1994 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/14043g 42 U.S.C. 14043g])for fiscal year 2013 and succeeding fiscal years.
