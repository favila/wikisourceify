{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 to clarify the treatment of administrative expenses of the Department of Veterans Affairs during sequestration.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Defending Veterans from Sequestration Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Treatment of administrative expenses of Department of Veterans Affairs during sequestration=

   Section 256(h)(4) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/906 2 U.S.C. 906(h)(4)] )is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote> 
:(G)   [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] . . 
</blockquote>
