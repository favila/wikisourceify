{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the continuing professional education reimbursement provided to health professionals employed by the Department of Veterans Affairs.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Assuring Quality Care for Veterans Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Improvement of continuing professional education reimbursement for health professionals employed by the Department of Veterans Affairs=


:(a) In general–  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7411  Section 7411of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
::(1) by striking“shall”and inserting“may”;
::(2) by striking“board-certified physician or dentist appointed under section 7401(1) of this title”and inserting“health professional appointed under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7401 paragraph (1)]or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7401 (3) of section 7401 of this title] ”;
::(3) by striking“$1,000”and inserting“$1,600”;
::(4) by inserting“required to maintain licensure”after“professional education”; and
::(5) by adding at the end the following new sentence:“No such health professional may receive reimbursement under this section and reimbursement for the same expenses incurred for continuing professional education provided by a [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]]medical center.”.
:(b) Clerical amendments– 
::(1) Section heading– The heading for such section is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 

===Sec. 7411. Full-time health professionals: reimbursement of continuing professional education expenses===

 . 
</blockquote>

::(2) Table of sections– The table of sections at the beginning ofchapter 74 of such titleis amended by striking the item relating to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/7411 section 7411]and inserting the following new item:

<blockquote>  7411. Full-time health professionals: reimbursement of continuing professional education expenses.  . 
</blockquote>
