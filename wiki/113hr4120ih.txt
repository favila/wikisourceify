{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the National Law Enforcement Museum Act to extend the termination date.   

=Section 1. National Law Enforcement Museum Act termination date extended=

   Section 4(f) of the National Law Enforcement Museum Act ( [[Public Law 106-492|Public Law 106–492]]  )is amended by striking“13 years”and inserting“16 years”. 

=Sec. 2. Effective date=

The provisions of this Act shall take effect as if this Act were enacted on November 8, 2013.
