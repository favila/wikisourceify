{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 to require that supplemental nutrition assistance benefits be used to purchase only supplemental foods that are eligible for purchase under section 17 of the Child Nutrition Act of 1966 (commonly known as the WIC program).

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Healthy Food Choices Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Amendment=

 Section 3(k) of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2012 7 U.S.C. 2012(k)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>
:(k)  “Food” means a supplemental food of the kind prescribed by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary]] under section 17(b)(14) of the Child Nutrition Act of 1966( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1786 42 U.S.C. 1786(b)(14)] )..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. Effective date=

This Act shall take effect on January 1, 2014.
