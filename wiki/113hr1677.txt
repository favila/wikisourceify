{{Center|A BILL}}

To modify the boundary of Yosemite National Park, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|HB1C0BDEF62FF4F4BB90AB5B6CA4636E2}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Yosemite National Park Boundary Expansion Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H5F763AE0E0F74DDA81B2F82BD4BA5520}}Sec. 2. Purpose=

The purpose of this Act is to modify the boundary of Yosemite National Park to protect from potential development the scenic and biological value of property adjacent to the Park.

={{anchor|HD9A720DE629949CDB673B4C2F7E5CD92}}Sec. 3. Definitions=

In this Act:

=={{anchor|HE2E81B29FBF14D7DB0D7B48A75AC9D8A}}(1) Map– ==

The term map means the map entitled“Yosemite National Park Location Map for Boundary Line Adjustment (BLA) Areas”.

=={{anchor|H717AD8F3263B456CA7CE3FFAA4606826}}(2) Park– ==

The term Park means the Yosemite National Park in the State of California.

=={{anchor|H603757BB45CC4AE9A4BF8097C1940841}}(3) Secretary– ==

The term Secretary means the Secretary of the Interior.

={{anchor|HC43F8E0AD3F2431EB54526849047A1DC}}Sec. 4. Yosemite national park=



=={{anchor|H17C69533E4ED45FDAEF7696274B5F4B6}}(a) Acquisition of land– ==



==={{anchor|HBAEE0B706FEA4C7E80789F3571722A78}}(1) In general– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] may acquire the land or an interest in the land described in subsections (b)and (c)for addition to the Park.

==={{anchor|H39E5387A7F0D4C3E901B861590B04E67}}(2) Means– ===

An acquisition of land under paragraph (1)may be made by donation, purchase from a willing seller with donated or appropriated funds, or exchange.

=={{anchor|HBAD891ADE63E41449BC7298F40757ECA}}(b) Description of land– ==

The land referred to in subsection (a)(1)is the approximately 1,575 acres of land adjacent to the Park, as generally depicted on the map.

=={{anchor|HCD2FB7E5135F41B4A470C02AB15934D8}}(c) Availability of map– ==

The map shall be on file and available for inspection in the appropriate offices of the [[w:National Park Service|National Park Service]] .

=={{anchor|HC9FAF04F1E164B5AB8F6C8FC58B1554C}}(d) Boundary modification– ==

The boundary of the Park shall be revised to reflect the acquisition of the land under subsection (a).

=={{anchor|H53D2CDA68D5A4F83A9128A0699861521}}(e) Administration– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] shall administer any land or interest in land acquired under subsection (a)(1)as part of the Park in accordance with the laws (including regulations) applicable to the Park.
