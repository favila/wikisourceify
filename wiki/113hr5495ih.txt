{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit the Federal Housing Finance Agency from reducing or limiting the multifamily housing business of the Federal National Mortgage Association and the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corporation.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Preserving Multifamily Housing Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition on reducing or limiting multifamily housing business of GSEs=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the [[w:Federal Housing Finance Agency|Director of the Federal Housing Finance Agency]]may not, acting in the [[w:Federal Housing Finance Agency|Director’s]]supervisory or regulatory capacity under  section 1311 of the Federal Housing Enterprises Financial Safety and Soundness Act of 1992 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4511 12 U.S.C. 4511] )or as conservator or receiver of an enterprise (as such term is defined in  section 1303 of such Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4502 12 U.S.C. 4502])) pursuant to  section 1367 of such Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/4617 12 U.S.C. 4617] ), take any action that has the effect of reducing or limiting the volume or scope of the business of an enterprise in mortgages for multifamily housing unless—
:(1) the [[w:Federal Housing Finance Agency|Director]]determines that there is substantial evidence that such action is necessary to ensure the financial safety and soundness of such enterprise;
:(2) causes such determination to be published in the Federal Register; and
:(3) submits written notice of such determination to the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]].
