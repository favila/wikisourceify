{{Center|A BILL}}

To reform the public debt limit.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Debt Limit Reform Act” .

=Sec. 2. Reform of the public debt limit=



==(a) Authority of President To increase public debt limit– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 Section 3101of title 31, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>
:(d) The dollar amount in effect under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 subsection (b)] shall be increased at such times and in such amounts as the [[w:President of the United States|President of the United States]] , or his designee, may provide..
</blockquote>


==(b) Government-Held debt not taken into account for purposes of the public debt limit– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 Section 3101of title 31, United States Code] , as amended by [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 subsection (a)] is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>
:(e) Obligations held by the United States Government (including any obligation which is classified as an intragovernmental holding by the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]] or which is held by any agency or instrumentality of the United States) shall not be taken into account for purposes of applying the limitation imposed under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 subsection (b)] ..
</blockquote>
