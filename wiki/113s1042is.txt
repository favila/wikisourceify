{{Center|A BILL}}

To authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to provide support to university law school programs that are designed to provide legal assistance to veterans, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Veterans Legal Support Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|id75f66dc55c75499cb25a77b2b4e720c2}}Sec. 2. Department of Veterans Affairs support for university legal clinics that assist veterans=



=={{anchor|idae8b983eaf854f12b848dae072b8734e}}(a) Support authorized– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] may provide support to one or more university law school programs that are designed to provide legal assistance to veterans.

=={{anchor|id787608CB1BA3465BAFE320BED19ECE1A}}(b) Eligible programs– ==

Programs of university law schools which may receive support under subsection (a)may include programs that assist veterans with—
:(1) filing and appealing claims for benefits under laws administered by the Secretary; and
:(2) such other civil, criminal, and family legal matters as the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] considers appropriate.

=={{anchor|idf1a54e192d254720b47d1f5dc220cea0}}(c) Financial support– ==



==={{anchor|id312faf80f697451f88e9e528f25514ea}}(1) In general– ===

The support provided a program under subsection (a)may include financial support of the program.

==={{anchor|id0b0d2be2ef46463499b02a87b3458186}}(2) Limitation on amount– ===

The total amount of financial support provided under subsection (a)in any fiscal year may not exceed $1,000,000.

==={{anchor|id7754676bf7a84e708e8af0e5c2ee173f}}(3) Funding– ===

Amounts for financial support under subsection (a)shall be derived from amounts appropriated or otherwise made available to the Medical Services account of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] .
