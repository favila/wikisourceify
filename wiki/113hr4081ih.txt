{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit funds made available to the Department of Education or the Department of Justice from being used to provide postsecondary courses in prisons.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on use of funds for college courses in prisons=

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no funds made available to the [[w:United States Department of Education|Department of Education]]or the [[w:United States Department of Justice|Department of Justice]]shall be used to provide or assist in providing a postsecondary course to an inmate in a Federal or State prison.
