{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend chapter 31 of title 31, United States Code, to provide for an orderly process by which the debt ceiling is increased.

=Section 1. Extension of presidential modification of the debt ceiling=



==(a) In general– ==

Chapter 31 of title 31, United States Code, is amended by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101A sec tion 3101A] the following new section:

<blockquote>

==Sec. 3101B. Extension of presidential modification of the debt ceiling==



===(a) Extension of debt limit– ===

Whenever the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] submits a written certification to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] that the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] has determined that the debt subject to limit is within $100,000,000,000 of the limit in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101 section 3101(b)] and that further borrowing is required to meet existing commitments, the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]] may exercise authority to borrow an additional amount that the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] estimates is sufficient to meet commitments for one year, subject to the enactment of a joint resolution of disapproval enacted pursuant to this section. The written certification shall also contain the amount that the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] so estimates is sufficient.

===(b) Joint resolution of disapproval– ===



====(1) In general– ====

The debt limit may not be raised under this section if, within 50 calendar days after the date on which [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] receives a certification described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101B subsection (a)] , there is enacted into law a joint resolution disapproving the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President’s]] exercise of authority with respect to such additional amount.

====(2) Contents of joint resolution– ====

For the purpose of this section, the term joint resolution means only a joint resolution—
:(A) for the certification described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101B subsection (a)] ;
:(B) which does not have a preamble;
:(C) the title of which is only as follows:“Joint resolution relating to the disapproval of the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President’s]] exercise of authority to increase the debt limit by $________, as submitted under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101B section 3101B of title 31, United States Code] , on ______”(with the first blank containing the amount of such proposed increase and the second blank containing the date of such submission); and
:(D) the matter after the resolving clause of which is only as follows:“That [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] disapproves of the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President’s]] exercise of authority to increase the debt limit, as exercised pursuant to the certification under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101B section 3101B(a) of title 31, United States Code] .”.

===(c) Expedited consideration; amendment not in order; coordination with action by other House– ===

The provisions of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101A subsections (c)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101A (d)] ,[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101A (e)] , and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101A (f) of section 3101A] shall apply to any joint resolution of disapproval under this section, except that—
:(1) the date set forth in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101A section (d)(3)] of such section;
:(2) the exception for the $400,000,000,000 increase described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101A subsection (f)(5) of such section] ; and
:(3) [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3101A subsection (f)(6)] ;shall be inapplicable..
</blockquote>
