{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the National Labor Relations Act and the Railway Labor Act to prohibit the preemption of State identity theft laws.

=Section 1. National Labor Relations Act=

The National Labor Relations Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/151 29 U.S.C. 151 et seq.] )is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote>

=20. =

Nothing in this Act shall be construed to preempt a law of any State, Territory, or the District of Columbia that prohibits, criminalizes, or creates a civil cause of action for identity theft or the release of an individual’s personally identifiable information..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 2. Railway Labor Act=

 Title I of the Railway Labor Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/45/151 45 U.S.C. 151 et seq.] )is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote>

=Sec. 16. Conflicts with State identity theft laws=

Nothing in this Act shall be construed to preempt a law of any State, Territory, or the District of Columbia that prohibits, criminalizes, or creates a civil cause of action for identity theft or the release of an individual’s personally identifiable information..
</blockquote>
