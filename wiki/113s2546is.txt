{{Center|A BILL}}

 To repeal a requirement that new employees of certain employers be automatically enrolled in the employer’s health benefits plan.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Auto Enroll Repeal Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal=

   Section 18A of the Fair Labor Standards Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/29/218a 29 U.S.C. 218a] ), as added by section 1511 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act , is repealed.
