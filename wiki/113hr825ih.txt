{{Center|A BILL}}

To exclude from consideration as income under the United States Housing Act of 1937 payments of pension made under section 1521 of title 38, United States Code, to veterans who are in need of regular aid and attendance.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Vulnerable Veterans Housing Reform Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Exclusion from income=

 Paragraph (4) of section 3(b) of the United States Housing Act of 1937( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1437a 42 U.S.C. 1437a(b)(4)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking “and any amounts” and inserting “, any amounts” ;
:(2) by striking “or any deferred” and inserting “, any deferred” ; and
:(3) by inserting after “prospective monthly amounts” the following: “, and any expenses related to aid and attendance as detailed under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1521 section 1521 of title 38, United States Code] ” .
