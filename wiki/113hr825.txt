{{Center|A BILL}}

To exclude from consideration as income under the United
		  States Housing Act of 1937 payments of pension made under section 1521 of title
		  38, United States Code, to veterans who are in need of regular aid and
		  attendance.

={{anchor|H9C0A03ED7D684DC4866F978BA1955FE9}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Vulnerable Veterans Housing Reform Act
			 of 2013”.

={{anchor|HE9B478C7AE924F4EA1C304D9697C3EA8}}Sec. 2. Exclusion from
			 income=

Paragraph (4) of
			 section 3(b) of the United States Housing Act of 1937 (42 U.S.C. 1437a(b)(4))
			 is amended—
:(1) by striking
			 “and any amounts” and inserting “, any
			 amounts”;
:(2) by striking
			 “or any deferred” and inserting “, any deferred”;
			 and
:(3) by inserting after
			 “prospective monthly amounts” the following: “, and any
			 expenses related to aid and attendance as detailed under section 1521 of title
			 38, United States Code”.
