{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to increase
		  the Federal medical assistance percentage for the territories.

={{anchor|H02FD6B1CFDB24F0E9D7EF0CDA47B25E4}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Medicaid Payment Fairness to the
			 Territories Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H4A9076BF09004515A44DB931A645FF25}}Sec. 2. Increase in the
			 Federal medical assistance percentage for the territories=

Section 1905(b)(2) of the Social Security
			 Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1396d 42 U.S.C. 1396d(b)(2)]) is amended by inserting after “55
			 percent” the following: “(or, beginning with fiscal year 2014, the
			 highest such Federal medical assistance percentage applicable to any of the 50
			 States for the fiscal year involved)”.
