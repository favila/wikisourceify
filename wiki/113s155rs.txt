{{Center|A BILL}}

 To designate a mountain in the State of Alaska as Denali.   

=Section 1. Designation of Denali in the State of Alaska=



==(a) In general– ==

The mountain located at 63° 04′ 12″ N, by 151° 00′ 18″ W, in the State of Alaska shall be known and designated as“Denali”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the mountain described in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to“Denali”.
