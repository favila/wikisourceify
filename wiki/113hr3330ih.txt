{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to repeal the limitation on the number of veterans authorized to be enrolled in programs of independent living services and assistance administered by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Veterans’ Independent Living Enhancement Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Repeal of limitation on number of veterans enrolled in programs of independent living services and assistance administered by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs=



==(a) Repeal– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3120  Section 3120of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3120 subsection (e)]; and
:(2) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3120 subsection (f)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3120 subsection (e)].

==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3120 Subsection (a) of such section]is amended by striking“ subsection (f) ”and inserting“  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3120 subsection (e)]  ”.

==(c) Report to Congress– ==

Not later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall submit to the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committees on Veterans’ Affairs of the Senate]] and [[w:United States House Committee on Veterans' Affairs|House of Representatives]] a plan to educate employees of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department of Veterans Affairs]] whose responsibilities relate to vocational rehabilitation counseling or employment counseling about the benefits and appropriate use of the independent living services provided under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3120  section 3120of title 38, United States Code].
