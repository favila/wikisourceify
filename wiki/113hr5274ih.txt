=Section 1. Voluntary agreements for Social Security and Medicare coverage of employees of Guam and American Samoa=

   Section 218(b)(1) of the Social Security Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/418 42 U.S.C. 418(b)(1)] )is amended by striking“, Guam, or American Samoa”.
