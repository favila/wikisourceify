{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the use of funds for United States participation in joint military exercises with Egypt if the Government of Egypt abrogates, terminates, or withdraws from the 1979 Egypt-Israel peace treaty.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Conditioning United States-Egypt Military Exercises Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Contingent limitation on availability of funds for United States participation in joint military exercises with Egypt=



==(a) Limitation– ==

No amounts appropriated or otherwise made available for the United States Government may be used for United States participation in joint military exercises with Egypt if the Government of Egypt abrogates, terminates, or withdraws from the 1979 Egypt-Israel peace treaty signed at Washington, DC, on March 26, 1979.

==(b) Waiver– ==

The [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] may waive the limitation in subsection (a)if the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] certifies to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] in writing that the waiver is in the national security interests of the United States.
