{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Migratory Bird Hunting and Conservation Stamp Act to increase the price of Migratory Bird Hunting and Conservation Stamps to fund the acquisition of conservation easements for migratory birds, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Federal Duck Stamp Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Increase in price of Migratory Bird Hunting and Conservation Stamp to fund acquisition of conservation easements for migratory birds=

The Migratory Bird Hunting and Conservation Stamp Act is amended—
:(1) in  section 2(b) ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/718b 16 U.S.C. 718b(b)] )—
::(A) by striking“1990, and”and inserting“1990,”; and
::(B) by striking“for each hunting year thereafter”and inserting“for hunting years 1991 through 2013, and $25 for each hunting year thereafter”;
:(2) by adding at the end of  section 2 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/718b 16 U.S.C. 718b] )the following:

<blockquote> 

===(c) Reduction in price of stamp– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]may reduce the price of each stamp sold under the provisions of this section for a hunting year if the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]determines that the increase in the price of the stamp after hunting year 2013 resulted in a reduction in revenues deposited into the fund; ; and 
</blockquote>

:(3) in  section 4 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/718d 16 U.S.C. 718d] )—
::(A) in subsection (a)(3) , by inserting before the period the following:“, in which there shall be a subaccount to which the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]]shall transfer all amounts in excess of $15 that are received from the sale of each stamp sold for each hunting year after hunting year 2013”;
::(B) in subsection (b)(1) , by striking“So much”and inserting“except as provided in paragraph (4) , so much”;
::(C) in subsection (b)(2) , by striking“paragraph (3)”and inserting“  paragraphs (3) and (4)  ”; and
::(D) by adding at the end of subsection (b) the following:

<blockquote> 

====(4) Conservation easements– ====

Amounts in the subaccount referred to in subsection (a)(3) shall be used by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]solely to acquire easements in real property for conservation of migratory birds. . 
</blockquote>
 

=Sec. 3. Annual report on expenditures=

   Section 4 of the Migratory Bird Hunting and Conservation Stamp Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/718d 16 U.S.C. 718d] )is further amended—
:(1) in subsection (c) —
::(A) by striking so much as precedes“The Secretary may”and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

====(c) Promotion of Stamp Sales– ====

 ; and 
</blockquote>

::(B) by striking paragraph (2) ; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

===(e) Annual report– ===

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]]shall include in each annual report of the [[w:Migratory Bird Conservation Commission|Commission]]under  section 3 of the Migratory Bird Conservation Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/715b 16 U.S.C. 715b] )—
:(1) a description of activities conducted under subsection (c) in the year covered by the report; and
:(2) an annual assessment of the status of wetlands conservation projects for migratory bird conservation purposes, including a clear and accurate accounting of—
::(A) all expenditures by Federal and State agencies under this section;
::(B) all expenditures made for fee-simple acquisition of Federal lands in the United States, including the amount paid and acreage of each parcel acquired in each acquisition. . 
</blockquote>
