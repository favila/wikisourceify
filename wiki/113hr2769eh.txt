{{Center|H. R. 2769}}


{{Center|IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES}}


{{Center|AN ACT}}

To impose a moratorium on conferences held by the Internal Revenue Service.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Stop Playing on Citizens’ Cash Act” .

=Sec. 2. Moratorium on IRS conferences=

The [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]] shall not hold any conference until the [[w:Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration|Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration]] submits a report to [[w:|Congress]] —
:(1) certifying that the [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]] has implemented all of the recommendations set out in such Inspector General’s report titled Review of the August 2010 Small Business/Self-Employed Division’s Conference in Anaheim, California , and
:(2) describing such implementation.Passed the House of Representatives July 31, 2013.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
