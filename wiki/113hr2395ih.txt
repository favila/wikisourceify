{{Center|A BILL}}

 To provide for donor contribution acknowledgments to be displayed at projects authorized under the Commemorative Works Act, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Donor contributions=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8905 Section 8905(b) of title 40, United States Code]is amended by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8905 paragraph (7)]and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

==(7) Donor contributions– ==



===(A) In general– ===

Except as otherwise provided in this paragraph, the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] or [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]] , as applicable, may permit a sponsor described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8905 subsection (a)]to acknowledge donor contributions at the commemorative work.

===(B) Requirements– ===

Acknowledgments shall—
:(i) be displayed inside a visitor center or other ancillary structure associated with the commemorative work; and
:(ii) conform to applicable [[w:National Park Service|National Park Service]] or [[w:General Services Administration|General Services Administration]] guidelines for donor recognition, as applicable.

===(C) Limitations– ===

Acknowledgments shall—
:(i) be limited to an appropriate statement or credit recognizing the contribution;
:(ii) be displayed in a form approved by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] or [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]] ;
:(iii) be displayed for a period of time determined by the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] or [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]] to be appropriate, commensurate with the level of the contribution;
:(iv) be limited to short, discrete, and unobtrusive acknowledgments or credits; and
:(v) not include any advertising slogans or company logos.

===(D) Submittal of plan– ===



====(i) In general– ====

Prior to the display of donor acknowledgments, the sponsor shall submit to the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] or [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]] , as applicable, for approval a plan for displaying the donor acknowledgments, including—
:(I) the sample text and types of acknowledgments to be displayed; and
:(II) the form and location of all displays.

====(ii) Notification and resubmittal– ====

If the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] or [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]] does not approve the plan submitted under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/40/8905 clause (i)], the [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary]] or [[w:General Services Administration|Administrator]] shall—
:(I) not later than 60 days after the date on which the plan is received, notify the sponsor of the reasons the plan is not approved; and
:(II) allow the sponsor to resubmit a revised donor acknowledgment plan.

===(E) Cost– ===

The sponsor shall bear all expenses related to the display of donor acknowledgments.

===(F) Applicability– ===

This paragraph shall apply to any commemorative work dedicated after January 1, 2010. . 
</blockquote>
