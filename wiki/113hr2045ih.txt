{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit officers and employees of the Internal Revenue Service from initiating any new audits for 180 days.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “HALT the IRS Act” .

=Sec. 2. Suspension of authority of IRS to initiate new audits=

An officer or employee of the [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]] shall not initiate any new audit of any taxpayer during the 180-day period beginning with the date of the enactment of this Act.
