{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title XI of the Social Security Act to eliminate civil money penalties for inducements to physicians to limit services that are not medically necessary, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Eliminating civil money penalties for inducements to physicians to limit services that are not medically necessary=



==(a) In general– ==

   Section 1128A(b)(1) of the Social Security Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1320a–7a 42 U.S.C. 1320a–7a(b)(1)])is amended by inserting“medically necessary”after“reduce or limit”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made bysubsection (a)shall apply to payments made on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
