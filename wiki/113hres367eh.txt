{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Providing for consideration of the Senate amendment to the joint resolution (H.J. Res. 59) making continuing appropriations for fiscal year 2014, and for other purposes, and waiving a requirement of clause 6(a) of rule XIII with respect to consideration of certain resolutions reported from the Committee on Rules.That upon adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to take from the Speaker's table the joint resolution (H.J. Res. 59) making continuing appropriations for fiscal year 2014, and for other purposes, with the House amendments to the Senate amendment thereto, and to consider in the [[w:|House]] , without intervention of any point of order, a motion offered by the chair of the [[w:United States House Committee on Appropriations|Committee on Appropriations]] or his designee that the [[w:|House]] recede from its amendments and concur in the Senate amendment with the amendment printed in the report of the [[w:United States House Committee on Rules|Committee on Rules]] accompanying this resolution. The [[w:|Senate]] amendment and the motion shall be considered as read. The motion shall be debatable for 40 minutes equally divided and controlled by the chair and ranking minority member of the [[w:United States House Committee on Appropriations|Committee on Appropriations]] . The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the motion to its adoption without intervening motion or demand for division of the question.

=2. =

The requirement of clause 6(a) of rule XIII for a two-thirds vote to consider a report from the [[w:United States House Committee on Rules|Committee on Rules]] on the same day it is presented to the [[w:|House]] is waived with respect to any resolution reported through the legislative day of October 7, 2013.Karen L. Haas,Clerk.
