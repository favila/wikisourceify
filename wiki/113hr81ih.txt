{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend subtitle B of title I of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act to extend the temporary high-risk insurance pool program to the territories.

=Section 1. Inclusion of territories in temporary high-risk insurance pool program=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 1101 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18001 42 U.S.C. 18001] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

===(h) State defined– ===

Notwithstanding section 1304(d), for purposes of this section the term State means each of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, American Samoa, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands of the United States, and any other territory or possession of the United States..
</blockquote>


==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 Section 1304(d) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/18024 42 U.S.C. 18024(d)] )is amended by inserting“(except for purposes of section 1101)”after“In this title”.
