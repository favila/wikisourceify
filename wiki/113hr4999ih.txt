{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend and expand the membership of the Advisory Committee on Minority Veterans to include veterans who are lesbian, gay, or bisexual and veterans who are transgender.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Voices for Veterans Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension and expansion of membership of the Advisory Committee on Minority Veterans to include veterans who are lesbian, gay, or bisexual and veterans who are transgender=



==(a) Expansion of membership– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/544 Subsection (a)(2)(A) ofsection 544of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/544 clause (iv)], by striking“and”at the end;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/544 clause (v)], by striking the period at the end and inserting“; and”; and
:(3) by inserting after[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/544 clause (v)]the following new clauses:

<blockquote> 
::(vi) veterans who are lesbian, gay, bisexual, or transgender. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Extension– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/544 Subsection (e) of such section]is amended by striking“December 31, 2014”and inserting“December 31, 2017”.

==(c) Effective date– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/544 Clause (vi) ofsection 544(a)(2)(A)of title 38, United States Code], shall apply to appointments made on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
