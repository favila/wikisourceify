{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the provision of guide dogs to veterans blinded by a service-connected injury.

=Section 1. Provision of guide dogs to certain blind veterans=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1714 Subsection (b) ofsection 1714of title 38, United States Code] , is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>
:(b) 
::(1) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall provide guide dogs trained for the aid of the blind to a veteran who—
:::(A) is blind by reason of a service-connected injury;
:::(B) is enrolled under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1705 section 1705 of this title] ; and
:::(C) elects to receive such a dog.
::(2) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may provide guide dogs trained for the aid of the blind to a veteran who is—
:::(A) enrolled under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1705 section 1705 of this title] ; and
:::(B) not otherwise described under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1714 paragraph (1)(A)] .
::(3) The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may also provide a veteran described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1714 paragraph (1)] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1714 (2)] with mechanical or electronic equipment for aiding them in overcoming the disability of blindness..
</blockquote>


==(b) Conforming amendment– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1714 Section 1714(d) of such title] is amended by striking “subsection (b) or (c)” and inserting “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1714 paragraph (1)] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1714 (2) of subsection (b)] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1714 subsection (c)] ” .

==(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date that is 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
