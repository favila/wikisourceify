=Section 1. Extension of Special Immigrant Visa Programs for certain Afghanis and Iraqis=



==(a) Refugee Crisis in Iraq Act of 2007– ==

 Section 1244(c) of the Refugee Crisis in Iraq Act of 2007( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1157 8 U.S.C. 1157note] )is amended—
:(1) in paragraph (1), by striking “five fiscal years” and inserting “6 fiscal years” ; and
:(2) in paragraph (3)—
::(A) in subparagraph (A)—
:::(i) in the subparagraph heading, by striking “four” and inserting “five” ; and
:::(ii) by striking “four” and inserting “five” ;
::(B) in subparagraph (B)—
:::(i) in the subparagraph heading, by striking “five and six” and inserting “six and seven” ;
:::(ii) by striking “sixth” and inserting “seventh” ; and
:::(iii) by striking “fifth” every place it appears and inserting “sixth” .

:(2) in subparagraph (C)—
::(A) in clause (i)—
:::(i) in the clause heading, by striking “2013” and inserting “2014” ; and
:::(ii) by striking “or 2013” and inserting “2013, or 2014” ;

:::(i) by striking “2014” and inserting “2015” ;

:::(iii) by striking “2013” each place it appears and inserting “2014” .
