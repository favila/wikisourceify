{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize
		  certain programs relating to traumatic brain injury and to trauma
		  research.

={{anchor|H9F651BB86E8C47E58015BAED64D134F0}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Traumatic Brain Injury Reauthorization
			 Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HFDDE97B6A3F04D2781A6D2B4FCB7A7E9}}Sec. 2. CDC programs for
			 prevention and surveillance of traumatic brain injury=

Section 394A of the Public Health Service
			 Act (42 U.S.C. 280b–3) is amended—
:(1) by striking the
			 section heading and all that follows through “For the purpose” and
			 inserting the following:

<blockquote>

=={{anchor|H9D9622DBD7064671B2B96A33BDACC50B}}Sec. 394. Authorization
				of appropriations==



==={{anchor|H7D78F3B5BBAC42E8A9BD0E4FB403EC15}}(a) In
				general– ===

For the
				purpose;
				and
</blockquote>

:(2) by adding at the
			 end the following:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|HCD0D8794049C452ABA6845B385A38852}}(b) Traumatic brain
				injury– ===

To carry out sections
				393B and 393C, there are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be
				necessary for each of fiscal years 2014 through
				2018..
</blockquote>


={{anchor|HC980AE52D04D4A69AC33E99DAF73E036}}Sec. 3. State grants for
			 projects regarding traumatic brain injury=

Section 1252 of the Public Health Service
			 Act (42 U.S.C. 300d–52) is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a),
			 by striking “, acting through the Administrator of the Health Resources
			 and Services Administration,”; and
:(2) in subsection (j),
			 by striking “2012” and inserting “2018”.

={{anchor|HD745D1EC957947A490399633BC25F1FD}}Sec. 4. State grants for
			 protection and advocacy services=

Section 1253 of the Public Health Service
			 Act (42 U.S.C. 300d–53) is amended—
:(1) in subsection (a),
			 by striking “, acting through the [[Wikipedia:Health Resources and Services Administration|Administrator of the Health Resources
			 and Services Administration]] (referred to in this section as the
			 “Administrator”),”;
:(2) in subsections
			 (c), (d)(1), (e)(1), (e)(4), (g), (h), and (j)(1), by striking
			 “Administrator” each place it appears and inserting
			 “Secretary”;
:(3) in subsection
			 (i)—
::(A) by striking
			 “[[Wikipedia:Health Resources and Services Administration|Administrator of the Health Resources and Services
			 Administration]]” and inserting “Secretary”; and
::(B) by striking
			 “by the Administrator” and inserting “by the
			 Secretary”; and
:(4) in subsection (l),
			 by striking “2012” and inserting “2018”.

={{anchor|H4951733591B34364B79E436E204DBAA1}}Sec. 5. Interagency
			 program for trauma research=

Section 1261(i) of the Public Health Service
			 Act (42 U.S.C. 300d–61(i)) is amended by striking “2012” and
			 inserting “2018”.
