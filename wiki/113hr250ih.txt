{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Antiquities Act of 1906 to place additional requirements on the establishment of national monuments under that Act, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Requirement for establishment of national monument=

 Section 2of the Act of June 8, 1906 (commonly known as the “Antiquities Act of 1906” ; [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/431 16 U.S.C. 431] )is amended by striking “, in his discretion, to declare by public proclamation” and inserting “to declare, subject to approval by an Act of Congress,” .
