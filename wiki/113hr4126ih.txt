{{Center|A BILL}}

 To authorize the construction of the expansion of San Luis Reservoir in California.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   San Luis Reservoir Expansion Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. San Luis Reservoir expansion=

Section 103(f) of Title I of [[Public Law 108-361|Public Law 108–361]]  (the Calfed Bay-Delta Authorization Act ,118 Stat. 1681) is amended by—
:(a) Striking[[Public Law 108-361|subparagraph (1)]] and inserting the following:

<blockquote> 

===(1) Conveyance and expansion– ===

The amounts authorized to be appropriated under[[Public Law 108-361|section 109]] may be expended for the following.  
</blockquote>

:(b) Inserting“including the increase of storage capacity at San Luis Reservoir,”after“improvement project,”.
