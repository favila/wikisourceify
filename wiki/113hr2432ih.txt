{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the obligation or expenditure of funds made available to any Federal department or agency for any fiscal year to provide military assistance to any of the armed combatants in Syria absent express prior statutory authorization from Congress.

=Section 1. Prohibition on obligation and expenditure of funds to provide military assistance to any of the armed combatants in Syria=

None of the funds made available to any Federal department or agency for any fiscal year may be obligated or expended to provide military assistance to any of the armed combatants in Syria absent express prior statutory authorization from [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] .
