{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 23, United States Code, to establish a Transportation Infrastructure Finance and Innovation Act Revolving Fund, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   TIFIA 2.0 Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. TIFIA funding=



==(a) In general– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/608  Section 608of title 23, United States Code], is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 608. Funding==



===(a) TIFIA Revolving Fund– ===



====(1) Establishment– ====

There is established in the Treasury of the United States a revolving fund to be known as the Transportation Infrastructure Finance and Innovation Act Revolving Fund (in this section referred to as the“Fund”).

====(2) Deposits– ====

There shall be deposited in the Fund the following:
:(A) Amounts made available to carry out this chapter.
:(B) Amounts received from the repayment of principal and interest on a direct loan made under this chapter.
:(C) Unobligated and uncommitted budget authority under this chapter in a fiscal year.
:(D) Proceeds from the sale of secured loans under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/603 section 603(d)].
:(E) Amounts received from interest on investments under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/608 paragraph (6)].
:(F) Amounts received from the collection of fees established by the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]](in this section referred to as the“Secretary”) pursuant to this chapter.

====(3) Disbursements– ====

Disbursements from the Fund may be made by the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]]for the purpose of carrying out this chapter.

====(4) Rural set aside– ====



=====(A) In general– =====

Of the amounts deposited in the Fund in a fiscal year, not more than 10 percent shall be set aside for use in the following fiscal year for rural infrastructure projects.

=====(B) Reinvestment– =====

Any amounts set aside for a fiscal year under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/608 subparagraph (A)]that remain unobligated by June 1 of that fiscal year shall be invested pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/608 paragraph (6)].

====(5) Transfers– ====

The [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]]shall transfer from the Fund to the general fund of the Treasury amounts equivalent to moneys deposited in the Fund as a result of repayment of principal and interest on a direct loan made under this chapter before the date of enactment of the TIFIA 2.0 Act .

====(6) Investments authority– ====

The [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]]shall invest any portion of the Fund that, as determined by the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]], is not required to meet current expenses. Each such investment shall be made in an interest-bearing obligation of the United States or an obligation guaranteed both as to principal and interest by the United States that, as determined by the Secretary, has a maturity date suitable for the purposes of the Fund. The [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]]shall credit interest earned on the obligations to the Fund.

====(7) Administrative costs– ====

Of the amounts in the Fund, the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]]may use not more than 0.50 percent for each fiscal year for the administration of this chapter, excluding amounts to be transferred under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/23/608 paragraph (5)].

===(b) Contracting authority– ===



====(1) In general– ====

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, execution of a term sheet by the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]]of a Federal credit instrument that uses amounts in the Fund shall impose on the United States a contractual obligation to fund the Federal credit investment.

====(2) Availability– ====

Amounts in the Fund shall be available for obligation without fiscal year limitation and without further appropriation until expended. . 
</blockquote>
