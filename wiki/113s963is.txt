Preventing an unrealistic future Medicaid augmentation plan.

={{anchor|idFCD3EEDCDA9A40548226C59BC4CD4EDD}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Preventing an Unrealistic FMAP Act”.

={{anchor|id6CB3BBFCFE2E4BD09FE2F8AF1A91FB03}}Sec. 2. Preventing an unrealistic Federal medical assistance percentage by repealing the Affordable Care Act enhanced Federal medical assistance percentages for Medicaid expansions=



=={{anchor|idCF9A0F025CDD4C4C9AE90CCE31076914}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 1905 of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 42 U.S.C. 1396d] )is amended by striking subsections (y)and (z).

=={{anchor|idCCC0FA1C08B3440DA9E3D68A6BE861B6}}(b) Conforming amendments– ==

 Section 1905 of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 42 U.S.C. 1396d] )is amended—
:(1) in the first sentence of subsection (b)—
::(A) by striking“ subsections (y), (z), and”and inserting“subsection”; and
::(B) by striking“and subsection (y)(without regard to paragraph (1)(C) of such subsection)”;
:(2) in subsection (aa), in each of paragraphs (1)(A), (2)(A), and (3), by striking“, subsections (y)and (z),”each place it appears; and
:(3) in subsection (cc), by striking“ subsections (y), (z), and”and inserting“subsection”.

=={{anchor|id10CB7EB593A349CD924AECD1ABC2F4FC}}(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the earlier of—
:(1) the date of enactment of this Act; or
:(2) December 31, 2013.
