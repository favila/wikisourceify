{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to provide for the payment of monthly annuities under the Survivor Benefit Plan to a supplemental or special needs trust established for the sole benefit of a disabled dependent child of a participant in the Survivor Benefit Plan.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Disabled Military Child Protection Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Survivor Benefit Plan annuities for special needs trusts established for the benefit of dependent children incapable of self-support=



==(a) Special needs trust as eligible beneficiary– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 Subsection (a) ofsection 1450of title 10, United States Code] , is amended—
:(A) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 paragraph (4)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 paragraph (5)] ; and
:(B) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 paragraph (3)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 paragraph (4)] :

<blockquote>

=====(4) Special needs trusts for sole benefit of certain dependent children– =====

Notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 subsection (i)] , a supplemental or special needs trust described in subparagraph (A)or (C)of section 1917(d)(4) of the Social Security Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1396p 42 U.S.C. 1396p(d)(4)] )that is established for the sole benefit of a dependent child considered disabled under section 1614(a)(3) of that Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1382c 42 U.S.C. 1382c(a)(3)] )who is incapable of self-support because of mental or physical incapacity..
</blockquote>


===(2) Conforming amendment– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1450 Subsection (i) of such section] is amended by inserting “(a)(4) or” after “subsection” .

==(b) Regulations– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 Section 1455(d) of such title] is amended—
:(1) in the subsection heading, by striking “and fiduciaries” and inserting “, fiduciaries, and special needs trusts” ;
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 paragraph (1)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraph (A)] , by striking “and” at the end;
::(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraph (B)] , by striking the period at the end and inserting “; and” ; and
::(C) by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote>
:::(C) a dependent child incapable of self-support because of mental or physical incapacity for whom a supplemental or special needs trust, as described in subparagraph (A)or (C)of section 1917(d)(4) of the Social Security Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1396p 42 U.S.C. 1396p(d)(4)] ), has been established.;
</blockquote>

:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 paragraph (2)] —
::(A) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraphs (C) through (H)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraphs (D) through (I)] , respectively;
::(B) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraph (B)] the following new [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraph (C)] :

<blockquote>
:::(C) In the case of an annuitant referred to in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 paragraph (1)(C)] , payment of the annuity to the supplemental or special needs trust established for the annuitant.;
</blockquote>

::(C) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraph (D)] , as redesignated, by striking “subparagraphs (D) and (E)” and inserting “ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraphs (E)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 (F)] ” ; and
::(D) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 subparagraph (H)] , as so redesignated—
:::(i) by inserting “or (1)(C)” after “paragraph (1)(B)” in the matter preceding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 clause (i)] ;
:::(ii) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 clause (i)] , by striking “and” at the end;
:::(iii) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 clause (ii)] , by striking the period at the end and inserting “; and” ; and
:::(iv) by adding at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote>
::::(iii) procedures for determining when annuity payments to a supplemental or special needs trust shall end based on the death or marriage of the dependent child for which the trust was established.; and
</blockquote>

:(4) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1455 paragraph (3)] , by striking “to guardian or fiduciary” in the paragraph heading.
