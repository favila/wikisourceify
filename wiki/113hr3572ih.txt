{{Center|A BILL}}

 To revise the boundaries of certain John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System units in North Carolina.   

=Section 1. Replacement of John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System maps=



==(a) In general– ==

The maps subtitled“Lea Island Complex L07”;“Wrightsville Beach Unit L08, Masonboro Island Unit L09”; and“Masonboro Island Unit L09”, included in the set of maps entitled“John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System”referred to in  section 4(a) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/3503 16 U.S.C. 3503(a)] )and relating to certain John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System units in North Carolina, are hereby replaced by other maps relating to the units entitled“Lea Island Complex L07”;“Wrightsville Beach Unit L08, Masonboro Island Unit L09”; and“Masonboro Island Unit L09”and dated _____.

==(b) Availability– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Interior|Secretary of the Interior]]shall keep the replacement maps referred to in subsection (a) on file and available for inspection in accordance with  section 4(b) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/3503 16 U.S.C. 3503(b)] ).
