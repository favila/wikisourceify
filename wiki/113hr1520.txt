{{Center|A BILL}}

To require the Secretary of Defense to allow civilian employees of the Department of Defense to delay furloughs until returning from a deployment in support of accounting and recovery efforts by the Joint POW/MIA Accounting Command.

={{anchor|HFC588EAB6961469AAE97C6BD10083518}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“POW/MIA Accounting and Recovery Support Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HCFD54A2262914B0B86AFC8579C837703}}Sec. 2. Exception to civilian furlough requirements for Department of Defense employees deployed in support of Joint POW/MIA Accounting Command accounting and recovery efforts=

The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall require that, with respect to a civilian employee of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] who is required to take a furlough that would otherwise occur during the deployment of that employee in support of accounting and recovery efforts by the Joint POW/MIA Accounting Command, the employee may take the furlough after returning from the deployment.
