{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to expand the types of livestock for which bonus depreciation is available.

=Section 1. Expansion of livestock available for bonus depreciation=



==(a) In general– ==

 Subparagraph (E) of section 168(k)(2) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:

<blockquote>

===(v) Dairy producing calves and cows– ===

In the case of any dairy producing calf or cow, subparagraph (A)shall be applied without regard to clause (ii) thereof..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2012, in taxable years ending after such date.
