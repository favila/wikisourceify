{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to allow qualified scholarship funding corporations to access tax-exempt financing for alternative private student loans.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Student Loan Opportunity Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Qualified scholarship funding corporation tax-exempt financing for alternative private student loans=



==(a) In general– ==

 Paragraph (2) of section 150(d) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking “established and” and “incurred under the Higher Education Act of 1965” in subparagraph (A)thereof.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to bonds issued after the date of enactment of this Act.
