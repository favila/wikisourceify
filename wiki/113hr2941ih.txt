{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to make certain grants to assist nursing homes for veterans located on tribal lands.

=Section 1. Grants for State homes located on tribal lands=



==(a) State home defined– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/101 Section 101(19) of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by inserting “or federally recognized Indian tribe” after “(other than a possession)” .

==(b) Payments to State homes– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1741 Section 1741of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>
:(g) In this subchapter, the term State means each of the several States and each federally recognized Indian tribe..
</blockquote>


==(c) State home construction– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8131 Section 8131(2)of title 38, United States Code] , is amended by inserting “includes each federally recognized Indian tribe but” before “does not” .

===(2) Conforming amendment– ===

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/8132 Section 8132 of such title] is amended by striking “several” .
