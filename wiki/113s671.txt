={{anchor|HE0D55AE4B125432BA12F2153DED28250}}Section 1. Naturalization for Deniss Nikanorov=

Notwithstanding title III of the Immigration and Nationality Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/8/1401 8 U.S.C. 1401 et seq.] ] ), or any other provision of law relating to naturalization, Deniss Nikanorov is deemed to be a naturalized citizen of the United States as of the date of the enactment of this Act.
