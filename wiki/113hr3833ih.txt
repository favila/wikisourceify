{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to modify the Medicare durable medical equipment face-to-face encounter documentation requirement.

=Section 1. Modifying Medicare durable medical equipment face-to-face encounter documentation requirement=

 Section 1834(a)(11)(B)(ii) of the Social Security Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395m 42 U.S.C. 1395m(a)(11)(B)(ii)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking “the physician documenting that” ; and
:(2) by striking “has had a face-to-face encounter” and inserting “documenting such physician, physician assistant, practitioner, or specialist has had a face-to-face encounter” .
