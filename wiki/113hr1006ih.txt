{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 39, United States Code, to lower the maximum rate of compensation for United States Postal Service employees, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Postal Executive Accountability Act”.

=Sec. 2. Limits on executive pay=



==(a) Limitation on compensation– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/1003 Section 1003 of title 39, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/1003 subsection (a)] , by striking the last sentence; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(e) 
:::(1) Subject to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/1003 paragraph (2)] , an officer or employee of the [[w:United States Postal Service|Postal Service]] may not be paid at a rate of basic pay that exceeds the rate of basic pay for level II of the Executive Schedule under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5312 section 5312 of title 5] .
:::(2) Not more than 6 officers or employees of the [[w:United States Postal Service|Postal Service]] that are in very senior executive positions, as determined by the Board of Governors, may be paid at a rate of basic pay that does not exceed the rate of basic pay for level I of the Executive Schedule under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5312 section 5312 of title 5] .
:::(3) For any fiscal year, an officer or employee of the [[w:United States Postal Service|Postal Service]] who is in a very senior executive position, as designated under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/1003 paragraph (2)] , may not receive fringe benefits (within the meaning given that term under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/1005 section 1005(f)] ) that are greater than the fringe benefits received by supervisory and other managerial personnel who are not subject to collective-bargaining agreements underchapter 12..
</blockquote>


==(b) Limitation on bonuses– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 Section 3686 of title 39, United States Code] , is amended as follows:
:(1) In [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 subsection (a)] , by adding at the end the following:“Notwithstanding the authority granted under this section and except as provided under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/1003 section 1003(e)] , no officer or employee of the [[w:United States Postal Service|Postal Service]] shall be paid compensation at a rate in excess of the rate for level II of the Executive Schedule under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5312 section 5312 of title 5] .”.
:(2)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 Subsections (b)] and [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 (c)] are repealed.
:(3) In [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 subsection (d)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 paragraph (1)] —
:::(i) by striking“which would not have been allowable but for the provisions of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 subsection (b)] or [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 (c)] ;”; and
:::(ii) by adding“and”at the end;
::(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 paragraph (2)] , by striking“; and”and inserting a period; and
::(C) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/3686 paragraph (3)] .
