{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

Raising a question of the privileges of the House.

= =

That the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] shall not consider H.R. 3, the “Northern Route Approval Act” because: (1) it violates rule XXI of the House, and (2) it affects the dignity and integrity of the proceedings of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House]] since it is unconstitutional.
