{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 to require oil polluters to pay the full cost of oil spills, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Big Oil Bailout Prevention Unlimited Liability Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Removal of limits on liability for offshore facilities=

 Section 1004(a)(3) of the Oil Pollution Act of 1990( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/33/2704 33 U.S.C. 2704(a)(3)] )is amended by striking “plus $75,000,000” and inserting “and the liability of the responsible party under section 1002” .
