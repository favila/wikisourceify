{{Center|A BILL}}

To require prompt responses from the heads of covered Federal agencies when the Secretary of Veterans Affairs requests information necessary to adjudicate claims for benefits under laws administered by the Secretary, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Accountability for Veterans Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Requirement for prompt responses from covered agencies when Secretary of Veterans Affairs requests information necessary to adjudicate benefits claims=



==(a) Initial requests for information– ==

Whenever the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] submits a request to the head of a covered agency for information that the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] determines is necessary to adjudicate a claim for a benefit under a law administered by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] , the head of the covered agency shall, not later than 30 days after receiving the request from the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] —
:(1) furnish the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] with the information requested; or
:(2) submit to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] —
::(A) an explanation as to why the head is unable to furnish the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] with the requested information within 30 days; and
::(B) an estimate as to when the head will furnish the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] with the requested information.

==(b) Continuous resubmittal of requests– ==

In each case in which the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] has submitted or resubmitted under this section a request to the head of a covered agency for information that the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] determines is necessary to adjudicate a claim for a benefit under a law administered by the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] , has received under this section in response to such request an estimate as to when the head will furnish the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] with the requested information, has not received such information on or before the date that is 15 days after the estimated date, and still requires such information to adjudicate the claim, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall resubmit such request and the head of the covered agency receiving such request shall, not later than 30 days after receiving the resubmitted request from the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] —
:(1) furnish the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] with the information requested; or
:(2) submit to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] —
::(A) an explanation as to why the head has been unable to furnish the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] with the requested information; and
::(B) an estimate as to when the head will furnish the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] with the requested information.

==(c) Notice to claimants– ==



===(1) In general– ===

Each time the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] receives an explanation and a time estimate under subsection (a)(2)or (b)(2)from the head of a covered agency regarding information the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] requested to adjudicate a claim of a claimant, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall, not later than 45 days after the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] submitted or resubmitted the request, notify the claimant—
:(A) that the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] requires information from the covered agency to adjudicate the claim;
:(B) in the case of receiving a time estimate under subsection (a)(2), that the covered agency was unable to furnish the information to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] within 30 days of the request for the information;
:(C) in the case of receiving a time estimate under subsection (b)(2), that the covered agency was unable to furnish the information to the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] before the previously estimated date; and
:(D) of the current estimate of the head of the covered agency of the date by which the head will furnish the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] with the information requested.

===(2) Manner– ===

Notice provided under paragraph (1)shall be made by mail or by electronic means.

==(d) Records– ==

The [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall keep records of the time it takes to receive—
:(1) responses from heads of covered agencies under this section; and
:(2) the information requested from the heads of covered agencies under this section.

==(e) Semiannual reports– ==

Not less frequently than once every six months, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall submit to the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans' Affairs of the Senate]] and the [[w:United States House Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans' Affairs of the House of Representatives]] a report that summarizes the records kept under subsection (d)regarding the most recent six-month period.

==(f) Covered agency defined– ==

In this section, the term covered agency includes the following:
:(1) The [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] .
:(2) The [[w:Social Security Administration|Social Security Administration]] .
:(3) The [[w:National Archives and Records Administration|National Archives and Records Administration]] .
