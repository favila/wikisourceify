{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit students who have been convicted of a criminal hazing offense under State law from receiving assistance under title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965.   

=Section 1. Loss of eligibility for hazing crime conviction=

   Section 484 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1091 20 U.S.C. 1091] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote> 

==(u) Loss of eligibility for hazing crime convictionâ€“ ==

No student who is convicted of a criminal hazing offense under State law shall be eligible to receive any grant, loan, or work assistance under this title. . 
</blockquote>
