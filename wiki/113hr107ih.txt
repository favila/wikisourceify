{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 5, United States Code, to limit the circumstances in which official time may be used by a Federal employee.

={{anchor|HB80D9A1AF4654A84A0E4CB8B2225584D}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Federal Employee Accountability Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H435E545461E04205A7123143D5717D9D}}Sec. 2. Repeal of certain provisions relating to official time=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 Section 7131 of title 5, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 subsections (a)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 (c)] ;
:(2) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 subsections (b)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 (d)] as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 subsections (a)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 (b)] , respectively; and
:(3) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 subsection (b)] (as so redesignated by paragraph (2)), by striking“Except as provided in the preceding subsections of this section—”and inserting“Except as provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/7131 subsection (a)] —”.
