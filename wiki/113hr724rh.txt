{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Clean Air Act to remove the requirement for dealer certification of new light-duty motor vehicles.    

=Section 1. Removal of requirement for dealer certification of new light-duty motor vehicles=

   Section 207(h) of the Clean Air Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/7541 42 U.S.C. 7541(h)] )is amended—
:(1) by striking paragraph (1) ; and
:(2) by redesignating paragraphs (2) and (3) as paragraphs (1) and (2) , respectively.
