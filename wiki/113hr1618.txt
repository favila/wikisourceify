{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to increase the dollar limitation on employer-provided group term life insurance that can be excluded from the gross income of the employee.

={{anchor|H629FA19B40564CDB9FA7625CAE974143}}Section 1. Increase in limitation on exclusion for employer-provided group term life insurance purchased for employees=



=={{anchor|H8FF4079B71774F7A8BDE404A7CF378CB}}(a) In general– ==

 Paragraph (1) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 79(a)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking“$50,000”and inserting“$375,000”.

=={{anchor|H3630D6829D414636A9F24204ADC7BE99}}(b) Inflation adjustment– ==

 Section 79 of such Codeis amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H3CCE27C7DB264046B9F21AD50281579A}}(g) Inflation adjustment– ===



===={{anchor|HCFA7EEC16E454C15AD5A3C57CFF71296}}(1) In general– ====

In the case of any taxable year beginning after 2013, the $375,000 amount under subsection (a)(1)shall be increased by an amount equal to—
:(A) such dollar amount, multiplied by
:(B) the cost-of-living adjustment determined under section 1(f)(3)for the calendar year in which the taxable year begins, determined by substituting“2012”for“1992”in subparagraph (B)thereof.

===={{anchor|H58A7DECD6AC443F1B47BBAC20E3BA8BA}}(2) Rounding– ====

If any amount as adjusted under paragraph (1)is not a multiple of $10,000, such amount shall be rounded to the nearest multiple of $10,000..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H63870AB63E86416FA881A4B6FA015B3F}}(c) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2012.
