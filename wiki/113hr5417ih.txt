{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit certain nutrition rules with respect to foods sold at schools as a fundraiser.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Bringing Awareness and Knowledge to Exempt Schools Against Legislative Encroachment Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Prohibition of certain rules=

The [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Secretary of Agriculture]]shall not implement, administer, or enforce the rule entitled“National School Lunch Program and School Breakfast Program: Nutrition Standards for All Foods Sold in School as Required by the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010  ”published by the [[w:United States Department of Agriculture|Department of Agriculture]]in the Federal Register on June 28, 2013 (78 Fed. Reg. 39068 et seq.) with respect to food sold as a fundraiser that takes place on the grounds of a school.
