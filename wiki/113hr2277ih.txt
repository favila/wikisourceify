{{Center|A BILL}}

 To eliminate the sporting purposes distinction in the gun laws.   

=Section 1. Elimination of sporting purposes distinction=



==(a) Amendments to the Internal Revenue Code– ==

   [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/5845 Section 5845(f)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended—
:(1) by striking“which the Secretary finds is generally recognized as particularly suitable for sporting purposes”; and
:(2) by striking“which the owner intends to use solely for sporting purposes”.

==(b) Amendments to title, united states code– ==


:(1)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921  Section 921(a)(4)(B)of title 18, United States Code], is amended by striking“which the Attorney General finds is generally recognized as particularly suitable for sporting purposes”.
:(2)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921 Section 921(a)(4) of such title]is amended in the 2nd sentence by striking“which the owner intends to use solely for sporting, recreational or cultural purposes”.
:(3)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921 Section 921(a)(17)(C) of such title]is amended by striking“a projectile which the Attorney General finds is primarily intended to be used for sporting purposes,”.
:(4)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 Section 922 of such title]is amended in each of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 subsections (a)(5)],[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 (a)(9)], and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 (b)(3)]by striking“sporting”.
:(5)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 Section 922(r) of such title]is amended by striking“of this chapter as not being particularly suitable for or readily adaptable to sporting purposes”.
:(6)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 Section 923(j) of such title]is amended by striking“devoted to the collection, competitive use, or other sporting use of firearms in the community”.
:(7)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/925 Section 925(a)(3) of such title]is amended by striking“determined by the Secretary of the Treasury to be generally recognized as particularly suitable for sporting purposes and”.
:(8)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/925 Section 925(a)(4) of such title]is amended by striking“(A) determined by the Attorney General to be generally recognized as particularly suitable for sporting purposes, or determined by the Department of Defense to be a type of firearm normally classified as a war souvenir, and (B)”.
:(9)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/925 Section 925(d)(3) of such title]is amended by striking“and is generally recognized as particularly suitable for or readily adaptable to sporting purposes”.
:(10)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/925 Section 925(e)(2) of such title]is amended by striking“, provided that such handguns are generally recognized as particularly suitable for or readily adaptable to sporting purposes”.
