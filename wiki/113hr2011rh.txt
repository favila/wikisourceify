{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide for a two-year extension of the Veterans’ Advisory Committee on Education.    

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Veterans' Advisory Committee on Education Improvement Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Two-year extension of Veterans’ Advisory Committee on Education=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3692  Section 3692of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3692 subsection (a)]—
::(A) by inserting“ 31, ”after“ 30, ”; and
::(B) by striking“ and the Persian Gulf War ”and inserting“ the Persian Gulf War, and the post-9/11 operations in Iraq and Afghanistan ”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/3692 subsection (c)], by striking“December 31, 2013”and inserting“December 31, 2015”.
