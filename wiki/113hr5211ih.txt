{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend section 811 of [[Public Law 111-84|Public Law 111–84]]  to apply that section to all contractors for all sole-source contracts exceeding $20,000,000.   

=Section 1. Amendments to justification and approval requirements related to certain sole-source contracts=



==(a) Expansion of sole-Source contracts covered– ==

   Paragraph (1) of section 811(c) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2010 ( [[Public Law 111-84|Public Law 111–84]]  ;123 Stat. 2405;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/3304  41 U.S.C. 3304note])is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 

===(1) Covered procurement– ===

The term“covered procurement”means either of the following:
:(A) A procurement covered by chapter 137of title 10, United States Code.
:(B) A procurement covered by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/ division C of subtitle I of title 41, United States Code.]  . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Treatment of other justification and approval actions– ==

  Section 811 of such Act is further amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote> 

===(d) Treatment of other justification and approval actions– ===

In the case of any contract for which a justification and approval is required under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/2304 section 2304(f) of title 10, United States Code], or[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/3304  section 3304(e)of title 41, United States Code], a justification and approval meeting the requirements of such section shall be treated as meeting the requirements of this section for purposes of the award of a sole-source contract. . 
</blockquote>


==(c) Technical and conforming amendments– ==

  Section 811 of such Act is further amended—
:(1) in subsection (a) , by striking“Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Federal Acquisition Regulation shall be revised to provide that the”and inserting“The”;
:(2) in subsection (a)(3) , by striking“sections 303(f)(1)(C) and 303(j) of the Federal Property and Administrative Services Act of 1949 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/253 41 U.S.C. 253(f)(1)(C)]and 253(j))”and inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/3304 sections 3304(e)(1)(C)]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/3304 3304(f) of title 41, United States Code] ”;
:(3) in subsection (c) —
::(A) in paragraph (2)(B) , by striking“section 309(a)”and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/151 section 151 of title 41], United States Code.”; and
::(B) in paragraph (3)(B) , by striking“section 303(f)(1)(B)”and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/41/3304 section 3304(e)(1)(B) of title 41], United States Code.”; and
:(4) by adding at the end the following new subsection:

<blockquote> 

====(e) Regulations– ====

The Federal Acquisition Regulation shall be revised to implement this section. . 
</blockquote>
