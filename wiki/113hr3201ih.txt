{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the National Coal Heritage Area Act of 1996 to reauthorize the Augusta Canal National Heritage Area.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Augusta Canal National Heritage Area Reauthorization Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Reauthorization of Augusta Canal National Heritage Area=

 Section 310 of the National Coal Heritage Area Act of 1996( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/16/461 16 U.S.C. 461note] ;[[Public Law 104-333|Division II of[[Public Law -|Public Law 104–333]] ]] )is amended by striking “September 30, 2013” and inserting “September 30, 2023” .
