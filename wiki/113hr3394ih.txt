{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make the work opportunity tax credit permanent.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ American Job Opportunity Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Work opportunity tax credit made permanent=



==(a) In general– ==

   [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/51 Section 51(c)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking paragraph (4) .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to wages paid after December 31, 2013.
