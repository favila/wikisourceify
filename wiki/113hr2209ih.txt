To establish a chain of command for Army National Military Cemeteries.

={{anchor|HC28DAFEBF8EA45B8AFE0A489B1E76B29}}Section 1. Establishment of chain of command for Army National Military Cemeteries=



=={{anchor|H4CF4BCD1F493467CB0E57EBE88B3A574}}(a) Military chain of command required– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Army|Secretary of the Army]] shall establish a chain of command for the Army National Military Cemeteries, to include a military commander of the Army National Military Cemeteries to replace the current civilian director upon the termination of the tenure of the director.

=={{anchor|H9C0B9E33855A49C2BCFC5614F4185513}}(b) Conforming amendment– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/4724 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// Section 4724(a)(1)] of title 10, United States Code] , is amended by striking“who shall meet”and inserting“who is a commissioned officer and meets”.
