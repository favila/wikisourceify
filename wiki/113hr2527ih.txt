{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide veterans with counseling and treatment for sexual trauma that occurred during inactive duty training.

=Section 1. Counseling and treatment for sexual trauma occurring during inactive duty for training=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720D Section 1720Dof title 38, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720D subsection (a)(1)] , by striking “active duty or active duty for training” and inserting “active duty, active duty for training, or inactive duty training” ; and
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720D subsection (f)] —
::(A) by striking “this section, the” and inserting the following:

<blockquote>this section:
:::(1) The; and
</blockquote>

::(B) by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
:::(2) The term “veteran” , with respect to inactive duty training described in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/1720D subsection (a)(1)] , also includes an individual who—
::::(A) is not otherwise eligible for the benefits of this chapter; and
::::(B) while serving in the reserve components of the Armed Forces, performed such inactive duty training but did not serve on active duty..
</blockquote>
