{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit United States assistance to the East-West Center.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on United States assistance to the East-West Center=

No funds available to the [[w:United States Department of State|Department of State]]or any other department or agency may be used to provide assistance to the Center for Cultural and Technical Interchange Between East and West (commonly known as the East-West Center).
