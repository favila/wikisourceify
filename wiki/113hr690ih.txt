{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to modify the per-fiscal year calculation of days of certain active duty or active service used to reduce the minimum age at which a member of a reserve component of the uniformed services may retire for non-regular service.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Reserve Retirement Deployment Credit Correction Act” .

=Sec. 2. Modification of per-fiscal year calculation of days of certain active duty or active service to reduce eligibility age for retirement for non-regular service=



==(a) Accumulation of 90-Day periods of service within any two consecutive fiscal years– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/12731 Section 12731(f)(2)(A) of title 10, United States Code] , is amended by striking “in any fiscal year” and inserting “in any two consecutive fiscal years” .

==(b) Retroactive effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall take effect as of January 28, 2008, and as if included in the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2008([[Public Law 110-181|Public Law 110–181]] )as enacted.
