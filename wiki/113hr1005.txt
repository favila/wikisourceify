{{Center|A BILL}}

To deauthorize appropriation of funds, and
		  to rescind unobligated appropriations, to carry out the Patient Protection and
		  Affordable Care Act and the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of
		  2010.


{{section|H2E5D2F9766A849919B624E43E278A132|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Defund Obamacare
			 Act

        
.

{{section|idD0BFF6CCD83742A192F3C232F62FB909|2. Deauthorization of appropriations to carry out PPACA and HCERA}}
Notwithstanding any other provision of law,
			 no funds are authorized to be appropriated to carry out the provisions of the
			 Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act
 (Public Law 111–148
)
, the Health
			 Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010
 (Public Law 111–152
)
, and any
			 amendment made by either such Act.

{{section|idB5D984E9541846F5B7F7A67BBB9E65CD|3. Rescission of unobligated appropriations under PPACA and HCERA}}
Of the funds made available under any
			 provision of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act
 (Public Law
			 111–148
)
, the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010
 (Public Law
			 111–152
)
, and any amendment made by either such Act, the unobligated balance is
			 rescinded.
