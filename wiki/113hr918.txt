{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the issuance of a semipostal to benefit
		  the Peace Corps.


{{section|HD116442CDE6B4618BD927C105CF716FA|1. Short title}}
This Act may be cited as the
			 
          Peace Stamp for the Peace Corps
			 Act

        
.

{{section|H3E24EB950F8240B4B254070E88EF705A|2. Peace Corps stamp}}
(a)
In
			 general
The [[United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] shall, for a 2-year
			 period beginning no later than 12 months after the date of the enactment of
			 this Act, issue a semipostal to benefit the [[Peace Corps|Peace Corps]].

(b)
Requirements and
			 authorities
The issuance of such semipostal and the disposition
			 of the amounts becoming available from the issuance of such semipostal shall be
			 governed by section 416 of title 39, United States Code
, subject to the
			 following:
(1)
The differential
			 included in the rate of postage established for such semipostal under section
			 416(c)
 of such title may not exceed 25 percent.

(2)
The [[Peace Corps|Peace Corps]]
			 shall be the sole agency to which any amounts becoming available from the sale
			 of such semipostal may be transferred under section 416(d)(1)
 of such
			 title.


(c)
Semipostal
			 defined
For purposes of this section, the term
			 semipostal
 has the meaning given such term by section 416(a)(1)
 of
			 such title.
