{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the issuance of a semipostal to benefit the Peace Corps.

={{anchor|HD116442CDE6B4618BD927C105CF716FA}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Peace Stamp for the Peace Corps Act”.

={{anchor|H3E24EB950F8240B4B254070E88EF705A}}Sec. 2. Peace Corps stamp=



=={{anchor|H1339CB7A3F5B405FB26639E4E6051C98}}(a) In general– ==

The [[w:United States Postal Service|United States Postal Service]] shall, for a 2-year period beginning no later than 12 months after the date of the enactment of this Act, issue a semipostal to benefit the [[w:Peace Corps|Peace Corps]] .

=={{anchor|H13DC8E7787AB49D994AE54DECC627BC0}}(b) Requirements and authorities– ==

The issuance of such semipostal and the disposition of the amounts becoming available from the issuance of such semipostal shall be governed by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/416 section 416 of title 39, United States Code] , subject to the following:
:(1) The differential included in the rate of postage established for such semipostal under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/416 section 416(c)] of such title may not exceed 25 percent.
:(2) The [[w:Peace Corps|Peace Corps]] shall be the sole agency to which any amounts becoming available from the sale of such semipostal may be transferred under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/416 section 416(d)(1)] of such title.

=={{anchor|H5E573CD6C24246668E9DB5F6DD1E00E0}}(c) Semipostal defined– ==

For purposes of this section, the term semipostal has the meaning given such term by[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/39/416 section 416(a)(1)] of such title.
