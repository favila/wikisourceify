{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to extend the reduced interest rate for Federal Direct Stafford Loans.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Student Loan Affordability Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Interest rate extension=

 Section 455(b)(7)(D) of the Higher Education Act of 1965( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e(b)(7)(D)] )is amended—
:(1) in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking “2013” and inserting “2015” ; and
:(2) in clause (v), by striking “2013” and inserting “2015” .
