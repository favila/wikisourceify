{{Center|A BILL}}

To authorize States to enforce pipeline safety requirements related to wellbores at interstate storage facilities.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Underground Gas Storage Facility Safety Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Definitions=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/60101 Section 60101(a)of title 49, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/60101 paragraphs (20), (21), (22), (23), (24), and (25)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/60101 paragraphs (23), (24), (25), (20), (21), and (22)] , respectively;
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/60101 paragraph (21)] , as redesignated, by striking “and” at the end;
:(3) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/60101 paragraph (22)] , as redesignated, by striking the period at the end and inserting a semicolon; and
:(4) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(26)  “underground gas storage wellbore” means the piping and hole drilled connecting the surface of the earth with the underground gas storage reservoir; and
::(27)  “underground hazardous liquid storage wellbore” means the piping and hole drilled connecting the surface of the earth with the underground hazardous liquid storage reservoir..
</blockquote>


=Sec. 3. State enforcement of pipeline safety requirements=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/60104 Section 60104(c)of title 49, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking “A State authority that” and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
::(1) A State authority that;
</blockquote>

:(2) by striking “A State authority may not” and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
::(2) A State authority may not;
</blockquote>

:(3) by striking “Notwithstanding the preceding sentence, a State authority may enforce a requirement” and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
::(3) Notwithstanding the limitation set forth in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/60104 paragraph (2)] , a State authority may—
:::(A) enforce a requirement;
</blockquote>

:(4) by striking “chapter 61.” and inserting “chapter 61; and” ; and
:(5) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(B) enforce a State requirement for the safe construction and operation of underground gas storage wellbores and underground hazardous liquid storage wellbores if—
:::(i) the State requirement has been approved by the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Federal Energy Regulatory Commission]] (referred to in this subparagraph as the “Commission” ); or
:::(ii) the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Commission]] fails to make a decision with respect to the State requirement during the 30-day period beginning on the date on which the State submits the State requirement to the [[w:Federal Energy Regulatory Commission|Commission]] ..
</blockquote>
