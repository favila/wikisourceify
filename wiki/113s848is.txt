{{Center|A BILL}}

 To promote transparency by permitting the Public Company Accounting Oversight Board to allow its disciplinary proceedings to be open to the public, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ PCAOB Enforcement Transparency Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Open meetings authorized=

   Section 105(c)(2) of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/7215 15 U.S.C. 7215(c)(2)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote> 

==(2) Public hearings– ==

Hearings under this section shall be open to the public, unless the Board, on its own motion or after considering the motion of a party, orders otherwise. . 
</blockquote>
 

=Sec. 3. Publication of determinations=

   Section 105(d)(1)(C) of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/7215 15 U.S.C. 7215(d)(1)(C)] )is amended by striking“(once any stay on the imposition of such sanction has been lifted)”.
