{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 31, United States Code, to provide for lessened penalties for certain violations of the anti-structuring laws when violations are with respect to certain domestic financial institutions and are not taken in connection with another crime, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Small Business Deposit Relief Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Limitation on penalties for certain structuring not involving another crime=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 Section 5324of title 31, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 subsection (d)] —
::(A) by redesignating [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 paragraph (2)] as [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 paragraph (3)] ; and
::(B) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 paragraph (1)] the following:

<blockquote>

====(2) Limitation on criminal penalties for certain non-aggravated cases– ====



=====(A) In general– =====

Notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 paragraph (1)] , but subject to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 paragraph (3)] , whoever violates this section shall be fined in accordance with [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/ title 18, United States Code] , imprisoned for not more than 1 year, or both, where such violation—
:(i) was of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 section 5324(a)] ;
:(ii) was not made while also violating another law of the United States; and
:(iii) was made with respect to a transaction involving a domestic financial institution described under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5312 subparagraphs (A) through (F) of section 5312(a)(2)] .

=====(B) No imprisonment for first offense– =====

Notwithstanding [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 subparagraph (A)] , a person committing a violation described under that subparagraph shall not be subject to imprisonment for such violation if the person has not previously been convicted of a violation described under such subparagraph.; and
</blockquote>

:(2) by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>

===(e) Limitation on property forfeiture and monetary penalties for certain structuring not involving another crime– ===

With respect to a violation, the aggregate amount of property forfeited under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5317 section 5317(c)(1)] , property seized and forfeited under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5317 section 5317(c)(2)] , any money penalty imposed under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5321 section 5321(a)(4)(A)] , and any fine imposed under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 subsection (d)] may not exceed 10 percent of the amount of coins and currency (or such other monetary instruments as the Secretary may prescribe) involved in the transaction with respect to which the violation occurred, if the violation—
:(1) was of [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5324 section 5324(a)] ;
:(2) was not made while also violating another law of the United States; and
:(3) was made with respect to a transaction involving a domestic financial institution described under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/5312 subparagraphs (A) through (F) of section 5312(a)(2)] ..
</blockquote>
