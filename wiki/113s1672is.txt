{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to empower individuals with disabilities to establish their own supplemental needs trusts.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Special Needs Trust Fairness Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Fairness in Medicaid supplemental needs trusts=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 1917(d)(4)(A) of the Social Security Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text//  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1396p 42 U.S.C. 1396p(d)(4)(A)] ] )is amended by inserting “the individual,” after “for the benefit of such individual by” .

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to trusts established on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
