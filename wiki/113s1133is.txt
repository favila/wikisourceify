{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permanently extend the new markets tax credit, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H6062B9647E0D4829A115EC3FDD85AE0F}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“New Markets Tax Credit Extension Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H912189D724564730912747BDBF792962}}Sec. 2. Permanent extension of new markets tax credit=



=={{anchor|H5D2FFA0D83E542758292E48A51D6A20F}}(a) Extension– ==



==={{anchor|H888FB372A77944069901DBF8E8174654}}(1) In general– ===

 Subparagraph (G) of section 45D(f)(1) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by striking“, 2011, 2012, and 2013”and inserting“and each calendar year thereafter”.

==={{anchor|H93856DF424444C888D79D007342AAAFB}}(2) Conforming amendment– ===

 Section 45D(f)(3) of such Codeis amended by striking the last sentence.

=={{anchor|H189D019BA2844249A4C41BDB5F0CDCBD}}(b) Inflation adjustment– ==

 Subsection (f) of section 45D of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H983539EC17E24A60812B7AA74505F9AB}}(4) Inflation adjustment– ===



===={{anchor|HF95F82BB57804FE39CB4D6EC15AAC7C5}}(A) In general– ====

In the case of any calendar year beginning after 2013, the dollar amount in paragraph (1)(G)shall be increased by an amount equal to—
:(i) such dollar amount, multiplied by
:(ii) the cost-of-living adjustment determined under section 1(f)(3)for the calendar year, determined by substituting“calendar year 2000”for“calendar year 1992”in subparagraph (B)thereof.

===={{anchor|H93C2E1721415481FA8DC075D0C0C977B}}(B) Rounding rule– ====

Any increase under subparagraph (A)which is not a multiple of $1,000,000 shall be rounded to the nearest multiple of $1,000,000..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HA5D8B066F70248138D5E87C86A65A802}}(c) Alternative minimum tax relief– ==

 Subparagraph (B) of section 38(c)(4) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986is amended—
:(1) by redesignating clauses (v) through (ix)as clauses (vi) through (x), respectively, and
:(2) by inserting after clause (iv)the following new clause:

<blockquote>
::(v) the credit determined under section 45D, but only with respect to credits determined with respect to qualified equity investments (as defined in section 45D(b)) initially made before January 1, 2014,.
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H16B779B67CBB43CAAA1B5627542101DF}}(d) Effective dates– ==



==={{anchor|H10E86A4BCCD34B11AB8A3543B2005369}}(1) In general– ===

Except as provided in paragraph (2), the amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.

==={{anchor|HF11C0A7DEC234FE3A909F84B4906DB15}}(2) Alternative minimum tax relief– ===

The amendments made by subsection (c)shall apply to credits determined with respect to qualified equity investments (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 45D(b)] of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986) initially made after the date of the enactment of this Act.
