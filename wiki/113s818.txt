{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide the Secretary of Transportation with the flexibility to transfer certain funds to prevent furloughs by the Federal Aviation Administration, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Reducing Flight Delays Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|id7641eb662d61464aafd39b08bbdcf9f8}}Sec. 2. Authorization to transfer certain funds to prevent furloughs by the Federal Aviation Administration=



=={{anchor|id12259d1f46584b0e8b3f7e967df45edb}}(a) In general– ==

Notwithstanding division G of the Consolidated and Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2013([[Public Law -|Public Law 113–6]] ), any other provision of law, or a sequestration order issued or to be issued by the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] pursuant to section 251A(7)(A) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/2/901a 2 U.S.C. 901a(7)(A)] ] ), the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] may transfer during fiscal year 2013 an amount equal to the amount specified in subsection (c)to the appropriations account providing for the operations of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] , for any activity or activities funded by that account, from—
:(1) the amount made available for obligation in that fiscal year as discretionary grants-in-aid for airports pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/47117 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// section 47117(f)] of title 49, United States Code] ; or
:(2) any other program or account of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] .

=={{anchor|id8314AC8729E046E0B0EEA584A0E883EA}}(b) Availability and obligation of transferred amounts– ==

An amount transferred under subsection (a)(1)shall—
:(1) be available immediately for obligation and expenditure as directly appropriated budget authority; and
:(2) be deemed as obligated for grants-in-aid for airports under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/ part B of subtitle VII of title 49, United States Code] , for purposes of complying with the limitation on incurring obligations during that fiscal year under the heading“Grants-in-Aid for Airports”under title I of the Transportation, Housing and Urban Development, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2012([[Public Law 112-55|division C of[[Public Law -|Public Law 112–55]] ]] ;125 Stat. 647), and made applicable to fiscal year 2013 by division F of the Consolidated and Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2013([[Public Law -|Public Law 113–6]] ).

=={{anchor|id12290d8be5bb45898c202862fd2a9570}}(c) Amount specified– ==

The amount specified in this subsection is the amount, not to exceed $253,000,000, that the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary of Transportation]] determines to be necessary—
:(1) to prevent during fiscal year 2013 furloughs of employees of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] whom the [[w:United States Department of Transportation|Secretary]] determines are necessary for ensuring a safe and efficient air transportation system; and
:(2) to continue during that fiscal year the operations of air traffic control towers that were operational as of January 1, 2013, under the contract tower program of the [[w:Federal Aviation Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]] .
