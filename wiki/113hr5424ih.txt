{{Center|A BILL}}

 To create the Made-in-America Bank.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Made-in-America Bank Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Redesignation of the Export-Import Bank as the Made-in-America Bank=



==(a) Redesignation– ==

   Paragraph (1) of subsection (a) of section 2 of the Export-Import Bank Act of 1945 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/635 12 U.S.C. 635(a)(1)])is amended by striking“  [[w:Export-Import Bank of the United States|Export-Import Bank]] ”and inserting“  [[w:Export-Import Bank of the United States|Made-in-America Bank]] ”.

==(b) References– ==

Any reference in any law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal agency renamed in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the“  [[w:Export-Import Bank of the United States|Made-in-America Bank]] ”. 

=Sec. 3. Financing made available to American entities purchasing American made goods=

All guarantees, loans, insurance, and any other benefits historically provided by the [[w:Export-Import Bank of the United States|Export-Import Bank]]may only be made to a domestic entity that is not majority-owned by any foreign person or entity and only for the purpose of acquiring goods or services made in America. 

=Sec. 4. Extension of authority=

   Section 7 of the Export-Import Bank Act of 1945 ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/635f 12 U.S.C. 635f] )is amended by striking“2014”and inserting“2016”. 

=Sec. 5. Effective date=

The amendments made by this Act shall take effect on October 1, 2014, or the date of enactment of this Act, whichever is later.
