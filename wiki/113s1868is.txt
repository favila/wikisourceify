{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the conversion of temporary judgeships for the districts of Hawaii and Kansas to permanent judgeships.

=Section 1. Conversion of temporary judgeships to permanent judgeships for the districts of Hawaii and Kansas=



==(a) In general– ==

The existing judgeship for the district of Hawaii and the existing judgeship for the district of Kansas authorized by section 203(c) of the Judicial Improvements Act of 1990( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/133 28 U.S.C. 133 note] ;[[Public Law -|[[Public Law 101-650|Public Law 101–650]] ]] ;104 Stat. 5089)shall, as of the date of enactment of this Act, be authorized under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/133 section 133of title 28, United States Code] , and the incumbent in each such office shall hold the office under [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/133 section 133of title 28, United States Code] , as amended by this Act.

==(b) Tables– ==

In order that the table contained in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/133 section 133(a)of title 28, United States Code] , will reflect the change in the total number of permanent district judgeships authorized as a result of subsection (a)of this section—
:(1) the item relating to Hawaii is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>Hawaii4;
</blockquote>
and
:(2) the item relating to Kansas is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>Kansas6.
</blockquote>
