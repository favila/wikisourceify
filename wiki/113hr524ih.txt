{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to clarify that the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency does not have the authority to disapprove a permit after it has been issued by the Secretary of the Army under section 404 of such Act.

=Section 1. Permits for dredged or fill material=

 Section 404(c) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/33/1344 33 U.S.C. 1344(c)] )is amended by striking “The [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]] is authorized” and inserting “Until such time as the [[w:21st Theater Army Area Command|Secretary]] has issued a permit under this section, the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]] is authorized” .
