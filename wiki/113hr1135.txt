{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer
		  Protection Act to repeal certain additional disclosure requirements, and for
		  other purposes.

={{anchor|H4F3A5E2AD5AC46DEADE412037AA7CBB4}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Burdensome Data Collection Relief
			 Act”.

={{anchor|H0569709337B94897BE13B6B8BF10B2DA}}Sec. 2. Repeal of
			 additional disclosure requirements=

Section 953(b) of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street
			 Reform and Consumer Protection Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 15 U.S.C. 781] note) is hereby repealed and
			 any regulations issued pursuant to such subsection shall have no force or
			 effect.
