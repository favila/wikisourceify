{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 Requesting that the President of the United States transmit to the House of Representatives copies of any emails in the possession of the Executive Office of the President that were transmitted to or from the email account(s) of former Internal Revenue Service Exempt Organizations Division Director Lois Lerner between January 2009 and April 2011.   

= =

That the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]]request that the [[w:President of the United States|President of the United States]]transmit to the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]], no later than 14 days after the date of the adoption of this resolution, copies of any electronic communication in the possession of the President or the [[w:Executive Office of the President|Executive Office of the President]]that was transmitted to or from any electronic mail account(s) used by former [[w:Internal Revenue Service|Internal Revenue Service]]Exempt Organizations Division Director Lois Lerner at any time between January 1, 2009, and April 30, 2011.
