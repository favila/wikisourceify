{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit the National Endowment for the Humanities to provide funds to carry out the Popular Romance Project or any similar project relating to love or romance.   

=Section 1. Prohibition=

The [[w:National Endowment for the Humanities|National Endowment for the Humanities]]may not provide funds to carry out the Popular Romance Project or any similar project relating to love or romance.
