{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 10, United States Code, to improve access to mental health services under the TRICARE program.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Caring for America's Heroes Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Improvement of access to mental health services under the TRICARE program=



==(a) Elimination of limit on days of inpatient services– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079  Section 1079of title 10, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 subsection (a)]—
::(A) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 paragraph (6)]; and
::(B) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 paragraphs (7) through (17)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 paragraphs (6) through (16)], respectively;
:(2) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 subsection (i)]; and
:(3) by redesignating[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 subsections (j) through (q)]as[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 subsections (i) through (p)], respectively.

==(b) Repeal of requirement for nonavailability statement or preauthorization– ==

   Section 721(a) of the Floyd D. Spence National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2001 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1073 10 U.S.C. 1073 note])is amended by striking“(other than mental health services)”.

==(c) Conforming amendments– ==

  Chapter 55of title 10, United States Code, is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 section 1079(e)(7)], by striking“subsection (a)(13)”and inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 subsection (a)(12)] ”;
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1086 section 1086]—
::(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1086 subsection (d)(4)(A)(ii)], by striking“section 1079(j)(1)”and inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 section 1079(i)(1)] ”; and
::(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1086 subsection (g)], by striking“Section 1079(j)”and inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 Section 1079(i)] ”; and
:(3) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1105 section 1105(c)], by striking“section 1079(a)(7)”and inserting“ [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1079 section 1079(a)(6)] ”.
