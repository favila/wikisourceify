{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act to set the rate of pay for employees of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection in accordance with the General Schedule.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “CFPB Pay Fairness Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Rate of pay for employees of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection=



==(a) In general– ==

 Section 1013(a)(2) of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/12/5493 12 U.S.C. 5493(a)(2)] )is amended to read as follows:

<blockquote>

===(2) Compensation– ===

The rates of basic pay for all employees of the [[w:|Bureau]] shall be set and adjusted by the Director in accordance with the General Schedule set forth in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5332 section 5332of title 5, United States Code] ..
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to service by an employee of the [[w:|Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection]] following the 90-day period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act.
