{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend for one year the 15-year straight-line cost recovery for qualified leasehold improvements, qualified restaurant buildings and improvements, and qualified retail improvements.   

=Section 1. Extension of 15-year straight-line cost recovery for qualified leasehold improvements, qualified restaurant buildings and improvements, and qualified retail improvements=



==(a) In general– ==

  Clauses (iv) , (v) , and (ix) of[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/168 section 168(e)(3)(E)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 are each amended by striking“January 1, 2014”and inserting“January 1, 2015”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2013.
