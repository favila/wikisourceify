{{Center|RESOLUTION}}

 Authorizing the Speaker to appoint a committee to notify the President of the assembly of the Congress.    That a committee of two Members be appointed by the [[w:United States House of Representatives|Speaker]]on the part of the [[w:United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]]to notify the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President of the United States]]that a quorum of the House has assembled and that the House is ready to receive any communication that he may be pleased to make.     Karen L. Haas, Clerk.
