{{Center|A BILL}}

To protect victims of stalking from gun violence.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Protecting Domestic Violence and Stalking Victims Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Addition of dating partners and individuals subject to restraining orders=



==(a) Definition– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921 Section 921(a) of title 18, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) by striking [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921 paragraph (32)] and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
::(32) The term intimate partner —
:::(A) means with respect to a person, the spouse of the person, a former spouse of the person, an individual who is a parent of a child of the person, and an individual who cohabitates or has cohabited with the person; and
:::(B) includes—
::::(i) a dating partner (as defined in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/2266 section 2266] ); and
::::(ii) any other person similarly situated to a spouse who is protected by the domestic or family violence laws of the State or tribal jurisdiction in which the injury occurred or where the victim resides.; and
</blockquote>

:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921 paragraph (33)(A)(ii)] —
::(A) by inserting “intimate partner,” after “former spouse,” ; and
::(B) by inserting “intimate partner,” after “a spouse,” each place it appears.

==(b) Addition of stalking– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 Section 922 of title 18, United States Code] , is amended—
:(1) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 subsection (d)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 paragraph (8)(ii)] , by striking “or” at the end;
::(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 paragraph (9)] , by striking the period at the end and inserting “; or” ; and
::(C) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 paragraph (9)] the following:

<blockquote>
:::(10) has been convicted in any court of a misdemeanor crime of stalking.; and
</blockquote>

:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 subsection (g)] —
::(A) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 paragraph (8)(C)(ii)] , by striking “or” at the end;
::(B) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 paragraph (9)] , by striking the comma at the end and inserting “; or” ; and
::(C) by inserting after [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 paragraph (9)] the following:

<blockquote>
:::(10) has been convicted in any court of a misdemeanor crime of stalking,.
</blockquote>
