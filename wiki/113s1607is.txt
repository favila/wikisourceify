{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide conformity in Native small business opportunities and promote job creation, manufacturing, and American economic recovery.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Native Small Business Conformity Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Small business conformity=



==(a) HUBZone eligibility– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 Section 3(p)(3) of the Small Business Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/632 15 U.S.C. 632(p)(3)] )is amended—
:(A) by redesignating subparagraphs (D)and (E)as subparagraphs (E)and (F), respectively; and
:(B) by inserting after subparagraph (C)the following:

<blockquote>
::(D) a small business concern that is owned and controlled by an organization described in section 8(a)(15);.
</blockquote>


===(2) Technical and conforming amendment– ===

 Section 3(p)(5)(A)(i)(I)(aa) of the Small Business Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/632 15 U.S.C. 632(p)(5)(A)(i)(I)(aa)] )is amended by striking “subparagraph (A), (B), (C), (D), or (E) of paragraph (3)” and inserting “ subparagraph (A), (B), (C), (D), (E)or (F) of paragraph (3)” .

==(b) 8program– ==



===(1) In general– ===

 Section 8(a)(6) of the Small Business Act( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/637 15 U.S.C. 637(a)(6)] )is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(F) If an organization described in paragraph (15)establishes that it is economically disadvantaged under this paragraph in connection with an application for 1 small business concern owned or controlled by the organization, the organization shall not be required to reestablish that it is economically disadvantaged in order to have other businesses that it owns or controls certified for participation in the program under this subsection, unless specifically requested to do so by the [[w:Small Business Administration|Administration]] ..
</blockquote>


===(2) Applicability– ===

The amendment made by this subsection shall take effect on the date of enactment of this Act and apply to determinations of economic disadvantage made before, on, or after the date of enactment of this Act.
