{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to cover screening computed tomography colonography as a colorectal cancer screening test under the Medicare program.

={{anchor|H5CE26B0C064A41CC8FAD93563C509E55}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“CT Colonography Screening for Colorectal Cancer Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H92F4300B1B1E47FEA1E00918C1B88527}}Sec. 2. Coverage of computed tomography colonography screening as a colorectal cancer screening test under Medicare=



=={{anchor|H367F4BDD83F54E378DCEF7EC53ED46EB}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 1861(pp)(1) of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395x 42 U.S.C. 1395x(pp)(1)] )is amended—
:(1) by redesignating subparagraph (D)as subparagraph (E); and
:(2) by inserting after subparagraph (C)the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote>
::(D) Screening computed tomography colonography..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|H7FAE5A2BE0784BE49D5D1C5CBD29AB5D}}(b) Frequency limits and payment– ==

 Section 1834(d) of such Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395m 42 U.S.C. 1395m(d)] )is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|H476D7D91DEB94D00AAA52BFB7B4EA8D5}}(4) Screening computed tomography colonography– ===



===={{anchor|HABF43C85CE1C4AA3A4A48625510B370C}}(A) Fee schedule– ====

With respect to a colorectal cancer screening test consisting of screening computed tomography colonography, subject to subparagraph (B), payment under section 1848shall be consistent with payment under such section for similar or related services.

===={{anchor|H1DDEB0104189405BA6DCB87FA58BF8C7}}(B) Payment limit– ====

In the case of screening computed tomography colonography, payment under this part shall not exceed such amount as the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] specifies, based upon rates recognized for diagnostic computed tomography colonography.

===={{anchor|H33B2A6522C1E4DBDB2EC2CAA0EAFFE8E}}(C) Facility payment limit– ====

Notwithstanding any other provision of this title, in the case of an individual who receives screening computed tomography colonography—
:(i) in computing the amount of any applicable coinsurance, the computation of such coinsurance shall be based upon the fee schedule under which payment is made for the services; and
:(ii) the amount of such coinsurance shall not exceed 25 percent of the payment amount under the fee schedule described in subparagraph (A).

===={{anchor|HDD4B8EA1C2DD4A7EB0DBC3BA3B030154}}(D) Frequency limit– ====

No payment may be made under this part for a colorectal cancer screening test consisting of a screening computed tomography colonography—
:(i) if the individual is under 50 years of age; or
:(ii) 
::(I) in the case of individuals at high risk for colorectal cancer, if the procedure is performed within the 23 months after a previous screening computed tomography colonography or a previous screening colonoscopy; or
::(II) in the case of an individual who is not at high risk for colorectal cancer, if the procedure is performed within the 119 months after a previous screening co­lo­nos­co­py or within the 59 months after a previous screening flexible sigmoidoscopy or a previous screening computed tomography colonography..
</blockquote>


=={{anchor|HE1840A1C655C48B089D8ABA1C52FFA10}}(c) Conforming frequency limits for other colorectal cancer screening tests– ==



==={{anchor|H21A18D67B07D456196EACF34D19A94EB}}(1) Screening flexible sigmoidoscopy– ===

 Paragraph (2)(E)(ii) of section 1834(d) of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395m 42 U.S.C. 1395m(d)] )is amended by inserting“or screening computed tomography colonography”after“previous screening flexible sigmoidoscopy”.

==={{anchor|HC12F7A7723354E27901E4B67F8E84477}}(2) Screening colonoscopy– ===

 Paragraph (3)(E) of such sectionis amended—
:(A) by inserting“or screening computed tomography colonography”after“23 months after a previous screening colonoscopy”; and
:(B) by inserting“or screening computed tomography colonography”after“screening flexible sigmoidoscopy”.

=={{anchor|HABFDF64C27F34BA8BFF297873C12CFE9}}(d) Effective date– ==

The amendments made by this section shall apply to items and services furnished on or after January 1, 2013.

={{anchor|H44256F56CFCD40559BEC017909BF84A6}}Sec. 3. Exemption of screening computed tomography colonography from special rule on payment for imaging services=



=={{anchor|H554E0D44A81041E189192B905A7F53E5}}(a) In general– ==

 Section 1848(b)(4)(B) of the Social Security Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395w–4 42 U.S.C. 1395w–4(b)(4)(B)] )is amended by inserting“and screening computed tomography colonography”after“diagnostic and screening mammography”.

=={{anchor|H67ACBA9877E14DD8903C72F5BE51570B}}(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a)shall apply to items and services furnished on or after January 1, 2013.

={{anchor|HE3C626E560634A7DB41797B7F35CBB8F}}Sec. 4. Reports on the status of covering computed tomography colonography as a colorectal cancer screening test under Medicare=



=={{anchor|H3C6F6A7A80C44BC0B2472FAE5A9040FA}}(a) Preliminary report– ==

Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary of Health and Human Services]] shall submit a preliminary report to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] on the status of coverage of computed tomography colonography as a colorectal cancer screening test under the Medicare program under title XVIII of the Social Security Act, including the extent to which such coverage as required by the amendments made by sections 2and 3has been implemented.

=={{anchor|H200D1943A6AD4C5DBD297F4959873482}}(b) Annual report– ==

Not later than September 30 of each fiscal year during the 5-year period beginning with fiscal year 2014, the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] shall submit to the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] , a status report on the following:
:(1) The impact of screening computed tomography colonography on the change in colorectal cancer screening compliance of Medicare beneficiaries.
:(2) The various utilization rates with respect to Medicare beneficiaries for each available colorectal cancer screening option before and after the availability of and coverage of screening computed tomography colonography under the Medicare program pursuant to the enactment of this Act, including—
::(A) by initial CRC screening performed with respect to a Medicare beneficiary per year, including the age of the beneficiary when the initial screening was performed; and
::(B) by follow-on screening performed, whereby the analysis demonstrates to what extent screening computed tomography co­lo­nog­ra­phy was used as a substitute for a previous screening procedure.
:(3) Access to screening computed tomography colonography by Medicare beneficiaries, especially in rural areas or underserved populations, before and after the date of implementation of coverage of such screening benefit under the Medicare program pursuant to the enactment of this Act.
:(4) Recommendations for such legislation and administrative action as the [[w:Department of Health and Human Services|Secretary]] determines appropriate to implement this Act.
