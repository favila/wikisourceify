{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 31, United States Code, to require the Secretary of the Treasury to provide for the purchase of paper United States savings bonds with tax refunds.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Save Access to a Valuable Investment Needed to Generate Savings Act of 2013” or the “SAVINGS Act” .

=Sec. 2. Purchase paper United States savings bonds with tax refund=

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3106 Section 3106of title 31, United States Code] , is amended by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
:(d) 
::(1) During the period ending on December 31, 2018, the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary]] shall provide an option on individual returns of tax under subtitle A of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986to purchase United States savings bonds in paper form with a portion or all of a refund of overpayment of such tax for the purchaser or anyone.
::(2)  [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/31/3106 Paragraph (1)] shall not apply if the Secretary implements an alternative option which—
:::(A) allows for the gifting of United States savings bonds,
:::(B) serves the unbanked, and
:::(C) retains the ability to sign-up on the return of tax..
</blockquote>
