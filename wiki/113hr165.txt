{{Center|A BILL}}

To authorize the placement at the former Navy Dive School at the Washington Navy Yard of a memorial to honor the members of the Armed Forces who have served as divers and whose service in defense of the United States has been carried out beneath the waters of the world.

={{anchor|H46EABEC1BCF84493872EA0505DF8E4A8}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Man in the Sea Memorial Act”.

={{anchor|HAE3E1570D8C442DCAEDE448FCCB80D0C}}Sec. 2. Establishment of military divers memorial at Washington Navy Yard=



=={{anchor|HC84733A4B60244CDB52AFB5CA4D0C925}}(a) Memorial authorized– ==

Consistent with the sense of the [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] expressed in section 2855 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2013, the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary of the Navy]] may permit a third party to establish and maintain, at a suitable location at the former Navy Dive School at the Washington Navy Yard in the District of Columbia, a memorial to honor the members of the United States Armed Forces who have served as divers and whose service in defense of the United States has been carried out beneath the waters of the world.

=={{anchor|H2F1A9498DB14471FB5DC9DF21EEBE921}}(b) Location and design of monument– ==

The actual location at the Washington Navy Yard for the memorial authorized by subsection (a)and the final design of the memorial shall be subject to the approval of the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] . In selecting the site to serve as the location for the memorial, the Secretary shall seek to maximize visitor access to the memorial.

=={{anchor|H4FA39B6657264C748DA8074D77F38ECC}}(c) Military support– ==

The [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] shall provide military ceremonial support at the dedication of the memorial authorized by subsection (a).

=={{anchor|H8E95C404840C4B088E8E9B6458F8D947}}(d) Use of Federal funds prohibited– ==

Federal funds may not be used to design, procure, prepare, install, or maintain the memorial authorized by subsection (a), but the [[w:United States Department of the Navy|Secretary]] may accept and expend contributions of non-Federal funds and resources for such purposes.
