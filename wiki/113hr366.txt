{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit attendance of an animal fighting venture, and
		  for other purposes.

={{anchor|H7929797985D845BCB17180C9C4EF2C84}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the
			 “Animal Fighting Spectator Prohibition
			 Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|HFBC377C23AB349C69DB92BA82C1C3916}}Sec. 2. Prohibition on
			 attending an animal fight or causing a minor to attend an animal
			 fight=

Section 26 of the Animal
			 Welfare Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2156 7 U.S.C. 2156]) is amended—
:(1) in subsection
			 (a)—
::(A) in the heading, by striking
			 “Sponsoring or Exhibiting
			 an Animal in” and inserting “Sponsoring or Exhibiting an Animal in,
			 Attending, or Causing a Minor To Attend”;
::(B) in paragraph (1)—
:::(i) in
			 the heading, by striking “In general” and inserting
			 “Sponsoring or
			 Exhibiting”; and
:::(ii) by
			 striking “paragraph (2)” and inserting “paragraph
			 (3)”;
::(C) by redesignating
			 paragraph (2) as paragraph (3); and
::(D) by inserting after
			 paragraph (1) the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>

===={{anchor|H9499DF2E0C2344248C684EA65F39A9DB}}(2) Attending or
				causing a minor to attend– ====

It
				shall be unlawful for any person to—
:(A) knowingly attend
				an animal fighting venture; or
:(B) knowingly cause a
				minor to attend an animal fighting
				venture.;
				and
</blockquote>

:(2) in subsection (g),
			 by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

<blockquote>
::(5) the term minor means a person
				under the age of 18 years
				old..
</blockquote>


={{anchor|HEB8F412BBE7A42CCB13714490DF87F05}}Sec. 3. Enforcement of
			 animal fighting prohibitions=

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/49 Section 49 of title 18, United States Code],
			 is amended—
:(1) by striking
			 “Whoever” and inserting “(a) In
			 general.—Whoever”;
:(2) in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/49 subsection (a)],
			 as designated by paragraph (1) of this section, by striking “[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/49 subsection
			 (a)],” and inserting “[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/49 subsection (a)(1)],”; and
:(3) by adding at the
			 end the following new subsections:

<blockquote>

==={{anchor|HB5B49DE06BCB407A81F18A674C2E60C1}}(b) Attending an
				animal fighting venture– ===

Whoever violates subsection (a)(2)(A) of
				section 26 of the Animal Welfare Act ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2156 7 U.S.C. 2156]) shall be fined under this
				title, imprisoned for not more than 1 year, or both, for each violation.

==={{anchor|H6544CA1D3D49473A9DAE576F0FB00C9A}}(c) Causing a minor
				To attend an animal fighting venture– ===

Whoever violates subsection (a)(2)(B) of
				section 26 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2156 7 U.S.C. 2156]) of the Animal Welfare Act shall be fined under this
				title, imprisoned for not more than 3 years, or both, for each
				violation..
</blockquote>
