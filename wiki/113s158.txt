For the relief of Dr. Shakil Afridi.
1. Naturalization for Dr. Shakil Afridi


Notwithstanding title III of the Immigration and Nationality Act , or any other provision of law relating to naturalization, Dr. Shakil Afridi is deemed to be a naturalized citizen of the United States as of the date of the enactment of this Act.
