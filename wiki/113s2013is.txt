{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to provide for the removal of Senior Executive Service employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs for performance, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Department of Veterans Affairs Management Accountability Act of 2014   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Removal of Senior Executive Service employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs for performance=



==(a) In general– ==

  Chapter 7of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote> 

==Sec. 713. Senior Executive Service: removal based on performance==



===(a) In general– ===


:(1) Notwithstandingsubchapter V ofchapter 35of title 5,subchapter V ofchapter 75of title 5, or any other provision of law, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may remove any individual who is an employee of the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Department]] from a Senior Executive Service position (as defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/3132  section 3132(a)of title 5]) if the Secretary determines the performance of the individual warrants such removal.
:(2) If the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] so removes such an individual, the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] may—
::(A) remove the individual from the civil service (as defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/2101  section 2101of title 5]); or
::(B) appoint the individual to a General Schedule position at any grade of the General Schedule the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] determines appropriate.

===(b) Notice to Congress– ===

Not later than 30 days after removing an individual from the Senior Executive Service under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/713 subsection (a)], the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary]] shall submit to the [[w:United States Senate Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans’ Affairs of the Senate]] and the [[w:United States House Committee on Veterans' Affairs|Committee on Veterans' Affairs of the House of Representatives]] notice in writing of such removal and the reason for such removal.

===(c) Manner of removal– ===

A removal under this section shall be done in the same manner as the removal of a professional staff member employed by a Member of Congress. . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by adding at the end the following new item:

<blockquote>  713. Senior Executive Service: removal based on performance.  . 
</blockquote>
