{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act
		  of 1977 to clarify that uncertified States and Indian tribes have the authority
		  to use certain payments for certain noncoal reclamation
		  projects.

={{anchor|HB382D0E820374F69B41749FA035638AC}}Section 1. Abandoned mine
			 reclamation=



=={{anchor|H9024AF7367804802953CED1204825C2F}}(a) Reclamation
			 fee– ==

Section 402(g)(6)(A) of the Surface Mining Control and
			 Reclamation Act of 1977 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/30/1232 30 U.S.C. 1232(g)(6)(A)]) is amended by inserting
			 “and section 411(h)(1)” after “paragraphs (1) and
			 (5)”.

=={{anchor|HBDB67A4D153E45CAACF246C1BCB70B32}}(b) Filling voids
			 and sealing tunnels– ==

Section 409(b) of the Surface Mining Control
			 and Reclamation Act of 1977 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/30/1239 30 U.S.C. 1239(b)]) is amended by inserting
			 “and section 411(h)(1)” after “section
			 402(g)”.

=={{anchor|HE0A9578A886043CD8BA94A8CFED4492F}}(c) Use of
			 funds– ==

Section 411(h)(1)(D)(ii) of the Surface Mining Control and
			 Reclamation Act of 1977 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/30/1240a 30 U.S.C. 1240a(h)(1)(D)(ii)]) is amended by striking
			 “section 403” and inserting “section 402(g)(6), 403, or
			 409”.
