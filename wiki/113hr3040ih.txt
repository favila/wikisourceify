{{Center|A BILL}}

To provide for the minimum size of crews of freight trains, and for other purposes.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Safe Freight Act” .

=Sec. 2. Freight train crew size=



==(a) Amendment– ==

Subchapter II ofchapter 201of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote>

==Sec. 20168. Freight train crew size==

Effective 30 days after the date of enactment of the Safe Freight Act, no freight train or light engine used in connection with the movement of freight may be operated unless it has a crew consisting of at least 2 individuals, one of whom is certified under regulations promulgated by the [[w:|Federal Railroad Administration]] as a locomotive engineer pursuant to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/20135 section 20135] , and the other of whom is certified under regulations promulgated by the [[w:|Federal Railroad Administration]] as a conductor pursuant to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/49/20163 section 20163] ..
</blockquote>
