{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 to repeal the authority to make performance-based bonus payments to States.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Stopping Needless Additional Performance Bonuses (SNAP) Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Amendments=



==(a) Repealer– ==

 Section 16 of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2025 7 U.S.C. 2025] )is amended by striking subsection (d).

==(b) Conforming amendments– ==

 Section 16(c) of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/7/2025 7 U.S.C. 2025(c)] )is amended—
:(1) in the first sentence of paragraph (4)by striking “, or performance under the performance measures under subsection (d)” , and
:(2) in the first sentence of paragraph (5)by striking “, or performance under the performance measures under subsection (d)” .
