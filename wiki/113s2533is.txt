{{Center|A BILL}}

 To require the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency to include in any proposed rule that limits greenhouse gas emissions and imposes increased costs on other Federal agencies an offset from funds available to the Administrator for all projected increased costs that the proposed rule would impose on other Federal agencies.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Agency PAYGO for Greenhouse Gases Act   ”. 

=Sec. 2. Offsets for increased costs to Federal agencies for regulations limiting greenhouse gas emissions=



==(a) In general– ==

If the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency]]proposes a rule that limits greenhouse gas emissions and imposes increased costs on one or more other Federal agencies, the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]shall include in the proposed rule an offset from funds available to the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]for all projected increased costs that the proposed rule would impose on other Federal agencies.

==(b) No offsets– ==

If the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]proposes a rule that limits greenhouse gas emissions and imposes increased costs on one or more other Federal agencies but does not provide an offset in accordance with paragraph (1) , the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator]]may not finalize the rule until the promulgation of the final rule is approved by law.

==(c) Restriction– ==

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, if the [[w:United States Environmental Protection Agency|Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency]]proposes a rule described in subsection (a) —
:(1) a Federal agency on which the rule imposes increased costs may not pass the costs imposed by the rule on to any rate payer of the Federal agency; and
:(2) the proposed rule—
::(A) shall be considered to be a rule that imposes increased costs on a Federal agency; and
::(B) shall be subject to this section.
