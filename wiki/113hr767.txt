{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Energy Policy Act of 2005 to modify the Pilot Project offices of the Federal Permit Streamlining Pilot Project.
1. Pilot Project offices of Federal Permit Streamlining Pilot Project


Section 365 of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 ( 42 U.S.C. 15924 ) is amended by striking subsection (d) and inserting the following:


=(d) Pilot project offices=

The following [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Land Management|Bureau of Land Management ]]Offices shall serve as the Pilot Project offices:
: [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Land Management|Rawlins Field Office, Wyoming ]].

: [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Land Management|Buffalo Field Office, Wyoming ]].

: [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Land Management|Montana/Dakotas State Office, Montana ]].

: [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Land Management|Farmington Field Office, New Mexico ]].

: [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Land Management|Carlsbad Field Office, New Mexico ]].

: [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Land Management|Grand Junction/Glenwood Springs Field Office, Colorado ]].

: [[Wikipedia:Bureau of Land Management|Vernal Field Office, Utah ]].
