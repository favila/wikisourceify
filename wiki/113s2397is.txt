{{Center|A BILL}}

 To increase the rates of pay under the General Schedule and other statutory pay systems and for prevailing rate employees by 3.3 percent, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“   Federal Adjustment of Income Rates Act of 2014   ”or the“ FAIR Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Adjustment to rates of pay=



==(a) Statutory pay systems– ==

The adjustment in rates of basic pay for employees under the statutory pay systems (as defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5302  section 5302of title 5, United States Code]) that takes effect in fiscal year 2015 under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5303  section 5303of title 5, United States Code], shall be an increase of 3.3 percent, and such adjustments shall be effective as of the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after January 1, 2015.

==(b) Prevailing rate employees– ==

The adjustment in rates of basic pay for the statutory pay systems that take place in fiscal year 2015 under[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5344 sections5344 ]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5348  5348of title 5, United States Code], shall be equal to the percentage adjustment received by employees in the same location whose rates of basic pay are adjusted pursuant to the statutory pay systems under subsection (a) of this section and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5304 5304 of title 5, United States Code]. Prevailing rate employees at locations where there are no employees whose pay is increased pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5303 sections 5303]and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5304 5304 of title 5]and prevailing rate employees described in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5343 section 5343(a)(5) of title 5], United States Code, shall be considered to be located in the pay locality designated as“Rest of US”pursuant to[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/5304  section 5304of title 5, United States Code], for purposes of this subsection.
