To amend title 10, United States Code, to direct the Secretary of Defense to provide support for Boy Scout Jamborees.
1. Department of Defense support for Boy Scout Jamborees


Section 2554 of title 10, United States Code , is amended—
: in subsection (a) —:: by striking “is hereby authorized ”and inserting “shall ”;:: by striking “to lend to ”and inserting “lend to ”; and:: by striking “to the extent that such items are in stock and items or services are available ”;: in subsection (d) —:: by striking “is hereby authorized ”and inserting “shall ”;:: by striking “to provide ”and inserting “provide ”; and:: in paragraph (2) , by striking “the requirements of military operations ”and inserting “the national security of the United States ”; and: in subsection (g) , by striking “may provide ”and inserting “shall provide ”.
