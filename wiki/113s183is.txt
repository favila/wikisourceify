{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to provide for fairness in hospital payments under the Medicare program.

={{anchor|S1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Hospital Payment Fairness Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|id4292CCD9FAB149BA8B8D63B906B3FB37}}Sec. 2. Sunset of Medicare hospital payment provision=

 Section 3141 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/1395ww 42 U.S.C. 1395ww note] )is amended by inserting“and before October 1, 2013,”after“October 1, 2010,”.
