{{Center|A BILL}}

To direct the Secretary of Defense to reprogram amounts appropriated for fiscal year 2013 for the Department of Defense for the Medium Extended Air Defense System.

={{anchor|HBC3FA84F0D4348FEA3FE9EE4D54E122A}}Section 1. Reprogramming of amounts for fiscal year 2013 for the Department of Defense for the Medium Extended Air Defense System=



=={{anchor|H6FF464BCD23C49FB822FB7FD82779C30}}(a) Reprogramming required– ==



==={{anchor|H611F909A770B4E6DBE9CAC63CF94D9A8}}(1) In general– ===

To carry out section 221 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2013([[Public Law -|Public Law 112–239]] ;126 Stat. 1674), the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] shall reprogram fiscal year 2013 MEADS funds to other missile defense programs and projects that are not part of the Medium Extended Air Defense System.

==={{anchor|HC11E5B172DB84276891FF98952CA7164}}(2) Report– ===

Not later than 15 days after the date on which the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] reprograms fiscal year 2013 MEADS funds under paragraph (1), the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] shall submit to [[w:United States Congress|Congress]] a report describing such reprogramming.

=={{anchor|H47E8114EC87347F4A5E22278969C3676}}(b) Fiscal year 2013 MEADS funds defined– ==

In this section, the term fiscal year 2013 MEADS funds means the $380,900,000 appropriated or otherwise made available by title IV of division C of the Consolidated and Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2013([[Public Law 113-6|Public Law 113–6]] )under the heading“Research, Development, Test and Evaluation, Army”for the Medium Extended Air Defense System.
