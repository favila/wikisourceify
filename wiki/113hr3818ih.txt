{{Center|A BILL}}

 To repeal certain amendments to the Energy Policy and Conservation Act with respect to lighting energy efficiency.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Thomas Edison BULB Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Lighting energy efficiency=



==(a) In general– ==

   Subtitle B of title III of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 ( [[Public Law 110-140|Public Law 110–140]]  )is repealed.

==(b) Application– ==

The  Energy Policy and Conservation Act ( [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/6201 42 U.S.C. 6201 et seq].)shall be applied and administered as if subtitle B of title III of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 (and the amendments made by that subtitle) had not been enacted.
