{{Center|A BILL}}

 To make technical corrections to[[Public Law -|Public Law 110–229]]to reflect the renaming of the Bainbridge Island Japanese American Exclusion Memorial, and for other purposes.    

=Section 1. Bainbridge Island Japanese American Exclusion Memorial=

   Section 313 of the Consolidated Natural Resources Act of 2008 ( [[Public Law 110-229|Public Law 110–229]]  )is amended as follows:
:(1) In the heading of subsection (b), by striking“ Japanese American Memorial ”and inserting“ Japanese American Exclusion Memorial ”.
:(2) In the heading of subsection (c)(5)(C), by striking“ Japanese American Memorial ”and inserting“ Japanese American Exclusion Memorial ”.
:(3) In subsection (c)(5)(C), by striking“Japanese American Memorial”and inserting“Japanese American Exclusion Memorial”.
