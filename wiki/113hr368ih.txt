{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend title 10, United States Code, to require an audiometric test of each member of the Armed Forces before the separation of the member.

=Section 1. Audiometric test required before separation of members of the Armed Forces=



==(a) In general– ==

Chapter 59 of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:

<blockquote>

==Sec. 1179. Audiometric test required==

Under regulations prescribed by the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] , the Secretary of a military department shall ensure that a member of the armed forces under the jurisdiction of the Secretary receives an audiometric test at the 8000 Hz frequency (or other test that the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] determines has the ability to discover potential future hearing loss) to evaluate the hearing of the member during the 90-day period before the date on which the member is discharged, separated, or retired..
</blockquote>


==(b) Clerical amendment– ==

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by adding after the item relating to [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1178 section 1178] the following new item:

<blockquote>1179. Audiometric test required..
</blockquote>


==(c) Effective date– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/10/1179 Section 1179of title 10, United States Code] , as added by subsection (a), shall apply with respect to a member being discharged, separated, or retired from the Armed Forces on or after the date that is 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
