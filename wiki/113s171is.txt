{{Center|A BILL}}

 To appropriate such funds as may be necessary to ensure that members of the Armed Forces, including reserve components thereof, and supporting civilian and contractor personnel continue to receive pay and allowances for active service performed when a Governmentwide shutdown occurs.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Military Pay Continuation Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Emergency appropriation of funds to provide pay and allowances for members of the Armed Forces and supporting civilian and contractor personnel during Governmentwide shutdown=

During a Governmentwide shutdown, the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Secretary of the Treasury]] shall make available to the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] (and the [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary of Homeland Security]] in the case of the [[w:United States Coast Guard|Coast Guard]] ), out of any amounts in the general fund of the [[w:United States Department of the Treasury|Treasury]] not otherwise appropriated, such amounts as the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] (and the [[w:Department of Homeland Security|Secretary of Homeland Security]] in the case of the [[w:United States Coast Guard|Coast Guard]] ) determines to be necessary to continue to provide pay and allowances (without interruption) to the following:
:(1) Members of the Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps, and Coast Guard, including reserve components thereof, who perform active service during the funding gap.
:(2) At the discretion of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] , such civilian personnel of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] who are providing support to the members of the Armed Forces described in paragraph (1) as the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] considers appropriate.
:(3) At the discretion of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary of Defense]] , such personnel of contractors of the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Department of Defense]] who are providing direct support to the members of the Armed Forces described in paragraph (1) as the [[w:United States Department of Defense|Secretary]] considers appropriate. 

=Sec. 3. Governmentwide shutdown defined=

In this Act, the term Governmentwide shutdown means any portion of a fiscal year during which none of the appropriation bills for the fiscal year have become law and an Act or joint resolution making continuing appropriations for the fiscal year is not in effect.
