{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to extend the reduced interest rate for Federal Direct Stafford Loans.

={{anchor|H6E3BF0B6442B45A3B2E427F4A6D1D764}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Stability to Ensure the American Dream for Youth Act”.

={{anchor|H9A6489E659084121A87F4C3AB2648455}}Sec. 2. Interest rate extension=

 Subparagraph (D) of section 455(b)(7) of the Higher Education Act of 1965([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/20/1087e 20 U.S.C. 1087e(b)(7)] ] )is amended—
:(1) in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking“2013”and inserting“2017”; and
:(2) in clause (v), by striking“2013”and inserting“2017”.
