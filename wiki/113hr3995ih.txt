{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the deduction for mortgage insurance premiums.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ Mortgage Tax Cut Extension Act ”. 

=Sec. 2. Extension of deduction for mortgage insurance premiums=



==(a) In general– ==

   [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/163 Section 163(h)(3)(E)(iv)(I)]of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking“December 31, 2013”and inserting“December 31, 2015”.

==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or incurred after December 31, 2013.
