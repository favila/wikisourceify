{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the further extension or establishment of national monuments in New Mexico except by express authorization of Congress.

={{anchor|HD39C7EA0845A43FCBB5DB42C1A4FC6E1}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“New Mexico Land Sovereignty Act”.

={{anchor|HA2B3E0BA793C44BE906DEDAF633EE20F}}Sec. 2. Limitation on further extension or establishment of national monuments in new mexico=

This proviso of the last sentence of the first section of the Act of September 14, 1950(64 Stat. 849, chapter 950;[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text// 16 U.S.C. 431a] ), is amended by inserting“or New Mexico”after“Wyoming”.
