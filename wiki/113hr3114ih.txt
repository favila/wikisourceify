{{Center|A BILL}}

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to repeal the increase in the income threshold used in determining the deduction for medical care.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Helping Families and Seniors Save on Health Care Act of 2013” .

=Sec. 2. Repeal of increase in income threshold for determining medical care deduction=

 Section 9013 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act(and the amendments made by such section) are hereby repealed, and the Internal Revenue Code of 1986shall be applied and administered as if such section (and amendments) had not been enacted.
