{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend title 38, United States Code, to clarify that the estate of a deceased veteran may receive certain accrued benefits upon the death of the veteran, and for other purposes.   

=Section 1. Clarification of eligible recipients of certain accrued benefits upon death of beneficiary=



==(a) Eligibility of estate– ==

 [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5121  Section 5121(a)(2)of title 38, United States Code], is amended—
:(1) in the matter preceding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5121 subparagraph (A)], by inserting“, or estate,”after“person”; and
:(2) by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

<blockquote> 
::(D) The estate of the veteran (unless the estate will escheat). . 
</blockquote>


==(b) Effective date– ==

The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to the death of an individual on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.

==(c) Relief of estate of Shelton Hickerson– ==

Notwithstanding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/38/5121  section 5121of title 38, United States Code], the [[w:United States Department of Veterans Affairs|Secretary of Veterans Affairs]] shall pay to the estate of Shelton Hickerson, formerly of Indianapolis, Indiana, the sum of $377,342, representing the amount that the Secretary awarded to Shelton Hickerson on the date of his death but was not payable to any survivor or the estate of Shelton Hickerson.
