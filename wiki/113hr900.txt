{{Center|A BILL}}

To eliminate the sequestration under section 251A of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985, and for other purposes.

={{anchor|H2F03B571ED244BF3A54BAE79BC72D6EF}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Cancel the Sequester Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H6FB48A6728E049D0AFF2377A8E9E85B1}}Sec. 2. Repeal of 251A sequestration=

 Section 251A of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985is repealed.
