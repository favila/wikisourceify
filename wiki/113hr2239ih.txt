{{Center|A BILL}}

To reduce the number of Federal judgeships for the U.S. Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit.

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the “Stop Court-Packing Act” .

=Sec. 2. Reduction of Federal judgeships for U.S. Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit=

The table contained in [http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/28/44 section 44(a)of title 28, United States Code] , is amended in the item relating to the District of Columbia circuit court of appeals, by striking “11” and inserting “8” .
