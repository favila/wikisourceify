{{Center|A BILL}}

 To amend the Help America Vote Act of 2002 to eliminate straight-party voting from any voting system used for Federal elections.   

=Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“ People Before Party Act of 2013 ”. 

=Sec. 2. Elimination of straight-party voting in Federal elections=

   Section 301(a)(6) of the Help America Vote Act of 2002 ([http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/42/15481 42 U.S.C. 15481(a)(6)])is amended by striking the period at the end of the sentence and inserting“, except that no State may provide a voter with the opportunity to indicate the selection of a political party as a representation of the selection of an individual candidate.”.
