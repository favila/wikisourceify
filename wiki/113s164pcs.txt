{{Center|A BILL}}

To prohibit the United States from providing financial assistance to Pakistan until Dr. Shakil Afridi is freed.

=Section 1. Limitation on foreign assistance to Pakistan=

The United States may not obligate or expend any direct financial assistance to the Government of Pakistan unless the [[w:Executive Office of the President|President]] certifies to [[w:|Congress]] thatâ€”
:(1) Dr. Shakil Afridi has been released from prison in Pakistan;
:(2) any criminal charges brought against Dr. Afridi, including treason, have been dropped; and
:(3) Dr. Afridi has been allowed to leave Pakistan, if such departure is necessary to ensure his freedom.
