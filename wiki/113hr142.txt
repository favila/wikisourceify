{{Center|A BILL}}

To require face to face purchases of ammunition, to require licensing of ammunition dealers, and to require reporting regarding bulk purchases of ammunition.

={{anchor|H8492608F7C0845909B288697B720F428}}Section 1. Short title=

This Act may be cited as the“Stop Online Ammunition Sales Act of 2013”.

={{anchor|H513C1D75DD674F068E1ABBC361913813}}Sec. 2. Limitations on purchases of ammunition=



=={{anchor|H63C5D6EABB304ED484618D1BB63FDFDF}}(a) Licensing of ammunition dealers– ==



==={{anchor|H688B9E82BCCB46CC839DCD2E3C96A31E}}(1) In general– ===

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 Section 923(a) of title 18, United States Code] , is amended in the matter preceding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 paragraph (1)] , in the first sentence, by striking“, or importing or manufacturing”and inserting“or”.

==={{anchor|H50F969AB6B6C4026B0CD707729825393}}(2) Conforming amendment– ===

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/921 Section 921(a)(11)(A) of title 18, United States Code] , is amended by inserting“or ammunition”after“firearms”.

=={{anchor|H2F33846DC9D942BA80019BAE87C8E376}}(b) Requirement for face to face sales of and licensing To sell ammunition– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 Section 922 of such title] is amended—
:(1) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 subsection (a)(1)] —
::(A) by striking“for any person—”and all that follows through“(A) except”and inserting“(A) for any person except”; and
::(B) by striking[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 subparagraph (B)] and inserting the following:

<blockquote>
:::(B) for—
::::(i) any person except a licensed importer, licensed manufacturer, or licensed dealer, to—
:::::(I) sell ammunition, except that this subclause shall not apply to a sale of ammunition by a person to a licensed importer, licensed manufacturer, or licensed dealer; or
:::::(II) engage in the business of importing or manufacturing ammunition, or in the course of such business, to ship, transport, or receive any ammunition; or
::::(ii) a licensed importer, licensed manufacturer, or licensed dealer to transfer ammunition to a person unless the licensed importer, licensed manufacturer, or licensed dealer has verified the identity of the transferee by examining a valid identification document (as defined in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1028 section 1028(d) of this title] ) of the transferee containing a photograph of the transferee;; and
</blockquote>

:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 subsection (b)(5)] , by striking“or armor-piercing”.

=={{anchor|H138AFB1A1BFC4ADF9C484C42A40C950F}}(c) Limit on shipping and transporting of ammunition– ==

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 Section 922(a)(2) of such title] is amended—
:(1) in the matter preceding[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 subparagraph (A)] , by inserting“, or to ship or transport any ammunition,”after“any firearm”; and
:(2) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/922 subparagraph (B)] , by inserting“or ammunition”after“a firearm”.

=={{anchor|H539FE1C0B67240E9BAF658CE24CC034E}}(d) Recordkeeping regarding ammunition– ==



==={{anchor|HC2C5423FDC38481BBE6633277A846BE3}}(1) In general– ===

[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 Section 923(g) of such title] is amended—
:(A) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 paragraph (1)(A)] —
::(i) in the first sentence, by inserting“or ammunition”after“other disposition of firearms”; and
::(ii) in the third sentence, by striking“, or any licensed importer or manufacturer of ammunition,”and inserting“, or any licensed importer, manufacturer, or dealer of ammunition,”; and
:(B) in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 paragraph (3)] , by adding at the end the following:

<blockquote>
::(C) Each licensee shall prepare a report of multiple sales or other dispositions whenever the licensee sells or otherwise disposes of, at one time or during any 5 consecutive business days, more than 1,000 rounds of ammunition to an unlicensed person. The report shall be prepared on a form specified by the [[w:United States Department of Justice|Attorney General]] and forwarded to the office specified thereon and to the department of State police or State law enforcement agency of the State or local law enforcement agency of the local jurisdiction in which the sale or other disposition took place, not later than the close of business on the day that the multiple sale or other disposition occurs..
</blockquote>


==={{anchor|H6F99B11C048C47A4817039BAFE22C715}}(2) Conforming amendment– ===

 Section 4182(d) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986(relating to exemptions relating to firearms) is amended by inserting“and except as provided in[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 paragraph (1)(A)] and[http://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/923 (3)(C) of section 923(g) of title 18, United States Code] ,”before“no person holding a Federal license”.
