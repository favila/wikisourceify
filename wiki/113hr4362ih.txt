{{Center|A BILL}}

 To prohibit United States contributions to the United Nations Population Fund.   

=Section 1. Prohibition on United States contributions to the United Nations Population Fund=

No funds available to the [[w:United States Department of State|Department of State]]or any other department or agency may be used to provide contributions directly or indirectly to the [[w:United Nations|United Nations]]Population Fund.
